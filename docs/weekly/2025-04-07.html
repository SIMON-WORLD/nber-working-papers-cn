The Political Consequences of Controversial Education Reform: Lessons from Wisconsin’s Act 10

有争议的教育改革的政治后果：威斯康星州第10号法案的教训

Barbara Biasi, Wayne Aaron Sandholtz

Programs：ED, LS, PE, POL；JEL：I20, J31, J45, P11, P46

Abstract

Public service reforms often provoke political backlash. Can they also yield political benefits for the politicians who champion them? We study a Wisconsin law that weakened teachers’ unions and liberalized pay, prompting mass protests. Exploiting its staggered implementation across school districts, we find that the reform cut union revenues, raised student test scores, and increased pay for some teachers. Exposure to the law increased the incumbent governor’s vote share by about 20% of his margin of victory and reduced campaign contributions to his opponent. Gains were larger in districts with stronger unions ex ante and in those where more voters benefited from the reform. Our findings highlight how even politically risky reforms can generate electoral benefits under the right circumstances.

中文摘要

公共服务改革常常引发政治反弹。它们能否也为倡导改革的政客带来政治利益？我们研究了一项威斯康星州的法律，该法律削弱了教师工会并放开了薪酬，引发了大规模抗议。利用该法律在各学区分阶段实施的特点，我们发现这项改革削减了工会收入，提高了学生考试成绩，并增加了部分教师的薪酬。接触该法律使现任州长的得票率增加了约其胜选差额的20%，并减少了对其对手的竞选捐款。在工会原先实力更强的学区和更多选民从改革中受益的学区，收益更大。我们的发现突显了即使在政治上有风险的改革，在适当条件下也能产生选举收益。

No. 2NBER w33664

Taxes on Lifetime Income: A Good Idea?

对终生收入征税：一个好主意？

Dirk Krueger, Chunzan Wu

Programs：EFG, PE；JEL：E60, H20

Abstract

In standard life-cycle models, household consumption and welfare more strongly depend on lifetime income, but most countries base income taxes on current income and use progressive taxes to reduce inequality and provide social insurance. Is lifetime income a better tax base for governments seeking to provide such social insurance and redistribution? To answer this question, we build a quantitative life-cycle model of heterogeneous households with idiosyncratic wage risks and endogenous labor supply, and calibrate it to the U.S. economy. We document that switching to a lifetime income tax leads to a more efficient distribution of hours worked over time and across states of the world. This benefit rises with tax progressivity under a lifetime income tax, whereas the opposite is true under an annual income tax. Consequently, the optimal lifetime income tax is more progressive and achieves larger ex-ante welfare for a cohort of households than the optimal annual income tax.

中文摘要

在标准的生命周期模型中，家庭消费和福利更强烈地依赖于终生收入，但大多数国家基于当前收入征收所得税，并使用累进税来减少不平等和提供社会保险。对于寻求提供此类社会保险和再分配的政府而言，终生收入是更好的税基吗？为了回答这个问题，我们构建了一个包含异质性家庭、具有特质性工资风险和内生劳动供给的定量生命周期模型，并将其校准到美国经济。我们证明，转向终生收入税会导致工作时间在时间维度和不同世界状态下更有效的分配。在终生收入税下，这种收益随着税收累进性的提高而增加，而在年度收入税下则相反。因此，最优的终生收入税比最优的年度收入税更具累进性，并且能为一代家庭实现更大的事前福利。

Forward Selection Fama-MacBeth Regression with Higher Order Asset-Pricing Factors

具有高阶资产定价因子的前向选择Fama-MacBeth回归

Nicola Borri, Denis Chetverikov, Yukun Liu, Aleh Tsyvinski

Programs：AP；JEL：G0

Abstract

We show that the higher-orders and their interactions of the common sparse linear factors can effectively subsume the factor zoo. We propose a forward selection Fama-MacBeth procedure as a method to estimate a high-dimensional stochastic discount factor model, isolating the most relevant higher-order factors. Applying this approach to terms derived from six widely used factors (the Fama-French five-factor model and the momentum factor), we show that the resulting higher-order model with only a small number of selected higher-order terms significantly outperforms traditional benchmarks both in-sample and out-of-sample. Moreover, it effectively subsumes a majority of the factors from the extensive factor zoo, suggesting that the pricing power of most zoo factors is attributable to their exposure to higher-order terms of common linear factors.

中文摘要

我们证明，常见稀疏线性因子的高阶项及其交互项可以有效地囊括因子动物园。我们提出了一种前向选择Fama-MacBeth程序，作为估计高维随机贴现因子模型的方法，以分离出最相关的高阶因子。将这种方法应用于从六个广泛使用的因子（Fama-French五因子模型和动量因子）推导出的项，我们表明，仅包含少量选定高阶项的结果高阶模型在样本内和样本外均显著优于传统基准模型。此外，它有效地囊括了来自广泛因子动物园中的大多数因子，这表明大多数动物园因子的定价能力可归因于它们对常见线性因子高阶项的暴露。

No. 4NBER w33662

Measuring Human Leadership Skills with AI Agents

用人工智能代理衡量人类领导技能

Ben Weidmann, Yixian Xu, David J. Deming

Programs：ED, LS, PR；JEL：J24, M54, O30

Abstract

We show that leadership skill with artificially intelligent (AI) agents predicts leadership skill with human groups. In a large pre-registered lab experiment, human leaders worked with AI agents to solve problems. Their performance on this “AI leadership test” was strongly correlated (ρ=0.81) with their causal impact as leaders of human teams, which we estimate by repeatedly randomly assigning leaders to groups of human followers and measuring team performance. Successful leaders of both humans and AI agents ask more questions and engage in more conversational turn-taking; they score higher on measures of social intelligence, fluid intelligence, and decision-making skill, but do not differ in gender, age, ethnicity or education. Our findings indicate that AI agents can be effective proxies for human participants in social experiments, which greatly simplifies the measurement of leadership and teamwork skills.

中文摘要

我们展示了，与人工智能（AI）代理合作时的领导技能能够预测与人类群体合作时的领导技能。在一个预先注册的大型实验室实验中，人类领导者与AI代理一起解决问题。他们在这一“AI领导力测试”中的表现，与他们作为人类团队领导者所产生的因果影响高度相关（ρ=0.81），我们通过反复将领导者随机分配到人类追随者小组并衡量团队绩效来估计这种因果影响。无论是人类还是AI代理的成功领导者，都会提出更多问题，并进行更多的对话轮换；他们在社会智力、流体智力和决策技能方面的得分更高，但在性别、年龄、种族或教育程度上没有差异。我们的研究结果表明，AI代理可以成为社会实验中人类参与者的有效替代品，这极大地简化了领导力和团队合作技能的衡量。

No. 5NBER w33661

Credit, Land Speculation, and Low-Interest-Rate Policy

信贷、土地投机与低利率政策

Tomohiro Hirano, Joseph E. Stiglitz

Programs：EFG；JEL：E44, O11

Abstract

This paper analyses the impact of credit expansions arising from increases in collateral values or lower interest rate policies on long-run productivity and economic growth in a two-sector endogenous growth economy with credit frictions, with the driver of growth lying in one sector (manufacturing) but not in the other (real estate). We show that it is not so much aggregate credit expansion that matters for long-run productivity and economic growth but sectoral credit expansions. Credit expansions associated mainly with relaxation of real estate financing (capital investment financing) will be productivity-and growth-retarding (enhancing). Without financial regulations, low interest rates and more expansionary monetary policy may so encourage land speculation using leverage that productive capital investment and economic growth are decreased. Unlike in standard macroeconomic models, in ours, the equilibrium price of land will be finite even if the safe rate of interest is less than the rate of output growth.

中文摘要

本文在一个存在信贷摩擦的两部门内生增长经济体中，分析了由抵押品价值上升或低利率政策引发的信贷扩张对长期生产率和经济增长的影响，其中增长的驱动力位于一个部门（制造业）而非另一个部门（房地产）。我们表明，对长期生产率和经济增长而言，重要的并非总信贷扩张，而是部门信贷扩张。主要与房地产融资（资本投资融资）放松相关的信贷扩张将阻碍（促进）生产率和增长。在没有金融监管的情况下，低利率和更具扩张性的货币政策可能会过度鼓励利用杠杆进行土地投机，从而减少生产性资本投资和经济增长。与标准宏观经济模型不同，在我们的模型中，即使安全利率低于产出增长率，土地的均衡价格也是有限的。

No. 6NBER w33660

Voluntary Report of Standardized Test Scores: An Experimental Study

标准化考试成绩的自愿报告：一项实验研究

Marty Haoyuan Chen, Ginger Zhe Jin

Programs：ED, IO；JEL：D61, D63, D8, I23, I24

Abstract

The past few years have seen a shift in many universities’ admission policies from test-required to either test-optional or test-blind. This paper uses laboratory experiments to examine students’ reporting behavior given their application package and the school’s interpretation of non-reported standardized test scores. We find that voluntary disclosure is incomplete and selective, supporting both the incentive of partial unraveling (students with higher scores are more likely to report) and the incentive of reverse unraveling (students facing a more favorable school interpretation of non-reporting are less likely to report). Subjects exhibit some ability to learn about the hidden school interpretation, though their learning is imperfect. Using a structural model of student reporting behavior, we simulate the potential tradeoff between academic preparedness and diversity in a school’s admission cohort. We find that, if students have perfect information about the school’s interpretation of non-reporting, test-blind is the worst and test-required is the best in both dimensions, while test-optional lies between the two extremes. When students do not have perfect information, some test-optional policies can generate more diversity than test-required, because some students with better observable attributes may underestimate the penalty on their non-reporting. This allows the school to admit more students that have worse observable attributes but report standardized test scores. The results are robust to a variety of extensions, including when schools have access to alternative signals of academic ability and standardized test score is a noisy but sufficiently informative measure of student ability.

中文摘要

过去几年，许多大学的招生政策已从要求考试成绩转变为考试可选或考试盲审。本文通过实验室实验，考察了学生根据其申请材料以及学校对未报告标准化考试成绩的解释所做出的报告行为。我们发现，自愿披露是不完全且具有选择性的，这既支持了部分解开的激励（分数较高的学生更有可能报告），也支持了反向解开的激励（面对学校对未报告行为更有利解释的学生报告的可能性更低）。受试者表现出一定的学习能力来了解隐藏的学校解释，尽管他们的学习并不完美。利用学生报告行为的结构模型，我们模拟了学校招生群体中学术准备与多样性之间的潜在权衡。我们发现，如果学生对学校关于未报告行为的解释拥有完全信息，那么考试盲审在两个维度上都是最差的，而要求考试成绩在两个维度上都是最好的，考试可选则介于这两个极端之间。当学生没有完全信息时，一些考试可选政策可以比要求考试政策产生更多的多样性，因为一些具有更好可观察属性的学生可能低估了对其未报告行为的惩罚。这使得学校能够录取更多可观察属性较差但报告了标准化考试成绩的学生。该结果在多种扩展情况下都是稳健的，包括当学校可以获得其他学术能力信号，且标准化考试成绩是衡量学生能力的一个有噪声但信息量足够充分的指标时。

No. 7NBER w33659

The Effects of Eviction on Children

驱逐对儿童的影响

Robert Collinson, Deniz Dutz, John Eric Humphries, Nicholas S. Mader, Daniel Tannenbaum, Winnie van Dijk

Programs：CH, ED, LE, LS, PE；JEL：H0, I24, I30, J0, K0, R0

Abstract

Eviction may be an important channel for the intergenerational transmission of poverty, and concerns about its effects on children are often raised as a rationale for tenant protection policies. We study how eviction impacts children's home environment, school engagement, educational achievement, and high school completion by assembling new data sets linking eviction court records in Chicago and New York to administrative public school records and restricted Census records. To disentangle the consequences of eviction from the effects of correlated sources of economic distress, we use a research design based on the random assignment of court cases to judges who vary in their leniency. We find that eviction increases children's residential mobility, homelessness, and likelihood of doubling up with grandparents or other adults. Eviction also disrupts school engagement, causing increased absences and school changes. While we find little impact on elementary and middle school test scores, eviction substantially reduces high school course credits. Lastly, we find that eviction reduces high school graduation and use a novel bounding method to show that this finding is not driven by differential attrition. The disruptive effects of eviction appear worse for older children and boys. Our evidence suggests that the impact of eviction on children runs through the disruption to the home environment or school engagement rather than deterioration in school or neighborhood quality, and may be moderated by access to family support networks.

中文摘要

驱逐可能是贫困代际传递的一个重要渠道，其对儿童影响的担忧常被作为租户保护政策的理由。我们通过将芝加哥和纽约的驱逐法庭记录与行政公立学校记录及受限人口普查记录相关联，构建了新的数据集，研究了驱逐如何影响儿童的家庭环境、学校参与度、学业成就和高中毕业。为了将驱逐的后果与相关经济困境来源的影响区分开来，我们采用了一种基于案件随机分配给不同宽严程度法官的研究设计。我们发现，驱逐增加了儿童的居住流动性、无家可归的可能性以及与祖父母或其他成年人合住的概率。驱逐还扰乱了学校参与度，导致缺勤增加和转学。虽然我们发现对小学和初中考试成绩影响不大，但驱逐显著减少了高中课程学分。最后，我们发现驱逐降低了高中毕业率，并使用一种新的边界方法表明，这一发现并非由差异性流失驱动。驱逐的破坏性影响对年龄较大的儿童和男孩似乎更严重。我们的证据表明，驱逐对儿童的影响是通过对家庭环境或学校参与度的破坏，而非学校或社区质量的恶化来传导的，并且可能受到家庭支持网络可及性的调节。

No. 8NBER w33658

Information-Concealing Credit Architecture

隐藏信息的信贷架构

Gary B. Gorton, Ye Li, Guillermo Ordoñez

Programs：AP, EFG, ME；JEL：D82, D85, G23

Abstract

When the value of a pledgeable asset (or project) is uncertain, investors are tempted to examine it. The information cost is ultimately borne by the asset owner, reducing her financing capacity. A pecking order emerges. Debt generates a greater financing capacity than equity: unlike equity investors who own the asset directly, creditors own the asset only if the borrower defaults and, therefore, have weaker incentives to acquire information. The probabilistic asset ownership can be further diluted by introducing intermediaries between the borrower and the creditor, leading to a new theory of financial intermediation and credit chains. We demonstrate that the optimal financial architecture involves systematically sequencing multiple intermediaries with heterogeneous information costs and asset correlations, rationalizing the seemingly excessive complexity of intermediated credit flows.

中文摘要

当可质押资产（或项目）的价值不确定时，投资者有动机去检查它。信息成本最终由资产所有者承担，从而减少了她的融资能力。一种融资优序理论由此产生。债务比股权产生更大的融资能力：与直接拥有资产的股权投资者不同，债权人仅在借款人违约时才拥有资产，因此，他们有更弱的动机去获取信息。通过在借款人和债权人之间引入中介机构，这种概率性的资产所有权可以进一步被稀释，从而产生了一种关于金融中介和信贷链的新理论。我们证明了，最优的金融架构涉及系统地安排多个具有异质性信息成本和资产相关性的中介机构，这为看似过度复杂的间接信贷流动提供了合理解释。

No. 9NBER w33657

Leaving Money on the Table

把钱留在桌上

Mackenzie Alston, Tatyana Deryugina, Olga Shurchkov

Programs：PE；JEL：C83, C90

Abstract

There is much disagreement about the extent to which financial incentives motivate study participants. We elicit preferences for being paid for completing a survey, including a one-in-twenty chance of winning a $100 electronic gift card, a guaranteed electronic gift card with the same expected value, and an option to refuse payment. More than twice as many participants chose the lottery as chose the guaranteed payment. Given that most people are risk averse, this pattern suggests that factors beyond risk preferences—such as hassle costs—influenced their decision-making. Almost 20 percent of participants actively refused payment, demonstrating low monetary motivation. We find both systematic and unobserved heterogeneity in the characteristics of who turned down payment. The propensity to refuse payment is more than four times as large among individuals 50 and older compared to younger individuals, suggesting a tradeoff between financially motivating participants and obtaining a representative sample. Overall, our results suggest that modest electronic gift card payments violate key requirements of Vernon Smith’s induced value theory.

中文摘要

关于经济激励在多大程度上能激励研究参与者，存在很大分歧。我们引出了参与者对完成调查获得报酬的偏好，包括二十分之一的机会赢得100美元电子礼品卡、一张具有相同期望值的保证性电子礼品卡，以及拒绝付款的选项。选择抽奖的参与者人数是选择保证性付款的两倍多。鉴于大多数人是风险厌恶的，这种模式表明，除了风险偏好之外的因素——例如麻烦成本——影响了他们的决策。近20%的参与者主动拒绝付款，显示出低货币动机。我们发现，在拒绝付款者的特征上，存在系统性和未观测到的异质性。50岁及以上个体拒绝付款的倾向是较年轻个体的四倍多，这表明在通过经济手段激励参与者与获得代表性样本之间存在权衡。总体而言，我们的结果表明，适度的电子礼品卡付款违反了弗农·史密斯的诱导价值理论的关键要求。

No. 10NBER w33656

Counting the Poor: The Liquidity-Adjusted Supplemental Expenditure Poverty Measure

贫困人口统计：流动性调整后的补充性支出贫困衡量指标

Sung Ah Bahk, John Fitzgerald, Robert A. Moffitt

Programs：PE；JEL：I3

Abstract

Most countries use income as a measure of available resources when calculating household-level poverty status. We introduce a new measure of poverty status to the literature which is based on household expenditure instead. We show that an expenditure-based measure is the theoretically correct measure of resources actually transferred into a given period in the life cycle because it includes spending financed by borrowing and asset drawdowns, which income does not. We also show that the illiquidity of both service flows from durables and the expenditure needed to maintain them, as well as the value of in-kind transfers received, requires an adjustment to both the poverty threshold and available resources because those flows and transfers cannot be used to satisfy any minimum consumption need other than that for a single specific good. Our expenditure-based poverty measure constitutes an internally consistent comparison of liquidity-adjusted needs to the liquid resources available to meet those needs when calculating who is poor and who is not. When applying our new poverty measure to the Consumer Expenditure Survey data from 2009 to 2022, we find expenditure poverty to be below that using income but that the relationship between the two varies strongly with where the poverty threshold is drawn. We show that the impact of tax and transfer programs on poverty using our measure differs from that using the standard approach and could yield a slight reduction in the anti-poverty impact of those programs, that the impact of adding a transportation need to the minimum bundle required to not be poor could raise the poverty rate by about two percentage points, and that our expenditure resource-based measure is lower than that yielded by a consumption measure when using a poverty threshold similar to that used by the U.S. government.

The Effects of Deleting Medical Debt from Consumer Credit Reports

从消费者信用报告中删除医疗债务的影响

Victor Duarte, Julia Fonseca, Divij Kohli, Julian Reif

Programs：CF, EH, PE；JEL：G5, I1

Abstract

One in seven Americans carry medical debt, with $88 billion reported on consumer credit reports. In April 2023, the three major credit bureaus stopped reporting medical debts below $500. We study the effects of this information deletion on consumer credit scores, credit limits and utilization, repayment behavior, and payday borrowing. Using a machine learning model, we show that small medical debts are not meaningfully predictive of defaults, suggesting their deletion should have minimal effect on lending decisions. We test this prediction using two complementary research designs. First, a regression discontinuity analysis comparing individuals above and below the $500 threshold finds no direct benefits from the information deletion, ruling out small changes in credit access. Second, to assess indirect effects, we classify consumers based on whether their predicted default probability increases or decreases when debts are deleted. A difference-in-differences analysis comparing these groups before and after the 2023 policy change reveals no evidence of negative spillover effects. Finally, we show that larger medical debts (≥ $500) are also not meaningfully predictive of default, suggesting that eliminating medical debts entirely from credit reports, as planned under a January 2025 decision by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, is unlikely to affect credit outcomes.

中文摘要

七分之一的美国人背负医疗债务，消费者信用报告中报告的医疗债务总额达880亿美元。2023年4月，三大信用报告机构停止报告低于500美元的医疗债务。我们研究了这一信息删除对消费者信用评分、信用额度及使用率、还款行为以及发薪日借贷的影响。使用机器学习模型，我们表明小额医疗债务对违约的预测能力并不显著，这表明删除这些债务对贷款决策的影响应该微乎其微。我们使用两种互补的研究设计来检验这一预测。首先，一项比较500美元阈值上下个体的断点回归分析发现，信息删除并未带来直接益处，排除了信贷准入方面的微小变化。其次，为了评估间接影响，我们根据债务被删除时其预测违约概率是增加还是减少对消费者进行分类。比较这些群体在2023年政策变化前后的双重差分分析显示，没有证据表明存在负面溢出效应。最后，我们表明，较大的医疗债务（≥500美元）对违约的预测能力同样不显著，这表明，正如消费者金融保护局2025年1月决定所计划的那样，将医疗债务完全从信用报告中删除，不太可能影响信贷结果。

Non-Wage Amenities

非工资福利

Alexandre Mas

Programs：LS；JEL：J0, J3, J30, J31, J32, J33

Abstract

This chapter reviews the analysis of non-wage amenities in the workplace. The competitive model is the point of departure, but the emphasis is on models of imperfect competition that have greater empirical relevance. In addition to the traditional hedonic model for estimating preferences over job characteristics, I review revealed-preference methods that are better suited when there are deviations from perfect competition.

Non-User Utility and Market Power: The Case of Smartphones

非用户效用与市场势力：以智能手机为例

Leonardo Bursztyn, Rafael Jiménez-Durán, Aaron Leonard, Filip Milojević, Christopher Roth

Programs：IO, POL；JEL：D83, D91, J15, P16

Abstract

Firms can increase the demand for their products and consolidate their market power not only by increasing user utility but also by decreasing non-user utility. In this paper, we examine this mechanism by considering the case of smartphones. In particular, Apple has faced criticism for allegedly degrading the Android user experience by making messages to Android devices appear as green bubbles on iPhones—a salient signal often perceived as reflecting a lower socioeconomic status. Using samples of US college students, we show that green bubbles are widely stigmatized and that a majority of both iPhone and Android users would prefer green bubbles to no longer exist. We then conduct an incentivized deactivation experiment, revealing that iPhone users have a significant willingness to pay to prevent their messages from appearing as green bubbles on other iPhones. Next, we examine the market implications of non-user utility and find that respondents are substantially more likely to choose an Android over an iPhone when green bubbles are removed. We conclude by presenting case studies that illustrate how companies use product features to reduce non-user utility in various markets.

中文摘要

企业不仅可以通过提高用户效用，还可以通过降低非用户效用来增加对其产品的需求并巩固其市场势力。在本文中，我们通过考虑智能手机的案例来研究这一机制。具体而言，苹果公司因涉嫌降低安卓用户体验而受到批评，其做法是让发送给安卓设备的信息在iPhone上显示为绿色气泡——这是一个显著的信号，通常被认为反映了较低的社会经济地位。通过对美国大学生样本的研究，我们表明绿色气泡被广泛污名化，并且大多数iPhone和安卓用户都希望绿色气泡不再存在。随后，我们进行了一项激励性的停用实验，揭示出iPhone用户有显著的支付意愿，以防止他们的信息在其他iPhone上显示为绿色气泡。接下来，我们考察了非用户效用的市场影响，发现当绿色气泡被移除时，受访者选择安卓而非iPhone的可能性显著增加。最后，我们通过案例研究来说明企业如何利用产品功能来降低各种市场中的非用户效用。

The Cybernetic Teammate: A Field Experiment on Generative AI Reshaping Teamwork and Expertise

控制论队友：关于生成式人工智能重塑团队合作与专业知识的实地实验

Fabrizio Dell'Acqua, Charles Ayoubi, Hila Lifshitz, Raffaella Sadun, Ethan Mollick, Lilach Mollick, Yi Han, Jeff Goldman, Hari Nair, Stewart Taub, Karim Lakhani

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：M15, M2, O3, O31, O33

Abstract

We examine how artificial intelligence transforms the core pillars of collaboration—performance, expertise sharing, and social engagement—through a pre-registered field experiment with 776 professionals at Procter & Gamble, a global consumer packaged goods company. Working on real product innovation challenges, professionals were randomly assigned to work either with or without AI, and either individually or with another professional in new product development teams. Our findings reveal that AI significantly enhances performance: individuals with AI matched the performance of teams without AI, demonstrating that AI can effectively replicate certain benefits of human collaboration. Moreover, AI breaks down functional silos. Without AI, R&D professionals tended to suggest more technical solutions, while Commercial professionals leaned towards commercially-oriented proposals. Professionals using AI produced balanced solutions, regardless of their professional background. Finally, AI’s language-based interface prompted more positive self-reported emotional responses among participants, suggesting it can fulfill part of the social and motivational role traditionally offered by human teammates. Our results suggest that AI adoption at scale in knowledge work reshapes not only performance but also how expertise and social connectivity manifest within teams, compelling organizations to rethink the very structure of collaborative work.

中文摘要

我们通过一项在宝洁公司（一家全球性消费品公司）与776名专业人士进行的预先注册的实地实验，考察了人工智能如何改变协作的核心支柱——绩效、专业知识共享和社交参与。在应对真实的产品创新挑战时，专业人士被随机分配为与人工智能合作或不合作，以及单独工作或与另一位专业人士在新产品开发团队中工作。我们的研究结果表明，人工智能显著提升了绩效：配备人工智能的个人达到了没有人工智能的团队的水平，这表明人工智能可以有效地复制人类协作的某些益处。此外，人工智能打破了职能壁垒。在没有人工智能的情况下，研发专业人士倾向于提出更多技术性解决方案，而商业专业人士则倾向于提出商业导向的提案。使用人工智能的专业人士，无论其专业背景如何，都产生了平衡的解决方案。最后，人工智能基于语言的界面引发了参与者更积极的自我报告情绪反应，这表明它可以部分履行传统上由人类队友提供的社会和激励角色。我们的结果表明，在知识工作中大规模采用人工智能不仅重塑了绩效，还重塑了专业知识和社交联系在团队中的体现方式，迫使组织重新思考协作工作的基本结构。

The Tokenomics of Staking

质押的代币经济学

Lin William Cong, Zhiheng He, Ke Tang

Programs：AP, IFM；JEL：C73, E42, F43, L86

Abstract

Tokens offer convenience in digital networks and earn rewards when staked for consensus generation or economic activities. In our continuous-time model, agents dynamically allocate wealth over on-platform transactions and staking. Aggregate staking ratio crucially shapes platform productivity, grows userbase, and links staking to endogenous reward rates and price dynamics. Transaction fees, token issuance, and user heterogeneity all affect platform lifecycle. Empirical findings support the theoretical predictions: (i) correlation between staking ratio and reward rate is cross-sectionally positive, but time-series-wise negative; (ii) staking ratios positively predict excess returns; (iii) the convenience wedge generates UIP violations and significant crypto carry premia.

中文摘要

代币在数字网络中提供便利，并在为共识生成或经济活动进行质押时获得奖励。在我们的连续时间模型中，代理人动态地将财富分配于平台内交易和质押。总质押比率关键性地塑造了平台生产力，增长了用户基础，并将质押与内生奖励率和价格动态联系起来。交易费用、代币发行和用户异质性都影响着平台生命周期。实证发现支持理论预测：(i) 质押比率与奖励率之间的相关性在横截面上为正，但在时间序列上为负；(ii) 质押比率正向预测超额收益；(iii) 便利楔子导致了无抛补利率平价的违反和显著的加密货币利差溢价。

Financial and Informational Integration Through Oracle Networks

通过预言机网络实现金融和信息整合

Lin William Cong, Eswar S. Prasad, Daniel Rabetti

Programs：CF, IFM；JEL：F15, F36, G15, G29

Abstract

Oracles are software components that enable data exchange between siloed blockchains and external environments, enhancing smart contract capabilities and platform interoperability. Oracles play key roles in decentralized finance and blockchain applications in centralized finance. We find that integration into decentralized oracle networks is positively associated with key measures of economic activity such as Total Value Locked, triggered by positive network effects in adoption and usage. Our study reveals symbiotic gains from enhanced interoperability and network effects across protocols on a given chain and among integrated chains. Oracle integration appears to improve risk-sharing and mitigates contagion, increasing resilience during turbulent periods in crypto markets. Overall, oracles emerge as a crucial component to enable informational and economic integration in decentralized finance ecosystems.

中文摘要

预言机是软件组件，能够实现孤立的区块链与外部环境之间的数据交换，增强智能合约能力和平台互操作性。预言机在去中心化金融和中心化金融的区块链应用中扮演关键角色。我们发现，接入去中心化预言机网络与总锁仓价值等关键经济活动指标呈正相关，这是由采用和使用中的正网络效应触发的。我们的研究揭示了在特定链上的协议之间以及集成链之间，增强互操作性和网络效应所带来的共生收益。预言机集成似乎改善了风险分担并减轻了传染效应，在加密货币市场的动荡时期增强了韧性。总体而言，预言机成为在去中心化金融生态系统中实现信息和经济整合的关键组成部分。

The Bullwhip: Time-to-Build and Sectoral Fluctuations

牛鞭效应：建设周期与行业波动

Yan Leng, Ernest Liu, Yifei Ren, Aleh Tsyvinski

Programs：EFG, ITI；JEL：E0, E23, E32

Abstract

We develop a theory of sectoral fluctuations driven by the propagation of demand shocks along supply chains with heterogeneous time-to-build production. We solve the model in closed form. Downstream producers respond directly to current demand. Upstream producers, due to time-to-build delays, respond to anticipated future demand. Consequently, hump-shaped demand shocks to downstream goods propagate and amplify along the supply chain, generating pronounced volatility in upstream sectors and creating the bullwhip effect. Empirically and quantitatively, we show that the bullwhip is significant across downstream sectors that are important for final consumption.

中文摘要

我们发展了一个行业波动理论，该理论由需求冲击沿着具有异质性建设周期生产的供应链传播所驱动。我们以闭式解形式求解了模型。下游生产商直接对当前需求做出反应。上游生产商由于建设周期的延迟，则对未来预期需求做出反应。因此，对下游商品的驼峰形需求冲击沿着供应链传播并放大，在上游行业产生显著的波动性，并创造了牛鞭效应。在实证和定量上，我们表明，对于最终消费重要的下游行业，牛鞭效应是显著的。

Interim Valuations, Predictability, and Outcomes in Private Equity

私募股权中的中期估值、可预测性与结果

Ege Y. Ercan, Steven N. Kaplan, Ilya A. Strebulaev

Programs：CF；JEL：G11, G23

Abstract

Using a novel dataset of U.S. buyout and VC investments, we study the informativeness of fund managers’ interim valuation reports about portfolio companies on final outcomes. We find that when investors assess the performance of individual portfolio companies, they can do better than just relying on the most recent reported valuation. The history of reported valuations is also informative. Investments with greater past staleness or more frequent markdowns tend to perform more poorly in the future than other investments. We also find that the timing of an investment’s exit is predictable. Moreover, the combined knowledge of interim realized and unrealized returns, past staleness, and past markdown frequency can help predict whether an investment will end up in the left or right tail of all investments. These predictions are informative as early as the first year of the investment.

中文摘要

利用一个关于美国并购和风险投资的新数据集，我们研究了基金管理人关于投资组合公司的中期估值报告对最终结果的信息含量。我们发现，当投资者评估单个投资组合公司的业绩时，他们可以做得比仅仅依赖最近报告的估值更好。报告估值的过往历史也具有信息量。过去估值更陈旧或更频繁减记的投资，其未来表现往往比其他投资更差。我们还发现，投资退出的时机是可预测的。此外，结合中期已实现和未实现回报、过去估值陈旧度和过去减记频率的知识，可以帮助预测一项投资最终会落在所有投资的左尾还是右尾。这些预测在投资的第一年就具有信息量。

Firm Selection and Growth in Carbon Offset Markets: Evidence from the Clean Development Mechanism

碳抵消市场中的企业选择与增长：来自清洁发展机制的证据

Qiaoyi Chen, Nicholas Ryan, Daniel Xu

Programs：DEV, EEE, IO；JEL：L51, O13, Q54

Abstract

We study carbon offsets sold by firms in China under the Clean Development Mechanism (CDM). We find that offset-selling firms, meant to cut carbon emissions, instead increase them by 49% after starting an offset project. In a model of firm investment decisions and offset review, we estimate that CDM firms increase emissions due to both the selection of higher-growth firms into projects (35 pp) and because offset projects themselves boost firm growth and therefore emissions (14 pp). The CDM reduces global surplus by causing damages from increased emissions four times greater than private gains from trade in the offset market.

Instrumental Variables Methods Reveal Larger Effects of Menopausal Hormone Therapy in the Landmark Women’s Health Initiative Clinical Trial

工具变量方法揭示标志性妇女健康倡议临床试验中绝经期激素治疗的更大效应

Joshua Angrist, Amanda E. Kowalski, Ljubica Ristovska, Marcia L. Stefanick

Programs：AG, EH, LS, PE, TWP；JEL：C26, H0, I1

Abstract

Landmark results from the Women's Health Initiative trial showed that random assignment to menopausal hormone therapy (MHT) elevated risks of breast cancer and other adverse events. Recent analyses argue that MHT risks are small. These analyses report intention-to-treat (ITT) effects, ignoring the fact that many women assigned intervention were non-adherent, while many women assigned control initiated treatment. Instrumental variable (IV) methods and adherence data allow us to estimate effects of MHT on compliers who took MHT if and only if assigned. IV estimates show risks and benefits that are substantially larger than the ITT estimates used to inform MHT guidelines.
