Achieving Universal Health Insurance Coverage in the United States: Addressing Market Failures or Providing a Social Floor?

在美国实现全民健康保险覆盖：解决市场失灵还是提供社会底线？

Katherine Baicker, Amitabh Chandra, Mark Shepard

Programs：EH, PE；JEL：H4, H51, I13

Abstract

The United States spends substantially more on health care than most developed countries, yet leaves a greater share of the population uninsured. We suggest that incremental insurance expansions focused on addressing market failures will propagate inefficiencies and are not likely to facilitate active policy decisions that align with societal coverage goals. By instead defining a basic bundle of services that is publicly financed for all, while allowing individuals to purchase additional coverage, policymakers could both expand coverage and maintain incentives for innovation, fostering universal access to innovative care in an affordable system.

中文摘要

美国在医疗保健上的支出远高于大多数发达国家，但却有更大比例的人口没有保险。我们认为，专注于解决市场失灵的渐进式保险扩张将传播低效率，并且不太可能促进与社会覆盖目标一致的积极政策决策。相反，通过定义一个由公共资金为所有人提供的基本服务包，同时允许个人购买额外保险，政策制定者既可以扩大覆盖范围，又可以保持创新激励，从而在一个可负担的系统中促进全民获得创新医疗服务。

Fixed and Variable Longevity Annuities in Defined Contribution Plans: Optimal Retirement Portfolios Taking Social Security into Account

固定与可变长寿年金在固定缴款计划中的应用：考虑社会保障的最优退休投资组合

Vanya Horneff, Raimond Maurer, Olivia S. Mitchell

Programs：AG, LS；JEL：D91, G11, G14, G22, G53

Abstract

This paper investigates retirees’ optimal purchases of fixed and variable longevity income annuities using their defined contribution (DC) plan assets and given their expected Social Security benefits. As an alternative, we also evaluate using plan assets to boost Social Security benefits through delayed claiming. We determine that including deferred income annuities in DC accounts is welfare enhancing for all sex/education groups examined. We also show that providing access to well-designed variable deferred annuities with some equity exposure further enhances retiree wellbeing, compared to having access only to fixed annuities. Nevertheless, for the least educated, delaying claiming Social Security is preferred, whereas the most educated benefit more from using accumulated DC plan assets to purchase deferred annuities.

中文摘要

本文研究了退休人员如何利用其固定缴款计划资产，并在考虑预期社会保障福利的情况下，最优地购买固定和可变长寿收入年金。作为替代方案，我们还评估了通过延迟领取社会保障来利用计划资产增加福利的效果。我们确定，在固定缴款账户中包含递延收入年金对所有性别/教育群体都有福利提升作用。我们还表明，与仅能获得固定年金相比，提供具有部分股票敞口的精心设计的可变递延年金能进一步提高退休人员的福祉。然而，对于受教育程度最低的人群，延迟领取社会保障更受青睐，而受教育程度最高的人群则更能从使用积累的固定缴款计划资产购买递延年金中受益。

No. 3NBER w30852

Predicting and Preventing Gun Violence: An Experimental Evaluation of READI Chicago

预测与预防枪支暴力：READI芝加哥项目的实验评估

Monica P. Bhatt, Sara B. Heller, Max Kapustin, Marianne Bertrand, Christopher Blattman

Programs：LS；JEL：C53, C93, I38, J08, K42

Abstract

Gun violence is the most pressing public safety problem in American cities. We report results from a randomized controlled trial (N = 2, 456) of a community-researcher partnership called the Rapid Employment and Development Initiative (READI) Chicago. The program offered an 18-month job alongside cognitive behavioral therapy and other social support. Both algorithmic and human referral methods identified men with strikingly high scope for gun violence reduction: for every 100 people in the control group, there were 11 shooting and homicide victimizations during the 20-month outcome period. Fifty-five percent of the treatment group started programming, comparable to take-up rates in programs for people facing far lower mortality risk. After 20 months, there is no statistically significant change in an index combining three measures of serious violence, the study’s primary outcome. Yet there are signs that this program model has promise. One of the three measures, shooting and homicide arrests, declines 65 percent (p = 0.13 after multiple testing adjustment). Because shootings are so costly, READI generates estimated social savings between $182,000 and $916,000 per participant (p = 0.03), implying a benefit-cost ratio between 4:1 and 18:1. Moreover, participants referred by outreach workers—a pre-specified subgroup—show enormous declines in both arrests and victimizations for shootings and homicides (79 and 43 percent, respectively) that remain statistically significant even after multiple testing adjustments. These declines are concentrated among outreach referrals with higher predicted risk, suggesting that human and algorithmic targeting may work better together.

中文摘要

枪支暴力是美国城市最紧迫的公共安全问题。我们报告了一项名为“快速就业与发展倡议”芝加哥项目的社区-研究者合作随机对照试验（N=2,456）的结果。该项目提供为期18个月的工作，同时提供认知行为疗法和其他社会支持。算法和人工推荐方法都识别出男性中枪支暴力减少潜力极高的人群：对照组中每100人就有11人在20个月的结果期内成为枪击和凶杀受害者。55%的治疗组开始参与项目，这与针对死亡风险低得多人群的项目的参与率相当。20个月后，作为研究主要结果的、结合了三种严重暴力衡量指标的指数没有统计学显著变化。然而，有迹象表明这种项目模式具有前景。其中一项衡量指标——枪击和凶杀逮捕——下降了65%（多重检验调整后p=0.13）。由于枪击事件代价高昂，READI项目为每位参与者带来了估计在182,000美元至916,000美元之间的社会节省（p=0.03），意味着效益成本比在4:1至18:1之间。此外，由外展工作人员推荐的参与者——一个预先指定的子群体——在枪击和凶杀的逮捕与受害方面均显示出巨大下降（分别为79%和43%），即使在多重检验调整后仍具有统计学显著性。这些下降集中在预测风险较高的外展推荐人群中，表明人工和算法定位可能协同作用更好。

No. 4NBER w30851

Mandatory Seatbelt Laws and Traffic Fatalities: A Reassessment

强制安全带法与交通事故死亡：重新评估

D. Mark Anderson, Yang Liang, Joseph J. Sabia

Programs：EH, LE, PE；JEL：I12, K32, K42

Abstract

Using data from the Fatality Analysis Reporting System for the period 1983-1997, Cohen and Einav (Review of Economics and Statistics 2003; 85(4): 828–843) found that mandatory seatbelt laws were associated with a 4 to 6 percent reduction in traffic fatalities among motor vehicle occupants. After successfully replicating their two-way fixed effects estimates, we (1) add 22 years of data (1998-2019) to capture additional seatbelt policy variation and observe a longer post-treatment period, (2) employ the interaction-weighted estimator proposed by Sun and Abraham (2021) to address potential bias due to heterogeneous and dynamic treatment effects, and (3) estimate event-study models to investigate pre-treatment trends and explore lagged post-treatment effects. Consistent with Cohen and Einav (2003), our updated estimates show that primary seatbelt laws are associated with a 5 to 9 percent reduction in fatalities among motor vehicle occupants. Estimated effects of secondary seatbelt laws are smaller in magnitude and sensitive to model choice.

中文摘要

Cohen和Einav（《经济学与统计学评论》2003；85(4): 828–843）使用1983-1997年期间致命事故分析报告系统的数据发现，强制安全带法与机动车乘员交通事故死亡人数减少4%至6%相关。在成功复制他们的双向固定效应估计后，我们（1）增加了22年（1998-2019年）的数据以捕捉额外的安全带政策变化并观察更长的处理后期，（2）采用Sun和Abraham（2021）提出的交互加权估计量来解决因异质性和动态处理效应导致的潜在偏差，以及（3）估计事件研究模型以调查处理前趋势并探索滞后的处理后效应。与Cohen和Einav（2003）一致，我们更新的估计显示，主要安全带法与机动车乘员死亡人数减少5%至9%相关。次要安全带法的估计效应幅度较小，且对模型选择敏感。

A Simple Rational Expectations Model of the Voltage Effect

电压效应的一个简单理性预期模型

Omar Al-Ubaydli, Chien-Yu Lai, John A. List

Programs：EEE, PE, POL；JEL：C93, D61, D90

Abstract

The “voltage effect” is defined as the tendency for a program’s efficacy to change when it is scaled up, which in most cases results in the absolute size of a program’s treatment effects to diminish when the program is scaled. Understanding the scaling problem and taking steps to diminish voltage drops are important because if left unaddressed, the scaling problem can weaken the public’s faith in science, and it can lead to a misallocation of public resources. There exists a growing literature illustrating the prevalence of the scaling problem, explaining its causes, and proposing countermeasures. This paper adds to the literature by providing a simple model of the scaling problem that is consistent with rational expectations by the key stakeholders. Our model highlights that asymmetric information is a key contributor to the voltage effect.

中文摘要

“电压效应”被定义为项目在扩大规模时其有效性发生变化的趋势，在大多数情况下，这会导致项目处理效应的绝对规模在项目扩大时减小。理解规模问题并采取措施减少电压下降非常重要，因为如果不加以解决，规模问题会削弱公众对科学的信心，并可能导致公共资源的错配。已有越来越多的文献说明了规模问题的普遍性，解释了其原因，并提出了对策。本文通过提供一个与关键利益相关者理性预期相一致的简单规模问题模型，为文献做出了贡献。我们的模型强调，信息不对称是电压效应的一个关键促成因素。

The Aggregate Effects of Global and Local Supply Chain Disruptions: 2020–2022

全球与局部供应链中断的总体效应：2020–2022年

George A. Alessandria, Shafaat Y. Khan, Armen Khederlarian, Carter B. Mix, Kim J. Ruhl

Programs：EFG, IFM, ITI；JEL：E0, F1, F4

Abstract

We study the aggregate effects of supply-chain disruptions in the post-pandemic period in a heterogeneous-firm, general equilibrium model with input-output linkages and a rich set of supply chain frictions: uncertain shipping delays, fixed order costs, and storage costs. Firms optimally hold inventories that depend on the source of supply, domestic or imported. Increases in shipping times are contractionary, raise prices, and increase stockouts, particularly for goods intensive in delayed inputs. These effects are larger when inventories are already at low levels. We fit the model to the U.S. and global economies from 2020–2022 and estimate large aggregate effects of supply disruptions. Our model predicts that the boost in output from reducing delays will be smaller than the contraction from the waning effects of stimulus.

中文摘要

我们研究了后疫情时期供应链中断在异质性企业一般均衡模型中的总体效应，该模型包含投入产出关联以及一系列供应链摩擦：不确定的运输延误、固定订单成本和仓储成本。企业根据国内或进口供应来源，最优地持有库存。运输时间的增加具有紧缩效应，推高价格，并加剧缺货，尤其是对于依赖延迟投入的商品。当库存已处于低位时，这些效应更为显著。我们将模型拟合至2020–2022年的美国和全球经济，并估计出供应中断的巨大总体效应。我们的模型预测，减少延误带来的产出提升将小于刺激措施效应消退所导致的产出收缩。

Suboptimal Household Investment and Information-Processing Frictions: Evidence from 529 College Savings Plans

家庭次优投资与信息处理摩擦：来自529大学储蓄计划的证据

James J. Li, Olivia S. Mitchell, Christina Zhu

Programs：AG；JEL：G11, G14, G15, G23, G53

Abstract

We use the 529 college savings plan setting to investigate whether and why households make suboptimal choices to invest in local assets. We estimate that 67% of open accounts between 2010 and 2020 were located suboptimally, due to the plans’ tax inefficiencies and high expenses. Over the accounts’ projected lifetimes, such investments yielded expected losses of 8% on average, or $15.6 billion in 2020 alone. We then investigate why suboptimal investment is so prevalent. Consistent with households’ lack of understanding of state-level tax benefits, we find that a meaningful proportion of households does not account for the potential tax benefits and costs of local versus nonlocal 529 investment. Household financial literacy and plan disclosure complexity appear to explain suboptimal investment patterns, which further supports the role of information-processing frictions. Our study presents novel evidence on individuals’ preferences for local assets and how information-processing frictions shape their investment decisions, reducing their financial well-being.

中文摘要

我们利用529大学储蓄计划这一场景，研究家庭是否以及为何做出投资本地资产的次优选择。我们估计，2010年至2020年间，67%的开立账户因计划的税收低效和高费用而处于次优位置。在账户的预计存续期内，此类投资平均造成8%的预期损失，仅2020年就达156亿美元。随后，我们探究次优投资为何如此普遍。与家庭缺乏对州级税收优惠的理解相一致，我们发现相当一部分家庭并未考虑本地与非本地529投资的潜在税收收益和成本。家庭金融素养和计划披露的复杂性似乎解释了次优投资模式，这进一步支持了信息处理摩擦的作用。我们的研究提供了关于个人对本地资产偏好的新证据，以及信息处理摩擦如何塑造其投资决策并降低其财务福祉。

No. 8NBER w30847

Like an Ink Blot on Paper: Testing the Diffusion Hypothesis of Mass Migration, Italy 1876-1920

如墨渍在纸上：检验大规模移民的扩散假说，意大利1876-1920年

Yannay Spitzer, Ariell Zimran

Programs：DAE, DEV, LS；JEL：F22, J61, N33, N34

Abstract

To explain the evolution of mass migration from Italy (1876--1920), we introduce the diffusion hypothesis, which augments traditional models of migration by allowing the spatial diffusion of migration over social networks in the country of origin. We test and validate the predictions of this theory using a new and comprehensive municipality- and district-level panel of emigration data over four decades. Italian migration began in a few separate epicenters from which it expanded gradually over time in an orderly spatial movement that dominated local push factors. While the main patterns that we document are parsimoniously explained by the diffusion hypothesis, they are difficult to rationalize by alternative theories. Confirming the diffusion hypothesis in the Italian case suggests that networks may be far more important than is commonly understood in shaping other movements of mass migration and may shape them in ways that have not previously been understood.

中文摘要

为解释意大利（1876–1920年）大规模移民的演变，我们提出了扩散假说，该假说通过允许移民在原籍国社会网络中的空间扩散，对传统移民模型进行了补充。我们利用跨越四十年的全新、全面的市镇和地区层面移民面板数据，检验并验证了这一理论的预测。意大利移民始于几个独立的中心，随后随着时间的推移，以一种有序的空间运动逐渐扩展，主导了当地的推力因素。虽然我们记录的主要模式被扩散假说简洁地解释，但它们很难通过其他理论得到合理化。在意大利案例中确认扩散假说表明，网络在塑造其他大规模移民运动中的重要性可能远超普遍认知，并且可能以先前未被理解的方式塑造这些运动。

The Health of Democracies During the Pandemic: Results from a Randomized Survey Experiment

疫情期间民主的健康状况：来自随机调查实验的结果

Marcella Alsan, Luca Braghieri, Sarah Eichmeyer, Minjeong Joyce Kim, Stefanie Stantcheva, David Y. Yang

Programs：EH, POL；JEL：I1, P0

Abstract

Concerns have been raised about the “demise of democracy”, possibly accelerated by pandemic-related restrictions. Using a survey experiment involving 8,206 respondents from five Western democracies, we find that subjects randomly exposed to information regarding civil liberties infringements undertaken by China and South Korea to contain COVID-19 became less willing to sacrifice rights and more worried about their long-term-erosion. However, our treatment did not increase support for democratic procedures more generally, despite our prior evidence that pandemic-related health risks diminished such support. These results suggest that the start of the COVID-19 crisis was a particularly vulnerable time for democracies.

中文摘要

人们对“民主的消亡”表示担忧，这可能因疫情相关限制而加速。通过对来自五个西方民主国家的8206名受访者进行一项调查实验，我们发现，随机接触到有关中国和韩国为遏制COVID-19而采取的侵犯公民自由信息的受试者，变得更不愿意牺牲权利，并更担心权利的长期侵蚀。然而，尽管我们先前有证据表明疫情相关的健康风险削弱了对民主程序的支持，但我们的处理并未普遍增加对民主程序的支持。这些结果表明，COVID-19危机的开始对民主国家来说是一个特别脆弱的时期。

No. 10NBER w30845

The Strange and Awful Path of Productivity in the U.S. Construction Sector

美国建筑业生产率奇怪而可怕的路径

Austan Goolsbee, Chad Syverson

Programs：EFG, IO, PR；JEL：D2, E23, L7

Abstract

Aggregate data show a large and decades-long decline in construction sector productivity. This decline in such a large sector has had a material effect on secular productivity growth for the economy as a whole. Prior work has focused on the role of potential measurement problems in construction, particularly output deflators in the measurement of productivity. This paper brings some new evidence to bear on the industry’s measured productivity problems and suggests that measurement error is probably not the sole source of the stagnation. First, using measures of physical productivity in housing construction, productivity is falling or, at best, stagnant over multiple decades. Second, there has been a noticeable decline over time in the efficiency with which construction firms translate materials inputs into output, and a corresponding shift toward more value-added-intensive production. Third, using state-level data, we do not find evidence of patterns of within-industry reallocation that might be expected of efficiently operating input and output markets. States with more productive construction sectors do not see growth in their shares of total U.S. construction activity; if anything, their shares fall. This may point to frictions in these markets that slow or stop what is in many other markets an important channel for productivity growth.

中文摘要

总体数据显示，建筑业生产率出现了长达数十年的巨大下滑。这一庞大部门的下滑对整个经济的长期生产率增长产生了实质性影响。先前的研究侧重于建筑业中潜在的衡量问题，特别是生产率衡量中的产出平减指数。本文为行业衡量的生产率问题提供了一些新证据，并表明测量误差可能并非停滞的唯一来源。首先，使用住房建筑中的实物生产率衡量指标，生产率在数十年间呈下降趋势，或充其量停滞不前。其次，建筑企业将材料投入转化为产出的效率随时间显著下降，并相应转向更依赖增加值的生产。第三，利用州级数据，我们没有发现行业内重新配置模式的证据，而这种模式在高效运作的投入和产出市场中是可以预期的。生产率较高的建筑业的州，其在美国建筑活动总量中的份额并未增长；如果有的话，它们的份额反而下降。这可能表明这些市场存在摩擦，减缓或阻止了在许多其他市场中成为生产率增长重要渠道的进程。

No. 11NBER w30844

Interest Rates and World Trade: An 'Austrian' Perspective

利率与国际贸易：一个“奥地利学派”视角

Pol Antràs

Programs：IFM, ITI；JEL：F1, F10, F12, F65

Abstract

This paper develops a framework to study the interplay between world trade and interest rates. The model incorporates an explicit notion of time and of production length, along the lines of the ‘Austrian’ tradition of Böhm-Bawerk (1889). Changes in the interest rate affect production lengths, labor productivity, and the financial costs of exporting. I decompose the response of the volume of world trade to changes in the interest rate into four components: (i) a labor productivity effect, (ii) a propensity to consume out of labor income effect, (iii) a temporal dimension of variable trade costs effect, and (iv) a selection into exporting effect.

中文摘要

本文构建了一个研究国际贸易与利率之间相互作用的框架。该模型沿袭了Böhm-Bawerk（1889）的“奥地利学派”传统，纳入了时间和生产长度的明确概念。利率的变化会影响生产长度、劳动生产率和出口的财务成本。我将世界贸易量对利率变化的响应分解为四个组成部分：（i）劳动生产率效应，（ii）劳动收入中消费倾向效应，（iii）可变贸易成本的时间维度效应，以及（iv）出口选择效应。

No. 12NBER w30843

Two Decades of Social Security Claiming

社会保障申领的二十年

Sita Slavov

Programs：AG；JEL：G59, H55, J26

Abstract

Twenty years ago, the adjustment to monthly Social Security benefits for early or delayed claiming was, on average, roughly actuarially fair, although some subsets of individuals could gain from delay. Since then, delaying claiming has become much more attractive thanks to three factors: a more generous delayed retirement credit, improvements in mortality, and historically low real interest rates. In this article, I examine how these three factors influence optimal claiming behavior. I also discuss empirical patterns of claiming across individuals and over time, as well as explanations for these patterns. I argue that although many people appear to claim suboptimally early, this behavior may be changing as information spreads about the importance of the claiming decision. Finally, I discuss policy towards claiming and the impact that an increase in strategic claiming could have on Social Security’s finances.

中文摘要

二十年前，对提前或延迟申领月度社会保障福利的调整，平均而言大致是精算公平的，尽管某些人群可能从延迟中获益。自那以后，由于三个因素，延迟申领变得更具吸引力：更慷慨的延迟退休积分、死亡率的改善以及历史低位的实际利率。在本文中，我考察了这三个因素如何影响最优申领行为。我还讨论了个人之间和随时间变化的申领经验模式，以及对这些模式的解释。我认为，尽管许多人似乎过早地申领，但这种情况可能随着关于申领决策重要性的信息传播而改变。最后，我讨论了针对申领的政策，以及战略性申领增加可能对社会保障财务状况产生的影响。

No. 13NBER w30842

Internalizing Externalities through Public Pressure: Transparency Regulation for Fracking, Drilling Activity and Water Quality

通过公众压力内部化外部性：水力压裂透明度监管、钻井活动与水质

Pietro Bonetti, Christian Leuz, Giovanna Michelon

Programs：CF, EEE, POL；JEL：D62, G38, K22, K32, L71, L72, M41, M48, Q53

Abstract

The rise of shale gas and tight oil development has triggered a major debate about hydraulic fracturing (HF). In an effort to bring light to HF practices and their potential risks to water quality, many U.S. states have mandated disclosure for HF wells and the fluids used. We employ this setting to study whether targeting corporate activities that have dispersed externalities with transparency reduces their environmental impact. Examining salt concentrations that are considered signatures for HF impact, we find significant and lasting improvements in surface water quality between 9-14% after the mandates. Most of the improvement comes from the intensive margin. We document that operators pollute less per unit of production, cause fewer spills of HF fluids and wastewater and use fewer hazardous chemicals. Turning to how transparency regulation works, we show that it increases public pressure and enables social movements, which facilitates internalization.

中文摘要

页岩气和致密油开发的兴起引发了关于水力压裂（HF）的重大辩论。为了揭示水力压裂实践及其对水质的潜在风险，美国许多州已强制要求披露水力压裂井及其使用的压裂液。我们利用这一场景，研究针对具有分散外部性的企业活动实施透明度监管，是否能减少其环境影响。通过考察被视为水力压裂影响标志的盐浓度，我们发现强制披露后地表水质量显著且持久地改善了9-14%。大部分改善来自集约边际。我们记录到，运营商单位产量的污染减少，水力压裂液和废水的泄漏减少，并且使用的危险化学品减少。关于透明度监管如何发挥作用，我们表明它增加了公众压力并促进了社会运动，从而有助于外部性的内部化。

No. 14NBER w30841

Feedback and Contagion through Distressed Competition

困境竞争中的反馈与传染效应

Hui Chen, Winston Wei Dou, Hongye Guo, Yan Ji

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, IO, LE, PR；JEL：C73, D43, G12, L13, O33

Abstract

Firms tend to compete more aggressively in financial distress; this intensified competition, in turn, reduces profit margins, pushing themselves further into distress and adversely affecting their industry peers. To study such feedback and contagion effects, we incorporate strategic competition into a dynamic model with long-term defaultable debt, exploring various peer interactions like predation and price war. The feedback effect represents a novel source of financial distress costs associated with leverage, which helps explain the negative profitability-leverage relation across industries. Owing to the contagion effect, firms’ optimal leverage is often excessively high from an industry perspective, undermining the industry's financial stability.

中文摘要

企业在财务困境中往往竞争更为激烈；这种加剧的竞争反过来会降低利润率，使企业自身进一步陷入困境，并对行业内的其他企业产生不利影响。为了研究这种反馈与传染效应，我们将战略竞争纳入一个包含长期可违约债务的动态模型中，探讨了掠夺性定价和价格战等多种同业互动形式。反馈效应代表了与杠杆相关的一种新的财务困境成本来源，这有助于解释跨行业观察到的盈利与杠杆之间的负相关关系。由于传染效应，从行业角度来看，企业的最优杠杆水平往往过高，从而损害了行业的金融稳定性。

No. 15NBER w30840

Opiates of the Masses? Deaths of Despair and the Decline of American Religion

大众的鸦片？绝望之死与美国宗教的衰落

Tyler Giles, Daniel M. Hungerman, Tamar Oostrom

Programs：EH, PE；JEL：I18, J11, Z12

Abstract

In recent decades, death rates from poisonings, suicides, and alcoholic liver disease have dramatically increased in the United States. We show that these "deaths of despair" began to increase relative to trend in the early 1990s, that this increase was preceded by a decline in religious participation, and that both trends were driven by middle-aged white Americans. Using repeals of blue laws as a shock to religiosity, we confirm that religious practice has significant effects on these mortality rates. Our findings show that social factors such as organized religion can play an important role in understanding deaths of despair.

中文摘要

近几十年来，美国因中毒、自杀和酒精性肝病导致的死亡率急剧上升。我们表明，这些“绝望之死”相对于趋势的上升始于20世纪90年代初，而在此之前，宗教参与度已经下降，并且这两种趋势都是由中年美国白人推动的。利用蓝色法规的废除作为对宗教信仰的冲击，我们证实了宗教实践对这些死亡率具有显著影响。我们的发现表明，有组织的宗教等社会因素在理解绝望之死方面可以发挥重要作用。

No. 16NBER w30839

Economics and Measurement: New measures to model decision making

经济学与测量：用于建模决策的新方法

Ingvild Almås, Orazio Attanasio, Pamela Jervis

Programs：CH, DEV, ED, LS；JEL：A1, D1

Abstract

Most empirical work in economics has considered only a narrow set of measures as meaningful and useful to characterize individual behavior, a restriction justified by the difficulties in collecting a wider set. However, this approach often forces the use of strong assumptions to estimate the parameters that inform individual behavior and identify causal links. In this paper, we argue that a more flexible and broader approach to measurement could be extremely useful and allow the estimation of richer and more realistic models that rest on weaker identifying assumptions. We argue that the design of measurement tools should interact with, and depend on, the models economists use. Measurement is not a substitute for rigorous theory, it is an important complement to it, and should be developed in parallel to it. We illustrate these arguments with a model of parental behavior estimated on pilot data that combines conventional measures with novel ones.

中文摘要

经济学中的大多数实证工作只考虑了一小部分被认为有意义且有用的测量指标来刻画个体行为，这种限制是由于收集更广泛数据的困难而导致的。然而，这种方法常常迫使研究者使用强假设来估计反映个体行为的参数并识别因果联系。在本文中，我们认为，采用更灵活、更广泛的测量方法可能极为有用，并能够估计出更丰富、更现实的模型，这些模型依赖于更弱的识别假设。我们认为，测量工具的设计应与经济学家使用的模型相互作用并依赖于这些模型。测量并非严谨理论的替代品，而是其重要的补充，并且应与理论并行发展。我们通过一个基于试点数据估计的父母行为模型来阐述这些论点，该模型结合了传统测量方法与新颖测量方法。
