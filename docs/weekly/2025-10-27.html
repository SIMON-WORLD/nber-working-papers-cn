No. 1NBER w34413

The Musk Partisan Effect on Tesla Sales

马斯克党派效应对特斯拉销量的影响

Kenneth T. Gillingham, Matthew Kotchen, James A. Levinsohn, Barry J. Nalebuff

Programs：EEE, IO, PE；JEL：G3, Q48

Abstract

We study how Elon Musk's polarizing and partisan actions have impacted Tesla vehicle sales in the United States. Using county-level, monthly data on new vehicle registrations, we leverage how changes in vehicle sales over time diverge across counties with differing shares of Democratic and Republican voters. Without the Musk partisan effect, Tesla sales between October 2022 and April 2025 would have been 67-83% higher, equivalent to 1-1.26 million more vehicles. Musk’s partisan activities also increased the sales of other automakers' electric and hybrid vehicles 17-22% because of substitution, and undermined California’s progress in meeting its zero-emissions vehicle target.

中文摘要

我们研究了埃隆·马斯克的两极分化且带有党派色彩的行为如何影响特斯拉在美国的汽车销量。利用县级月度新车注册数据，我们利用了不同民主党与共和党选民比例县之间汽车销量随时间变化的差异。如果没有马斯克的党派效应，2022年10月至2025年4月期间特斯拉的销量本应高出67-83%，相当于多售出100万至126万辆汽车。马斯克的党派活动还因替代效应使其他汽车制造商的电动和混合动力汽车销量增加了17-22%，并削弱了加利福尼亚州在实现其零排放车辆目标方面的进展。

No. 2NBER w34412

The Cook County Tax on Sweetened Beverages: The Impact on Purchases of its Announcement, Implementation, and Repeal

库克县含糖饮料税：其宣布、实施和废除对购买行为的影响

Felipe Lozano Rojas, John Cawley, David E. Frisvold

Programs：EH, LE, PE；JEL：H2, I12, I18, Q18

Abstract

Taxes on sweetened beverages have been widely adopted in response to increases in diet-related chronic disease. An episode of particular interest occurred in Cook County, Illinois, where a beverage tax was announced, implemented, and then repealed. This paper is the first to estimate the effects of this tax using household-level data on purchases. We estimate difference-in-differences models that compare the change in beverage purchases over time in Cook County to that in comparison areas. The results indicate that consumer purchases did not detectably respond to the announcement of the tax. Implementation of the tax reduced purchases by 22.5% for all taxed beverages, 16.5% for high-calorie taxed beverages and a 33% for low-calorie (e.g. diet) taxed beverages. This implies a price elasticity of demand of -0.66 for all taxed beverages, -0.48 for high-calorie taxed beverages and -0.97 for low-calorie taxed beverages. The impact of the tax did not vary by household income, and there is no detectable impact of the tax on purchases of possible substitutes (bottled water, fruit juice, milk). After repeal of the tax, purchases of taxed beverages returned to their baseline quantities; there was no evidence of habit formation.

中文摘要

为应对与饮食相关的慢性病增加，含糖饮料税已被广泛采用。一个特别值得关注的案例发生在伊利诺伊州库克县，那里的一项饮料税被宣布、实施，随后又被废除。本文首次使用家庭层面的购买数据来估计这项税收的影响。我们估计了双重差分模型，比较库克县与对照地区饮料购买量随时间的变化。结果表明，消费者购买行为对税收的宣布没有显著反应。税收的实施使所有应税饮料的购买量减少了22.5%，高热量应税饮料减少了16.5%，低热量（如无糖）应税饮料减少了33%。这意味着所有应税饮料的需求价格弹性为-0.66，高热量应税饮料为-0.48，低热量应税饮料为-0.97。税收的影响不因家庭收入而异，并且没有发现税收对可能的替代品（瓶装水、果汁、牛奶）购买量有显著影响。税收废除后，应税饮料的购买量恢复到基线水平；没有发现习惯形成的证据。

No. 3NBER w34411

Lessons for Automatic Fiscal Stabilizers from the Great Recession and the COVID Recession

从大衰退和新冠疫情衰退中汲取的自动财政稳定器教训

Karen Dynan, Douglas Elmendorf

Programs：EFG, PE；JEL：E32, E62, E63, H12

Abstract

This paper simulates economic developments as if the discretionary fiscal stimulus enacted in the past two recessions had not occurred and additional automatic fiscal stabilizers had been deployed instead. For the calibration of key economic relationships most consistent with the empirical literature, we find that more sustained fiscal stimulus would have pushed unemployment down more rapidly following the Great Recession and that more limited stimulus would have caused inflation to increase much less following the COVID recession. We caution, though, that our estimates are uncertain given the large number of assumptions embedded in the calculations. Under different assumptions about the supply side of the economy when resource utilization is high, the stimulus enacted in early 2021 was not a significant cause of the observed runup in inflation that followed, and substituting an automatic stabilizer would have made little difference to inflation.

中文摘要

本文模拟了经济情景，假设过去两次衰退中实施的自愿性财政刺激措施未曾发生，而是部署了额外的自动财政稳定器。对于与实证文献最为一致的关键经济关系校准，我们发现，更持续的财政刺激本会在“大衰退”后更快地降低失业率，而更有限的刺激本会使“新冠疫情衰退”后的通胀上升幅度小得多。然而，我们提醒，鉴于计算中包含了大量假设，我们的估计存在不确定性。在关于资源利用率高时经济供给侧的不同假设下，2021年初实施的刺激措施并非随后观察到的通胀飙升的重要原因，而用自动稳定器替代也不会对通胀产生显著影响。

No. 4NBER w34410

Managing Financial Crises

管理金融危机

Gianluca Benigno, Alessandro Rebucci, Aliaksandr Zaretski

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, IFM, ME, PE；JEL：E61, F38, H23

Abstract

In this paper, we revisit the question of how to manage financial crises using the framework proposed by Bianchi and Mendoza (2018). We show that this model economy exhibits a multiplicity of constrained-efficient equilibria, which arises because the private shadow value of collateral influences the forward-looking asset price. Among these equilibria, the specific one studied in Bianchi and Mendoza (2018) can be implemented using a tax/subsidy on debt alone. In that case, both the ex ante tax and ex post subsidy are quantitatively important for welfare under the optimal time-consistent policy. Limiting either component can lead to a welfare loss relative to the unregulated competitive equilibrium, highlighting the complementarity between crisis prevention and crisis resolution tools. We also show that, under certain conditions, all Pareto-dominant constrained-efficient equilibria entail the unconstrained allocation chosen by a social planner subject to the country budget constraint, and this allocation can be implemented with purely ex post policies.

中文摘要

在本文中，我们使用Bianchi和Mendoza（2018）提出的框架重新审视如何管理金融危机的问题。我们表明，该模型经济表现出多种受约束的有效均衡，这是由于抵押品的私人影子价值影响前瞻性资产价格而产生的。在这些均衡中，Bianchi和Mendoza（2018）研究的特定均衡可以仅通过债务税收/补贴来实现。在这种情况下，在最优时间一致性政策下，事前税收和事后补贴对福利都具有数量上的重要性。限制其中任何一个都可能导致相对于无监管竞争均衡的福利损失，这凸显了危机预防工具与危机解决工具之间的互补性。我们还表明，在某些条件下，所有帕累托占优的受约束有效均衡都包含由受国家预算约束的社会计划者选择的非约束性配置，并且这种配置可以通过纯粹的事后政策来实现。

No. 5NBER w34409

Interpreting Turbulent Episodes in International Finance

解读国际金融中的动荡时期

Hélène Rey, Vania Stavrakeva

Programs：AP, IFM；JEL：F3, F32, G15

Abstract

We study the anatomy of the international portfolio finance network. As global financial linkages have become denser over time, cross-border portfolio equity positions have grown in importance relative to debt for Emerging markets and Advanced economies. Using the framework developed by Stavrakeva and Rey (2024), we construct a novel proxy of daily foreign investor holdings in both equity and long-term sovereign debt markets across 32 currency areas. Leveraging an instrumental variable strategy, we identify an effect of foreign equity ETF inflows on exchange rates and local stock market prices. Our high-frequency proxy enables us to interpret episodes of turbulence in international finance. It should prove useful to assess how persistent the current shocks to the international financial system are likely to be.

中文摘要

我们研究了国际组合金融网络的结构。随着全球金融联系随时间推移变得更加密集，对于新兴市场和发达经济体而言，跨境股权投资相对于债务的重要性日益增长。利用Stavrakeva和Rey（2024）开发的框架，我们构建了一个新颖的代理变量，用于衡量32个货币区域内每日的外国投资者在股票和长期主权债务市场中的持有量。借助工具变量策略，我们识别出外国股票ETF流入对汇率和当地股票市场价格的影响。我们的高频代理变量使我们能够解读国际金融中的动荡时期。它应有助于评估当前对国际金融体系的冲击可能持续多久。

No. 6NBER w34408

Trade Policy Ambiguity

贸易政策模糊性

Joseph B. Steinberg

Programs：IFM, ITI；JEL：F12, F13, F17

Abstract

This paper studies how Knightian uncertainty about the distribution of future trade policies affects current trade flows using a dynamic trade model with a sunk cost of exporting. Qualitatively, trade-policy ambiguity reduces export participation in a similar manner to standard trade-policy risk, but also increases sensitivity to tariff bounds, decreases sensitivity to the likelihood of a tariff increase, and can either increase or decrease sensitivity to tariff persistence. Quantitatively, ambiguity dampens the response of trade to persistent reforms and strengthens the response to transitory reforms.

中文摘要

本文利用一个包含出口沉没成本的动态贸易模型，研究关于未来贸易政策分布的奈特不确定性如何影响当前贸易流动。从定性角度看，贸易政策模糊性以类似于标准贸易政策风险的方式减少了出口参与，但也增加了对关税上限的敏感性，降低了对关税上调可能性的敏感性，并且可能增加或减少对关税持续性的敏感性。从定量角度看，模糊性抑制了贸易对持久性改革的反应，并强化了对暂时性改革的反应。

Intergenerational Mobility in Measures of Wellbeing: Consumption, Health and Life Satisfaction

福祉指标中的代际流动性：消费、健康与生活满意度

Jonathan Davis, Nathan Deutscher, Bhashkar Mazumder

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：E2, I14, I3, J62

Abstract

Studies of intergenerational mobility have begun to expand outside of the traditional outcomes such as income, education and occupation, into using alternative measures of wellbeing. In this chapter we survey the evidence on studies of these alternative measures with a focus on health, consumption and life satisfaction. We also incorporate lessons from the income mobility literature that may be relevant for how we think about these alternative measures both conceptually and empirically. We highlight a few approaches that researchers can consider to incorporate alternative measures and we conclude that a widening of our conception of intergenerational mobility to incorporate measures of wellbeing may identify shortfalls in some of our current approaches.

中文摘要

代际流动性的研究已开始从传统的收入、教育和职业等结果指标，扩展到使用替代性福祉指标。在本章中，我们综述了这些替代性指标的研究证据，重点关注健康、消费和生活满意度。我们还借鉴了收入流动性文献中的经验教训，这些教训可能对我们在概念上和实证上思考这些替代性指标的方式有所启发。我们强调了研究人员可以考虑纳入替代性指标的几种方法，并得出结论：拓宽我们对代际流动性的概念以纳入福祉指标，可能会揭示我们当前某些方法中的不足之处。

No. 8NBER w34406

Carbon Taxation and Firm Behavior in Emerging Economies: Evidence from South Africa

碳税与新兴经济体的企业行为：来自南非的证据

Johannes Gallé, Rodrigo Oliveira, Daniel Overbeck, Nadine Riedel, Edson R. Severnini

Programs：DEV, EEE, LE, PE, POL, PR；JEL：H23, O13, O55, Q52, Q58

Abstract

This paper provides the first comprehensive evidence on how firms in emerging economies respond to carbon taxation. Using detailed administrative data, we study the announcement and implementation of South Africa’s 2019 carbon tax—a potential trailblazer for other developing countries with limited state capacity amid the global expansion of carbon pricing. Contrary to concerns that carbon taxes might hinder growth or employment, we find no negative effects on firm performance or jobs. Firms facing higher effective tax rates increased activity following the tax’s announcement, four years before implementation, likely reflecting the resolution of regulatory uncertainty and efforts to mitigate stranded asset costs. While we find no measurable reduction in emissions—likely due to this anticipatory behavior—our results suggest that carbon taxation can be implemented without harming economic outcomes, even in the short term and in low- and middle-income settings.

中文摘要

本文提供了关于新兴经济体企业如何应对碳税的首个全面证据。利用详细的行政数据，我们研究了南非2019年碳税的宣布与实施——在全球碳定价扩张的背景下，这可能是其他国家能力有限的发展中国家的潜在先行者。与碳税可能阻碍增长或就业的担忧相反，我们发现对企业绩效或就业没有负面影响。面临更高有效税率的企业在税收宣布后、实施前四年就增加了活动，这很可能反映了监管不确定性的解决以及减轻搁浅资产成本的努力。虽然我们没有发现可测量的排放减少——很可能是由于这种预期行为——但我们的结果表明，即使在短期内以及在低收入和中等收入环境中，碳税的实施也可以在不损害经济结果的情况下进行。

No. 9NBER w34405

Careers of Minimum Wage Workers

最低工资工人的职业生涯

Sari Pekkala Kerr, William R. Kerr, Louis J. Maiden

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：D31, E24, J21, J31, J38, J42, J62, J78

Abstract

We characterize the careers of minimum wage workers by merging SIPP panels covering 1992-2016 into the LEHD. A long-run analysis shows strong earnings growth for these workers in subsequent decades, becoming indistinguishable from peers earning modestly more initially. Most of this growth is due to the steep earnings trajectories of young workers. Older workers earning minimum wages show a modest dip in earnings at that moment compared to earlier and later periods. Increases in state minimum wages do not significantly alter the future careers of workers who are on the minimum wage when the increases occur.

中文摘要

我们通过将1992-2016年间的SIPP面板数据合并到LEHD中，来刻画最低工资工人的职业生涯。一项长期分析显示，这些工人在随后的几十年中收入增长强劲，最终与最初收入略高的同龄人难以区分。这种增长大部分归因于年轻工人陡峭的收入轨迹。与前后时期相比，领取最低工资的年长工人在该时刻的收入略有下降。州最低工资的提高并未显著改变在提高发生时处于最低工资水平的工人未来的职业生涯。

Monetary Policy and Housing Overvaluation

货币政策与住房市场高估

Nina Biljanovska, Eduardo Espuny Diaz, Amir Kermani, Rui C. Mano

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：D84, E52, R31

Abstract

This paper examines how housing market overvaluation—measured by the price-to-rent ratio and its deviations from long-term trends—affects the transmission of monetary policy. Using U.S. metropolitan-level data and three measures of monetary policy shocks, we find that house prices respond more strongly to policy rate changes in overvalued markets. Examining buyer heterogeneity, we show that investor demand, proxied by non-owner-occupied purchases, declines more sharply after monetary tightening in these markets. These results are consistent with models of extrapolative beliefs and suggest that monetary policy can serve a stabilizing role during housing booms.

中文摘要

本文考察了住房市场高估——以房价租金比及其对长期趋势的偏离来衡量——如何影响货币政策的传导。利用美国大都市层面的数据和三种货币政策冲击的衡量指标，我们发现，在高估市场中，房价对政策利率变化的反应更为强烈。通过考察购房者异质性，我们表明，在这些市场中，以非自住购买衡量的投资者需求在货币紧缩后下降得更剧烈。这些结果与外推信念模型一致，并表明货币政策在住房繁荣期间可以发挥稳定作用。

The Relationship Between Certificate of Need Laws and Mortality

需求证明法与死亡率之间的关系

Jonathan H. Cantor, Jill Horwitz, Christopher M. Whaley, Anthony Yu

Programs：EH；JEL：I0, I1, I11, I18

Abstract

Certificate of Need (CON) laws regulate entry and capital investments in healthcare with the goal of containing costs while preserving access and quality. This paper examines the relationship between these laws and overall mortality as well as leading causes of mortality: cancer and cardiovascular disease. Using county-level death records, we conducted an event-study analysis comparing mortality rates in states that repealed their CON laws to states that did not between 1979 and 2004. The repeal of CON laws was associated with short-run reductions in cancer mortality, primarily from reductions in lung cancer mortality. Cardiovascular mortality and all-cause mortality rates were unchanged.

中文摘要

需求证明法（CON）旨在通过控制成本同时保持可及性和质量，来监管医疗保健领域的进入和资本投资。本文考察了这些法律与总体死亡率以及主要死亡原因——癌症和心血管疾病——之间的关系。利用县级死亡记录，我们进行了一项事件研究分析，比较了1979年至2004年间废除CON法的州与未废除州的死亡率。CON法的废除与癌症死亡率的短期下降相关，这主要源于肺癌死亡率的下降。心血管疾病死亡率和全因死亡率没有变化。

No. 12NBER w34402

GDP Growth Expectations and Cash-flow Risk Premium

GDP增长预期与现金流风险溢价

William N. Goetzmann, Akiko Watanabe, Masahiro Watanabe

Programs：AP；JEL：E32, E44, G12, G14, G17

Abstract

We show that procyclical stocks–those whose cash flows rise with expected economic growth–earn higher average returns than countercyclical stocks. Leveraging nearly 75 years of economist survey data on real GDP growth expectations, we identify economic states while sidestepping model-driven forecast error. GDP forecasts comove with consumption and predict the market return, real GDP growth, and consumption growth. This approach builds on the literatures on both the Intertemporal and Consumption Capital Asset Pricing Models. We document a statistically significant, economically meaningful procyclicality premium that remains robust to standard factor controls.

中文摘要

我们表明，顺周期股票——即那些现金流随预期经济增长而上升的股票——获得的平均回报高于逆周期股票。利用近75年经济学家关于实际GDP增长预期的调查数据，我们识别了经济状态，同时避免了模型驱动的预测误差。GDP预测与消费共同变动，并预测市场回报、实际GDP增长和消费增长。这种方法建立在跨期资本资产定价模型和消费资本资产定价模型两方面的文献基础上。我们记录了一个统计上显著、经济上有意义的顺周期溢价，该溢价在标准因子控制下仍然稳健。

No. 13NBER w34401

Strategic Avoidance and the Welfare Impacts of U.S. Solar Panel Tariffs

战略性规避与美国太阳能电池板关税的福利影响

Todd D. Gerarden, Bryan K. Bollinger, Kenneth Gillingham, Daniel Xu

Programs：EEE, ITI；JEL：F13, Q48

Abstract

This study examines the effects of tariffs imposed by the U.S. on imported solar panels. We first provide clear evidence that tariff-exposed firms shifted production to locations that did not face tariffs, and that domestic prices increased relative to other markets. We then develop a structural model to analyze welfare effects. We find that the tariffs generated modest gains for domestic manufacturers and for government revenues, but larger losses in domestic consumer surplus and environmental benefits, thereby reducing domestic welfare. Furthermore, the tariffs reduced domestic solar industry employment and wages. By contrast, subsidizing solar panel manufacturing could increase domestic production, employment, and welfare.

中文摘要

本研究考察了美国对进口太阳能电池板征收关税的影响。我们首先提供明确证据，表明受关税影响的企业将生产转移到未面临关税的地区，并且国内价格相对于其他市场有所上涨。然后，我们开发了一个结构模型来分析福利效应。我们发现，关税为国内制造商和政府收入带来了适度收益，但国内消费者剩余和环境效益的损失更大，从而降低了国内福利。此外，关税减少了国内太阳能行业的就业和工资。相比之下，补贴太阳能电池板制造可以增加国内产量、就业和福利。

No. 14NBER w34400

Does Increasing Financial Access to Contraception in the U.S. Reduce Unintended Pregnancies? Evidence from the M-CARES Randomized Controlled Trial at Two Years

在美国增加避孕药具的经济可及性能否减少非意愿妊娠？来自M-CARES随机对照试验两年的证据

Martha J. Bailey, Emilia Brito Rebolledo, Deniz Gorgulu, Kelsey Figone, Vanessa W. Lang, Alexa Prettyman, Vanessa Dalton

Programs：CH, LS；JEL：I14, I18, J13, J18

Abstract

We use a randomized controlled trial to examine how out-of-pocket costs affect contraceptive method choice, pregnancy, abortion, and childbirth among uninsured U.S. women. The study recruited women seeking care through Title X—a national family planning program subsidizing reproductive health services for low-income Americans—and randomized vouchers, making the full spectrum of contraception highly discounted or free. We find that subsidizing contraception has large and persistent effects on the choice of contraceptive method, resulting in significantly fewer pregnancies and abortions at 26 months. The effects on births are inconclusive.

中文摘要

我们利用一项随机对照试验，考察自付费用如何影响未参保美国女性的避孕方法选择、怀孕、堕胎和生育。该研究招募了通过Title X——一项为低收入美国人提供生殖健康服务补贴的国家计划生育项目——寻求护理的女性，并随机发放代金券，使全系列避孕药具大幅折扣或免费。我们发现，补贴避孕药具对避孕方法的选择产生了巨大且持续的影响，导致在26个月时怀孕和堕胎显著减少，但对生育没有影响。

No. 15NBER w34399

Stimulus through Insurance: the Marginal Propensity to Repay Debt

通过保险进行刺激：偿还债务的边际倾向

Gizem Koşar, Davide Melcangi, Laura Pilossoph, David G. Wiczer

Programs：EFG；JEL：E21, E62

Abstract

Using detailed microdata, we document that households often use “stimulus” checks to pay down debt, especially those with low net wealth-to-income ratios. To rationalize these patterns, we introduce an empirically plausible borrowing price schedule into an otherwise standard incomplete markets model. Because interest rates rise with debt, borrowers have increasingly larger incentives to use an additional dollar to reduce debt service payments rather than consume. Using our calibrated model, we then study whether and how this marginal propensity to repay debt (MPRD) alters the aggregate implications of fiscal transfers. We uncover a trade-off between stimulus and insurance, as high–debt individuals gain considerably from transfers, but consume relatively little immediately. This mechanism lowers the immediate stimulus effect of fiscal transfers, but sustains aggregate consumption for longer.

中文摘要

利用详细的微观数据，我们记录了家庭经常使用“刺激”支票来偿还债务，尤其是那些净财富与收入比率较低的家庭。为了合理化这些模式，我们将一个经验上可信的借贷价格表引入到一个原本标准的不完全市场模型中。由于利率随债务增加而上升，借款人使用额外一美元来减少偿债支出而非消费的激励越来越大。利用我们校准后的模型，我们随后研究了这种偿还债务的边际倾向（MPRD）是否以及如何改变财政转移支付的总体影响。我们揭示了刺激与保险之间的权衡，因为高负债个体从转移支付中获益颇丰，但立即消费相对较少。这一机制降低了财政转移支付的即时刺激效应，但使总消费得以更持久地维持。

No. 16NBER w34397

The Austerity Threshold

紧缩阈值

Vadim Elenev, Tim Landvoigt, Stijn Van Nieuwerburgh

Programs：AP, EFG, PE；JEL：E10, E40, E60, G12, G18, G2

Abstract

We introduce a new indicator of fiscal capacity—the “austerity threshold”: the debt-to-GDP level above which the government must raise fiscal surpluses to ensure debt safety. In a model with realistic risk premia, nominal rigidities, and an intermediary sector, calibrated to the U.S., we estimate this threshold at 189%. We highlight the roles of safety premia and intermediation-driven convenience yields. The threshold varies with the source of surpluses: spending cuts reduce inflation and allow low interest rates, while tax increases distort labor supply and raise inflation. Uncertainty over the austerity regime—spending cuts or tax increases—sharply lowers fiscal capacity. The expected austerity regime affects asset prices and macro outcomes even when debt-to-GDP is well below the threshold.

中文摘要

我们引入了一个新的财政能力指标——“紧缩阈值”：即债务与GDP之比超过该水平时，政府必须提高财政盈余以确保债务安全。在一个包含现实风险溢价、名义刚性和中介部门、并针对美国进行校准的模型中，我们估计该阈值为189%。我们强调了安全溢价和中介驱动的便利收益率的作用。该阈值随盈余来源而变化：削减支出会降低通胀并允许低利率，而增税则会扭曲劳动供给并推高通胀。关于紧缩制度（削减支出或增税）的不确定性会急剧降低财政能力。即使债务与GDP之比远低于阈值，预期的紧缩制度也会影响资产价格和宏观经济结果。
