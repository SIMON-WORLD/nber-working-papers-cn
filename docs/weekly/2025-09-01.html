No. 1NBER w34195

The Impact of Geopolitical Risk on Consumer Expectations and Spending

地缘政治风险对消费者预期与支出的影响

Yuriy Gorodnichenko, Dimitris Georgarakos, Geoff Kenny, Olivier Coibion

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：D01, E20, E3, E50

Abstract

Using novel scenario-based survey questions that randomize the expected duration of the Russian invasion of Ukraine and Middle East conflict, we examine the causal impact of geopolitical risk on consumers’ beliefs about aggregate economic conditions and their own financial outlook. Expecting a longer conflict leads European households to anticipate a worsening of the aggregate economy, with higher inflation, lower economic growth, and lower stock prices. They also perceive negative fiscal implications, anticipating higher government debt and higher taxes. Ultimately, households view the geopolitical conflict as making them worse off financially and it leads them to reduce their consumption.

中文摘要

利用新颖的基于情景的调查问题，随机设定俄罗斯入侵乌克兰和中东冲突的预期持续时间，我们考察了地缘政治风险对消费者关于总体经济状况及其自身财务前景信念的因果影响。预期冲突持续时间更长，会导致欧洲家庭预期总体经济恶化，表现为通胀上升、经济增长放缓和股价下跌。他们还感知到负面的财政影响，预期政府债务和税收将增加。最终，家庭认为地缘政治冲突使其财务状况恶化，并导致他们减少消费。

No. 2NBER w34194

Superstar Firms through the Generations

跨越时代的超级明星企业

Yueran Ma, Benjamin Pugsley, Haomin Qin, Kaspar Zimmermann

Programs：CF, EFG, IO, PR；JEL：D2, E2, L1, M1

Abstract

We present new facts about the largest American companies over the past century. In manufacturing, top firms in the 1910s, 1950s, and 2010s predominantly date back to around 1900. Even as this special generation persists, turnover among top firms has been substantial. In contrast, in retail and wholesale, we do not observe a special generation among top firms. We show in a model of firm dynamics that a special generation can arise from an industrial revolution, through the adoption of a scalable technology and learning-by-doing. Top firm turnover is matched by standard idiosyncratic productivity shocks. Time-varying market size growth rates or entry costs are not sufficient to explain the facts. Among retailers and wholesalers, learning appears absent, so a special generation would be harder to sustain. Our results highlight the potential for lasting nonstationarity among the dynamics of top firms, which can result from the long echoes of technological change.

中文摘要

我们提出了关于过去一个世纪美国最大公司的新事实。在制造业中，1910年代、1950年代和2010年代的顶级公司主要可追溯至1900年左右。即使这一特殊世代持续存在，顶级公司之间的更替也相当显著。相比之下，在零售和批发业中，我们并未观察到顶级公司中存在特殊世代。我们在一个企业动态模型中表明，特殊世代可能源于一场工业革命，通过采用可扩展的技术和干中学而产生。顶级公司的更替可由标准的异质性生产率冲击来解释。随时间变化的市场规模增长率或进入成本不足以解释这些事实。在零售商和批发商中，学习效应似乎缺失，因此特殊世代更难维持。我们的结果凸显了顶级公司动态中可能存在的持久非平稳性，这可能是技术变革长期回响的结果。

Overlapping Generations Models, Multiplicity of Steady States and Momentary Equilibria, and Economic Fluctuations

世代交叠模型、稳态与瞬时均衡的多重性以及经济波动

Tomohiro Hirano, Joseph E. Stiglitz

Programs：EFG；JEL：C61, E32

Abstract

This paper examines the simplest OLG models with capital accumulation, demonstrating three results that stand in marked contrast to those of the standard model: first, the possibility of multiple steady states; second, the possibility of multiple momentary equilibria under rational expectations; third, one of implications of multiple momentary equilibria is that dynamics may be marked by complex fluctuations (lacking even periodicity), but still within well-defined bounds. We provide quite general conditions (with general utility and production functions) under which in the simplest of OLG models, there can be multiple steady states, multiple momentary equilibria, and complex dynamics. Furthermore, we present a simple illustration of wobbly growth by incorporating credit friction.

中文摘要

本文考察了最简单的具有资本积累的世代交叠模型，展示了与标准模型形成鲜明对比的三个结果：第一，存在多重稳态的可能性；第二，在理性预期下存在多重瞬时均衡的可能性；第三，多重瞬时均衡的一个含义是，动态过程可能以复杂波动（甚至缺乏周期性）为特征，但仍处于明确界定的界限内。我们提供了相当一般的条件（使用一般效用和生产函数），在此条件下，最简单的世代交叠模型中可能存在多重稳态、多重瞬时均衡和复杂动态。此外，我们通过引入信贷摩擦，提供了一个关于摇摆增长的简单例证。

No. 4NBER w34191

Navigating Medicaid: Experimental Evidence on Administrative Burden and Coverage Loss

导航医疗补助：关于行政负担与保障覆盖损失的实验证据

Rebecca Mary Myerson, Allison Espeseth, Laura Dague

Programs：AG, CH, EH, PE；JEL：C93, D73, H75, I13, I38

Abstract

We study how administrative burden can affect access to safety-net health insurance in a large-scale field experiment of outreach to Medicaid enrollees with an imminent renewal deadline. A pre-recorded call offering assistance increased Medicaid renewal by 1 percentage point (1.5%), including significant effects among tribal members, children, lower-income people, and people with chronic disease. Impacts were larger (21 percentage points) for people who sought assistance. Assuming the intervention could not affect underlying Medicaid eligibility, Medicaid-eligible people account for the findings. These data suggest administrative burden can cause people who are Medicaid eligible and need health care to lose their coverage.

中文摘要

我们在一项大规模实地实验中研究了行政负担如何影响获得安全网健康保险的机会，该实验针对面临即将到来的续保截止日期的医疗补助参保者进行外展服务。一段提供帮助的预录电话使医疗补助续保率提高了1个百分点（1.5%），对部落成员、儿童、低收入人群和慢性病患者产生了显著影响。对于寻求帮助的人群，影响更大（21个百分点）。假设干预措施不能影响潜在的医疗补助资格，那么符合医疗补助资格的人群解释了这些发现。这些数据表明，行政负担可能导致符合医疗补助资格且需要医疗保健的人失去其保险覆盖。

No. 5NBER w34190

Good Rents versus Bad Rents: R&D Misallocation and Growth

好租金与坏租金：研发错配与增长

Philippe Aghion, Antonin Bergeaud, Timo Boppart, Peter J. Klenow, Huiyu Li

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：O31, O38, O41, O52

Abstract

Firm price-cost markups may reflect (a) bigger step sizes from quality innovations that confer significant knowledge spillovers onto other firms, and/or (b) higher process efficiency than competing firms or other factors which bear no obvious knowledge externality. We write down an endogenous growth model with innovation step size and process efficiency as alternative sources of markup heterogeneity. Compared with the laissez-faire equilibrium, the social planner wants to reallocate research towards high step size firms but not high process efficiency firms. We then use price and productivity data across firms in French manufacturing to infer firm step sizes and process efficiency. We find that the planner could achieve faster growth by reallocating research toward high step size firms, and more so if high step size firms could freely license their innovations to high process efficiency firms.

中文摘要

企业的价格-成本加成可能反映：(a) 质量创新带来的更大步长，这些创新为其他企业提供了显著的知识溢出，和/或 (b) 比竞争对手更高的流程效率或其他不带有明显知识外部性的因素。我们构建了一个内生增长模型，将创新步长和流程效率作为加成率异质性的替代来源。与自由放任均衡相比，社会计划者希望将研究重新分配给高步长企业，而不是高流程效率企业。然后，我们利用法国制造业企业的价格和生产率数据来推断企业的步长和流程效率。我们发现，计划者可以通过将研究重新分配给高步长企业来实现更快的增长，如果高步长企业能够将其创新自由许可给高流程效率企业，则效果更佳。

The Future of Foreign Trade

对外贸易的未来

Elhanan Helpman

Programs：ITI；JEL：F1, F13, F15, F5

Abstract

Foreign trade has significantly contributed to global improvements in living standards, a reduction in global inequality since the mid-1990s, and the lifting of millions out of extreme poverty. These gains were supported by the rules-based international order established after World War II. However, these achievements are now under threat. Escalating trade wars risk not only causing significant economic harm to both the United States and low-income countries, but also exacerbating geopolitical tensions.

中文摘要

对外贸易极大地促进了全球生活水平的提高，自20世纪90年代中期以来减少了全球不平等，并使数百万人摆脱了极端贫困。这些成果得到了二战后建立的基于规则的国际秩序的支持。然而，这些成就如今正受到威胁。不断升级的贸易战不仅有可能对美国自身和低收入国家造成重大经济损失，还可能加剧地缘政治紧张局势。

No. 7NBER w34188

Minimum Wages and the Distribution of Firm Wage Premia

最低工资与企业工资溢价分布

Marcelo L. Bergolo, Rodrigo Ceni, Mathias Fondo, Damián Vergara

Programs：LS, PE；JEL：J01, J08, J30, J38

Abstract

This paper leverages a large minimum wage reform in Uruguay to study the effects of minimum wages on the distribution of firm wage premia. The reform significantly decreased wage inequality, mainly by reducing between-firm inequality. AKM and time-varying AKM analyses reveal a large compression in the distribution of firm fixed effects after the reform, driven by an increase in the fixed effects of low-premium firms. Firm-level and worker-level difference-in-differences analyses document a causal effect of the reform on the compression of firm fixed effects. Results suggest minimum wages can increase the supply of “good jobs” by “making bad jobs better”.

中文摘要

本文利用乌拉圭一次大规模的最低工资改革，研究最低工资对企业工资溢价分布的影响。该改革显著降低了工资不平等，主要是通过减少企业间不平等实现的。AKM和时变AKM分析显示，改革后企业固定效应的分布出现了大幅压缩，这是由低溢价企业固定效应的上升所驱动的。企业层面和工人层面的双重差分分析记录了改革对企业固定效应压缩的因果效应。结果表明，最低工资可以通过“让坏工作变好”来增加“好工作”的供给。

No. 8NBER w34187

Endogenous Firm Ownership in General Equilibrium

一般均衡中的内生企业所有权

Joshua S. Gans

Programs：PR；JEL：D23, D50, L22

Abstract

This paper examines firm ownership structures in competitive general equilibrium by introducing a model where ownership emerges endogenously rather than being assumed. By embedding the property rights theory of the firm into general equilibrium analysis, the model demonstrates how market forces determine both initial firm formation by entrepreneurs and subsequent trading of ownership rights. The key finding is that, in equilibrium, firms are created by managers who have long-term importance to performance, but these founder-managers nonetheless sell ownership to outside investors. This pattern emerges because managers can capture value through both the sale price and their ongoing employment relationship, while outside owners can only benefit through ownership. The model thus provides a novel theoretical foundation for observed patterns of entrepreneurial exit. Additionally, it identifies a distinct economic definition of an entrepreneur as an agent whose human capital is valuable for firms even when they do not remain long-term owners. The framework generates insights into firm formation, ownership dynamics, and the nature of entrepreneurship while maintaining the core structure of competitive general equilibrium analysis.

中文摘要

本文通过引入一个所有权内生出现而非假设的模型，考察了竞争性一般均衡中的企业所有权结构。通过将企业的产权理论嵌入一般均衡分析，该模型展示了市场力量如何决定企业家初始创立企业以及随后所有权权利的交易。关键发现是，在均衡中，企业由对绩效具有长期重要性的管理者创立，但这些创始人-管理者仍然将所有权出售给外部投资者。这种模式的出现是因为管理者可以通过出售价格和持续的雇佣关系来获取价值，而外部所有者只能通过所有权获益。因此，该模型为观察到的企业家退出模式提供了新颖的理论基础。此外，它识别出一个独特的经济学意义上的企业家定义，即其人力资本即使在他们不长期持有所有权时对企业也有价值的代理人。该框架在保持竞争性一般均衡分析核心结构的同时，对企业形成、所有权动态和企业家精神的本质产生了深刻见解。

No. 9NBER w34186

Union Bargaining Power and the Amenity-Wage Tradeoff

工会谈判力量与福利-工资权衡

Lorenzo Lagos

Programs：LS；JEL：J32, J42, J52, K31

Abstract

This paper studies how collective bargaining affects both wages and amenities. By merging collective bargaining agreements (CBAs) to linked employer-employee data in Brazil, I combine rich contracted amenities with wage information. I implement a difference-in-difference strategy that exploits a court ruling enforcing CBA continuation (i.e., ultractivity) to estimate the effects of union bargaining power. Strengthening unions raises wages and amenities without reducing employment—increasing retention despite some labor-labor substitution. A revealed preference approach shows that amenities account for 45% of total compensation gains. These findings suggest that collective bargaining can offset monopsony power, but employers retain the right-to-manage workforce composition.

中文摘要

本文研究集体谈判如何同时影响工资和福利。通过将集体谈判协议与巴西的关联雇主-雇员数据合并，我将丰富的合同约定福利与工资信息相结合。我采用了一项双重差分策略，利用一项强制延续集体谈判协议（即超期效力）的法院裁决来估计工会谈判力量的影响。加强工会力量会在不减少就业的情况下提高工资和福利——尽管存在一定的劳动力替代效应，但提高了员工留任率。一项显示性偏好方法表明，福利占总薪酬增长的45%。这些发现表明，集体谈判可以抵消买方垄断力量，但雇主保留了管理劳动力构成的权利。

No. 10NBER w34185

The Medium Matters: Medical Decision-Making in Telemedicine versus In-person Care

媒介至关重要：远程医疗与面对面诊疗中的医疗决策

Alexandra Steiny Wellsjo, Paul Gertler, Ada Kwan, Eric Remera, Piero Irakiza, Jeanine Condo, James Humuza

Programs：DEV, EH, LS, PR；JEL：I1, M50, O3

Abstract

The use of telemedicine has been on the rise. Although telemedicine has increased access to health care, little is known about how the medium changes providers’ medical decision-making. To evaluate how telemedicine differs from in-person care, we compare the quality and cost of in-person clinic visits and virtual visits over the phone for common conditions in Rwanda. To control for patient selection, we conduct an audit study with 2,532 standardized patient visits, where individuals portraying real patients presented standardized cases for malaria and upper respiratory infection (URI). We find that the quality of virtual care is higher than that of in-person care for URI and equally as good for malaria. Telemedicine providers also asked more questions about symptoms and medical history and prescribed more optional medicines for symptom management than in-person providers. We further find that telemedicine is more efficient than in-person care: virtual consultations were faster, had shorter wait times, resulted in fewer unnecessary medications and tests, and cost less for patients. Controlling for a rich set of provider characteristics, we show that provider selection does not appear to drive the results. Instead, better provider-patient communication over the phone emerges as a key mechanism. Providers report that it is easier to treat, get information from, and relate to patients over the phone. Providers also report feeling less social pressure during phone consultations. Consistent with survey evidence, we find that providers prescribe unnecessary antibiotics when asked by patients face-to-face, but not over the phone.

中文摘要

远程医疗的使用一直在增加。尽管远程医疗增加了医疗服务的可及性，但关于媒介如何改变提供者的医疗决策，我们知之甚少。为了评估远程医疗与面对面诊疗的差异，我们比较了在卢旺达针对常见疾病的面对面诊所就诊和电话虚拟就诊的质量和成本。为了控制患者选择，我们进行了一项包含2532次标准化患者就诊的审计研究，其中扮演真实患者的个体针对疟疾和上呼吸道感染呈现了标准化病例。我们发现，对于上呼吸道感染，虚拟医疗的质量高于面对面诊疗，而对于疟疾，两者质量相当。远程医疗提供者比面对面提供者询问了更多关于症状和病史的问题，并为症状管理开具了更多可选药物。我们进一步发现，远程医疗比面对面诊疗更高效：虚拟咨询速度更快，等待时间更短，导致更少的不必要药物和检查，并且患者花费更少。在控制了一组丰富的提供者特征后，我们表明提供者选择似乎并非驱动结果的原因。相反，更好的提供者-患者电话沟通成为一个关键机制。提供者报告称，通过电话更容易治疗、获取信息以及与患者建立联系。提供者还报告称，在电话咨询中感受到的社会压力较小。与调查证据一致，我们发现，当患者当面要求时，提供者会开具不必要的抗生素，但在电话中则不会。

No. 11NBER w34184

Equality Before the Law in U.S. Civil War Courts-Martial

美国内战军事法庭中的法律面前人人平等

Dora Costa, Ziqi Zhao

Programs：DAE, LE, LS；JEL：J71, K42, N41

Abstract

We examine disparities in acquittal rates and sentencing for Black and White soldiers in the US Civil War using all general courts-martial. We find that Blacks were disproportionately punished for group actions like mutiny and for violent crimes involving group violence, suggesting fears of large-scale rebellion influenced justice. However, we also uncover a system that was fair relative to many modern criminal courts in acquittals and in death sentences. The needs of the Army pushed towards fairness.

中文摘要

我们利用所有一般军事法庭的记录，研究了美国内战中黑人士兵和白人士兵在无罪判决率和量刑方面的差异。我们发现，黑人因集体行动（如兵变）和涉及群体暴力的暴力犯罪而受到不成比例的惩罚，这表明对大规模叛乱的恐惧影响了司法。然而，我们也发现了一个相对于许多现代刑事法庭在无罪判决和死刑判决方面更为公平的制度。军队的需求推动了公平性。

No. 12NBER w34183

Productivity and Wedges: Economic Convergence and the Real Exchange Rate

生产率与楔子：经济趋同与实际汇率

Michael B. Devereux, Ippei Fujiwara, Camilo Granados

Programs：IFM；JEL：F40, F41

Abstract

This paper explores the relationship between economic growth and the real exchange rate, specifically focusing on the convergence in price levels in Eastern European countries. While these countries have had significant convergence in GDP per capita (relative to the EU average) since the 1990s, convergence in real exchange rates for these countries stalled after the EU crisis. Using a standard theoretical framework, we estimate the main drivers of real exchange rates and show that a combination of productivity growth (Balassa-Samuelson effects) and labor market distortions help explain real exchange rate trends. We develop a structural two-country model that provides a rich decomposition of the long run determinants of the real exchange rate. Simulations based on observed sectoral productivities and labor market wedges show that the model can accurately account for the historical path of Eastern European real exchange rates, both before and after the EU crisis.

中文摘要

本文探讨了经济增长与实际汇率之间的关系，特别关注东欧国家价格水平的趋同。尽管自1990年代以来，这些国家的人均GDP（相对于欧盟平均水平）已显著趋同，但它们的实际汇率趋同在欧盟危机后停滞不前。利用一个标准理论框架，我们估计了实际汇率的主要驱动因素，并表明生产率增长（巴拉萨-萨缪尔森效应）和劳动力市场扭曲的结合有助于解释实际汇率趋势。我们开发了一个结构性两国模型，该模型对实际汇率的长期决定因素进行了丰富的分解。基于观察到的部门生产率和劳动力市场楔子的模拟表明，该模型能够准确解释东欧实际汇率在欧盟危机前后的历史路径。

No. 13NBER w34182

Local Problems, Local Solutions: Evidence from the Drug-Free Communities Program on Youth Development

地方问题，地方解决方案：来自无毒社区计划对青少年发展的证据

Shijun You, Wei Fu, Shin-Yi Chou

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I12, I18, I20, I31, J13, K42

Abstract

The Drug-Free Communities (DFC) Support Program aims to reduce youth substance use by fostering multi-sector collaboration and implementing locally tailored strategies within the community. This study is among the first to evaluate the program’s impact on youth development outcomes in the United States. Using the Callaway and Sant’Anna (2021) difference-in-differences (CSDID) estimator, we find that the DFC program significantly reduces drug-related juvenile crime and improves academic performance. The effects are particularly pronounced in communities where coalitions include government agencies, highlighting the critical role of institutional coordination in mobilizing local resources. We also explore behavioral mechanisms, documenting reductions in marijuana use and opioid-related inpatient hospitalizations. A cost-benefit analysis indicates substantial welfare gains, suggesting that early-life prevention represents a cost-effective investment in public health.

Openness and Growth: A Comparison of the Experiences of China and Mexico

开放与增长：中国和墨西哥经验的比较

Timothy J. Kehoe, Xing Xu

Programs：EFG, IFM, ITI；JEL：F43, O32, O47, O57

Abstract

In the late 1980s, Mexico opened itself to international trade and foreign investment, followed in the early 1990s by China. China and Mexico are still the two countries characterized as middle-income by the World Bank with the highest levels of merchandise exports. Although their measures of openness have been comparable, these two countries have had sharply different economic performances: China has achieved spectacular growth, whereas Mexico’s growth has been disappointingly modest. In this article, we extend the analysis of Kehoe and Ruhl (2010) to account for the differences in these experiences. We show that China opened its economy while it was still achieving rapid growth from shifting employment out of agriculture and into manufacturing while Mexico opened long after its comparable phase of structural transformation. China is only now catching up with Mexico in terms of GDP per working-age person, and it still lags behind in terms of the fraction of its population engaged in agriculture. Furthermore, we argue that China has been able to move up a ladder of quality and technological sophistication in the composition of its exports and production, while Mexico seems to be stuck exporting a fixed set of products to its North American neighbors.

中文摘要

在1980年代末，墨西哥向国际贸易和外国投资开放，随后在1990年代初，中国也采取了类似举措。中国和墨西哥至今仍是世界银行归类为中等收入国家中商品出口水平最高的两个国家。尽管它们的开放程度指标相当，但这两个国家的经济表现却截然不同：中国实现了惊人的增长，而墨西哥的增长则令人失望地温和。在本文中，我们扩展了Kehoe和Ruhl（2010）的分析，以解释这些经验的差异。我们表明，中国在开放经济时，仍通过将就业从农业转移到制造业实现快速增长，而墨西哥则在结构性转型的可比阶段过去很久之后才开放。中国现在才在人均劳动年龄人口GDP方面赶上墨西哥，并且在从事农业的人口比例方面仍然落后。此外，我们认为，中国已经能够在其出口和生产的构成中沿着质量和技术复杂性的阶梯向上移动，而墨西哥似乎停滞在向北美邻国出口固定产品组合的状态。

Labor and Product Market Power, Endogenous Quality, and the Consolidation of the US Hospital Industry

劳动力与产品市场势力、内生质量与美国医院行业的整合

Bradley Setzler

Programs：EH, IO, LE, LS；JEL：I11, J42, L41

Abstract

Existing structural analyses of the harmful effects of market consolidation focus on either product or labor markets in isolation, ignoring that product market competitors often compete for workers as well. This paper develops a unified framework for merger evaluation, finding that firms’ simultaneous exercise of oligopoly power in the product market and oligopsony power in the labor market amplifies the harm from mergers to both consumers and workers. The model also demonstrates how merger-induced gains in labor market power incentivize firms to reduce product quality, highlighting an additional channel for consumer harm. The model’s predictions are tested and quantified in the context of the recent consolidation of the US hospital industry. Linking panel data from several sources on all US hospitals from 1996-2022, a difference-in-differences design is estimated for nearly 150 high-concentration within-market mergers. Hospital mergers significantly reduce patient volume, increase prices, reduce employment, lower wages, and deteriorate quality of care, resulting in higher patient mortality. After recovering the structural parameters, the estimated model replicates observed merger impacts. Counterfactual exercises reveal that ignoring increased labor (product) concentration would lead one to under-predict the harm of mergers to consumers (workers).

中文摘要

The Effects of Parental Income and Family Structure on Intergenerational Mobility: A Trajectories-Based Approach

父母收入与家庭结构对代际流动性的影响：一种基于轨迹的方法

Yoosoon Chang, Steven N. Durlauf, Bo Hu, Joon Park

Programs：ED, EFG, LS, PE, TWP；JEL：C10, C14, C25, C50, D10, H0, J01, J10

Abstract

We examine how parental income and family structure during childhood and adolescence affect adult income, emphasizing the timing of these effects. Using an ordered multinomial probability model with functional covariates, we find that these familial influences are strongest in middle childhood and adolescence. We also uncover a complementary relationship in the effects of income and family structure trajectories during key developmental periods. By flexibly controlling for personal and family characteristics using nonparametric methods, our approach effectively handles high-dimensional covariates. The results advance the understanding of intergenerational income mobility and highlight the long-term importance of familial conditions for adult economic success.

中文摘要

我们研究了童年和青少年时期的父母收入与家庭结构如何影响成年收入，并强调了这些影响的时间点。使用一个带有函数协变量的有序多项概率模型，我们发现这些家庭影响在童年中期和青少年时期最为强烈。我们还发现了在关键发育时期，收入和家庭结构轨迹影响的互补关系。通过使用非参数方法灵活控制个人和家庭特征，我们的方法有效处理了高维协变量。这些结果推进了对代际收入流动性的理解，并突显了家庭条件对成年经济成功的长期重要性。

Serial Acquisitions in Tech

科技领域的连续收购

Ginger Zhe Jin, Mario Leccese, Liad Wagman, Yunfei Wang

Programs：CF, IO, LE, PR；JEL：G34, L10, L40, O38

Abstract

We document novel empirical patterns in technology-sector serial acquisitions from 2010 to 2023. Using a granular S&P taxonomy, we show that serial acquisitions—sequences of transactions by the same acquirer within the same or adjacent market categories—constitute approximately one-quarter to one-third of majority-control mergers and acquisitions. Follow on targets tend to be larger and older than initial targets, and publicly-traded non-private equity acquirers account for over half of these transactions, challenging the prevailing view that serial acquisitions primarily represent small-scale private equity roll-ups. We further examine a cumulative screening framework wherein regulatory review is triggered by the aggregate value of sequential transactions rather than individual deal thresholds. This approach typically maintains existing review timing or advances it moderately, though in extended acquisition series it can accelerate scrutiny by several transactions or multiple years. For GAFAM (Google/Alphabet, Apple, Facebook/Meta, Amazon, and Microsoft) and other top technology acquirers, the cumulative approach primarily expands coverage to transactions below traditional thresholds and those with incomplete valuation data, rather than accelerating reviews of already-reportable acquisitions. An algorithmic screening tool implementing such a framework could enable antitrust agencies to monitor consolidation patterns in real time and complement traditional transaction-by-transaction merger reviews.

中文摘要

我们记录了2010年至2023年科技行业连续收购的新实证模式。利用精细的标准普尔分类体系，我们表明连续收购——同一收购方在同一或相邻市场类别内进行的一系列交易——约占多数控制权并购的四分之一到三分之一。后续目标往往比初始目标更大、更成熟，而公开上市的非私募股权收购方占这些交易的一半以上，这挑战了普遍认为连续收购主要代表小规模私募股权整合的观点。我们进一步考察了一个累积筛选框架，其中监管审查由连续交易的累计价值触发，而非单个交易门槛。这种方法通常维持现有的审查时间或适度提前，但在扩展的收购序列中，它可以将审查提前几个交易或数年。对于GAFAM（谷歌/Alphabet、苹果、Facebook/Meta、亚马逊和微软）及其他顶级科技收购方，累积方法主要将覆盖范围扩大到低于传统门槛的交易以及估值数据不完整的交易，而非加速对已应报告收购的审查。实施此类框架的算法筛选工具可以使反垄断机构实时监控整合模式，并补充传统的逐笔交易并购审查。
