遗产

Charles Yuji Horioka

Programs：AG, CH, EFG, LS, PE；JEL：D11, D12, D14, D15, D31, D64, E21, H31, J22

Abstract

In this paper, we discuss bequests and other intergenerational transfers and what impact they have on the consumption, saving, and labor supply behavior of households. We show that bequests and other intergenerational transfers are prevalent in most countries, that they are sometimes motivated by altruism and sometimes by selfishness, that they affect the consumption and saving behavior of households to some extent, especially that of elderly households, that they affect the labor supply behavior of households, especially that of bequest recipients, and that they have important policy implications.

中文摘要

在本文中，我们讨论遗产和其他代际转移，以及它们对家庭消费、储蓄和劳动力供给行为的影响。我们表明，遗产和其他代际转移在大多数国家普遍存在，它们有时出于利他动机，有时出于自私动机，它们在一定程度上影响家庭的消费和储蓄行为，尤其是老年家庭，它们影响家庭的劳动力供给行为，尤其是遗产接受者，并且它们具有重要的政策含义。

No. 2NBER w33926

Welfare Programs and Crime Spillovers

福利计划与犯罪溢出效应

David Jinkins, Elira Kuka, Claudio Labanca

Programs：LE, LS, PE；JEL：I38, K42

Abstract

Research on the social safety net examines its effects on recipients and their families. We show that these effects extend beyond recipients’ families. Using a regression discontinuity design and administrative data, we study a Danish policy that cut welfare benefits for refugees, increasing crime among affected individuals. Linking refugees to neighbors, we find increased crime among non-Danish neighbors, with spillovers persisting even after direct effects stabilize. Accounting for these spillovers raises the marginal value of public funds by 20%. We explore several mechanisms and find evidence consistent with peer effects among young individuals from the same country of origin.

中文摘要

关于社会安全网的研究考察了其对受益者及其家庭的影响。我们表明，这些影响超出了受益者家庭的范围。利用断点回归设计和行政数据，我们研究了一项丹麦政策，该政策削减了难民的福利金，增加了受影响个体的犯罪。将难民与其邻居联系起来，我们发现非丹麦裔邻居的犯罪增加，即使在直接影响稳定后，溢出效应仍然持续。考虑这些溢出效应使公共资金的边际价值提高了20%。我们探讨了几种机制，并发现了与来自同一原籍国的年轻人之间的同伴效应相一致的证据。

Does Matching Contribution Incentivize Informal Workers to Participate in Retirement Saving Plans? A Randomized Evaluation Interacted with a Natural Experiment

匹配缴款能否激励非正规工人参与退休储蓄计划？一项与自然实验交互的随机评估

Noelia Bernal, Sebastian Galiani, Oswaldo Molina

Programs：DEV；JEL：J49

Abstract

We conducted a large field experiment in Peru on informal workers and studied whether offering them a matching contribution raise participation and contributions in their Individual Retirement Accounts. We had three groups: a control group receiving no match, and two treatments groups receiving 50 and 100 percent match, respectively. Additionally, due to the time span, we can also analyze the difference responses between pre and during Covid-19. The results were as follows. First, the match incentive increases participation. Workers in the 50 and 100 percent match groups show participation rates of 5.2 and 6.5 p.p. higher than workers in the control group, respectively. The participation effect is also present pre Covid-19 and disappears during it. Second, the 100 percent match incentive was the only effective in increasing savings among all individuals (1.4 p.p.), pre (2.3 p.p.) and during Covid-19 (0.97 p.p.). This effect still presents in LATE specification with higher p.p. Third, 100% match was again the only effective to make contribute more than once, in the full sample (1.2 p.p.), and pre Covid-19 (2.7 p.p.), including LATE specification (full sample – 5.6 p.p.; pre Covid-19 – 8.2p.p.). Fourth the 50 percent match is not effective in raising contribution in any specification.

中文摘要

我们在秘鲁对非正规工人进行了一项大型实地实验，研究向他们提供匹配缴款是否会提高其个人退休账户的参与率和缴款额。我们设置了三个组：一个不接受匹配的对照组，以及分别接受50%和100%匹配的两个处理组。此外，由于时间跨度，我们还可以分析新冠疫情前和期间的不同反应。结果如下。首先，匹配激励提高了参与率。50%和100%匹配组的工人参与率分别比对照组高5.2和6.5个百分点。参与效应在新冠疫情前也存在，但在疫情期间消失。其次，100%匹配激励是唯一有效提高所有个体储蓄的激励（1.4个百分点），在新冠疫情前（2.3个百分点）和期间（0.97个百分点）均有效。这一效应在LATE设定中仍然存在，且百分点更高。第三，100%匹配再次是唯一有效促使多次缴款的激励，在全样本（1.2个百分点）和新冠疫情前（2.7个百分点）均有效，包括LATE设定（全样本 – 5.6个百分点；新冠疫情前 – 8.2个百分点）。第四，50%匹配在任何设定下均未能有效提高缴款额。

Using AI to Generate Option C Scaling Ideas: A Case Study in Early Education

利用人工智能生成选项C扩展思路：以早期教育为例

Faith Fatchen, John A. List, Francesca Pagnotta

Programs：EEE, PE；JEL：C9, C90, C91, C92, C93, C99

Abstract

In recent years, field experiments have reshaped policy worldwide, but scaling ideas remains a thorny challenge. Perhaps the most important issue facing policymakers today is deciding which ideas to scale. One approach to attenuate this information problem is to augment traditional A/B experimental designs to address questions of scalability from the beginning. List 2024 denotes this approach as “Option C” thinking. Using early education as a case study, we show how AI can overcome a critical barrier in Option C thinking – generating viable options for scaling experimentation. By integrating AI-driven insights, this approach strengthens the link between controlled trials and large-scale implementation, ensuring the production of policy-based evidence for effective decision-making.

中文摘要

近年来，实地实验重塑了全球政策，但如何扩展思路仍是一个棘手挑战。当今政策制定者面临的最重要问题或许是决定哪些思路值得扩展。缓解这一信息问题的一种方法是从一开始就增强传统的A/B实验设计，以解决可扩展性问题。List（2024）将这种方法称为“选项C”思维。以早期教育为案例，我们展示了人工智能如何克服选项C思维中的一个关键障碍——为扩展实验生成可行的选项。通过整合人工智能驱动的洞察，这种方法加强了受控试验与大规模实施之间的联系，确保为有效决策提供基于政策的证据。

No. 5NBER w33923

Like Great-Grandparent, Like Great-Grandchild? Multigenerational Mobility in American History

曾祖辈如何，曾孙辈亦如何？美国历史上的多代际流动性

Zachary Ward, Kasey Buckles, Joseph Price

Programs：CH, DAE, LS；JEL：J62, N31, N32

Abstract

Using data on 2.5 million great-grandchildren linked to their great-grandfathers in the US (1850–1940), we show that economic gaps persisted strongly across four generations despite major structural change. We find that one-third of the initial differences in economic status across white great-grandfathers remained in their great-grandchildren. When including both Black and white families, this persistence rises to about 50 percent, largely because the gap between Black and white families closed slowly over time. Grandparent and great-grandparent status matter beyond the father's status, indicating slower convergence to the mean than predicted by two-generational estimates. However, this excess persistence is largely driven by enduring racial inequality as grandparent effects are small within the white population.

中文摘要

利用美国（1850-1940年）250万曾孙辈与其曾祖父相关联的数据，我们表明，尽管经历了重大结构性变革，经济差距在四代人之间依然持续存在。我们发现，白人曾祖父之间经济地位的初始差异中，有三分之一仍保留在其曾孙辈中。当同时纳入黑人和白人家庭时，这种持续性上升至约50%，这主要是因为黑人与白人家庭之间的差距随时间推移缓慢缩小。祖父母和曾祖父母的地位超越了父亲地位的影响，表明向均值的收敛速度慢于两代际估计所预测的速度。然而，这种额外的持续性在很大程度上是由持久的种族不平等驱动的，因为在白人群体内部，祖父母效应很小。

No. 6NBER w33922

The Neoclassical Theory of Firm Investment and Taxes: A Reassessment

新古典企业投资与税收理论：重新评估

Gabriel Chodorow-Reich

Programs：CF, EFG, ME, PE；JEL：E23, G31, H25

Abstract

This article corrects a 60-year history of mis-application of the neoclassical theory of investment to interpret empirical work and guide policy analysis. Empirical estimates of firm-level user cost elasticities of investment or capital have in fact studied two distinct objects, an elasticity holding output fixed that identifies the elasticity of substitution between capital and labor (Eisner and Nadiri, 1968) and an elasticity holding the wage fixed that sheds light on the revenue elasticity of capital (Coen, 1969). A review finds a consensus range for the Coen short-run user cost elasticity that translates into a priori plausible values for the revenue elasticity of capital, offering validation to the neoclassical theory. The long-run general equilibrium capital elasticity to the user cost instead holds labor fixed and depends on both firm-level parameters. This synthesis offers a framework for future work and helps to explain why policy institutions have differing views on the effects of corporate taxes.

中文摘要

本文纠正了60年来对新古典投资理论的误用历史，这些误用体现在解释实证工作和指导政策分析中。对企业层面投资或资本的用户成本弹性的实证估计实际上研究了两个不同的对象：一个是在产出固定时估计的弹性，它识别了资本与劳动之间的替代弹性（Eisner和Nadiri, 1968）；另一个是在工资固定时估计的弹性，它揭示了资本的收入弹性（Coen, 1969）。综述发现，Coen短期用户成本弹性的共识范围转化为先验合理的资本收入弹性值，从而验证了新古典理论。长期一般均衡中资本对用户成本的弹性则保持劳动固定，并取决于这两个企业层面参数。这一综合为未来工作提供了一个框架，并有助于解释为何政策机构对企业税的影响持有不同观点。

No. 7NBER w33921

Increasing Degree Attainment Among Low-Income Students: The Role of Intensive Advising and College Quality

提高低收入学生的学位获得率：强化咨询与大学质量的作用

Andrew C. Barr, Benjamin L. Castleman

Programs：CH, ED, LS, PE；JEL：H52, I24, J24

Abstract

A college degree offers a pathway to economic mobility for low-income students. Using a multi-site randomized controlled trial combined with administrative and survey data, we demonstrate that intensive advising during high school and college significantly increases bachelor’s degree attainment among lower-income students. We leverage unique data on pre-advising college preferences and causal forest methods to show that these gains are primarily driven by improvements in initial enrollment quality. Our results suggest that strategies targeting college choice may be a more effective and efficient means of increasing degree attainment than those focused solely on affordability.

中文摘要

大学学位为低收入学生提供了经济流动的途径。通过一项结合行政数据和调查数据的多地点随机对照试验，我们证明，在高中和大学期间提供强化咨询能显著提高低收入学生的学士学位获得率。我们利用咨询前大学偏好的独特数据和因果森林方法表明，这些收益主要源于初始入学质量的改善。我们的结果表明，与仅关注可负担性的策略相比，针对大学选择的策略可能是提高学位获得率更有效且更高效的手段。

No. 8NBER w33920

The Social Desirability Atlas

社会期望偏差图谱

Leonardo Bursztyn, Ingar K. Haaland, Nicolas Röver, Christopher Roth

Programs：POL, TWP；JEL：B41, C83

Abstract

Social desirability bias (SDB) is a pervasive threat to the validity of survey and experimental data. Respondents might often misreport sensitive attitudes and behaviors to appear more socially acceptable. We begin by synthesizing empirical evidence on the prevalence and magnitude of SDB across various domains, focusing on studies with individual-level benchmarks. We then critically assess commonly used strategies to mitigate SDB, highlighting how they can sometimes fail by creating confusion or inadvertently increasing perceived sensitivity. To help researchers navigate these challenges, we offer practical guidance on selecting the most suitable tools for different research contexts. Finally, we examine how SDB can distort treatment effects in experiments and discuss mitigation strategies.

中文摘要

社会期望偏差（SDB）是对调查和实验数据有效性的普遍威胁。受访者可能经常误报敏感态度和行为，以显得更符合社会期望。我们首先综合了关于SDB在各个领域普遍性和程度的实证证据，重点关注具有个体层面基准的研究。然后，我们批判性地评估了常用的缓解SDB的策略，强调它们有时可能因造成混淆或无意中增加感知敏感性而失败。为了帮助研究人员应对这些挑战，我们提供了针对不同研究背景选择最合适工具的实用指南。最后，我们考察了SDB如何扭曲实验中的处理效应，并讨论了缓解策略。

No. 9NBER w33919

Scalable Targeting of Social Protection: When Do Algorithms Out-Perform Surveys and Community Knowledge?

社会保障的可扩展精准瞄准：算法何时优于调查和社区知识？

Emily Aiken, Anik Ashraf, Joshua Blumenstock, Raymond Guiteras, Ahmed Mushfiq Mobarak

Programs：DEV, PE；JEL：C55, I32, I38, O1

Abstract

Innovations in big data and algorithms are enabling new approaches to target interventions at scale. We compare the accuracy of three different systems for identifying the poor to receive benefit transfers — proxy means-testing, nominations from community members, and an algorithmic approach using machine learning to predict poverty using mobile phone usage behavior — and study how their cost-effectiveness varies with the scale and scope of the program. We collect mobile phone records from all major telecom operators in Bangladesh and conduct community-based wealth rankings and detailed consumption surveys of 5,000 households, to select the 22,000 poorest households for $300 transfers from 106,000 listed households. While proxy-means testing is most accurate, algorithmic targeting becomes more cost-effective for national-scale programs where large numbers of households have to be screened. We explore the external validity of these insights using survey data and mobile phone records data from Togo, and cross-country information on benefit transfer programs from the World Bank.

中文摘要

大数据和算法的创新正在为大规模干预措施的精准瞄准提供新方法。我们比较了三种识别贫困人口以发放福利转移支付系统的准确性——代理家计调查、社区成员提名以及利用机器学习通过手机使用行为预测贫困的算法方法——并研究了它们的成本效益如何随项目的规模和范围而变化。我们收集了孟加拉国所有主要电信运营商的手机记录，并对5000户家庭进行了社区财富排名和详细消费调查，从106000户登记家庭中选出22000户最贫困家庭，每户发放300美元转移支付。虽然代理家计调查最准确，但对于需要筛选大量家庭的国家级项目，算法瞄准变得更具成本效益。我们利用多哥的调查数据和手机记录数据，以及世界银行的跨国福利转移项目信息，探讨了这些洞察的外部有效性。

No. 10NBER w33918

Who Benefits from Paid Family Leave? The Impact on Informal and Formal Care for Middle-Aged and Older Adults with Disabilities

谁从带薪家事假中受益？对中老年残疾人士非正式和正式照护的影响

Yuting Qian, Xi Chen

Programs：AG, EH, LS, PE；JEL：I10, I38, J14, J18

Abstract

This paper analyzes the impact of paid family leave (PFL) policies on informal and formal care for middle-aged and older adults with disabilities in the U.S., and how the heterogeneous benefits accrue to different families. We use data from the 1998-2018 Health and Retirement Study (HRS) and leverage the PFL programs implemented in California (2004), New Jersey (2009), and New York (2018) in a difference-in-differences (DiD) design. We deploy both the conventional two-way fixed effects (TWFE) model and an adapted DiD estimator developed by Sun and Abraham for staggered rollout designs. We find that PFL access is associated with a 5.7 percentage point increase in the likelihood that individuals with disabilities receive informal care from their children. We also show that PFL access significantly increases the use of home care services and nursing home care. These effects are primarily concentrated among individuals with disabilities who have both a spouse and children, and are almost non-existent among those who have only children and no spouse. Our findings demonstrate that PFL policies improve care access and help address unmet care needs for middle-aged and older adults with disabilities, but their impact remains limited for certain vulnerable subgroups, particularly those with only children.

中文摘要

本文分析了美国带薪家事假（PFL）政策对中老年残疾人士非正式和正式照护的影响，以及异质性收益如何在不同家庭中分配。我们使用1998-2018年健康与退休研究（HRS）的数据，并利用加利福尼亚州（2004年）、新泽西州（2009年）和纽约州（2018年）实施的PFL项目，采用双重差分（DiD）设计。我们同时部署了传统的双向固定效应（TWFE）模型和Sun与Abraham为交错推出设计改编的DiD估计量。我们发现，获得PFL与残疾人士从子女处获得非正式照护的可能性增加5.7个百分点相关。我们还表明，获得PFL显著增加了家庭护理服务和养老院护理的使用。这些效应主要集中在既有配偶又有子女的残疾人士中，而在仅有子女而无配偶的群体中几乎不存在。我们的发现表明，PFL政策改善了照护可及性，有助于满足中老年残疾人士未满足的照护需求，但其对某些脆弱亚群（特别是仅有子女的群体）的影响仍然有限。

No. 11NBER w33917

Nicotine Vaping and Youth Mental Health: New Evidence from E-Cigarette Regulations

尼古丁电子烟与青少年心理健康：来自电子烟监管的新证据

Chad D. Cotti, Tessie Krishna, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Erik T. Nesson, Joseph J. Sabia

Programs：AG, CH, EH, LE, PE；JEL：I10, I18

Abstract

The confluence of a youth mental health crisis and high rates of teenage nicotine vaping has led some U.S. tobacco control advocates to argue that reducing access to electronic nicotine delivery systems (ENDS) — through policies such as ENDS taxation — may improve youth and young adult mental health. Using data from several nationally representative surveys (Youth Risk Behavior Survey, Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System, and Population Assessment of Tobacco and Health) and a generalized difference-in-differences approach, we find no evidence that ENDS taxation improves youth and young adult mental health. With 95 percent confidence, we can rule out that the mean state ENDS tax increase adopted during our analysis sample ($0.34 per mL of e-liquid in 2023$) reduces persistent depressive symptoms among youths by more than 0.3 percent and suicide ideation by more than 1.0 percent. Moreover, discrete-time hazard models provide little evidence that ENDS taxes affect dynamics in youth mental health. A similar pattern of results emerges when we examine a wider set of ENDS regulations, including minimum legal purchasing ages, e-cigarette licensure laws, online sales restrictions, and restrictions on indoor nicotine vaping.

中文摘要

青少年心理健康危机与青少年尼古丁电子烟高使用率的交汇，导致一些美国烟草控制倡导者认为，减少电子尼古丁传送系统（ENDS）的可及性——例如通过ENDS税收等政策——可能改善青少年和年轻人的心理健康。利用来自多个全国代表性调查（青少年风险行为调查、行为风险因素监测系统和烟草与健康人口评估）的数据以及广义双重差分方法，我们没有发现ENDS税收能改善青少年和年轻人心理健康的证据。在95%的置信水平下，我们可以排除我们分析样本期间各州平均ENDS税收增加（2023年每毫升电子液体0.34美元）会使青少年持续性抑郁症状减少超过0.3%、自杀意念减少超过1.0%的可能性。此外，离散时间风险模型几乎没有提供ENDS税收影响青少年心理健康动态的证据。当我们考察更广泛的ENDS监管措施（包括最低法定购买年龄、电子烟许可法、在线销售限制和室内尼古丁电子烟限制）时，出现了类似的结果模式。

No. 12NBER w33916

Abortion Restrictions and Intimate Partner Violence in the Dobbs Era

多布斯时代堕胎限制与亲密伴侣暴力

Dhaval M. Dave, Christine Durrance, Bilge Erten, Yang Wang, Barbara L. Wolfe

Abstract

In overturning Roe v. Wade and triggering laws in many states that ban or severely restrict abortion, the Supreme Court’s landmark 2022 Dobbs decision dramatically altered the landscape of reproductive health in the U.S. Prior research has highlighted the far-reaching impact of abortion restrictions for women and families, which extend beyond their proximate effects on abortions, births, and fertility. IPV is the most common form of violence experienced by women, and changes in access to abortion may generate unintended effects on various inputs (economic resources, stress, intrahousehold bargaining) that could affect relationship dynamics and raise the risk of IPV. Programs：CH, DEV, EH, PE；JEL：I1, J10, J13

Abstract

Birth rates are falling worldwide, in every region. Falling birth rates can be decomposed into two components: (1) an increase in childlessness (i.e., lifetime nulliparity), and (2) fewer children ever born to women who have at least one child (completed cohort fertility among the parous). This paper quantifies the contributions of these two components for women in advanced economies in Europe, North and South America, and southeast Asia, and for recent cohorts in Indian districts. Janet Currie, Soodeh Saberian

Programs：EEE, EH；JEL：I1, Q54

Abstract

This study examines the relationship between wildfires and mental health-related hospitalizations in Canada from 2006 to 2018. Most previous estimates of the mental health costs of wildfire have focused on the impacts of exposure to PM₂.₅. We break new ground by highlighting other pathways for wildfires to affect mental health, including evacuation orders, direct local costs of fires, and climate anxiety, which is proxied using wildfire-related Twitter activity. We find that all these mechanisms affect mental health-related hospitalizations, with especially large impacts on hospitalizations for anxiety and substance abuse. Elderly people and those with pre-existing health conditions that make them more vulnerable, are more strongly affected. The results indicate that climate anxiety stoked by wildfire events may account for much of the overall mental health cost. Accounting for these additional mechanisms does little to diminish the estimated effect of PM ₂.₅ from wildfire smoke, however, suggesting that the additional factors have effects on mental health that are in addition to those of PM₂.₅.

Masao Fukui, Toshihiko Mukoyama

Programs：EFG, LS；JEL：E0, J01

Abstract

This paper examines the efficiency of a decentralized equilibrium in a broad class of random-search job-ladder models. We decompose the source of inefficiency into two margins: (i) the investment margin, that is, the difference between the private and social benefit of job creation given the surplus of a match, and (ii) the valuation margin, that is, the difference between the private valuation and the social valuation of a match surplus. In the presence of on-the-job searches, the well-known Hosios condition no longer guarantees the market equilibrium aligns with the efficient allocation along both margins. On-the-job searches contribute to the overvaluation of the match surplus in market equilibrium, especially at the top of the job ladder. Consequently, the decentralized equilibrium with the Hosios condition features excess creation of vacancies in the steady state. On-the-job searches also lead to excess volatility in unemployment in response to aggregate productivity shocks. Quantitatively, we find a significant difference between the equilibrium outcome and the efficient allocation under standard calibration. We also consider several decentralizations of the efficient allocation to shed light on the optimal policies under the frictional labor market.

Programs：DEV, LS；JEL：E24, J22, J23, J31, J63, J64, O15, O17

Abstract

This has relevance for understanding how labor markets change in response to, and help facilitate, the process of development.

Growth in AI Knowledge

人工智能知识的增长

Joshua S. Gans

Programs：PR；JEL：O30, O31, O40

Abstract

How does artificial intelligence affect the creation of new knowledge and long-run economic growth? This paper develops a general equilibrium model where AI enhances decision-making by interpolating between existing knowledge points, reducing uncertainty within the boundaries of what is already known. This capability creates competing incentives for knowledge creation: AI makes dense knowledge clusters more valuable by enabling interpolation, but also reduces the penalty for knowledge gaps. We show that AI’s impact on growth depends on a critical threshold in its capabilities. When AI has a modest interpolation range, it encourages incremental research that increases knowledge density, raising growth when ideas are getting harder to find. When AI has an extensive range, it promotes exploratory research that reduces knowledge density, paradoxically slowing growth despite direct productivity gains. The welfare effects depend on both AI capabilities and market structure— monopolistic AI provision can improve welfare by restricting adoption and preventing excessive exploration, while competitive provision amplifies research distortions. These results suggest that policies limiting AI capabilities or promoting market power in AI provision may have unexpected benefits for innovation and growth when research productivity depends on knowledge density.

Measuring Industrial Policy: A Text-Based Approach

衡量产业政策：一种基于文本的方法

Réka Juhász, Nathan J. Lane, Emily Oehlsen, Veronica C. Perez

Programs：IO, ITI, POL, PR；JEL：C38, L52, O25

Abstract

Since the 18th century, policymakers have debated the merits of industrial policy (IP). Yet, economists lack basic facts about its use due to measurement challenges. We propose a new approach to IP measurement based on information contained in policy text. We show how off-the-shelf supervised machine learning tools can be used to categorize industrial policies at scale. Using this approach, we validate long-standing concerns with earlier measurement approaches that conflate IP with other types of policy. We apply our methodology to a global database of commercial policy descriptions, and provide a first look at IP use at the country, industry, and year levels (2010-2022). The new data on IP suggest that i) IP is on the rise; ii) modern IP tends to use subsidies and export promotion measures as opposed to tariffs; iii) rich countries heavily dominate IP use; iv) IP tends to target sectors with an established comparative advantage, particularly in high-income countries.

AI and Social Media: A Political Economy Perspective

人工智能与社交媒体：一个政治经济学视角

Daron Acemoglu, Asuman Ozdaglar, James Siderius

Programs：POL；JEL：L10, M37, P40

Abstract

We consider the political consequences of the use of artificial intelligence (AI) by online platforms engaged in social media content dissemination, entertainment, or electronic commerce. We identify two distinct but complementary mechanisms, the social media channel and the digital ads channel, which together and separately contribute to the polarization of voters and consequently the polarization of parties. First, AI-driven recommendations aimed at maximizing user engagement on platforms create echo chambers (or “filter bubbles”) that increase the likelihood that individuals are not confronted with counter-attitudinal content. Consequently, social media engagement makes voters more polarized, and then parties respond by becoming more polarized themselves. Second, we show that party competition can encourage platforms to rely more on targeted digital ads for monetization (as opposed to a subscription-based business model), and such ads in turn make the electorate more polarized, further contributing to the polarization of parties. These effects do not arise when one party is dominant, in which case the profit-maximizing business model of the platform is subscription-based. We discuss the impact regulations can have on the polarizing effects of AI-powered online platforms.

中文摘要

A Theory of Endogenous Degrowth and Environmental Sustainability

内生性去增长与环境可持续性理论

Philippe Aghion, Timo Boppart, Michael Peters, Matthew Schwartzman, Fabrizio Zilibotti

Programs：EEE, EFG, PR；JEL：E0, O41, O44, Q5

Abstract

We develop and quantify a novel growth theory in which economic activity endogenously shifts from material production to quality improvements. Consumers derive utility from goods with differing environmental footprints: necessities are material-intensive and polluting, while luxuries are more service-based and emit less. Innovation can be directed toward either material productivity or product~quality. Because demand for luxuries is more sensitive to quality, the economy gradually becomes “weightless”: growth is driven by quality improvements, services become the dominant employment sector, and material production stabilizes at a finite level. This structural transformation enables rising living standards with declining environmental intensity, providing an endogenous path to degrowth in material output without compromising economic progress. Policy can accelerate the transition, but its burden is uneven, falling more heavily on the poor than on the rich.

中文摘要
