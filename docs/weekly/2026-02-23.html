Optimal Simple Ratings

最优简单评级

Hugo Hopenhayn, Maryam Saeedi

Programs：IO, TWP；JEL：D21, D47, D60, D82, L11

Abstract

We study optimal simple rating systems that partition sellers into a finite number of tiers. We show that optimal ratings must be threshold partitions, and that for linear supply and Cournot competition with constant marginal cost, optimal thresholds solve a k-means clustering problem requiring only the quality distribution. For convex (concave) supply functions, optimal thresholds are higher (lower) than the k-means solution. For log-concave distributions, two-tier certification captures at least 50 percent of maximum welfare gains from full disclosure, with five tiers typically achieving over 90 percent. Applications to eBay and Medicare Advantage data illustrate our method.

中文摘要

我们研究了将卖家划分为有限数量等级的最优简单评级系统。我们证明，最优评级必须是阈值划分，并且对于线性供给和具有恒定边际成本的古诺竞争，最优阈值解决了一个仅需质量分布的k均值聚类问题。对于凸（凹）供给函数，最优阈值高于（低于）k均值解。对于对数凹分布，两级认证至少能捕获完全信息披露下最大福利收益的50%，而五级认证通常能达到90%以上。应用于eBay和Medicare Advantage数据的例子说明了我们的方法。

No. 2NBER w34888

Drivers of Dollar Share in Foreign Exchange Reserves

外汇储备中美元份额的驱动因素

Linda S. Goldberg, Oliver Hannaoui

Programs：IFM；JEL：F33

Abstract

The share of US dollar assets in the official foreign exchange reserve portfolios of central banks, at times, is taken as an indicator of the dollar's global status. We provide a decomposition that shows two distinct channels contributing to the changes in the dollar share of reserves aggregated across countries: shifts in preferences for dollar assets, and changes in reserve balances driven by countries whose portfolio allocations differ from the aggregate. We document how the concentrated nature of foreign exchange reserve holdings allows countries that contribute a large share of aggregated reserves to exert substantial influence on aggregate dollar shares over time, potentially dominating the narrative. In recent periods, the key contributors to changes in aggregate preference shifts for dollar assets are changes in bilateral country trade with the United States and dollar debt share, with geopolitics additionally working through the investment tranches of central bank portfolios. Diversification away from dollar assets is more prevalent conditional on official reserves of countries being large enough to satisfy country liquidity needs.

中文摘要

各国央行官方外汇储备组合中美元资产的份额，有时被视为美元全球地位的指标。我们提供了一个分解，显示有两个不同的渠道导致了各国汇总的美元储备份额的变化：对美元资产偏好的转变，以及由投资组合配置与总体不同的国家所驱动的储备余额变化。我们记录了外汇储备持有的集中性质如何使得那些贡献了汇总储备很大份额的国家，能够随着时间的推移对总体美元份额施加实质性影响，并可能主导相关叙事。在近期，导致对美元资产总体偏好转变的关键贡献因素是双边国家与美国贸易的变化以及美元债务份额，而地缘政治则通过央行投资组合的投资部分额外发挥作用。当一国的官方储备足够大以满足其流动性需求时，从美元资产分散化的现象更为普遍。

No. 3NBER w34887

A Theory of Subsidy Harvesting in Livestock Price Insurance

牲畜价格保险中的补贴套利理论

Michael K. Adjemian, A. Ford Ramsey

Programs：AP, PE；JEL：Q0, Q02, Q18

Abstract

USDA designed the Livestock Risk Protection (LRP) program to help producers insure against declining prices for fed cattle, feeder cattle, and swine. Prices in derivatives markets determine program indemnities, making LRP policies similar to put options. Beginning in 2019, USDA made several changes to the program to encourage producer takeup, including increased premium subsidies. We introduce a theoretical model to show that subsidizing LRP premiums invites producers to “subsidy harvest,” i.e. extract the subsidy as an arbitrage by simultaneously taking an offsetting position in the options market—potentially removing the downside protection the program was intended to provide. The subsidy harvest is a pure rent and costlier than a direct payment because it requires administrative oversight and federally-subsidized delivery through approved insurance providers. According to the model, the government’s premium subsidy leads producers to favor LRP-oriented strategies over market options alone, while their choice to offset actual risk protection using options depends on individual risk tolerance and wealth objectives.

中文摘要

美国农业部设计了牲畜风险保护（LRP）计划，以帮助生产者防范育肥牛、架子猪和生猪价格下跌的风险。衍生品市场的价格决定了该计划的赔偿额，使得LRP保单类似于看跌期权。从2019年开始，美国农业部对该计划进行了多项修改以鼓励生产者参与，包括增加保费补贴。我们引入了一个理论模型来证明，补贴LRP保费会诱使生产者进行“补贴套利”，即通过在期权市场同时建立对冲头寸来提取补贴作为套利——这可能会消除该计划旨在提供的下行保护。这种补贴套利是一种纯粹的租金，并且比直接支付成本更高，因为它需要行政监督和通过批准的保险提供商进行联邦补贴交付。根据该模型，政府的保费补贴导致生产者倾向于选择LRP导向的策略而非单纯的市场期权，而他们选择使用期权来抵消实际风险保护的决定则取决于个人的风险承受能力和财富目标。

No. 4NBER w34886

Shielding Women from Violence: The Effects of Violence Prevention and Monitoring Centers on Intimate Partner Violence in Türkiye

保护妇女免受暴力侵害：土耳其暴力预防与监测中心对亲密伴侣暴力的影响

Gunes Asik, Bilge Erten, Erdal Tekin, Semih Tumen, Hakan Ulucan

Programs：CH, DEV, EH；JEL：I18, J12, J16, K42, O15

Abstract

Intimate partner violence remains one of the most serious threats to women's safety worldwide, yet existing evidence on the effectiveness of large-scale institutional protection systems is limited and mixed. This paper evaluates the impact of Violence Prevention and Monitoring Centers on intimate partner violence (IPV) against women in Türkiye. Exploiting the staggered rollout of centers across provinces, we find that the opening of a center reduces female homicides, particularly those perpetrated by intimate partners, by approximately 20 percent. Complementary survey evidence shows that the opening of a center also reduces women's self-reported exposure to physical IPV by 20 percent, with larger effects among younger and less-educated women. Exploring potential mechanisms, we find that centers primarily reduce violence by lowering barriers to help-seeking and improving coordination across police, legal, and social services: following the opening of a center, women become more likely to seek support from women's organizations and social services, while direct applications to prosecutors decline. We find no evidence of effects on gender attitudes, labor market outcomes, relationship status, marriage market outcomes, or mental health, suggesting violence reductions operate through improved access to protection rather than changes in norms or economic independence. The estimated effects are stronger in provinces with more gender-progressive norms and stronger institutional capacity, highlighting the importance of complementary social and institutional environments.

中文摘要

亲密伴侣暴力仍然是全球妇女安全面临的最严重威胁之一，然而关于大规模制度化保护系统有效性的现有证据有限且结论不一。本文评估了暴力预防与监测中心对土耳其妇女遭受亲密伴侣暴力（IPV）的影响。利用各中心在各省份的逐步设立，我们发现，一个中心的开设使女性他杀案件，特别是由亲密伴侣实施的案件，减少了约20%。补充性的调查证据表明，中心的开设也使女性自我报告的身体IPV暴露减少了20%，对年轻和受教育程度较低的女性影响更大。在探索潜在机制时，我们发现，这些中心主要通过降低寻求帮助的障碍以及改善警方、法律和社会服务之间的协调来减少暴力：在中心开设后，女性更有可能向妇女组织和社会服务机构寻求支持，而直接向检察官提出的申请则有所减少。我们没有发现对性别态度、劳动力市场结果、关系状况、婚姻市场结果或心理健康产生影响的证据，这表明暴力的减少是通过改善获得保护的途径实现的，而非通过规范或经济独立性的改变。在性别观念更进步、制度能力更强的省份，估计的影响更强，这凸显了互补的社会和制度环境的重要性。

No. 5NBER w34885

Demographics and Technology Diffusion: Evidence from Mobile Payments

人口结构与技术扩散：来自移动支付的证据

Nicolas Crouzet, Pulak Ghosh, Apoorv Gupta, Filippo Mezzanotti

Programs：AG, CF, EFG, PR；JEL：G23, J11, O33

Abstract

We show that the age composition of the population can shape the speed at which businesses adopt new technologies, using evidence from mobile payments in India. Consumers' propensity to use new payment technologies declines with age, creating stronger incentives for businesses serving younger customers to accept these technologies. We document this pattern in the rollout of a mobile payment option by a major fintech company. A model where consumer attitudes toward technology vary by age implies that business adoption is inefficiently low, with the young bearing disproportionate welfare losses from network externalities. Jointly subsidizing transaction and adoption costs restores efficiency.

中文摘要

我们利用印度移动支付的证据表明，人口的年龄构成可以影响企业采用新技术的速度。消费者使用新支付技术的倾向随年龄增长而下降，这为服务更年轻客户的企业接受这些技术创造了更强的激励。我们在一家大型金融科技公司推出移动支付选项的过程中记录了这一模式。一个消费者对技术的态度随年龄变化的模型意味着，企业采用率低效地偏低，年轻人因网络外部性而承受不成比例的福利损失。联合补贴交易成本和采用成本可以恢复效率。

No. 6NBER w34884

Industrial Policies for Multi-Stage Production: The Battle for Battery-Powered Vehicles

多阶段生产的产业政策：电动汽车之战

Keith Head, Thierry Mayer, Marc Melitz, Chenying Yang

Programs：ITI；JEL：C63, F14, F23, L50, L62

Abstract

We model a multi-stage supply chain for EVs from battery production to vehicle distribution. Given industrial policies, firms select where to open facilities at each stage. This is a difficult combinatorial choice problem that we solve with a fast mixed integer linear programming formulation. We estimate the variable and fixed costs parameters using SMM. Counterfactual simulations reveal a tension between boosting EV adoption and promoting domestic supply chains. Due to increasing returns, even unconditional subsidies raise the number of factories in the subsidizing region—by about 16% for EVs and 7% for cells in North America, and even more in Europe. Theoretically, local assembly requirements can push down delivered marginal costs relative to unconditional subsidies. Empirically, local content requirements quadruple the expansion of cell factories in America, but they drive up costs and reduce subsidy uptake, undoing more than half of the EV adoption stimulus coming from pure buyer subsidies.

中文摘要

我们构建了一个从电池生产到车辆分销的电动汽车多阶段供应链模型。给定产业政策，企业选择在每个阶段在何处开设工厂。这是一个困难的组合选择问题，我们通过一个快速的混合整数线性规划公式来解决。我们使用模拟矩估计法（SMM）估计了可变成本和固定成本参数。反事实模拟揭示了促进电动汽车普及与推动国内供应链之间的张力。由于规模报酬递增，即使是无条件补贴也会提高补贴地区的工厂数量——在北美，电动汽车工厂数量增加约16%，电池工厂增加约7%，在欧洲则更多。理论上，本地组装要求可以相对于无条件补贴降低交付的边际成本。实证上，本地成分要求使美国电池工厂的扩张规模翻了两番，但它们推高了成本并降低了补贴利用率，抵消了纯消费者补贴带来的电动汽车普及刺激效果的一半以上。

No. 7NBER w34883

Shopping From Home

在家购物

Scott R. Baker, Nicholas Bloom, Stephanie G. Johnson, Jana Obradović

Programs：CF, EFG, LS, ME, PR；JEL：G0

Abstract

Conducting a new survey on the Nielsen Consumer panel we examine the impact of working from home (WFH) on shopping, finding three key results. First, WFH changed shopping modes, increasing online shopping and the fraction of trips to stores on weekdays. Second, WFH increased total spending through increased product quantity and range, tilting expenditure towards food and general merchandise and away from health and beauty products. Finally, WFH increased prices paid by shoppers and lowered price elasticities due to a move towards higher-cost products and lower deal usage. The change in prices paid is concentrated among married households, driven by a redistribution of shopping responsibility within the household, especially when the new remote worker is male. We find that remote workers engage in more shopping trips but fewer minutes of shopping. Our results suggest that shifting to remote work increases prices paid for groceries by about 1%, highlighting how WFH is impacting households, retail and inflation.

中文摘要

通过对尼尔森消费者面板进行一项新调查，我们研究了在家工作（WFH）对购物的影响，发现了三个关键结果。首先，WFH改变了购物模式，增加了在线购物以及在工作日前往商店购物的比例。其次，WFH通过增加产品数量和种类提高了总支出，使支出向食品和日用商品倾斜，而远离健康和美容产品。最后，WFH提高了购物者支付的价格，并因转向成本更高的产品和减少优惠使用而降低了价格弹性。支付价格的变化集中在已婚家庭中，这是由家庭内部购物责任的重新分配所驱动的，尤其是当新的远程工作者为男性时。我们发现，远程工作者进行更多次购物行程，但每次购物的分钟数更少。我们的结果表明，转向远程工作会使杂货支付价格提高约1%，凸显了WFH对家庭、零售业和通货膨胀的影响。

No. 8NBER w34882

Intangible Intensity

无形强度

Andrea L. Eisfeldt, Barney Hartman-Glaser, Edward T. Kim, Ki Beom Lee

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, PR, TWP；JEL：A0, C0, E0, E01, G0

Abstract

We develop a text-based measure of intangible investment intensity derived from firms’ 10-K filings, and offer a general methodology for semantic theme scoring (STS). Our approach further classifies disclosure text into knowledge, customer, and organization capital. Firms with high intangible intensity are smaller, younger, and invest heavily in R&D and human capital. The three subcomponents map cleanly to distinct economic firm types: knowledge-intensive firms are R&D-driven with high valuations and skilled labor; customer-intensive firms are mature, profitable, and commercially oriented; and organization-intensive firms are large, asset-heavy incumbents. Managerial expenditure descriptions thus provide informative signals about intangible investment, complementing financial statements in capturing corporate capital stocks.

中文摘要

我们开发了一种基于文本的无形投资强度衡量指标，该指标源自公司的10-K文件，并提供了一种通用的语义主题评分（STS）方法。我们的方法进一步将披露文本分类为知识资本、客户资本和组织资本。无形强度高的公司规模更小、更年轻，并在研发和人力资本上投入巨资。这三个子成分清晰地对应到不同的经济企业类型：知识密集型企业是研发驱动型的，具有高估值和熟练劳动力；客户密集型企业是成熟、盈利且商业导向的；组织密集型企业是大型、资产密集型的老牌企业。因此，管理层的支出描述提供了关于无形投资的信息性信号，在捕捉企业资本存量方面补充了财务报表。

No. 9NBER w34881

The Local Origins of Business Formation: Entry as a Two-Stage Process

企业形成的本地起源：作为两阶段过程的进入

Emin Dinlersoz, Timothy Dunne, John C. Haltiwanger, Veronika Penciakova

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：L26, M13, R12

Abstract

The business entry literature typically observes firms only at the first hire. We provide a new perspective using linked administrative microdata tracking the universe of U.S. business applications and their transition into employer firms. We model entry as a two-stage process: pursuit of a business idea (proxied by a business application) and implementation (transition). Results show these margins are distinct and associate differently with local conditions. While both margins matter, high-startup locations are characterized by high application intensity, whereas low-startup locations exhibit low transition rates, suggesting geographic disparities in entry arise from different dynamics at each stage of the entrepreneurial process.

中文摘要

企业进入文献通常只在首次雇佣时观察企业。我们利用追踪美国所有企业申请及其向雇主企业转变的关联行政微观数据，提供了一个新的视角。我们将进入建模为一个两阶段过程：追求商业创意（以企业申请为代理变量）和实施（转变）。结果表明，这两个阶段是不同的，并且与当地条件有不同的关联。虽然两个阶段都很重要，但高创业率地区的特征是申请强度高，而低创业率地区则表现出较低的转变率，这表明企业进入的地理差异源于创业过程每个阶段的不同动态。

No. 10NBER w34880

Do Informal Social Markets and Online Sellers Help Youths to Avoid E-Cigarette Taxation?

非正式社交市场和在线卖家是否帮助年轻人规避电子烟税收？

Chad D. Cotti, Dhaval M. Dave, Tessie Krishna, Erik T. Nesson, Joseph J. Sabia

Programs：CH, EH, LE, PE；JEL：I12, I18

Abstract

Youths who vape nicotine rarely make in-person purchases of e-cigarettes from a vendor but instead rely on alternative methods to obtain electronic nicotine delivery system (ENDS) products. This study is the first to explore whether the informal social market and online purchasing options help to insulate youths from e-cigarette taxation. Using data from the State Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance Surveys, we find that ENDS taxation reduces both the likelihood of teens obtaining ENDS products through direct in-person retail purchases as well as the likelihood of relying on informal social sources for e-cigarettes such as borrowing vaping devices from friends or third-party purchases. Auxiliary analyses from the Population Assessment of Tobacco and Health are generally consistent with these findings and suggest that impacts are concentrated on the initiation margin; higher ENDS taxes deter youths initiating through direct retail purchases as well as through the social market. For older teens ages 18 and older, however, we find that informal social sources may attenuate the impacts of e-cigarette taxes. Finally, we detect some evidence that ENDS taxes increases the likelihood that teenagers shift to online sources to obtain e-cigarettes.

中文摘要

吸食尼古丁电子烟的年轻人很少亲自从供应商处购买电子烟，而是依赖替代方法获取电子尼古丁传送系统（ENDS）产品。本研究首次探讨非正式社交市场和在线购买选项是否有助于年轻人免受电子烟税收的影响。使用州青少年风险行为监测调查的数据，我们发现ENDS税收既降低了青少年通过直接亲自零售购买获得ENDS产品的可能性，也降低了他们依赖非正式社交来源（如从朋友处借用电子烟设备或第三方购买）获取电子烟的可能性。来自烟草与健康人口评估的辅助分析普遍与这些发现一致，并表明影响集中在起始阶段；更高的ENDS税收阻止了青少年通过直接零售购买以及通过社交市场开始使用。然而，对于18岁及以上的大龄青少年，我们发现非正式社交来源可能会减弱电子烟税收的影响。最后，我们检测到一些证据表明，ENDS税收增加了青少年转向在线来源获取电子烟的可能性。

No. 11NBER w34879

Bank Runs and Interest Rates: A Revolving Lines Perspective

银行挤兑与利率：循环信贷额度的视角

Falk Bräuning, Victoria Ivashina

Programs：CF；JEL：G1, G21, G32

Abstract

Revolving credit is at the core of the banking business. Corporate revolving lines are demandable claims; thus, similar to a traditional run on deposits, sudden and widespread drawdowns can destabilize banks. However, unlike deposits, credit line utilization exhibits substantial interest rate sensitivity. As a result, a run on revolving credit lines is less likely in a high-interest-rate environment but can introduce vulnerabilities when policy rates are lowered. Using an interest rate discontinuity commonly embedded in commercial credit contracts, we propose a methodology to estimate the elasticity of credit demand and apply it to measure the sensitivity of precautionary drawdowns.

Abstract

The standard approach to portfolio selection involves two stages: forecast the asset returns and then plug them into an optimizer. We argue that this separation is deeply problematic. The first stage treats cross-sectional prediction errors as equally important across all securities. However, given that final portfolios might differ given distinct risk preferences and investment restrictions, the standard approach fails to recognize that the investor is not just concerned with the average forecast error - but the precision of the forecasts for the specific assets that are most important for their portfolio. We propose a novel implementation utilizing machine learning tools that unifies the expected return generation process and the final optimized portfolio. Our empirical example provides convincing evidence that our end-to-end method outperforms the traditional two-stage approach. In our framework, each investor has their own, endogenously determined, efficient frontier that depends on risk preferences, investor-specific constraints, as well as exposure to market frictions.
