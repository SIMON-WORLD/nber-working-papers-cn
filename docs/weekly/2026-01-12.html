How In-School Supervised Ed-Tech Support Produces Massive Learning Gains: A Khan Academy Field Experiment in India

校内监督下的教育技术支持如何产生巨大的学习收益：印度可汗学院实地实验

Philip Oreopoulos, Oliver Keyes-Krysakowski, Deepak Agarwal

Programs：CH, ED, LS；JEL：I2, I25, I3, O2

中文摘要

计算机辅助学习平台在大规模推广时常常表现不佳，原因并非技术无效，而是学校面临巨大的实施摩擦：教师和管理人员必须克服最初的技术障碍、重组教学常规、管理相互竞争的日程压力，并且在不确定技术有效性的情况下进行这些操作——这些条件常常导致学生参与度低且效率低下。本研究探讨了加强实施结构是否能够提高印度北方邦83所公立寄宿制中学的计算机辅助学习使用数量和质量，并进而提高学习收益。所有学校都可以使用可汗学院，但随机选择的处理学校获得了现场实验室管理员，其唯一职责是通过确保可靠的网络连接、简化学生注册、保护每周练习时间、监督课堂使用、与教师协调内容以及监控进度来保证高保真度的实施。该干预措施将平台使用时间从每周7.2分钟增加到47.4分钟。在31周内，数学成绩提高了近半个标准差，且收益在不同成绩水平和问题难度上广泛存在。这些结果表明，有效且可扩展的计算机辅助学习的核心制约因素并非技术或内容，而是确保持续、高效教学使用的组织结构的存在。

Abstract

Computer-assisted learning (CAL) platforms frequently underperform at scale not because the technology is ineffective, but because schools face substantial implementation frictions: teachers and administrators must overcome initial technical hurdles, reorganize instructional routines, manage competing scheduling pressures, and do so while uncertain about the technology’s effectiveness—conditions that often lead to low and unproductive student engagement. This study explores whether strengthening implementation structure can raise both the quantity and quality of CAL usage in 83 residential government middle schools in Uttar Pradesh, India and, in turn, learning gains. All schools had access to Khan Academy, but randomly selected treatment schools received on-the-ground lab-in-charges whose sole responsibility was to ensure high-fidelity implementation by securing reliable connectivity, simplifying student rostering, protecting weekly practice time, supervising in-class use, coordinating content with teachers, and monitoring progress. The intervention increased platform usage from 7.2 to 47.4 minutes per week. Mathematics achievement rose by almost half a standard deviation over 31 weeks, with gains broad-based across achievement levels and question difficulty. These results show that the central constraint on effective and scalable CAL is not technology or content, but the presence of organizational structures that ensure sustained, productive instructional use.

Re-Examining Geographic Variation in Health and Health Care

重新审视健康与医疗保健的地理差异

Amy Finkelstein, Matthew Gentzkow

Programs：AG, EH, PE；JEL：H51, I1, I11

中文摘要

大量文献记录了美国各地区人均医疗保健支出的广泛差异，而这些差异并未带来相应更好的健康结果。近期研究采用迁移者设计来估计地点对医疗保健支出和死亡率的因果影响。在本文中，我们调查了那些增加医疗保健支出的地点是否也往往是促进健康的地点。我们发现并非如此，并讨论了其含义。

Abstract

A large literature has documented widespread variation in health care spending per capita across areas of the United States without correspondingly better health outcomes. Recent work has used mover designs to estimate the causal impact of place on both health care spending and mortality. In this paper, we investigate whether places that increase health care spending also tend to be places that increase health. We find that they do not and discuss the implications.

No. 3NBER w34681

Firm Emissions and Credit Allocation

企业排放与信贷配置

Grace Weishi Gu, Galina Hale, Bhavyaa Sharma, Jinhong Wu

Programs：IFM；JEL：F21, G21, Q54

中文摘要

银行是促进还是阻碍了绿色转型？为了回答这个问题，我们分析了美国、欧盟以及单独针对丹麦的银行向企业发放贷款的动态，并与借款人的二氧化碳排放量相关联。我们评估了银行贷款在行业间以及行业内企业间的配置，考虑了企业排放的异质性和这些排放的变化。为了促进绿色转型，银行贷款需要流向更绿色和正在变绿的企业，但不能从需要资金转型为更清洁生产方式的高排放行业流出。使用银团贷款数据，我们发现对于美国借款人，银行贷款可能阻碍了绿色转型，而在欧盟，银行贷款更有可能促进绿色转型。聚焦于丹麦（我们拥有所有企业和银行的完整数据），我们发现信贷更多地重新配置给了更绿色的企业，尤其是在行业内部。然而，资金向绿色企业的重新配置在很大程度上是绿色企业规模扩大的副产品。我们没有发现任何证据表明银行在积极引导绿色转型。

Abstract

Do banks help or hamper green transition? To answer this question, we analyze the dynamics of bank lending to firms in the US, EU, and separately Denmark in relation to the borrowers' emissions of CO2. We evaluate the allocation of bank loans across industries and within industries across firms, allowing for heterogeneity of firm emissions and changes in these emissions. To facilitate green transition, bank lending needs to flow to greener and greening firms, but not out of high-emission industries that need funding to transition to cleaner production methods. Using syndicated loan data, we find that for US borrowers, bank lending was likely hampering green transition, while in the EU bank lending is more likely to facilitate it. Zooming in on Denmark, for which we have data on the full universe of firms and banks, we find more significant credit reallocation to greener firms, especially within industries. However, the reallocation of funds to green firms is, to a large extent, a byproduct of green firms becoming bigger. We do not find any evidence consistent with banks active stewardship of green transition.

No. 4NBER w34680

Platform Work: Evidence from Drivers in India, Indonesia, and Kenya

平台工作：来自印度、印度尼西亚和肯尼亚司机的证据

Achyuta Adhvaryu, Martin Atela, Valentina Brailovskaya, Priyanka Dua, Jenny Susan John, Pratibha Joshi, Rivandra Royono

Programs：DEV, LS；JEL：J22, J30, J46, O12

中文摘要

利用来自印度、印度尼西亚和肯尼亚三个主要零工平台上工作的司机代表性样本的调查和行政数据，我们记录了平台工人的构成、经济经历和劳动力市场轨迹。结合平台收入与运营成本数据，我们估算了每个情境下扣除成本后的净收入（按购买力平价调整）。我们发现，平台工作的灵活性使司机能够工作远超全职等效时间，从而产生比低技能或临时工作更高的月净收入，尽管其小时净收入相对于这些外部选择相当或更低。在印度和印度尼西亚，退出平台工作的司机是为了从事薪酬更高的全职职位。相比之下，肯尼亚司机通常是非自愿退出，回到线下驾驶工作并面临不利的财务后果。各国约有三分之一的司机依赖平台工作来补充紧急情况或工作淡季的收入，这表明平台工作可能作为财务安全网发挥重要作用。

Abstract

Using surveys and administrative data from representative samples of drivers working on three leading gig platforms in India, Indonesia, and Kenya, we document the composition, economic experiences, and labor market trajectories of platform workers. Combining platform-based earnings with operating cost data, we estimate earnings net of costs (in PPP-adjusted terms) in each context. We find that the flexible nature of platform work enables drivers to work substantially more than the full-time equivalent, generating higher monthly net earnings than low-skill or casual employment, despite comparable or lower hourly net earnings relative to these outside options. Drivers who exit platform work in India and Indonesia do so to take up better-paying full-time positions. In contrast, Kenyan drivers often exit involuntarily, returning to offline driving with adverse financial consequences. One-third of drivers across countries rely on platform work to supplement earnings during emergencies or slow work periods, suggesting that platform work may play an important role as a financial safety net.

No. 5NBER w34679

Transformed Intermediation: Credit Risk to NBFIs, Liquidity Risk to Banks

转型的中介：非银行金融机构的信用风险与银行的流动性风险

Viral V. Acharya, Nicola Cetorelli, Bruce Tuckman

Programs：CF；JEL：G01, G21, G23, G28

中文摘要

我们认为，非银行金融中介相对于银行的快速资产增长，是银行与非银行金融中介之间风险转型的结果，这种转型增加了两个部门的相互关联性。这些转型与规避后全球金融危机时期更严格的银行监管相一致，同时利用了银行存款特许经营权的融资和流动性优势以及获得安全网的机会。具体来说，我们表明银行通过优先贷款和信贷额度为非银行金融中介提供资金，非银行金融中介则将其用于获取次级信用债权、仓储融资和流动性管理。我们实证证明，非银行金融中介遭受的冲击会溢出到为其提供信贷额度的银行，尤其是在压力时期。然后，我们描述了一系列与我们的转型观点相一致的政策方法，并最后提出了未来研究的建议。

Abstract

We argue that the rapid asset growth of nonbank financial intermediaries (NBFIs) relative to banks is the outcome of transformations of risks between banks and NBFIs that increase the interconnectedness of the two sectors. These transformations are consistent with avoiding tighter, post-GFC bank regulation while harnessing the funding and liquidity advantages of bank deposit franchises and access to safety nets. Specifically, we show that banks fund NBFIs through senior loans and credit lines, which NBFIs use for acquiring junior credit claims, warehouse financing, and liquidity management. We empirically demonstrate that shocks experienced by NBFIs spillover to the banks that provide them with credit lines, particularly in times of stress. We then describe a number of policy approaches that are consistent with our transformation view and conclude with suggestions for future research.

No. 6NBER w34678

Do GLP-1 Medications Pay for Themselves?

GLP-1 药物能收回成本吗？

Coady Wing, Sih-Ting Cai, Daniel W. Sacks, Kosali I. Simon

Programs：AG, EH；JEL：I11

中文摘要

胰高血糖素样肽-1 受体激动剂代表了治疗糖尿病、肥胖症和降低心血管风险方面的重大进步，但它们也是广泛使用中最昂贵的药物之一，并且是重大政策辩论的主题。如果这些药物带来的健康改善导致后续医疗保健使用和医疗支出减少（即成本抵消），那么它们的高价可能高估了其净成本。我们使用保险索赔数据来估计此类抵消，考察开始使用 GLP-1 对后续 GLP-1 使用和支出以及其他非 GLP-1 支出的影响。我们采用堆叠双重差分设计，将开始使用 GLP-1 药物的患者与几个月或几年后才开始使用 GLP-1 的尚未接受治疗的对照组进行比较，从而能够控制潜在的时间趋势和基线特征。总体而言，我们没有发现后续医疗支出减少。尽管开始使用 GLP-1 减少了其他糖尿病药物的支出，但非 GLP-1 总支出增加了，这是由门诊医疗使用增加所驱动的；GLP-1 药物支出则机械地增加。因此，对于医疗支付方来说，开始使用 GLP-1 的相关成本超出了药物的标价。我们在 GLP-1 开始使用者的亚组中发现了类似的结果，包括那些既往有心血管疾病的人和没有糖尿病的人（与肥胖适应症一致）。我们的主要结果考察了开始使用后第一年的支出反应。然而，我们也在一个较小的样本中估计了长期效应，发现即使在开始使用 GLP-1 五年后也没有成本抵消。综合来看，这些结果表明，面临 GLP-1 覆盖成本的支付方不太可能通过减少其他护理支出来实现大量节省。如果 GLP-1 疗法最终能带来成本节约，那很可能只会在更长的时期内或通过非医疗渠道实现。

Abstract

Glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists (GLP-1s) represent a major improvement in treatment of diabetes, obesity, and cardiovascular risk reduction, but they are also among the most expensive drugs in widespread use and the subject of significant policy debate. The high price of these drugs may overstate their net cost if the health improvements they produce lead to reduced downstream health care use and medical spending, that is, cost offsets. We estimate such offsets using insurance claims data, examining the effects of GLP-1 initiation on subsequent GLP-1 use and spending, and on other non-GLP-1 spending. We use a stacked difference-in-differences design, comparing patients initiating GLP-1 medication to not-yet-treated controls who initiate GLP-1s several months or years later, allowing us to control for underlying time trends and baseline characteristics. Overall, we do not find a reduction in downstream medical spending. Although GLP-1 initiation reduces spending on other diabetes medications, total non-GLP-1 spending increases, driven by higher outpatient health care use; GLP- 1 drug spending rises mechanically. For health care payers, the relevant cost of GLP-1 initiation therefore extends beyond the sticker price of the drug. We find similar results across subgroups of GLP1 initiators including those with prior cardiovascular disease and those without diabetes (consistent with obesity indication). Our main results examine spending responses over the first year after initiation. However, we also estimate longer run effects in a smaller sample and find no cost offsets even five years after GLP-1 initiation. Taken together, these results suggest that payers facing the costs of GLP-1 coverage are unlikely to see large savings from reduced spending on other care. If GLP-1 therapies ultimately yield cost savings, they are likely to occur only over longer horizons or through non-medical channels.

The Impact of Preschool Entry Age on Children’s Behavioral and Developmental Health in Medicaid

学前入学年龄对医疗补助计划中儿童行为与发育健康的影响

Maya Rossin-Slater, Adrienne Sabety, Aileen Wu

Programs：CH, ED, EH, PE；JEL：I18, J13

中文摘要

我们发现公立幼儿园是获得健康和社会服务的门户。与出生在入学截止日期之后的低收入家庭儿童相比，出生在所在州入学截止日期之前不久的儿童在三岁和四岁时更有可能获得学校服务、言语语言病理学、职业或物理治疗以及视力和牙科服务。他们也更有可能通过补充保障收入获得经济支持。这些发现表明，学前入学将低收入儿童与健康和社会服务体系联系起来，其方式超出了课堂范围。

Abstract

We find that public preschools are a gateway to health and social services. Low-income children born shortly before their state’s school-entry cutoff date are more likely to receive school-based services, speech-language pathology, occupational or physical therapy, and vision and dental services at ages three and four, compared to children born after the cutoff. They are also more likely to receive financial support through Supplemental Security Income. These findings suggest that preschool enrollment connects low-income children to the health and social service system in ways that extend beyond the classroom.

Corporate Intent

企业意图

Augustin Landier, Parinitha R. Sastry, David Thesmar

Programs：CF；JEL：D90, G3, G41

中文摘要

投资者是否关心企业为何采取负责任的行为，而不仅仅是企业做了什么以及赚了多少钱？在我们的主要实验中，参与者对减少污染（相对于同行）的公司的股票进行估值，同时首席执行官阐述了公司的意图：亲社会或为了盈利。如果一家公司采取给定的亲社会行为是出于社会关切而非利润最大化，那么投资者愿意为其股票支付更高的价格。这种“意图溢价”与现金流和环境外部性的估值效应不同。道义论者、政治自由派、女性或较少个人主义的受访者对公司的意图评价最高。此外，有点令人惊讶的是，受访者对同时追求利润和社会目标且不进行排序的公司持正面看法。总体而言，我们的发现缓和了这样一种说法，即当行善与做得好之间没有财务权衡时，股东价值最大化与利益相关者价值最大化是等价的。

Abstract

Do investors care about why a firm behaves responsibly, over and above what the firm does and how much cash it earns? In our main experiment, participants value shares in companies that reduce pollution relative to peers, while CEOs articulate the company’s intentions: prosocial or for-profit. Investors are willing to pay more for shares in a company that takes a given prosocial action if it is motivated by social concern rather than profit maximization. This "intention premium" is distinct from the valuation effects of cash flows and environmental externalities. Respondents who are deontological, politically liberal, female, or less individualistic value the company’s intent the most. Additionally, and somewhat surprisingly, respondents view favorably firms that embrace both profit and social objectives, without ranking them. Overall, our findings temper claims that when there is no financial trade-off between doing good and doing well, shareholder value and stakeholder value maximization are equivalent.

No. 9NBER w34675

Experiences, Expectations, and Asset Prices

经历、预期与资产价格

Stefan Nagel

Programs：AP, ME；JEL：E70, G12

中文摘要

在具有主观信念的模型中，投资者信念的动态变化对于解释资产价格变动至关重要。允许主观信念偏离完全信息理性预期需要建立一个投资者如何形成信念的模型。我回顾了近期探索基于经历信念形成的研究。基于经历的模型与贝叶斯更新非常相似，但允许个体在形成关于数据生成过程参数的信念时，过度加权其一生中观察到的数据。由于不同年龄的个体从不同的历史中学习，该模型预测了年龄组之间的异质性，这种异质性随着个体所经历的实际数据路径而随时间演变，这与调查微观数据中观察到的与年龄相关的变化高度吻合。在总体层面，基于经历的预期形成可以解释资产价格动态。在股票市场中，从经历中学习长期现金流增长会产生估值周期和回报可预测性。基于经历形成的长期通胀预期可以解释实际利率的长期变化。最后，我概述了未来研究的方向。

Abstract

In models with subjective beliefs, the dynamics of investor beliefs are central to explaining asset price movements. Allowing subjective beliefs to deviate from full-information rational expectations requires a model of how investors form beliefs. I review recent research that explores experience-based belief formation. The experience-based model closely resembles Bayesian updating but allows individuals to overweight data observed during their own lifetimes when forming beliefs about the parameters of the data-generating process. Because individuals of different age learn from different histories, the model predicts heterogeneity between age groups that evolves over time with the path of realized data individuals have experienced, closely mirroring the age-related variation observed in survey microdata. At the aggregate level, experience-based expectations formation can explain asset price dynamics. In the stock market, learning from experience about long-run cash flow growth generates valuation cycles and return predictability. Experience-based formation of long-term inflation expectations can explain secular changes of real interest rates. I conclude by outlining directions for future research.

No. 10NBER w34674

The Economics of Scaling Early Childhood Programs: Lessons from The Chicago School

早期儿童项目规模化经济学：来自芝加哥学派的启示

John A. List

Programs：LS, PE, PR；JEL：C9, C91, C93, H10, H4, H41, J24

中文摘要

许多理念在小规模试验中表现出显著的回报，但在推广到更广泛的人群和环境时却常常令人失望。本研究以早期儿童投资为案例，构建了一个动态人力资本形成模型，该模型将互补性技能投资与关于规模化挑战的“选项C思维”相结合。该模型是芝加哥传统风格的典型代表：以微观基础为起点，包含优化行为的代理人、动态技能生产以及评估规模化决策的政策制定者。它形式化了从试点研究中进行天真外推如何系统地高估政策有效性，原因在于忽略了“电压降”，即由于规模化后样本不具代表性而导致处理效应递减。该模型表明，最优规模化政策需要基于机制的设计，通过从实施现实出发进行反向归纳来预见这些失败。一系列近期研究的科学见解为该模型提供了有价值的视角。

Abstract

Many ideas show remarkable returns in small-scale trials but often disappoint when scaled to broader populations and contexts. Using early childhood investment as a case study, this study develops a dynamic human capital formation model that integrates complementary skill investment with “Option C thinking” on scaling challenges. The model is stylized in the Chicago tradition: micro-founded with optimizing agents, dynamic skill production, and a policymaker evaluating scaling decisions. It formalizes how naive extrapolation from pilot studies systematically overestimates policy efficacy by ignoring “voltage drops,” declining treatment effects due to unrepresentativeness at scale. The model demonstrates that optimal scaling policy requires mechanism-based design that anticipates these failures through backward induction from implementation realities. The scientific insights from a set of recent studies provide valuable perspectives on the model.

No. 11NBER w34673

Legalizing Cannabis: Implications for Child Maltreatment

大麻合法化：对儿童虐待的影响

Weiwei Chen, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Michael T. French

Programs：CH, EH, LE；JEL：H0, I12, K0

中文摘要

大麻是美国使用最广泛的非法药物。尽管联邦层面禁止持有和消费大麻，但各州越来越多地实施使该物质合法化的法律，最初是用于医疗用途，最近则用于娱乐用途。我们研究了娱乐性大麻法律对儿童虐待报告的影响。为此，我们采用双重差分法和事件研究法，分析了2010年至2022年关于儿童虐待报告以及儿童伤害相关死亡的行政数据。我们发现，近期将大麻用于娱乐消费合法化的努力并未导致儿童虐待报告的增加，并且可能减少特别严重的虐待行为。

Abstract

Cannabis is the most used illicit drug in the United States. Though cannabis possession and consumption are prohibited federally, states are increasingly implementing laws that legalize this substance, initially for medical and, more recently, for recreational use. We study the impact of recreational cannabis laws on child maltreatment reports. To do so, we employ difference-in-differences and event-study methods to analyze administrative data on child maltreatment reports as well as child injury-related deaths 2010-2022.
