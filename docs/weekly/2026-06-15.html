AI and the Collapse of the www

人工智能与万维网的崩溃

Alex Chan

Programs：IO, PR；JEL：D4, D43, D47, D49, D62, D8, D82, D83, L82, L86, O3, O33

Abstract

This paper studies market design for generative AI intermediation. AI answer systems can improve user experience while diverting visits that finance publisher content and generate source-level quality signals. I show that an AI platform that underinternalizes future content reproduction retains too little referral traffic and can make costly open-web information subcritical, even with truthful content, accurate answers, and rational users. The mechanism can be self-reinforcing: less source-level measurement weakens conventional search, inducing further AI reliance. Sustainable repair requires replacing displaced revenue and deleted measurement through visitor-replacement royalties, audited provenance, human-information audits, and keystone-topic compensation.

中文摘要

本文研究了生成式人工智能中介的市场设计。AI问答系统能够改善用户体验，但同时会分流原本用于资助出版商内容并产生源级质量信号的访问流量。研究表明，一个未能充分内化未来内容再生产的AI平台会保留过少的推荐流量，并可能导致代价高昂的开放网络信息变得次临界——即使内容真实、答案准确且用户理性。这一机制可能具有自我强化效应：源级测量的减少会削弱传统搜索，进而促使更多AI依赖。可持续修复需要借助访客替代版税、可审计溯源、人类信息审计以及关键主题补偿，来替代被取代的收入和被删除的测量数据。

Lack of Trust and Fiscal Dominance: Evidence from a Firm Survey Experiment

信任缺失与财政主导：来自企业调查实验的证据

Francesco Bianchi, Renato Faccini, Leonardo Melosi, Nils Wehrhöfer

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：C93, D84, E31, E32, E62

Abstract

We study how debt news shapes firms’ inflation expectations in a monetary union. In an active-control experiment, German firms receive optimistic or pessimistic projections of France, Italy, and Spain’s debt-to-GDP ratios. Pessimistic news raises debt beliefs and increases one- and three-year inflation expectations, with no detectable effect at five years. The response is driven by low-trust firms and by firms expecting relatively low ECB policy rates. A salient German debt-financed fiscal shock generates no comparable response. Within a Fisherian framework, the evidence suggests that debt news becomes inflationary when firms perceive incomplete fiscal backing and expect monetary accommodation.

中文摘要

我们研究债务新闻如何影响货币联盟中企业的通胀预期。在一项主动控制实验中，德国企业收到关于法国、意大利和西班牙债务与GDP比率的乐观或悲观预测。悲观消息提高了债务信念，并推高了一年期和三年期通胀预期，但对五年期通胀预期无显著影响。这一反应由低信任度企业以及预期欧洲央行政策利率相对较低的企业驱动。一次显著的德国债务融资财政冲击并未产生类似反应。在费雪框架下，证据表明当企业认为财政支持不完整并预期货币宽松时，债务新闻会引发通胀效应。

No. 3NBER w35340

Demographic Divergence: The Legacy of the Opioid Epidemic

人口分化：阿片类药物危机的遗产

Carolina Arteaga, Victoria Barone, Stephen Claassen

Programs：EH；JEL：I1, I3, J13, R23

Abstract

We study how the opioid epidemic shaped local population dynamics in the United States. Exploiting variation in exposure that stems from Purdue Pharma's targeted marketing of OxyContin to high-cancer-mortality areas, we find that more exposed commuting zones experienced lower population growth. By 2020, a one-standard-deviation increase in exposure reduced population growth among individuals aged 18 to 64 by 2.4 percentage points. Direct mortality from drug-induced deaths made only a limited contribution to these changes. Instead, population losses were primarily driven by migratory responses: exposure increased out-migration rates, especially among college-educated individuals. These responses are consistent with the opioid epidemic operating as a disamenity shock, deteriorating local quality of life. Working in the opposite direction, we also document a rise in fertility rates that, by 2020, partially counteracts these population losses. Our findings show that the opioid epidemic reshaped local demographic composition and contributed to the long-run divergence in population dynamics across U.S. commuting zones.

中文摘要

我们研究阿片类药物危机如何塑造美国地方人口动态。利用普渡制药公司针对高癌症死亡率地区定向营销奥施康定所导致的暴露程度差异，我们发现暴露程度更高的通勤区人口增长更低。到2020年，暴露程度每增加一个标准差，18至64岁人口增长率降低2.4个百分点。药物致死直接死亡率对这些变化的贡献有限。相反，人口损失主要由迁移反应驱动：暴露增加了向外迁移率，尤其是在大学学历人群中。这些反应与阿片类药物危机作为负外部性冲击、恶化地方生活质量的机制一致。相反方向，我们还发现生育率上升，到2020年部分抵消了这些人口损失。我们的研究结果表明，阿片类药物危机重塑了地方人口结构，并推动了美国各通勤区人口动态的长期分化。

No. 4NBER w35339

Blindly Discriminating: The GI Bill and Racial Inequality

盲目歧视：GI法案与种族不平等

Lukas Althoff, Christiane Szerman

Programs：CH, DAE, ED, LS, PE, POL；JEL：H4, H5, H7, J08, J24, J62, J7, N3, N4, N9

Abstract

The World War II GI Bill was the largest education subsidy in US history and a cornerstone of the postwar US transition to a knowledge economy. Although formally race-blind, the program's decentralized administration left implementation to local officials and segregated institutions, with sharply different consequences for Black and white veterans. This paper quantifies the GI Bill's impact on Black and white Americans' economic outcomes across two generations, using a regression discontinuity around WWII service eligibility cutoffs and a new data linkage from veterans in the 1940 and 1950 censuses to their sons' neighborhood outcomes between 1990 and 2025. The GI Bill widened racial inequality, doubling white veterans' college completion while steering Black veterans into often-fraudulent vocational programs with no earnings returns. The disparities persisted across generations, increasing the white-Black gap in sons' adult-neighborhood outcomes, including a 5-percentage-point (47 percent) widening of the racial college gap. Unequal returns to the same eligibility account for the intergenerational gap, with no contribution from prewar differences in socioeconomic status or geography. In sum, access to the GI Bill was not nearly the same economic opportunity for Black Americans as it was for white Americans, highlighting that race-blind policy does not guarantee racial equality.

中文摘要

二战GI法案是美国历史上规模最大的教育补贴，也是战后美国向知识经济转型的基石。尽管形式上种族中立，但该计划的分散管理将实施权交给地方官员和隔离机构，对黑人和白人退伍军人产生了截然不同的后果。本文利用二战服役资格截止点附近的断点回归，以及1940年和1950年人口普查中退伍军人与其儿子在1990年至2025年间社区结果的新数据链接，量化了GI法案对两代黑人和白人美国人经济结果的影响。GI法案扩大了种族不平等，使白人退伍军人大学完成率翻倍，同时将黑人退伍军人引导至通常具有欺诈性且无收入回报的职业培训项目。这种差异持续到下一代，扩大了白人与黑人在儿子成年社区结果上的差距，包括种族大学差距扩大5个百分点（47%）。相同资格的不平等回报解释了代际差距，而战前社会经济地位或地理差异并无贡献。总之，GI法案为黑人美国人提供的经济机会远不及白人美国人，凸显了种族中立政策并不能保证种族平等。

No. 5NBER w35338

Don’t Give Up on Lab Experiments: Why the Field Still Needs the Lab

不要放弃实验室实验：为什么田野研究仍需实验室

John A. List

Programs：DEV, IO, LE, PE；JEL：C83, C89, C9, C91, C92, C93, H0, J01

Abstract

Recent enthusiasm for field experiments, and especially for natural field experiments (NFEs), in which subjects go about their daily activities unaware that any study is taking place, has sometimes been read as a verdict against the laboratory. I argue that such a verdict is wrong. Through the lens of a simple rational-choice model, I show that the four standard experimental designs (laboratory, artefactual field experiment (AFE), framed field experiment (FFE), and NFE) are comparative-static restrictions of one maximization problem, each identifying a parameter the others cannot. The model reveals that each design type has distinctive strengths and weaknesses across various dimensions of knowledge creation, including the enforcement of the conditions for causal identification, the faithfulness of the experimental environment to the theory being tested, the identification of economic primitives via theoretical structure, and the ethics of studying human subjects. On each of the dimensions, the four design types are complements rather than rivals. Nowhere is this complementarity more evident than between the two extremes. The lab enforces the conditions for causal identification that the NFE must inherit from the market; the NFE recovers the parameter that governs behaviour in the wild, free of the selection, scrutiny, and environmental distortions the lab cannot escape. A research programme using all four designs together demonstrates something no single design can produce. The framework further accommodates the recent rise of online and survey experiments as natural extensions. Our discipline’s recent drift away from laboratory evidence is leaving an important structural gap that natural field experiments, however well conceived, cannot fill.

中文摘要

近期对田野实验，尤其是自然田野实验（NFE）的热情——受试者在日常活动中不知情地参与研究——有时被视为对实验室的否定。我认为这种否定是错误的。通过一个简单理性选择模型的视角，我展示了四种标准实验设计（实验室、人工田野实验AFE、框架田野实验FFE和NFE）是同一最大化问题的比较静态约束，每种设计都能识别其他设计无法识别的参数。该模型揭示了每种设计类型在知识创造的多个维度上具有独特的优势和劣势，包括因果识别条件的执行、实验环境对检验理论的忠实度、通过理论结构识别经济基元，以及研究人类受试者的伦理。在每个维度上，四种设计类型是互补而非竞争关系。这种互补性在两端极端设计之间最为明显。实验室强制执行NFE必须从市场继承的因果识别条件；NFE则恢复了在自然环境中支配行为的参数，避免了实验室无法逃脱的选择、审视和环境扭曲。一个同时使用所有四种设计的研究计划能展示任何单一设计无法产生的结果。该框架还容纳了近期在线和调查实验作为自然扩展的兴起。我们学科近期对实验室证据的偏离留下了一个重要的结构性空白，而自然田野实验无论设计多么精妙都无法填补。

US Monetary Spillovers, Foreign Exchange, and Gold Reserves at Times of Geopolitical Fragmentation

地缘政治碎片化时期的美国货币政策溢出效应、外汇储备与黄金储备

Joshua Aizenman, Jamel Saadaoui, Gazi Salah Uddin, Naoki Yago

Programs：IFM, ME；JEL：E52, F31, F32, F41

Abstract

This paper studies the role of foreign exchange and gold reserves in mitigating the US monetary policy spillovers to exchange rates at times of geopolitical fragmentation and de-dollarization. US dollar reserves mitigate depreciation driven by US monetary tightening, while non-dollar reserves do not. Gold reserves are also associated with smaller exchange-rate responses, though less strongly than dollar reserves, suggesting novel complementarity between dollar and gold reserves. Moreover, the estimated effects of dollar and gold reserves are concentrated in countries without swap and repo lines. These findings are consistent with recent large-scale purchases and sales of gold reserves by emerging economies amid sanctions-related restrictions and geopolitical concerns about access to dollar liquidity. Our results suggest that not only the aggregate volume but also the composition of foreign exchange and gold reserves and access to dollar liquidity facilities are empirically relevant for exchange-rate responses to US monetary shocks. Finally, we exploit cross-country heterogeneity and show that countries with large dollar exposure exhibit smaller exchange-rate responses when dollar and gold reserve holdings are larger.

中文摘要

本文研究在地缘政治碎片化和去美元化时期，外汇储备和黄金储备在缓解美国货币政策对汇率溢出效应中的作用。美元储备缓解了美国货币紧缩导致的贬值，而非美元储备则无此效果。黄金储备也与较小的汇率反应相关，但效果弱于美元储备，表明美元与黄金储备之间存在新颖的互补性。此外，美元和黄金储备的估计效应集中在没有货币互换和回购额度的国家。这些发现与新兴经济体在制裁相关限制和对美元流动性获取的地缘政治担忧背景下大规模买卖黄金储备的行为一致。我们的结果表明，不仅外汇和黄金储备的总量，而且其构成以及美元流动性便利的获取，对汇率对美国货币冲击的反应具有实证相关性。最后，我们利用跨国异质性，显示在美元和黄金储备持有量较大的国家，美元敞口大的国家汇率反应较小。

No. 7NBER w35336

Automation and Repression

自动化与镇压

Daron Acemoglu, A. Arda Gitmez, Mehdi Shadmehr

Programs：EFG, LS, POL；JEL：J23, O33, P10, P16

Abstract

We consider a model of automation embedded in a political environment where workers can undertake a revolt (modeled as a global game), and greater inequality between capital and labor increases the likelihood of a revolt. Decentralized automation decisions raise the share of capital in national income and increase the likelihood of a successful revolt. A capitalist state (representing capital-owners) prefers to regulate the level of automation to lessen the threat of a successful revolt. The capitalist state can also redistribute to workers via the tax system or repress political action, thus creating greater room for further automation. We characterize the trade-off between the regulation of automation, redistribution and repression. \nOur main result is a complementarity between automation and repression. Unless the threat of revolt is quite weak or the capital stock is very low, the capitalist state prefers repression. A higher capital stock in turn encourages more automation and thus more repression. In our full dynamic model with capital accumulation, in the long run the economy tends to repression (again unless the threat of revolt is very weak). We also prove that the same conclusions apply when firms can additionally invest in new labor-intensive tasks. Finally, we show that, starting in a democracy, capital accumulation and thus greater automation encourages the capitalists to support a coup against democracy and set up a repressive system.

中文摘要

我们考虑一个嵌入政治环境的自动化模型，其中工人可以发起反抗（建模为全局博弈），而资本与劳动之间更大的不平等会增加反抗的可能性。分散化的自动化决策提高了国民收入中资本的份额，并增加了成功反抗的可能性。一个代表资本所有者的资本主义国家倾向于调节自动化水平，以减轻成功反抗的威胁。资本主义国家还可以通过税收体系向工人再分配，或镇压政治行动，从而为进一步自动化创造更大空间。我们刻画了自动化调节、再分配与镇压之间的权衡。我们的主要结果是自动化与镇压之间的互补性。除非反抗威胁非常弱或资本存量非常低，否则资本主义国家倾向于镇压。更高的资本存量反过来鼓励更多自动化，从而更多镇压。在我们包含资本积累的完整动态模型中，长期经济趋向镇压（同样除非反抗威胁非常弱）。我们还证明，当企业可以额外投资于新的劳动密集型任务时，同样的结论成立。最后，我们表明，从民主制度出发，资本积累和由此带来的更大自动化会鼓励资本家支持推翻民主的政变，并建立镇压体系。

Monopsony Power and the Transmission of Monetary Policy

买方垄断势力与货币政策的传导

Bence Bardóczy, Gideon Bornstein, Sergio Salgado

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E0, E31, E52, J42, L13

Abstract

This paper studies how labor market power affects the transmission of monetary policy. Using administrative U.S. Census data, we show that firms with high monopsony power—defined as those accounting for over 10 percent of the local wage bill—respond less to monetary policy in terms of their wage bill and employment. We then develop a New Keynesian model with heterogeneous firms and oligopsonistic competition to interpret these findings. Wage stickiness combined with firms’ labor market power is key to generating the heterogeneous responses that we document. Our model highlights two channels through which oligopsony shapes the aggregate effects of monetary policy: partial passthrough and misallocation. Calibrated to U.S. labor markets, the model implies that the decline in labor market power since the 1980s has increased the output response to monetary policy by about 10 percent and accounts for about 15 percent of the estimated flattening of the Phillips curve.

中文摘要

本文研究劳动力市场势力如何影响货币政策的传导。利用美国人口普查局的行政数据，我们发现，具有高买方垄断势力（定义为占当地工资总额超过10%）的企业，其工资总额和就业对货币政策的反应较弱。随后，我们构建了一个包含异质性企业和寡头竞争的新凯恩斯主义模型来解释这些发现。工资粘性与企业劳动力市场势力的结合，是产生我们所记录的非对称反应的关键。我们的模型强调了寡头垄断通过两个渠道影响货币政策的总体效应：不完全传导和资源错配。根据美国劳动力市场校准，该模型表明，自20世纪80年代以来劳动力市场势力的下降，使产出对货币政策的反应增加了约10%，并解释了估计的菲利普斯曲线平坦化中约15%的部分。

No. 9NBER w35334

From Trade War to Green Transition: Optimal Electric Vehicle Tariffs with Revenue-Funded Subsidies

从贸易战到绿色转型：以收入补贴的最优电动汽车关税

Panle Jia Barwick, Jack Collison, Pinelopi K. Goldberg, Shanjun Li, Yucheng Wang

Programs：EEE, IO, ITI, PE；JEL：F13, F14, H23, L52, Q58

Abstract

We study the optimal design of trade and industrial policy when governments pursue environmental objectives alongside traditional welfare goals. Motivated by the global transition to electric vehicles (EVs) and growing concerns about competitiveness, energy security, and climate change, we develop a framework in which policymakers choose tariffs and domestic production subsidies to maximize welfare, defined as the sum of consumer surplus, domestic profits, environmental benefits, and tariff revenue. We combine a theoretical model of differentiated-product oligopoly with a structural demand model estimated using vehicle-level data from 13 countries that together account for the vast majority of global EV sales. Our central finding is that the optimal policy combines a moderate tariff on imported EVs with a subsidy to domestic EV production financed through tariff revenue. This policy substantially outperforms both outright protectionism and laissez-faire. Relative to current policies, it preserves consumer access to affordable EVs, accelerates fleet electrification, supports domestic producers, and remains budget-neutral. For the United States, the optimal policy more than doubles EV market share, generates over $45 billion in annual welfare gains, and avoids approximately 95 million tons of lifetime CO2 emissions. A key mechanism underlying these results is the pass-through of tariffs and subsidies to prices, which depends critically on demand curvature, product substitution, and market structure. More broadly, our results suggest that effective industrial policy requires careful attention to market structure and country-specific conditions, balancing consumer, producer, fiscal, and environmental objectives rather than adhering to ideological prescriptions.

Data-Driven Automation

数据驱动的自动化

Maryam Farboodi, Andrew J. Koh, Anchi Xia

Programs：EFG；JEL：J31, O33, O4

Abstract

We build a dynamic model of data-driven automation in which data (i) is heterogeneous and task-specific; (ii) accumulates endogenously as a byproduct of economic activity; and (iii) exhibits spillovers such that data generated by one task can augment the productivity of another. Along the transition path of automation, data plays a dual role in simultaneously augmenting the productivity of already-automated tasks and expanding the automation frontier. We derive tight conditions for the economy to be partially versus fully automated in the long-run. In the latter case, automation exhibits rich short-run dynamics that depend on the pattern of data spillovers but is always slow in the long-run: the share of tasks produced by labor decays asymptotically as a power law in time. We show that the economy is generically inefficient and analyze how a planner optimally tilts the direction of data accumulation. With endogenous capital accumulation, data-driven automation generates explosive growth but stagnant long-run wages.

Abstract

We study labor-market polarization by developing a framework with heterogeneous consumers, non-homothetic preferences, and endogenous labor supply a la Roy (1951). Because income-elastic sectors are intensive in high- and low-skilled occupations, shocks that raise either the average or the dispersion of income across households shift relative labor demand toward these occupations, generating an income channel to polarization. The income channel is sizable: it explains 42% and 210% of the increases in the relative wages of high- and low-skill workers observed between 1980 and 2016, and 26% and 51% of the increase in their hours-worked shares.
