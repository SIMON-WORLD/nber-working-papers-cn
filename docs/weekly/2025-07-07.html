No. 1NBER w33998

AI and the Fed

人工智能与美联储

Sophia Kazinnik, Erik Brynjolfsson

Programs：EFG, ME, PE, PR；JEL：C8, C9, G4

Abstract

This paper examines how central banks can strategically integrate artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance their operations. Using a dual-framework approach, we demonstrate how AI can transform both strategic decision-making and daily operations within central banks, taking the Federal Reserve System (FRS) as a representative example. We first consider a top-down view, showing how AI can modernize key central banking functions. We then adopt a bottom-up approach focusing on the impact of generative AI on specific tasks and occupations within the Federal Reserve and find a significant potential for workforce augmentation and efficiency gains. We also address critical challenges associated with AI adoption, such as the need to upgrade data infrastructure and manage workforce transitions.

中文摘要

本文研究了中央银行如何战略性地整合人工智能（AI）以增强其运营。通过采用双重框架方法，我们以美联储系统（FRS）为代表，展示了AI如何改变中央银行内部的战略决策和日常运营。我们首先从自上而下的视角出发，展示了AI如何现代化关键中央银行职能。然后，我们采用自下而上的方法，聚焦于生成式AI对美联储内部特定任务和职业的影响，并发现其在劳动力增强和效率提升方面具有显著潜力。我们还讨论了与AI采用相关的关键挑战，例如升级数据基础设施和管理劳动力转型的必要性。

No. 2NBER w33997

Transitory and Permanent Import Tariff Shocks in the United States: An Empirical Investigation

美国暂时性与永久性进口关税冲击：一项实证研究

Stephanie Schmitt-Grohé, Martín Uribe

Programs：EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E31, E52, F13, F41

Abstract

We estimate transitory and permanent import tariff shocks in the United States over the postwar period. We find that transitory tariff increases are neither inflationary nor contractionary, and are not associated with monetary tightening. In contrast, permanent tariff increases trigger a temporary rise in inflation (a one-off increase in the price level) and a brief tightening of monetary policy. Consistent with the intertemporal approach to the balance of payments, transitory tariff increases reduce imports and improve the trade balance, whereas permanent increases leave both largely unchanged. Transitory shocks account for approximately 80 percent of tariff movements. Overall, tariff shocks are estimated to be a minor driver of U.S. business cycle fluctuations on average and even during episodes of substantial tariff hikes, such as Nixon 1971, Ford 1975, and Trump 2018.

中文摘要

我们估计了战后时期美国的暂时性和永久性进口关税冲击。我们发现，暂时性关税增加既不会引发通胀，也不会导致经济收缩，并且与货币紧缩无关。相比之下，永久性关税增加会引发通胀的暂时上升（价格水平的一次性上涨）和货币政策的短暂收紧。与国际收支跨期方法一致，暂时性关税增加会减少进口并改善贸易平衡，而永久性关税增加则使两者基本保持不变。暂时性冲击约占关税变动的80%。总体而言，估计关税冲击平均而言是美国商业周期波动的次要驱动因素，即使在尼克松1971年、福特1975年和特朗普2018年等大幅加征关税时期也是如此。

No. 3NBER w33996

Person and Place Effects in Scientific Discovery

科学发现中的人与机构效应

Amitabh Chandra, Connie Xu

Programs：PR；JEL：H5, I2, O3

Abstract

How much of a scientist’s research output is attributable to individual talent, and how much to the institution where they work? Because markets systematically under-provide fundamental science, the answer matters for how societies allocate the resources that substitute for missing market incentives, how the scientific community evaluates researchers for promotion and grants, and whether the current distribution of scientists across institutions maximizes knowledge production. We study this question using citation-weighted publications in leading life-science journals and a movers design that exploits variation in the output of nearly 40,000 scientists who change institutions. We find that 50 to 60 percent of the variation in a scientist’s research output is attributable to institutional factors. After correcting for limited mobility bias, we document positive assortative matching between scientists and institutions (a correlation of 0.3 between person and place effects), a necessary condition for maximizing aggregate knowledge production under the multiplicative structure of our model. Around two-thirds of the institutional effect is explained by the presence of star researchers. A scientist’s output responds to both the total output of their institution and its funding level, but not to the size of the local research cluster, suggesting that institutional effects operate through the human capital environment within an institution rather than through agglomeration or research funding alone. The symmetry of effects for upward and downward movers implies that institutional advantages do not permanently transfer to scientists who leave. These effects have not diminished despite technologies facilitating remote collaboration, are robust to detailed field-by-year controls, and raise the possibility that similar patterns extend to other areas of fundamental science.

中文摘要

科学家的研究成果有多少归因于个人才能，又有多少归因于其所在机构？由于市场系统性地对基础科学投入不足，这个问题的答案对于社会如何分配资源以替代缺失的市场激励、科学界如何评估研究人员的晋升和资助、以及当前科学家在机构间的分布是否最大化知识生产至关重要。我们利用在顶级生命科学期刊上发表的引用加权出版物，以及一个利用近40,000名更换机构的科学家产出变化的研究者流动设计来研究这个问题。我们发现，科学家研究成果变异的50%到60%可归因于机构因素。在纠正有限流动性偏差后，我们记录了科学家与机构之间的正向分类匹配（人与机构效应之间的相关性为0.3），这是在模型乘法结构下最大化总知识生产的必要条件。大约三分之二的机构效应可由明星研究者的存在来解释。科学家的产出对其所在机构的总产出和资助水平均有反应，但对本地研究集群的规模没有反应，这表明机构效应是通过机构内的人力资本环境而非单纯的集聚或研究资助来运作的。向上和向下流动者效应的对称性意味着，机构优势不会永久性地转移给离开的科学家。尽管促进远程协作的技术已经存在，但这些效应并未减弱，并且在加入详细的领域-年份控制后依然稳健，这提高了类似模式可能扩展到基础科学其他领域的可能性。

No. 4NBER w33995

The Revenue and Distributional Impacts of Unemployment Insurance Reform: Evidence from California

失业保险改革的收入与分配影响：来自加利福尼亚州的证据

Mark Duggan, Jonathan Gruber, Audrey Guo

Programs：CF, EFG, LS, PE；JEL：H25, J38, J65

Abstract

In the United States, unemployment insurance (UI) is funded through employer-side payroll taxes that are experience-rated based on previous UI claims. States differ significantly with respect to the financing of their programs, and a majority of state programs do not currently meet minimum UI trust fund solvency standards. A common culprit in the least solvent states is a very low tax base of earnings on which UI taxes are levied. We focus on California, the least solvent of the 50 state UI programs, with debt currently to the federal government of $21 billion, and which has the lowest base of taxable earnings at $7000 per year. We use matched employer-employee administrative data to estimate the impact of financing reforms to California’s UI system. We find that raising the taxable earnings base would replenish the state’s UI trust fund and would increase experience rating by reducing the number of systematically subsidized firms. While this improves vertical equity of the UI system, it would also worsen horizontal equity by imposing much larger percentage increases in tax costs on the firms with the fewest layoffs. Alternatively, matching the higher tax base with both higher maximum and lower minimum rates could improve both experience rating and horizontal equity.

中文摘要

在美国，失业保险（UI）通过雇主方工资税来资助，该税根据以往的UI索赔进行经验评级。各州在项目融资方面存在显著差异，大多数州的项目目前未达到最低UI信托基金偿付能力标准。在偿付能力最差的州中，一个常见原因是征收UI税的工资税基非常低。我们聚焦于加利福尼亚州，它是50个州UI项目中偿付能力最差的，目前欠联邦政府210亿美元债务，并且其应税工资基数最低，为每年7000美元。我们使用匹配的雇主-雇员行政数据来估计加州UI系统融资改革的影响。我们发现，提高应税工资基数将补充该州的UI信托基金，并通过减少系统性地获得补贴的企业数量来增强经验评级。虽然这改善了UI系统的纵向公平性，但也会通过向裁员最少的公司施加更大比例的税收成本增加，从而恶化横向公平性。或者，将更高的税基与更高的最高税率和更低的最低税率相匹配，可以同时改善经验评级和横向公平性。

No. 5NBER w33994

Health Spillovers: The Broad Impact of Spousal Health Shocks

健康溢出效应：配偶健康冲击的广泛影响

Carolina Arteaga, Natalia Vigezzi, Pilar García-Gómez

Programs：EH；JEL：I12

Abstract

In this paper we provide new evidence on the health spillover effects of health shocks within couples. Using administrative data from the Netherlands and a matching event-study framework, we estimate the causal effect of experiencing a health shock within a couple on the health of the initially unaffected partner. Our findings reveal a significant deterioration in the partner’s health outlook, characterized by substantial increases in hospital visits, overnight stays, and mortality. The health decline is broad in scope, encompassing higher risk of infections, accidents, and digestive and cardiovascular conditions. This deterioration is accompanied by substantial increases in stress, anxiety and depression for both men and women, as well as sleep disorders for women. These effects are not driven by a heavy caregiving load, financial distress or worsening of health behaviors. On the contrary, the adverse outcomes persist despite suggestive positive changes, including increased exercise for both men and women, and reduced alcohol consumption among women.

中文摘要

在本文中，我们提供了关于夫妻内部健康冲击的健康溢出效应的新证据。利用来自荷兰的行政数据和匹配事件研究框架，我们估计了夫妻中一方经历健康冲击对最初未受影响伴侣健康的因果效应。我们的研究结果显示，伴侣的健康前景显著恶化，表现为住院次数、住院过夜次数和死亡率的大幅增加。健康恶化的范围很广，包括感染、事故、消化系统和心血管疾病风险的增加。这种恶化伴随着男性和女性压力、焦虑和抑郁的显著增加，以及女性睡眠障碍的增加。这些效应并非由沉重的照护负担、财务困境或健康行为恶化所驱动。相反，尽管出现了积极的改变迹象，包括男性和女性运动增加以及女性饮酒减少，但不良后果仍然持续存在。

No. 6NBER w33993

Technifying Ventures

技术化创业企业

Yoshiki Ando, Emin Dinlersoz, Jeremy Greenwood, Ruben Piazzesi

Programs：CF, EFG, PR；JEL：E13, G24, O30, O40

Abstract

What is the relationship between advanced technology adoption, venture capital (VC) funding, employment, and growth? Using US Census Bureau microdata, the analysis shows both factors are independently and positively associated with firm outcomes, but their combination matters most. VC backing is concentrated among a small group of firms that experience large employment gains post-funding. In contrast, advanced technology is adopted by a broader range of firms, which see smaller post-adoption employment increases. A startup model is calibrated with firm-level data to study reallocation effects associated with tax and subsidy changes, and quantify the economic roles of VC and advanced technology.

中文摘要

先进技术采用、风险投资（VC）融资、就业和增长之间的关系是什么？利用美国人口普查局的微观数据，分析表明这两个因素都与公司结果独立且正相关，但它们的组合最为重要。风险投资支持集中在少数公司，这些公司在获得融资后经历了大规模的就业增长。相比之下，先进技术被更广泛的公司采用，这些公司在采用后就业增长较小。我们利用企业层面数据校准了一个初创企业模型，以研究与税收和补贴变化相关的重新分配效应，并量化风险投资和先进技术的经济作用。

No. 7NBER w33992

Extending Healthspans in an Aging World

在老龄化世界中延长健康寿命

Stephen P. Utkus, Olivia S. Mitchell

Programs：AG, LS；JEL：I12, I24, J11, J14, J16

Abstract

Extensions in human longevity are prompting a growing interest in maximizing healthspan, or the number of years of life unencumbered by the chronic diseases of old age. This chapter reviews recent research on healthy life extension, including several measures and determinants of longer healthspans. We also provide an overview of recent efforts by medical and business enterprises to enhance longevity and healthspan, followed by a discussion of policy and workplace options to foster healthier lives. Such efforts hold the promise of improving quality of life, expanding labor supply, and lowering the cost of health care costs associated with population aging.

中文摘要

人类寿命的延长正促使人们越来越关注最大化健康寿命，即不受老年慢性病困扰的生命年数。本章回顾了关于健康寿命延长的近期研究，包括衡量更长健康寿命的几种方法和决定因素。我们还概述了医疗和商业企业为延长寿命和健康寿命所做的近期努力，随后讨论了促进更健康生活的政策和工作场所选择。这些努力有望改善生活质量、扩大劳动力供给，并降低与人口老龄化相关的医疗保健成本。

No. 8NBER w33991

The Real Costs of Washing Away Corruption: Evidence from Brazil’s Lava Jato Investigation

清洗腐败的真实代价：来自巴西“洗车行动”调查的证据

Claudio Ferraz, Luiz C. Moura, Lars Norden, Ricardo Schechtman

Programs：CF, DEV, LE, LS, POL；JEL：D73, G21, G30, K42

Abstract

We investigate the economic impacts of one of the world's largest anti-corruption crackdowns, Brazil's Operação Lava Jato (Car Wash). Using a difference-in-differences analysis of unique, matched firm-bank data, we find sizable declines in employment and wage bills for investigated firms, alongside reduced access to credit. We also find adverse indirect effects through the bank credit channel, where highly exposed banks reduce credit to non-investigated firms, particularly for politically connected existing borrowers. Non-investigated firms exposed to the economic shock through their bank relationships suffer declines in wage bills and employment. The findings highlight the intricate economic implications of anti-corruption policies targeting large corporations.

中文摘要

我们研究了全球最大规模的反腐行动之一——巴西的“洗车行动”（Operação Lava Jato）的经济影响。利用独特的匹配企业-银行数据进行双重差分分析，我们发现受调查企业的就业和工资总额显著下降，同时信贷获取渠道减少。我们还通过银行信贷渠道发现了不利的间接效应：风险敞口较大的银行减少了对非调查企业的信贷，尤其是对有政治关联的现有借款人的信贷。通过银行关系暴露于经济冲击的非调查企业，其工资总额和就业也出现下降。这些发现凸显了针对大型企业的反腐政策所蕴含的复杂经济影响。

No. 9NBER w33990

Effects of the Minimum Wage on Employment of Young Adults with Cognitive Disabilities

最低工资对认知障碍青年就业的影响

Barry Chiswick, Hope Corman, Dhaval M. Dave, Nancy Reichman

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：J02, J14

Abstract

We estimate the effects of increases in the minimum wage on labor market outcomes of working-age adults with cognitive disabilities, a vulnerable and low-skilled group, using data from the American Community Survey (2008-2023) and a generalized difference-in-differences research design. We found that a higher effective minimum wage reduces employment and labor force participation but has no effect on labor supply at the intensive margin for this group. Effects were stronger for those with lower educational attainment. We found no significant labor market effects of an increase in the minimum wage for individuals with physical disabilities or in the non-disabled population.

中文摘要

我们利用美国社区调查（2008-2023年）的数据和广义双重差分研究设计，估计了最低工资上涨对患有认知障碍的劳动年龄成年人（一个脆弱且低技能的群体）劳动力市场结果的影响。我们发现，更高的有效最低工资降低了该群体的就业率和劳动力参与率，但对集约边际上的劳动力供给没有影响。对于受教育程度较低的人群，这种影响更为强烈。我们发现，最低工资上涨对肢体残疾人群或非残疾人群没有显著的劳动力市场影响。

No. 10NBER w33989

Why Is Fertility So Low in High Income Countries?

为什么高收入国家的生育率如此之低？

Melissa Schettini Kearney, Phillip B. Levine

Programs：CH, LS；JEL：I12, J13

Abstract

We consider why fertility has fallen in recent decades in almost all high-income countries. We begin by documenting declining total fertility and rising childlessness across cohorts, highlighting the need to focus on cohort versus period-specific fertility rates. With this motivation, we propose a conceptual model of fertility determination that augments the standard Becker model with an explicit role for social norms and cohort-specific contextual factors, including broad social and economic influences and an expanded set of consumption and lifestyle options. We posit that these forces have led to “shifting priorities,” reducing the centrality of parenthood. We then review existing empirical evidence –and conclude that the decline in fertility likely reflects a complex mix of changing norms around work, parenting, gender roles, and leisure consistent with our cohort-based conceptual framework. We conclude with suggestions for future research and a brief discussion of policy implications.

中文摘要

我们探讨了近几十年来几乎所有高收入国家生育率下降的原因。我们首先记录了各队列中总生育率下降和终身不育率上升的情况，强调了关注队列特定生育率而非时期特定生育率的必要性。基于此动机，我们提出了一个生育率决定的概念模型，该模型在标准贝克尔模型的基础上，增加了社会规范和队列特定背景因素（包括广泛的社会经济影响以及更多样化的消费和生活方式选择）的明确作用。我们假设这些力量导致了“优先级的转变”，降低了为人父母的核心地位。然后，我们回顾了现有的实证证据，并得出结论：生育率下降可能反映了围绕工作、育儿、性别角色和休闲的规范变化这一复杂组合，这与我们基于队列的概念框架是一致的。最后，我们提出了未来研究的建议，并简要讨论了政策含义。

No. 11NBER w33988

Doctors as Gatekeepers in Social Insurance: Evidence from Workers’ Compensation Insurance

医生作为社会保险的守门人：来自工伤保险的证据

Marika Cabral, Marcus Dillender

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：H0, I11, J0

Abstract

In many social insurance programs, eligibility for disability benefits depends on medical evaluations performed by doctors. This paper studies the role of doctors as gatekeepers in workers’ compensation insurance and quantifies the discretion they exercise in medical evaluations of physical impairments. Using comprehensive administrative data and random assignment of doctors to independent medical examinations, we identify substantial systematic variation in evaluation decisions across doctors and estimate its consequences for claimant outcomes and program costs. Being evaluated by a one standard-deviation more generous doctor increases subsequent cash benefits by about 20%, compensated time out of work by 20%, injury-related medical spending by 12%, and total workers’ compensation costs by 17%. Doctor effects vary meaningfully with observable characteristics. We further examine how discretion interacts with institutional rules and economic incentives when claimants can select their own doctors. More generous doctors attract more claimants, while insurers are more likely to dispute their evaluations, indicating that both parties influence the allocation of gatekeepers in line with their incentives and thereby shape the distribution of program benefits. Finally, we show that alternative evaluator assignment rules can substantially affect claimant outcomes and program costs, underscoring the importance of policies governing the allocation and oversight of gatekeepers in the design of workers’ compensation and other disability-related social insurance programs.

中文摘要

在许多社会保险项目中，伤残津贴的资格取决于医生进行的医学评估。本文研究了医生在工伤保险中作为守门人的作用，并量化了他们在身体损伤医学评估中行使的自由裁量权。利用全面的行政数据和医生对独立医学检查的随机分配，我们识别出不同医生在评估决策上存在显著的系统性差异，并估计了其对索赔人结果和项目成本的影响。由慷慨程度高一个标准差的医生进行评估，会使后续现金津贴增加约20%，带薪休假时间增加20%，与工伤相关的医疗支出增加12%，工伤保险总成本增加17%。医生的影响随其可观测特征而有显著变化。我们进一步考察了当索赔人可以选择自己的医生时，自由裁量权如何与制度规则和经济激励相互作用。更慷慨的医生会吸引更多索赔人，而保险公司则更有可能对其评估提出异议，这表明双方都根据自身激励影响守门人的分配，从而塑造了项目福利的分配。最后，我们表明，替代性的评估者分配规则会显著影响索赔人结果和项目成本，这凸显了在设计工伤保险及其他与伤残相关的社会保险项目时，管理守门人分配和监督的政策的重要性。

No. 12NBER w33987

Due Diligence and the Allocation of Venture Capital

尽职调查与风险资本的配置

Xiaoyong (Jack) Fu, Lucian A. Taylor

Programs：CF；JEL：E22, G11, G24

Abstract

How do investors choose the intensity of their due diligence, and how does that choice affect investment outcomes? Using cell phone signal data, we measure the duration of pre-investment meetings between venture capitalists (VCs) and startup employees. This measure captures one important component of VC due diligence. Less due diligence is associated with hotter deals and markets, busier investors, and greater distance, consistent with a theory of costly learning. Also consistent with that theory, less due diligence is associated with more volatile investment performance, as VCs allocate capital under greater uncertainty. Overall, VCs appear to trade off the costs of due diligence with its improvements to capital allocation.

中文摘要

投资者如何选择其尽职调查的强度，以及这种选择如何影响投资结果？利用手机信号数据，我们衡量了风险投资家（VC）与初创企业员工在投资前会面的持续时间。这一指标捕捉了风险投资家尽职调查的一个重要组成部分。较少的尽职调查与更热门的交易和市场、更忙碌的投资者以及更大的地理距离相关，这与高成本学习的理论一致。同样与该理论一致的是，较少的尽职调查与更不稳定的投资业绩相关，因为风险投资家在更大的不确定性下配置资本。总体而言，风险投资家似乎在尽职调查的成本与其对资本配置的改进之间进行权衡。

Do Pensions Enhance Teacher Effort and Selective Retention?

养老金能否提升教师努力程度并实现选择性留任？

Michael D. Bates, Andrew C. Johnston

Programs：ED, LS, PE；JEL：H55, I21, J33, J45, M52

Abstract

Theoretical rationales for employer-provided pensions often focus on their ability to increase employee effort and selectively retain quality workers. We test these hypotheses using rich administrative data on public school teachers around the pension-eligibility threshold. When teachers cross the threshold, their effective compensation drops by over 50 percent of salary due to sharply reduced pension accrual rates. Standard economic models predict this compensation reduction should decrease teacher effort and output, yet we observe no such decline. This suggests that yearly pension accruals near retirement do not meaningfully increase effort. Similarly, if pensions selectively retained better teachers, we would expect average teacher quality to decline when the retentive incentive disappears at the threshold. Instead, we find no change in the composition of teacher quality, suggesting pensions do not selectively retain higher-performing workers in late career.

中文摘要

雇主提供养老金的理论依据通常侧重于其提高员工努力程度和选择性留住高质量员工的能力。我们利用公立学校教师在养老金资格门槛附近的丰富行政数据来检验这些假设。当教师跨过这一门槛时，由于养老金累积率急剧下降，其有效薪酬会下降超过工资的50%。标准经济模型预测，这种薪酬减少应会降低教师的努力程度和产出，然而我们并未观察到这种下降。这表明，临近退休时每年的养老金累积并未显著提高努力程度。同样，如果养老金能选择性留住更好的教师，那么当这一留任激励在门槛处消失时，我们应预期教师平均质量会下降。相反，我们发现教师质量的构成没有变化，这表明养老金在职业生涯后期并未选择性留住表现更优的员工。

What Do Market-Access Subsidies Do? Experimental Evidence from Tunisia

市场准入补贴有何作用？来自突尼斯的实验证据

Nadia Ali, Giacomo De Giorgi, Aminur Rahman, Eric Verhoogen

Programs：DEV, ITI；JEL：F14, O14

Abstract

Many countries seek to promote exports by subsidizing market access, but evidence on such efforts has been mixed. We present the first randomized evaluation of a government financial-support program explicitly targeting exports, the Tasdir+ program in Tunisia. The program offered matching grants for fixed market-access costs but not variable costs. Tracking outcomes in administrative data, we find positive effects on exports on average. We find limited impacts on the number of destinations or exported products, which were stated policy targets. The finding that the fixed-cost subsidies expanded exports on the intensive margin but not the extensive margins of destinations or products stands in contrast to the predictions of several workhorse trade models.

中文摘要

许多国家试图通过补贴市场准入来促进出口，但关于此类努力的证据好坏参半。我们首次对明确针对出口的政府财政支持项目——突尼斯的Tasdir+项目——进行了随机评估。该项目为固定市场准入成本（而非可变成本）提供配套赠款。通过追踪行政数据中的结果，我们发现平均而言对出口有积极影响。我们发现对目标目的地数量或出口产品数量的影响有限，而这两者都是既定的政策目标。固定成本补贴在集约边际上扩大了出口，但在目的地或产品的广延边际上没有效果，这一发现与几个主流贸易模型的预测形成对比。

Out-of-School Learning: Subtitling vs. Dubbing and the Acquisition of Foreign-Language Skills

校外学习：字幕配音与外语技能的习得

Frauke Baumeister, Eric A. Hanushek, Ludger Woessmann

Programs：CH, ED, LS, PE；JEL：H0, I2, J24

Abstract

The development of English-language skills, a near necessity in today’s global economy, is heavily influenced by historical national decisions about whether to subtitle or dub TV content. While prior studies of language acquisition have focused on schools, we show the overwhelming influence of out-of-school learning. We identify the causal effect of subtitling in a difference-in-differences specification that compares English to math skills in European countries that do and do not use subtitles. We find a large positive effect of subtitling on English-language skills of over one standard deviation. The effect is robust to accounting for linguistic similarity, economic incentives to learn English, and cultural protectiveness. Consistent with oral TV transmission, the effect is larger for listening and speaking skills than for reading.

中文摘要

英语技能的发展在当今全球经济中几乎是必需的，它深受历史上各国关于电视内容采用字幕还是配音的国家决策的影响。虽然以往的语言习得研究侧重于学校，但我们展示了校外学习的压倒性影响。我们在一个双重差分设定中识别了字幕的因果效应，该设定比较了使用和不使用字幕的欧洲国家中英语与数学技能。我们发现字幕对英语技能有超过一个标准差的巨大正向影响。在考虑了语言相似性、学习英语的经济动机以及文化保护性之后，这一结果依然稳健。与电视的口语传播特性一致，这种效应对听力和口语技能的影响大于对阅读技能的影响。

Basel Endgame: Bank Capital Requirements and the Future of International Standard Setting

巴塞尔终局：银行资本要求与国际标准制定的未来

Stephen G. Cecchetti, Jeremy C. Kress, Kermit L. Schoenholtz

Programs：ME；JEL：G21, G28

Abstract

In 2023, US regulators proposed the “Basel Endgame,” a long-awaited overhaul of bank capital requirements. The proposal aimed to bring the United States into compliance with international standards established by the Basel Committee on Banking Supervision in response to the 2008 Global Financial Crisis. However, fierce industry opposition to what banks viewed as a costly increase in capital requirements effectively killed the proposal. In this essay, we describe the purpose of bank capital and the history of international standard-setting in bank regulation. We then highlight the most important aspects of the Basel Endgame, as well as the arguments for and against adopting the rule. We show that the debate unnecessarily conflated two distinct questions: (1) whether the United States should comply with international regulatory standards, and (2) whether the United States should raise large banks’ capital requirements. While there are strong grounds to answer both questions in the affirmative, they need not be addressed together. That is, the United States can implement international standards in a capital-neutral manner to preserve global cooperation in bank regulation, leaving the separate question of raising capital requirements for another day.

中文摘要

2023年，美国监管机构提出了“巴塞尔终局”，这是一项期待已久的银行资本要求改革。该提案旨在使美国符合巴塞尔银行监管委员会为应对2008年全球金融危机而制定的国际标准。然而，银行业对银行视为成本高昂的资本要求增加进行了激烈反对，这实际上扼杀了该提案。在本文中，我们描述了银行资本的目的以及银行监管中国际标准制定的历史。然后，我们强调了巴塞尔终局最重要的方面，以及支持和反对采纳该规则的论点。我们表明，这场辩论不必要地将两个不同的问题混为一谈：（1）美国是否应遵守国际监管标准，以及（2）美国是否应提高大型银行的资本要求。虽然这两个问题都有充分理由得到肯定回答，但它们不必同时处理。也就是说，美国可以以资本中性的方式实施国际标准，以维护银行监管中的全球合作，而将提高资本要求的单独问题留待日后解决。

Lab Experiments in Developing Country Contexts

发展中国家背景下的实验室实验

Sara Lowes, Nathan Nunn

Programs：DEV, POL；JEL：C9, N01, N10, Z1

Abstract

Lab-in-the-field experiments, in which lab experiments are conducted in more naturalistic settings, are increasingly being implemented in developing country contexts. In this chapter, we outline the conceptual and logistical challenges typically associated with lab-in-the-field experiments in non-Western settings. We describe the importance of worldviews and how researcher preconceptions may inadvertently shape the types of research questions that are asked. We emphasize the importance of increasing diversity of subject pools and researchers. We also suggest a set of best practices when implementing lab-in-the-field experiments in developing countries.
