Empowering Inclusive Work

赋能包容性工作

Yanyou Chen, Mitchell Hoffman, Huilan Xu, Zhe Yuan

Programs：EH, IO, LS, PE, PR；JEL：J14, M50

Abstract

Can AI improve workplace outcomes for workers with disabilities? We examine the relative performance of deaf or hard of hearing (DHH) workers on one of China's largest food-delivery platforms. Pre-AI, DHH workers are slower than non-disabled workers and have worse customer ratings, although they supply more hours to the platform and are less likely to quit. Midway through our data, the platform suddenly introduces an AI-based intelligent outbound calling system designed to improve customer communication for DHH workers. Using a difference-in-differences design comparing DHH and non-disabled workers before and after the AI tool, we find that AI increases the speed and productivity of DHH workers, especially on tasks involving customer interaction; substantially reduces negative customer ratings; and increases labor supply and retention. AI eliminates one-third of the disability hourly pay gap and significantly increases the profitability of DHH workers for the platform.

中文摘要

人工智能能否改善残疾劳动者的职场表现？我们以中国最大外卖平台之一为场景，比较听障劳动者与非残疾劳动者的相对绩效。在引入AI前，听障劳动者配送速度较慢且客户评分较低，但工作时长更长、离职率更低。在数据中期，平台突然引入基于AI的智能外呼系统以改善听障劳动者的客户沟通。采用双重差分法比较AI工具引入前后听障与非残疾劳动者的差异，我们发现：AI提升了听障劳动者的速度与生产率，尤其在涉及客户互动的任务中；显著降低了负面客户评分；并增加了劳动供给与留任率。AI消除了三分之一的残疾时薪差距，并显著提升了听障劳动者为平台创造的利润。

No. 2NBER w35371

Climate and Prehistoric Migration

气候与史前迁徙

Peter Huybers, Marco Tabellini, Charles A. Taylor, Francesco Toti

Programs：DAE, EEE；JEL：N01, N50, O13, Q54, Z13

Abstract

What factors drove human migration before modern states, markets, and borders? We develop a sorting framework in which climate-specific subsistence knowledge depreciates with ecological distance. To test this, we use ancient DNA identity-by-descent segments to construct bilateral migration flows across Western Eurasia over the last 10,000 years. We document three main findings. First, migration flows decline with differences in growing degree days, precipitation, and soil characteristics between origins and destinations. Second, the binding factor varies across subsistence systems: farmers exhibit strong thermal and soil matching, while pastoralists match most strongly on precipitation. Third, periods of warming increase farmer expansion while cooling increases pastoral expansion in patterns that recover known archaeological migration episodes. Migration also acts as a margin of climate adaptation: populations exposed to temperature change move to destinations that partly offset the shift.

中文摘要

在现代国家、市场与边界形成之前，哪些因素驱动了人类迁徙？我们构建了一个分类框架，其中气候特定的生存知识随生态距离而贬值。为验证这一框架，我们利用古DNA的同源片段数据，构建了过去一万年间欧亚大陆西部的双边迁徙流。主要发现有三：第一，迁徙流随原住地与目的地之间生长度日、降水量和土壤特征的差异而递减；第二，约束因素因生存体系而异：农耕者表现出强烈的热量与土壤匹配，而游牧者则在降水量上匹配最强；第三，气候变暖期增加农耕者扩张，而变冷期增加游牧者扩张，其模式可复现已知的考古迁徙事件。迁徙还充当气候适应的调节机制：暴露于温度变化的人口会迁往能部分抵消温度偏移的目的地。

Empathy, Social Networks, and Academic Achievement: Experimental Evidence on Cross-Productivity in Skill Formation

共情、社交网络与学业成就：技能形成中交叉生产率的实验证据

Flavio Cunha, Qinyou Hu, Yiming Xia, Naibao Zhao

Programs：CH；JEL：C93, I21, J24

Abstract

We evaluate a parent-directed empathy development program for middle school students in China using a randomized trial with 2,246 students. The four-month mobile app intervention produced short-run gains in empathy and reduced bullying but no immediate academic effects. Over the longer term, treatment students were 3.2 percentage points more likely to enter elite high schools and 2.5 percentage points less likely to miss the entrance exam. We trace these gains to improved self-beliefs and a restructuring of classroom friendship networks that reduced academic homophily, revealing a social channel of cross-productivity: non-cognitive investments can reshape peer relationships to improve academic outcomes.

中文摘要

我们通过一项包含2246名中学生的随机实验，评估了一项面向家长的共情发展项目。为期四个月的手机应用干预在短期内提升了共情能力并减少了欺凌行为，但未产生即时学业效应。长期来看，实验组学生进入精英高中的概率提高3.2个百分点，错过入学考试的概率降低2.5个百分点。我们将这些收益归因于自我信念的改善以及课堂友谊网络的重构——后者减少了学业同质性，揭示了交叉生产率的社会渠道：非认知投资可通过重塑同伴关系来改善学业成果。

No. 4NBER w35369

The Search Costs of Inflation in the Labor Market

劳动力市场中通货膨胀的搜寻成本

Laura Pilossoph, Jane M. Ryngaert, Jesse J. Wedewer

Programs：EFG；JEL：E0, E24, E31, J63

Abstract

This paper studies the effect of unanticipated inflation in the labor market. When wages are contracted in nominal terms, inflation reduces real wages, leading workers to intensify on-the-job search to obtain wage-adjusting outside offers. Both the search effort and the resulting job mobility are costly, yet that same mobility raises output by moving workers toward more productive matches. To quantify these costs and benefits, we extend the canonical job ladder of Postel-Vinay and Robin (2002) to a nominal environment with privately chosen search effort, and calibrate it to standard moments from the pre-pandemic US labor market. A one-time inflation shock scaled to the COVID episode generates an average welfare loss of 0.44% of consumption, with workers at the top of the wage distribution bearing losses over six times those at the bottom. Once the offsetting transfer of surplus to firms and productivity gains from reallocation are accounted for, the net aggregate cost is close to zero. Second, inflation volatility imposes asymmetric costs: worker welfare is approximately invariant to the volatility regime because firms compensate workers for expected search costs at hiring, but social welfare declines meaningfully because the resource cost of search effort is not undone by compensation. This second finding identifies a distinct, labor-market source of welfare costs from inflation volatility, complementing the price-dispersion costs emphasized in the New Keynesian literature.

中文摘要

本文研究未预期通货膨胀对劳动力市场的影响。当工资以名义条款签订时，通胀降低实际工资，促使劳动者加强在职搜寻以获取能调整工资的外部工作机会。搜寻努力及由此产生的职业流动均具有成本，但该流动通过将劳动者匹配到更具生产率的岗位而提升产出。为量化这些成本与收益，我们将Postel-Vinay和Robin（2002）的经典职业阶梯模型扩展至包含私人选择搜寻努力的名义环境，并利用疫情前美国劳动力市场的标准矩进行校准。一次与COVID冲击规模相当的一次性通胀冲击导致平均福利损失为消费的0.44%，其中工资分布顶端的劳动者承受的损失是底端劳动者的六倍以上。在考虑企业盈余转移与再分配带来的生产率增益后，净总成本接近于零。其次，通胀波动性产生非对称成本：劳动者福利对波动性制度大致不变，因为企业在雇佣时补偿了预期搜寻成本；但社会福利显著下降，因为搜寻努力的资源成本无法通过补偿抵消。这一发现识别了通胀波动性福利成本的一个独特劳动力市场来源，补充了新凯恩斯主义文献中强调的价格离散成本。

Do Wage Floors Increase Employment Risk for Workers with Disabilities? Evidence from Minimum Wage Increases and Subminimum Wage Repeal

工资下限是否增加残疾劳动者的就业风险？来自最低工资上调与次最低工资废除的证据

Roisin O'Neill, Shailee Manandhar, Douglas L. Kruse

Programs：LS；JEL：J14, J21, J38

Abstract

People with disabilities are disproportionately represented in low-wage work, raising concerns that higher wage floors may reduce their employment opportunities, particularly for workers with more severe disabilities. We examine the effects of state minimum wage increases and state subminimum wage terminations on employment outcomes for people with disabilities using American Community Survey data from 2010–2023. We find little evidence that minimum wage increases reduce employment or labor force participation among people with disabilities, including those with more severe disabilities. While many estimates are statistically imprecise, confidence intervals generally rule out economically meaningful negative employment effects. We likewise find no evidence that terminating subminimum wages reduces employment opportunities for affected workers, and some estimates suggest positive employment effects for groups most likely to have been employed under subminimum wage arrangements. These gains may reflect complementary policies that often accompany repeal, such as Employment First initiatives. Overall, the results provide little support for the view that higher wage floors create disproportionate employment barriers for people with disabilities.

中文摘要

残疾人在低工资工作中占比过高，这引发担忧：更高的工资下限可能减少其就业机会，尤其对重度残疾劳动者而言。我们利用2010-2023年美国社区调查数据，考察州最低工资上调与次最低工资终止对残疾人就业结果的影响。几乎没有证据表明最低工资上调会减少残疾人的就业或劳动参与，包括重度残疾者。尽管许多估计在统计上不精确，但置信区间通常排除了经济意义上显著的负面就业效应。同样，我们没有发现终止次最低工资会减少受影响工人就业机会的证据，且部分估计表明，最可能受次最低工资安排影响的群体出现了正面就业效应。这些收益可能反映了废除政策常伴随的补充性措施，如“就业优先”倡议。总体而言，结果不支持更高工资下限会为残疾人制造不成比例就业障碍的观点。

Air Pollution and Early Childhood Outcomes: Evidence from Georgia Childcare Providers

空气污染与儿童早期发展：来自佐治亚州托幼机构的数据

Mahta Ghafarianghadim, Garth Heutel

Programs：EEE；JEL：I25, J13, Q50

Abstract

Many effects of pollution exposure on health and education outcomes of children have been identified, but little is known about education effects on preschool-aged children. We estimate the effect of particulate matter air pollution on preschool attendance using restricted administrative data from the state of Georgia. We use thermal inversions, weather phenomena that trap pollutants, as an instrumental variable for pollution. A one-unit increase in the county-week average ambient particulate pollution level decreases attendance by around 2 percentage points. Effects seem to be larger for racial minorities and for children with working parents.

中文摘要

已有研究识别了污染暴露对儿童健康与教育结果的诸多影响，但关于学龄前儿童教育效应的证据不足。我们利用佐治亚州的限制性行政数据，估计颗粒物空气污染对学前出勤率的影响。采用热逆温（一种困住污染物的天气现象）作为污染的工具变量。县-周平均环境颗粒物污染水平每增加一个单位，出勤率下降约2个百分点。效应在少数族裔儿童及父母工作的儿童中似乎更大。

No. 7NBER w35366

Misleading Estimates from Nonlinear Models with a Binary Outcome

二元结果非线性模型中的误导性估计

Brian Curran, Bruce D. Meyer, Derek Wu

Programs：LS；JEL：C21, C23, C25

Abstract

When estimating nonlinear models for binary outcomes, such as probit and logit models, researchers often rely on average partial effects (APEs) to summarize the effect of a regressor. Because the marginal effect of a variable in these models depends on the values of all other variables, the value of an APE hinges on the portion of the sample used for the calculations. When averaged over parts of the sample drawn from a subpopulation not used to define the object of interest, the APE may be misleading. This paper highlights common situations, such as differences-in-means with a secondary group and difference-in-differences designs, where APEs calculated for the full sample deviate from marginal effects for the appropriate part of the sample. We propose a simple and costless solution in specific cases and demonstrate through simulations that recalculating APEs over the appropriate subsample yields unbiased results. Reexamining published results from multiple papers, we find statistically significant discrepancies between the reported estimates and the appropriately calculated APEs.

中文摘要

在估计二元结果的非线性模型（如Probit和Logit模型）时，研究者常依赖平均部分效应（APEs）来概括解释变量的影响。由于这些模型中变量的边际效应取决于所有其他变量的取值，APE的值取决于用于计算的样本部分。当对来自未用于定义目标对象的子总体的样本部分进行平均时，APE可能具有误导性。本文强调了常见情形，例如包含次要组别的均值差异法和双重差分设计，在这些情形中，基于全样本计算的APE偏离了针对样本适当部分的边际效应。我们针对特定情况提出了一种简单且零成本的解决方案，并通过模拟证明，在适当的子样本上重新计算APE可以得到无偏结果。重新审视多篇已发表论文的结果，我们发现报告估计值与适当计算的APE之间存在统计上显著的差异。

No. 8NBER w35363

Paying for Quality vs. Paying for Rank: When Purifying a Metric Backfires

为质量付费与为排名付费：当净化指标适得其反时

Joshua S. Gans, Scott Duke Kominers

Programs：PR；JEL：C78, D47, D83, I23

Abstract

Matching markets allocate scarce opportunities using performance metrics that agents can game. When does making a metric less gameable actually reduce gaming? We show that the answer hinges on how the market converts evaluations into stakes. Where evaluations assign prices — wages, credit terms, score-indexed payments — partially purifying the metric raises gaming precisely when gameable variation dominates it: the market's trust in the metric then rises faster than its exposure to gaming falls. By contrast, where evaluations assign positions — fixed seats allocated by rank — purification never backfires: ranking cancels trust from the incentive, leaving a rank price that deflates with the metric's dispersion, so mean gaming falls monotonically. We solve both markets in closed form under a first-order equilibrium concept; transfers microfound the first regime, rank tournaments the second. A prize-curvature index then determines when gaming improves rather than harms sorting, and when perfecting the metric is itself sorting-suboptimal.

中文摘要

匹配市场使用代理人可以操纵的绩效指标来分配稀缺机会。何时让指标更不易被操纵实际上会减少操纵行为？我们表明，答案取决于市场如何将评估转化为利害关系。当评估赋予价格——工资、信贷条件、按分数索引的支付——时，部分净化指标恰恰会在可操纵的变异主导它时增加操纵行为：市场对指标的信任上升速度快于其对操纵的暴露下降速度。相比之下，当评估赋予职位——按排名分配的固定席位——时，净化永远不会适得其反：排名抵消了激励中的信任，留下一个随指标离散度而缩减的排名价格，因此平均操纵行为单调下降。我们在一个一阶均衡概念下以闭式解求解了两种市场；转移支付为第一种机制提供了微观基础，排名锦标赛为第二种提供了微观基础。然后，一个奖金曲率指数决定了何时操纵会改善而非损害分类，以及何时完善指标本身在分类上是次优的。

What Happens to Contractors After States Ban Affirmative Action?

各州禁止平权行动后承包商发生了什么？

Benjamin Rosa

Programs：IO, LE, LS, PE, PR；JEL：H57, J15, J16, L25

Abstract

Using restricted Census business records, I explore how banning affirmative action in state contracting affects minority- and women-owned business enterprises (MWBEs). I find that ending affirmative action led MWBE contractors to gradually downsize, with the most pronounced reductions in force experienced by Black-owned businesses and larger MWBEs. Despite these workforce changes, existing MWBEs were no more likely to shut down than other businesses. New MWBEs were relatively less common after a state's ban, highlighting how bans can shift the demographic composition of new contractors. A calibrated model suggests bans are equivalent to considerable reductions in MWBE productivity and scrap values.

中文摘要

利用受限的人口普查商业记录，我探讨了在州政府合同中禁止平权行动如何影响少数族裔和女性拥有的企业（MWBEs）。我发现，结束平权行动导致MWBE承包商逐渐缩减规模，其中黑人拥有的企业和较大的MWBE经历的裁员最为显著。尽管存在这些劳动力变化，现有的MWBE比其它企业更不可能关闭。在州禁令之后，新的MWBE相对较少，这突显了禁令如何改变新承包商的人口构成。一个校准模型表明，禁令相当于MWBE生产力和残值的大幅降低。

On the Origins of the Multinational Premium

论跨国企业溢价的起源

José L. Fillat, Stefania Garetto

Programs：IFM, ITI；JEL：F12, F23, F36

Abstract

We study the relationship between management, multinational expansion, and risk premia. We document two facts: firms run by better managers are more likely to become multinationals (MNEs), and risk premia are higher for current and future MNEs than for firms that remain exclusively domestic. We develop a model in which endogenous matching between heterogeneous firms and managers jointly determines selection into foreign direct investment (FDI) and risk premia. Quantitatively, we use the model to examine how corporate taxation and distortions in the market for managerial talent affect multinational activity, firm risk exposure, and financial market outcomes.

中文摘要

我们研究了管理、跨国扩张与风险溢价之间的关系。我们记录了两个事实：由更优秀管理者经营的公司更有可能成为跨国企业（MNEs），并且当前和未来的跨国企业的风险溢价高于那些始终只在国内经营的公司。我们开发了一个模型，其中异质性企业与管理者之间的内生匹配共同决定了对外直接投资（FDI）的选择和风险溢价。在量化分析中，我们利用该模型考察了公司税和管理人才市场的扭曲如何影响跨国活动、公司风险暴露以及金融市场结果。

No. 11NBER w35360

How Climate Shocks Produce Armed Rebellion: The 1970 Bhola Cyclone and the Birth of Bangladesh

气候冲击如何引发武装叛乱：1970年博拉气旋与孟加拉国的诞生

Sultan Mehmood, Ahmed Mushfiq Mobarak

Programs：DEV, EEE, POL；JEL：D74, N45, Q54

Abstract

Why do climate shocks produce conflict (Hsiang et al., 2013)? We study the 1970 Bhola cyclone in East Pakistan, one of the deadliest natural disasters in recorded history, which killed an estimated 350,000 people. Using satellite imagery, archival records, electoral returns, and insurgent biographies, we show that cyclone exposure amplified pre-existing separatist sentiment at the ballot box and increased citizens’ subsequent participation in guerrilla warfare against the state. Our estimates imply that exposure to the cyclone’s devastation induced an additional 48,613 insurgents to join the guerrilla war effort. Effects are larger in areas with pre-existing political and economic grievances and weaker relief provision. Our analysis reveals the step-by-step mechanisms through which environmental shocks can produce conflict: they shift voting behavior, expose state indifference, and convert grievance into armed mobilization.

Abstract

Abstract

Although use of generative AI tools has quickly become widespread in education settings, emerging evidence suggests that effects on learning will depend on how that use is supported and guided. This paper reports findings from an original national survey of K-12 school principals designed to measure institutional integration of AI in schools through policies, teacher training, guidance for student use, leadership engagement, and the availability of AI-enabled tools. Abstract

Rising elderly life expectancy is a well-known source of fiscal pressure on Social Security and Medicare – but how have declining mortality and morbidity affected the two programs’ relative finances? Using nearly three decades of Medicare Current Beneficiary Survey data (1992-2019), we estimate that these demographic changes raised expected lifetime Social Security spending by over twice as much as expected lifetime Medicare spending: 14% compared to 6%. Abstract

The emergence of wearable electroencephalography (EEG) technologies presents new opportunities to identify and quantify neural correlates of decision-making in real-time, financially consequential settings.
