No. 1NBER w34439

Employment Relationships, Wage Setting, and Labor Market Power

雇佣关系、工资设定与劳动力市场势力

Francesco Agostinelli, Domenico Ferraro, Giuseppe Sorrenti, Leonard Treuren

Programs：EFG, IO, LS, PE；JEL：J0, J42, L10

中文摘要

我们探究劳动力市场势力的量化在多大程度上依赖于对长期工人-企业雇佣关系的建模。我们构建了一个包含动态工资合同的寡头垄断模型。工人在提供不同福利的企业之间进行选择，决定是否工作以及在哪里工作，并解决一个包含企业特定人力资本的动态离散选择劳动力供给问题。因此，企业最优地选择工资-任期合同来吸引和留住工人。我们发现，与标准的静态工资设定模型相比，此类合同削弱了企业施加巨大瞬时工资压低的动机，从而减少了社会低效的工人-企业分离的份额。因此，我们表明，基于“充分统计量”的实证方法往往会高估劳动力市场势力的程度：低水平的企业特定劳动力供给弹性并不一定表明租金提取，而是反映了企业通过提供人力资本积累带来的长期价值来留住工人的能力。

Abstract

We ask to what extent the quantification of labor market power depends on the modeling of the long-term worker-firm employment relationship. We develop an oligopsony model with dynamic wage contracts. Workers decide whether and where to work, choosing among firms providing different amenities and solving a dynamic discrete choice labor supply problem with firm-specific human capital. As a result, firms optimally choose wage-tenure contracts to attract and retain workers. We find that such contracts mitigate firms' incentives to impose large instantaneous wage markdowns—compared to standard static wage-setting models—thereby reducing the share of socially inefficient worker-firm separations. As a consequence, we show that the empirical approaches based on "sufficient statistics" tend to overestimate the extent of labor market power: low levels of firm-specific labor supply elasticities do not necessarily indicate rent extraction, but instead reflect firms’ ability to retain workers by offering long-term value through human capital accumulation.

No. 2NBER w34438

Environmental Catastrophe and the Direction of Invention: Evidence from the American Dust Bowl

环境灾难与发明方向：来自美国沙尘暴的证据

Jacob Moscona

Programs：DAE, EEE, PR；JEL：O3, O31, O33, Q1, Q16, Q54

中文摘要

本文研究了创新如何响应并塑造了美国沙尘暴的经济影响，沙尘暴是20世纪30年代导致美国大平原地区大面积土壤侵蚀的一场环境灾难。结合县级侵蚀数据、作物生产的历史地理数据以及作物特定创新数据，我记录了在环境危机之后，农业技术发展被强烈且持续地重新导向更易受沙尘暴影响的作物，并且在作物内部，转向能够直接减轻环境困境造成的经济损失的生物化学和种植技术。县级层面暴露于沙尘暴引发的创新显著抑制了土地侵蚀对农业土地价值和收入的影响。这些结果凸显了危机在激发创新中的作用，以及内生技术进步作为面对灾难时的适应力量的重要性。

Abstract

This paper investigates how innovation responded to and shaped the economic impact of the American Dust Bowl, an environmental catastrophe that led to widespread soil erosion on the US Plains during the 1930s. Combining data on county-level erosion, the historical geography of crop production, and crop-specific innovation, I document that in the wake of the environmental crisis, agricultural technology development was strongly and persistently re-directed toward more Dust Bowl-exposed crops and, within crops, toward bio-chemical and planting technologies that could directly mitigate economic losses from environmental distress. County-level exposure to Dust Bowl-induced innovation significantly dampened the effect of land erosion on agricultural land values and revenue. These results highlight the role of crises in spurring innovation and the importance of endogenous technological progress as an adaptive force in the face of disasters.

No. 3NBER w34437

The (In)effectiveness of Targeted Payroll Tax Reductions

定向工资税减免的（无）效性

Alessandra Fenizia, Nicholas Y. Li, Luca Citino

Programs：LS, PE；JEL：H20, H32, J23

中文摘要

本文研究了定向工资税在刺激劳动力需求方面的成本效益。它利用丰富的行政数据来研究意大利一项改革的影响，该改革提高了学徒合同的社会保障缴款，但为拥有9名或更少员工的企业提供了大幅折扣。该折扣并未增加对学徒合同的需求。相反，它补贴了边际内雇佣。这项改革不具有成本效益。点估计表明，每减少一百万欧元的社会保障缴款，可以支持29名学徒一年的就业，并且没有永久合同（这些估计在统计上不显著异于零）。

Abstract

This paper studies the cost-effectiveness of targeted payroll taxes for stimulating labor demand. It uses rich administrative data to study the effects of an Italian reform that raised social security contributions for apprenticeship contracts but granted a substantial discount for firms with 9 employees or less. The discount does not increase demand for apprenticeship contracts. Instead, it subsidizes inframarginal hiring. This reform is not cost-effective. Point estimates imply that each million euros of foregone social security contributions supports the employment of 29 apprentices for one year and no permanent contracts (these estimates are not statistically different from zero).

No. 4NBER w34436

Monetary Policy without an Anchor

无锚的货币政策

Luigi Bocola, Alessandro Dovis, Kasper Jørgensen, Rishabh Kirpalani

Programs：EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E52, E58

中文摘要

政策制定者经常引用通胀预期可能“脱锚”的风险，作为对通胀冲击做出有力反应的关键原因。我们开发了一个模型来分析这种权衡，并量化稳定的长期通胀预期的好处。在我们的框架中，家庭和企业对央行的目标信息不完全，并从其政策选择中学习。认识到这种互动，央行在面临不利供给冲击后会更积极地提高利率，并接受短期产出成本，以确保更稳定的通胀预期。这种声誉渠道的强度取决于长期通胀预期对利率意外的敏感程度。利用高频识别，我们估计了新兴经济体和发达经济体的这些弹性，并发现巴西的数值很大且为负。我们将模型拟合到这些发现中，并用它来量化声誉建设动机如何影响货币政策决策，以及央行可信度在促进宏观经济稳定中的作用。

Abstract

Policymakers often cite the risk that inflation expectations might “de-anchor” as a key reason for responding forcefully to inflationary shocks. We develop a model to analyze this trade-off and to quantify the benefits of stable long-run inflation expectations. In our framework, households and firms are imperfectly informed about the central bank’s objective and learn from its policy choices. Recognizing this interaction, the central bank raises interest rates more aggressively after adverse supply shocks and accepts short-run output costs to secure more stable inflation expectations. The strength of this reputation channel depends on how sensitive long-run inflation expectations are to surprises in interest rates. Using high-frequency identification, we estimate these elasticities for emerging and advanced economies and find large negative values for Brazil. We fit our model to these findings and use it to quantify how reputation building motives affect monetary policy decisions, and the role of central bank's credibility in promoting macroeconomic stability.

No. 5NBER w34435

Cannabis and Respiratory Health

大麻与呼吸系统健康

Jayani Jayawardhana, Jialin Hou, Johanna Catherine Maclean

Programs：EH；JEL：I1

中文摘要

在过去二十年中，随着允许持有该产品的州级政策出台，大麻合法化显著增加。大麻合法化及相应消费增长的一个潜在担忧是——由于吸食是最常见的消费方式——呼吸系统健康可能会恶化。在这项研究中，我们提供了关于娱乐性大麻法律对哮喘结果影响的新证据。为此，我们将2008年至2022年期间的商业健康保险索赔数据和调查数据与双重差分法和事件研究法相结合。我们的研究结果表明，在娱乐性大麻合法化后，哮喘诊断没有变化，而哮喘相关的处方药配药量下降，哮喘相关的门诊就诊和住院治疗保持稳定。对法律实施后吸食和非吸食大麻使用变化的分析表明，净而言，合法化导致的大麻总体使用量增加是由非吸食大麻的使用驱动的，这或许可以解释为什么我们发现法律实施后哮喘结果恶化的证据有限。总的来说，这些结果表明，扩大合法大麻的获取并未总体上恶化呼吸系统健康，并暗示一些患者可能使用大麻来控制哮喘症状。

Abstract

Cannabis legalization has increased substantially in the past two decades with state-level policies that permit possession this product. A potential concern with cannabis legalization and the corresponding increase in consumption is that – because smoking is the most common consumption mode – respiratory health could worsen. In this study, we offer new evidence on the impact of recreational cannabis laws on asthma outcomes. To do so, we combine commercial health insurance claims and survey data over the period 2008 to 2022 with difference-in-differences and event-study methods. Our findings suggest that asthma diagnoses do not change following legalization of recreational cannabis, while asthma-related dispensed prescription medications decline, and asthma-related outpatient visits and inpatient hospitalizations are stable. An analysis of changes in use of smoked and non-smoked cannabis post-law suggests that, on net, the increase in overall cannabis use attributable to legalization is driven by use of non-smoked cannabis, which may explain why we find limited evidence that asthma outcomes worsen post-law. Collectively, these results suggest that expanded access to legal cannabis has not worsened respiratory health overall, and hint that some patients may use cannabis to manage asthma symptoms.

No. 6NBER w34434

The Financial Consequences of Being Denied Benefit Access

被拒绝福利准入的财务后果

Tatiana Homonoff, Min S. Lee, Katherine Meckel

Programs：CH, EH, PE；JEL：D14, H53, I38

中文摘要

我们研究了被拒绝福利准入对边际被排斥家庭财务福祉的因果影响。利用强制性入职面试灵活性的实验性和准随机变化，以及行政补充营养援助计划（SNAP）与信用报告数据之间的独特关联，我们发现，因行政障碍而被筛选出SNAP的家庭会遭受切实的下游经济后果。具体来说，我们发现与流程相关的拒绝增加了债务和拖欠，并降低了信用评分。这些结果凸显了实施设计的经济重要性，及其在加强（或削弱）社会保险计划提供的财务保护方面的作用。

Abstract

We examine the causal impact of being denied benefit access on the financial well-being of marginally-excluded households. Using experimental and quasi-random variation in the flexibility of mandatory intake interviews and a unique linkage between administrative Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) and credit report data, we find that households screened out of SNAP due to administrative barriers suffer tangible downstream economic consequences. Specifically, we find that process-related denials increase debt and delinquencies, and decrease credit scores. These results highlight the economic importance of implementation design and its role in strengthening (or undermining) the financial protection provided by social insurance programs.

No. 7NBER w34433

Pushing Back Against Private Practice: The Unintended Effects of Paying Public Doctors More

抵制私人执业：提高公立医生薪酬的意外后果

Jonathan Gruber, Núria Mas, Judit Vall Castelló, Jaume Vives-i-Bastida

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：I18, J30

中文摘要

世界上大多数国家都有并行的私人和公共医疗保健系统。政策制定者担心跨部门的“双重执业”可能会减少对公共部门的医疗服务。这种担忧导致西班牙各地区向仅在公共部门执业的医生提供“排他性奖金”。我们从理论上表明，这些奖金对公共部门的影响是不确定的。我们通过实证证明，这些奖金适得其反：它们确实增加了在公共部门的排他性参与，但显著减少了工作时间。当各地区增加针对双重执业的补偿性奖金时，这些奖金基本上无效。

Abstract

Most nations in the world have side-by-side private and public health care systems. Policymakers worry that “dual practice” across sectors might reduce care to the public sector. This concern led regions in Spain to offer “exclusivity bonuses” to physicians who practice exclusively in the public sector. We show theoretically that the impact of these bonuses on the public sector is ambiguous. We demonstrate empirically that the bonuses backfired: they did increase exclusive participation in the public sector, but significantly reduced hours of work. When regions added offsetting bonuses for dual practice, they were largely ineffective.

No. 8NBER w34432

Voting with Their Feet: Democratic Backsliding and Market-Minded Migration - Evidence from Israel and Europe

用脚投票：民主倒退与市场导向的移民——来自以色列和欧洲的证据

Assaf Razin

Programs：IFM；JEL：F0

中文摘要

本研究采用比较框架，考察了以色列和欧洲政治体制的差异如何塑造国际移民模式。在这两种背景下，民主倒退事件作为准外生冲击，揭示了制度侵蚀与向外流动之间的因果关系。在以色列，民主倒退的根源在于一场腐败冲击——总理的刑事起诉——这升级为一场行政-司法冲击，因为政府发起了一场反民主的司法改革。行政与司法之间的这场对抗为识别制度崩溃如何塑造移民决策提供了一个自然实验。在欧洲，民主倒退的根源在于一场“叙利亚冲击”——大规模难民涌入，这给行政能力带来压力，使政治两极分化，并削弱了自由制度。由此产生的治理侵蚀引发了与在以色列观察到的结构相似的移民反应。使用双重差分法估计，本研究识别了民主侵蚀对移民流动的因果效应。在这两种背景下，向外移民表现为对非自由体制变革的市场导向反应——这是一种行为信号，表明对以国有化产业、限制言论自由和破坏法治为特征的政策敏感。总之，以色列和欧洲的经验表明，非自由治理是移民的体制性推动因素，超越了标准的经济解释。

Abstract

Using a comparative framework, the study examines how variations in political regimes across Israel and Europe shape patterns of international migration. In both contexts, episodes of democratic backsliding serve as quasi-exogenous shocks that reveal the causal link between institutional erosion and outward mobility.\nIn Israel, the origin of democratic backsliding lies in a corruption shock—the criminal indictment of the prime minister—which escalated into an executive–judicial shock as the government launched an anti-democratic judicial overhaul. This confrontation between the executive and judiciary provides a natural experiment for identifying how institutional breakdowns shape migration decisions.\nIn Europe, the origin of democratic backsliding stems from a “Syrian shock”—a massive refugee inflow that strained administrative capacity, polarized politics, and weakened liberal institutions. The resulting governance erosion triggered emigration responses structurally like those observed in Israel.\nUsing a difference-in-differences (DiD) estimation, the study identifies causal effects of democratic erosion on migration flows. Across both settings, out-migration emerges as a market-minded response to illiberal regime change—a behavioral signal of sensitivity to policies that features nationalizing industries, restrict free speech, and undermine the rule of law. Together, the Israeli and European experiences demonstrate that illiberal governance functions as a systemic push factor for emigration, beyond standard economic explanations.

No. 9NBER w34431

Planes Overhead: How Airplane Noise Impacts Home Values

头顶飞机：航空噪音如何影响房价

Florian Allroggen, R. John Hansman, Christopher R. Knittel, Jing Li, Xibo Wan, Juju Wang

Programs：EEE, IO, PE；JEL：L51, L62, L85, Q53

中文摘要

航空运输支撑经济增长和全球连通性，但会带来局部环境成本，尤其是通过飞机噪音。我们利用美国联邦航空管理局在三个主要机场推行基于性能的导航程序和跑道重新配置的准实验变化，估计了航空噪音对房价的因果效应。结合高分辨率飞行轨迹数据与地理编码的住房交易数据，我们应用双重差分特征模型来识别居民未预料到的噪音暴露变化。年昼夜平均声级每增加一分贝，房价下降0.6%至1.0%。在替代噪音指标中，平均暴露量对房产价值的影响解释力最强。为更安静环境付费的意愿随收入和种族系统性地变化，表明飞机噪音外部性具有显著分配后果。我们的结果凸显了将局部环境成本纳入航空和城市土地利用政策的必要性。

Abstract

Air transportation supports economic growth and global connectivity but imposes localized environmental costs, particularly through aircraft noise. We estimate the causal effect of aviation noise on housing prices using quasi-experimental variation from the Federal Aviation Administration's rollout of performance-based navigation (PBN) procedures and runway reconfigurations at three major U.S. airports. Combining high-resolution flight trajectory data with geocoded housing transactions, we apply a difference-in-differences hedonic framework to identify changes in exposure unanticipated by residents. A one-decibel increase in annual day-night average sound level reduces house prices by 0.6 to 1.0 percent. Among alternative noise metrics, average exposure explains property value impacts most strongly. Willingness to pay for quieter conditions varies systematically with income and race, indicating that aircraft noise externalities have meaningful distributional consequences. Our results highlight the need to incorporate localized environmental costs into aviation and urban land-use policy.

No. 10NBER w34430

Political Information and Network Effects

政治信息与网络效应

Georgy Egorov, Sergei Guriev, Maxim Mironov, Ekaterina Zhuravskaya

Programs：POL；JEL：C93, D72, P00

中文摘要

为什么政治竞选常常产生出乎意料的结果？我们通过分别估计竞选对目标选民的直接影响和对同一社会环境中其他人的间接效应来探讨这一问题。在阿根廷2023年总统选举期间，我们与一家当地非政府组织合作，随机分发传单，提供专家对局外人候选人哈维尔·米莱某些提案可能后果的评估。利用阿根廷独特的子选区选举报告系统，我们显示，该竞选按预期减少了直接受处理选民对米莱的支持，但在受处理选区的未处理选民中增加了对他的支持，产生了适得其反的净正面效应。一项预先注册的复制实验证实了这些符号相反的效应。通过理论和调查实验，我们表明，少数不相信该竞选的选民更有动力与同伴讨论，从而说服他们支持米莱。当竞选批评局外人候选人时，这种动员效应似乎尤其可能发生。我们的结果凸显了针对反精英候选人的竞选如何可能无意中动员对他们的支持。

Abstract

Why do political campaigns so often yield unexpected results? We address this question by separately estimating the direct effect of a campaign on targeted voters and the indirect effect on others in the same social environment. Partnering with a local NGO during Argentina’s 2023 presidential election, we randomized the distribution of leaflets providing an expert assessment of the likely consequences of certain proposals by the outsider candidate Javier Milei. Exploiting Argentina’s unique sub-precinct election reporting system, we show that the campaign reduced Milei’s support among directly treated voters, as expected, but increased his support among untreated voters in treated precincts, producing a backfiring, net-positive effect for Milei. A pre-registered replication confirmed these opposite-signed effects. Using theory and a survey experiment, we show that the minority of voters who disbelieved the campaign were more motivated to discuss it with peers, convincing them to support Milei. This mobilization effect appears especially likely when campaigns criticize outsider candidates. Our results highlight how campaigns aimed at anti-elite candidates can unintentionally mobilize support for them.

No. 11NBER w34429

AVE Tariffs are a Misleading Measure of Tariff Policy

从价关税等值是关税政策的误导性衡量指标

Andrew Greenland, James Lake, John Lopresti

Programs：EFG；JEL：E31, F02

中文摘要

我们表明，从价关税等值的变化不足以确定关税对经济结果的影响。利用1972-1988年美国关税的新数据库，我们将总从价关税等值分解为法定成分和内生成分。1979年之前，通货膨胀通过侵蚀从量关税造成了“意外自由化”，而进口构成变化掩盖了1980年代东京回合的关税削减。总体而言，总从价关税等值变化中多达70%是由法定关税变化以外的机制驱动的。将从量关税嵌入帽子代数框架，我们显示意外自由化解释了1972-1988年从价关税等值变化的一半。其对进口和价格指数的最终影响取决于价格变动是由进口需求还是出口供给冲击驱动的。如果由出口供给冲击驱动，意外自由化与进口下降和进口价格指数上升相关。

Abstract

We show that changes in ad valorem equivalent (AVE) tariffs are insufficient to sign the effect of tariffs on economic outcomes. Using a novel database of 1972-1988 US tariffs, we decompose aggregate AVEs into statutory and endogenous components. Pre-1979, inflation creates an “accidental liberalization” by eroding specific tariffs, while import composition changes mask 1980s Tokyo Round tariff cuts. Overall, as much as 70% of the variation in aggregate AVE changes is driven by mechanisms other than statutory tariff changes. Embedding specific tariffs in a hat algebra framework, we show that the accidental liberalization accounts for half of 1972-1988 AVE changes. The resulting impact on imports and price indices depends on whether price movements were driven by import demand or export supply shocks. If driven by export supply shocks, the accidental liberalization is associated with falling imports and rising import price indices.

No. 12NBER w34428

Vulnerability of the Public Safety System: Evidence from Micro-Shocks

公共安全系统的脆弱性：来自微观冲击的证据

Shooshan Danagoulian, Monica Deza, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Alberto Ortega

There will be a continued decline in intermediate exchange rate arrangements, and gains for hard pegs, as emerging markets move in this direction, and for more freely floating rates, driven by developing countries. There will be a further increase in the share of countries with open capital accounts, driven by emerging markets and developing countries.

Ideology in Government: Evidence from the Office of Indian Affairs and the Assimilation Era

Eric Chyn, Kareem Haggag, Christian Maruthiah

Programs：DAE, LS, PE, POL；JEL：D73, J15, M5, N41

中文摘要

本文通过研究印第安事务局（一个对美国原住民的土地、教育和法律治理拥有广泛权力的机构）来考察政府官员的意识形态与强制性政策制定。我们将该机构官僚的详细报告数字化，并使用计算工具来衡量他们在19世纪和20世纪初对同化主义政策的支持强度。我们记录了意识形态承诺的重大转变，这些转变与宗教组织提名担任高级机构职位的社会改革者的进入——以及最终退出——相吻合。我们发现，尽管该组织总体上继续推行主要的同化政策，例如推广农业和招收原住民进入保留地外的印第安寄宿学校，但官僚机构内部的意识形态似乎在世纪之交趋于温和。为了考察官僚机构内部意识形态对绩效的影响，我们最后分析了《道斯法案》（一项旨在瓦解集体土地所有权的里程碑式法律）通过后的政策实施情况。我们提供的证据表明，在《道斯法案》成为法律后，那些当地工作人员对同化主义目标表现出更强过往承诺的机构，执行了更多的土地重新分配。

Abstract

This paper studies the ideology of government officials and coercive policymaking by examining the Office of Indian Affairs, an institution that held broad authority over the land, education, and legal governance of Indigenous populations in the United States. We digitize the detailed reports of the agency's bureaucrats and use computational tools to measure the strength of their support for assimilationist policies during the 19th and early 20th centuries. We document major shifts in ideological commitments that coincide with the entry—and eventual exit—of social reformers nominated for high-level agency positions by religious organizations. We find that ideology within the bureaucracy appears to moderate around the turn of the century despite the organization's overall continued pursuit of major assimilation policies, such as the promotion of farming and enrollment in off-reservation Indian boarding schools. To examine performance implications of ideology within the bureaucracy, we conclude with an analysis of policy implementation after the passage of the Dawes Act, a landmark law that aimed to dismantle collective land holdings. We provide evidence showing that the agencies with local staff who express greater past commitment to assimilationist goals carried out more land redistribution immediately after Dawes became law.

Fast Action for Floods: RCT Evidence on Forecast-based Cash Transfers from Bangladesh and Nepal

洪水快速行动：来自孟加拉国和尼泊尔基于预报的现金转移的随机对照试验证据

Paul J. Christian, Felipe A. Dunsch, Jonas Heirman, Erin M. Kelley, Florence Kondylis, Gregory Lane, Jennifer Waidler, Nidhila Adusumalli, Odbayar Batmunkh, Kriti Malhotra

Programs：DEV；JEL：O10

中文摘要

随着日益频繁的极端天气事件扰乱人们的生活，各机构正转向早期预警系统和预先准备以加速援助交付。我们提供了来自孟加拉国和尼泊尔的一项随机对照试验的证据，该试验测试了在洪水发生数天内提供现金是否比数月后提供相同援助带来更大的益处——或者仅仅是改变了受益的时间而不改善整体福利。结果表明，及时的现金援助在粮食安全和心理健康方面带来了整体改善。这一证据支持了预测危机并迅速发放救灾物资的努力。

Abstract

As increasingly frequent extreme weather events disrupt lives, institutions are turning to early-warning systems and advance preparation to accelerate aid delivery. We present evidence from a randomized controlled trial in Bangladesh and Nepal testing whether providing cash within days of a flood leads to greater benefits than delivering the same assistance months later—or if it simply shifts the timing of benefits without improving overall welfare. Results suggest that timely cash assistance leads to overall gains in food security and psychosocial well-being. This evidence supports efforts to forecast crises and release disaster relief quickly.
