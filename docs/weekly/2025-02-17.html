Does Peer Review Penalize Scientific Risk Taking? Evidence from NIH Grant Renewals

同行评审会惩罚科学冒险吗？来自NIH资助续期的证据

Pierre Azoulay, Wesley H. Greenblatt

Programs：PR；JEL：H51, O32, O38

Abstract

Scientific projects that carry a high degree of risk may be more likely to lead to breakthroughs yet also face challenges in winning the support necessary to be carried out. We analyze the determinants of renewal for more than 100,000 R01 grants from the National Institutes of Health between 1980 and 2015. We use four distinct proxies to measure risk taking: extreme tail outcomes, disruptiveness, pivoting from an investigator’s prior work, and standing out from the crowd in one’s field. After carefully controlling for investigator, grant, and institution characteristics, we measure the association between risk taking and grant renewal. Across each of these measures, we find that risky grants are renewed at markedly lower rates than less risky ones. We also provide evidence that the magnitude of the risk penalty is magnified for more novel areas of research and novice investigators, consistent with the academic community’s perception that current scientific institutions do not motivate exploratory research adequately.

中文摘要

具有高风险的科学项目可能更有可能带来突破，但也面临在获得开展所需支持方面的挑战。我们分析了1980年至2015年间美国国立卫生研究院超过10万个R01资助续期的决定因素。我们使用四个不同的代理指标来衡量冒险行为：极端尾部结果、颠覆性、偏离研究者先前工作的转向，以及在自身领域中脱颖而出。在仔细控制了研究者、资助和机构特征后，我们衡量了冒险行为与资助续期之间的关联。在这些指标中的每一个上，我们发现高风险资助的续期率明显低于低风险资助。我们还提供证据表明，风险惩罚的幅度对于更新颖的研究领域和新手研究者来说更大，这与学术界认为当前科学机构未能充分激励探索性研究的看法一致。

Guaranteed Employment in Rural India: Intra-Household Labor and Resource Allocation Consequences

印度农村的就业保障：家庭内部劳动力和资源配置后果

Jorge Luis García

Programs：CH, DEV, LS；JEL：I31, I32, J12, J13, O12, O15

Abstract

I investigate the intra-household labor and resource allocation consequences of an employment guarantee targeting rural households in India. The guarantee insures household earnings, replacing women as added workers and shutting down a motive for saving. Despite sizable program-job take-up, the guarantee decreases participation in other working activities, and, thus, the labor force participation of married women and total time worked by their husbands. The guarantee accounts for up to 30% of a recent countrywide decrease in rural female labor force participation. Though it increases household consumption, the guarantee reduces the command of household earnings by women, and, thereby, their well-being.

中文摘要

我调查了针对印度农村家庭的就业保障对家庭内部劳动力和资源配置的后果。该保障为家庭收入提供了保险，取代了作为附加劳动者的女性，并消除了储蓄的动机。尽管项目工作参与率可观，但该保障减少了其他工作活动的参与，从而降低了已婚女性的劳动参与率及其丈夫的总工作时间。该保障解释了近期全国范围内农村女性劳动参与率下降的30%。尽管它增加了家庭消费，但该保障减少了女性对家庭收入的支配权，从而降低了她们的福祉。

Optimal Spatial Policies

最优空间政策

Pablo D. Fajgelbaum, Cecile Gaubert

Programs：ITI, LS, PE；JEL：H21, H23, R12, R13

Abstract

We summarize recent methods to study optimal spatial policies. We center the discussion on policies that implement the optimal distribution of population in the presence of spatial spillovers, spatial transfers to optimally tackle redistribution between rich and poor regions, and optimal transportation investments.

The Effect of Application Fees on Entry into Patenting

申请费对专利进入的影响

Gaétan de Rassenfosse, Adam B. Jaffe

Programs：PR；JEL：O30, O31

Abstract

Ensuring broad access to the patent system is crucial for fostering innovation and promoting economic growth. To support this goal, the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office offers reduced fees for small and micro entities. This paper investigates whether fee rates affect the filing of applications by small and micro entities. Exploiting recent fee reforms, the study evaluates the relationship between fee changes and the number of new entrants, controlling for potential confounding factors such as legislative changes. The findings suggest that fee reductions alone are insufficient to significantly increase participation in the patent system among small and micro entities.

中文摘要

确保专利制度的广泛可及性对于促进创新和推动经济增长至关重要。为支持这一目标，美国专利商标局为小型和微型实体提供减费优惠。本文研究了费率是否影响小型和微型实体的申请提交。利用近期的费用改革，本研究评估了费用变化与新进入者数量之间的关系，并控制了诸如立法变化等潜在混淆因素。研究结果表明，仅靠减费不足以显著提高小型和微型实体对专利制度的参与度。

Hot and Crowded: Temperature, Healthcare Utilization and Patient Outcomes

炎热与拥挤：温度、医疗保健利用与患者结局

Sandra Aguilar-Gomez, Joshua S. Graff Zivin, Matthew J. Neidell

Programs：DEV, EEE, EH, PE；JEL：I15, I18, Q54

Abstract

Extreme heat raises emergency demand and may increase mortality through hospital congestion when shocks hit many people at once. Using administrative records from Mexico’s largest public health system, we separate direct heat effects from congestion spillovers. Days with maximum temperature above 34°C increase ED visits by 6.9 percent and hospitalizations by 4.2 percent, with sicker ED patients discharged home more often. In-hospital deaths rise for already-admitted patients, suggesting important spillover effects, and deaths disproportionately increase outside hospitals. These results identify health-system capacity as an important margin of adaptation to extreme heat.

中文摘要

极端高温会增加急诊需求，并且当冲击同时影响许多人时，可能通过医院拥堵增加死亡率。利用墨西哥最大公共卫生系统的行政记录，我们将直接热效应与拥堵溢出效应区分开来。最高温度超过34°C的日子会使急诊就诊次数增加6.9%，住院人数增加4.2%，且病情更重的急诊患者更常被送回家。已入院患者的院内死亡人数增加，表明存在重要的溢出效应，而院外死亡人数则不成比例地增加。这些结果确定了卫生系统能力是适应极端高温的一个重要方面。

Declining Life Satisfaction and Happiness Among Young Adults in Six English-speaking Countries

六个英语国家年轻人生活满意度和幸福感的下降

Jean Twenge, David G. Blanchflower

Programs：CH, LS；JEL：I31, J13

Abstract

We report eleven studies that show declines in life satisfaction and happiness among young adults in the last decade or so, with less uniform trends among older adults. We found consistent evidence for this for the U.S. in the recent sweeps of several micro data sets including the Behavioral Risk Factor Survey, the General Social Survey, and the American National Election Survey. In the U. S. life satisfaction rises with age. This is broadly confirmed in several other datasets including four from the European Commission across five other English-speaking countries: Australia, Canada, Ireland New Zealand and the UK. Declining wellbeing of the young was also found in the World Values Survey, the Global Flourishing Study and Global Minds. There is broad evidence across all of these English-speaking countries that happiness and life satisfaction since 2020 rise with age. In several of these surveys we also find that ill-being declines in age. The U-shape in wellbeing by age that used to exist in these countries is now gone, replaced by a crisis in wellbeing among the young.

中文摘要

我们报告了十一项研究，这些研究表明在过去十年左右，年轻人的生活满意度和幸福感有所下降，而老年人的趋势则不那么一致。我们在美国近期的几轮微观数据集中发现了这一点的持续证据，包括行为风险因素监测系统、综合社会调查和美国全国选举研究。在美国，生活满意度随年龄增长而上升。这在其他几个数据集中也得到了广泛证实，包括来自欧盟委员会的四个数据集，涵盖其他五个英语国家：澳大利亚、加拿大、爱尔兰、新西兰和英国。在世界价值观调查、全球繁荣研究和全球心智调查中也发现了年轻人幸福感的下降。有广泛证据表明，在所有英语国家中，自2020年以来，幸福感和生活满意度随年龄增长而上升。在其中几项调查中，我们还发现不良状态随年龄增长而下降。这些国家过去存在的幸福感随年龄的U型分布现已消失，取而代之的是年轻人中的幸福感危机。

High-Speed Rail and China's Electric Vehicle Adoption Miracle

高铁与中国电动汽车普及奇迹

Hanming Fang, Ming Li, Long Wang, Zoe Yang

Programs：DEV, EEE, PE；JEL：L52, L53, O18, Q55, R41

Abstract

Using China's expansion of the high-speed rail system (HSR) as a quasi-natural experiment, we analyze the comprehensive vehicle registration data from 2010 to 2023 to estimate the causal impact of HSR connectivity on the adoption of electric vehicles (EVs). Implementing several identification strategies, including staggered difference-in-differences (DID), Callaway and Sant'Anna (CS) DID, and two instrumental-variable approaches, we consistently find that, by alleviating range anxiety, the expansion of HSR can account for up to one third of the increase in EV market share and EV sales in China during our sample period, with effects particularly pronounced in cities served by faster HSR lines. The results remain robust when controlling for local industrial policies, charging infrastructure growth, supply-side factors, and economic development. We also find that HSR connectivity amplifies the effectiveness of charging infrastructure and consumer purchase subsidies in promoting EV adoption.

中文摘要

利用中国高铁系统的扩张作为准自然实验，我们分析了2010年至2023年的综合车辆登记数据，以估计高铁连通性对电动汽车普及的因果影响。通过实施多种识别策略，包括交错双重差分法、Callaway和Sant'Anna双重差分法以及两种工具变量方法，我们一致发现，通过缓解里程焦虑，高铁的扩张可以解释样本期间中国电动汽车市场份额和电动汽车销量增长的三分之一，其影响在高铁线路速度更快的城市尤为显著。在控制地方产业政策、充电基础设施增长、供给侧因素和经济发展后，结果依然稳健。我们还发现，高铁连通性增强了充电基础设施和消费者购买补贴在促进电动汽车普及方面的有效性。

Monetary Policy Under Okun’s Hypothesis

奥肯定律下的货币政策

Felipe Alves, Giovanni L. Violante

Programs：EFG；JEL：E10, E30, E5, J63

Abstract

The current monetary policy framework of the Fed intends to be more ’inclusive’ by running the economy hot for longer during expansions. The logic of this strategy rests on Okun’s (1973) hypothesis that sustaining a ‘high-pressure economy’ persistently improves labor market outcomes of low-wage workers. To evaluate this conjecture, we develop a Heterogeneous Agent New Keynesian framework with a three-state frictional model of the labor market where low-skilled workers are more exposed to the business cycle and recessions have a long-lasting effect on their labor force participation and earnings, in line with the evidence. Under a canonical Inflation Targeting rule, the ZLB generates a deflationary bias and severely amplifies the persistent scars of recessions at the bottom of the wage distribution. The Lower-for-Longer strategy is an effective antidote to the ZLB-driven hysteresis and leads to notable earnings gains for low-wage workers and a reduction to overall earnings inequality. If pursued aggressively, however, the policy reverts the inflation bias from negative to positive. Since policymakers might prioritize differently inflation relative to inclusion, we conclude by quantifying the inflation-inclusion trade-off implied by various monetary policy rules.

中文摘要

美联储当前的货币政策框架旨在更具“包容性”，即在经济扩张期间让经济更长时间地保持过热。这一策略的逻辑基于奥肯（1973）的假设，即维持“高压经济”能够持续改善低工资工人的劳动力市场结果。为了评估这一猜想，我们构建了一个异质性代理人新凯恩斯框架，其中包含一个三状态摩擦性劳动力市场模型，在该模型中，低技能工人更容易受到商业周期的影响，且衰退对其劳动力参与率和收入有长期影响，这与证据一致。在标准的通胀目标制规则下，零利率下限会产生通缩偏差，并严重放大工资分布底层的持久衰退伤痕。“更长时间维持低利率”策略是零利率下限驱动滞后的有效解药，并导致低工资工人的收入显著增加，整体收入不平等程度降低。然而，如果该政策执行得过于激进，则会将通胀偏差从负值转为正值。由于政策制定者对通胀和包容性的优先顺序可能不同，我们最后通过量化各种货币政策规则所隐含的通胀-包容性权衡来得出结论。

Economic Shocks and Healthcare Capital Investments

经济冲击与医疗保健资本投资

Michael R. Richards, Maggie Shi, Christopher M. Whaley

Programs：EH；JEL：H42, I1, I11, I13, I18, L21, L23, L24

Abstract

Information technology (IT) can enhance firms’ long-run performance but is also a risky investment, with high fixed costs and uncertain returns. Whether market events influence this tradeoff has received limited attention. We leverage the healthcare context to empirically examine hospitals’ IT investments following economic downturns and public insurance expansions––i.e., large industry shocks in opposite directions. We find novel and symmetrical responses. Recessions restrain investments while expansion policy indirectly stimulates them. Importantly, the IT margin is more elastic than other spending responses to market fluctuations. Supplementary analyses suggest that hospitals’ finances and perceptions of uncertainty drive these capital investment adjustments.

中文摘要

信息技术可以提升企业的长期绩效，但也是一种高风险投资，具有高固定成本和不确定的回报。市场事件是否会影响这种权衡，此前受到的关注有限。我们利用医疗保健背景，实证研究了医院在经济衰退和公共保险扩张（即方向相反的重大行业冲击）后的信息技术投资。我们发现了新颖且对称的反应。经济衰退抑制了投资，而扩张政策则间接刺激了投资。重要的是，信息技术这一边际比其他支出对市场波动的反应更具弹性。补充分析表明，医院的财务状况和对不确定性的感知驱动了这些资本投资的调整。

Charting the Uncharted: The (Un)Intended Consequences of Oil Sanctions and Dark Shipping

绘制未知领域：石油制裁与黑暗航运的（非）预期后果

Jesús Fernández-Villaverde, Yiliang Li, Le Xu, Francesco Zanetti

Programs：EFG；JEL：C32, C38, E32, Q43, R40

Abstract

We examine the rise of dark shipping – oil tankers disabling AIS transceivers to evade detection – amid Western sanctions on Iran, Syria, North Korea, Venezuela, and Russia. Using a machine learning-based ship clustering model, we track dark-shipped crude oil trade flows worldwide and detect unauthorized ship-to-ship transfers. From 2017 to 2023, dark ships transported an estimated 7.8 million metric tons of crude oil monthly – 43% of global seaborne crude exports – with China absorbing 15%. These sanctioned flows offset recorded declines in global oil exports but create distinct economic shifts. The U.S., a net oil exporter, faces lower oil prices but benefits from cheaper Chinese imports, driving deflationary growth. The EU, a net importer, contends with rising energy costs yet gains from Chinese demand, fueling inflationary expansion. China, leveraging discounted oil, boosts industrial output, propagating global economic shocks. Our findings expose dark shipping’s central role in reshaping oil markets and macroeconomic dynamics.

中文摘要

我们研究了在西方对伊朗、叙利亚、朝鲜、委内瑞拉和俄罗斯实施制裁的背景下，黑暗航运（即油轮关闭自动识别系统收发器以逃避检测）的兴起。利用基于机器学习的船舶聚类模型，我们追踪了全球范围内通过黑暗航运运输的原油贸易流，并检测了未经授权的船对船转运。从2017年到2023年，黑暗船舶每月运输估计780万公吨原油——占全球海运原油出口的43%——其中中国吸收了15%。这些受制裁的流动抵消了全球石油出口记录在案的下降，但造成了独特的经济转变。作为净石油出口国的美国面临较低的油价，但受益于更便宜的中国进口，推动了通缩型增长。作为净进口国的欧盟面临能源成本上升，但得益于中国需求，助长了通胀型扩张。中国利用折扣石油提高了工业产出，向全球传播了经济冲击。我们的发现揭示了黑暗航运在重塑石油市场和宏观经济动态中的核心作用。

Policy Interventions and China’s Stock Market in the Early Stages of the COVID-19 Pandemic

新冠疫情初期政策干预与中国股市

Steven J. Davis, Dingqian Liu, Xuguang Simon Sheng, Yan Wang

Programs：AP, EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E52, E58, E65, G12, G18

Abstract

China’s stock market greatly outperformed other national markets during the first several months of the COVID-19 pandemic, and it did so even before it became evident that early containment efforts would flounder in the United States and many other countries. As to why, one view holds that aggressive monetary and credit easing propped up Chinese equity values. To assess this view, we consider several interventions that eased monetary and credit conditions in the first six months of 2020. Our analysis finds clear evidence that these interventions raised implied stock market volatility but little evidence that they influenced stock price levels. We also consider policy actions that restricted short selling, limited stock sales, and boosted stock purchases. These efforts to raise net equity demand were small in scale and highly time-limited, as we discuss, suggesting that any direct effects on stock prices were also modest. Neither our study nor other work known to us provides a ready explanation for the extraordinary performance of China’s stock market in the first half of 2020. This performance is even more striking in hindsight, given later developments in China’s economy and stock market.

中文摘要

在新冠疫情最初几个月，中国股市的表现远超其他国家的市场，而且这种表现甚至在美国和许多其他国家的早期遏制努力明显失败之前就已出现。至于原因，一种观点认为，积极的货币和信贷宽松政策支撑了中国股票价值。为了评估这一观点，我们考虑了2020年前六个月放松货币和信贷条件的几项干预措施。我们的分析发现明确证据表明这些干预措施提高了隐含股票市场波动率，但几乎没有证据表明它们影响了股票价格水平。我们还考虑了限制卖空、限制股票出售和促进股票购买的政策行动。正如我们所讨论的，这些提高净股票需求的努力规模较小且高度有时间限制，表明对股票价格的任何直接影响也是温和的。我们的研究或我们所知的其他研究都没有为2020年上半年中国股市的非凡表现提供一个现成的解释。鉴于后来中国经济和股市的发展，这种表现事后看来更加引人注目。

The Welfare Benefits of Pay-As-You-Go Financing

现收现付融资的福利收益

Paul Gertler, Brett Green, Renping Li, David Sraer

Programs：CF, DEV, IO, PR；JEL：D14, D86, G21, G23

Abstract

Pay-as-you-go (PAYGo) financing is a novel contract that has recently become a popular form of credit, especially in low- and middle-income countries (LMICs). PAYGo financing relies on lockout technology that enables the lender to remotely disable the flow benefits of collateral when the borrower misses payments. This paper quantifies the welfare implications of PAYGo financing. We develop a dynamic structural model of consumers and estimate the model using a multi-arm, large scale pricing experiment conducted by a fintech lender that offers PAYGo financing for smartphones. We find that the welfare gains from access to PAYGo financing are equivalent to a 3.4% increase in income while remaining highly profitable for the lender. The welfare gains are larger for low-risk consumers and consumers in the middle of the income distribution. Under reasonable assumptions, PAYGo financing outperforms traditional secured loans for all but the riskiest consumers. We explore contract design and identify variations of the PAYGo contract that further improve welfare.

Abstract

Abstract
