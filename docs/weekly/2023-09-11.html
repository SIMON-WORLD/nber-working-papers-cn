From Retributive to Restorative: An Alternative Approach to Justice in Schools

从惩罚到修复：学校正义的替代路径

Anjali Adukia, Benjamin Feigenberg, Fatemeh Momeni

Programs：CH, ED；JEL：I0, I20, I21, I24, J0, J01, J08, J18, K39

Abstract

School districts historically approached conflict-resolution from the perspective that suspending disruptive students was necessary to protect their classmates, even if this caused harm to perceived offenders. Restorative practices (RP) – focused on reparation, accountability, and shared ownership of disciplinary justice – are designed to address undesirable behavior without harming students. We study Chicago Public Schools’ adoption of RP and find that suspensions and arrests decreased, driven by effects for Black students. We find null effects on test-score value added, ruling out meaningful average declines. We estimate a 15% decrease in out-of-school arrests, consistent with RP substantively changing student behavior.

中文摘要

学区历来以这样的视角处理冲突：认为停学处分扰乱秩序的学生对于保护其同学是必要的，即使这会对被视为违规者造成伤害。修复性实践（RP）——聚焦于赔偿、问责以及对纪律正义的共同拥有——旨在在不伤害学生的情况下解决不良行为。我们研究了芝加哥公立学校对RP的采纳，发现停学和逮捕数量有所下降，这一效应主要由黑人学生驱动。我们发现对考试成绩增值的影响为零，排除了有意义的平均下降。我们估计校外逮捕减少了15%，这与RP实质性改变学生行为的结果一致。

No. 2NBER w31674

Economic Decision-Making Skill Predicts Income in Two Countries

经济决策技能预测两个国家的收入

Andrew Caplin, David J. Deming, Søren Leth-Petersen, Ben Weidmann

Programs：ED, EFG, LS, PR；JEL：D8, J24

Abstract

Jobs increasingly require good decision-making. Workers are valued not only for how much they can do, but also for their ability to decide what to do. In this paper we measure the ability to make good decisions about resource allocation, which we call economic decision-making skill. Our assessment requires an intuitive understanding of comparative advantage and is motivated by a model where decision-makers strategically acquire information about factor productivity under time and effort constraints. Economic decision-making skill strongly predicts labor earnings in representative samples of full-time workers in the U.S. and Denmark, conditional on education, IQ, numeracy, and other covariates. Economic decision-making skill is more valuable in management and other decision-intensive occupations.

中文摘要

工作越来越需要良好的决策能力。员工的价值不仅取决于他们能完成多少工作，还取决于他们决定做什么的能力。在本文中，我们衡量了关于资源分配做出良好决策的能力，我们称之为经济决策技能。我们的评估要求对比较优势有直观理解，并受一个模型的启发，在该模型中，决策者在时间和精力约束下策略性地获取关于要素生产率的信息。在针对美国和丹麦全职工作者的代表性样本中，经济决策技能在控制教育、智商、计算能力及其他协变量的情况下，强烈预测劳动收入。经济决策技能在管理和其他决策密集型职业中更具价值。

Externalities from Medical Innovation: Evidence from Organ Transplantation

医疗创新的外部性：来自器官移植的证据

Kevin Callison, Michael E. Darden, Keith F. Teltser

Programs：EH；JEL：I10, I11, I14, O3

Abstract

We evaluate the introduction of direct-acting antiviral (DAA) therapy for Hepatitis C (HCV) on liver transplant allocation in the United States. We develop a model of listing and organ acceptance behavior for patients with both HCV-positive and HCV-negative end-stage liver disease. In the model, DAAs obviate the need for transplant for some HCV-positive patients, which shortens the waiting list, potentially benefiting HCV-negative registrants and inducing marginal HCV-negative patients to list. Using data from the universe of transplants between 2005 and 2019, we find that DAA availability resulted in an additional 5,682 liver transplants to HCV-negative recipients between 2014 and 2019, driven in part by a 37% average annual increase in HCV-negative waiting list registrations. Our estimates imply that DAAs generated $7.52 billion in positive externalities for HCV-negative patients during this period.

中文摘要

我们评估了针对丙型肝炎（HCV）的直接抗病毒（DAA）疗法引入对美国肝移植分配的影响。我们为HCV阳性和HCV阴性终末期肝病患者建立了一个关于登记和器官接受行为的模型。在该模型中，DAA使部分HCV阳性患者无需移植，从而缩短了等待名单，可能使HCV阴性登记人受益，并诱导边缘HCV阴性患者登记。利用2005年至2019年间所有移植数据，我们发现DAA的可及性在2014年至2019年间为HCV阴性受者额外带来了5682例肝移植，部分原因是HCV阴性等待名单登记人数年均增长37%。我们的估计表明，在此期间，DAA为HCV阴性患者产生了75.2亿美元的正外部性。

Households' Response to the Wealth Effects of Inflation

家庭对通货膨胀财富效应的反应

Philip Schnorpfeil, Michael Weber, Andreas Hackethal

Programs：AP, EFG, ME；JEL：D12, D14, D83, D84, E21, E31, E52

Abstract

We study the redistributive effects of inflation combining administrative bank data with an information provision experiment during an episode of historic inflation. On average, households are well-informed about prevailing inflation and are concerned about its impact on their wealth; yet, while many households know about inflation eroding nominal assets, most are unaware of nominal-debt erosion. Once they receive information on the debt-erosion channel, households update upwards their beliefs about nominal debt and their own real net wealth. These changes in beliefs causally affect actual consumption and hypothetical debt decisions. Our findings suggest that real wealth mediates the sensitivity of consumption to inflation once households are aware of the wealth effects of inflation.

中文摘要

我们结合行政银行数据与一次历史性通胀时期的信息提供实验，研究了通胀的再分配效应。平均而言，家庭对当前通胀了解充分，并担忧其对自身财富的影响；然而，尽管许多家庭知道通胀侵蚀名义资产，但大多数人不知道名义债务被侵蚀。一旦他们收到关于债务侵蚀渠道的信息，家庭会向上更新他们对名义债务和自身实际净财富的信念。这些信念的变化因果性地影响了实际消费和假设的债务决策。我们的发现表明，一旦家庭意识到通胀的财富效应，实际财富会调节消费对通胀的敏感性。

Breakthroughs in Historical Record Linking Using Genealogy Data: The Census Tree Project

利用家谱数据实现历史记录链接的突破：人口普查树项目

Kasey Buckles, Adrian Haws, Joseph Price, Haley E.B. Wilbert

Programs：CH, DAE, LS；JEL：C81, J10, N01

Abstract

The Census Tree is the largest-ever database of record links among the historical U.S. censuses, with over 700 million links for people living in the United States between 1850 and 1940. These high-quality links allow researchers in the social sciences and other disciplines to construct a longitudinal dataset that is highly representative of the population. In this paper, we describe our process for creating the Census Tree, beginning with a collection of links contributed by the users of a free online genealogy platform. We then use these links as training data for a machine learning algorithm to make new matches and incorporate other recent efforts to link the historical U.S. censuses. Finally, we introduce a procedure for filtering the links and adjudicating disagreements. Our complete Census Tree achieves match rates between adjacent censuses that are between 69 and 86% for men, and between 58 and 79% for women. To demonstrate the advantages of the Census Tree, we extend the work of Abramitzky, Boustan, Jácome, and Pérez (2021) to include intergenerational mobility estimates for additional immigrant nationalities and for women.

中文摘要

人口普查树是有史以来最大的历史美国人口普查记录链接数据库，包含超过7亿个链接，涵盖1850年至1940年间居住在美国的人口。这些高质量的链接使社会科学及其他学科的研究人员能够构建一个高度代表人口的面板数据集。在本文中，我们描述了创建人口普查树的过程，首先从免费在线家谱平台的用户贡献的链接集合开始。然后，我们使用这些链接作为机器学习算法的训练数据，以进行新的匹配，并整合其他近期链接历史美国人口普查的努力。最后，我们引入了一个过滤链接和裁决分歧的程序。我们完整的人口普查树在相邻普查之间的匹配率，男性为69%至86%，女性为58%至79%。为展示人口普查树的优势，我们扩展了Abramitzky、Boustan、Jácome和Pérez（2021）的工作，纳入了更多移民国籍和女性的代际流动性估计。

No. 6NBER w31670

The Digital Welfare of Nations: New Measures of Welfare Gains and Inequality

国家的数字福利：福利增益与不平等的新度量

Erik Brynjolfsson, Avinash Collis, Asad Liaqat, Daley Kutzman, Haritz Garro, Daniel Deisenroth, Nils Wernerfelt, Jae Joon Lee

Programs：EFG, IO, PR；JEL：O30, O4

Abstract

Digital goods can generate large benefits for consumers, but these benefits are largely unmeasured in the national accounts, including GDP and productivity. In this paper, we measure welfare gains from 10 popular digital goods across 13 countries by conducting large-scale incentivized online choice experiments on representative samples of nearly 40,000 people. We estimate that these goods—many of which are free to users—generate over $2.5 trillion in aggregate consumer welfare across these countries per year, which is roughly equivalent to 6% of their combined GDP. We find that lower-income individuals and lower-income countries obtain relatively larger welfare gains from these goods compared to higher-income individuals and countries. This suggests that digital goods may reduce inequality in welfare within and across countries by disproportionately benefiting lower-income groups.

中文摘要

数字商品可以为消费者带来巨大收益，但这些收益在国民账户（包括GDP和生产率）中基本未被衡量。在本文中，我们通过对近4万人的代表性样本进行大规模激励性在线选择实验，衡量了13个国家中10种流行数字商品的福利增益。我们估计，这些商品——其中许多对用户免费——每年在这些国家产生超过2.5万亿美元的消费者总福利，大致相当于其GDP总和的6%。我们发现，与高收入个人和高收入国家相比，低收入个人和低收入国家从这些商品中获得的相对福利增益更大。这表明，数字商品可能通过不成比例地惠及低收入群体，减少国家内部和国家之间的福利不平等。

No. 7NBER w31669

The Intergenerational Transmission of Housing Wealth

住房财富的代际传递

N. Meltem Daysal, Michael F. Lovenheim, David N. Wasser

Programs：CH, LS, PE；JEL：D31, J62

Abstract

Rising wealth inequality has spurred an increased interest in understanding how and why wealth is correlated across generations. We exploit plausibly exogenous variation in housing wealth driven by home price changes in different areas to isolate the causal impact of parental housing wealth during different childhood periods on children’s long-run wealth accumulation. Using population-level Danish administrative data, we find that 27% and 25% of each Krone of parental housing wealth change during early-childhood is transmitted to children’s overall and housing wealth in adulthood, respectively. The corresponding transmission rates for parental housing wealth changes during middle-childhood are 25% and 15%, with a transmission to non-housing wealth of 10%. There is little evidence of transmission of parental housing wealth changes that occur during the teenage years. Examining mechanisms, we find that parental housing wealth changes in early and middle-childhood lead to modest increases in adult children’s home ownership, educational attainment, and earnings. However, earnings and education can explain only 20-30% of the intergenerational transmission of parental wealth gains during these periods. We argue that the transmission of parental housing wealth changes in childhood are driven in large part by changes to unobserved household environment and parental behaviors that are passed on to children and shape their savings behavior in adulthood.

中文摘要

财富不平等加剧激发了对理解财富为何以及如何在代际间相关的兴趣。我们利用不同地区房价变动所驱动的住房财富外生变化，来隔离不同童年时期父母住房财富对子女长期财富积累的因果影响。使用丹麦人口层面的行政数据，我们发现，父母在儿童早期住房财富变化的27%和25%分别传递给了子女成年后的总财富和住房财富。父母在儿童中期住房财富变化的相应传递率为25%和15%，其中对非住房财富的传递率为10%。几乎没有证据表明青少年时期父母住房财富变化存在传递。在考察机制时，我们发现父母在儿童早期和中期住房财富变化导致成年子女的住房拥有率、教育程度和收入适度增加。然而，收入和受教育程度只能解释这些时期父母财富收益代际传递的20-30%。我们认为，童年时期父母住房财富变化的传递在很大程度上是由未观测到的家庭环境和父母行为的变化驱动的，这些变化传递给子女并塑造了他们成年后的储蓄行为。

No. 8NBER w31668

Incentivizing Innovation in Open Source: Evidence from the GitHub Sponsors Program

激励开源创新：来自GitHub赞助者计划的证据

Annamaria Conti, Vansh Gupta, Jorge Guzman, Maria P. Roche

Programs：PR；JEL：J24, L86, O3, O31, O36

Abstract

Open source is key to innovation, but we know little about how to incentivize it. In this paper, we examine the impact of a program providing monetary incentives to motivate innovators to contribute to open source. The Sponsors program was introduced by GitHub in May 2019 and enabled organizations and individuals alike to reward developers for their open source work on the platform. To study this program, we collect fine-grained data on about 100,000 GitHub users, their activities, and sponsorship events. Using a difference-in-differences approach, we document two main effects. The first is that developers who opted into the program, which does not entail receiving a financial reward, increased their output after the program’s launch. The second is that the actual receipt of sponsorship has a long-lasting negative effect on innovation, as measured by new repository creation, regardless of the amount of money received. We estimate a similar decline in other community-oriented tasks, but not in coding effort. While the program’s net effect on users’ innovative output appears to be positive, our study shows that receiving an extrinsic reward may crowd out developers’ intrinsic motivation, diverting their effort away from community and service-oriented activities on open source.

中文摘要

开源是创新的关键，但我们对其激励方式知之甚少。在本文中，我们考察了一个为激励创新者为开源做贡献而提供货币激励的计划的影响。GitHub于2019年5月推出了赞助者计划，使组织和个人都能奖励开发者在平台上的开源工作。为研究该计划，我们收集了约10万GitHub用户、其活动及赞助事件的细粒度数据。使用双重差分方法，我们记录了两个主要效应。第一，选择加入该计划（这并不涉及获得财务奖励）的开发者在计划启动后增加了产出。第二，实际获得赞助对创新（以新仓库创建衡量）产生了持久的负面影响，无论收到的金额多少。我们估计在其他面向社区的任务中也出现了类似的下降，但在编码努力方面没有。尽管该计划对用户创新产出的净效应似乎是正的，但我们的研究表明，接受外在奖励可能会挤出开发者的内在动机，将他们的努力从开源社区和服务导向的活动转移开。

No. 9NBER w31667

Ending Pay for PBM Performance: Consequences for Prescription Drug Prices, Utilization, and Government Spending

终止按绩效支付药品福利管理：对处方药价格、使用及政府支出的影响

Casey B. Mulligan

Programs：IO, LE, PE；JEL：D43, D71, I13, J24, L14, L51

Abstract

Proposed “delinking” legislation would prohibit Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBMs) from being remunerated based on the rebates and discounts they negotiate for drug insurance plans serving Medicare beneficiaries. This policy would significantly change drug pricing and utilization and shift billions of dollars annually from patients and taxpayers to drug manufacturers and retail pharmacy companies. Annual federal spending on Medicare Part D premiums would increase $3 billion to $10 billion plus any concomitant increase in Medicare subsidies for out-of-pocket expenses. All of these consequences stem from the fact that PBMs are hired to obtain rebates and discounts but would no longer be compensated based on their results. The quantitative estimates utilize a large body of economic research showing how much “pay for performance” matters for economic outcomes. The price-theoretic models also account for various market frictions and imperfections including market power, coordination costs, tax distortions, and incomplete innovation incentives.

中文摘要

拟议的“脱钩”立法将禁止药品福利管理公司（PBM）根据其为服务医疗保险受益人的药品保险计划协商的返利和折扣获得报酬。这项政策将显著改变药品定价和使用模式，并每年将数十亿美元从患者和纳税人转移至药品制造商和零售药房公司。联邦在医疗保险D部分保费上的年度支出将增加30亿至100亿美元，同时医疗保险对自付费用的补贴也可能随之增加。所有这些后果源于一个事实：PBM被雇佣来获取返利和折扣，但将不再根据其业绩获得报酬。定量估计利用了大量经济研究，这些研究表明“按绩效付费”对经济结果的重要性。价格理论模型还考虑了各种市场摩擦和不完善因素，包括市场势力、协调成本、税收扭曲和不完全的创新激励。

How Credible is the Credibility Revolution?

可信性革命有多可信？

Kevin Lang

Programs：CH, DEV, ED, LS, PE；JEL：A10, C12

Abstract

When economists analyze a well-conducted RCT or natural experiment and find a statistically significant effect, they conclude the null of no effect is unlikely to be true. But how frequently is this conclusion warranted? The answer depends on the proportion of tested nulls that are true and the power of the tests. I model the distribution of t-statistics in leading economics journals. Using my preferred model, 65% of narrowly rejected null hypotheses and 41% of all rejected null hypotheses with |t|<10 are likely to be false rejections. For the null to have only a .05 probability of being true requires a t of 5.48.

中文摘要

当经济学家分析一项设计良好的随机对照试验或自然实验并发现统计显著效应时，他们得出结论认为无效应原假设不太可能成立。但这一结论在多大程度上是合理的？答案取决于被检验的原假设中真实的比例以及检验的统计功效。我对顶级经济学期刊中t统计量的分布进行了建模。使用我偏好的模型，65%的勉强拒绝的原假设和41%的所有被拒绝且|t|<10的原假设很可能是错误拒绝。要使原假设为真的概率仅为0.05，需要t值达到5.48。

No. 11NBER w31665

Wage and Earnings Inequality Between and Within Occupations: The Role of Labor Supply

职业间与职业内的工资和收入不平等：劳动供给的作用

Andrés Erosa, Luisa Fuster, Gueorgui Kambourov, Richard Rogerson

Programs：EFG；JEL：E20, J2, J3

Abstract

We document systematic differences in wage and earnings inequality between and within occupations and show that these differences are intimately related to systematic differences in labor supply across occupations. We then develop a variant of a Roy model in which earnings are a non-linear function of hours, with the extent of this non-linearity differing across occupations. In our theory, the interplay between heterogeneity in tastes for leisure and occupational differences in non-linearities affects the sorting of workers. Moreover, this interplay is crucial to account for the facts on the distributions of hours, wages, and earnings within and across occupations.

中文摘要

我们记录了职业间和职业内工资与收入不平等的系统性差异，并表明这些差异与职业间劳动供给的系统性差异密切相关。然后，我们开发了一个罗伊模型的变体，其中收入是工时的非线性函数，且这种非线性程度在不同职业间存在差异。在我们的理论中，休闲偏好的异质性与职业间非线性的差异相互作用，影响了工人的分类。此外，这种相互作用对于解释职业内部和跨职业的工时、工资和收入分布的事实至关重要。

No. 12NBER w31664

Portfolio Management in Private Equity

私募股权中的投资组合管理

Gregory W. Brown, Celine Yue Fei, David T. Robinson

Programs：CF, PR；JEL：G10, G11, G2, G20, G24

Abstract

General Partners (GPs) in private equity face a trade-off between focusing their skills and effort on fewer investments to earn higher returns, or investing more broadly to reduce risk through diversification. A Cognitive View of Policing

Oeindrila Dube, Sandy Jo MacArthur, Anuj K. Shah

Programs：LE, LS, POL；JEL：C91, C93, D03, D91, J08, K40, K42

Abstract

What causes adverse policing outcomes, such as excessive uses of force and unnecessary arrests? Prevailing explanations focus on problematic officers or deficient regulations and oversight. Here, we introduce a new, overlooked perspective. We suggest that the cognitive demands inherent in policing can undermine officer decision-making. Unless officers are prepared for these demands, they may jump to conclusions too quickly without fully considering alternative ways of seeing a situation. This can lead to adverse policing outcomes. To test this perspective, we created a new training that teaches officers to more deliberately consider different ways of interpreting the situations they encounter. We evaluated this training using a randomized controlled trial with 2,070 officers from the Chicago Police Department. In a series of lab assessments, we find that treated officers were significantly more likely to consider a wider range of evidence and develop more explanations for subjects' actions. Critically, we also find that training affected officer performance in the field, leading to reductions in uses of force, discretionary arrests, and arrests of Black civilians. Meanwhile, officer activity levels remained unchanged, and trained officers were less likely to be injured on duty. Our results highlight the value of considering the cognitive aspects of policing and demonstrate the power of using behaviorally informed approaches to improve officer decision-making and policing outcomes.

中文摘要

是什么导致了不良的警务结果，例如过度使用武力和不必要的逮捕？主流的解释聚焦于有问题的警官或监管与监督的不足。在此，我们引入一个被忽视的新视角。我们认为，警务工作固有的认知需求可能损害警官的决策能力。除非警官对这些需求有所准备，否则他们可能在没有充分考虑理解情境的其他方式的情况下，过快地下结论。这可能导致不良的警务结果。为了检验这一视角，我们创建了一项新的培训，教导警官更审慎地考虑解读他们所遇到情境的不同方式。我们通过一项随机对照试验评估了这项培训，试验对象为来自芝加哥警察局的2070名警官。在一系列实验室评估中，我们发现接受培训的警官更有可能考虑更广泛的证据，并对受试者的行为提出更多解释。关键的是，我们还发现培训影响了警官在现场的表现，导致使用武力、酌情逮捕以及逮捕黑人平民的情况减少。与此同时，警官的活动水平保持不变，且接受培训的警官在执勤时受伤的可能性更低。我们的结果凸显了考虑警务认知方面的价值，并展示了使用行为知情方法来改善警官决策和警务结果的力量。
