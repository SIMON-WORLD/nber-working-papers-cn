The Debauchery of Currency and Inflation: Chile, 1970-1973

货币与通胀的堕落：智利，1970-1973

Sebastian Edwards

Programs：IFM, ITI；JEL：E31, E40, E52, F38, F42

Abstract

In this essay, I analyze Salvador Allende’s economic policies in Chile during the early 1970s. I argue that the explosion of inflation during his administration (above 1,500% on a six-month annualized measure) was predictable, and that the government’s response to it, through massive and strict price controls, generated acute macroeconomic imbalances. I postulate that the combination of runaway inflation, shortages, and black markets generated major disaffection among the middle class and that that unhappiness reduced the support for the Unidad Popular government.

中文摘要

在这篇文章中，我分析了萨尔瓦多·阿连德在20世纪70年代初期的智利经济政策。我认为，他执政期间通胀的爆发（按六个月年化衡量超过1500%）是可以预测的，而政府通过大规模且严格的价格管制对此做出的反应，导致了严重的宏观经济失衡。我假设，失控的通胀、短缺和黑市相结合，在中产阶级中引发了重大不满，这种不满情绪削弱了对人民团结政府（Unidad Popular）的支持。

No. 2NBER w31889

Long-term Care in Denmark

丹麦的长期护理

Mette Gørtz, Bent Jesper Christensen, Nabanita Datta Gupta

Programs：AG；JEL：H51, I1

Abstract

The population is aging in Denmark, as in many other countries, due to increasing life expectancy and a low total fertility rate. This potentially puts the Danish welfare state under pressure. This paper discusses the demographic and socioeconomic situation of the elderly in Denmark, focusing on the health status and financial situation of the elderly, and the provision of long-term care (LTC). We rely on a combination of survey data, mainly from the Survey of Health and Retirement in Europe (SHARE), and high-quality register data covering the entire Danish population. We find that a large fraction of the elderly is in good health, but that those in the older age group, 85+, face considerably more functional limitations in daily living. One in three of the elderly receives some form of long-term care, and more than half the 85+ group. The paper further identifies a number of current challenges regarding organization of the long-term care sector, including recruitment of personnel for health care and LTC. Finally, the paper sheds light on the extent of informal care provided by family and friends. While informal care is offered voluntarily and is generally unpaid, it represents a substantial opportunity cost to society.

中文摘要

与许多其他国家一样，由于预期寿命延长和低总和生育率，丹麦人口正在老龄化。这可能给丹麦福利国家带来压力。本文讨论了丹麦老年人的人口和社会经济状况，重点关注老年人的健康状况、财务状况以及长期护理（LTC）的提供。我们依赖于调查数据（主要来自欧洲健康、老龄化和退休调查（SHARE））以及覆盖整个丹麦人口的高质量登记数据的组合。我们发现，很大一部分老年人健康状况良好，但85岁及以上的高龄群体在日常生活中的功能限制明显更多。三分之一的老年人接受某种形式的长期护理，而85岁及以上群体中这一比例超过一半。本文进一步指出了长期护理部门组织方面当前面临的若干挑战，包括医疗保健和长期护理人员的招聘。最后，本文揭示了家人和朋友提供的非正式护理的程度。虽然非正式护理是自愿提供且通常是无偿的，但它对社会构成了巨大的机会成本。

Heterogeneous and Racialized Impacts of State Incarceration Policies on Birth Outcomes in the U.S.

美国州级监禁政策对出生结局的异质性和种族化影响

Courtney Boen, Elizabeth Bair, Hedwig Lee, Atheendar Venkataramani

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I10, I14, I18, K4

Abstract

While state incarceration policies have received much attention in research on the causes of mass incarceration in the U.S., their roles in shaping population health and health disparities remain largely unknown. We examine the impacts of two signature state incarceration policies adopted during the “tough on crime” era of the 1990s—three strikes and truth in sentencing—on Black and White birth outcomes. Using a difference-in-differences event study research design that models the dynamic impacts of these policies over time, we find that these policies had opposing effects on birth outcomes. We find that birth weight outcomes—including mean birth weight and low birth weight—for Black infants worsened markedly in the year three strikes policies were adopted. By contrast, birth outcomes for Black and White infants gradually improved after truth in sentencing policies were adopted. The discordant findings point to distinct, countervailing mechanisms by which sentencing policies can affect population health. We provide suggestive evidence that three strikes policies adversely impacted Black birth outcomes through affective mechanisms, by inducing highly racialized, stigmatizing public discourse around the time of policy adoption, while truth in sentencing likely impacted birth outcomes via material mechanisms, namely gradually reductions in community incarceration and crime rates. Altogether, these findings point to the need to further interrogate state criminal legal system policies for their impacts on population health, considering whether, how, and for whom these policies result in health impacts.

中文摘要

尽管州级监禁政策在关于美国大规模监禁原因的研究中受到广泛关注，但它们在塑造人口健康和健康差异方面的作用仍然很大程度上未知。我们考察了20世纪90年代“严打犯罪”时代通过的两项标志性州级监禁政策——“三振出局法”和“真实量刑”——对黑人和白人出生结局的影响。使用一种模拟这些政策随时间动态影响的双重差分事件研究设计，我们发现这些政策对出生结局产生了相反的影响。我们发现，在“三振出局法”通过的那一年，黑人婴儿的出生体重结局——包括平均出生体重和低出生体重——显著恶化。相比之下，在“真实量刑”政策通过后，黑人和白人婴儿的出生结局逐渐改善。这些不一致的发现指向了量刑政策影响人口健康的独特且相互抵消的机制。我们提供了提示性证据，表明“三振出局法”通过情感机制对黑人出生结局产生了不利影响，即在政策通过前后引发了高度种族化、污名化的公共话语；而“真实量刑”可能通过物质机制影响出生结局，即社区监禁率和犯罪率逐渐降低。总的来说，这些发现表明，需要进一步审视州刑事法律体系政策对人口健康的影响，考虑这些政策是否、如何以及为谁带来了健康影响。

No. 4NBER w31887

Pandemic-Era Inflation Drivers and Global Spillovers

疫情时期的通胀驱动因素与全球溢出效应

Julian di Giovanni, Ṣebnem Kalemli-Özcan, Alvaro Silva, Muhammed A. Yildirim

Programs：EFG, IFM；JEL：F40

Abstract

We develop a multi-country, multi-sector New Keynesian model to examine the drivers of global inflation during the pandemic and recovery periods. The model incorporates both country-sector-specific and aggregate shocks that propagate through international trade and production networks, leading to global demand-supply imbalances. Production complementarities raise non-labor costs, resulting in widespread sectoral cost-push inflation, even though the original negative labor supply shock is heterogeneous across country-sectors. Flexible exchange rate regimes absorb cross-country monetary policy differences, curbing global demand reallocation and the inflationary spillovers relative to fixed-rate regimes. The interaction of sectoral supply and aggregate demand shocks is the key to match the inflation experience of several countries, as the sectoral shocks spread through the global production network.

中文摘要

我们开发了一个多国、多部门的新凯恩斯模型，以考察疫情期间和复苏时期全球通胀的驱动因素。该模型包含了国家-部门特定的冲击和总体冲击，这些冲击通过国际贸易和生产网络传播，导致全球供需失衡。生产互补性提高了非劳动力成本，导致广泛的部门成本推动型通胀，尽管最初的负劳动力供给冲击在不同国家-部门间是异质的。与固定汇率制度相比，浮动汇率制度吸收了跨国货币政策差异，抑制了全球需求再配置和通胀溢出效应。部门供给冲击与总需求冲击的相互作用是匹配多个国家通胀经历的关键，因为部门冲击通过全球生产网络传播。

The Extent of the Market for Early American Bank Notes

美国早期银行券的市场范围

Howard Bodenhorn

Programs：DAE；JEL：N21

Abstract

How far did antebellum bank notes travel? Up to now, we did not know. Using previously overlooked data on interbank holdings of bank notes and the records of a small-time note broker, I find that most bank notes circulated within about 50 miles of the issuing banks. Few notes were observed from as far as 200 miles away. Several studies of secondary markets for privately issued currencies assume that notes moved across vast geographic space, but these new findings suggest that we may need new models of bank note pricing and the efficiency of relatively unfettered markets in private currencies.

中文摘要

内战前的银行券流通范围有多广？到目前为止，我们并不清楚。利用之前被忽视的银行间银行券持有数据以及一位小型票据经纪人的记录，我发现大多数银行券在距发行银行约50英里的范围内流通。很少有票据在远达200英里的地方被观察到。几项关于私人发行货币二级市场的研究假设票据跨越广阔的地理空间流动，但这些新发现表明，我们可能需要关于银行券定价以及相对不受约束的私人货币市场效率的新模型。

Importing the Opioid Crisis? International Trade and Fentanyl Overdoses

进口阿片危机？国际贸易与芬太尼过量致死

Timothy J. Moore, William W. Olney, Benjamin Hansen

Programs：EH, ITI, LE, PE；JEL：F1, F6, I1, K4

Abstract

The U.S. opioid crisis is now driven by fentanyl, a powerful synthetic opioid that currently accounts for 90% of all opioid deaths. Fentanyl is smuggled from abroad, with little evidence of how this happens. We find a positive relationship between state-level imports and drug overdoses, which is consistent with fentanyl smuggling occurring via legal trade flows. This relationship accounts for 14,000-20,000 deaths per year, and is not explained by geographic differences in “deaths of despair,” general demand for opioids, or import competition. Our results suggest that fentanyl smuggling via imports is pervasive and a key determinant of recent opioid problems.

中文摘要

美国阿片危机目前由芬太尼驱动，这种强效合成阿片类药物目前占所有阿片类药物死亡人数的90%。芬太尼从国外走私入境，但关于其如何发生的证据很少。我们发现州级进口与药物过量之间存在正相关关系，这与芬太尼通过合法贸易流进行走私的情况一致。这种关系每年导致14,000至20,000人死亡，并且不能用“绝望之死”、对阿片类药物的普遍需求或进口竞争的地理差异来解释。我们的结果表明，通过进口走私芬太尼是普遍现象，并且是近期阿片问题的一个关键决定因素。

Insurance Coverage and Provision of Opioid Treatment: Evidence from Medicare

保险覆盖与阿片类药物治疗的提供：来自医疗保险的证据

Denis Agniel, Jonathan H. Cantor, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Kosali I. Simon, Erin Taylor

Programs：AG, EH, PE；JEL：I1, I11, I18

Abstract

Opioid overdose deaths in older adults increased substantially over the past two decades. This increase occurred despite the availability of effective treatments. Methadone, one of three medications approved for opioid use disorder (OUD) treatment, was not covered by Medicare — the primary insurer of older Americans — for OUD until 2020. We study the response of opioid treatment programs (OTPs), the only healthcare providers that can dispense methadone for OUD in the U.S., to this policy change using administrative data and a difference-in-differences framework. We examine provider acceptance of Medicare payment and the number of treatment episodes, before and after the policy change, in OTPs relative to other substance use disorder treatment facilities. Our findings show a surge in Medicare acceptance by OTPs and an increase in the number of treatment episodes post-policy, signalling the importance of insurance for OUD treatment provision.

中文摘要

在过去二十年中，老年人因阿片类药物过量死亡的人数大幅增加。尽管存在有效的治疗方法，这一增长仍然发生。美沙酮是获批用于治疗阿片类药物使用障碍的三种药物之一，但直到2020年，医疗保险——美国老年人的主要保险机构——才将其纳入阿片类药物使用障碍治疗的覆盖范围。我们利用行政数据和双重差分框架，研究了阿片类药物治疗项目——美国唯一可以配发美沙酮用于治疗阿片类药物使用障碍的医疗服务提供者——对这一政策变化的反应。我们考察了在政策变化前后，阿片类药物治疗项目相对于其他物质使用障碍治疗机构，在提供者接受医疗保险支付以及治疗次数方面的变化。我们的研究结果显示，政策实施后，阿片类药物治疗项目接受医疗保险的比例激增，治疗次数也有所增加，这表明保险对于阿片类药物使用障碍治疗的提供至关重要。

No. 8NBER w31883

Ten Facts about Son Preference in India

关于印度重男轻女偏好的十个事实

Seema Jayachandran

Programs：CH, DEV；JEL：J13, O12

Abstract

This article discusses son preference in India, including both greater investment in sons and the fertility preference for sons. Regarding differential investment, I focus on child health and show that gender gaps in inputs and outcomes have narrowed in recent years. Nonetheless, girls remain disadvantaged in important ways, and making health services free is unlikely to be enough to close these remaining gaps. In addition to gender gaps, there are also stark health gaps between eldest sons, whom parents favor, and other sons. Fertility preferences likewise center on eldest sons. The desire to have at least one son — who can fill that eldest son role in the family — drives the skewed sex ratio, and this preference shows little sign of abating. In fact, the downward trend in family size is exacerbating how the desire for a son translates into sex-selection. Families’ quest for a son also imposes collateral damage on sisters’ health. The policy challenge, particularly around reducing the desire for sons, is large. Empowering women is not a panacea, and offering financial incentives to have daughters risks further concentrating girls in poorer families. While we do not know which policies will erase the disadvantages girls face, some that might advance this goal are public pensions as an alternative to old-age support from sons, increased delivery of health services through schools, and norm-change interventions that aim to increase the intrinsic value that Indian families place on girls.

中文摘要

本文讨论了印度的重男轻女偏好，包括对儿子更多的投资以及对儿子的生育偏好。关于差异化投资，我重点关注儿童健康，并表明投入和结果方面的性别差距近年来已经缩小。尽管如此，女孩在某些重要方面仍然处于不利地位，而让医疗服务免费不太可能足以弥合这些剩余的差距。除了性别差距之外，父母偏爱的长子与其他儿子之间也存在明显的健康差距。生育偏好同样集中在长子身上。拥有至少一个儿子——能够承担家庭中长子角色的儿子——的愿望导致了失衡的性别比例，而且这种偏好几乎没有减弱的迹象。事实上，家庭规模下降的趋势正在加剧生儿子的愿望如何转化为性别选择。家庭对儿子的追求也对姐妹的健康造成了附带损害。政策挑战，特别是在减少生儿子愿望方面，是巨大的。赋予女性权力并非万能药，而为生女儿提供经济激励则有风险，会使女孩进一步集中在较贫困的家庭。虽然我们不知道哪些政策能够消除女孩面临的不利因素，但一些可能推进这一目标的政策包括：提供公共养老金作为儿子赡养父母的替代方案，通过学校增加提供医疗服务，以及旨在提高印度家庭对女孩内在价值的规范改变干预措施。

Long-term Care Around the World

全球长期照护

Jonathan Gruber, Kathleen M. McGarry, Charles Hanzel

Programs：AG, EH, PE；JEL：H4, I10, I13, I18

Abstract

The developed world is in the midst of an enormous demographic transition, with life expectancy increasing and fertility falling, leading to a rapid aging of the population. This trend has critical implications for long-term care around the world. This paper serves as the introduction to a volume that brings together experts from ten countries to compare long term care systems. We find a number of important similarities: only a minority of those elderly receiving assistance rely solely on formal care (i.e. care in an institution or through paid home care) while the majority of care is provided informally by family or other unpaid caregivers; without public support, the cost of long-term care would be beyond the financial means of a large fraction of the elderly in each country, particularly for the oldest and most disabled; and the public sector bears the majority of the costs of formal long-term care in every country. There are, however, important differences across countries, particularly in the extent to which formal care is delivered in institutions or at home, and in the division between the use of formal and informal care. Given the importance of informal care across all countries studied, we conclude that any estimate of the social costs of long-term care must account for the implicit costs of informal care. In undertaking such an evaluation of informal care, we find that it comprises at least one-third of all long-term care spending for all countries studied, with an average portion of nearly fifty percent.

中文摘要

发达国家正处于一场巨大的人口结构转型之中，预期寿命延长而生育率下降，导致人口迅速老龄化。这一趋势对全球长期照护具有关键影响。本文作为一本汇集了来自十个国家的专家以比较长期照护体系的著作的引言。我们发现了一些重要的相似之处：在接受援助的老年人中，只有少数人完全依赖正式照护（即在机构中照护或通过付费家庭照护），而大多数照护是由家人或其他无偿照护者提供的非正式照护；如果没有公共支持，长期照护的费用将超出每个国家大部分老年人的经济能力，尤其是最年长和残疾程度最重的人群；在每个国家，公共部门承担了正式长期照护的大部分费用。然而，各国之间存在重要差异，特别是在正式照护是在机构中还是在家庭中提供的程度，以及正式照护与非正式照护使用之间的划分方面。鉴于非正式照护在所有被研究国家中的重要性，我们得出结论，任何对长期照护社会成本的估算都必须考虑非正式照护的隐性成本。在对非正式照护进行此类评估时，我们发现，在所有被研究国家中，它至少占长期照护总支出的三分之一，平均比例接近百分之五十。

Long-term Care in the United States

美国的长期照护

Jonathan Gruber, Kathleen M. McGarry

Programs：AG, EH, PE；JEL：H4, H50, I13, I18

Abstract

The population of the United States, as with the rest of the world, is aging rapidly, with the most rapid growth occurring among the age 85 and older population, those who rely most on long-term care. In this chapter, we review the delivery and financing of long-term care in the U.S. We show that the resources of most elderly in the U.S. are insufficient to finance these ongoing long-term care needs and the public sector finances the majority of long-term care spending. At the same time, informal care plays a critical role, with the elderly at every age and every disability level receiving informal care more frequently than formal care. Indeed, when properly valued, informal care accounts for more than one-third of the nearly 2 percent of U.S. GDP devoted to long-term care.

中文摘要

美国人口与世界其他地区一样正在迅速老龄化，增长最快的是85岁及以上人口，他们是最依赖长期照护的群体。在本章中，我们回顾了美国长期照护的提供和融资情况。我们表明，美国大多数老年人的资源不足以支付这些持续的长期照护需求，而公共部门为大部分长期照护支出提供了资金。同时，非正式照护发挥着关键作用，每个年龄和每个残疾水平的老年人接受非正式照护的频率都高于正式照护。事实上，如果进行适当估值，非正式照护占美国GDP近2%的长期照护支出的三分之一以上。

No. 11NBER w31880

The Power of Proximity to Coworkers

与同事邻近的力量

Natalia Emanuel, Emma Harrington, Amanda Pallais

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：J16, J24, M15, M53, M54, O33, R23

Abstract

How does proximity to coworkers affect training and productivity? We study software engineers at a Fortune 500 firm from 2019 to 2024, leveraging two shocks to proximity: (i) the office closures in 2020 and (ii) the subsequent return-to-office mandates in 2022 and 2023. In both cases, co-located teams experienced bigger changes in proximity than distributed ones, facilitating difference-in-differences designs. We find that sitting near teammates increases coding feedback by 18.3% and improves code quality. Gains are concentrated among less-tenured and younger employees, who are building human capital. However, there is a tradeoff: experienced engineers write less code when sitting near teammates. In national US data, we find evidence that the rise of remote work has had scarring effects on young college graduates. In remotable jobs, young graduates’ unemployment rate increased relative to older graduates’ post-pandemic (2022−2024) compared to pre-pandemic (2017−2019), a pattern we do not observe in non-remotable jobs.

中文摘要

与同事的邻近程度如何影响培训和生产力？我们研究了2019年至2024年间一家财富500强公司的软件工程师。我们利用了两次对同地办公的冲击：（i）2020年的办公室关闭和（ii）随后的重返办公室要求。在这两种情况下，同地办公的团队比分散的团队经历了更大的邻近性变化，这有助于进行双重差分设计。我们发现，坐在队友附近可使编码反馈增加18.3%，并提高代码质量。收益集中在资历较浅和较年轻的员工身上，他们正在积累人力资本。然而，存在一个权衡：经验丰富的工程师在坐在同事附近时编写的代码较少。在全美数据中，我们发现了暗示性证据，表明远程工作的兴起对年轻大学毕业生产生了疤痕效应：在可远程的工作中，他们的失业率相对于年长毕业生仍然较高，而这种模式在不可远程的工作中并未出现。

Supply and Demand and the Term Structure of Interest Rates

供给与需求及利率期限结构

Robin Greenwood, Samuel Hanson, Dimitri Vayanos

Programs：AP, IFM, ME；JEL：E4, E40, E49, E52, E7, G02, G1, G10, G11, G12

Abstract

We survey the growing literature emphasizing the role that supply-and-demand forces play in shaping the term structure of interest rates. Our starting point is the Vayanos and Vila (2009, 2021) model of the term structure of default-free bond yields, which we present in both discrete and continuous time. The key friction in the model is that the bond market is partially segmented from other financial markets: the prices of short-rate and bond supply risk are set by specialized bond arbitrageurs who must absorb shocks to the supply and demand for bonds from other “preferred-habitat” agents. We discuss extensions of this model in the context of default-free bonds and other asset classes.

我们综述了日益增长的文献，这些文献强调了供需力量在塑造利率期限结构中的作用。我们的出发点是Vayanos和Vila（2009, 2021）的无违约债券收益率期限结构模型，我们以离散时间和连续时间两种形式呈现该模型。模型中的关键摩擦在于债券市场与其他金融市场部分分割：短期利率和债券供给风险的价格由专门的债券套利者设定，他们必须吸收来自其他“偏好栖息地”代理人的债券供给和需求冲击。我们讨论了该模型在无违约债券和其他资产类别背景下的扩展。

Recent Developments in Financial Risk and the Real Economy

金融风险与实体经济的最新发展

Ian Dew-Becker, Stefano Giglio

Programs：AP, DAE, EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E10, G10, G12, G13, N1, N2

Abstract

This paper reviews recent developments in macro and finance on the relationship between financial risk and the real economy. We focus on three specific topics: the term structure of uncertainty, time variation - and specifically the long-term decline - in the variance risk premium, and time variation in conditional skewness. We also introduce two new data series: implied volatility from one-day options on grains for the period 1906-1936, and on cliquet options, which provide insurance against single-day crashes on the S&P 500, both of which give some context to the recent rise in trade in extremely short-dated options. Finally, we discuss new avenues for future research.

Combinatorial Discrete Choice: Theory and Application to Multinational Production

组合离散选择：理论与跨国生产应用

Costas Arkolakis, Fabian Eckert, Rowan Shi

Programs：IFM, ITI；JEL：F12, F21, F6

Abstract

Discrete choice problems with complementarities among options quickly grow infeasible to solve, since they generically require evaluating all combinations of choices. We develop a solution method that applies whenever choices are weakly complementary or substitutable, using the implied choice monotonicity to discard suboptimal combinations without computing their payoff. It is orders of magnitude faster than existing approaches, finds the global solution, and extends to heterogeneous-agent settings. Using our method, we calibrate a general equilibrium model of multinational firms selecting global production locations to show that complementarities among locations can amplify, dampen, or even reverse the welfare gains from multinational production.

中文摘要

具有选项间互补性的离散选择问题会迅速变得难以求解，因为通常需要评估所有选择组合。我们开发了一种适用于选项呈弱互补或弱替代关系的求解方法，利用隐含的选择单调性来剔除次优组合，而无需计算其收益。该方法比现有方法快数个数量级，能够找到全局最优解，并可扩展至异质性主体设定。利用该方法，我们校准了一个跨国企业选择全球生产地点的一般均衡模型，以展示地点间的互补性可能放大、减弱甚至逆转跨国生产带来的福利收益。

International Trade Responses to Labor Market Regulations

国际贸易对劳动力市场规制的反应

Mathilde Muñoz

Programs：ITI, LS, PE；JEL：F14, H25, J23, J31, J8

Abstract

This paper studies how differences in labor market regulations shape countries' comparative advantage in the cross-border provision of labor-intensive services, using administrative data in Europe for the last two decades. I exploit exogenous variation in labor taxes and minimum wages faced by exporting firms engaged in a large European trade program. Firms from different countries compete to supply the same physical service in the same location but their employees are subject to different payroll taxes and minimum wages. These rules varied across countries, sectors, and over time. Reduced-form country case-studies as well as model-implied gravity estimates show evidence of large trade responses to lower labor taxes and minimum wages, with an elasticity that is around one. The Bolkestein directive, by exempting foreign firms from all labor regulations in the destination country, would have doubled exports of physical services from Eastern European countries, rationalizing the wave of protests in high-wage countries that led to the withdrawal of the proposal.

中文摘要

本文利用欧洲过去二十年的行政数据，研究劳动力市场规制的差异如何塑造各国在跨境提供劳动密集型服务方面的比较优势。我利用参与大型欧洲贸易计划的出口企业所面临的劳动税和最低工资的外生变化。来自不同国家的企业在同一地点竞争提供相同的实体服务，但其雇员需遵守不同的工资税和最低工资标准。这些规则因国家、行业和时间而异。简化形式的国别案例研究以及模型隐含的引力估计显示，贸易对较低的劳动税和最低工资有显著反应，弹性约为1。博尔克斯坦指令通过豁免外国企业在目的地国的所有劳动法规，将使东欧国家的实体服务出口翻倍，这解释了高工资国家导致该提案撤回的抗议浪潮。

The Economics of Long-Term Care in Canada

加拿大长期护理的经济学

Kevin S. Milligan, Tammy Schirle

Programs：AG, EH；JEL：H51, I13, I18, J18

Abstract

This paper contributes a broad overview of the Canadian long-term care system. Taking an economist’s viewpoint, we bring together supply and demand factors to provide an economic analysis of the current and future path for long-term care. Like other OECD countries, the coming demographic wave of older baby boomers will put tremendous stress on the existing financial, organizational, and physical structures of the long-term care system. Unlike other OECD countries, Canada’s system is organized almost entirely at the subnational level, with provinces and territories having by far the largest role in financing and regulating long-term care. We provide institutional and empirical details on the evolution and future of Canada's long-term care system.

Late Bloomers: The Aggregate Implications of Getting Education Later in Life

大器晚成者：晚年接受教育的总体影响

Zsófia L. Bárány, Moshe Buchinsky, Pauline Corblet

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：I20, I26, J30

Abstract

It is generally agreed upon that most individuals who acquire a college degree do so in their early 20s. Despite this consensus, we show that in the US from the 1930 birth cohort onwards a large fraction – around 20% – of college graduates obtained their degree after age 30. We explore the implications of this phenomenon. First, we show that these so-called late bloomers have significantly contributed to the narrowing of gender and racial gaps in the college share, despite the general widening of the racial gap. Second, late bloomers are responsible for more than half of the increase in the aggregate college share from 1960 onwards. Finally, we show that the returns to having a college degree vary depending on the age at graduation. Ignoring the existence of late bloomers therefore leads to a significant underestimation of the returns to college education for those finishing college in their early 20s.

中文摘要

人们普遍认为，大多数获得大学学位的人是在20岁出头完成的。尽管存在这一共识，我们表明，在美国从1930年出生队列开始，很大一部分——约20%——的大学毕业生在30岁之后才获得学位。我们探讨了这一现象的影响。首先，我们表明，这些所谓的大器晚成者显著促进了大学学历占比中性别和种族差距的缩小，尽管种族差距总体上在扩大。其次，大器晚成者贡献了自1960年以来大学学历占比总增长的一半以上。最后，我们表明，拥有大学学位的回报因毕业年龄而异。因此，忽视大器晚成者的存在会导致严重低估那些在20岁出头完成大学教育者的大学教育回报。

The Environmental Impacts of Protected Area Policy

保护区政策的环境影响

Mathias Reynaert, Eduardo A. Souza-Rodrigues, Arthur A. van Benthem

Programs：EEE；JEL：Q23, Q24, Q57, R14

Abstract

The world has pledged to protect 30 percent of its land and waters by 2030 to halt the rapid deterioration of critical ecosystems. We summarize the state of knowledge about the impacts of protected area policies, with a focus on deforestation and vegetation cover. We discuss critical issues around data and measurement, identify the most commonly-used empirical methods, and summarize empirical evidence across multiple regions of the world. In most cases, protection has had at most a modest impact on forest cover, with stronger effects in areas that face pressure of economic development. We then identify several open areas for research to advance our understanding of the effectiveness of protected area policies: the use of promising recent econometric advancements, shifting focus to direct measures of biodiversity, filling the knowledge gap on the effect of protected area policy in advanced economies, investigating the long-run impacts of protection, and understanding its equilibrium effects.

A Model of Behavioral Manipulation

行为操纵模型

Daron Acemoglu, Ali Makhdoumi, Azarakhsh Malekian, Asuman Ozdaglar

Programs：IO, PR；JEL：D83, D90, D91, L86

Abstract

We build a model of online behavioral manipulation driven by AI advances. A platform dynamically offers one of n products to a user who slowly learns product quality. User learning depends on a product’s “glossiness,’ which captures attributes that make products appear more attractive than they are. AI tools enable platforms to learn glossiness and engage in behavioral manipulation. We establish that AI benefits consumers when glossiness is short-lived. In contrast, when glossiness is long-lived, users suffer because of behavioral manipulation. Finally, as the number of products increases, the platform can intensify behavioral manipulation by presenting more low-quality, glossy products.

中文摘要

Geographic Variation in Cesarean Sections in the United States: Trends, Correlates, and Other Interesting Facts

美国剖宫产率的地理差异：趋势、相关因素及其他有趣事实

Sarah Robinson, Heather Royer, David Silver

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I1

Abstract

Analyzing data spanning three decades covering the near universe of births, we study county-level differences in Cesarean section (C-section) rates among first-time mothers of singleton births. Our research reveals persistent geographic variation in C- section rates for both low- and high-risk groups. Counties with elevated C-section rates consistently perform more C-sections across mothers at all levels of appropriateness for the procedure. These elevated rates of C-section in high C-section counties are associated with reduced maternal and infant morbidity. We also find that C-section decisions are less responsive to underlying risks for Black mothers relative to white mothers, suggesting potential welfare-reducing disparities.

中文摘要
