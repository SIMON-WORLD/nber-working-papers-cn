Private Benefits from Public Investment in Climate Adaptation and Resilience

气候适应与韧性公共投资的私人收益

Jacob T. Bradt, Joseph E. Aldy

Programs：EEE；JEL：H22, H23, Q54, Q58

Abstract

Flood protection infrastructure investments, such as Army Corps of Engineers levees, can enhance resilience to flood risks amplified by climate change. We estimate levees’ benefits by exploiting repeat residential property transactions. In areas protected by levees, home values increase 3-4 percent. Levees impose adverse spillover flood risks that reduce home values in nearby areas by 1-5 percent. Capitalized benefits in protected areas are progressive, but adverse spillover impacts are regressive. Capitalized benefits at levee construction do not vary by race, but racial sorting occurs post-construction. The local political economy of levee construction can explain the distribution of winners and losers.

中文摘要

防洪基础设施投资，例如陆军工程兵团的堤坝，可以增强对因气候变化而加剧的洪水风险的抵御能力。我们通过利用重复的住宅房地产交易来估算堤坝的收益。在受堤坝保护的地区，房屋价值上涨了3%至4%。堤坝会带来不利的溢出性洪水风险，使附近地区的房屋价值降低1%至5%。受保护地区的资本化收益是累进性的，但不利的溢出影响是累退性的。堤坝建设时的资本化收益不因种族而异，但建设后会出现种族分选。堤坝建设的地方政治经济学可以解释赢家和输家的分布。

No. 2NBER w33632

Investing in Children to Address the Child Mental Health Crisis

投资儿童以应对儿童心理健康危机

Janet Currie

Programs：CH, EH, LE；JEL：I12, I18

Abstract

The child mental health crisis has been described as the “defining public health crisis of our time.” This article addresses three myths about the crisis: (i) the idea that the crisis is new; (ii) the belief that increases in youth suicide mainly reflect deterioration in children’s underlying mental health; and (iii) the myth that investments in children have little impact on children’s mental health. In fact, the crisis has existed for decades, youth suicides vary asynchronously with other mental health measures and are impacted by external factors such as firearms legislation, and investments can improve child mental health and prevent suicide.

中文摘要

儿童心理健康危机被描述为“我们这个时代决定性的公共卫生危机”。本文探讨了关于该危机的三个迷思：(i) 认为危机是新出现的观点；(ii) 认为青少年自杀率上升主要反映了儿童潜在心理健康状况恶化的信念；以及 (iii) 认为对儿童的投资对儿童心理健康影响甚微的迷思。事实上，这场危机已存在数十年，青少年自杀率与其他心理健康指标呈非同步变化，并受到枪支立法等外部因素的影响，而且投资可以改善儿童心理健康并预防自杀。

No. 3NBER w33631

Where Have All the Good Jobs Gone? Changes in the Geography of Work in the US, 1980-2021

好工作都去哪儿了？美国工作地理分布的变化，1980-2021

Gordon H. Hanson, Enrico Moretti

Programs：LS, PE, PR；JEL：J01, R0

Abstract

We examine changes in the spatial distribution of good jobs across US commuting zones over 1980-2000 and 2000-2021. We define good jobs as those in industries in which full-time workers attain high wages, accounting for individual and regional characteristics. The share of good jobs in manufacturing has plummeted; for college graduates, good jobs have shifted to (mostly tradable) business, professional, and IT services, while for those without a BA they have shifted to (non-tradable) construction. There is strong persistence in where good jobs are located. Over the last four decades, places with larger concentrations of good job industries have tended to hold onto them, consistent with a model of proportional growth. Turning to regional specialization in good job industries, we find evidence of mean reversion. Commuting zones with larger initial concentrations of good jobs have thus seen even faster growth in lower-wage (and mostly non-tradable) services. Changing regional employment patterns are most pronounced among racial minorities and the foreign-born, who are relatively concentrated in fast growing cities of the South and West. Therefore, good job regions today look vastly different than in 1980: they are more centered around human-capital-intensive tradable services, are surrounded by larger concentrations of low-wage, non-tradable industries, and are more demographically diverse.

中文摘要

我们研究了1980-2000年和2000-2021年间美国通勤区好工作空间分布的变化。我们将好工作定义为那些全职工作者在考虑个人和地区特征后能获得高工资的行业。制造业中好工作的份额急剧下降；对于大学毕业生而言，好工作已转向（主要是可贸易的）商业、专业和IT服务业，而对于没有学士学位的人来说，好工作则转向了（不可贸易的）建筑业。好工作所在地点具有很强的持续性。在过去四十年中，好工作行业集中度较高的地区倾向于保持这种集中度，这与比例增长模型一致。转向好工作行业的区域专业化，我们发现了均值回归的证据。因此，最初好工作集中度较高的通勤区，其低工资（且大多不可贸易）服务业增长更快。不断变化的区域就业模式在少数族裔和外国出生者中最为明显，他们相对集中在南部和西部快速发展的城市。因此，今天的好工作区域与1980年相比大相径庭：它们更集中于人力资本密集型可贸易服务业，周围是更集中的低工资、不可贸易行业，并且在人口结构上更加多样化。

No. 4NBER w33630

Experiments: Why, How, and A Users Guide for Producers as well as Consumers

实验：为何、如何，以及为生产者和消费者提供的用户指南

Muriel Niederle

Programs：LS, TWP；JEL：C9

Abstract

This chapter is intended as an introduction to laboratory experiments, when to use, how to evaluate them, why they matter and what are the pitfalls when designing them. I hope that users as well as consumers will find Sections that broaden their views. I start with when an economist might want to run an experiment. I then discuss basic lessons when designing experiments. I introduce a language to start a systematic description of tools we have when designing experiments to show the importance or role of a new model or force in explaining behavior. The penultimate chapter provides an advanced toolkit for running experiments. I end this chapter with my views on pre-registration, pre-analysis plans and the need for replications, robustness tests and extensions.

中文摘要

本章旨在作为实验室实验的导论，介绍何时使用、如何评估、为何重要以及设计时的陷阱。我希望用户和消费者都能找到拓宽视野的章节。我首先从经济学家何时可能想要进行实验开始。然后讨论设计实验时的基本经验教训。我引入一种语言，开始系统描述我们在设计实验时拥有的工具，以展示新模型或力量在解释行为中的重要性或作用。倒数第二章提供了进行实验的高级工具包。我以对预注册、预分析计划以及复制、稳健性检验和扩展需求的看法结束本章。

No. 5NBER w33629

Class Disparities and Discrimination in Traffic Stops and Searches

交通拦截与搜查中的阶级差异与歧视

Benjamin Feigenberg, Conrad Miller

Programs：LE, LS；JEL：K42

Abstract

We examine class disparities and discrimination in police searches and stops using data on traffic stops conducted by Texas Highway Patrol. Low-income motorists are more likely to be searched for contraband, less likely to be found with contraband when searched, and more likely to be stopped for infractions associated with pretext stops. We measure class-based discrimination in searches per potential stop, accounting for both the search and stop margins. Our research design leverages motorists stopped in multiple vehicles conveying different class signals. Motorists are more likely to be searched when stopped in a low-status vehicle, and evidence suggests that they are also more likely to be stopped when driving one. Marginal searches triggered by vehicle status are also less likely to yield contraband when the motorist is low-income. We argue that lower hassle costs associated with arrests of low-income motorists help explain trooper behavior.

中文摘要

我们利用德克萨斯州公路巡逻队进行的交通拦截数据，研究了警察搜查和拦截中的阶级差异与歧视。低收入驾驶者更有可能因违禁品被搜查，被搜查时发现违禁品的可能性更低，并且更有可能因与借口拦截相关的违规行为而被拦停。我们衡量了每次潜在拦截中搜查方面的基于阶级的歧视，同时考虑了搜查和拦截两个环节。我们的研究设计利用了驾驶者在传达不同阶级信号的多辆车辆中被拦停的情况。驾驶者在驾驶低地位车辆时更有可能被搜查，并且有证据表明他们在驾驶此类车辆时也更有可能被拦停。由车辆地位引发的边际搜查在驾驶者低收入时发现违禁品的可能性也更低。我们认为，与逮捕低收入驾驶者相关的较低麻烦成本有助于解释巡警的行为。

No. 6NBER w33628

Hotelling Meets Keynes: Aggregate Adjustment with Spatial Competition and Nominal Rigidity

霍特林遇见凯恩斯：空间竞争与名义刚性下的总量调整

Stephanie Schmitt-Grohé, Martín Uribe

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E31, E32, L13

Abstract

This paper embeds a circular Hotelling model of spatial competition into a new-Keynesian model with staggered price setting. The resulting framework provides microfoundations for a cost-push shock, taking the form of random variations in transportation costs. An increase in transportation costs raises price markups and is contractionary and inflationary. Spatial frictions also have consequences for the propagation of aggregate demand disturbances (including monetary shocks), as they dampen their output effects and amplify their inflationary effects. Empirically, the paper shows that the cost of time spent shopping represents a significant fraction of consumption expenditure, suggesting that spatial frictions broadly defined are a nonnegligible feature of aggregate demand.

中文摘要

本文将空间竞争的环形霍特林模型嵌入到具有交错价格设定的新凯恩斯模型中。由此产生的框架为成本推动冲击提供了微观基础，其形式是运输成本的随机变化。运输成本的增加会提高价格加成，并导致经济收缩和通货膨胀。空间摩擦还对总需求扰动（包括货币冲击）的传导产生影响，因为它们削弱了这些扰动的产出效应并放大了其通胀效应。实证上，本文表明购物所花费的时间成本占消费支出的很大一部分，这表明广义上的空间摩擦是总需求的一个不可忽视的特征。

No. 7NBER w33627

Indirect Cost Recovery in U.S. Innovation Policy: History, Evidence, and Avenues for Reform

美国创新政策中的间接成本回收：历史、证据与改革路径

Pierre Azoulay, Daniel P. Gross, Bhaven N. Sampat

Programs：EH, PE, POL, PR；JEL：H51, O31, O32, O38

Abstract

The U.S. government has funded university research for nearly 80 years, with a significant share of this funding supporting the fixed costs of science through indirect cost recovery (ICR). We explain the history, objectives, and mechanics of ICR policy and review key controversies. We also provide new empirical evidence on indirect costs at the NIH, a major target of past and present ICR reform. Using data from over 350 institutions, we find that while negotiated ICR rates average 58%, effective rates—what NIH actually pays—average 42%, with relatively little variation across institutions or over time. Our analyses also suggest that a proposed 15% at rate would significantly cut NIH funding for many grantees, disproportionately affecting institutions most linked to commercial patenting and drug development. We conclude by assessing the current system, and major reform proposals, across several ICR policy objectives: support for research and infrastructure, cost-efficiency incentives, implementation costs, and transparency. No single approach dominates on all dimensions.

中文摘要

美国政府资助大学研究已有近80年历史，其中很大一部分资金通过间接成本回收（ICR）来支持科学的固定成本。我们解释了ICR政策的历史、目标和机制，并回顾了主要争议。我们还提供了关于NIH间接成本的新实证证据，NIH是过去和现在ICR改革的主要目标。利用来自350多个机构的数据，我们发现，虽然协商的ICR费率平均为58%，但实际费率——NIH实际支付的费率——平均为42%，各机构之间或随时间推移的变化相对较小。我们的分析还表明，提议的15%统一费率将大幅削减许多受资助者的NIH资金，对与商业专利和药物开发联系最紧密的机构影响尤为严重。最后，我们根据ICR政策的几个目标——支持研究和基础设施、成本效率激励、实施成本和透明度——评估了当前系统和主要改革提案。没有一种方法在所有维度上都占主导地位。

No. 8NBER w33626

Revolving Door Laws and Political Selection

旋转门法律与政治选拔

Raymond Fisman, Jetson Leder-Luis, Catherine M. O'Donnell, Silvia Vannutelli

Programs：LS, POL；JEL：D72, D73, K16

Abstract

Revolving door laws restrict public officials from representing private interests before government after leaving office. While these laws mitigate potential conflicts of interest, they also may affect the pool of candidates for public positions by lowering the financial benefits of holding office. We study the consequences of revolving door laws for political selection in U.S. state legislatures, exploiting the staggered roll-out of laws across states over time. We find that fewer new candidates enter politics in treated states and that incumbent legislators are less likely to leave office, leading to an increase in uncontested elections. The decline in entry is particularly strong for independent and more moderate candidates, which may increase polarization. We provide a model of politician career incentives to interpret the results.

中文摘要

旋转门法律限制公职人员在离职后代表私人利益与政府打交道。虽然这些法律减轻了潜在的利益冲突，但它们也可能通过降低担任公职的经济利益来影响公职候选人的储备。我们利用各州法律在不同时间交错推出的情况，研究了旋转门法律对美国州议会政治选拔的影响。我们发现，在受法律影响的州，进入政界的新候选人减少，现任议员离职的可能性降低，导致无竞争选举增加。候选人进入的减少在独立和更温和的候选人中尤为明显，这可能会加剧两极分化。我们提供了一个政治家职业激励模型来解释这些结果。

No. 9NBER w33625

The Research Behavior of Individual Investors

个人投资者的研究行为

Toomas Laarits, Jeffrey Wurgler

Programs：AP；JEL：G02, G11, G12, G4, G40, G41, G5, G50, G53

Abstract

Browser data from an approximately representative sample of individual investors offers a detailed account of their search for information, including how much time they spend on stock research, which stocks they research, what categories of information they seek, and when they gather information relative to events and trades. The median individual investor spends approximately six minutes on research per trade on traded tickers, mostly just before the trade; the mean spends around half an hour. Individual investors spend the most time reviewing price charts, followed by analyst opinions, and exhibit little interest in traditional risk statistics. Aggregate research interest is highly correlated with stock size, and salient news and earnings announcements draw more attention. Individual investors have different research styles, and those that focus on short-term information are more likely to trade more speculative stocks.

中文摘要

来自一个大致具有代表性的个人投资者样本的浏览器数据，详细描述了他们的信息搜索行为，包括他们在股票研究上花费的时间、他们研究的股票、他们寻求的信息类别，以及他们相对于事件和交易收集信息的时间。个人投资者中位数在每笔交易中花费大约六分钟研究交易标的，且主要就在交易之前；均值大约为半小时。个人投资者花费最多时间查看价格图表，其次是分析师意见，并且对传统风险统计指标兴趣不大。总的研究兴趣与股票规模高度相关，而突出的新闻和盈利公告会吸引更多关注。个人投资者有不同的研究风格，那些关注短期信息的人更有可能交易更具投机性的股票。

No. 10NBER w33624

Mechanism Design for Personalized Policy: A Field Experiment Incentivizing Exercise

个性化政策的机制设计：一项激励锻炼的实地实验

Rebecca Dizon-Ross, Ariel D. Zucker

Programs：DEV, EH, LS, PE；JEL：D82, I12

Abstract

Personalizing policies can theoretically increase their effectiveness. However, personalization is difficult when individual types are unobservable and the preferences of policymakers and individuals are not aligned, which could cause individuals to misreport their type. Mechanism design offers a strategy to overcome this issue: offer an “incentive-compatible” menu of policy choices designed to induce participants to select the variant intended for their type. Using a field experiment that personalized incentives for exercise among 6,800 adults with diabetes and hypertension in urban India, we show that personalizing with an incentive-compatible choice menu substantially improves program performance, increasing the treatment effect of incentives on exercise by 80% without increasing program costs relative to a one-size-fits-all benchmark. Mechanism design achieves similar performance to personalizing with an extensive set of observable variables, but without the high data requirements or the risk that participants might manipulate their observables.

中文摘要

个性化政策理论上可以提高其有效性。然而，当个体类型不可观察，且政策制定者和个体的偏好不一致时，个性化是困难的，这可能导致个体虚报其类型。机制设计提供了一种克服此问题的策略：提供一个“激励相容”的政策选择菜单，旨在引导参与者选择为其类型设计的变体。通过一项在印度城市中对6800名患有糖尿病和高血压的成年人进行个性化锻炼激励的实地实验，我们表明，使用激励相容的选择菜单进行个性化设计显著提高了项目绩效，与一刀切的基准相比，在不增加项目成本的情况下，将激励对锻炼的处理效应提高了80%。机制设计实现了与使用大量可观察变量进行个性化相似的绩效，但没有高数据要求或参与者可能操纵其可观察变量的风险。

No. 11NBER w33623

The Impact of Increased Exposure of Diversity on Suburban Students' Outcomes: An Analysis of the METCO Voluntary Desegregation Program

增加多样性接触对郊区学生成果的影响：对METCO自愿废除种族隔离计划的分析

Elizabeth Setren

Programs：CH, ED；JEL：I20, I21, I24, J15

Abstract

Over sixty years following Brown vs. Board of Education, racial and socioeconomic segregation and lack of equal access to educational opportunities persist. Across the country, voluntary desegregation busing programs aim to ameliorate these imbalances and disparities. A longstanding Massachusetts program, METCO, buses K-12 students of color from Boston and Springfield, Massachusetts to 37 suburban districts that voluntarily enroll urban students. Supporters of the program argue that it prepares students to be active citizens in our multicultural society. Opponents question the value of the program and worry it may have a negative impact on suburban student outcomes. I estimate the causal effect of exposure to diversity through the METCO program by using two types of variation: difference-in-difference analysis of schools stopping and starting their METCO enrollment and two-stage least squares analysis of space availability for METCO students. Both methods rule out substantial test score, attendance, or suspension effects of having METCO peers. Classroom ability distribution and classroom suspension rates remain similar when METCO programs start and stop. There is no negative impact on college preparation, competitiveness, persistence, or graduation.

中文摘要

在布朗诉教育委员会案六十多年后，种族和社会经济隔离以及缺乏平等的教育机会仍然存在。全国各地的自愿废除种族隔离校车计划旨在改善这些不平衡和差距。马萨诸塞州一项历史悠久的计划METCO，将来自波士顿和马萨诸塞州斯普林菲尔德的K-12有色人种学生送往37个自愿招收城市学生的郊区学区。该计划的支持者认为，它使学生为在我们的多元文化社会中成为积极公民做好准备。反对者质疑该计划的价值，并担心它可能对郊区学生的成果产生负面影响。我通过使用两种变异来估计通过METCO计划增加多样性接触的因果效应：对学校停止和开始其METCO招生的双重差分分析，以及对METCO学生可用空间的两阶段最小二乘分析。两种方法都排除了拥有METCO同学对考试成绩、出勤率或停学率的实质性影响。当METCO项目开始和停止时，课堂能力分布和课堂停学率保持相似。对大学准备、竞争力、坚持或毕业没有负面影响。

No. 12NBER w33622

Putting School Surveys to the Test

将学校调查置于检验之下

Joshua Angrist, Peter Hull, Russell Legate-Yang, Parag A. Pathak, Christopher R. Walters

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：I21, J01

Abstract

School districts increasingly gauge school quality with surveys that ask about school climate and student engagement. The Role of Risk and Ambiguity Preferences on Early-Childhood Investment: Evidence from Rural India

风险与模糊偏好对早期儿童投资的作用：来自印度农村的证据

Michael Cuna, Lenka Fiala, Min Sok Lee, John A. List, Sutanuka Roy

Programs：DEV, EH；JEL：D91, I14, I15, I24, J13

Abstract

Understanding the role of preferences, beliefs, and constraints on social and wealth inequities is a key unlock for economic growth. This study focuses on the inter-relationship between risk and ambiguity preferences of mothers, their early childhood investments, and their children’s outcomes. To do so, we elicit ambiguity attitude and risk aversion preference parameters from more than 6000 randomly sampled mothers from nearly 500 villages in Rajasthan, India. Across several measures of mothers’ investment in nutrition of their children between the ages of 0-6, we find a robust and stable positive correlation of estimated ambiguity attitude and risk aversion parameters with maternal investments: the more risk and ambiguity averse the mother, the greater her investments. Such investments are correlated with better children’s cognitive and non-cognitive skills, as mothers with greater risk and ambiguity aversion have children with superior skills, even after accounting for socio-economic differences. Importantly, the positive effect of ambiguity and risk aversion on early-life outcomes can attenuate the negative impact of proxies of socio-economic disadvantage, such as illiteracy of the mothers, belonging to historically discriminated social groups, no exposure to radio, television, or zero access to mobile phones for all measures of cognitive and non-cognitive early-life skills.

Spatial Sorting and Inequality

空间分类与不平等

Rebecca Diamond, Juan Carlos Suárez Serrato

Programs：LS, PE；JEL：J2, R1, R2

Abstract

This chapter examines the role of spatial sorting in shaping economic inequality in the United States. We first document the evolution of firm and worker sorting by skill level between 1980 and 2017. We highlight a shift since 2000, where both high-education workers and firms increasingly sort away from high-wage, high-rent areas. Throughout the entire time period, high-education workers continue to sort to high amenity areas. We then develop a spatial equilibrium model that incorporates idiosyncratic worker and firm sorting and discuss estimation techniques to identify model parameters. We review recent empirical advancements in spatial sorting, including firm and worker location choices and their interactions with housing policy. We conclude by outlining the model’s limitations and proposing directions for future research.

The Inverted U-Shaped Relationship between Female Entrepreneurship and Economic Development

女性创业与经济发展之间的倒U型关系

Nava Ashraf, Alexia Delfino, Edward L. Glaeser, Irene Solmone

Programs：DEV, EFG, LS, PR；JEL：J70, L26, O14

Abstract

In the World Bank Enterprise Survey, the share of entrepreneurs who are women first rises and then falls with national income, which reverses the well-known U-shaped relationship between female labor force participation and economic development. This paper presents a model of entrepreneurship in which women face disadvantages, including discrimination from workers and household demands on their time, that deter firm formation in the poorest countries. In richer countries, these forces mean that women are less likely to start more complex organizations. In the model, women face the most level playing field in middle-income countries, where the returns to entrepreneurship are high enough to offset gender-based costs and both sexes typically form simple forms. We document three facts that are compatible with the model: male-owned firms are typically much larger than female-owned firms, large firms yield far higher revenues per employee and female-owned firms are more usually in industries with low levels of skilled workers. Despite the entrepreneurship rate’s reversal of the U-shaped link between female labor-force-participation and economic development, female labor-force-participation and the entrepreneurship rate are highly correlated across countries. The female entrepreneurship rate is also strongly associated with female education, weak kinship ties and Buddhism.

中文摘要
