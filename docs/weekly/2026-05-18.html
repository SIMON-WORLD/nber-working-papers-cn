Proposed Mergers Where Efficiencies Are Needed Most Might Be the Least Likely to Deliver Them

最需要效率的拟议合并可能最不可能带来效率

Robert D. Metcalfe, Alexandre B. Sollaci, Chad Syverson

Programs：IO, PR；JEL：L0

Abstract

Mergers are commonly evaluated by weighing their expected market power effects against any efficiency gains they create. The larger the market power effect of a proposed merger, the larger must be any efficiencies for it to raise social welfare. We show selection into merger proposal distorts the observed relationship between market power and efficiency effects. Even if market power and efficiency gains are independent (or even positively correlated) across all potential mergers, they will generally be negatively related among proposed mergers. This is because parties propose to merge only if the merger’s expected profitability exceeds a threshold, so the underlying components of profitability become substitutes in clearing that hurdle. It does not rely on managerial bias, behavioral frictions, or strategic misrepresentation. We demonstrate this negative correlation is present under very general conditions when the two effects are uncorrelated among all mergers. We also characterize conditions where this still holds even in the presence of positive underlying correlations and firms’ uncertainty about their own merger’s profitability. Policies that might raise the selection hurdle for proposed mergers do not alleviate the negative correlation; indeed, they would exacerbate it. Our analysis has direct implications for interpreting empirical merger retrospectives and for evaluating efficiency defenses in antitrust policy.

中文摘要

合并通常通过权衡其预期的市场势力效应与所产生的效率收益来评估。拟议合并的市场势力效应越大，其提高社会福利所需的效率就必须越大。我们表明，合并提案的选择扭曲了观察到的市场势力与效率效应之间的关系。即使市场势力与效率收益在所有潜在合并中是独立的（甚至正相关），它们在拟议合并中通常也会呈负相关。这是因为各方只有在合并的预期盈利能力超过阈值时才提议合并，因此盈利能力的潜在组成部分在跨越该门槛时成为替代品。这并不依赖于管理偏见、行为摩擦或策略性误报。我们证明，当这两个效应在所有合并中不相关时，这种负相关在非常一般的条件下存在。我们还描述了即使在存在正的基础相关性以及企业对其自身合并盈利能力存在不确定性的情况下，这种负相关仍然成立的条件。可能提高拟议合并选择门槛的政策并不会缓解这种负相关；实际上，它们会加剧这种负相关。我们的分析对解释实证合并回顾以及评估反垄断政策中的效率抗辩具有直接意义。

No. 2NBER w35220

Trust, Financial Literacy, and Financial Behaviors: Shaping Retirement Security

信任、金融素养与金融行为：塑造退休保障

Maya Haran Rosen, Annamaria Lusardi, Olivia S. Mitchell

Programs：AG；JEL：D14, G53

Abstract

We extend the literature on the importance of trust for financial behaviors by examining trust, financial literacy, and financial behavior related to retirement security. Using the Health and Retirement Study, we show that Trust in Financial Institutions aligns with behaviors supportive of retirement security, while Trust in Government Programs does not. We further document racial/ethnic differences: for White respondents, Trust in Financial Institutions relates positively to retirement outcomes, but not for Blacks or Hispanics. Moreover, Trust in Government Programs among minority households is linked to reduced stockholding and lower wealth accumulation. These findings inform efforts to strengthen retirement security.

中文摘要

我们通过考察与退休保障相关的信任、金融素养和金融行为，拓展了关于信任对金融行为重要性的文献。利用健康与退休研究，我们表明对金融机构的信任与支持退休保障的行为一致，而对政府项目的信任则不然。我们进一步记录了种族/族裔差异：对于白人受访者，对金融机构的信任与退休结果正相关，但对黑人或西班牙裔受访者则不然。此外，少数族裔家庭对政府项目的信任与股票持有减少和财富积累降低相关。这些发现为加强退休保障的努力提供了信息。

Lands of Opportunity: Differences in the Geography of Wealth and Income Mobility in the United States

机遇之地：美国财富与收入流动性的地理差异

Ariel J. Binder, Max Risch, John L. Voorheis

Programs：PE；JEL：D31, E24, O18

Abstract

We provide new county-level estimates of intergenerational mobility, covering multiple economic concepts: total income, labor income, homeownership, housing wealth, and total wealth. This is possible via small-area estimation techniques and linked survey and administrative data covering millions of U.S. children born between 1978 and 1986. We find that relative mobility in wealth concepts shows less spatial clustering and more spatial variation than relative mobility in income concepts. Many cities and their suburbs exhibit lower relative mobility (i.e. higher intergenerational persistence) in wealth concepts than in income concepts. Next, we show that various local characteristics are associated with some concepts of economic mobility but not with others. For example, we estimate a strong negative association between the local severity of the Great Recession and child income, regardless of parent position in the income distribution. However, the negative association between recession severity and wealth only exists among children from poorer families. We provide a public-use data package to facilitate further research.

中文摘要

我们提供了新的县级代际流动性估计，涵盖多个经济概念：总收入、劳动收入、房屋所有权、住房财富和总财富。这通过小区域估计技术以及覆盖数百万1978年至1986年间出生的美国儿童的关联调查和行政数据得以实现。我们发现，财富概念的相对流动性比收入概念的相对流动性表现出更少的空间聚集和更多的空间变异。许多城市及其郊区在财富概念上的相对流动性（即更高的代际持续性）低于收入概念。接下来，我们表明各种地方特征与某些经济流动性概念相关，但与其他概念无关。例如，我们估计大衰退的地方严重程度与儿童收入之间存在强烈的负相关，无论父母在收入分配中的位置如何。然而，衰退严重程度与财富之间的负相关仅存在于来自较贫困家庭的儿童中。我们提供了一个公共使用的数据包以促进进一步研究。

No. 4NBER w35218

California Billionaires: Wealth, Taxes, and Wealth Tax Revenue Estimates

加州亿万富翁：财富、税收与财富税收入估算

Jasper Boll, Emmanuel Saez, Gabriel Zucman

Programs：PE；JEL：H20

Abstract

This paper documents the wealth of California’s billionaires and the taxes they pay. California billionaires’ wealth exceeds $2 trillion today, the equivalent of 50% of California’s GDP. It has grown 144% from 2023 to 2025, fueled by the AI boom. Over the longer run, the real wealth of California’s billionaire class—the 0.0002% richest households—has been multiplied by 30 from 1982 to 2025, while average real family income in California has about doubled. California billionaires pay about 0.2% of their wealth in California income tax ($3.2 billion/year), representing 2.4% of total California income tax revenue on average over 2023-2025. Using Securities and Exchange Commission data from Alphabet, Meta, Oracle, and Nvidia since 2004, we estimate the trajectory of wealth, income, and taxes paid by the top 4 California billionaires—Page, Brin, Zuckerberg, Ellison (through 2020), and Huang (since 2021)—focusing on their business wealth. This group alone holds nearly $1 trillion in business wealth, almost half of total California billionaire wealth. For this group, wealth growth (+322% over 2023-2025) and low taxation (0.04% of wealth in annual California income tax) are more pronounced. The proposed one-off California billionaire tax of 5%, payable over 5 years, is both small relative to California billionaires’ wealth gains and large relative to the taxes they currently pay. We estimate that it could raise about $100 billion, with comparatively minor impacts on income tax revenue. Using empirical estimates of mobility responses to wealth taxation, we find that an annual wealth tax on California billionaires could raise substantial additional revenue even after accounting for income tax losses due to mobility.

中文摘要

本文记录了加州亿万富翁的财富及其缴纳的税款。如今，加州亿万富翁的财富超过2万亿美元，相当于加州GDP的50%。受人工智能热潮推动，其财富从2023年到2025年增长了144%。从更长期来看，加州亿万富翁阶层——最富有的0.0002%家庭——的实际财富从1982年到2025年增长了30倍，而加州实际平均家庭收入大约翻了一番。加州亿万富翁缴纳的加州所得税约占其财富的0.2%（每年32亿美元），在2023-2025年间平均占加州所得税总收入的2.4%。利用自2004年以来来自Alphabet、Meta、Oracle和Nvidia的美国证券交易委员会数据，我们估算了前四位加州亿万富翁——佩奇、布林、扎克伯格、埃里森（截至2020年）和黄仁勋（自2021年起）——的财富、收入和缴纳税款的轨迹，重点关注其商业财富。仅这一群体就持有近1万亿美元的商业财富，几乎占加州亿万富翁总财富的一半。对于这一群体，财富增长（2023-2025年间增长322%）和低税率（每年缴纳的加州所得税占财富的0.04%）更为显著。拟议的一次性加州亿万富翁税（税率为5%，分五年缴纳）相对于加州亿万富翁的财富增长而言较小，但相对于他们目前缴纳的税款而言则较大。我们估计，该税可能筹集约1000亿美元，对所得税收入的影响相对较小。利用对财富税流动性反应的实证估计，我们发现，即使在考虑因流动性导致的所得税损失后，对加州亿万富翁征收年度财富税仍可筹集大量额外收入。

The Limits of Political Representation: Evidence from India

政治代表性的局限：来自印度的证据

Veda Narasimhan, Jeffrey Weaver

Programs：DEV；JEL：D72, H41, H75, O12

Abstract

Political inclusion is widely believed to improve governance, motivating the creation of elected representatives for highly localized constituencies. This paper studies 1.2 million "hyperlocal" representatives across 150,000 local governments in rural India. Exploiting discontinuities that determine the number and identity of these representatives, we assess how expanded representation affects governance outcomes. We find precisely estimated null effects on core functions, including public project management, intermediation in access to benefit programs, alignment of policy with citizen preferences, equity of benefit allocation, and oversight of public finances. These findings highlight the limits of expanding political representation if representative capacity is weak.

中文摘要

政治包容被广泛认为能够改善治理，这推动了为高度地方化的选区设立民选代表。本文研究了印度农村15万个地方政府中的120万名“超地方”代表。利用决定这些代表人数和身份的不连续性，我们评估了扩大代表性对治理结果的影响。我们发现，在核心职能方面，包括公共项目管理、福利项目获取的中介服务、政策与公民偏好的一致性、福利分配的公平性以及公共财政监督，均存在精确估计的零效应。这些发现凸显了在代表能力薄弱的情况下扩大政治代表性的局限性。

No. 6NBER w35216

Divergence in Climate Change Communication: LLM-Based Evidence from the IPCC and the Press

气候变化传播中的分歧：基于大语言模型的IPCC与媒体证据

Sebastian Galiani, Franco Mettola La Giglia, Raul A. Sosa

Programs：DEV；JEL：D83, Q54

Abstract

Public summaries of IPCC climate assessments lean toward the more severe end of the technical evidence. The pattern appears at two stages: the IPCC’s lead authors and member governments produce the Summary for Policymakers (SPM) from the Technical Summary (TS), and newspapers then cover the SPM. We use LLMs to score about 114,000 matched claim pairs from all six Assessment Reports (1990 to 2023) and ten major US and UK outlets. Both stages systematically shift toward the more severe end of the source while staying inside the IPCC’s accepted scientific ranges. The shift comes mainly from emphasizing higher-impact magnitudes within reported ranges, less from uncertainty compression, and almost none from selecting worst-case emissions scenarios. Left- and right-leaning outlets show similar patterns.

中文摘要

IPCC气候评估的公开摘要倾向于技术证据中更严重的一端。这种模式出现在两个阶段：IPCC的主要作者和成员国政府从技术摘要中生成决策者摘要，随后报纸对决策者摘要进行报道。我们使用大语言模型对所有六次评估报告（1990年至2023年）以及美国与英国十家主要媒体的约11.4万对匹配声明进行评分。两个阶段都系统性地向来源中更严重的一端偏移，同时保持在IPCC认可的科学范围内。这种偏移主要源于在报告范围内强调更高影响幅度，较少来自不确定性压缩，几乎不来自选择最坏情景的排放路径。左倾和右倾媒体表现出相似的模式。

No. 7NBER w35215

Valuing Disaster Prevention: Desert Locust Monitoring and Control

灾害预防的价值评估：沙漠蝗虫监测与控制

Joséphine Gantois, Anouch Missirian, Evelina Linnros, Anna Tompsett, Amir Jina, Gordon C. McCord, Eyal G. Frank

Programs：DEV, EEE；JEL：I15, O13, Q0, Q5, Q54, Q57

Abstract

Monitoring systems for disaster prevention are costly, and measuring benefits is difficult when monitoring effort is endogenous. We provide the first causal estimate of one such system's impact using three decades of desert locust monitoring data. We document conflict-induced interruptions to monitoring in remote breeding areas, reconstruct how infestations spread to populated areas, and show that exposure to locust swarms around birth decreases child height-for-age, increasing stunting risk by over 7 percentage points. Eliminating the locust monitoring system would induce annual losses of US$25 billion, implying a benefit-cost ratio between 160:1 and 680:1 from child nutrition benefits alone.

中文摘要

灾害预防监测系统成本高昂，而当监测努力具有内生性时，衡量其效益十分困难。我们利用三十年的沙漠蝗虫监测数据，首次提供了此类系统影响的因果估计。我们记录了冲突导致的偏远繁殖区监测中断，重建了虫害如何蔓延至人口稠密地区，并表明出生前后暴露于蝗虫群会降低儿童年龄别身高，使发育迟缓风险增加超过7个百分点。取消蝗虫监测系统将导致每年250亿美元的损失，仅从儿童营养效益来看，效益成本比就在160:1至680:1之间。

No. 8NBER w35214

The Psychology of Macroeconomic Expectations

宏观经济预期的心理学

Pedro Bordalo, Nicola Gennaioli, Florencio Lopez-de-Silanes, Simon G. Schröder, Andrei Shleifer, Maarten van Rooij

Programs：AP, EFG, ME；JEL：D84, E03, E31, E71

Abstract

We present a model of “animal spirits” in which context and emotions affect macroeconomic beliefs by shaping which experiences people recall to simulate similar future aggregate states. We test this mechanism by priming Dutch National Bank Survey respondents to recall personal financial or health adversities before eliciting inflation and home price expectations. The treatment causes instability in beliefs and reasoning tied to the measured similarity of the primed experiences to different macro states. This similarity structure also accounts for heterogeneity in beliefs and reasoning based on a range of other personal experiences we measure. The model micro founds several features of macroeconomic beliefs, including narratives, and yields a “confidence multiplier”: spending by some agents cues optimistic context for others, raising their spending.

中文摘要

我们提出了一个“动物精神”模型，其中情境和情绪通过塑造人们回忆哪些经历来模拟相似的未来总体状态，从而影响宏观经济信念。我们通过启动荷兰国家银行调查的受访者回忆个人财务或健康困境，然后引出通胀和房价预期，来检验这一机制。这种处理导致信念和推理的不稳定性，这与被启动经历与不同宏观状态之间测量的相似性相关。这种相似性结构也解释了基于我们测量的其他个人经历在信念和推理上的异质性。该模型为宏观经济信念的若干特征（包括叙事）提供了微观基础，并产生了一个“信心乘数”：部分主体的支出为其他主体提示了乐观情境，从而提高了他们的支出。

No. 9NBER w35213

Sophisticated Borrowing Constraints and Macroeconomic Dynamics

复杂的借贷约束与宏观经济动态

Al-Mahdi Ebsim, Chen Lian, Yueran Ma, Pablo Ottonello, Diego J. Perez

Programs：CF, EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E32, E44

Abstract

In traditional macro-finance models, firms' debt contracts impose hard borrowing constraints, which require indiscriminate reductions of borrowing and investment when adverse shocks tighten these limits, giving rise to financial acceleration. We study the macroeconomic implications of "sophisticated borrowing constraints" akin to financial covenants among large U.S. nonfinancial firms, commonly specified based on firms' debt relative to operating earnings. We model these constraints as debt thresholds that trigger a transfer of control rights to creditors when they are violated, in which case creditors influence firms' decisions to maximize their value instead of cutting credit unconditionally to adhere to fixed ratios. At the micro level, our model is quantitatively consistent with empirical patterns of investment and earnings around covenant violations. At the macro level, sophisticated borrowing constraints do not generate financial acceleration, because constraint tightening and violations do not induce creditors to downscale firms indiscriminately.

中文摘要

在传统的宏观金融模型中，企业的债务合同施加了硬性借贷约束，当不利冲击收紧这些限制时，要求不加区分地减少借贷和投资，从而产生金融加速器效应。我们研究了类似于美国大型非金融企业财务契约的“复杂借贷约束”的宏观经济含义，这些约束通常基于企业债务相对于营业利润来设定。我们将这些约束建模为债务阈值，当违反时触发控制权向债权人转移，此时债权人影响企业决策以最大化其价值，而不是无条件地削减信贷以遵守固定比率。在微观层面，我们的模型在数量上与违反契约时投资和盈利的经验模式一致。在宏观层面，复杂的借贷约束不会产生金融加速器效应，因为约束收紧和违反不会导致债权人不加区分地缩减企业规模。

No. 10NBER w35212

Old Space, New Space: A Commercial Revolution in Innovation?

旧太空，新太空：创新的商业革命？

Ruben Gaetani, Alexander T. Whalley

Programs：PR；JEL：L64, O31, O38, R48

Abstract

The emergence of firms like SpaceX and Blue Origin has made space a leading example of how private enterprise drives innovation, marking what many see as a sharp break between Old Space and New Space. Yet little systematic evidence documents when the transition to this new phase of space innovation occurred and which firms drove it. We use patent data to provide this measurement and find that the largest surge in space innovation occurred in the 1990s, coinciding with demand-side market creation, and preceding the entry of high-profile startups after 2005. Throughout this period and since, incumbent aerospace firms account for most of the space-related patenting, with entrants contributing a growing but minority share. The same geographic regions that dominated space innovation during the post-Apollo era remain dominant today. These patterns are consistent with directed technical change: incumbents direct R&D toward policy-created markets accessible from existing capabilities, while entrants bring science-based insights into domains requiring new paradigms. Our findings suggest that New Space is more closely connected to Old Space than prevailing narratives imply, and that government's most consequential role in space innovation may lie in constructing appropriable markets. We make patent data on space-related technologies available for future research.

Abstract

More broadly, attribute propagation provides a new lens for evaluating environmental, safety, antitrust, and technology policies in a global economy.
