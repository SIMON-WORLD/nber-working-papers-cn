Intra-Household Insurance and the Intergenerational Transmission of Income Risk

家庭内部保险与收入风险的代际传递

Francesco Agostinelli, Domenico Ferraro, Xincheng Qiu, Giuseppe Sorrenti

Programs：CH, ED, LS；JEL：D1, J13, J22

Abstract

This paper studies the mechanisms and the extent to which parental wage risk passes through to children's skill development. Through a quantitative dynamic labor supply model in which two parents choose whether to work short or long hours or not work at all, time spent with children, and child-related expenditures, we find that income risk impacts skill accumulation, permanently lowering children's skill levels. To the extent that making up for cognitive skill losses during childhood is hard--as available evidence suggests--uninsurable income risk can negatively impact the labor market prospects of future generations.

中文摘要

本文研究了父母工资风险传递给子女技能发展的机制及其程度。通过一个定量动态劳动供给模型，其中两位父母选择是否工作短时间、长时间或不工作，以及选择与子女相处的时间和子女相关支出，我们发现收入风险会影响技能积累，永久性地降低子女的技能水平。鉴于童年时期认知技能损失的弥补是困难的——正如现有证据所表明的那样——不可保险的收入风险可能对后代的劳动力市场前景产生负面影响。

No. 2NBER w32095

Do Double Majors Face Less Risk? An Analysis of Human Capital Diversification

双专业面临的风险更小吗？人力资本多样化的分析

Andrew S. Hanks, Shengjun Jiang, Xuechao Qian, Bo Wang, Bruce A. Weinberg

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：D81, I23, J24, J30

Abstract

We study how human capital diversification, in the form of double majoring, affects the response of earnings to labor market shocks. Double majors experience substantial protection against earnings shocks, of 56%. This finding holds across different model specifications and data sets. Furthermore, the protection double majors experience is more pronounced when the two majors are more distantly related, highlighting the importance of diverse skill sets. Additional analyses demonstrate that double majors are more likely to work in jobs that require a diverse set of skills and knowledge and are less likely to work in occupations that are closely related to their majors.

中文摘要

我们研究了以双专业形式存在的人力资本多样化如何影响收入对劳动力市场冲击的反应。双专业者经历了显著的收入冲击保护，幅度达56%。这一发现在不同的模型设定和数据集下均成立。此外，当两个专业关联度较低时，双专业者所经历的保护更为显著，凸显了多样化技能组合的重要性。进一步分析表明，双专业者更有可能从事需要多样化技能和知识的工作，而不太可能从事与其专业密切相关的职业。

No. 3NBER w32094

Non-Linear Inflation Dynamics in Menu Cost Economies

菜单成本经济中的非线性通胀动态

Andres Blanco, Corina Boar, Callum J. Jones, Virgiliu Midrigan

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E0, E30

Abstract

Canonical menu cost models, when parameterized to match the micro-price data, cannot reproduce the extent to which the fraction of price changes increases with inflation. They also predict implausibly large menu costs and misallocation in the presence of strategic complementarities. We resolve these shortcomings by extending the multi-product menu cost model along two dimensions. First, the products sold by a firm are imperfect substitutes. Second, strategic complementarities are at the firm, not product level. In contrast to standard models, the fraction of price changes increases rapidly with the size of monetary shocks, so our model implies a non-linear Phillips curve.

中文摘要

经典的菜单成本模型在参数化以匹配微观价格数据时，无法再现价格变动比例随通胀上升而增加的程度。在存在战略互补性的情况下，这些模型还预测了不切实际的高菜单成本和资源错配。我们通过沿两个维度扩展多产品菜单成本模型来解决这些缺陷。首先，企业销售的产品是不完全替代品。其次，战略互补性存在于企业层面，而非产品层面。与标准模型相比，价格变动比例随货币冲击规模迅速增加，因此我们的模型意味着非线性的菲利普斯曲线。

No. 4NBER w32093

Maternal Cash Transfers for Gender Equity and Child Development: Experimental Evidence from India

针对性别平等和儿童发展的母亲现金转移：来自印度的实验证据

Jeffrey Weaver, Sandip Sukhtankar, Paul Niehaus, Karthik Muralidharan

Programs：CH, DEV；JEL：D13, I12, I15, I38, O15, Q53

Abstract

Cash transfer programs to women in India now reach over 130 million beneficiaries at an annual cost of 0.6% of GDP, yet evidence on their effects remains limited. We study the impact of unconditional transfers to new mothers in India using a large-scale randomized evaluation. Treated households saw a 9.6–15.5% increase in calorie intake for mothers and children, along with gains in dietary diversity and nutrient consumption. Gender disparities in food consumption narrowed. We find significant gains in children’s functional development. While anthropometric indicators did not improve on average, they may have in areas with better sanitation.

中文摘要

印度针对女性的现金转移项目现已覆盖超过1.3亿受益人，年成本占GDP的0.6%，但其效果的证据仍然有限。我们利用一项大规模随机评估研究了印度对新母亲无条件转移的影响。接受干预的家庭中，母亲和儿童的热量摄入增加了9.6%至15.5%，同时膳食多样性和营养素摄入也有所改善。食物消费中的性别差距缩小了。我们发现儿童功能发展有显著改善。虽然人体测量指标平均而言没有改善，但在卫生条件较好的地区可能有所改善。

No. 5NBER w32092

Convenience Yields and Exchange Rate Puzzles

便利收益率与汇率谜题

Zhengyang Jiang, Arvind Krishnamurthy, Hanno Lustig, Jialu Sun

Programs：AP, IFM；JEL：F30, G15

Abstract

We introduce safe asset demand for dollar-denominated bonds into a tractable incomplete-market model of exchange rates. The convenience yield on dollar bonds enters as a stochastic wedge in the Euler equations for exchange rate determination. This wedge reduces the pass-through from marginal utility shocks to exchange rate movements, resolving the exchange rate volatility puzzle. The wedge also exposes the dollar's exchange rate to convenience yield shocks, giving rise to exchange rate disconnect from macro fundamentals and a quantitatively important driver of currency risk premium. This endogenous exposure identifies a novel safe-asset-demand channel by which the Fed's QE impacts the dollar and long-term U.S. Treasury bond yields.

中文摘要

我们将对美元计价债券的安全资产需求引入一个易于处理的不完全市场汇率模型。美元债券的便利收益率作为随机楔子进入汇率决定的欧拉方程。这个楔子减少了边际效用冲击对汇率变动的传导，解决了汇率波动性谜题。该楔子还使美元汇率暴露于便利收益率冲击，导致汇率与宏观基本面脱节，并成为货币风险溢价的一个数量上重要的驱动因素。这种内生暴露识别出一个新颖的安全资产需求渠道，美联储的量化宽松通过该渠道影响美元和长期美国国债收益率。

No. 6NBER w32091

Power Flows: Transmission Lines, Allocative Efficiency, and Corporate Profits

电力流动：输电线路、配置效率与企业利润

Catherine Hausman

Programs：EEE；JEL：L94, P18, Q41, Q42, Q48

Abstract

Accelerated investment in electricity transmission could reduce total costs and enhance renewable integration. I document static allocative inefficiencies induced by incomplete market integration in two major U.S. markets; these have risen over time and totaled $2 billion in 2022. I also argue that estimating firm-level impacts is important, as incumbents may have the power to block new lines and other reforms. I show that four firms would have experienced a collective $1.3 billion drop in net revenues in 2022 had the market been integrated, and there are reports of some of these firms blocking transmission projects.

中文摘要

加速电力传输投资可以降低总成本并促进可再生能源整合。我记录了美国两个主要市场中由不完全市场整合引起的静态配置低效率；这些低效率随时间上升，2022年总计达20亿美元。我还认为，估计企业层面的影响很重要，因为现有企业可能有权阻止新线路和其他改革。我表明，如果市场实现整合，四家企业在2022年的净收入合计将减少13亿美元，并且有报告称其中一些企业正在阻止输电项目。

No. 7NBER w32090

Policy Responses to Tax Competition: An Introduction

应对税收竞争的政策：导论

David R. Agrawal, James M. Poterba, Owen M. Zidar

Programs：ITI, PE；JEL：H2, H3, H73

Abstract

This paper catalogues policies that have been deployed by jurisdictions seeking to mitigate the effects of tax competition. There are many instruments in this policy arsenal, since the tax base associated with a particular tax instrument may be affected by multiple policy choices, including some such as capital controls and development incentives that are outside the traditional realm of tax policy. This paper describes sixteen instruments that both federal and sub-federal governments have adopted in an effort to limit tax competition. It classifies them into three groups: those that can be pursued unilaterally, those that require bilateral or multilateral agreement, and those that require action by an external actor such as an overarching government. It also discusses the set of economic responses that are relevant to the evaluation of these policies, and then summarizes new evidence on the impact of a subset of these policy instruments in the United States and several other nations.

中文摘要

本文列举了各司法管辖区为减轻税收竞争影响而部署的政策。这一政策工具库中有许多工具，因为特定税收工具对应的税基可能受到多种政策选择的影响，包括一些传统税收政策领域之外的工具，如资本管制和发展激励。本文描述了联邦和次联邦政府为限制税收竞争而采用的十六种工具。将其分为三类：可以单方面推行的工具、需要双边或多边协议的工具，以及需要外部行为者（如上级政府）采取行动的工具。本文还讨论了与这些政策评估相关的一系列经济反应，然后总结了关于其中一部分政策工具在美国及其他几个国家影响的新证据。

No. 8NBER w32089

Customer Data Access and Fintech Entry: Early Evidence from Open Banking

客户数据访问与金融科技进入：来自开放银行的早期证据

Tania Babina, Saleem A. Bahaj, Greg Buchak, Filippo De Marco, Angus K. Foulis, Will Gornall, Francesco Mazzola, Tong Yu

Programs：CF, IO, LE, ME, PR；JEL：G21, G23, G24, G28, G5, G50, K21, L10, L51, O31, O36, O38, O50

Abstract

Open banking (OB) empowers bank customers to share transaction data with fintechs and other banks. 49 countries have adopted OB policies. Consumer trust in fintechs predicts OB policy adoption and adoption spurs investment in fintechs. UK microdata shows that OB enables: i) consumers to access both financial advice and credit; ii) SMEs to establish new fintech lending relationships. In a calibrated model, OB universally improves welfare through entry and product improvements when used for advice. When used for credit, OB promotes entry and competition by reducing adverse selection, but higher prices for costlier or privacy-conscious consumers partially offset these benefits.

中文摘要

开放银行赋予银行客户与金融科技公司及其他银行共享交易数据的权力。已有49个国家采用了开放银行政策。消费者对金融科技公司的信任预示着开放银行政策的采用，而采用则刺激了对金融科技公司的投资。英国微观数据显示，开放银行使：i) 消费者能够获得财务建议和信贷；ii) 中小企业能够建立新的金融科技借贷关系。在一个校准模型中，当开放银行用于提供建议时，通过进入和产品改进普遍改善福利。当用于信贷时，开放银行通过减少逆向选择促进进入和竞争，但对成本较高或注重隐私的消费者收取更高价格部分抵消了这些收益。

No. 9NBER w32088

Energy Transitions in Regulated Markets

受监管市场中的能源转型

Gautam Gowrisankaran, Ashley Langer, Mar Reguant

Programs：EEE, IO；JEL：L51, L94, Q48, Q50

Abstract

Natural gas has replaced coal as the dominant fuel for U.S. electricity generation. However, utilities in regulated U.S. states have retired coal more slowly than others. We build a structural model of rate-of-return regulation during an energy transition where utilities face tradeoffs between lowering costs and maintaining and using legacy capacity. A regulated utility facing carbon taxes lowers short-run coal generation 48% as much as a cost minimizer would. Thirty years after a sudden energy transition, a cost minimizer has retired 71% more coal capacity than the regulated utility. Alternative regulations may jeopardize affordability and reliability goals during energy transitions.

中文摘要

天然气已取代煤炭成为美国发电的主要燃料。然而，美国受监管州的公用事业公司退役燃煤电厂的速度比其他州更慢。我们构建了一个能源转型期间的投资回报率监管的结构模型，其中公用事业公司在降低成本与维持和使用遗留产能之间面临权衡。面临碳税的受监管公用事业公司，其短期燃煤发电量的降幅仅为成本最小化者所能达到降幅的48%。在突发能源转型三十年后，成本最小化者已退役的燃煤产能比受监管公用事业公司多71%。替代性监管措施可能会在能源转型期间危及可负担性和可靠性目标。

No. 10NBER w32087中国相关

Constructing Quarterly Chinese Time Series Usable for Macroeconomic Analysis

构建可用于宏观经济分析的季度中国时间序列

Kaiji Chen, Patrick C. Higgins, Tao Zha

Programs：DEV, EFG, ME；JEL：C82, E02

Abstract

During episodes such as the global financial crisis and the Covid-19 pandemic, China experienced notable fluctuations in its GDP growth and key expenditure components. To explore the primary sources of these fluctuations, we construct a comprehensive dataset of GDP and its components in both nominal and real terms at a quarterly frequency. Applying two SVAR models to this dataset, we uncover the principal drivers of China's economic fluctuations across different episodes. In particular, our findings underscore the distinct impacts of consumption-constrained shocks on household consumption and its various subcomponents throughout the Covid-19 pandemic.

中文摘要

在全球金融危机和新冠疫情等时期，中国的GDP增长及其主要支出组成部分经历了显著波动。为了探究这些波动的主要来源，我们构建了一个包含名义和实际GDP及其组成部分的季度频率综合数据集。将两个SVAR模型应用于该数据集，我们揭示了中国在不同时期经济波动的主要驱动因素。特别是，我们的研究结果强调了在新冠疫情期间，消费受限冲击对家庭消费及其各个子组成部分的独特影响。

No. 11NBER w32086中国相关

The Economic Impact of Heritable Physical Traits: Hot Parents, Rich Kid?

可遗传身体特征的经济影响：父母貌美，子女富有？

Daniel S. Hamermesh, Anwen Zhang

Programs：LS；JEL：D31, D64, J71

Abstract

Since the mapping of the human genome in 2004, biologists have demonstrated genetic links to the expression of several income-enhancing physical traits. To illustrate how heredity produces intergenerational economic effects, this study uses one trait, beauty, to infer the extent to which parents’ physical characteristics transmit inequality across generations. Analyses of a large-scale longitudinal dataset in the U.S., and a much smaller dataset of Chinese parents and children, show that a one standard-deviation increase in parents’ looks is associated with a 0.4 standard-deviation increase in their child’s looks. A large data set of U.S. siblings shows a correlation of their beauty consistent with the same expression of their genetic similarity, as does a small sample of billionaire siblings. Coupling these estimates with parameter estimates from the literatures describing the impact of beauty on earnings and the intergenerational elasticity of income suggests that one standard-deviation difference in parents’ looks generates a 0.06 standard-deviation difference in their adult child’s earnings, which amounts to additional annual earnings in the U.S. of about $2300.

中文摘要

自2004年人类基因组图谱绘制完成以来，生物学家已经证明了多种能提高收入的生理特征与基因之间的联系。为了说明遗传如何产生代际经济效应，本研究利用“美貌”这一特征，推断父母的身体特征在多大程度上将不平等传递给后代。对美国大规模纵向数据集以及一个规模小得多的中国父母与子女数据集的分析表明，父母外貌每增加一个标准差，其子女的外貌相应增加0.4个标准差。一个包含美国兄弟姐妹的大型数据集显示，他们美貌的相关性与其基因相似性的表达一致，一个小样本的亿万富翁兄弟姐妹数据也支持这一结论。将这些估计值与文献中关于美貌对收入影响以及收入代际弹性的参数估计相结合，结果表明，父母外貌一个标准差的差异会导致其成年子女收入产生0.06个标准差的差异，这相当于在美国每年约2300美元的额外收入。

No. 12NBER w32083

The Effects of Immigration on Agricultural Development: Brazil in the Age of Mass Migration

移民对农业发展的影响：大规模移民时代的巴西

David Escamilla-Guerrero, Andrea Papadia, Ariell Zimran

Programs：DAE, DEV, LS；JEL：F22, J61, N36, N56, O13, O15, Q15

Abstract

We study the effects of immigration during the Age of Mass Migration (1855-1920) on Brazil's agricultural development in 1920. Instrumenting for a municipality's immigrant share using the interaction of aggregate immigrant inflows and the expansion of Brazil's railway network, we find that a greater share of European immigrants in a municipality led to an increase in farm values. The bulk of the effect was the product of more intense cultivation of land. We also find that it is unlikely that immigration slowed Brazil’s structural transformation. Our results imply that immigration into an emerging agricultural economy can substantially contribute to agricultural development.

中文摘要

我们研究了大规模移民时代（1855-1920年）移民对巴西1920年农业发展的影响。利用移民总流入量与巴西铁路网络扩张的交互项作为市政区移民比例的工具变量，我们发现，市政区中欧洲移民比例越高，农场价值越高。这种效应主要源于对土地更集约的耕作。我们还发现，移民不太可能减缓巴西的结构转型。我们的结果表明，移民进入一个新兴农业经济体可以极大地促进农业发展。

No. 13NBER w32082中国相关

Help for the Heartland? The Employment and Electoral Effects of the Trump Tariffs in the United States

对“心脏地带”的帮助？美国特朗普关税的就业与选举效应

David Autor, Anne Beck, David Dorn, Gordon H. Hanson

Programs：ITI, LS；JEL：D72, F14, F16, J23

Abstract

We study the economic and political consequences of the 2018-2019 trade war between the United States, China and other US trade partners at the detailed geographic level, exploiting measures of local exposure to US import tariffs, foreign retaliatory tariffs, and US compensation programs. The trade-war has not to date provided economic help to the US heartland: import tariffs on foreign goods neither raised nor lowered US employment in newly-protected sectors; retaliatory tariffs had clear negative employment impacts, primarily in agriculture; and these harms were only partly mitigated by compensatory US agricultural subsidies. Consistent with expressive views of politics, the tariff war appears nevertheless to have been a political success for the governing Republican party. Residents of regions more exposed to import tariffs became less likely to identify as Democrats, more likely to vote to reelect Donald Trump in 2020, and more likely to elect Republicans to Congress. Foreign retaliatory tariffs only modestly weakened that support.

中文摘要

我们在详细的地理层面研究了2018-2019年美国、中国及其他美国贸易伙伴之间贸易战的经济和政治后果，利用了衡量地方对美国进口关税、外国报复性关税以及美国补偿计划暴露程度的指标。迄今为止，贸易战并未为美国“心脏地带”提供经济帮助：对外国商品征收的进口关税既未提高也未降低新受保护部门的美国就业；报复性关税则产生了明显的负面就业影响，主要是在农业领域；而美国补偿性的农业补贴仅部分缓解了这些损害。与政治表达性观点一致，这场关税战对执政的共和党而言似乎仍是一场政治成功。受进口关税影响更大的地区居民，更不可能认同民主党，更可能在2020年投票支持特朗普连任，并且更可能选举共和党人进入国会。外国的报复性关税仅适度削弱了这种支持。

No. 14NBER w32081

Export-Platform FDI: Cannibalization or Complementarity?

出口平台型外商直接投资：替代还是互补？

Pol Antràs, Evgenii Fadeev, Teresa C. Fort, Felix Tintelnot

Programs：ITI；JEL：F1, F2, F4

Abstract

We develop a model of export-platform foreign direct investment (FDI) in which final goods are produced only with labor and there are no fixed costs of exporting. We derive a simple condition that determines whether an MNE's plants are substitutes or complements. This condition is shaped by the relative size of (i) the cross-firm elasticity of demand the MNE faces for its goods and (ii) the within-firm elasticity of labor substitution across the MNE's plants. In two extensions of the model, we show that this complementarity is enhanced by firm-level (rather than plant-level) fixed costs of exporting and of sourcing inputs.

中文摘要

Durables and Size-Dependence in the Marginal Propensity to Spend

耐用品与边际支出倾向的规模依赖性

Martin Beraja, Nathan Zorzi

Programs：EFG, ME, PE；JEL：C0, E0, H0

Abstract

Stimulus checks have become an increasingly important policy tool in recent U.S. recessions. How does the households' marginal propensity to spend (MPX) vary as checks become larger? To quantify this size-dependence in the MPX, we augment a canonical model of durable spending by introducing a smooth adjustment hazard. We discipline this hazard by matching a rich set of micro moments. We find that the MPX declines slowly with the size of checks. In contrast, the MPX is flatter in a purely state-dependent model of durables, and declines sharply in a two-asset model of non-durables. Finally, we embed our spending model into an open-economy heterogeneous-agent New-Keynesian model. In a typical recession, a large check of $2,000 increases output by 25 cents per dollar, compared to 37 cents for a $300 check. Large checks thus remain effective but extrapolating from the response out of small checks overestimates their impact.

中文摘要

刺激支票已成为近期美国经济衰退中日益重要的政策工具。随着支票金额增大，家庭的边际支出倾向（MPX）如何变化？为了量化MPX的这种规模依赖性，我们通过引入平滑调整概率来扩展一个经典的耐用品支出模型。我们通过匹配一组丰富的微观矩来校准这一调整概率。我们发现，MPX随支票金额增大而缓慢下降。相比之下，在纯状态依赖的耐用品模型中，MPX更为平坦；而在非耐用品的双资产模型中，MPX则急剧下降。最后，我们将我们的支出模型嵌入一个开放经济异质性主体新凯恩斯模型中。在一次典型衰退中，一张2000美元的大额支票每美元可增加25美分的产出，而一张300美元的小额支票则为37美分。因此，大额支票仍然有效，但从小额支票的反应进行外推会高估其影响。

User Rights for Ocean Ecosystem Conservation

海洋生态系统保护的用户权利

Gary D. Libecap

Programs：DAE, EEE；JEL：H41, K32, L38, N5, Q22, Q57

Abstract

Non-target marine fish species and ocean ecosystems are increasingly valuable. Ongoing efforts to preserve them emphasize spatial controls on human entry and use via Marine Protected Areas (MPAs). They cover 7.6% of world oceans and aim for 30% by 2030 under the Convention for Biological Diversity (CBD) and International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN). MPAs are Pigouvian-style, polluter-pays, controls with rare direct compensation and uncertain projected fishery benefits. Under this policy design, they impose differential economic costs and benefits and likely are inequitable. Absent economic cost/benefit analysis at inauguration, they may be too large, extensive, and restrictive. In the empirical cases below, MPAs are controversial with political pushback, threatening long-term conservation. User rights and Coasean bargaining may avoid some of these outcomes.

Diminishing Marginal Utility Revisited

边际效用递减再探讨

Miles S. Kimball, Daniel Reck, Fudong Zhang, Fumio Ohtake, Yoshiro Tsutsui

Programs：AG, AP, EFG, PE；JEL：D01, D03, D15, D19

Abstract

How quickly does marginal utility fall with increasing consumption? It depends on the dimension along which we consider concavity of the utility function. This paper estimates the distribution of heterogeneous curvature parameters in individuals’ utility functions from hypothetical choice data, while accounting for survey response error. Types of curvature examined include relative risk aversion, intertemporal substitution, the reciprocal of the altruism elasticity, and a new measure of inequality aversion, which queries how much more a dollar means to a poor family than to a rich family. \nMedian values of curvature parameters ranging from 0.6 to 13.2. Utility functions are most concave for situations involving altruism, followed by risk aversion, inequality aversion, and intertemporal substitution. Heterogeneity of curvature in the population also varies: altruism is the most heterogeneous, followed by risk aversion, the elasticity of intertemporal substitution, and inequality aversion. Nonetheless, curvature parameters are highly correlated (ρ > .8) over different elicitations within parameter type, and modestly correlated across dimensions in some cases, including inequality aversion and risk aversion (ρ ≈ 0.3), altruism and risk aversion (ρ ≈ 0.3), and altruism and inequality aversion (ρ ≈ 0.14).

中文摘要

边际效用随消费增加而下降的速度有多快？这取决于我们考虑效用函数凹性的维度。本文利用假设性选择数据，在考虑调查响应误差的同时，估计了个人效用函数中异质性曲率参数的分布。考察的曲率类型包括相对风险厌恶、跨期替代、利他弹性的倒数，以及一种新的不平等厌恶度量，该度量询问一美元对贫困家庭比对富裕家庭意味着多少。曲率参数的中位数在0.6到13.2之间。效用函数在涉及利他主义的情境中最凹，其次是风险厌恶、不平等厌恶和跨期替代。曲率在人群中的异质性也有所不同：利他主义异质性最大，其次是风险厌恶、跨期替代弹性和不平等厌恶。尽管如此，在参数类型内部的不同诱导方式下，曲率参数高度相关（ρ > 0.8），并且在某些维度之间适度相关，包括不平等厌恶与风险厌恶（ρ ≈ 0.3）、利他主义与风险厌恶（ρ ≈ 0.3），以及利他主义与不平等厌恶（ρ ≈ 0.14）。
