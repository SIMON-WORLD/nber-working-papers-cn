The Hidden Cost of Firearm Violence on Infants in Utero

枪支暴力对子宫内婴儿的隐性成本

Janet Currie, Bahadir Dursun, Michael Hatch, Erdal Tekin

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I12, I18, J13, K40

Abstract

Firearm violence is a critical public health crisis in the U.S., marked by a significant number of homicides involving firearms, including indiscriminate shootings in public spaces. This study investigates the largely unexplored impact of such violence on newborn health. We adopt two approaches. First, we analyze the 2002 'beltway sniper' attacks in the Washington DC metropolitan area, using administrative birth records with maternal residential addresses in Virginia. The beltway sniper attacks, a series of random shootings in the Washington DC metropolitan area, caused widespread terror and disruption over three weeks. Leveraging both spatial and temporal variation, we compare outcomes of children exposed to the attacks in utero due to timing or having a residential address near a shooting location to those who were not exposed. Second, we investigate the impact of in-utero exposure to mass shootings on infant health using restricted-access U.S. Vital Statistics Natality records between 2006 and 2019 and leveraging variation in the timing of mass shootings in counties with at least one shooting. We document substantial, previously overlooked costs on pregnant women and infants. Exposure to the beltway sniper attacks during pregnancy increased the likelihood of very low birthweight and very premature birth by 25%. The analysis of national mass shootings confirms these findings with slightly smaller effect sizes. These results emphasize the need to consider the broader impact of violence on vulnerable populations when assessing the cost of firearm violence. The estimates suggest additional costs of $155 million (2023 dollars) for the beltway sniper attacks and $75 million annually for mass shootings.

中文摘要

枪支暴力是美国一个严重的公共卫生危机，其特点是涉及枪支的凶杀案数量巨大，包括在公共场所的无差别枪击。本研究调查了此类暴力对新生儿健康这一很大程度上未被探索的影响。我们采用两种方法。首先，我们分析了2002年华盛顿特区大都会区的“环城狙击手”袭击事件，使用了弗吉尼亚州包含母亲居住地址的行政出生记录。环城狙击手袭击是华盛顿特区大都会区一系列随机枪击事件，在三周内造成了广泛的恐怖和混乱。利用空间和时间变化，我们将因时间或居住地址靠近枪击地点而在子宫内暴露于袭击的儿童与未暴露的儿童的结果进行比较。其次，我们利用2006年至2019年间受限访问的美国生命统计出生记录，并利用至少发生一次枪击的县中大规模枪击事件的时间变化，研究了子宫内暴露于大规模枪击事件对婴儿健康的影响。我们记录了先前被忽视的、对孕妇和婴儿的巨大成本。怀孕期间暴露于环城狙击手袭击使极低出生体重和极早产的可能性增加了25%。对全国大规模枪击事件的分析证实了这些发现，但效应大小略小。这些结果强调，在评估枪支暴力成本时，需要考虑暴力对弱势群体的更广泛影响。估算表明，环城狙击手袭击带来了1.55亿美元（2023年美元）的额外成本，而大规模枪击事件每年带来7500万美元的额外成本。

No. 2NBER w31773

Labor Market Impacts of Reducing Felony Convictions

减少重罪定罪对劳动力市场的影响

Amanda Y. Agan, Andrew Garin, Dmitri K. Koustas, Alexandre Mas, Crystal Yang

Programs：LE, LS, PE；JEL：J0, K0

Abstract

We study the labor market impacts of retroactively reducing felonies to misdemeanors in San Joaquin County, CA, where criminal justice agencies implemented Proposition 47 reductions in a quasi-random order, without requiring input or action from affected individuals. Linking records of reductions to administrative tax data, we find employment benefits for individuals who (likely) requested their reduction, consistent with selection, but no benefits among the larger subset of individuals whose records were reduced proactively. A field experiment notifying a subset of individuals about their proactive reduction also shows null results, implying that lack of awareness is unlikely to explain our findings.

中文摘要

我们研究了在加州圣华金县将重罪追溯性减为轻罪对劳动力市场的影响，该县的刑事司法机构以准随机顺序实施了第47号提案的减刑，无需受影响个人的意见或行动。将减刑记录与行政税收数据相关联，我们发现，对于（可能）主动申请减刑的个人，存在就业收益，这与选择效应一致；但在更大范围的、被主动减刑的个人中，没有发现收益。一项针对部分被主动减刑个人的实地实验通知也显示出零结果，这意味着缺乏认知不太可能解释我们的发现。

No. 3NBER w31772

Contagion Effects of the Silicon Valley Bank Run

硅谷银行挤兑的传染效应

Dong Beom Choi, Paul Goldsmith-Pinkham, Tanju Yorulmazer

Programs：CF；JEL：G01, G21

Abstract

This paper analyzes the contagion effects associated with the failure of Silicon Valley Bank (SVB) and identifies bank-specific vulnerabilities contributing to the subsequent declines in banks’ stock returns. We find that uninsured deposits, unrealized losses in held-to-maturity securities, bank size, and cash holdings had a significant impact, while better-quality assets or holdings of liquid securities did not help mitigate the negative spillovers. Interestingly, banks whose stocks performed worse post-SVB also experienced lower returns in the previous year, following Federal Reserve interest rate hikes. Stock investors appeared to anticipate risks associated with uninsured deposit reliance, but did not foresee the realization of implied losses. While mid-sized banks experienced particular stress immediately after the SVB failure, over time negative spillovers became widespread except for the largest banks.

中文摘要

本文分析了与硅谷银行（SVB）倒闭相关的传染效应，并识别了导致随后银行股票回报下降的银行特定脆弱性。我们发现，未保险存款、持有至到期证券的未实现损失、银行规模和现金持有量具有显著影响，而更优质的资产或流动性证券的持有并未有助于缓解负面溢出效应。有趣的是，在SVB倒闭后股票表现较差的银行，在之前一年美联储加息后也经历了较低的回报。股票投资者似乎预见到了与未保险存款依赖相关的风险，但未能预见到隐含损失的实现。虽然中型银行在SVB倒闭后立即经历了特别压力，但随着时间的推移，除最大银行外，负面溢出效应变得普遍。

No. 4NBER w31771

When Product Markets Become Collective Traps: The Case of Social Media

当产品市场成为集体陷阱：以社交媒体为例

Leonardo Bursztyn, Benjamin R. Handel, Rafael Jimenez, Christopher Roth

Programs：IO, PE, POL；JEL：D62, D91

Abstract

Individuals might experience negative utility from not consuming a popular product. With such externalities to non-users, standard consumer surplus measures, which take aggregate consumption as given, fail to appropriately capture consumer welfare. We propose an approach to account for these externalities and apply it to estimate consumer welfare from two social media platforms: TikTok and Instagram. Incentivized experiments with college students indicate positive welfare based on the standard measure, but negative welfare when accounting for these non-user externalities. Our findings highlight the existence of product market traps, where large shares of active users prefer each platform not to exist.

中文摘要

个人可能会因不消费一种流行产品而经历负效用。在这种对非用户的负外部性下，将总消费视为给定的标准消费者剩余衡量指标无法恰当捕捉消费者福利。我们提出了一种考虑这些外部性的方法，并将其应用于估算来自两个社交媒体平台（TikTok和Instagram）的消费者福利。对大学生进行的激励实验表明，基于标准衡量指标，福利为正；但在考虑这些非用户外部性时，福利为负。我们的发现凸显了产品市场陷阱的存在，即大量活跃用户更希望每个平台不存在。

No. 5NBER w31770

The Labor Demand and Labor Supply Channels of Monetary Policy

货币政策的劳动力需求与劳动力供给渠道

Sebastian Graves, Christopher K. Huckfeldt, Eric T. Swanson

Programs：EFG, LS, ME；JEL：E32, E52, J22, J23, J64

Abstract

Monetary policy is conventionally understood to influence labor demand, with little effect on labor supply. We estimate the response of labor market flows to high-frequency changes in interest rates around FOMC announcements and Fed Chair speeches and find evidence that, in contrast to the consensus view, a contractionary monetary policy shock leads to a significant increase in labor supply: workers reduce the rate at which they quit jobs to nonemployment, and non-employed individuals increase their job-seeking behavior. These effects are quantitatively important: holding supply-driven labor market flows constant, the decline in employment from a contractionary monetary policy shock would be twice as large. To interpret our findings, we estimate a heterogeneous agent model with frictional labor markets and an active labor supply margin. The model rationalizes existing estimates of small labor supply responses to idiosyncratic transfers with our new evidence of a large labor supply response to an aggregate shock.

中文摘要

传统观点认为货币政策影响劳动力需求，而对劳动力供给影响甚微。我们估算了在联邦公开市场委员会公告和美联储主席演讲前后，劳动力市场流动对利率高频变化的反应，并发现证据表明，与共识相反，紧缩性货币政策冲击会导致劳动力供给显著增加：工人减少离职至非就业状态的比率，非就业个体增加求职行为。这些效应在数量上很重要：在保持供给驱动的劳动力市场流动不变的情况下，紧缩性货币政策冲击导致的就业下降幅度将是原来的两倍。为解释我们的发现，我们估计了一个包含摩擦性劳动力市场和活跃劳动力供给边际的异质性主体模型。该模型将现有关于劳动力供给对个体转移支付反应较小的估计，与我们关于劳动力供给对总体冲击反应较大的新证据进行了合理化解释。

No. 6NBER w31769

The Environmental Cost of Easy Credit: The Housing Channel

宽松信贷的环境成本：住房渠道

Manuel Adelino, David T. Robinson

Programs：CF, EEE；JEL：G30, Q43, R21, R31

Abstract

Heating, cooling, and powering the residential housing stock accounts for about one-fifth of total annual greenhouse gas emissions in the US. Home size is a key determinant of energy intensity. The average newly built single-family home is 50% larger than in the 1950s. Using distinct identification strategies spanning the last four decades of banking history, we show that more abundant credit increases average new home size. It also facilitates more construction but does not produce offsetting increases in home quality or durability. These results highlight potential environmental costs associated with monetary policies that expand access to credit.

中文摘要

住宅供暖、制冷和供电约占美国年度温室气体排放总量的五分之一。房屋面积是能源强度的关键决定因素。新建独栋住宅的平均面积比20世纪50年代大50%。利用跨越过去四十年银行业历史的不同识别策略，我们表明更充裕的信贷会增加新建住宅的平均面积。它还促进了更多的建设，但并未带来房屋质量或耐久性的补偿性提升。这些结果凸显了与扩大信贷获取渠道的货币政策相关的潜在环境成本。

No. 7NBER w31768

Institutions, Comparative Advantage, and the Environment

制度、比较优势与环境

Joseph S. Shapiro

Programs：DEV, EEE, ITI, PE, POL；JEL：F18, F55, F6, F64, H23, O4, O44, P48, Q50, Q56

Abstract

This paper proposes that strong financial, judicial, and labor market institutions provide comparative advantage in clean industries, and thereby improve a country's environmental quality. Five complementary tests support this hypothesis. First, industries that depend on institutions are disproportionately clean. Second, strong institutions increase relative exports in clean industries, even conditional on environmental regulation and factor endowments. Third, an industry's complexity helps explain the link between institutions and clean goods. Fourth, a quantitative general equilibrium model indicates that strengthening a country's institutions decreases its pollution through relocating dirty industries abroad, though increases pollution in other countries. Fifth, cross-country differences in the composition of output between clean and dirty industries explain more of the global distribution of emissions than differences in the techniques used for production do. The comparative advantage that strong institutions provide in clean industries gives one under-explored reason why developing countries have relatively high pollution levels.

中文摘要

本文提出，强大的金融、司法和劳动力市场制度为清洁行业提供了比较优势，从而改善了一国的环境质量。五项互补性检验支持了这一假说。第一，依赖制度的行业不成比例地属于清洁行业。第二，强大的制度增加了清洁行业的相对出口，即使在控制了环境规制和要素禀赋之后也是如此。第三，行业的复杂性有助于解释制度与清洁产品之间的联系。第四，一个定量的一般均衡模型表明，加强一国制度会通过将污染行业转移到国外来减少其污染，但会增加其他国家的污染。第五，各国清洁与污染行业产出构成的差异，比生产所用技术的差异更能解释全球排放分布。强大制度在清洁行业提供的比较优势，是解释发展中国家污染水平相对较高的一个未被充分探讨的原因。

No. 8NBER w31767

The Turing Transformation: Artificial Intelligence, Intelligence Augmentation, and Skill Premiums

图灵转型：人工智能、智能增强与技能溢价

Ajay K. Agrawal, Joshua S. Gans, Avi Goldfarb

Programs：PR；JEL：J2, O3

Abstract

We ask whether a technical objective of using human performance of tasks as a benchmark for AI performance will result in the negative outcomes highlighted in prior work in terms of jobs and inequality. Instead, we argue that task automation, especially when driven by AI advances, can enhance job prospects and potentially widen the scope for employment of many workers. The neglected mechanism we highlight is the potential for changes in the skill premium where AI automation of tasks exogenously improves the value of the skills of many workers, expands the pool of available workers to perform other tasks, and, in the process, increases labor income and potentially reduces inequality. We label this possibility the “Turing Transformation.” As such, we argue that AI researchers and policymakers should not focus on the technical aspects of AI applications and whether they are directed at automating human-performed tasks or not and, instead, focus on the outcomes of AI research. In so doing, our goal is not to diminish human-centric AI research as a laudable goal. Instead, we want to note that AI research that uses a human-task template with a goal to automate that task can often augment human performance of other tasks and whole jobs. The distributional effects of technology depend more on which workers have tasks that get automated than on the fact of automation per se.

中文摘要

我们探究以人类任务表现作为人工智能性能基准的技术目标，是否会导致先前研究中强调的关于就业和不平等的负面结果。相反，我们认为任务自动化，尤其是由人工智能进步驱动的自动化，可以改善就业前景，并可能扩大许多工人的就业范围。我们强调的被忽视的机制是技能溢价变化的潜力：人工智能对任务的自动化外生地提升了许多工人技能的价值，扩大了可用于执行其他任务的工人池，并在此过程中增加了劳动收入，可能减少不平等。我们将这种可能性称为“图灵转型”。因此，我们认为人工智能研究人员和政策制定者不应关注人工智能应用的技术方面以及它们是否旨在自动化人类执行的任务，而应关注人工智能研究的结果。这样做，我们的目标不是贬低以人为中心的人工智能研究这一值得称赞的目标。相反，我们想指出，使用人类任务模板并以自动化该任务为目标的人工智能研究，通常可以增强人类执行其他任务和整体工作的能力。技术的分配效应更多地取决于哪些工人的任务被自动化，而不是自动化本身这一事实。

No. 9NBER w31761

Intergenerational Transmission of Inequality: Maternal Endowments, Investments, and Birth Outcomes

不平等的代际传递：母亲禀赋、投资与出生结局

Sadegh Eshaghnia, James J. Heckman

Programs：CH, ED, EH；JEL：I12, I14, J24

Abstract

Newborn health is an important component in the chain of intergenerational transmission of disadvantage. This paper contributes to the literature on the determinants of health at birth in two ways. First, we analyze the role of maternal endowments and investments (education and smoking in pregnancy) on the probability of having a baby who is small for gestational age (SGA). We estimate both the total impact of maternal endowments on birth outcomes, and we also decompose it into a direct, “biological” effect and a “choice” effect, mediated by maternal behaviors. Second, we estimate the causal effects of maternal education and smoking in pregnancy, and investigate whether women endowed with different traits have different returns. We find that maternal cognition affects birth outcomes primarily through maternal education, that personality traits mainly operate by changing maternal smoking, and that the physical fitness of the mother has a direct, “biological” effect on SGA. We find significant heterogeneity in the effects of education and smoking along the distribution of maternal physical traits, suggesting that women with less healthy physical constitutions should be the primary target of prenatal interventions.

中文摘要

新生儿健康是劣势代际传递链条中的一个重要组成部分。本文从两个方面对出生健康决定因素的文献做出了贡献。首先，我们分析了母亲禀赋和投资（教育和孕期吸烟）对生出小于胎龄儿概率的作用。我们估计了母亲禀赋对出生结局的总影响，并将其分解为直接的“生物”效应和由母亲行为中介的“选择”效应。其次，我们估计了母亲教育和孕期吸烟的因果效应，并探究具有不同特质的女性是否具有不同的回报。我们发现母亲认知主要通过母亲教育影响出生结局，人格特质主要通过改变母亲吸烟行为起作用，而母亲的身体健康对小于胎龄儿有直接的“生物”效应。我们发现教育和吸烟的效应在母亲身体特质分布上存在显著异质性，这表明体质较弱的女性应成为产前干预的主要目标。

Dynamics of the Long Term Housing Yield: Evidence from Natural Experiments

长期住房收益率的动态：来自自然实验的证据

Verónica Bäcker-Peral, Jonathon Hazell, Atif R. Mian

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E4, E40, E5, G5

Abstract

Every month, a fraction of UK property leases are extended for another 90 years or more. We build a new dataset of thousands of these natural experiments from 2000 onwards to estimate the expected long term housing yield, y*. Starting from a level of 5.3%, y* starts to fall during the Great Recession, reaching a low of 2.8% in 2023. Real time data shows y* has not risen since 2021, despite rising shorter term yields. Cross-sectional estimates show that y* is higher in areas with more housing risk, and falls by more in areas with more inelastic housing supply.

中文摘要

每个月，英国一部分房产租约会被延长90年或更久。我们构建了一个包含自2000年以来数千个此类自然实验的新数据集，以估计预期的长期住房收益率y*。从5.3%的水平开始，y*在大衰退期间开始下降，到2023年达到2.8%的低点。实时数据显示，尽管短期收益率上升，但自2021年以来y*并未上升。横截面估计表明，在住房风险较高的地区y*更高，而在住房供给缺乏弹性的地区y*下降得更多。

No. 11NBER w31759

The Effect of Childhood Environment on Political Behavior: Evidence from Young U.S. Movers, 1992–2021

童年环境对政治行为的影响：来自1992-2021年美国年轻迁移者的证据

Jacob R. Brown, Enrico Cantoni, Sahil Chinoy, Martin Koenen, Vincent Pons

Programs：POL；JEL：D72, P0

Abstract

We ask how childhood environment shapes political behavior. We measure young voters’ participation and party affiliation in nationally comprehensive voter files and reconstruct their childhood location histories based on their parents’ addresses. We compare outcomes of individuals who moved between the same origin and destination counties but at different ages. Those who spend more time in the destination are more influenced by it: Growing up in a county where their peers are 10 percentage points more likely to become Republicans makes them 4.7 percentage points more likely to become Republican themselves upon entering the electorate. The effects are of similar magnitude for Democratic partisanship and turnout. These exposure effects are primarily driven by teenage years, and they persist but decay after the first election. They reflect both state-level factors and factors varying at a smaller scale such as peer effects.
