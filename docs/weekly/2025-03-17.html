The Short-Run Effects of Congestion Pricing in New York City

纽约市拥堵收费的短期效应

Cody Cook, Aboudy Kreidieh, Shoshana Vasserman, Hunt Allcott, Neha Arora, Freek van Sambeek, Andrew Tomkins, Eray Turkel

Programs：IO, PE；JEL：R4, R41, R48, R5

Abstract

We study the impacts of New York City’s Central Business District (CBD) Tolling Program, the first cordon-based congestion pricing scheme in the United States. Using a generalized synthetic controls design, we find that the policy increased speeds on CBD roads by 11%, with little-to-no effect on air quality, transactions at shops and restaurants, or overall foot traffic in the CBD. Speeds also increased on roads outside the CBD that are commonly traversed by drivers headed to/from the CBD. These spillovers lead to faster trips throughout the metro area, including for many unpriced trips. We develop a simple model to bound the driver welfare effects, and estimate gains of at least $14.3 million/week (before any revenue recycling). These gains are largely driven by diffuse time savings for the many unpriced trips outside the CBD, highlighting the importance of accounting for network-wide spillovers. Finally, we show how characteristics of local travel patterns and road networks can inform the potential impacts of introducing cordon-based congestion pricing in other cities.

中文摘要

我们研究了纽约市中央商务区（CBD）收费计划的影响，这是美国首个基于环线的拥堵收费方案。采用广义合成控制设计，我们发现该政策使CBD道路的车速提高了11%，但对空气质量、商店和餐厅的交易量或CBD的整体人流量几乎没有影响。在CBD之外、司机往返CBD常经过的道路上，车速也有所提高。这些溢出效应使得整个都市区的出行时间缩短，包括许多未收费的行程。我们开发了一个简单模型来界定司机福利效应，并估计出每周至少1430万美元的收益（在任何收入循环利用之前）。这些收益主要来自CBD之外许多未收费行程的广泛时间节省，凸显了考虑全网络溢出效应的重要性。最后，我们展示了当地出行模式和道路网络的特征如何为在其他城市引入基于环线的拥堵收费的潜在影响提供信息。

No. 2NBER w33583

The Rise of Healthcare Jobs

医疗保健岗位的崛起

Joshua D. Gottlieb, Neale Mahoney, Kevin Rinz, Victoria Udalova

Programs：AG, EH, LS；JEL：I11, J21, J31, J44

Abstract

Healthcare employment has grown more than twice as fast as the labor force since 1980, overtaking retail trade to become the largest industry by employment in 2009. We document key facts about the rise of healthcare jobs. Earnings for healthcare workers have risen nearly twice as fast as those in other industries, with relatively large increases in the middle and upper-middle parts of the earnings distribution. Healthcare workers have remained predominantly female, with increases in the share of female doctors offsetting increases in the shares of male nurses and aides. Despite a few high-profile examples to the contrary, regions experiencing manufacturing job losses have not systematically reinvented themselves by pivoting from ``manufacturing to meds.''

中文摘要

自1980年以来，医疗保健行业的就业增长速度是劳动力增长速度的两倍多，并于2009年超过零售业，成为就业人数最多的行业。我们记录了医疗保健岗位崛起的几个关键事实。医疗保健工作者的收入增长速度几乎是其他行业的两倍，收入分配的中部和中部偏上部分增长相对较大。医疗保健工作者仍然以女性为主，女性医生比例的增加抵消了男性护士和护理员比例的增加。尽管存在少数引人注目的反例，但经历制造业岗位流失的地区并未通过从“制造业转向医药业”而系统地实现自我转型。

The New Geography of Labor Markets

劳动力市场的新地理格局

Mert Akan, Jose Maria Barrero, Nicholas Bloom, Thomas Bowen, Shelby R. Buckman, Steven J. Davis, Hyoseul Kim

Programs：CF, EFG, IO, LE, LS, PE, PR；JEL：J20

Abstract

We study where Americans live in relation to their employer’s worksite using matched employer-employee data, and how that relationship changes with the rise of work from home (WFH). Mean distance from home to employer’s worksite rose more than 70% between 2019 and 2024 in our dataset. Twelve percent of employees hired after March 2020 reside fifty or more miles from their employer by 2024, triple the pre-pandemic share. Distance to employer rose most for those in their 30s and 40s, among highly paid employees, and in Finance, Information, and Professional Services. Especially for the affluent, the pandemic-instigated rise in WFH initiated a multi-year pattern of net migration to areas with cheaper housing and states with lower tax rates. Finally, we show that distant employees exhibit more sensitivity to firm-level adjustments on hiring and separation margins. These developments have implications for residential location, state-level tax revenues, labor markets, and household welfare.

中文摘要

我们利用匹配的雇主-雇员数据研究美国人居住地与其雇主工作地点之间的关系，以及这种关系如何随着居家办公（WFH）的兴起而变化。在我们的数据集中，从家到雇主工作地点的平均距离在2019年至2024年间增长了超过70%。2020年3月之后入职的员工中，有12%到2024年时居住在距离雇主50英里或更远的地方，是疫情前比例的三倍。与雇主距离的增加在30多岁和40多岁的人群、高薪员工以及金融、信息和专业服务行业中最为显著。对于富裕人群而言，疫情引发的WFH兴起开启了一个多年期的净迁移模式，即迁往住房更便宜、税率更低的州。最后，我们表明，远程员工对公司在招聘和解雇方面的调整表现出更高的敏感性。这些发展对居住地选择、州级税收收入、劳动力市场和家庭福利都有影响。

Why Are Nordic Workers so Remote?: Potential Causes and (Some) Indirect Labor Market Consequences

为什么北欧员工如此倾向于远程办公？潜在原因及（部分）间接劳动力市场后果

Adam Gill, Lena E. Hensvik, Oskar Nordström Skans

Programs：LS；JEL：J32, M54, R30

Abstract

In this article, we show that working from home is much more prevalent in the Nordic countries than in the rest of Europe and we discuss potential causes and labor market consequences of this stylized fact. Likely contributing causes include a good technological infrastructure and comparatively widespread digital preparedness in the population. Recent research also suggests that trust is a crucial prerequisite for maintaining a spatial separation between supervisors and workers in marginal occupations. We show that survey measures of trust are extremely high in all Nordic countries, and that these measures correlate very strongly with work-from-home across countries and industries in Europe even controlling for a range of cross-country differences. Finally, we discuss potential spillover effects of increased hybrid work on the Nordic labor markets. We show evidence suggesting that increased hybrid work has caused a relocation of the production of local service firms from business centers to high-work-from-home residential areas, without affecting average commuting distances of service workers.

中文摘要

在本文中，我们表明居家办公在北欧国家比在欧洲其他地区更为普遍，并讨论了这一典型事实的潜在原因和劳动力市场后果。可能的促成因素包括良好的技术基础设施和人口中相对广泛的数字准备度。近期研究还表明，信任是维持边缘职业中监管者与员工之间空间分离的关键前提。我们表明，所有北欧国家的信任度调查指标都非常高，并且这些指标与欧洲各国和各行业的居家办公率存在非常强的相关性，即使在控制了一系列跨国差异后也是如此。最后，我们讨论了混合办公增加对北欧劳动力市场的潜在溢出效应。我们提供的证据表明，混合办公的增加已导致本地服务企业的生产从商业中心向高居家办公率的住宅区转移，但这并未影响服务工人的平均通勤距离。

No. 5NBER w33580

The Origins and Evolution of Occupational Licensing in the United States

美国职业许可的起源与演变

Nicholas A. Carollo, Jason F. Hicks, Andrew Karch, Morris M. Kleiner

Programs：LS；JEL：J01, J29, J4, J44, J48

Abstract

The analysis of occupational licensing has concentrated largely on its labor market and consumer welfare effects. By contrast, relatively little is known about how occupational licensing laws originated or the key factors in their evolution. In this paper, we study the determinants of U.S. licensing requirements from 1870 to 2020. We begin by developing a model where licensing arises as an endogenous political outcome and use this framework to study how market characteristics and political incentives influence regulators’ choices. Our empirical analysis draws on a novel database tracking the initial enactment of licensing legislation for hundreds of unique occupations, as well as changes to the specific qualifications required to obtain a subset of licenses over time. We first show that, consistent with the predictions of our model, licensing requirements are more common and were adopted earlier for occupations whose tasks plausibly pose some risk to consumers. Second, large, urbanized states are significantly more likely to produce new policies. Third, among occupations regulated before 1940, licensing requirements appeared earlier in states with more practitioners and where incumbent workers likely experienced greater labor market competition. After 1980, state-level factors are more strongly associated with the timing of policy adoption. Finally, political organization, as measured by the establishment of a state professional association, significantly increases the probability of regulation. Together, our findings suggest that both public and private interests have contributed to the diffusion of licensing requirements across states and occupations.

中文摘要

对职业许可的分析主要集中在劳动力市场和消费者福利效应上。相比之下，关于职业许可法律如何起源或其演变的关键因素，我们所知相对较少。在本文中，我们研究了1870年至2020年美国许可要求的决定因素。我们首先构建了一个模型，其中许可是作为内生政治结果出现的，并利用这一框架研究市场特征和政治激励如何影响监管者的选择。我们的实证分析基于一个新颖的数据库，该数据库追踪了数百种独特职业的许可立法初始颁布情况，以及随时间推移对获取部分特定许可证所需资格条件的变更。我们首先表明，与模型预测一致，对于那些任务可能对消费者构成风险的职业，许可要求更为普遍且被更早采用。其次，大型、城市化程度高的州更有可能制定新政策。第三，在1940年之前受到监管的职业中，许可要求出现在从业者更多、且在职工人可能面临更大劳动力市场竞争的州更早。1980年之后，州级因素与政策采纳时间的关联性更强。最后，以州级专业协会的成立为衡量标准的政治组织，显著提高了监管的可能性。我们的发现共同表明，公共利益和私人利益都促进了许可要求在各州和各职业间的扩散。

Recombinant Innovation, Novel Ideas, and the Start of Nobel Prize-Winning Work

重组式创新、新颖思想与诺贝尔奖获奖工作的开端

John Ham, Brian Quistorff, Bruce A. Weinberg

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：C41, O31, O38

Abstract

We draw on a recombinant view of innovation, where being in a new location and/or multiple locations leads to exposure to novel combinations of ideas that increase the creativity of top scientists. Using a rich, unique dataset we helped assemble, we estimate the empirical relationship between being in a new location and/or multiple locations and the expected interval before an eventual Nobel laureate (ENL) commences their prize-winning work. We find that being in a new location and in multiple locations are substantially and significantly associated with a shorter expected interval before ENLs commence their prize-winning work.

中文摘要

我们借鉴了创新的重组观点，即身处新地点和/或多个地点，会使顶尖科学家接触到新颖的思想组合，从而增强其创造力。利用我们协助构建的一个丰富且独特的数据集，我们估计了身处新地点和/或多个地点与未来诺贝尔奖得主（ENL）开始其获奖工作之前的预期间隔之间的实证关系。我们发现，身处新地点和身处多个地点，都与ENL开始其获奖工作之前的预期间隔显著缩短密切相关。

No. 7NBER w33578

Rules versus Discretion in Capital Regulation

资本监管中的规则与自由裁量权

Urban Jermann, Haotian Xiang

Programs：CF, EFG；JEL：E44, G21, G28

Abstract

We study capital regulation in a dynamic model for bank deposits. Capital regulation addresses banks’ incentive for excessive leverage that dilutes depositors, but preserves some dilution to reduce bank defaults. We show theoretically that capital regulation is subject to a time inconsistency problem. In a model with non-maturing deposits where optimal withdrawals make deposits endogenously long-term, we find commitment to have important effects on the optimal level and cyclicality of capital adequacy. Our results call for a systematic framework that limits capital regulators’ discretion.

中文摘要

我们研究了一个银行活期存款动态模型中的资本监管。资本监管旨在解决银行过度杠杆化（这会稀释存款人利益）的激励问题，但同时保留一定程度的稀释以减少银行违约。我们从理论上证明，资本监管存在时间不一致性问题。在一个具有非到期存款的模型中，最优取款行为使存款内生地具有长期性，我们发现承诺机制对最优资本充足水平及其周期性具有重要影响。我们的研究结果呼吁建立一个限制资本监管者自由裁量权的系统性框架。

Should Friday be the New Saturday? Hours Worked and Hours Wanted

周五应该成为新的周六吗？工作时长与期望工作时长

Gregor Jarosch, Laura Pilossoph, Anthony Swaminathan

Programs：EFG, LS；JEL：E0, H0, J0

Abstract

This paper investigates self-reported wedges between how much people work and how much they want to work, at their current wage. More than two-thirds of full-time workers in German survey data are overworked—usual hours exceed desired hours. We combine this evidence with a simple model of labor supply to assess the welfare consequences of tighter weekly hours limits via willingness-to-pay calculations. According to counterfactuals, the optimal length of the workweek in Germany is 37 hours. Introducing such a cap would raise welfare by 0.6-1.2% of GDP. The gains from a shortened workweek are largest for workers who are married, female, college-educated, white-collar, middle aged, and high income. An extended analysis integrates a non-constant wage-hours relationship, falling capital returns, and a shrinking tax base.

中文摘要

本文研究了在现行工资水平下，人们自我报告的实际工作时长与期望工作时长之间的差距。根据德国调查数据，超过三分之二的全职工作者存在过度劳动——通常工作时长超过了期望时长。我们将这一证据与一个简单的劳动力供给模型相结合，通过意愿支付计算来评估更严格的每周工时上限的福利后果。根据反事实分析，德国最佳每周工作长度为37小时。引入这样一个上限将使福利提高GDP的0.6%-1.2%。缩短工作周的收益对于已婚、女性、大学学历、白领、中年和高收入的工作者最大。一项扩展分析整合了非恒定的工资-工时关系、下降的资本回报以及缩小的税基。

Supply Constraints do not Explain House Price and Quantity Growth Across U.S. Cities

供给约束无法解释美国各城市的房价与数量增长

Schuyler Louie, John A. Mondragon, Johannes Wieland

Programs：CF, EFG, LS, ME, PE, PR；JEL：E22, J61, R21, R31, R52

Abstract

The standard view of housing markets holds that differences in the flexibility of local housing supply—shaped by factors like geography and regulation—explain differences in how house price and quantity growth respond to rising demand across U.S. cities. However, from 2000 to 2020, we find that higher income growth predicts the same growth in house prices, housing quantity, and population regardless of a city’s estimated housing supply elasticity. We find the same results when we examine rents, expand the sample to 1980 to 2020, use different elasticity measures, use per capita income or population growth instead of total income growth, and when exploiting a variety of plausibly exogenous variation in local housing demand. Using a general demand-and-supply framework, we show that these results imply that estimated housing supply constraints are unimportant in explaining differences in rising house prices among U.S. cities. Our conclusions challenge the prevailing view of local housing and labor markets and suggest that relaxing regulatory housing supply constraints may not materially affect housing affordability.

中文摘要

关于住房市场的标准观点认为，当地住房供给灵活性的差异——由地理和监管等因素塑造——解释了美国各城市房价和住房数量增长对需求上升的不同反应。然而，从2000年到2020年，我们发现，无论一个城市估计的住房供给弹性如何，更高的收入增长都预示着相同的房价、住房数量和人口增长。当我们考察租金、将样本扩展到1980年至2020年、使用不同的弹性衡量指标、使用人均收入或人口增长而非总收入增长，以及利用当地住房需求的各种外生变化时，我们得到了相同的结果。使用一个通用的供需框架，我们表明这些结果意味着，估计的住房供给约束在解释美国各城市房价上涨差异方面并不重要。我们的结论挑战了关于当地住房和劳动力市场的主流观点，并表明放松监管性的住房供给约束可能不会实质性地影响住房可负担性。

No. 10NBER w33575

Racial Disparities and Bias in Consumer Bankruptcy

消费者破产中的种族差异与偏见

Bronson Argyle, Sasha Indarte, Benjamin Iverson, Christopher Palmer

Programs：CF, LE, LS, PE；JEL：C21, D63, G51, J15, K35

Abstract

We document substantial racial disparities in consumer bankruptcy outcomes and investigate the role of racial bias in contributing to these disparities. Using data on the near universe of US bankruptcy cases and self-reported and manually-identified measures of race, we show that minority filers are unconditionally 12.7 and 2.3 percentage points more likely to have their bankruptcy cases dismissed without debt relief in Chapters 13 and 7, respectively. We uncover strong evidence of racial homophily: in Chapter 13, where trustees have more discretion, minority filers are 2.3 percentage points more likely to be dismissed when randomly assigned to a White bankruptcy trustee than to a minority trustee. Black and Hispanic Chapter 13 filers are the most negatively affected by homophily. To interpret our findings, we develop a general decision model and new identification results relating homophily to bias. Our homophily approach is particularly useful in settings where traditional outcome tests for bias are not feasible because the decision-maker's objective is not well defined or decision-relevant outcomes or risk factors are unobserved. Applying this framework to our homophily estimate implies that at least 15% of the unconditional Chapter 13 dismissal gap and 53% of the conditional dismissal gap for minority filers is due to either taste-based or inaccurate statistical discrimination.

中文摘要

我们记录了消费者破产结果中显著的种族差异，并研究了种族偏见在造成这些差异中的作用。利用涵盖美国几乎所有破产案件的数据以及自我报告和人工识别的种族衡量指标，我们显示少数族裔申请人在第13章和第7章破产案件中，其案件在没有债务减免的情况下被驳回的可能性分别无条件高出12.7和2.3个百分点。我们发现了强烈的种族同质性证据：在第13章（受托人拥有更多自由裁量权）中，当少数族裔申请人被随机分配给一位白人破产受托人而非少数族裔受托人时，其案件被驳回的可能性高出2.3个百分点。黑人和西班牙裔的第13章破产申请人受同质性影响最为负面。为了解释我们的发现，我们开发了一个通用决策模型以及将同质性与偏见联系起来的新识别结果。我们的同质性方法在传统的结果检验因决策者目标不明确或决策相关结果或风险因素未被观测而不可行的情况下尤为有用。将这一框架应用于我们的同质性估计，意味着少数族裔申请人无条件第13章驳回差距中至少有15%，以及条件驳回差距中至少有53%，是由基于偏好的或不准确的统计歧视造成的。

No. 11NBER w33574

Human Capital at Home: Evidence from a Randomized Evaluation in the Philippines

家庭中的人力资本：来自菲律宾随机评估的证据

Noam Angrist, Sarah Kabay, Dean Karlan, Lincoln Lau, Kevin Wong

Programs：CH, ED；JEL：I20

Abstract

Children spend most of their time at home in their early years, yet efforts to promote human capital at home in many low- and middle-income settings remain limited. We conduct a randomized controlled trial to evaluate an intervention which encourages parents and caregivers to foster human capital accumulation among their children between ages 3 and 5, with a focus on math and phonics skills. Children gain 0.52 and 0.51 standard deviations relative to the control group on math and phonics tests, respectively (p<0.001). A year later effects persist, but math gains dissipate to 0.15 (p=0.06) and phonics to 0.13 (p=0.12). We also measure impacts on parents, including both fathers and mothers. Effects appear to be mediated largely through instructional support by parents and not other parent investment mechanisms, such as more positive parent-child interactions or additional time spent on education at home beyond the intervention. We also do not find any crowd-out effects on labor market outcomes, likely since the approach tested is efficient, delivering learning gains in a short period of time.

中文摘要

儿童在早期大部分时间都在家中度过，然而在许多中低收入环境中，促进家庭人力资本的努力仍然有限。我们进行了一项随机对照试验，以评估一项鼓励父母和照顾者在3至5岁儿童中促进人力资本积累的干预措施，重点关注数学和语音技能。与对照组相比，儿童在数学和语音测试中分别获得了0.52和0.51个标准差的提升（p<0.001）。一年后，效果仍然存在，但数学收益降至0.15（p=0.06），语音收益降至0.13（p=0.12）。我们还测量了对父母（包括父亲和母亲）的影响。效果似乎主要通过父母的教学支持来中介，而非其他父母投资机制，例如更积极的亲子互动或超出干预范围的在家庭教育上投入的额外时间。我们也没有发现对劳动力市场结果的任何挤出效应，这可能是因为所测试的方法效率较高，能在短时间内带来学习收益。

No. 12NBER w33573

Expert Patients’ Use of Avoidable Health Care

专家患者对可避免医疗服务的利用

Pragya Kakani, Simone Matecna, Amitabh Chandra

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I0, I10, I11, I12

Abstract

We measure whether expert patients – those trained as physicians and nurses – have fewer emergency department visits and the reasons for these differences. Compared to similar patients, physicians and nurses had 19.8% and 5.1% fewer ED visits, principally due to fewer avoidable visits. The differences in avoidable visits between physicians and other patients were largest for diagnoses that often require prescriptions, which physicians often self-prescribed. Our results suggest that improving access to legitimate prescriptions for acute symptoms, more than improving patient education, can reduce avoidable health care.

关税的最优货币政策应对是什么？本文在一个开放经济的新凯恩斯主义模型中探讨了这一问题，描述了关税的宏观经济效应，并表明最优货币政策应对是扩张性的，通胀上升幅度超过关税的直接效应。无论关税适用于消费品还是中间投入品，无论冲击是暂时的还是永久的，无论贸易条件是外生的还是内生的，这一结果都成立。当关税用于解决其他扭曲时，货币政策仍然是扩张性的，但通胀效应会减弱。
