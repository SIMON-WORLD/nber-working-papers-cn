Political Sentiment and Innovation: Evidence from Patenters

政治情绪与创新：来自专利持有者的证据

Joseph Engelberg, Runjing Lu, William Mullins, Richard R. Townsend

Programs：LS, POL, PR；JEL：D72, J24, M5, O31

Abstract

We document political sentiment effects on US inventors. Democratic inventors are more likely to patent (relative to Republicans) after the 2008 election of Obama but less likely after the 2016 election of Trump. These effects are 2-3 times as strong among politically active partisans and are present even within firms over time. Patenting by immigrant inventors (relative to non-immigrants) also falls following Trump’s election. Finally, we show partisan concentration by technology class and firm. This concentration aggregates up to more patenting in Democrat-dominated technologies (e.g., Biotechnology) compared to Republican-dominated technologies (e.g., Weapons) following the 2008 election of Obama.

中文摘要

我们记录了政治情绪对美国发明家的影响。民主党发明家在2008年奥巴马当选后更有可能申请专利（相对于共和党人），但在2016年特朗普当选后则不太可能。这些影响在政治活跃的党派人士中强2-3倍，并且即使在企业内部也随时间存在。移民发明家（相对于非移民）的专利申请在特朗普当选后也有所下降。最后，我们展示了按技术类别和公司划分的党派集中度。这种集中度累积起来，导致在2008年奥巴马当选后，民主党主导的技术（例如生物技术）的专利申请比共和党主导的技术（例如武器）更多。

No. 2NBER w31618

Greed? Profits, Inflation, and Aggregate Demand

贪婪？利润、通胀与总需求

Florin O. Bilbiie, Diego R. Känzig

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：D11, E32, E52, E62

Abstract

We investigate whether corporate profits can drive elevated inflation through the interplay of income distribution dynamics and aggregate demand—our narrow definition of the “greed” narrative—within the New Keynesian framework. We first derive an analytical condition for profits to be procyclical and thus inflationary in response to demand expansions. Yet when distributional mechanisms are of the essence, as under the greed view, a conundrum emerges: procyclical profits accruing to low-MPC asset-holders imply a dampening of aggregate demand, yielding deflationary forces—the opposite of greedflation. Adding capital investment undoes part of this as it delivers aggregate-demand amplification even under procyclical profits, but the deflationary effects of the latter are still operating in a compensating way. Countercyclical income risk can deliver the amplified inflationary response; yet since this operates through a precautionary-saving channel and not profits, it is still inconsistent with the narrative directly attributing inflation to corporate greed.

中文摘要

我们研究了在新凯恩斯主义框架下，企业利润能否通过收入分配动态与总需求的相互作用（即我们对“贪婪”叙事的狭义定义）来推动通胀上升。我们首先推导出一个分析条件，说明利润何时具有顺周期性，从而在需求扩张时引发通胀。然而，当分配机制至关重要时（正如贪婪观点所认为的那样），一个难题出现了：流向低边际消费倾向资产持有者的顺周期利润意味着总需求受到抑制，从而产生通缩压力——这与贪婪通胀恰恰相反。加入资本投资部分抵消了这一效应，因为即使在利润顺周期的情况下，它也能放大总需求，但利润的通缩效应仍在以补偿方式发挥作用。反周期的收入风险可以产生放大的通胀反应；然而，由于这是通过预防性储蓄渠道而非利润渠道发挥作用，因此仍然与直接将通胀归因于企业贪婪的叙事不一致。

Protests

抗议活动

Davide Cantoni, Andrew Kao, David Y. Yang, Noam Yuchtman

Programs：POL；JEL：P0

Abstract

Citizens have long taken to the streets to demand change, expressing political views that may otherwise be suppressed. Protests have produced change at local, national, and international scales, including spectacular moments of political and social transformation. We document five new empirical patterns describing 1.2 million protest events across 218 countries between 1980 and 2020. First, autocracies and weak democracies experienced a trend break in protests during the Arab Spring. Second, protest movements also rose in importance following the Arab Spring. Third, protest movements geographically diffuse over time, spiking to their peak, before falling off. Fourth, a country’s year-to-year economic performance is not strongly correlated with protests; individual values are predictive of protest participation. Fifth, the US, China, and Russia are the most over-represented countries by their share of academic studies. We discuss each pattern’s connections to the existing literature and anticipate paths for future work.

中文摘要

长期以来，公民们走上街头要求变革，表达那些可能被压制的政治观点。抗议活动在地方、国家和国际层面带来了变革，包括政治和社会转型的壮观时刻。我们记录了描述1980年至2020年间218个国家120万起抗议事件的五个新实证模式。第一，威权政体和脆弱民主国家在阿拉伯之春期间经历了抗议活动的趋势性转折。第二，抗议运动在阿拉伯之春之后的重要性也显著上升。第三，抗议运动在时间上呈现地理扩散，达到顶峰后逐渐消退。第四，一个国家逐年的经济表现与抗议活动之间没有强相关性；个人价值观可以预测抗议参与。第五，美国、中国和俄罗斯在学术研究中所占份额被过度代表。我们讨论了每个模式与现有文献的联系，并展望了未来研究的路径。

No. 4NBER w31616

CEO Gender Bias in the Formation of Firm-to-Firm Transactions

企业间交易形成中的CEO性别偏见

Yutaro Izumi, Hitoshi Shigeoka, Masayuki Yagasaki

Programs：AG, ITI, LS, PR；JEL：D22, J16

Abstract

This study examines an unexplored channel of female CEO disadvantage: CEO gender bias in forming business networks. Using supplier-buyer linkage data from Japanese firms, we find that CEOs of the same gender are more likely to trade than those of the opposite gender, particularly in small and medium-sized rms. As most CEOs are male, this same-gender bias restricts the trading opportunities for female CEOs compared with their male counterparts. Our CEO survey revealed frictions that hinder cross-gender encounters, suggesting that creating venues to promote female-male CEO interactions could effectively achieve gender equity through expanding the business networks of female CEOs.

中文摘要

本研究探讨了女性CEO劣势的一个未被探索的渠道：在形成商业网络中的CEO性别偏见。利用日本企业的供应商-买家关联数据，我们发现同性别的CEO之间比异性别的CEO更有可能进行交易，尤其是在中小企业中。由于大多数CEO是男性，这种同性别偏见限制了女性CEO相对于男性同行的交易机会。我们的CEO调查揭示了阻碍跨性别接触的摩擦，这表明创造促进男女CEO互动的场所可以通过扩大女性CEO的商业网络来有效实现性别平等。

No. 5NBER w31615

Clean Growth

清洁增长

Costas Arkolakis, Conor Walsh

Programs：EEE, EFG, ITI；JEL：F11, Q40, Q41, Q42, Q43, R13

Abstract

We provide a spatial theory of clean growth to assess the global impact of the rise of renewable energy. We model the details of the combined production and transmission network of electricity (“the grid”) that determine the supply and losses of energy in space. The local rate of clean energy adoption depends on learning-by-doing, the global electricity and trade network, and regional comparative advantage in renewable resources. We use the model to measure the aggregate and spatial implications of clean growth. We find that the world’s power system is likely to be dominated by renewables by 2040 in a range of scenarios, with substantial welfare gains, even in the absence of policy. Incorporating policy, we find that the US Inflation Reduction Act significantly accelerates renewable uptake, and generates substantial economic benefits. In addition, planned grid improvements lower prices substantially in many areas of the US, justifying their cost of construction.

中文摘要

我们提供了一个清洁增长的空间理论，以评估可再生能源崛起的全球影响。我们对决定能源在空间上供给和损耗的电力联合生产与传输网络（即“电网”）的细节进行了建模。本地清洁能源采用率取决于干中学、全球电力和贸易网络以及区域在可再生资源上的比较优势。我们利用该模型衡量了清洁增长的总体和空间影响。我们发现，在一系列情景下，即使没有政策干预，到2040年世界电力系统很可能由可再生能源主导，并带来显著的福利收益。结合政策因素，我们发现美国《通胀削减法案》显著加速了可再生能源的采用，并产生了巨大的经济效益。此外，计划中的电网改进大幅降低了美国许多地区的电价，证明了其建设成本的合理性。

No. 6NBER w31613

Managing Mental Accounts: Payment Cards and Consumption Expenditures

管理心理账户：支付卡与消费支出

Michael Gelman, Nikolai Roussanov

Programs：AG, AP, CF, EFG, LS, ME；JEL：D01, D12, D31, D91, D99, G02, G40, G41, G5, G50, G51, G53

Abstract

Does mental accounting matter for total consumption expenditures? We exploit a unique setting in which individuals exogenously received a new credit card, without requesting one. Using random variation in the time of receipt we show that individuals temporarily increase total consumption expenditure by making purchases with the new card without reducing spending on the others. We do not observe a corresponding increase in indebtedness. Total consumption expenditure rises even for the least liquidity-constrained individuals. The evidence is consistent with consumers treating methods of payment as nonfungible budget categories, as suggested by models of mental accounting and narrow bracketing.

中文摘要

心理账户对总消费支出是否重要？我们利用一个独特的情境，即个体在未主动申请的情况下外生地收到了一张新信用卡。利用收到时间的随机变化，我们表明个体通过使用新卡进行购买而暂时增加了总消费支出，同时并未减少其他卡上的支出。我们没有观察到相应的负债增加。即使对于流动性约束最小的个体，总消费支出也会上升。这些证据与消费者将支付方式视为不可替代的预算类别的观点一致，正如心理账户和窄框架模型所建议的那样。

No. 7NBER w31612

An Experimental Evaluation of Deferred Acceptance: Evidence from Over 100 Army Officer Labor Markets

延迟接受算法的实验评估：来自超过100个陆军军官劳动力市场的证据

Jonathan M.V. Davis, Kyle Greenberg, Damon Jones

Programs：LS；JEL：D47, J01, M5

Abstract

Internal labor markets are increasingly important for matching workers to jobs within organizations. We present evidence from a randomized trial that compares matching workers to jobs using the deferred acceptance (DA) algorithm to the traditional manager-directed matching process. Our setting is the U.S. Army’s internal labor market, which matches over 14,000 officers to units annually. We find that DA reduces administrative burden and increases match quality as measured by reduced justified envy, increased truthful preference reporting, and officers’ and units’ preferences over their matches. The overall impact of DA on officer retention and performance in the two years after officers started their new jobs is limited by strategic preference coordination between officers and units. However, DA leads to significant improvements in officer retention and promotions in markets with inexperienced managers. Our findings suggest that cross-market communication between agents in internal labor markets can attenuate the benefits of strategy-proof matching algorithms.

中文摘要

内部劳动力市场在组织内部将工人与工作岗位匹配方面日益重要。我们展示了一项随机试验的证据，该试验比较了使用延迟接受算法将工人与工作岗位匹配与传统管理者主导的匹配过程。我们的背景是美国陆军的内部劳动力市场，该市场每年将超过14000名军官匹配到各个单位。我们发现，延迟接受算法减少了行政负担，并提高了匹配质量，具体表现为减少了合理的嫉妒、增加了真实偏好报告，以及军官和单位对其匹配结果的偏好。在军官开始新工作后的两年内，延迟接受算法对军官留任和绩效的总体影响受到军官与单位之间战略性偏好协调的限制。然而，在管理者经验不足的市场中，延迟接受算法显著改善了军官的留任和晋升。我们的研究结果表明，内部劳动力市场中代理人之间的跨市场沟通可能会削弱防策略匹配算法的益处。

No. 8NBER w31611

Psychosocial Constraints, Impact Heterogeneity and Spillovers in a Multifaceted Graduation Program in Kenya

肯尼亚多层面脱贫项目中的心理社会约束、影响异质性与溢出效应

Geyi Zheng, Michael Carter, Nathan Jensen, Laurel Krovetz

Programs：DEV；JEL：D91, O15

Abstract

Poverty reduction programs modeled on BRAC's graduation approach build up both tangible productive assets and intangible psychosocial assets such as self-confidence and the aspiration for upward mobility. The goal of this paper is to better understand how psychosocial factors operate and shape the impact of graduation programs. After deriving a set of hypotheses about the impacts of psychosocial constraints from a dynamic optimization model of the choice between a low income, casual wage-labor occupation and a higher earning entrepreneurial activity, this paper exploits a randomized controlled trial of a graduation program implemented in the pastoralist regions of Northern Kenya. Key empirical findings include that the estimated highly favorable average treatment effects disguise substantial heterogeneity, with beneficiaries who began with severe depressive symptoms gaining little from the program. The RCT's saturation design also allows us to identify substantial spillover effects onto the asset accumulation of women who were not enrolled in the graduation program. Spillovers are also estimated to positively affect non-beneficiary women's preference for upward economic mobility, providing a plausible explanation for their accumulation of capital despite no direct support from the graduation program. The paper draws out the implications of these findings for the cost-effective design and implementation of graduation programs.

中文摘要

以BRAC脱贫方法为蓝本的减贫项目既积累有形生产性资产，也积累无形的心理社会资产，如自信和向上流动的愿望。本文旨在更好地理解心理社会因素如何运作并塑造脱贫项目的影响。在从动态优化模型中推导出一组关于心理社会约束影响的假设后（该模型模拟了在低收入、临时工资劳动职业与高收入创业活动之间的选择），本文利用在肯尼亚北部牧区实施的一个脱贫项目的随机对照试验。关键实证发现包括：估计出的非常有利的平均处理效应掩盖了显著的异质性，那些最初有严重抑郁症状的受益者从项目中获益甚少。该随机对照试验的饱和设计还使我们能够识别出对未参加脱贫项目的女性资产积累的显著溢出效应。据估计，溢出效应还积极影响了非受益女性对向上经济流动的偏好，这为她们尽管没有直接从脱贫项目中获得支持却仍能积累资本提供了合理解释。本文阐述了这些发现对脱贫项目成本效益设计和实施的意义。

No. 9NBER w31610

Firm Export Dynamics in Interdependent Markets

相互依存市场中的企业出口动态

Alonso Alfaro-Urena, Juanma Castro-Vincenzi, Sebastian Fanelli, Eduardo Morales

Programs：ITI；JEL：F12, F13, F14

Abstract

We estimate a model of firm export dynamics featuring cross-country complementarities. The firm decides where to export by solving a dynamic combinatorial discrete choice problem, for which we develop a solution algorithm that overcomes the computational challenges inherent to the large dimensionality of its state space and choice set. According to our estimated model, firms enjoy cost reductions when exporting to countries geographically or linguistically close to each other, or that share deep trade agreements. Countries, especially small ones, sharing these traits with attractive destinations receive significantly more exports than in the absence of complementarities.

中文摘要

我们估计了一个具有跨国互补性的企业出口动态模型。企业通过解决一个动态组合离散选择问题来决定出口目的地，为此我们开发了一种求解算法，克服了其状态空间和选择集维度高所带来的计算挑战。根据我们估计的模型，当企业向地理或语言相近、或共享深度贸易协定的国家出口时，会享受成本降低。与没有互补性的情况相比，与有吸引力的目的地共享这些特征的国家（尤其是小国）获得的出口显著更多。

No. 10NBER w31609

From Addiction to Aggression: The Spillover Effects of Opioid Policies on Intimate Partner Violence

从成瘾到攻击：阿片类药物政策对亲密伴侣暴力的溢出效应

Dhaval M. Dave, Bilge Erten, Pinar Keskin, Shuo Zhang

Programs：CH, EH, LE, PE；JEL：H0, I12, I18, K0

Abstract

Substance abuse is a major risk factor for intimate partner violence (IPV). We investigate how a key supply-side intervention – the abuse-deterrent reformulation of a widely-diverted opioid, OxyContin – affected IPV. Our results indicate counties with greater baseline rates of prescription opioid usage experienced relatively larger declines in IPV after OxyContin’s reformulation. The reformulation reduced IPV only in states with small illicit drug markets, while states with large illicit drug markets experienced increased heroin-involved IPV due to substitution towards illicit opioids. Our results underscore the importance of identifying populations at high risk of substitution to illicit opioids and moderating this risk with evidence-based policies.

中文摘要

药物滥用是亲密伴侣暴力的主要风险因素。我们研究了一项关键的供给侧干预措施——对广泛被滥用的阿片类药物奥施康定进行防滥用配方改革——如何影响亲密伴侣暴力。我们的结果表明，在奥施康定配方改革后，处方阿片类药物使用基线率较高的县，亲密伴侣暴力出现了相对较大的下降。该改革仅在非法药物市场规模较小的州减少了亲密伴侣暴力，而在非法药物市场规模较大的州，由于转向非法阿片类药物，涉及海洛因的亲密伴侣暴力有所增加。我们的结果强调了识别转向非法阿片类药物高风险人群，并通过循证政策来调节这一风险的重要性。

No. 11NBER w31608

Distributional Equity in the Employment and Wage Impacts of Energy Transitions

能源转型对就业和工资影响的分配公平性

Ben Gilbert, Hannah Gagarin, Ben Hoen

Programs：EEE；JEL：Q4, Q42, Q43, R11, R12

Abstract

We use restricted-access, geocoded data on the near-universe of workers in 23 U.S. states in order to quantify the impact of wind energy development on local earnings and employment, by race, ethnicity, sex, and educational attainment. We find the largest relative impacts for workers without a high school education, or workers with a college education, in addition to other systematic differences across sub-populations. We compare these results to estimates using county aggregates of the worker-level data, such as can be obtained using publicly available data. We find that (a) county-level estimates are dramatically dampened relative to geocoded worker-level estimates, and (b) the degree of bias differs by sub-population such that qualitative comparisons of impacts are not consistent using restricted-access data versus county-level data for most sub-populations. We discuss implications for achieving equity goals within energy transition policies.

中文摘要

我们使用美国23个州近乎所有工人的受限访问地理编码数据，按种族、民族、性别和教育程度量化风能发展对当地收入和就业的影响。我们发现，除各子群体之间存在其他系统性差异外，对未接受高中教育或接受大学教育的工人的相对影响最大。我们将这些结果与使用县级工人数据汇总（例如可从公开数据中获得）的估计值进行比较。我们发现：(a) 县级估计值相对于地理编码工人层面的估计值显著减弱，并且 (b) 偏差程度因子群体而异，因此对于大多数子群体，使用受限访问数据与县级数据得出的影响定性比较并不一致。我们讨论了在能源转型政策中实现公平目标的意义。

No. 12NBER w31607

Biased Surveys

有偏的调查

Luca Gemmi, Rosen Valchev

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：C53, D84, E31, E37, E70

Abstract

We find evidence suggesting that surveys of professional forecasters are biased by strategic incentives. First, we find that individual forecasts overreact to idiosyncratic information but underreact to common information. Second, we show that this bias is not present in forecasts data that is not subject to strategic incentives. We show that our evidence is consistent with a model of strategic diversification incentives in forecast reporting. Our results caution against the use of surveys of forecasts as a direct measure of expectations, as this would overestimate the likely deviations from rational expectations, the information precision and the degree of disagreement.

Seasonal Allergies and Accidents

季节性过敏与事故

Mika Akesaka, Hitoshi Shigeoka

Programs：AG, EEE, EH, LS, PE, PR；JEL：I12, J24, Q51, Q53, Q54

Abstract

Seasonal allergies affect over 400 million people globally, yet the broader economic consequences of pollen exposure remain understudied. Evidence from Japan’s ambulance records suggests that high-pollen days are associated with increases in accidents, including traffic accidents and work-related injuries, which may reflect impaired cognitive performance. Retail scanner data and cellphone mobility records indicate that individuals already engage in avoidance behaviors, such as purchasing allergy products and limiting outdoor activities on weekends. This suggests that relying on individual self-protection may be insufficient to offset these risks, and thus greater government intervention may be warranted to mitigate pollen-related harm.

Causal Effects of Renewable Portfolio Standards on Renewable Investments and Generation: The Role of Heterogeneity and Dynamics

可再生能源组合标准对可再生能源投资与发电的因果效应：异质性与动态性的作用

Olivier Deschenes, Christopher Malloy, Gavin G. McDonald

Programs：EEE；JEL：Q20, Q42, Q50

Abstract

Despite a 30-year long history, Renewable Portfolio Standards (RPS) remain controversial and debates continue to surround their efficacy in leading the low-carbon transition in the electricity sector. Contributing to the ongoing debates is the lack of definitive causal evidence on their impact on investments in renewable capacity and generation. This paper provides the most detailed analysis to date of the impact of RPSs on renewable electricity capacity investments and on generation. We use state-level data from 1990-2019 and recent econometric methods designed to address dynamic and heterogeneous treatment effects in a staggered adoption panel data design. We find that, on average, RPS policies increase wind generation capacity by 600-1200 MW, a 44% increase, but have no significant effect on investments in solar capacity. Additionally, we demonstrate that RPSs have slow dynamic effects: most of the capacity additions occur 5 years after RPS implementation. Estimates for wind and solar electricity generation mimic those for capacity investments. We also find similar results using an alternate treatment definition that allows states to meet their RPS requirements with pre-existing renewable generation and renewable generation from nearby states.
