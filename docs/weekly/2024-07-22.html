The Social Value of Temporary Carbon Removals and Delayed Emissions

临时碳移除与延迟排放的社会价值

Ben Groom, Frank Venmans

Programs：EEE；JEL：Q54, Q57

Abstract

An economic approach to calculating the Social Value of Temporary Reductions (SVTR) in atmospheric carbon is discussed. The SVTR allows different carbon removals projects to be prioritised in a way that maximises welfare and establishes equivalence between temporary, risky removals with permanent ones in terms of avoided welfare losses from climate damages. The approach is compared to previous attempts in the physical and natural sciences and economics to price temporary emissions reductions, none of which successfully integrate economics and climate science. Applications of the SVTR exist in public project appraisal, Life Cycle Analysis, pricing carbon debts and determining short term carbon credit and offset contracts. Potential criticisms of equivalence measures and tonne-year accounting stemming from concerns that temporary removals do not impact long-term temperatures are shown to be special cases of our integrated economic approach. Temporary removals provide transitory cooling benefits and if repeated are equivalent to permanent solutions. They also can have permanent effects via learning by doing or reducing the likelihood of tipping points. The SVTR helps determine how temporary removals can fit into an efficient response to climate. Ruling out temporary removals and equivalence, and the intertemporal transfers that they imply, could unnecessarily tie the hands of policy makers.

中文摘要

本文讨论了一种计算大气碳临时减排社会价值的经济学方法。临时减排社会价值允许以最大化福利的方式对不同碳移除项目进行优先排序，并在避免气候损害造成的福利损失方面，建立临时性、有风险的移除与永久性移除之间的等价关系。该方法与以往物理学、自然科学和经济学中为临时减排定价的尝试进行了比较，这些尝试均未能成功整合经济学与气候科学。临时减排社会价值的应用存在于公共项目评估、生命周期分析、碳债务定价以及确定短期碳信用和碳抵消合同之中。对等价措施和吨年核算的潜在批评——源于担忧临时移除不会影响长期温度——被证明是我们综合经济方法的特例。临时移除提供暂时的降温效益，如果重复进行，则等同于永久性解决方案。它们还可以通过干中学或降低临界点可能性来产生永久性影响。临时减排社会价值有助于确定临时移除如何融入对气候的有效应对。排除临时移除和等价性及其所隐含的跨期转移，可能会不必要地束缚政策制定者的手脚。

The Shifting Finance of Electricity Generation

发电领域的金融格局变迁

Aleksandar Andonov, Joshua Rauh

Programs：CF, EEE；JEL：G23, G24, G32, H54, L51, L71, L94, O13, Q41, Q48

Abstract

Private equity (PE), institutional investors, and foreign corporations own 58% of wind, 47% of solar, and 34% of natural gas electricity generation. These new entrants are twice as likely to create new power plants as incumbent domestic listed utilities, highlighting a new role for PE in large-scale asset creation. They also acquire existing plants. While fossil-fuel plant sales to foreign corporations extend operations, PE has similar decommissioning rates to incumbents. The new owners create more efficient plants and improve acquired ones. Market deregulation drives the results, highlighting the dual importance of competition and new financing for both creation and acquisitions.

中文摘要

私募股权、机构投资者和外国公司拥有58%的风力发电、47%的太阳能发电和34%的天然气发电。这些新进入者创建新发电厂的可能性是现有国内上市公用事业公司的两倍，突显了私募股权在大规模资产创建中的新角色。它们也收购现有电厂。虽然化石燃料电厂出售给外国公司会延长运营，但私募股权的退役率与现有企业相似。新所有者创建了更高效的工厂，并改进了收购的工厂。市场放松管制推动了这些结果，凸显了竞争和新融资对于创建和收购的双重重要性。

Different Paths to College Success: The Impact of Massachusetts’ Charter Schools on College Trajectories

通往大学成功的不同路径：马萨诸塞州特许学校对大学轨迹的影响

Sarah Cohodes, Astrid Pineda

Programs：CH, ED, LS, PE；JEL：H75, I21

Abstract

The charter school movement encompasses many school models. In Massachusetts in the 2010’s, the site of our study, urban charter schools primarily used “No Excuses” practices, whereas nonurban charters had greater model variety. Using randomized admissions lotteries, we estimate the impact of charter schools by locality on college preparation, enrollment, and graduation. Urban charter schools boost all of these outcomes. Nonurban charter schools raise college enrollment and graduation despite reducing state test scores and AP enrollment. Our results suggest that there is more than one path to a college degree and that test score impacts may not predict college outcomes.

中文摘要

特许学校运动包含多种学校模式。在我们研究的所在地马萨诸塞州，2010年代的城市特许学校主要采用“不找借口”实践，而非城市特许学校则有更大的模式多样性。利用随机入学抽签，我们估算了按地区划分的特许学校对大学准备、入学和毕业的影响。城市特许学校提升了所有这些结果。非城市特许学校提高了大学入学率和毕业率，尽管降低了州考试成绩和大学先修课程注册率。我们的结果表明，通往大学学位的路径不止一条，考试成绩的影响可能无法预测大学结果。

The Speed of Firm Response to Inflation

企业对通胀的反应速度

Ivan Yotzov, Nicholas Bloom, Philip Bunn, Paul Mizen, Gregory Thwaites

Programs：ME；JEL：C83, D22, D84, E31

Abstract

This paper analyses the response of UK firms to monthly CPI inflation releases using high-frequency data from a large business survey. Firms’ inflation perceptions and expectations respond within hours of new inflation data releases. Firm expectations are most responsive when inflation coverage in the media is elevated, suggesting a key role for the media in focusing attention on data releases. Furthermore, firms respond to changes in inflation data, but not to surprises relative to professional forecasts. This highlights a distinction between “Wall Street”, where financial markets respond to inflation surprises, and “Main Street”, where firms respond to media inflation headlines.

中文摘要

本文利用一项大型商业调查的高频数据，分析了英国企业对月度消费者价格指数通胀发布的反应。企业的通胀感知和预期会在新的通胀数据发布后数小时内做出反应。当媒体对通胀的报道增多时，企业预期的反应最为强烈，这表明媒体在引导关注数据发布方面发挥着关键作用。此外，企业对通胀数据的变化做出反应，但对相对于专业预测的意外情况没有反应。这凸显了“华尔街”（金融市场对通胀意外做出反应）与“主街”（企业对媒体通胀头条做出反应）之间的区别。

Mispricing Narratives after Social Unrest

社会动荡后的错误定价叙事

Bocar A. Ba, Abdoulaye Ndiaye, Roman Rivera, Alexander Whitefield

Programs：LE, POL；JEL：D72, D74, D84, G41

Abstract

We study how negative sentiment around an industry impacts beliefs and behaviors, focusing on demands for racial justice after the murder of George Floyd and the salience of the “defund the police” movement. We assess stakeholder beliefs on the impact of protests on the stock prices of police-affiliated firms. In our survey experiment, laypeople and finance professionals predicted more negative stock price outcomes when they lacked details on the products supplied by such firms. Exposure to narratives about the context of the protests further reduced the prediction accuracy of these groups. In contrast, product information improved the prediction accuracy of respondents. Turning to real-life behavior, we find that mutual funds exposed to protests were 20% less likely to hold police stocks, after the protests, than funds in areas without protests. Political support for maintaining police funding, though in the majority, declined by 4.3 percentage points in protest areas. The salience of the “defund the police” narrative led to significant overreactions in both financial predictions and real-life behavior.

中文摘要

我们研究了围绕某个行业的负面情绪如何影响信念和行为，重点关注乔治·弗洛伊德被谋杀后对种族正义的诉求以及“撤资警察”运动的凸显性。我们评估了利益相关者对抗议活动对警察关联公司股价影响的信念。在我们的调查实验中，当缺乏这些公司所供应产品的详细信息时，普通人和金融专业人士预测了更负面的股价结果。接触关于抗议背景的叙事进一步降低了这些群体的预测准确性。相比之下，产品信息提高了受访者的预测准确性。转向现实行为，我们发现，与没有抗议地区的基金相比，暴露于抗议活动的共同基金在抗议后持有警察股票的可能性降低了20%。尽管维持警察经费的政治支持仍占多数，但在抗议地区下降了4.3个百分点。“撤资警察”叙事的凸显性导致了金融预测和现实行为中的显著过度反应。

The Effects of Wealth on Health Care Spending: Evidence from the Housing Market

财富对医疗支出的影响：来自房地产市场的证据

Michael F. Lovenheim, Jun Hyun Yun

Programs：EH, PE；JEL：G51, I11, I14

Abstract

The U.S. healthcare system requires substantial out-of-pocket payments by most consumers, which can prevent some from receiving needed medical services. Recent policy proposals seek to address this problem by increasing government health care spending in order to reduce out-of-pocket costs. The social value of these policies rests in part on the extent to which consumers face credit constraints in financing medical spending. We present a novel analysis of whether elderly households face such credit constraints by estimating the effects of wealth variation from home price changes on out-of-pocket medical expenditures. Using data from the Health and Retirement Study and various measures of home price changes, we find no evidence that housing wealth impacts out-of-pocket medical spending. The estimates are universally small and precise, allowing us to rule out even modest-sized effects. Effects are zero across the expenditure distribution, for specific categories of expenditure, and for different types of consumers split by health insurance status and SES. Our results suggest that further subsidizing health care for elderly homeowners, the majority of older Americans, would increase moral hazard costs without increasing access to needed care.

中文摘要

美国医疗体系要求大多数消费者承担大量自付费用，这可能阻碍部分人获得必要的医疗服务。近期政策提案试图通过增加政府医疗支出来降低自付成本，从而解决这一问题。这些政策的社会价值部分取决于消费者在融资医疗支出时面临信贷约束的程度。我们通过估计房价变动带来的财富变化对自付医疗支出的影响，对老年家庭是否面临此类信贷约束进行了新颖的分析。利用健康与退休研究的数据以及多种房价变动指标，我们没有发现住房财富影响自付医疗支出的证据。估计结果普遍较小且精确，使我们能够排除即使是中等程度的影响。在支出分布的各分位点、特定支出类别以及按医疗保险状况和社会经济地位划分的不同类型消费者中，影响均为零。我们的结果表明，进一步补贴老年房主（占美国老年人多数）的医疗保健，将增加道德风险成本，而不会增加对必要医疗服务的获取。

A Welfare Analysis of Policies Impacting Climate Change

影响气候变化的政策福利分析

Robert W. Hahn, Nathaniel Hendren, Robert D. Metcalfe, Ben Sprung-Keyser

Programs：DEV, EEE, PE, PR；JEL：H0, Q5

Abstract

We study the welfare impacts of 96 climate-related tax and spending policies. We extend and apply the marginal value of public funds (MVPF) framework, most notably providing a new method for incorporating learning-by-doing spillovers. We find subsidies for the production of clean energy (such as wind production tax credits) have higher MVPFs than all other subsidies in our sample, including EV subsidies. Conservation nudges have large MVPFs when targeting regions with dirty grids. Fuel taxes and cap-and-trade policies are highly efficient means of raising revenue. We also construct traditional cost-per-ton estimates and compare and contrast the lessons they provide.

中文摘要

我们研究了96项与气候相关的税收和支出政策的福利影响。我们扩展并应用了公共资金边际价值框架，最显著的是提供了一种纳入干中学溢出效应的新方法。我们发现，清洁能源生产补贴（如风电生产税收抵免）的公共资金边际价值高于我们样本中的所有其他补贴，包括电动汽车补贴。当针对电网污染严重的地区时，节能助推措施具有较高的公共资金边际价值。燃油税和限额交易政策是高效的财政收入筹集手段。我们还构建了传统的每吨成本估算，并比较和对比了它们提供的经验教训。

Do Mergers and Acquisitions Improve Efficiency? Evidence from Power Plants

并购能提高效率吗？来自发电厂的证据

Mert Demirer, Ömer Karaduman

Programs：IO；JEL：L22, L25, L40

Abstract

Using rich data on hourly physical productivity and thousands of ownership changes from US power plants, we study the effects of acquisitions on efficiency and underlying mechanisms. We find a 2% average increase in efficiency for acquired plants, beginning five months after acquisitions. Efficiency gains rise to 5% under direct ownership changes, with no significant change when only parent ownership changes. Investigating the mechanisms, three-quarters of the efficiency gain is attributed to increased productive efficiency, while the rest comes from dynamic efficiency through changes in production allocation. Our evidence suggests that high-productivity firms buy underperforming assets from low-productivity firms and make them as productive as their existing assets through operational improvements. Finally, acquired plants improve their performance beyond efficiency by increasing output and reducing outages.

中文摘要

利用美国发电厂每小时实物生产率的丰富数据以及数千次所有权变更，我们研究了收购对效率的影响及其潜在机制。我们发现，被收购工厂的效率平均提高2%，这一效应在收购后五个月开始显现。在直接所有权变更的情况下，效率提升达到5%，而当仅母公司所有权变更时，没有显著变化。通过探究机制，四分之三的效率提升归因于生产效率的提高，其余部分则来自通过生产分配变化实现的动态效率。我们的证据表明，高生产率企业从低生产率企业购买表现不佳的资产，并通过运营改进使其达到与现有资产相同的生产率水平。最后，被收购工厂通过增加产出和减少停机时间，在效率之外还改善了整体绩效。

Digital Advertising and Market Structure: Implications for Privacy Regulation

数字广告与市场结构：对隐私监管的启示

Daniel Deisenroth, Utsav Manjeer, Zarak Sohail, Steven Tadelis, Nils Wernerfelt

Programs：IO, PR；JEL：D22, D40, L10, L59, M38

Abstract

We study how changes in the effectiveness of targeted digital advertising shape firm behavior and product-market outcomes. We leverage the introduction of Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, which limited online advertisers' access to user data and thus substantially reduced their advertising effectiveness. Using proprietary advertiser-level data from Meta and industry-level outcomes from the Bureau of Labor Statistics, we show that greater exposure to the ATT shock led to lower ad spend, fewer establishments, and higher product prices. The findings suggest that digital advertising primarily expands markets and that privacy policies can have unintended consequences on product market competition.

中文摘要

我们研究了定向数字广告有效性的变化如何塑造企业行为和产品市场结果。我们利用了苹果应用追踪透明度框架的引入，该框架限制了在线广告商对用户数据的访问，从而大幅降低了其广告效果。利用来自Meta的专有广告商层面数据以及劳工统计局的行业层面结果，我们发现，对应用追踪透明度冲击暴露程度越高，导致广告支出越低、企业数量越少、产品价格越高。这些发现表明，数字广告主要起到扩大市场的作用，而隐私政策可能对产品市场竞争产生意想不到的后果。

Production Function Estimation Using Subjective Expectations Data

利用主观预期数据估计生产函数

Agnes Norris Keiller, Áureo de Paula, John Van Reenen

Programs：PR；JEL：C21, C23, L11, L23, O31

Abstract

Standard methods for estimating production functions in the Olley and Pakes (1996) tradition require assumptions on input choices. We introduce a new method that exploits (increasingly available) data on a firm’s expectations of its future output and inputs that allows us to obtain consistent production function parameter estimates while relaxing these input demand assumptions. In contrast to dynamic panel methods, our proposed estimator can be implemented on very short panels (including a single cross-section), and Monte Carlo simulations show it outperforms alternative estimators when firms’ material input choices are subject to optimization error. Implementing a range of production function estimators on UK data, we find our proposed estimator yields results that are either similar to or more credible than commonly-used alternatives. These differences are larger in industries where material inputs appear harder to optimize. We show that TFP implied by our proposed estimator is more strongly associated with future jobs growth than existing methods, suggesting that failing to adequately account for input endogeneity may underestimate the degree of dynamic reallocation in the economy.

中文摘要

在Olley和Pakes（1996）传统中估计生产函数的标准方法需要对投入选择做出假设。我们引入了一种新方法，该方法利用（日益可得的）企业对其未来产出和投入的预期数据，使我们能够在放宽这些投入需求假设的情况下获得一致的生产函数参数估计。与动态面板方法相比，我们提出的估计量可以在非常短的面板数据（包括单个横截面）上实施，并且蒙特卡洛模拟表明，当企业的材料投入选择存在优化误差时，该估计量优于其他替代估计量。在英国数据上实施一系列生产函数估计量，我们发现，我们提出的估计量得出的结果与常用的替代方法要么相似，要么更可信。在材料投入似乎更难优化的行业中，这些差异更大。我们表明，与现有方法相比，我们提出的估计量所隐含的全要素生产率与未来就业增长的相关性更强，这表明未能充分解释投入内生性可能会低估经济中的动态再配置程度。

Is Maturity-Transformation Risk Priced into Bank Deposit Rates?

期限转换风险是否被定价在银行存款利率中？

Matthias Fleckenstein, Francis A. Longstaff

Programs：AP；JEL：G12, G21

Abstract

We use the term structure of bank CD rates to examine whether maturity-transformation risk is priced into the rates banks offer customers. We find that depositors pay a significant cost for the liquidity provided by bank deposits. This cost is strongly related to the amount of maturity-transformation risk that these deposit accounts create. The cost is also negatively correlated with the convenience premia in Treasury markets, which suggests that households do not view deposit liquidity and Treasury liquidity as perfect substitutes. The results have important implications about the role of deposit franchises and market power in banking markets.

中文摘要

我们利用银行存单利率的期限结构来检验期限转换风险是否被定价在银行向客户提供的利率中。我们发现，存款人为银行存款提供的流动性支付了显著的成本。这一成本与这些存款账户所产生的期限转换风险量密切相关。该成本也与国债市场中的便利收益负相关，这表明家庭并不将存款流动性和国债流动性视为完全替代品。这些结果对于存款特许经营权的作用以及银行市场中的市场势力具有重要启示。

Intergenerational Spillovers of Driving Privileges for Undocumented Immigrants: Evidence from Early Childhood Education

Abstract

We study the intergenerational impact of granting driver’s licenses to undocumented immigrants on early childhood education (ECE) participation among their U.S.-born children. Abstract

Armed groups routinely delegate domains of rule to village customary chiefs—indirect rule. The larger a chief’s power over the villagers relative to the group’s, the more there is indirect rule. Abstract

Applied general-equilibrium (AGE) models have often made compromises to circumvent difficult modeling problems. One of these is avoiding endogenous zeros, ruling out important questions. Traditional perfect competition models: when do technologies or trade links switch from active to inactive or vice versa? Heterogeneous firms: what types of firms are active in equilibrium? Multinationals: when do firms switch from exporting to foreign production? Capacity constraints: could trade links or production sectors hit capacity limits? Here I exploit the complementarity approach to general equilibrium, focusing on modeling heterogeneous firms and endogenous multinational production. Instead of the traditional continuum formulation, there is a discrete and finite set of firm types, differing in marginal costs across but not within types. There is an upper bound on the number of firms that can enter in each firm type. Formulated as a non-linear complementarity problem, we can solve for the set of active firm types in relation to characteristics of the economy such as size or trade costs and their modes of operation: no entry, domestic, exporting, multinational. The analysis easily incorporates endogenous markups and positive aggregate profits. Productivities can be calculated directly from data and no integrals/integration/parametric distributions are required.

Programs：POL；JEL：C91, D74, D91, N46

Abstract

Social norms may underpin such behavior, but they can also undermine it.
