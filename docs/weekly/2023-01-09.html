No. 1NBER w30838

Urban Renewal and Inequality: Evidence from Chicago's Public Housing Demolitions

城市更新与不平等：来自芝加哥公共住房拆除的证据

Milena Almagro, Eric Chyn, Bryan A. Stuart

Programs：LS, PE；JEL：I31, R23, R28

Abstract

This paper studies one of the largest spatially targeted redevelopment efforts implemented in the United States: public housing demolitions sponsored by the HOPE VI program. Focusing on Chicago, we study welfare and racial disparities in the impacts of demolitions using a structural model that features a rich set of equilibrium responses. Our results indicate that demolitions had notably heterogeneous effects where welfare decreased for low-income minority households and increased for White households. Counterfactual simulations explore how housing policy mitigates negative effects of demolitions and suggest that increased public housing site redevelopment is the most effective policy for reducing racial inequality.

中文摘要

本文研究了美国实施的最大规模的空间定向再开发项目之一：由HOPE VI计划资助的公共住房拆除。以芝加哥为研究对象，我们利用一个包含丰富均衡反应的结构模型，研究了拆除对福利和种族差异的影响。我们的结果表明，拆除产生了显著异质性的影响，其中低收入少数族裔家庭的福利下降，而白人家庭的福利上升。反事实模拟探讨了住房政策如何减轻拆除的负面影响，并表明增加公共住房场地再开发是减少种族不平等的最有效政策。

No. 2NBER w30837

Language Environment and Maternal Expectations: An Evaluation of the LENA Start Program

语言环境与母亲期望：LENA Start项目评估

Flavio Cunha, Marsha Gerdes, Qinyou Hu, Snejana Nihtianova

Programs：CH；JEL：I21, I24

Abstract

Research documents that parental beliefs influence early investments in children, which, in turn, determine early human capital and, eventually, other skills children acquire in later stages of the lifecycle, such as literacy. Our paper reports the results of an experimental evaluation of the LENA Start Program, a group- and center-based parenting program that teaches the science of early language development, models verbal interaction behaviors with children, and provides objective feedback to improve the early language environment. The intervention changes parental beliefs and impacts the quantity and quality of parental linguistic input.

中文摘要

研究表明，父母的信念会影响对儿童的早期投资，而这又决定了早期人力资本，并最终影响儿童在生命周期后期阶段获得的其他技能，例如读写能力。本文报告了LENA Start项目的实验评估结果，该项目是一个以小组和中心为基础的育儿项目，教授早期语言发展的科学知识，示范与儿童进行语言互动的行为，并提供客观反馈以改善早期语言环境。该干预措施改变了父母的信念，并影响了父母语言输入的数量和质量。

No. 3NBER w30836

Willingness to Accept, Willingness to Pay, and Loss Aversion

意愿接受、意愿支付与损失厌恶

Jonathan Chapman, Mark Dean, Pietro Ortoleva, Erik Snowberg, Colin Camerer

Programs：EEE, PE；JEL：C90, D81, D91

Abstract

We use four incentivized representative surveys to study the endowment effect for lotteries in 4,000 U.S. adults. We replicate the standard finding of an endowment effect—the divergence between Willingness to Accept (WTA) and Willingness to Pay (WTP), but document three new findings. First, we find little evidence that the endowment effect is related to loss aversion for risky prospects, counter to predictions of popular theories in economics. Second, WTA and WTP not only diverge, but are, at best, weakly correlated. Third, WTA and WTP strongly relate to other aspects of risk preferences. The structure of these behaviors points to different theories of the endowment effect.

中文摘要

我们利用四项激励相容的代表性调查，研究了4000名美国成年人对彩票的禀赋效应。我们复制了禀赋效应的标准发现——意愿接受（WTA）与意愿支付（WTP）之间的差异，但记录了三个新发现。首先，我们发现几乎没有证据表明禀赋效应与风险前景的损失厌恶有关，这与经济学中流行理论的预测相反。其次，WTA和WTP不仅存在差异，而且充其量只是弱相关。第三，WTA和WTP与风险偏好的其他方面密切相关。这些行为的结构指向了关于禀赋效应的不同理论。

No. 4NBER w30835

Water Treatment And Child Mortality: A Meta-Analysis And Cost-effectiveness Analysis

水处理与儿童死亡率：一项荟萃分析与成本效益分析

Michael Kremer, Stephen P. Luby, Ricardo Maertens, Brandon Tan, Witold Więcek

Programs：DEV；JEL：I1

Abstract

Randomized controlled trials (RCTs) of water treatment are typically powered to detect effects on caregiver-reported diarrhea but not child mortality, as detecting mortality effects requires prohibitively large sample sizes. To increase statistical power, we conducted a systematic review and meta-analysis. We replicated search and selection criteria from previous meta-analyses of RCTs aimed at improving water quality to prevent diarrhea in low- or middle-income countries which included children under 5 years old. We identified 52 RCTs and then obtained child mortality data from each study for which these data were collected and available, contacting authors of the study where necessary; this resulted in 18 studies. Frequentist and Bayesian methods were used to estimate the intent-to-treat effect of water treatment on child mortality among included studies. We estimated a mean cross-study reduction in the odds of all-cause under-5 mortality of 24-27% (frequentist odds ratio, OR, 0.76; 95% CI 0.63 to 0.93; Bayes OR 0.73; 95% CI 0.52 to 0.95). The results were qualitatively similar under alternative modeling and data inclusion choices. Taking into account heterogeneity across studies, the expected reduction in a new study is 23%. We used the results to examine the cost-effectiveness of three water treatment approaches, point-of-collection chlorine dispensers, inline chlorination, and a program providing free chlorine solution through maternal and child health (MCH) services. After accounting for delivery costs, we estimate a cost per expected DALY averted due to water treatment between USD 28 and USD 72, depending on approach. This suggests that water treatment is one of the most cost-effective health programs available.

中文摘要

水处理的随机对照试验（RCT）通常以检测看护者报告的腹泻效应为统计功效设计目标，而非儿童死亡率，因为检测死亡率效应需要过大的样本量。为了提高统计功效，我们进行了系统综述和荟萃分析。我们复制了先前旨在改善中低收入国家5岁以下儿童饮用水质量以预防腹泻的RCT荟萃分析中的检索和筛选标准。我们识别出52项RCT，然后从每项收集并提供了儿童死亡率数据的研究中获取这些数据，必要时联系了研究作者；最终得到18项研究。我们使用频率学派和贝叶斯方法估计了纳入研究中水处理对儿童死亡率的意向性治疗效应。我们估计，跨研究平均而言，全因5岁以下儿童死亡率的比值降低了24-27%（频率学派比值比OR为0.76，95%置信区间0.63至0.93；贝叶斯比值比OR为0.73，95%置信区间0.52至0.95）。在替代建模和数据纳入选择下，结果定性相似。考虑到研究间的异质性，一项新研究中预期的降低幅度为23%。我们利用这些结果检验了三种水处理方法的成本效益：取水点氯分配器、管道氯化以及通过妇幼保健服务免费提供氯溶液的项目。在考虑交付成本后，我们估计因水处理而避免的每预期伤残调整生命年（DALY）的成本在28美元至72美元之间，具体取决于方法。这表明水处理是可用的最具成本效益的健康项目之一。

No. 5NBER w30834

An Anatomy of Crypto-Enabled Cybercrimes

加密货币网络犯罪剖析

Lin William Cong, Campbell R. Harvey, Daniel Rabetti, Zong-Yu Wu

Programs：AP, CF, IFM, PR；JEL：H56, K24, O30

Abstract

While the advent of cryptocurrencies and digital assets holds promise for improving and disrupting financial systems by offering cheap, quick, and secure transfer of value, it also opens up new payment channels for cybercrimes. A prerequisite to solving a problem is understanding the nature of the problem. Assembling a diverse set of public, proprietary, and hand-collected data, including dark web conversations in Russian, we conduct the first detailed anatomy of crypto-enabled cybercrimes and highlight relevant economic issues. Our analyses reveal that a few organized ransomware gangs dominate the space and have evolved into sophisticated corporate-like operations with physical offices, franchising, and affiliation programs. Their techniques have also become more aggressive over time, entailing multiple layers of extortion and reputation management. Blanket restrictions on cryptocurrency usage may prove ineffective in tackling crypto-enabled cybercrime and hinder innovations. Instead, blockchain transparency and digital footprints enable effective forensics for tracking, monitoring, and shutting down dominant cybercriminal organizations.

中文摘要

尽管加密货币和数字资产的出现有望通过提供廉价、快速和安全的价值转移来改善和颠覆金融体系，但它也为网络犯罪开辟了新的支付渠道。解决问题的前提是理解问题的本质。通过整合包括俄语暗网对话在内的多种公开、专有和手工收集的数据，我们首次对加密货币网络犯罪进行了详细的剖析，并强调了相关的经济问题。我们的分析显示，少数有组织的勒索软件团伙主导了这一领域，并且已经演变成类似企业的复杂运作模式，拥有实体办公室、特许经营和联盟计划。随着时间的推移，他们的技术也变得更加激进，涉及多层勒索和声誉管理。对加密货币使用的全面限制可能无法有效打击加密货币网络犯罪，反而会阻碍创新。相反，区块链的透明度和数字足迹能够为追踪、监控和打击主要网络犯罪组织提供有效的取证手段。

No. 6NBER w30833

Where Are the Workers? From Great Resignation to Quiet Quitting

工人在哪里？从“大辞职潮”到“安静辞职”

Dain Lee, Jinhyeok Park, Yongseok Shin

Programs：EFG；JEL：J21, J22

Abstract

To better understand the tight post-pandemic labor market in the US, we decompose the decline in aggregate hours worked into the extensive (fewer people working) and the intensive margin changes (workers working fewer hours). Although the pre-existing trend of lower labor force participation especially by young men without a bachelor's degree accounts for some of the decline in aggregate hours, the intensive margin accounts for more than half of the decline between 2019 and 2022. The decline in hours among workers was larger for men than women. Among men, the decline was larger for those with a bachelor's degree than those with less education, for prime-age workers than older workers, and also for those who already worked long hours and had high earnings. Workers' hours reduction can explain why the labor market is even tighter than what is expected at the current levels of unemployment and labor force participation.

中文摘要

为了更好地理解美国疫情后紧张的劳动力市场，我们将总工作时间的下降分解为广延边际（工作人数减少）和集约边际（工人工作时间减少）的变化。尽管劳动力参与率下降的既有趋势（尤其是没有学士学位的年轻男性）解释了总工作时间下降的一部分原因，但集约边际解释了2019年至2022年间下降的一半以上。工人工作时间的减少在男性中比女性更大。在男性中，拥有学士学位的男性比受教育程度较低的男性减少得更多，壮年工人比老年工人减少得更多，而且那些已经工作时间长、收入高的人减少得也更多。工人工作时间的减少可以解释为什么劳动力市场比当前失业率和劳动力参与率水平所预期的更加紧张。

No. 7NBER w30832

Unstable Prosperity: How Globalization Made the World Economy More Volatile

不稳定的繁荣：全球化如何使世界经济更加波动

Enrique G. Mendoza, Vincenzo Quadrini

Programs：IFM；JEL：F34, F36, F62, F65

Abstract

The sharp, secular decline in the world real interest rate of the past thirty years suggests that the surge in global demand for financial assets outpaced the growth in their supply. We argue that this phenomenon was driven by: (i) faster growth in emerging markets, (ii) changes in the financial structure of both emerging and advanced economies, and (iii) changes in demand and supply of public debt issued by advanced economies. We then show that the low-interest-rate environment made the world economy more vulnerable to financial crises. These findings are the quantitative predictions of a two-region model in which privately-issued financial assets (i.e., inside money) provide productive services but can be defaulted on.

中文摘要

过去三十年世界实际利率急剧的长期下降表明，全球对金融资产的需求激增超过了其供给的增长。我们认为，这一现象是由以下因素驱动的：（i）新兴市场增长加快，（ii）新兴经济体和发达经济体金融结构的变化，以及（iii）发达经济体发行的公共债务需求和供给的变化。然后我们表明，低利率环境使世界经济更容易受到金融危机的影响。这些发现是一个两区域模型的定量预测，在该模型中，私人发行的金融资产（即内部货币）提供生产性服务，但可能发生违约。

No. 8NBER w30831

The Unintended Consequences of Test-Based Remediation

基于测试的补救措施的非预期后果

David N. Figlio, Umut Özek

Programs：CH, ED, LS；JEL：I21, I24, I28

Abstract

School systems around the world use achievement tests to assign students to schools, classes, and instructional resources, including remediation. Using a regression discontinuity design, we study a Florida policy that places middle school students who score below a proficiency cutoff into remedial classes. Students scoring below the cutoff receive more educational resources, but they are also placed in classes that are more segregated by race, socio-economic status, and prior achievement. Increased tracking occurs not only in the remedial subject, but also in other core subjects. These tracking effects are significantly larger and more likely to persist beyond the year of remediation for Black students.

中文摘要

世界各地的学校系统使用学业成就测试将学生分配到学校、班级和教学资源（包括补救措施）中。利用断点回归设计，我们研究了佛罗里达州的一项政策，该政策将成绩低于熟练度截止分数的中学生安排到补习班。分数低于截止分数的学生获得了更多的教育资源，但他们也被安排到按种族、社会经济地位和先前学业成就划分的更加隔离的班级中。增加的追踪不仅发生在补习科目上，也发生在其他核心科目上。对于黑人学生来说，这些追踪效应显著更大，并且更有可能持续到补习年份之后。

International Spillover Effects of Air Pollution: Evidence from Mortality and Health Data

空气污染的国际溢出效应：来自死亡率和健康数据的证据

Seonmin Will Heo, Koichiro Ito, Rao Kotamarthi

Programs：DEV, EEE, EH；JEL：Q53

Abstract

We study the international spillover effects of air pollution by developing a framework that integrates recent advances in atmospheric science into econometric estimation with microdata on mortality and health. Combining transboundary particle trajectory data with the universe of individual-level mortality and emergency room visit data in South Korea, we find that transboundary air pollution from China significantly increases mortality and morbidity in South Korea. Using these estimates, we show that a recent Chinese environmental policy “war on pollution” generated a substantial international spillover benefit. Finally, we examine China’s strategic pollution reductions and provide their implications for the potential Coasian bargaining.

中文摘要

我们通过开发一个框架来研究空气污染的国际溢出效应，该框架将大气科学的最新进展整合到使用死亡率和健康微观数据的计量经济学估计中。将跨境颗粒物轨迹数据与韩国个人层面的死亡率和急诊就诊数据相结合，我们发现来自中国的跨境空气污染显著增加了韩国的死亡率和发病率。利用这些估计值，我们表明中国最近的“污染防治攻坚战”环境政策产生了巨大的国际溢出效益。最后，我们考察了中国战略性的污染减排，并提供了其对潜在科斯式谈判的启示。

No. 10NBER w30829

A Simple Approach to Valuing Intangibles and Rents

评估无形资产和租金的一种简单方法

Wan-Chien Chiu, Ravi Jagannathan, Yo-Lan Lin, Kevin Tseng

Programs：AP；JEL：G0, G00, G12

Abstract

We decompose the gap between a firm’s market and book values into capitalized-intangible-assets and a residual, consisting of value of capacity-adjustment-costs, economic-rents and potential mispricing. Our estimated parameter-values for capitalizing expenditures creating intangible-assets are consistent with values reported in the literature, even though we use a different approach. Firms with higher residuals have higher profitability and markups, lower labor share, face fewer product market threats, exhibit less sensitivity of investment to traditional as well as intangibles-adjusted Tobin’s Q, and benefited more from globalization during this century. This is consistent with there being a significant ex post rents-component in the residual.

中文摘要

我们将公司市场价值与账面价值之间的差距分解为资本化的无形资产和剩余部分，剩余部分包括产能调整成本的价值、经济租金和潜在的错误定价。我们用于资本化创造无形资产的支出的估计参数值与文献中报告的值一致，尽管我们使用了不同的方法。剩余部分较高的公司具有更高的盈利能力和加成率、更低的劳动份额、面临更少的产品市场威胁、对传统以及无形资产调整后的托宾Q的投资敏感性较低，并且在本世纪从全球化中受益更多。这与剩余部分中存在显著的ex post租金成分是一致的。

No. 11NBER w30828

Price Destabilizing Speculation: The Role of Strategic Limit Orders

价格不稳定的投机：战略限价订单的作用

Suman Banerjee, Ravi Jagannathan, Kai Wang

Programs：AP；JEL：G0, G1, G10, G12, G18, G19

Abstract

Using a two-period model of a commodity market with a large number of atomistic consumers and two strategic sellers, we show that a speculator with access to storage can lower the market price while buying and raise the price while selling by clever use of limit, stop-loss, and market orders. The speculator profits from it. This creates price volatility even though there is no demand or supply uncertainty, and all market participants act rationally. Prices are more volatile when the speculator has access to free disposal. Such speculative activity makes the strategic sellers worse off and consumers better off. Our results are robust to introducing demand uncertainty, having more than one large speculator, and more than two strategic sellers. When there are multiple strategic sellers consumers can be worse off.

中文摘要

使用一个包含大量原子消费者和两个战略卖家的商品市场两期模型，我们表明，一个能够使用仓储的投机者可以通过巧妙使用限价订单、止损订单和市价订单，在买入时压低市场价格，在卖出时抬高价格。投机者从中获利。即使没有需求或供给的不确定性，并且所有市场参与者都理性行事，这也会造成价格波动。当投机者可以免费处置时，价格波动更大。这种投机活动使战略卖家境况变差，消费者境况变好。我们的结果在引入需求不确定性、存在多个大型投机者和超过两个战略卖家时是稳健的。当存在多个战略卖家时，消费者可能境况变差。

No. 12NBER w30827中国相关

Reducing Bullying: Evidence from a Parental Involvement Program on Empathy Education

减少欺凌：一项关于共情教育的家长参与项目的证据

Flavio Cunha, Qinyou Hu, Yiming Xia, Naibao Zhao

Programs：CH, ED；JEL：I10, I20, J24, O10

Abstract

According to UNESCO, one-third of the world’s youths are victims of bullying, which deteriorates academic performance and mental health, and increases suicide ideation and the risk of committing suicide. This paper analyzes a four-month parent-directed intervention designed to foster empathy in middle schoolers in China. Our implementation and evaluation study enrolled 2,246 seventh and eighth graders and their parents, whom we assigned, at the classroom level, to the control or intervention condition randomly. We measured, before and after the intervention, parental investments, children’s empathy, and self-reported bullying perpetration and victimization incidents. Our analyses show that the intervention increased investments and empathy and reduced bullying incidents. In addition, we measured costs and found that it costs $12.50 for our intervention to reduce one bullying incident. Our study offers a scalable and low-cost strategy that can inform public policy on bullying prevention in other similar settings.
