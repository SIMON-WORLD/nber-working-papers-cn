No. 1NBER w31400

Gender-Neutral Language and Gender Disparities

性别中立语言与性别差异

Alma Cohen, Tzur Karelitz, Tamar Kricheli-Katz, Sephi Pumpian, Tali Regev

Programs：ED, LE, LS, PE；JEL：D83, I20, I24, J16, Z13

Abstract

This study investigates empirically whether and how the use of gender-neutral language affects the performance of women and men in real high-stakes exams. We make use of a natural experiment in which the institute administering Israel’s standardized college admission tests amended the language used in its exams, making test language more gender neutral. We find that the change to a more gender-neutral language was associated with a significant improvement in the performance of women on quantitative questions, which meaningfully reduced the gender gap between male and female performance on these questions. However, the change did not affect female performance on verbal questions nor male performance on either quantitative or verbal questions. Our findings are consistent with the hypothesis that gendered language may introduce a "stereotype threat" that adversely affects women’s performance in tasks in which they are stereotypically perceived to underperform. Our findings have significant implications for the ongoing academic and policy discussions regarding the use and effects of gender-neutral language.

中文摘要

本研究通过实证调查，探讨在真实高风险考试中使用性别中立语言是否以及如何影响女性和男性的表现。我们利用一个自然实验，其中管理以色列标准化大学入学考试的机构修改了考试语言，使考试语言更加性别中立。我们发现，转向更性别中立的语言与女性在定量问题上的表现显著改善相关，这有意义地缩小了男女在这些问题上的性别差距。然而，这一变化并未影响女性在语言问题上的表现，也未影响男性在定量或语言问题上的表现。我们的发现与以下假设一致：性别化语言可能引入一种“刻板印象威胁”，对女性在刻板印象中表现不佳的任务中产生不利影响。我们的发现对当前关于性别中立语言使用及其影响的学术和政策讨论具有重要意义。

No. 2NBER w31399

Exporting Ideology: The Right and Left of Foreign Influence

输出意识形态：外国影响的左右之分

Pol Antràs, Gerard Padró I Miquel

Programs：IFM, ITI, PE, POL；JEL：D7, F2, F5, H77, P33

Abstract

We present an economic rationale for countries resorting to foreign influence to export their ideology to other nations. Our model incorporates two fundamental elements: redistribution of the tax burden between capital owners and workers, and international capital mobility. The model highlights the role of ideology in shaping both the taxes implemented by governments and the cross-border externalities of these policy choices. Pro-capital governments set lower capital taxes than pro-labor governments. Importantly, pro-capital governments benefit from other countries setting low capital taxes, while pro-labor governments' efforts to shift the tax burden onto capital owners are facilitated by higher capital taxes abroad. These cross-border externalities create strong incentives for engaging in foreign influence activities. We solve for a political equilibrium in which incumbent governments may exert costly actions that probabilistically affect the electoral outcome in other countries. In equilibrium, pro-capital parties exert influence aimed at promoting pro-capital parties and policies worldwide, while pro-labor governments carry out foreign influence activities aimed at boosting pro-labor parties and policies in other countries.

中文摘要

我们提出了一个经济理由，解释国家为何诉诸外国影响以向其他国家输出其意识形态。我们的模型包含两个基本要素：资本所有者与工人之间的税收负担再分配，以及国际资本流动性。该模型强调了意识形态在塑造政府实施的税收以及这些政策选择的跨境外部性方面的作用。亲资本政府设定的资本税率低于亲劳动政府。重要的是，亲资本政府受益于其他国家设定低资本税率，而亲劳动政府将税收负担转嫁给资本所有者的努力则因国外更高的资本税率而得到促进。这些跨境外部性产生了参与外国影响活动的强烈激励。我们求解了一个政治均衡，其中现任政府可能采取代价高昂的行动，以概率性方式影响其他国家的选举结果。在均衡中，亲资本政党施加影响，旨在全球范围内推广亲资本政党和政策，而亲劳动政府则开展外国影响活动，旨在提升其他国家的亲劳动政党和政策。

No. 3NBER w31398

How Do Firms Respond to State Retirement Plan Mandates?

企业如何应对州退休计划强制要求？

Adam Bloomfield, Kyung Min Lee, Jay Philbrick, Sita Slavov

Programs：AG, LS, PE；JEL：D14, H75, J26

Abstract

We investigate how state “Auto-IRA” mandates affect firm offerings of employer-sponsored retirement plans (ESRPs). These policies require firms without ESRPs to facilitate automatic employee contributions to state-created individual retirement accounts (IRAs). We find that these policies increase an individual’s probability of working for a firm with an ESRP by 6-9 percent and of being included in the ESRP by 8-13 percent. At the firm level, these policies increase the probability of offering an ESRP by 7, the probability of establishing a new ESRP by 41-44 percent, and the number of ESRP participants by 6 percent.

中文摘要

我们研究州“自动IRA”强制要求如何影响企业提供雇主赞助退休计划（ESRP）。这些政策要求没有ESRP的企业促进员工自动向州创建的个人退休账户（IRA）缴款。我们发现，这些政策将个人在提供ESRP的企业工作的概率提高了6-9%，将被纳入ESRP的概率提高了8-13%。在企业层面，这些政策将提供ESRP的概率提高了7个百分点，将建立新ESRP的概率提高了41-44%，并将ESRP参与者数量提高了6%。

No. 4NBER w31397

How Costs Limit Contraceptive Use among Low-Income Women in the U.S.: A Randomized Control Trial

成本如何限制美国低收入女性使用避孕措施：一项随机对照试验

Martha J. Bailey, Vanessa Wanner Lang, Alexa Prettyman, Iris Vrioni, Lea J. Bart, Daniel Eisenberg, Paula Fomby, Jennifer Barber, Vanessa Dalton

Programs：CH, EH, LS, PE；JEL：I18, J13, J18

Abstract

The Affordable Care Act eliminated cost-sharing for contraception for Americans with health insurance, but substantial cost sharing remains for uninsured individuals who seek care through Title X—a national family planning program that provides patient-centered, subsidized contraception and reproductive health services in the U.S. This paper uses a randomized control trial (RCT) to examine how cost-sharing at Title X providers affects the choice of contraceptive method. The study randomizes vouchers that cover any contraceptive method up to the cost of 50% or 100% of a name-brand intra-uterine device (IUD). The results show that Title X clients are highly constrained by the out-of-pocket costs of contraception. The offer of free contraception is associated with a 40% increase in the use of any birth control method (ITT effect), a 94% increase in the value of birth control purchased, a 328-day (226%) increase in the period covered by contraceptives purchased, and a 324% increase in the likelihood of choosing a long-acting, reversible method (an IUD or implant). The results imply that eliminating the costs of contraception for Title X clients nationwide would reduce undesired pregnancies by 5.3%, birth rates by 3.9%, and abortions by 8.3%, and save $1.43 billion in the first year of the program.

中文摘要

《平价医疗法案》取消了有健康保险的美国人的避孕费用分摊，但对于通过Title X（一项美国全国性的家庭计划项目，提供以患者为中心的补贴避孕和生殖健康服务）寻求护理的无保险个人，仍存在大量费用分摊。本文使用随机对照试验（RCT）来研究Title X提供者的费用分摊如何影响避孕方法的选择。该研究随机发放代金券，覆盖任何避孕方法，最高可达名牌宫内节育器（IUD）成本的50%或100%。结果显示，Title X客户受到避孕自付费用的高度限制。提供免费避孕与任何节育方法使用率增加40%（ITT效应）、购买的节育价值增加94%、购买的避孕措施覆盖期限增加328天（226%）、以及选择长效可逆方法（IUD或植入物）的可能性增加324%相关。结果表明，在全国范围内取消Title X客户的避孕费用，将使意外怀孕减少5.3%，出生率降低3.9%，堕胎减少8.3%，并在项目第一年节省14.3亿美元。

Deposit Regulation and Monetary Transmission in China

中国存款监管与货币政策传导

Kaiji Chen, Yiqing Xiao, Tao Zha

Programs：AP, EFG, ME；JEL：E02, E5, G11, G12, G28

Abstract

In well-developed financial markets, retail deposits and wholesale funding comove negatively in response to monetary policy changes. This negative comovement weakens the transmission of monetary policy. By contrast, our study finds that in emerging markets such as China, where deposit rate ceilings are regulated, (i) retail deposits and wholesale funding comove positively as the policy rate changes, and (ii) wholesale funding strengthens the transmission of monetary policy to bank lending. We develop a theoretical model that highlights the significant influence of deposit regulation on monetary policy transmission in the context of the world’s largest emerging market economy.

中文摘要

在发达的金融市场中，零售存款和批发融资在货币政策变化时呈负相关变动。这种负相关削弱了货币政策的传导。相比之下，我们的研究发现，在中国等新兴市场，存款利率上限受到监管，因此（i）当政策利率变化时，零售存款和批发融资呈正相关变动，并且（ii）批发融资强化了货币政策向银行贷款的传导。我们构建了一个理论模型，突显了在全球最大新兴市场经济体的背景下，存款监管对货币政策传导的显著影响。

China's Macroeconomic Development: The Role of Gradualist Reforms

中国宏观经济发展：渐进式改革的作用

Kaiji Chen, Tao Zha

Programs：DEV, EFG, ME；JEL：E02, E2, E65, F43

Abstract

This paper provides analytic guides to recent literature on China's macroeconomic development, emphasizing the critical role of the gradualist reform approach. Our analysis suggests that from 1978 to 1997, the gradualist approach contributed to China's aggregate total factor productivity and economic growth primarily through policies that facilitated the reallocation of surplus labor from agriculture to non-agricultural sectors. Since 1998, the government's focus shifted, with various reforms encouraging large enterprises, whether state-owned or privately-owned, to enter capital-intensive sectors, making capital deepening the main driver of economic growth. While this strategy sustained China's GDP growth, it also increased trade tensions with global partners, created barriers to transitioning to a consumption-led economy, and threatened China's long-term financial stability, casting long shadows over the Chinese economy.

中文摘要

本文为近期关于中国宏观经济发展的文献提供了分析指南，强调了渐进式改革方法的关键作用。我们的分析表明，从1978年到1997年，渐进式方法主要通过促进剩余劳动力从农业向非农业部门重新配置的政策，推动了中国全要素生产率和经济增长。自1998年以来，政府重心发生转变，各项改革鼓励大型企业（无论是国有还是私营）进入资本密集型部门，使资本深化成为经济增长的主要驱动力。这一战略虽然维持了中国GDP增长，但也加剧了与全球伙伴的贸易紧张关系，为向消费主导型经济转型设置了障碍，并威胁到中国的长期金融稳定，给中国经济投下了长长的阴影。

No. 7NBER w31394

In-Kind Welfare Benefits and Reincarceration Risk: Evidence from Medicaid

实物福利与再监禁风险：来自医疗补助的证据

Marguerite Burns, Laura Dague

Programs：EH；JEL：H75, I13, J22, K42

Abstract

Most of the 600,000 adults returning to the community from state and federal prisons annually in the U.S. carry substantial debt, have low income and low education, and limited formal employment prior to entering prison. Upon reentry, they face financial hardship, high rates of morbidity and mortality, and high incidence of re-offense. Medicaid coverage, as a means-tested transfer program providing subsidized health insurance, may influence reincarceration through both financial and health channels. In this paper, we provide a comprehensive look at the effects of public health insurance coverage on post-release behavior of formerly incarcerated adults. We study a natural experiment in which two separate state policy changes resulted in a 60 percentage point increase in Medicaid enrollment at release. Using a series of linked individual level administrative datasets, we estimate the effects of this change in Medicaid enrollment, finding declines in reincarceration, increased employment, and higher health care use. Leveraging data on financial concerns and need for mental health and substance use treatment to examine mechanisms, we find support for a financial channel and mixed support for health channels. Policies that enhance access to healthcare and provide financial security for this population may have significant benefits for individuals and society.

中文摘要

美国每年有60万成年人从州立和联邦监狱重返社区，其中大多数人背负巨额债务、收入低、教育水平低，且在入狱前正式就业有限。重返社会后，他们面临经济困难、高发病率和死亡率以及高再犯罪率。医疗补助作为一种基于经济状况调查的转移支付项目，提供补贴性健康保险，可能通过经济和健康两种渠道影响再监禁。在本文中，我们全面考察了公共健康保险覆盖对曾有监禁经历的成年人释放后行为的影响。我们研究了一项自然实验，其中两项独立的州政策变化导致释放时医疗补助注册率提高了60个百分点。利用一系列关联的个人层面行政数据集，我们估计了医疗补助注册变化的影响，发现再监禁率下降、就业增加以及医疗保健使用增加。通过利用关于经济担忧以及心理健康和物质使用治疗需求的数据来检验机制，我们发现了支持经济渠道的证据，而对健康渠道的支持则不一。增强这一人群获得医疗保健的机会并为其提供经济保障的政策可能对个人和社会产生显著益处。

No. 8NBER w31393

Design-Based Identification with Formula Instruments: A Review

基于设计的公式工具识别：综述

Kirill Borusyak, Peter Hull, Xavier Jaravel

Programs：ITI, LS；JEL：C21, C26

Abstract

Many studies in economics use instruments or treatments which combine a set of exogenous shocks with other predetermined variables by a known formula. Examples include shift-share instruments and measures of social or spatial spillovers. We review recent econometric tools for this setting, which leverage the assignment process of the exogenous shocks and the structure of the formula for identification. We compare this design-based approach with conventional estimation strategies based on conditional unconfoundedness, and contrast it with alternative strategies that leverage a model for unobservables.

中文摘要

经济学中的许多研究使用工具或处理变量，这些变量通过已知公式将一组外生冲击与其他预定变量结合起来。例子包括份额转移工具以及社会或空间溢出效应的度量。我们回顾了针对这一情境的最新计量经济学工具，这些工具利用了外生冲击的分配过程和公式结构进行识别。我们将这种基于设计的方法与基于条件无混杂性的传统估计策略进行比较，并将其与利用不可观测因素模型的其他策略进行对比。

No. 9NBER w31392

The Impact of Fear on Police Behavior and Public Safety

恐惧对警察行为与公共安全的影响

Sungwoo Cho, Felipe Gonçalves, Emily Weisburst

Programs：LE；JEL：J28, J45, K42

Abstract

We examine how changes in the salience of workplace risk affect police behavior and public safety. Specifically, we investigate cases of police officer deaths while on duty. Officers respond to a peer death by decreasing arrest activity for one to two months, consistent with heightened fear. Reductions are largest for low-level arrests and are more pronounced in smaller cities. Crime does not increase on average during this period, nor do we observe crime spikes in cities with larger or longer arrest declines. While shocks in fatality risk generate substantial enforcement responses, officer fear is unlikely to harm public safety.

中文摘要

我们研究了工作场所风险显著性的变化如何影响警察行为和公共安全。具体而言，我们调查了警察执勤死亡案例。警察对同事死亡的反应是，逮捕活动减少一到两个月，这与恐惧加剧相一致。逮捕减少在低级别逮捕中最为显著，并且在较小城市更为明显。在此期间，犯罪率平均并未上升，我们也没有在逮捕下降幅度更大或时间更长的城市观察到犯罪激增。尽管致命风险冲击引发了显著的执法反应，但警察的恐惧不太可能损害公共安全。

No. 10NBER w31391

Treatment for Mental Health and Substance Use: Spillovers to Police Safety

心理健康与物质使用治疗：对警察安全的溢出效应

Monica Deza, Thanh Lu, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Alberto Ortega

Programs：EH, LE；JEL：I10, I12

Abstract

We study the effect of community access to mental health and substance use treatment on police officer safety, proxied with on-duty assaults on officers. Police officers often serve as first-responders to people experiencing mental health and substance use crises, which can place police officers at risk. Combining agency-level data on police officer on-duty assaults and county-level data on treatment centers that offer mental health and substance use care, we estimate panel fixed-effects regressions. An additional four centers per county (the average annual increase observed in our data) leads to a 1.3% reduction per police agency in on-duty police officers assaults.

中文摘要

我们研究了社区获得心理健康和物质使用治疗对警察安全的影响，以执勤期间警察遇袭作为代理指标。警察通常是应对经历心理健康和物质使用危机人群的第一响应者，这可能使警察面临风险。结合关于警察执勤遇袭的机构层面数据和关于提供心理健康及物质使用护理的治疗中心的县级数据，我们估计了面板固定效应回归。每个县每增加四个治疗中心（我们数据中观察到的年均增长），每个警察机构的执勤警察遇袭事件减少1.3%。

No. 11NBER w31390

International Trade, Noise Pollution, and Killer Whales

国际贸易、噪音污染与虎鲸

M. Scott Taylor, Fruzsina Mayer

Programs：EEE, ITI；JEL：F1, Q01, Q20, Q53

Abstract

Orcinus Orca is the world’s largest predator, and simultaneously a significant tourist asset and cultural icon for much of the Pacific Northwest. In the past two decades, the Southern Resident Killer whale (SRKW) population has declined by more than 25 percent, putting them at risk of extinction. The cause of this decline is hotly debated. This paper employs novel data, an innovative noise pollution model, and quasi-experimental methods borrowed from environmental economics to solve this puzzle. We find consistent evidence that vessel noise pollution from international shipping has lowered fertility and raised the mortality of the SRKW significantly. Had noise pollution remained at its pre-1998 levels, the SRKW population would be 30% larger. Noise pollution is a growing threat to marine mammals worldwide.

中文摘要

虎鲸是世界上最大的捕食者，同时也是太平洋西北地区重要的旅游资产和文化标志。在过去二十年中，南方定居虎鲸种群数量下降了超过25%，面临灭绝风险。这种下降的原因存在激烈争论。本文利用新颖数据、创新的噪音污染模型以及借鉴自环境经济学的准实验方法来解决这一难题。我们发现一致的证据表明，国际航运产生的船舶噪音污染显著降低了南方定居虎鲸的生育率并提高了死亡率。如果噪音污染保持在1998年之前的水平，南方定居虎鲸种群规模将比现在大30%。噪音污染是全球海洋哺乳动物日益面临的威胁。

No. 12NBER w31389

Do Municipal Bond Investors Pay a Convenience Premium to Avoid Taxes?

市政债券投资者是否为避税支付便利溢价？

Matthias Fleckenstein, Francis A. Longstaff

Programs：AP；JEL：G12

Abstract

We study the valuation of state-issued tax-exempt municipal bonds and find that there are significant convenience premia in their prices. These premia parallel those identified in Treasury markets. We find evidence that these premia are tax related. Specifically, the premia are related to measures of tax and fiscal uncertainty, forecast flows into state municipal bond funds, and are directly linked to outmigration from high-tax to low-tax states and to other measures of tax aversion such as IRA and retirement plan contributions. These results suggest that investors are willing to pay a substantial premium to avoid taxes.

中文摘要

我们研究了州发行免税市政债券的估值，发现其价格中存在显著的便利溢价。这些溢价与国债市场中识别的溢价相似。我们发现有证据表明这些溢价与税收相关。具体而言，这些溢价与税收和财政不确定性指标、预测流入州市政债券基金的资金流有关，并且直接与从高税州向低税州的迁出以及其他税收规避措施（如IRA和退休计划缴款）相关联。这些结果表明，投资者愿意支付可观的溢价来避税。

Abstract
