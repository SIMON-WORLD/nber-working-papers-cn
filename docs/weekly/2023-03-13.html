No. 1NBER w31029

Macroeconomic Effects of ‘Free’ Secondary Schooling in the Developing World

发展中国家“免费”中学教育的宏观经济效应

Junichi Fujimoto, David Lagakos, Mitchell VanVuren

Programs：DEV, ED, EFG；JEL：E24, O11

Abstract

This paper studies the macroeconomic effects of publicly funded (‘free’) secondary schooling in the developing world. Our analysis is based on an over-lapping generations model of human capital accumulation that we estimate to match experimental evidence on the effects of scholarships for poor but talented students in Ghana. The model predicts that nationwide free secondary schooling increases average education levels but reduces GDP per capita in the long run. The human capital gains from free schooling in the model are offset by lost income during schooling years, negative selection of new students, and reductions in fertility by high-ability households.

中文摘要

本文研究了发展中国家公共资助（“免费”）中学教育的宏观经济效应。我们的分析基于一个我们估计的人力资本积累重叠世代模型，该模型与加纳针对贫困但有才华学生的奖学金实验证据相匹配。该模型预测，全国性的免费中学教育会提高平均教育水平，但长期内会降低人均GDP。模型中免费教育带来的人力资本收益被受教育年份的收入损失、新生的负向选择以及高能力家庭生育率的下降所抵消。

No. 2NBER w31028

The Race Between Education, Technology, and the Minimum Wage

教育、技术与最低工资之间的竞赛

Jonathan Vogel

Programs：EFG, ITI, LS；JEL：E24, J0, J2, J3, J42

Abstract

What is the impact of the minimum wage on the college wage premium? I develop a theory that implies that the effect should be small on impact—raising only the wages of workers bound by the minimum wage—and grow over time. Guided by my theory, I present evidence that these dynamic effects are present and powerful. Estimated at the national and state levels, I show that minimum wages—together with supply and demand—play a central role in shaping the evolution of the U.S. college premium and that the elasticity of the college premium to the minimum wage is small on impact and grows dramatically over time. To verify my theory’s mechanisms, I additionally document the dynamic impact of the minimum wage over the full wage distribution: on impact, wages rise only for the lowest centiles (consistent with the literature) but over time this effect spills over up the wage distribution (consistent with my theory and my empirical results on the college premium). On the basis of these results, I conclude that the minimum wage plays a central role in shaping the U.S. college premium and its variation across states, in spite of relatively small effects on impact.

中文摘要

最低工资对大学教育溢价的影响是什么？我发展了一个理论，该理论暗示这种影响在短期内应该很小——仅提高受最低工资约束的工人的工资——并随时间推移而增长。在我的理论指导下，我提供的证据表明这些动态效应存在且强大。在国家和州层面的估计中，我表明最低工资——连同供给和需求——在塑造美国大学教育溢价的演变中发挥着核心作用，并且大学教育溢价对最低工资的弹性在短期内很小，但随时间推移显著增长。为了验证我的理论机制，我还记录了最低工资在整个工资分布上的动态影响：短期内，工资仅对最低百分位数上升（与文献一致），但随着时间的推移，这种效应会向上蔓延至整个工资分布（与我的理论以及关于大学教育溢价的实证结果一致）。基于这些结果，我得出结论，尽管最低工资在短期内影响相对较小，但它在塑造美国大学教育溢价及其在各州之间的差异方面发挥着核心作用。

No. 3NBER w31027

Identification in a Binary Choice Panel Data Model with a Predetermined Covariate

具有预定协变量的二元选择面板数据模型中的识别

Stéphane Bonhomme, Kevin Dano, Bryan S. Graham

Programs：IO, LS, TWP；JEL：C23

Abstract

We study identification in a binary choice panel data model with a single predetermined binary covariate (i.e., a covariate sequentially exogenous conditional on lagged outcomes and covariates). The choice model is indexed by a scalar parameter θ, whereas the distribution of unit-specific heterogeneity, as well as the feedback process that maps lagged outcomes into future covariate realizations, are left unrestricted. We provide a simple condition under which θ is never point-identified, no matter the number of time periods available. This condition is satisfied in most models, including the logit one. We also characterize the identified set of θ and show how to compute it using linear programming techniques. While θ is not generally point-identified, its identified set is informative in the examples we analyze numerically, suggesting that meaningful learning about θ may be possible even in short panels with feedback. As a complement, we report calculations of identified sets for an average partial effect, and find informative sets in this case as well.

中文摘要

我们研究了一个具有单个预定二元协变量（即，以滞后结果和协变量为条件的序列外生协变量）的二元选择面板数据模型中的识别问题。选择模型由一个标量参数θ索引，而单位特定异质性的分布以及将滞后结果映射到未来协变量实现的反馈过程则不加限制。我们提供了一个简单条件，在该条件下，无论可用的时间周期数有多少，θ都永远无法被点识别。该条件在大多数模型（包括Logit模型）中都得到满足。我们还刻画了θ的识别集，并展示了如何使用线性规划技术计算它。虽然θ通常无法被点识别，但在我们数值分析的例子中，其识别集提供了信息，这表明即使在存在反馈的短面板中，也可能对θ进行有意义的推断。作为补充，我们报告了平均部分效应的识别集计算结果，并发现这种情况下识别集也具有信息量。

No. 4NBER w31026

The Macroeconomics of Trade Credit

商业信用的宏观经济学

Luigi Bocola, Gideon Bornstein

Programs：CF, EFG, IFM, ITI, ME；JEL：E44, G01, G30

Abstract

In most countries, suppliers of intermediate goods and services are also major providers of short-term financing to firms. This paper studies the macroeconomic implications of these financial links. In our model, trade credit arises from long-term relationships between firms linked in production and is sustained by reputation: customers repay in order to preserve their relationships with suppliers. These financial links generate a credit multiplier. Suppliers can enforce repayment from their customers and discount receivables with banks to obtain liquidity. This process can either dampen or amplify the output effects of financial shocks, depending on suppliers’ borrowing capacity. Using Italian data, we find that the credit multiplier is sizable and that trade credit substantially amplified the output costs of the Great Recession.

中文摘要

在大多数国家，中间产品和服务的供应商也是企业短期融资的主要提供者。本文研究了这些金融联系的宏观经济含义。在我们的模型中，商业信用是生产过程中相互关联的企业之间长期合同的结果，并通过声誉力量在均衡中得以维持，因为客户在违约时会失去与供应商的关系。这些金融联系产生了一个信用乘数：供应商可以强制执行这些借据的偿还，并且可以将这些票据向银行贴现以获得流动性。这个过程可以放大或抑制金融冲击对产出的影响，具体取决于供应商的借贷能力。利用意大利的数据，我们发现信用乘数相当大，并表明商业信用使大衰退的产出成本增加了45%。

No. 5NBER w31025

A Major Shock Makes Prices More Flexible and May Result in a Burst of Inflation or Deflation

重大冲击使价格更具弹性，并可能导致通胀或通缩爆发

Robert E. Hall

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E31, E42, E44

Abstract

The US and other advanced countries suffered bursts of severe inflation in 2021 and the first half of 2022, followed by declines of inflation later in 2022, in some countries. In times of high volatility of price determinants—cost and productivity—inflation can jump upward and fall downward at high speed, contrary to the uniformly sticky behavior associated with traditional Phillips curves. This paper establishes that sectors with standard New Keynesian price stickiness are vulnerable to rapid transitions from stickiness to flexibility, as sellers elect to reset their prices and abandon anchoring. The paper shows that the cross-industry volatility of price determinants grew substantially in the inflation episode accompanying the pandemic. Volatility remained elevated even in late 2022. The logic of the New Keynesian model of the Phillips curve links inflation to volatility, because a larger fraction of sellers are pushed out of their regions of inaction when volatility is elevated. The New Keynesian Phillips curve becomes much steeper in volatile times.

中文摘要

美国和其他发达国家在2021年和2022年上半年经历了严重的通胀爆发，随后在2022年下半年，一些国家的通胀有所下降。在价格决定因素（成本和生产率）高度波动的时期，通胀可能快速上升和下降，这与传统菲利普斯曲线所关联的普遍粘性行为相反。本文证实，具有标准新凯恩斯价格粘性的行业容易从粘性快速过渡到弹性，因为销售者选择重新设定价格并放弃锚定。本文表明，伴随疫情的通胀事件中，价格决定因素的跨行业波动性大幅增长。即使在2022年末，波动性仍然居高不下。新凯恩斯菲利普斯曲线的逻辑将通胀与波动性联系起来，因为当波动性升高时，更大比例的销售者被推出其不行动区域。新凯恩斯菲利普斯曲线在波动时期变得陡峭得多。

No. 6NBER w31024

International Sanctions and Dollar Dominance

国际制裁与美元主导地位

Javier Bianchi, César Sosa-Padilla

Programs：IFM；JEL：E42, F31, F32, F34, F41, P48

Abstract

We propose a simple monetary model to investigate the implications of international financial sanctions for the preeminence of the US dollar in the international financial system. We show how the anticipation of financial sanctions can reduce the US dollar convenience yield and the holdings of US dollar assets. We also evaluate the implications for welfare and show that they are generally detrimental for all countries.

中文摘要

我们提出了一个简单的货币模型，以研究国际金融制裁对美元在国际金融体系中主导地位的影响。我们展示了金融制裁的预期如何降低美元便利收益以及美元资产的持有量。我们还评估了这些影响对福利的含义，并表明它们通常对所有国家都是不利的。

No. 7NBER w31023

Do Older Adults Accurately Forecast Their Social Security Benefits?

老年人能否准确预测其社会保障福利？

Grant M. Seiter, Sita Slavov

Programs：AG；JEL：E21, H55, J14, J26

Abstract

How accurate are older people’s expectations about their future Social Security benefits? Using panel data from the Health and Retirement Study, we compare respondents’ observed Social Security claiming ages and benefits with subjective expectations provided during their 50s and early 60s. We find that, while older adults generally have accurate expectations about their claiming age, they underestimate their annual Social Security income by approximately $1,896 (11.5 percent) on average. However, both accuracy and precision increase with age, and the forecast error for people in their early 60s is not statistically different from zero. Exploiting plausibly exogenous variation in the mailing of Social Security statements, which contain personalized information about future benefits, we show that information provision reduces the forecast error in annual income by $344 (2.1 percent of the average benefit).

中文摘要

老年人对其未来社会保障福利的预期有多准确？利用健康与退休研究的面板数据，我们将受访者观察到的社会保障领取年龄和福利与他们50多岁和60岁出头时提供的主观预期进行了比较。我们发现，虽然老年人通常对其领取年龄有准确的预期，但他们平均低估了其年度社会保障收入约1,896美元（11.5%）。然而，准确性和精确度都随年龄增长而提高，并且60岁出头人群的预测误差在统计上与零无显著差异。利用邮寄包含个性化未来福利信息的社会保障对账单这一看似外生的变化，我们表明信息提供使年度收入的预测误差减少了344美元（占平均福利的2.1%）。

No. 8NBER w31022

Biodiversity Finance

生物多样性融资

Caroline Flammer, Thomas Giroux, Geoffrey Heal

Programs：AP, CF, EEE；JEL：G11, G23, G3, Q14, Q2, Q5, Q57

Abstract

We study biodiversity finance—the use of private capital to finance biodiversity conservation and restoration—which is a new practice in sustainable finance. First, we provide a conceptual framework that lays out how biodiversity can be financed by pure private capital and blended financing structures. In the latter, private capital is blended with public or philanthropic capital, whose aim is to de-risk private capital investments. The main element underlying both types of financing is the “monetization” of biodiversity, that is, using investments in biodiversity to generate a financial return for private investors. Second, we provide empirical evidence using deal-level data from a leading biodiversity finance institution. Our findings are consistent with a three-dimensional efficient frontier (return, risk, and biodiversity impact)—deals with a favorable risk-return profile tend to be financed by pure private capital, whereas for other deals the biodiversity impact needs to be sufficiently large for blended finance to be used. Overall, our results suggest that blended finance is an important tool for improving the risk-return profile of these projects, thereby increasing their appeal to private investors and crowding in private capital. Finally, our results suggest that private capital is unlikely to substitute for effective public policies in addressing the biodiversity crisis.

中文摘要

我们研究生物多样性融资——即利用私人资本为生物多样性保护和恢复提供资金——这是可持续金融中的一种新实践。首先，我们提供了一个概念框架，阐述了生物多样性如何通过纯私人资本和混合融资结构进行融资。在后者中，私人资本与公共或慈善资本混合，其目的是降低私人资本投资的风险。这两种融资类型背后的主要元素是生物多样性的“货币化”，即利用对生物多样性的投资为私人投资者产生财务回报。其次，我们使用来自一家领先的生物多样性融资机构的交易层面数据提供了实证证据。我们的发现与三维有效前沿（回报、风险和生物多样性影响）一致——具有有利风险回报特征的交易往往由纯私人资本融资，而对于其他交易，生物多样性影响需要足够大才能使用混合融资。总体而言，我们的结果表明，混合融资是改善这些项目风险回报状况的重要工具，从而增加其对私人投资者的吸引力并吸引私人资本。最后，我们的结果表明，在应对生物多样性危机方面，私人资本不太可能替代有效的公共政策。

Intellectual Property Rights Design and Academic Innovation: Evidence from University Patent Ownership Reform

知识产权制度设计与学术创新：来自大学专利所有权改革的证据

Jun Wang, Yi Qian

Programs：PR；JEL：K11, O30, O32, O38

Abstract

China has sought to promote the commercialization of patents at universities, notably through the three rights and mixed ownership reforms. These two reforms adopted different models for the allocation of university patent ownership. The three rights reform completely allocates patent ownership to the universities, while the mixed ownership approach allocates the majority of patent ownership to the inventors upon the generation of outcomes. We empirically tested the effects of the two patent ownership allocation models on the patent performance using Chinese patent data and university statistics. We found that external institutional environment influenced the anticipated outcomes of the reform pilot in both reform models. The three rights reform has a significant impact on patent licensing without affecting the quantity or quality of patents, while the mixed ownership reform has significantly increased patent sales and applications while tilting research and development toward research with relatively low creativity. This paper contributes to the literature on innovation policies governing the conditions for effective institutional changes.

中文摘要

中国寻求促进大学专利的商业化，特别是通过“三权”改革和混合所有制改革。这两项改革采用了不同的大学专利所有权分配模式。“三权”改革将专利所有权完全分配给大学，而混合所有制方法则在成果产生后将大部分专利所有权分配给发明人。我们利用中国专利数据和大学统计数据，实证检验了两种专利所有权分配模式对专利绩效的影响。我们发现，外部制度环境影响了两种改革模式中改革试点的预期结果。“三权”改革对专利许可有显著影响，但不影响专利数量或质量，而混合所有制改革则显著增加了专利销售和申请，同时将研发转向创造力相对较低的研究。本文为关于有效制度变革条件的创新政策文献做出了贡献。

A Trajectories-Based Approach to Measuring Intergenerational Mobility

基于轨迹的代际流动性测量方法

Yoosoon Chang, Steven N. Durlauf, Seunghee Lee, Joon Y. Park

Programs：ED, EFG, LS, PE；JEL：C13, J62

Abstract

This paper develops an approach to intergenerational mobility in which the trajectories of parental incomes during childhood and adolescence are the conditioning objects for characterizing dependence across generations. We use functional regression methods to produce an intergenerational elasticity curve that measures how marginal changes in income at each age affect expected offspring permanent income. Using the PSID, estimates of this curve exhibit near monotonicity with respect to age, so that parental incomes in middle childhood and adolescence have larger marginal effects than incomes in early childhood. When interactions are allowed to occur between incomes at different ages, we find a complex pattern of substitutability between incomes at ages that are close in time versus complementarity between parental incomes for ages early childhood and adolescence. Qualitatively similar results hold for offspring education while we do not find evidence of age-specific effects for occupation. We conclude that important information about the links between parental incomes and children exists beyond the scalar characterization of parental permanent income.

中文摘要

本文提出了一种代际流动性的方法，其中儿童期和青少年期父母收入的轨迹是描述代际依赖关系的条件对象。我们使用函数回归方法生成一条代际弹性曲线，该曲线衡量每个年龄收入边际变化如何影响预期的后代永久收入。利用PSID数据，该曲线的估计值随年龄呈现近乎单调性，因此童年中期和青少年期的父母收入比童年早期收入具有更大的边际效应。当允许不同年龄收入之间存在交互作用时，我们发现时间上接近的年龄收入之间存在复杂的替代模式，而童年早期和青少年期父母收入之间存在互补性。对于后代教育，结果在性质上相似，而对于职业，我们没有发现年龄特定效应的证据。我们得出结论，关于父母收入与子女之间联系的重要信息超出了父母永久收入的标量特征。

No. 11NBER w31019

Subjective Earnings Risk

主观收入风险

Andrew Caplin, Victoria Gregory, Eungik Lee, Søren Leth-Petersen, Johan Sæverud

Programs：LS；JEL：J30

Abstract

We introduce a survey instrument to measure earnings risk allowing for the possibility of quitting or being fired from the current job. We find these transitions to be the key drivers of subjective risk. A link with administrative data provides multiple credibility checks for correspondingly aggregated data. Yet it reveals subjective earning risk to be many times smaller than traditional estimates imply even when conditioning richly on demographics and job history. A life-cycle search model calibrated to match data on job transitions and earnings can replicate the distribution of subjective beliefs reported in the survey. Job-match quality, which directly impacts subjective risk but is impossible to identify in administrative data, contributes significantly to earnings risk. This highlights the importance of administratively-linked subjective risk measures.

中文摘要

我们引入了一种调查工具来衡量收入风险，该工具允许从当前工作中辞职或被解雇的可能性。我们发现这些转变是主观风险的关键驱动因素。与行政数据的链接为相应汇总数据提供了多重可信度检查。然而，它揭示了主观收入风险比传统估计所暗示的要小得多，即使在对人口统计特征和工作历史进行丰富条件设定时也是如此。一个校准以匹配工作转换和收入数据的生命周期搜索模型可以复制调查中报告的主观信念分布。工作匹配质量直接影响主观风险，但在行政数据中无法识别，它对收入风险有显著贡献。这凸显了与行政数据相关联的主观风险测量的重要性。

No. 12NBER w31018

The Simple Math of Royalties and Drug Competition During the 180-Day Generic Exclusivity Period

版税与180天仿制药独占期内药品竞争的简单数学

Keith M. Drake, Thomas McGuire

Programs：EH, LE；JEL：D22, D43, K21, L41

Abstract

In agreements settling patent challenges in the drug industry, the plaintiff brand commonly licenses the defendant generic to sell prior to patent expiry. Some agreements require the generic to pay the brand royalties. Despite the superficial flow of profits, royalty terms may function as part of an anticompetitive “reverse payment” made to the generic in exchange for delayed entry. Typically, the brand launches its own authorized generic (AG) during the first-to-file generic’s 180-day exclusivity period so there are two initial generic competitors. A royalty structure that deters the brand’s AG launch reduces the number of entrants to one, conveying net value to the generic and potentially inducing it to delay its entry. Our simple model shows that royalties usually have no place in a brand-generic agreement between rational actors. However, when royalties are conditioned on the brand’s AG launch, a range of royalty rates exists that both conveys net value to the generic and deters the brand from rationally introducing an AG. Declining royalty terms in a brand’s settlement with a first-to-file generic thus raise a red flag that the agreement is a pay-for-delay. Machine Learning as a Tool for Hypothesis Generation

Abstract

While hypothesis testing is a highly formalized activity, hypothesis generation remains largely informal. We propose a systematic procedure to generate novel hypotheses about human behavior, which uses the capacity of machine learning algorithms to notice patterns people might not. We illustrate the procedure with a concrete application: judge decisions about who to jail. We begin with a striking fact: The defendant’s face alone matters greatly for the judge’s jailing decision. It provides a way to produce novel, interpretable hypotheses from any high-dimensional dataset (e.g. cell phones, satellites, online behavior, news headlines, corporate filings, and high-frequency time series). A central tenet of our paper is that hypothesis generation is in and of itself a valuable activity, and hope this encourages future work in this largely “pre-scientific” stage of science.

Abstract

Comparing children coded as “Black” or “Mulatto” by census enumerators and linking these children across population censuses, we first document large gaps in educational attainment and income among African Americans with darker and lighter perceived skin tones. Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E62, N12

Abstract

We analyze whether government spending multipliers differ by the sign of the shock. Using aggregate historical U.S. data, we apply Ben Zeev’s (2020) nonlinear diagnostic tests and find evidence of nonlinearities in the impulse response functions of both government spending and GDP. Abstract
