Shifting Work Patterns with Generative AI

生成式人工智能带来的工作模式转变

Eleanor W. Dillon, Sonia Jaffe, Nicole Immorlica, Christopher T. Stanton

Programs：IO, LS, PR；JEL：L23, M1, M15, M5, O33

Abstract

We present evidence from a field experiment across 66 firms and 7,137 knowledge workers. Workers were randomly selected to access a generative AI tool integrated into applications they already used at work for email, meetings, and writing. In the second half of the 6-month experiment, the 80% of treated workers who used this tool spent two fewer hours on email each week and reduced their time working outside of regular hours. Apart from these individual time savings, we do not detect shifts in the quantity or composition of workers’ tasks resulting from individual-level AI provision.

中文摘要

我们展示了一项涵盖66家企业和7137名知识工作者的现场实验证据。工作者被随机分配使用一种生成式人工智能工具，该工具集成在他们工作中已使用的电子邮件、会议和写作应用程序中。在为期6个月的实验后半段，使用该工具的80%的受处理工作者每周在电子邮件上花费的时间减少了两个小时，并减少了在常规工作时间之外的工作时间。除了这些个人时间节省之外，我们没有检测到因个体层面的人工智能提供而导致的工作任务数量或构成的变化。

No. 2NBER w33794

Revisiting the Forecasts of Others

重新审视他人预测

Ryan Chahrour, Kyle Jurado

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：D83, E20, E21

Abstract

In macroeconomic models with dispersed information, agents have an incentive to learn from endogenous variables, which themselves depend on the forecasts of others. This paper revisits the model of Townsend (1983) to characterize how this mechanism affects equilibrium dynamics. The first part of the paper simplifies, revises, and extends past results about situations when prices are fully revealing. The second part shows that full revelation does not occur in the original model and proves that the equilibrium state vector is infinite-dimensional. It also provides a new numerical solution procedure for such cases, which operates entirely in the frequency domain.

中文摘要

在信息分散的宏观经济模型中，经济主体有动机从内生变量中学习，而这些内生变量本身又依赖于他人的预测。本文重新审视Townsend（1983）的模型，以刻画这一机制如何影响均衡动态。论文的第一部分简化、修正并扩展了关于价格何时完全揭示的过往结果。第二部分表明，在原始模型中完全揭示并未发生，并证明了均衡状态向量是无限维的。它还为此类情况提供了一种新的数值求解程序，该程序完全在频域中运行。

No. 3NBER w33793

Migration and Regime Change: Outflows Follow Democratic Decline, Inflows Fuel Illiberal Drift

移民与政权更迭：外流伴随民主衰退，流入助长非自由化倾向

Assaf Razin

Programs：CF, IFM；JEL：F02, H7

Abstract

This paper explores the two-way relationship between international migration and political regime change, emphasizing the potential for a feedback loop: political shifts influence migration patterns, and migration can, in turn, affect political developments. Using a Difference-in-Differences (DiD) approach and a dataset combining migration flows, regime quality indicators (CHRI), and measures of economic integration such as EU membership, the study identifies three key findings. First, substantial immigration into politically fragile democracies can further weaken their institutions. Second, democratic decline tends to increase emigration, undermining a country's ability to a democratic institutional recovery. Third, international economic integration, particularly in our study, through EU accession—shapes how emigration responds to political change.

中文摘要

本文探讨了国际移民与政治政权更迭之间的双向关系，强调了反馈循环的可能性：政治变化影响移民模式，而移民反过来又可能影响政治发展。采用双重差分方法，并结合移民流动、政权质量指标（CHRI）以及欧盟成员国身份等经济一体化衡量标准的数据集，研究确定了三个关键发现。首先，大量移民进入政治脆弱的民主国家可能进一步削弱其制度。其次，民主衰退往往会增加移民外流，削弱国家实现民主制度复苏的能力。第三，国际经济一体化——在我们的研究中，特别是通过加入欧盟——塑造了移民外流对政治变化的反应方式。

The 2025 Trade War: Dynamic Impacts Across U.S. States and the Global Economy

2025年贸易战：对美国各州和全球经济的动态影响

Andrés Rodríguez-Clare, Mauricio Ulate, Jose P. Vasquez

Programs：ITI；JEL：F10

Abstract

We use a dynamic trade and reallocation model with downward nominal wage rigidities to quantitatively assess the economic consequences of recent U.S. tariff increases on imports from Mexico, Canada, and China, as well as the “reciprocal” tariff changes announced on “Liberation Day” and retaliatory measures by other countries. Higher tariffs lead to a rise in U.S. manufacturing employment, but this comes at the cost of declines in service and agricultural jobs. Overall employment falls, as lower real wages reduce labor-force participation. Real income in the U.S. declines by about 1% by 2028, the final year we assume the tariffs remain in effect. A key feature of our analysis is the disaggregation of the U.S. into its 50 states, incorporating cross-state redistribution of tariff revenue. This allows us to identify which states gain or lose more from the policy shock. Around half of the states experience real income losses, with some seeing declines greater than 3%. At the international level, close U.S. trading partners—including Canada, Mexico, China, and Ireland—suffer the largest real income losses.

中文摘要

我们使用一个包含名义工资向下刚性的动态贸易与再分配模型，定量评估近期美国对来自墨西哥、加拿大和中国的进口商品加征关税，以及在“解放日”宣布的“对等”关税变化和其他国家报复性措施的经济后果。更高的关税导致美国制造业就业增加，但这以服务业和农业岗位的减少为代价。总体就业下降，因为实际工资降低减少了劳动力参与。到2028年（我们假设关税持续生效的最后一年），美国实际收入下降约1%。我们分析的一个关键特征是将美国分解为50个州，并纳入关税收入的跨州再分配。这使我们能够识别哪些州从政策冲击中获益或损失更多。大约一半的州经历了实际收入损失，其中一些州的降幅超过3%。在国际层面，美国密切的贸易伙伴——包括加拿大、墨西哥、中国和爱尔兰——遭受了最大的实际收入损失。

No. 5NBER w33791

Separation of Church and State Curricula? Public Standards, Private Values, and Textbook Content

政教课程分离？审视公立与宗教私立学校教科书

Anjali Adukia, Emileigh Harrison

Programs：CH, ED, LS；JEL：I0, I20, I21, I28, I29, J0, J10, J15, J16, J18, J19, Z10, Z11, Z12, Z13, Z18

Abstract

Curricula are a critical site of cultural transmission, yet we know little about the values conveyed in textbooks across educational settings or the forces that shape them. We examine textbooks from Texas and California public schools and religious-private and home schools spanning 1980-2022, using computational and AI tools to measure presence and portrayal of people, topics, and values over time. Despite narratives of political polarization, Texas and California textbooks show substantial similarity. In contrast, religious private-school textbooks place greater emphasis on religious and character values and depict characters with lighter skin colors; we show these differences align with preferences and demographics of target markets. To explain public-school convergence, we develop a framework in which publishers serve a national market constrained by state standards and provide evidence consistent with cross-state spillovers in textbook content following standards revisions. Conversely, private-school publishers serve a more segmented, less regulated market, leading to greater differentiation.

中文摘要

课程传授知识、灌输价值观并塑造集体记忆。尽管通过美国择校项目，宗教学校的公共资金不断增加，但人们对它们教授的内容知之甚少。我们考察了来自公立学校、宗教私立学校和家庭学校的教科书，应用计算方法（包括人工智能工具）来测量人物、主题和价值观随时间的存在与描绘方式。尽管存在政治极化的叙事，我们的发现显示德克萨斯州和加利福尼亚州的公立学校教科书之间几乎没有有意义的差异。然而，宗教学校教科书中的女性代表性较少，描绘的人物肤色较浅，并且对进化论和宗教等主题的呈现方式不同。每个集合中超过三分之一的页面传达了品格价值观，宗教学校教科书中的比例更高。重要的相似之处也浮现出来：所有教科书集合都很少包含LGBTQIA+讨论，将女性描绘得比男性更积极但更不活跃或更不强大，并且对美国建国时代和奴隶制的描绘背景相似。

No. 6NBER w33790

Litigation Risk and the Valuation of Legal Claims: A Real Option Approach

诉讼风险与法律索赔估值：一种实物期权方法

Jose Portela, Eduardo S. Schwartz, Jaime Aparicio Garcia

Programs：CF；JEL：G01, G11, K0

Abstract

Legal claims are increasingly being considered as an alternative asset class, however, there appears to be a lack of a standard methodology for valuing litigation risk. This paper proposes a dynamic real options framework for the valuation of legal claims, explicitly incorporating the uncertainty and sequential nature of litigation processes. We develop a continuous-time stochastic model that accounts for the main procedural milestones and uncertainties, enabling the simulation of diverse litigation trajectories to estimate the net present value of a claim. The model permits the decision-maker to optimally continue or abandon the litigation at various stages, thereby capturing the embedded option value and enhancing claim valuation. This approach offers a novel risk management and valuation tool for a range of stakeholders, including investors, third-party funders, claimants, defendants, legal practitioners, auditors, and insurers. We demonstrate the practical relevance of the methodology by applying it to an actual international investment arbitration case.

中文摘要

法律索赔正越来越多地被视作一种另类资产类别，然而，似乎缺乏评估诉讼风险的标准方法。本文提出了一个用于法律索赔估值的动态实物期权框架，明确纳入了诉讼过程的不确定性和序列性。我们开发了一个连续时间随机模型，该模型考虑了主要的程序性里程碑和不确定性，能够模拟多样化的诉讼轨迹以估计索赔的净现值。该模型允许决策者在不同阶段最优地选择继续或放弃诉讼，从而捕捉嵌入的期权价值并增强索赔估值。这种方法为包括投资者、第三方资助者、索赔人、被告、法律从业者、审计师和保险公司在内的各类利益相关者提供了一种新颖的风险管理和估值工具。我们通过将该方法应用于一个实际的国际投资仲裁案例来证明其实践相关性。

No. 7NBER w33789

Renewable Asset Price Volatility and Its Implications for Decarbonization

可再生资产价格波动性及其对脱碳的影响

Harrison Hong, Jeffrey D. Kubik, Edward P. Shore

Programs：AP, EEE, EFG, IO；JEL：G10, G11, Q20, Q4, Q54, Q56

Abstract

We find that the price volatility of renewable assets is significantly greater than that of brown assets. Our causal estimates leverage the response of electricity and credit markets to US state-level renewable portfolio standards that require some utilities to use renewables while exempting others. This extra risk is related to more volatile electricity prices and revenues, consistent with uncertainties including renewables intermittency. Using a growth model where the share of green capital balances climate damages and diversification benefits, we find that greater green-asset volatility is a more important determinant of economy-wide decarbonization than productivity differences of green versus brown capital.

中文摘要

我们发现可再生资产的价格波动性显著高于棕色资产。我们的因果估计利用了电力市场和信贷市场对美国各州可再生组合标准的反应，该标准要求部分公用事业公司使用可再生能源，同时豁免其他公司。这种额外风险与更不稳定的电价和收入相关，与包括可再生能源间歇性在内的不确定性一致。使用一个绿色资本份额平衡气候损害与多元化收益的增长模型，我们发现，与绿色资本相对于棕色资本的生产率差异相比，更大的绿色资产波动性是决定整个经济脱碳的更重要的因素。

No. 8NBER w33787

Job Amenity Shocks and Labor Reallocation

工作便利性冲击与劳动力重新配置

Sadhika Bagga, Lukas Friedrich Mann, Ayşegül Şahin, Giovanni L. Violante

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：J01, J2

Abstract

We introduce aggregate shocks to workers' value of job amenities in a frictional equilibrium model of the labor market with on-the-job search, where the job creation cost is sunk and quits trigger vacancies. We examine how key labor market indicators respond to this shock: when the valuation of the amenity is heterogeneous in the population, labor reallocation ensues. A calibrated version of the model can quantitatively account for many distinct traits of the post-pandemic labor market recovery through three aggregate shocks: a temporary fall in productivity to account for the short, but sharp, downturn; a decline in the willingness to work; and, crucially, a persistent increase in workers' evaluation of job amenities. Cross-sectoral patterns of vacancies, quit rates, job-filling rates, and wages—where sectors are ranked by their share of teleworkable jobs—provide support to the view that the key amenity in question is the ability to work remotely.

中文摘要

我们在一个包含在职搜索的摩擦性均衡劳动力市场模型中引入了工人对工作便利性价值的总体冲击，其中工作创造成本是沉没的，辞职会触发职位空缺。我们考察了关键劳动力市场指标如何应对这一冲击：当人群中对便利性的估值存在异质性时，劳动力重新配置就会发生。该模型的校准版本可以通过三个总体冲击定量解释疫情后劳动力市场复苏的许多独特特征：生产力暂时下降以解释短暂但急剧的衰退；工作意愿下降；以及关键的是，工人对工作便利性评估的持续上升。跨部门的职位空缺率、辞职率、职位填补率和工资模式（按可远程工作职位份额对部门进行排序）支持了这样一种观点，即所讨论的关键便利性是远程工作的能力。

No. 9NBER w33786

Culture and Contemporary Political Preferences

文化与当代政治偏好

Vasiliki Fouka, Marco Tabellini

Programs：POL；JEL：D72, N30, P0, Z1

Abstract

This chapter reviews the literature on the relationship between culture and political preferences. We distinguish conceptually between the direct cultural transmission of political ideology and the transmission of more primitive preferences and beliefs that influence preferences over policies, parties, and forms of government. While there is substantial evidence that political preferences persist across generations and within communities, the literature often does not specify which primitive values and beliefs drive this persistence. A growing body of work points to a multifaceted mapping from underlying preferences and beliefs over the material world and social relations to political ideology. In this chapter, we summarize these studies, organize their findings in a coherent framework, and suggest possible directions for future research.

中文摘要

本章回顾了关于文化与政治偏好之间关系的文献。我们在概念上区分了政治意识形态的直接文化传播，以及影响对政策、政党和政府形式偏好的更原始偏好和信念的传播。虽然有大量证据表明政治偏好会在代际和社区内持续存在，但文献通常没有具体说明是哪些原始价值观和信念驱动了这种持续性。越来越多的研究指出，从对物质世界和社会关系的潜在偏好和信念到政治意识形态之间存在多方面的映射关系。在本章中，我们总结了这些研究，将其发现组织在一个连贯的框架内，并为未来研究提出了可能的方向。

No. 10NBER w33785

Who Benefits from Place-Based Policies? Evidence from Matched Employer-Employee Data

谁从地方导向政策中受益？来自匹配雇主-雇员数据的证据

Philipp Grunau, Florian Hoffmann, Thomas Lemieux, Mirko Titze

Programs：LS；JEL：H71, J21, J31, J63, R12

Abstract

We study the granular wage and employment effects of a German place-based policy using a research design that leverages EU-wide rules governing program parameters at the regional level. The program subsidizes investments to create jobs with a subsidy rate that varies across labor market regions. We use matched data on the universe of establishments and their employees, establishment-level panel data on program participation, and regional scores that generate spatial discontinuities in program eligibility and generosity. Spatial spillovers of the program linked to changing commuting patterns can be assessed using information on place of work and place of residence, a unique feature of the data. We find that the program helps establishments create jobs that disproportionately benefit younger and less-educated workers. Funded establishments increase their wages, but, unlike employment, wage gains do not persist in the long run. Employment effects estimated at the local level are slightly larger than establishment-level estimates, suggesting limited economic spillover effect. Spatial spillovers are large as over half of the employment increase comes from commuters. Using subsidy rates as an instrumental variable for actual subsidies indicates that it costs approximately EUR 25,000 to create a new job in the economically disadvantaged areas targeted by the program.

中文摘要

我们利用一种研究设计来研究德国地方导向政策对工资和就业的微观影响，该设计利用了欧盟层面管理区域层面项目参数的规则。该计划以补贴率补贴投资以创造就业机会，补贴率因劳动力市场区域而异。我们使用了涵盖所有机构及其雇员的匹配数据、机构层面关于项目参与的的面板数据，以及产生项目资格和慷慨程度空间不连续性的区域评分。与通勤模式变化相关的项目空间溢出效应可以通过工作地点和居住地信息来评估，这是数据的一个独特特征。我们发现，该计划帮助机构创造了不成比例地惠及年轻和受教育程度较低工人的就业岗位。获得资助的机构提高了工资，但与就业不同，工资增长在长期内无法持续。在地方层面估计的就业效应略大于机构层面的估计，表明经济溢出效应有限。空间溢出效应很大，因为超过一半的就业增长来自通勤者。使用补贴率作为实际补贴的工具变量表明，在该计划针对的经济弱势地区，创造一个新工作岗位的成本约为25,000欧元。

No. 11NBER w33784

The Tariff Tax Cut: Tariffs as Revenue

关税减税：作为收入的关税

George A. Alessandria, Jiaxiaomei Ding, Shafaat Y. Khan, Carter B. Mix

Programs：EFG, IFM, ITI；JEL：E6, F10, F4

Abstract

We evaluate the aggregate effects of a change in tariffs on the US and world economies when tariff revenue is used to enact fiscal reform. Our model combines a standard international model of fiscal policy with taxes and a dynamic model of trade participation and tariffs that allows for uncertainty and transitions. We consider effects of permanent and temporary tariffs–with and without retaliation–when tariff revenue is used to reduce taxes on capital or labor or to subsidize investment. Compared to a lump sum redistribution, using tariff revenue for these reforms always boosts economic activity. Key to our analysis is the effect of trade dynamics on import substitution, such that tariff revenue after an increase in tariffs is higher in the short run than in the long run. When increasing the tariff by 20 percentage points, the revenue raised is largest when tariffs are temporary, unilateral, and used to subsidize investment, increasing by about 2 percent of GDP. This case also yields a large temporary increase in the trade balance. We find the welfare-maximizing unilateral tariff is close to 18 percent when tariff revenue is used to subsidize investment compared to 0 percent under a lump sum redistribution. We also find cutting capital taxes does not generate as much growth as introducing an investment subsidy since tariffs raise the price of investment substantially.
