No. 1NBER w33714

The Global Macro Database: A New International Macroeconomic Dataset

全球宏观数据库：一个新的国际宏观经济数据集

Karsten Müller, Chenzi Xu, Mohamed Lehbib, Ziliang Chen

Programs：CF, DAE, IFM, ITI, ME；JEL：E01, F01, N01, N10, O10

中文摘要

全球宏观数据库是一个开源、持续更新的宏观经济统计数据数据集，它统一并扩展了现有资源。通过协调和整合来自32个主要当代来源（包括国际货币基金组织、世界银行和经合组织）的数据以及来自78个额外数据集的历史记录，我们为243个国家的46个变量构建了完整的年度时间序列。该数据库涵盖了从现代数据收集起源到2030年预测估计的全球宏观经济趋势。利用这个广泛的数据库，我们研究了金融危机和全球温度冲击的长期产出损失，这是两个历史时间序列作为关键输入的实证应用。我们的研究结果表明，金融危机与未来五十年内可统计检测到的实际GDP收缩相关，其规模比先前估计的要大得多。温度冲击也能预测未来长达30年的实际GDP收缩，尤其是在新兴经济体中。

Abstract

The Global Macro Database is an open-source, continuously updated dataset of macroeconomic statistics that unifies and extends existing resources. By harmonizing and integrating data from 32 major contemporary sources—including the IMF, World Bank, and OECD—with historical records from 78 additional datasets, we construct comprehensive annual time series for 46 variables across 243 countries. This database covers global macroeconomic trends from the origins of modern data collection to projected estimates for 2030. Using this extensive database, we study the long-run output losses of financial crises and global temperature shocks, two applications in which historical time series are a crucial input. Our findings show that financial crises are associated with statistically detectable contractions in real GDP for five decades into the future, which are considerably larger than previously estimated. Temperature shocks also predict real GDP contractions up to 30 years ahead, especially in emerging economies.

No. 2NBER w33713

The Rise of Absorptive Research in Corporate America: 1945-1980

美国企业吸收性研究的兴起：1945-1980

Ashish Arora, Sharon Belenzon, Jungkyu Suh, Hansen Zhang

Programs：PR；JEL：O3, O31, O33, O34, O35, O36, O38, O39

中文摘要

我们使用企业研究活动的多个指标，研究了1945年至1980年二战后美国企业研究的“黄金时代”。我们使用集成学习方法将企业分类为科学领导者、吸收者或追随者。我们的分析表明，只有一小部分企业（我们称之为领导者）投资于处于科学前沿的内部研究，其目标是产生突破性发明。吸收者投资于研究主要是为了吸收外部科学发现以推动其发明活动。追随者通常利用较旧的科学知识产生增量创新。与此一致，我们发现领导者更有可能处于技术前沿，享有更大的市场力量，并从政府采购合同中受益。随着大学和初创公司开始将学术发现商业化，对“吸收性企业实验室”的需求下降了。这种转变最终改变了美国的创新格局，加深了大学、初创公司和现有企业之间的创新劳动分工，只有少数领导者企业继续投资于基础科学。

Abstract

We study the post-World War II “Golden Age” of American corporate research from 1945 to 1980, using multiple indicators of corporate research activity. We use an ensemble learning approach to classify firms as either Science Leaders, Absorbers or Followers. Our analysis reveals that only a small fraction of firms, whom we call Leaders, invest in internal research that is on the scientific frontier, with the objective to generate breakthrough inventions. Absorbers invest in research principally to absorb external scientific discoveries to fuel their inventive activity. Followers typically generate incremental innovations, using older scientific knowledge. Consistent with this, we find Leaders were more likely to be at the technological frontier, enjoy greater market power, and benefit from government procurement contracts. As universities and startups began to commercialize academic discoveries, the need for “absorptive corporate labs” declined. The shift ultimately transformed the American innovation landscape, deepening the division of innovative labor between universities, startups, and incumbent corporations, with only a select group of Leader firms continuing to invest in basic science.

No. 3NBER w33712

The Political Economy of Firm Networks: CEO Ideology and Global Trade

企业网络的政治经济学：CEO意识形态与全球贸易

Elisabeth Kempf, Mancy Luo, Margarita Tsoutsoura

Programs：CF, ITI, POL；JEL：F1, G41

中文摘要

我们研究了企业领导人的政治意识形态如何塑造跨境企业网络。利用美国企业CEO与外国政府在势均力敌的外国选举前后意识形态一致性的变化，我们表明，当外国政府领导人的意识形态与其CEO的意识形态距离变得更远时，美国企业更有可能终止与该国政府的贸易关系。这种影响集中在持有强烈政治观点的CEO身上，并且在较短的贸易关系中尤为显著，这表明意识形态一致性在更灵活和可替代的联系中更为相关。我们的发现强调了意识形态在塑造国际企业网络形成和持续性中的作用。

Abstract

We examine how the political ideology of corporate leaders shapes cross-border firm networks. Exploiting changes in ideological alignment between U.S. firm CEOs and foreign governments around close foreign elections, we show that U.S. firms are more likely to terminate trade relationships with countries led by governments whose ideology becomes more distant from that of their CEOs. The impact is concentrated among CEOs holding strong political views, and is particularly pronounced for shorter trade relationships, suggesting ideological alignment is more relevant in more flexible and substitutable connections. Our findings highlight the role of ideology in shaping the formation and persistence of international firm networks.

No. 4NBER w33711

A Theory of Supply Function Choice and Aggregate Supply

供给函数选择与总供给理论

Joel P. Flynn, Georgios Nikolakoudis, Karthik Sastry

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E31, E32, E50

中文摘要

现代总供给理论建立在企业设定价格并承诺按市场需求生产的假设之上。我们取消了这一战略限制，允许企业选择供给函数，即描述在每个生产数量下所收取价格的映射。在理论上，我们刻画了企业在一般均衡中最优供给函数选择的特征，并研究了其对总供给的影响。在通胀不确定性较低、异质性需求不确定性较高以及需求弹性较低的情况下，总供给曲线趋于平坦。在定量上，我们的理论可以解释大缓和时期总供给曲线的平坦化以及1970年代和2020年代总供给曲线的陡峭化。

Abstract

Modern theories of aggregate supply are built on the foundation that firms set prices and commit to producing whatever the market demands. We remove this strategic restriction and allow firms to choose supply functions, mappings that describe the prices charged at each quantity of production. Theoretically, we characterize firms’ optimal supply function choices in general equilibrium and study the resulting implications for aggregate supply. Aggregate supply flattens under lower inflation uncertainty, higher idiosyncratic demand uncertainty, and less elastic demand. Quantitatively, our theory can rationalize the flattening of aggregate supply during the Great Moderation and steepening during the 1970s and 2020s.

No. 5NBER w33710

The Invention of Corporate Governance

公司治理的发明

Yueran Ma, Andrei Shleifer

Programs：CF；JEL：G0, G3

中文摘要

公司治理的分析始于现代资本主义的一个核心特征——大型企业中所有权与控制权的分离——这一特征首先由Berle和Means（1932）进行了实证记录。这种分离带来了若干代理问题，反映了经理人与股东之间的冲突，例如经理人的自我交易、低努力、在职消费以及过度增长和多元化。Berle和Means认为自我交易是核心代理问题，并强调法律是解决该问题的基本机制。Jensen和Meckling（1976）考虑了在职消费，并强调私人机制（如对经理人的财务激励）来对抗浪费性的额外津贴。Jensen（1986）则聚焦于过度增长和多元化，这使他依赖杠杆和收购。公共公司治理机制（主要是法律）与市场治理的结合塑造了理论与实践。

Abstract

The analysis of corporate governance begins with a central feature of modern capitalism—the separation of ownership and control in large corporations—first empirically documented by Berle and Means (1932). Such separation entails several agency problems reflecting conflicts between managers and shareholders, such as self-dealing by managers, low effort, consumption of perquisites, and excessive growth and diversification. Berle and Means saw self-dealing as the central agency problem and stressed the law as the fundamental mechanism of addressing it. Jensen and Meckling (1976) considered the consumption of perquisites and emphasized private mechanisms, such as financial incentives for managers, to counter wasteful perks. Jensen (1986) instead focused on excessive growth and diversification, which led him to count on leverage and takeovers. The combination of public corporate governance mechanisms, mostly the law, and market governance shaped both theory and practice.

No. 6NBER w33709

How Tariffs Affect Trade Deficits

关税如何影响贸易逆差

Arnaud Costinot, Iván Werning

Programs：EFG, IFM, ME, PE；JEL：F40

中文摘要

我们研究永久性进口关税对贸易逆差的正面（而非规范）影响。我们考虑一个具有一般偏好和技术的两期贸易模型。我们首先发展出一个加总结果，表明可以基于对总进口和总出口的诱导偏好进行分析。我们的主要结果提供了关税中性以及关税减少逆差的条件。一个核心主题是，总进口和总出口的静态恩格尔曲线是理解这一动态问题的关键。

Abstract

We study the positive (not normative) effect of a permanent import tariff on trade deficits. We consider a two-period trade model with general preferences and technology. We first develop an aggregation result showing one can work with induced preferences over aggregate imports and exports. Our main results provide conditions under which tariffs are neutral as well as conditions under which they reduce deficits. A central theme is that the static Engel curve for aggregate imports and exports holds the key to this dynamic question.

The Law and Economics of Lawyers: Evidence from the Revolving Door in China’s Judicial System

律师的法律经济学：来自中国司法系统旋转门的证据

John Zhuang Liu, Wenwei Peng, Shaoda Wang, Daniel Xu

Programs：DEV, LE, POL；JEL：K0, P48

中文摘要

本文通过研究中国司法系统中法官与律师之间的“旋转门”——即辞去法官职务转而从事律师职业的前法官——来考察权力律师如何塑造司法和经济结果。在原本相同的诉讼案件中，旋转门律师为其客户带来的胜诉率高出8-23%。他们在主场法院与客场法院的表现差异表明，这些收益既源于“懂行”也源于“识人”。我们扩展了Dewatripont和Tirole（1999）的理论框架，以表明旋转门律师在社会中创造了两种相反的力量：他们通过证据和推理提升司法决策质量，但也利用战略论证和关系使结果偏向其客户。我们估计了一个司法过程的结构模型来量化这些权衡，并发现增加权力律师的供给对均衡司法质量可能产生非单调的影响。

Abstract

This paper examines how power lawyers shape judicial and economic outcomes by studying the “revolving door” between judges and lawyers in China’s judicial system—namely, former judges who quit the bench to practice law. In otherwise identical lawsuits, revolving-door lawyers deliver 8−23% higher win rate for their clients. Their performance in home versus away courts suggests these gains stem from both “know how” and “know who.” We extend the theoretical framework of Dewatripont and Tirole (1999) to show that revolving-door lawyers create countervailing forces in society: they enhance judicial decision-making through evidence and reasoning, but also exploit strategic arguments and connections to bias outcomes in favor of their clients. We estimate a structural model of the judicial process to quantify these trade-offs and find that increasing the supply of power lawyers can have a non-monotonic effect on equilibrium judicial quality.

No. 8NBER w33707

Firm Presence, Pollution, and Agglomeration: Evidence from a Randomized Environmental Place-Based Policy

企业存在、污染与集聚：来自一项随机环境地点导向政策的证据

Michael Gechter, Namrata Kala

Programs：DEV, EEE；JEL：D22, L20, O10, Q52, Q53, Q56, Q58, R38

中文摘要

企业的选址决策由于竞争或集聚力量对其他企业产生外部性，并对环境产生影响。我们研究了一项环境地点导向政策，该政策随机迁移了新德里的20,000家企业。搬迁减少了污染，但企业退出增加。我们将企业向工业地块的外生分配与一个模型相结合，以估计对邻近企业的溢出效应，表明如果根据投入产出关联分配企业到地块，企业存活率本可以提高。这些结果提供了关于企业存在如何影响环境质量以及如何利用溢出效应最小化受监管企业成本的因果证据。

Abstract

Firm location decisions impose externalities on other firms due to competitive or agglomerative forces, and on the environment. We study an environmental place-based policy that randomly moved 20,000 firms in New Delhi. Relocation reduces pollution, but firm exit increases. We combine the exogenous assignment of firms to industrial plots with a model to estimate spillovers on neighboring firms, showing that firm survival rates could have been increased by allocating firms to plots accounting for input-output linkages. These results provide causal evidence on how firm presence impacts environmental quality, and how spillovers can be used to minimize costs on regulated firms.

No. 9NBER w33706

Reducing Nutritional Disparities Through Targeted Pricing: Evidence from a Large Restaurant Chain

通过定向定价减少营养差异：来自大型连锁餐厅的证据

Weijia (Daisy) Dai, Ginger Zhe Jin, Ben Zou

Programs：EH, IO；JEL：D12, I12, I14, L83, R20

中文摘要

不同社会经济群体之间的营养差异加剧了美国的健康不平等。本文考察了由企业主导的、切实可行的定价干预措施是否可以通过引导弱势消费者选择更健康的选项来减少这些差异。利用一家大型快餐连锁店的详细交易层面数据，我们表明，弱势社区的消费者不成比例地选择了热量更高、更不健康的食品。我们利用来自连锁店范围促销折扣的准实验变化来估计混合逻辑离散选择模型，并恢复不同人口群体之间的偏好异质性。我们发现，较低社会经济地位的消费者在所有营养特征上对价格都更敏感，并且在健康与不健康选项之间的替代方面表现出灵活性。反事实模拟表明，在弱势社区进行适度、有针对性的价格调整可以促进更健康的饮食，同时对收入的影响最小。

Abstract

Nutritional disparities across socioeconomic groups contribute to health inequality in the U.S. This paper examines whether practical, firm-led pricing interventions can reduce these disparities by shifting choices toward healthier options among disadvantaged consumers. Using detailed transaction-level data from a large fast-food restaurant chain, we show that consumers in disadvantaged neighborhoods disproportionately select higher-calorie, less healthy items. We exploit quasi-experimental variation from chain-wide promotional discounts to estimate a mixed logit discrete choice model and recover preference heterogeneity across demographic groups. We find that lower-SES consumers are more price-sensitive across all nutritional profiles and exhibit flexibility in substituting between healthy and unhealthy options. Counterfactual simulations demonstrate that modest, targeted price adjustments in disadvantaged neighborhoods can promote healthier eating with minimal impact on revenue.

Did War Mobilization Cause Aggregate and Regional Growth?

战争动员是否导致了总量与区域增长？

Taylor Jaworski, Dongkyu Yang

Programs：DAE；JEL：N92

中文摘要

美国参与第二次世界大战导致国内资源大量动员，以生产用于欧洲和太平洋战场的物资。我们为战争动员对战后时期美国长期经济增长和区域发展的影响提供了新的估计。在经济地理模型的指导下，我们将我们的估计解释为动员对地方生产率的直接影响。研究结果表明，尽管存在区域差异，但最可能的总量福利影响是适度的。此外，工业动员对东北部和中西部制造业增长的贡献相对较大，而对南部和西部的贡献较小。

Abstract

The participation of the United States in World War II led to a substantial mobilization of domestic resources to produce the materiel used on the battlefields of Europe and in the Pacific. We produce new estimates for the impact of war mobilization on long-run economic growth and regional development in the United States over the postwar period. Guided by an economic geography model, we interpret our estimates as the direct effect of mobilization on local productivity. The findings suggest the largest likely aggregate welfare impact was modest, although there is variation across region. In addition, industrial mobilization contributed to manufacturing growth relatively more in the Northeast and Midwest, and less in the South and West.

No. 11NBER w33704

Option Value Neglect: Evidence from Centralized College Admission

期权价值忽视：来自集中式大学录取的证据

Ao Wang, Shaoda Wang, Xiaoyang Ye

Programs：ED；JEL：D47, D81, D91, I21, I28

中文摘要

顺序选择在日常生活中无处不在，然而在此类情境中做出最优决策——其中正确考虑期权价值至关重要——可能具有挑战性。本文提供了关于在高风险决策中忽视期权价值的现场实验证据，并量化了相关的福利后果。我们研究了一个集中式大学录取系统，学生在此系统中提交简短的偏好列表。尽管期权价值应鼓励更冒险的顶层选择，但许多学生过于谨慎，未能包含更安全的较低排名选项。我们认为，定向认知——即做出短视决策而忽略后续选项的价值——解释了这种行为。一项针对基于框架的关键预测的现场实验表明，约50%的申请者表现出这种模式，尤其是在弱势学生中。反事实分析表明，去偏干预可以显著缩小结果差距并提高整体效率。

Abstract

Sequential choices are ubiquitous in daily life, yet making optimal decisions in such settings—where properly accounting for option value is crucial—can be challenging. This paper provides field experimental evidence on the neglect of option value in high-stakes decisions and quantifies the associated welfare consequences. We study a centralized college admissions system where students submit brief preference lists. Although option value should encourage riskier top choices, many students are overly cautious and fail to include safer lower-ranked options. We argue that directed cognition—making myopic decisions while ignoring the value of subsequent options—explains this behavior. An in-field experiment targeting a key framing-based prediction shows that about 50% of applicants exhibit this pattern, especially among disadvantaged students. Counterfactual analysis suggests that de-biasing interventions could significantly reduce outcome gaps and improve overall efficiency.

No. 12NBER w33703

From Distraction to Dedication: Commitment and Incentives Against Phone Use in the Classroom

从分心到专注：针对课堂手机使用的承诺与激励措施

Billur Aksoy, Lester R. Lusher, Scott E. Carrell

Programs：ED, PE；JEL：I21

中文摘要

课堂上的手机使用已被证明与学业成绩下降有关。我们展示了一项与一款被宣传为软性承诺设备的应用程序合作，在一所大型公立大学进行的实地实验结果，该应用程序通过提供激励措施来减少课堂上的手机使用。我们发现，使用该应用程序改善了课堂专注度、出勤率和整体学业满意度。对课堂外时间花费的分析表明存在潜在的替代效应：使用该应用程序的学生分配给学习的时间更少，尤其是在校园内。总体而言，尽管统计上不显著，但我们发现与使用该应用程序相关的成绩单分数有所改善。

Abstract

Phone usage in the classroom has been linked to worsened academic outcomes. We present findings from a field experiment conducted at a large public university in partnership with an app marketed as a soft commitment device that provides incentives to reduce phone use in the classroom. We find that app usage led to improvements in classroom focus, attendance, and overall academic satisfaction. Analysis of time spent outside the classroom suggests a potential substitution effect: students using the app allocated less time to study, particularly on campus. Overall, though statistically insignificant, we find improvements in transcript grades associated with app usage.

No. 13NBER w33702

Long-Run Effects of Trade Wars

贸易战的长期影响

David Baqaee, Hannes Malmberg

Programs：EFG, ITI；JEL：F0, F1, F30, F40

中文摘要

这篇简短的论文表明，在分析贸易战对实际工资和消费的长期影响时，考虑资本调整至关重要。原因是贸易战通过对进口投资品及其投入品征税，提高了投资品相对于劳动力的价格。这种价格变化抑制了资本需求，缩减了长期资本存量，并与资本固定不变的情景相比，压低了消费和实际工资。我们通过使用动态量化贸易模型研究美国最近的关税来阐述这一机制。当允许资本存量调整时，长期消费和工资的反应都更大且更负面。在资本调整的情况下，美国消费可能下降2.6%，而如静态模型那样资本固定不变时则下降0.6%。也就是说，资本存量调整成为长期结果的主导驱动因素，比静态贸易模型中的标准机制——贸易条件效应和生产在国家间的错配——更为重要。

Abstract

This short note shows that accounting for capital adjustment is critical when analyzing the long-run effects of trade wars on real wages and consumption. The reason is that trade wars increase the relative price between investment goods and labor by taxing imported investment goods and their inputs. This price shift depresses capital demand, shrinks the long-run capital stock, and pushes down consumption and real wages compared to scenarios when capital is fixed. We illustrate this mechanism by studying recent US tariffs using a dynamic quantitative trade model. When the capital stock is allowed to adjust, long-run consumption and wage responses are both larger and more negative. With capital adjustment, U.S. consumption can fall by 2.6%, compared to 0.6% when capital is held fixed, as in a static model. That is, capital stock adjustment emerges as a dominant driver of long-run outcomes, more important than the standard mechanisms from static trade models — terms-of-trade effects and mis- allocation of production across countries.

No. 14NBER w33701

Skewed Fluctuations and Propagation Through Production Networks

偏态波动与通过生产网络的传导

Sai Krishna Kamepalli, Serena Ng, Francisco Ruge-Murcia

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：C3, C5, E03

中文摘要

宏观经济时间序列中的偏度可能外生地产生（如果冲击呈不对称分布），也可能内生地产生（当冲击通过生产网络传导时）。以往的理论工作通常孤立地考虑这两种可能性。为了评估这些渠道的相对重要性，我们将所有可能的偏度来源嵌套在一个经验模型中，在该模型中，产出具有网络成分、共同成分和异质成分。在这个模型中，偏度不仅可能来自这三个成分，还可能来自它们的高阶协方差导致的共偏度。对美国43个部门产出增长的分析表明，共偏度，尤其是网络成分与共同成分之间的共偏度，是数据中不对称性的一个关键来源，并构成了文献中此前未曾探索过的连通性渠道。为了帮助解释我们的结果，我们构建并估计了一个基于微观基础的多部门一般均衡模型，并表明该模型能够产生与数据一致的偏度和共偏度。

Abstract

Skewness in macroeconomic time series may arise exogenously if shocks are asymmetrically distributed, or endogenously, as shocks propagate through production networks. These two possibilities are often considered in isolation by previous theoretical work. To evaluate the relative importance of these channels, we nest all possible sources of skewness in an empirical model where output has a network, a common, and an idiosyncratic component. In this model, skewness can arise not only from the three components, but also from coskewness due to their higher order covariation. An analysis of output growth in 43 U.S. sectors shows that coskewness, especially between the network and the common component, is a key source of asymmetry in the data and constitutes a connectivity channel not previously explored in the literature. To help interpret our results, we construct and estimate a micro-founded multi-sector general equilibrium model and show that it can generate skewness and coskewness consistent with the data.

Abstract

In this Handbook chapter, I examine how integrating ethnographic and folklore records has shaped research on culture and economics in the 21st century. Advances in text analysis techniques and the incorporation of historical and satellite data have transformed the field. I explore how George Peter Murdock's ethnographic contributions and Yuri Berezkin's seminal folklore motif index have been utilized to shed light on the roots of comparative development. I conclude by proposing a methodology for leveraging Large Language Models to extract cultural insights from folklore motifs, demonstrating how ancestral narratives can complement ethnographic records and offer valuable perspectives on societal norms and the historical forces shaping economic behavior today.

Health Impacts of Federal Pandemic Aid to State and Local Governments

联邦疫情援助对州和地方政府健康的影响

Jeffrey Clemens, Anwita Mahajan

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：H12, H72, I18

中文摘要

COVID-19疫情导致联邦向州和地方政府的转移支付达到了前所未有的水平。这笔资金是否影响了人口健康？为了回答这个问题，我们利用了这样一个事实：在国会中享有超额代表性的美国州获得的财政援助远多于代表性相对不足的州。我们发现，由超额代表性驱动的援助对人口健康产生了实质性影响。我们估计，每增加1000美元的联邦财政援助（按州居民人均计算），各州在2020年至2022年期间每10万居民中所有原因导致的死亡人数减少38人，其中三分之二来自COVID-19死亡率的降低。我们估计，最后3310亿美元的联邦疫情援助（与我们的样本内变化相对应）通过挽救的生命年产生了5910亿美元的价值。额外的援助还降低了与COVID-19相关的住院率和急诊就诊率，但并未减少检测到的阳性病例总数。这些改善结果的合理机制包括更高的COVID-19疫苗接种率（这大概可以解释我们观察到的近一半的死亡率降低）和更高的COVID-19检测率。医疗补助注册人数和医院容量似乎并未发挥重要的中介作用。我们的稳健性分析提供的证据表明，我们估计的影响不能用已有的死亡率趋势、疫情对相对密集地区与农村地区的差异化影响，或者疫情对老年人口较多或慢性病患病率较高人群的差异化影响来解释。我们估计的死亡率影响对非西班牙裔黑人美国人的影响远大于对非西班牙裔白人美国人的影响，因此联邦资金与疫情期间人口健康差距的缩小相关。

Abstract

The COVID-19 pandemic led to unprecedented levels of federal transfers to state and local governments. Did this funding impact population health? To answer this question, we leverage the fact that U.S. states that enjoy excess representation in Congress received substantially more fiscal assistance than did relatively underrepresented states. We find that the aid driven by excess representation had substantial impacts on population health. For each $1,000 increase in federal fiscal aid per state resident, we estimate that states experienced 38 fewer deaths from all causes per 100,000 residents from 2020 through 2022, of which 2/3 came from reductions in COVID-19 mortality. We estimate that the last $331 billion in federal pandemic aid, which corresponds with our in-sample variation, generated $591 billion in value through life years saved. Additional aid also reduced rates of COVID-19 related hospitalizations and emergency room visits, though not in the total number of positive cases detected. Plausible mechanisms for these improved outcomes include higher rates of COVID-19 vaccination, which plausibly account for nearly half of the mortality reductions we observe, and higher rates of COVID-19 testing. Medicaid enrollments and hospital capacity do not appear to play substantial mediating roles. Our robustness analyses provide evidence that the effects we estimate cannot be explained by pre-existing mortality trends, by the pandemic's differential impacts on relatively dense vs. rural areas, or by the pandemic's differential impacts on populations with more elderly individuals or with higher prevalence of chronic conditions. The mortality impacts we estimate were substantially greater for non-Hispanic Black Americans than for non-Hispanic White Americans, such that federal funds are associated with a reduction in population-wide health disparities over the course of the pandemic.

Fiscal Policy as a Stabilization Tool. The Case for Quasi-Automatic Stabilizers, With an Application to the VAT

作为稳定工具的财政政策。准自动稳定器的案例，及其在增值税上的应用

Olivier J. Blanchard

Programs：EFG；JEL：E62, E63, E65

中文摘要

近期稳定政策研究和实践的大部分焦点都集中在货币政策而非财政政策上。本文探讨了在货币政策存在限制的情况下，财政政策如何能够发挥更大的作用。它探讨了准自动稳定器的使用，即由某个总量变量触发的税收或转移支付的变化。它讨论了设计问题，特别是如何使此类稳定器真正实现债务中性，以及使用哪个总量变量作为触发条件。然后，它聚焦于一个特定的稳定器——可变增值税税率。它在一个极简的新凯恩斯主义模型中展示了其效果，然后讨论了该极简模型内外的一系列分析性问题，例如对通胀的影响、税基的选择、流动性约束的影响以及预期效应的影响。最后，它回顾了关于增值税变化传导以及永久性或临时性增值税变化对需求影响的实证证据。

Abstract

Most of the focus of recent stabilization policy research and practice has been on monetary rather than fiscal policy. This paper explores how, given the limits on monetary policy, fiscal policy could play a larger role. It explores the use of quasi-automatic stabilizers, i.e. changes in taxes or transfers triggered by an aggregate variable. It discusses design issues, in particular how to make such stabilizers truly debt neutral, and what aggregate variable to use as a trigger. It then focuses on a specific stabilizer, a variable VAT rate. It shows its effect in a minimalist NK model, and then discusses a number of analytical issues, both within and out of the minimalist model, such as the implications for inflation, the choice of the tax base, the implications of liquidity constraints, the implication of anticipation effects. It ends by reviewing the empirical evidence on passthrough of VAT changes, and the effects on demand of VAT changes, permanent or temporary.
