No. 1NBER w34613

The Benefits of Public Transit to Households: Evidence from India

公共交通对家庭的好处：来自印度的证据

Palak Suri, Maureen L. Cropper

Programs：EEE；JEL：O18, R1, R2, R4

Abstract

We measure benefits to households from Mumbai’s new Metro rail system. We estimate a commute mode choice model to value commute time savings in the short run and a housing choice model to value the improved commuting utility that households experience due to spatial sorting. Aggregate benefits from Metro rail are over 10 times higher when spatial sorting occurs. In the short run women, college-educated workers, and workers with above median incomes experience higher benefits than their opposites. In the long run, households with lower incomes and assets and less than college education benefit more than their wealthier counterparts.

中文摘要

我们衡量了孟买新地铁系统给家庭带来的好处。我们估计了一个通勤方式选择模型，以评估短期内的通勤时间节省价值，以及一个住房选择模型，以评估家庭因空间分选而获得的通勤效用改善。当发生空间分选时，地铁带来的总收益超过原来的10倍。短期内，女性、受过大学教育的工人以及收入高于中位数的工人比其对应群体获得了更高的收益。长期来看，收入较低、资产较少且未受过大学教育的家庭比更富裕的家庭受益更多。

No. 2NBER w34612

The Impact of Spousal Social Security Claiming Decisions on the Financial Shock of Widowhood

配偶社会保障申领决策对丧偶财务冲击的影响

Sita Slavov

Programs：AG；JEL：D14, H55, J26

Abstract

This paper presents evidence suggesting that delayed Social Security claiming by husbands – resulting in an actuarially enhanced benefit – attenuates the financial shock of widowhood for their wives. Under Social Security survivor benefit rules, primary earners (usually husbands) pass on the actuarial adjustments from delayed claiming to their surviving spouses. Using a staggered difference-in-differences approach, I find women whose husbands delayed claiming to full retirement age or later face a post-widowhood increase of 6.9 percentage points in the probability of falling below the 5th percentile of the pre-widowhood income distribution. This effect is almost 12 percent smaller for each year of delayed claiming by the husband (though the attenuation is concentrated in the first 4 years of widowhood). The general findings are robust to instrumenting for the husband’s claiming age using the loosening of the retirement earnings test in 2000 – a policy change that incentivized earlier claiming.

中文摘要

本文提供的证据表明，丈夫延迟申领社会保障（从而获得精算增强的福利）会减轻其妻子在丧偶时面临的财务冲击。根据社会保障遗属福利规则，主要收入者（通常是丈夫）将延迟申领带来的精算调整传递给其遗属。使用交错双重差分方法，我发现，如果丈夫将申领延迟至完全退休年龄或更晚，其妻子在丧偶后落入丧偶前收入分布第5百分位以下的概率会增加6.9个百分点。丈夫每延迟申领一年，这一效应会减少近12%（尽管这种减弱集中在丧偶后的前4年）。利用2000年退休收入测试放宽（一项鼓励提前申领的政策变化）作为丈夫申领年龄的工具变量，这些总体发现是稳健的。

No. 3NBER w34611

New Keynesian Economics with Household and Firm Heterogeneity

具有家庭和企业异质性的新凯恩斯经济学

Thomas Winberry, Adrien Auclert, Matthew Rognlie, Ludwig Straub

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：D1, D2, E21, E22, E31, E32, E43, E52, E62

Abstract

The Heterogeneous-Agent New Keynesian literature has revisited the transmission of monetary and fiscal policy to consumption using models where heterogeneous households face idiosyncratic income risk and borrowing constraints. We show that the key lessons from this literature also apply to investment using a model where heterogeneous firms face idiosyncratic productivity risk and financial frictions: constrained firms’ investment depends on their free cash flow, generating indirect effects of monetary policy and implying that transfer payments stimulate investment demand. Quantitatively, the strength of these new mechanisms is governed by firms’ marginal propensities to invest (MPIs), similar to the role of marginal propensities to consume (MPCs) for households. But unlike MPCs, we currently lack quasi-experimental evidence about MPIs that we can use to directly discipline the new mechanisms.

中文摘要

异质性代理人新凯恩斯文献利用异质性家庭面临特质收入风险和借贷约束的模型，重新审视了货币与财政政策向消费的传导。我们表明，该文献的关键教训同样适用于投资，这通过一个异质性企业面临特质生产率风险和金融摩擦的模型得以体现：受约束企业的投资取决于其自由现金流，从而产生货币政策的间接效应，并意味着转移支付会刺激投资需求。在数量上，这些新机制的强度由企业的边际投资倾向（MPI）决定，类似于边际消费倾向（MPC）对家庭的作用。但与MPC不同，我们目前缺乏可用于直接约束新机制的关于MPI的准实验证据。

No. 4NBER w34608

The Emerging Market for Intelligence: Pricing, Supply, and Demand for LLMs

新兴的智能市场：大语言模型的定价、供给与需求

Mert Demirer, Andrey Fradkin, Nadav Tadelis, Sida Peng

Programs：IO；JEL：L0, L10

Abstract

We document six facts about the structure and dynamics of the LLM market using API usage data from OpenRouter and Microsoft Azure. First, we show rapid growth in the number of models, creators, and inference providers, driven by open-source entrants. Second, we show price declines and persistent price heterogeneity across and within intelligence tiers, with open-source models being 90% cheaper than comparable closed-source models of the same intelligence. Third, we document market dynamism, with frequent turnover among leading models and creators. Fourth, we present evidence of horizontal and vertical differentiation, with no single model dominating across use cases, and demand for intelligence varying widely across applications. Fifth, we estimate preliminary short-run price elasticities just above one, suggesting limited scope for Jevons-Paradox effects. Finally, we show that although the share of firms that use multiple models increased over time, most firms concentrate their use on a single model, consistent with experimentation rather than persistent reliance on multiple models.

中文摘要

我们利用来自OpenRouter和Microsoft Azure的API使用数据，记录了关于大语言模型（LLM）市场结构和动态的六个事实。第一，我们展示了模型、创造者和推理提供者数量的快速增长，这由开源进入者推动。第二，我们展示了价格下降以及跨智能层级和层级内部的持续价格异质性，开源模型比同等智能的同类闭源模型便宜90%。第三，我们记录了市场动态性，领先模型和创造者频繁更替。第四，我们提供了横向和纵向差异化的证据，没有单一模型在所有用例中占据主导地位，且对智能的需求在不同应用间差异很大。第五，我们初步估计短期价格弹性略高于1，表明杰文斯悖论效应的范围有限。最后，我们表明，尽管使用多个模型的企业份额随时间增加，但大多数企业将其使用集中在一个模型上，这与实验行为一致，而非持续依赖多个模型。

No. 5NBER w34607

One Instrument, Many Treatments: Instrumental Variables Identification of Multiple Causal Effects

一个工具变量，多种处理：工具变量识别多重因果效应

Joshua Angrist, Andres Santos, Otávio Tecchio

Programs：ED, LS, PE, TWP；JEL：C14, C21, C26, I23, I26

Abstract

Many instrumental variables applications specify a single Bernoulli treatment. But instruments may change outcomes through multiple pathways or by varying treatment intensity. Lottery instruments that boost charter school enrollment, for instance, may affect outcomes by lengthening time enrolled in a charter school and by moving students between charter schools of different types. We analyze the identification problem such scenarios present in a framework that generalizes the always-taker/never-taker/complier partition of treatment response types to cover a wide range of multinomial and ordered treatments with heterogenous potential outcomes. This framework yields novel estimators in which a single randomly assigned instrument identifies (i) causal effects averaged over complier types and (ii) a causal conditional expectation function that captures effects for each element in a set of response types. Three empirical applications demonstrate the utility of these results. The first extends an earlier analysis of the Head Start Impact Study allowing for multiple fallbacks. The second examines two causal channels for the impact of post-secondary financial aid on degree completion. The third estimates effects of additional births (an ordered treatment) on mothers’ earnings.

中文摘要

许多工具变量应用指定了一个单一的伯努利处理。但工具变量可能通过多种路径或改变处理强度来影响结果。例如，提高特许学校入学率的彩票工具可能通过延长在特许学校的就读时间以及让学生在不同类型的特许学校之间转学来影响结果。我们在一个框架中分析了此类情景下的识别问题，该框架将处理反应类型的总是接受者/从不接受者/依从者划分推广到涵盖具有异质性潜在结果的广泛多项和有序处理。该框架产生了新颖的估计量，其中单一随机分配的工具变量识别了（i）依从者类型上的平均因果效应，以及（ii）一个因果条件期望函数，该函数捕捉了一组反应类型中每个元素的效应。三个实证应用展示了这些结果的实用性。第一个应用扩展了先前对“启蒙计划影响研究”的分析，允许存在多种备选方案。第二个应用考察了高等教育助学金对学位完成影响的两种因果渠道。第三个应用估计了额外生育（一种有序处理）对母亲收入的影响。

No. 6NBER w34606

The Long Run Economic Effects of Medical Innovation and the Role of Opportunities

医疗创新的长期经济效应与机会的作用

Sonia R. Bhalotra, Damian Clarke, Atheendar Venkataramani

Programs：CH, DAE, EH；JEL：I0, I10, I14, I18, I3, J71

Abstract

We leverage the introduction of the first antibiotic therapies in 1937 to examine the long run effects of early childhood pneumonia on adult educational attainment, employment, income, and work-related disability. Using census data, we document large average gains on all outcomes, alongside substantial heterogeneity by race and gender. On average, Black men exhibit smaller schooling gains than white men but larger employment and earnings gains. Among Black men (and women), we identify a pronounced gradient in gains linked to systemic racial discrimination in the pre–Civil Rights era: individuals born in more discriminatory Jim Crow states realized much smaller gains than those born in less discriminatory states. There is no similar gradient among white Americans. Women of both races exhibit smaller education and earnings gains than men on average, consistent with cultural and institutional barriers to women’s work. Our findings highlight the role of opportunities in shaping the extent to which investments in early-life health translate into longer run economic gains.

中文摘要

我们利用1937年首次抗生素疗法的引入，考察了儿童早期肺炎对成年后教育程度、就业、收入和工作相关残疾的长期影响。利用人口普查数据，我们记录了所有结果上的巨大平均收益，同时伴随着种族和性别方面的显著异质性。平均而言，黑人男性在教育方面的收益小于白人男性，但在就业和收入方面的收益更大。在黑人男性（和女性）中，我们识别出一个与民权运动前系统性种族歧视相关的显著收益梯度：出生在歧视性更强的吉姆·克劳州的个体，其收益远小于出生在歧视性较弱州的个体。在美国白人中则没有类似的梯度。两性女性在教育和收入方面的平均收益均小于男性，这与阻碍女性工作的文化和制度障碍一致。我们的发现强调了机会在塑造早期健康投资转化为长期经济收益程度方面的作用。

No. 7NBER w34605

Women's Power in the Household

家庭中的女性权力

Seema Jayachandran, Alessandra Voena

Programs：CH, DEV, LS；JEL：D13, O12

Abstract

We examine women’s household power in low- and middle-income countries, synthesizing theoretical frameworks and empirical evidence on its measurement, determinants, and consequences. We define women’s household power as their influence over household choices, distinguishing it from broader empowerment concepts. We review economic models, including unitary, collective, and bargaining frameworks, and map these to empirical approaches. We then discuss measurement methods such as structural estimation of consumption allocation, survey measures, and laboratory experiments. On the determinants of women’s power, we find that some approaches, such as transfers targeted to women, show mixed results, while others, such as increasing women’s control over their earnings, show clearer positive impacts. On the effects of women’s power, we pay special attention to children’s human capital. Few studies provide strong evidence that mothers invest more in children than fathers do, but collectively the evidence suggests such an effect. We conclude by highlighting research and methodological gaps.

中文摘要

我们考察了中低收入国家女性的家庭权力，综合了关于其衡量、决定因素和后果的理论框架与实证证据。我们将女性家庭权力定义为其对家庭选择的影响力，并将其与更广泛的赋权概念区分开来。我们回顾了包括单一模型、集体模型和讨价还价模型在内的经济模型，并将这些模型映射到实证方法上。然后，我们讨论了衡量方法，如消费分配的结构估计、调查测量和实验室实验。关于女性权力的决定因素，我们发现某些方法（如针对女性的转移支付）结果不一，而其他方法（如增加女性对其收入的控制）则显示出更明确的积极影响。关于女性权力的影响，我们特别关注儿童的人力资本。很少有研究提供强有力的证据表明母亲比父亲在子女身上投资更多，但总体证据表明存在这种效应。最后，我们指出了研究和方法的空白。

No. 8NBER w34604

Connected for Better or Worse? The Role of Production Networks in Financial Crises

连接是好是坏？生产网络在金融危机中的作用

Jorge Miranda-Pinto, Eugenio I. Rojas, Felipe Saffie, Alvaro Silva

Programs：EFG, IFM；JEL：E32, F32, G01

Abstract

We study how production networks shape the severity of Sudden Stops. We build a small open economy model with collateral constraints and input–output linkages, derive a sufficient statistic that maps network structure into the amplification of tradable shocks, and show that a planner optimally introduces sectoral wedges to reduce amplification. Using OECD input–output data and Sudden Stop episodes, we document systematic network differences between emerging and advanced economies and show they predict crisis severity. A calibrated three-sector DSGE model disciplined by these differences reveals that endowing an advanced economy with an emerging-market production network moves most of the way toward the observed emerging–advanced Sudden Stop gap.

中文摘要

我们研究了生产网络如何影响“突然停止”的严重程度。我们构建了一个具有抵押品约束和投入产出联系的小型开放经济模型，推导出一个充分统计量，将网络结构映射为可贸易冲击的放大效应，并表明一个计划者会最优地引入部门楔子以减少放大效应。利用经合组织的投入产出数据和“突然停止”事件，我们记录了新兴经济体和发达经济体之间系统的网络差异，并表明这些差异可以预测危机的严重程度。一个由这些差异约束的校准三部门动态随机一般均衡模型显示，赋予发达经济体一个新兴市场的生产网络，可以在很大程度上弥合观察到的新兴与发达经济体之间“突然停止”的差距。

No. 9NBER w34603

Open Devices and Slices: Evidence From Wi-Fi Equipment

开放设备与切片：来自Wi-Fi设备的证据

Do Yoon Kim, Roberto Fontana, Shane Greenstein

Programs：IO, PR；JEL：L23, L63, O36

Abstract

The study examines the quasi-natural experiments provided by the staggered introduction of open drivers in the supply chains for routers. It is rare to observe components become open and measure whether openness generates a statistical impact on more products and innovative products. This study collects novel data on all routers and subcomponents introduced between 2000 and 2018, characterizing each firm's position in a supply chain as either an upstream component provider or a downstream router assembler. Following prior literature, openness influences a firm's ability to negotiate with current and potential partners, which is labeled as autonomy. Evidence suggests that openness enhances supplier autonomy, increases the introduction of new products, and leads to a greater number of products located closer to the technical frontier. These estimates suggest that openness increased product introductions by enlarging the options available to component suppliers. The largest component suppliers benefited from greater sales, and no evidence indicates that openness aided entrants, small firms, or assemblers.

中文摘要

本研究考察了路由器供应链中分阶段引入开放驱动程序所提供的准自然实验。观察组件变得开放并衡量开放性是否对更多产品和创新产品产生统计影响是罕见的。本研究收集了2000年至2018年间推出的所有路由器及子组件的新数据，将每家公司在供应链中的位置特征化为上游组件供应商或下游路由器组装商。遵循先前文献，开放性影响公司与现有及潜在合作伙伴的谈判能力，这被称为自主性。证据表明，开放性增强了供应商的自主性，增加了新产品的推出，并导致更多产品位于技术前沿附近。这些估计表明，开放性通过扩大组件供应商可用的选择范围来增加产品推出。最大的组件供应商从更高的销售额中受益，且没有证据表明开放性帮助了新进入者、小公司或组装商。

No. 10NBER w34602

Human Capital and Development

人力资本与发展

Philippe Aghion, Ingvild Almås, Costas Meghir

Programs：CH, DEV, ED, EFG, LS, PE；JEL：Z0

Abstract

Human capital is central to efforts to promote growth, convergence, and the elimination of poverty. Drawing on the seminal macroeconomic frameworks by Nelson-Phelps, Lucas and subsequent developments, alongside macro and microeconomic evidence, we examine the role of human capital in driving innovation and growth. We highlight how different types of human capital, characterized by education level, matter in different stages of development. Despite documented increases in years of schooling, the world’s poorest regions still see stagnating outcomes in learning and education quality, potentially creating poverty traps where investments in neither physical nor human capital materialize. We discuss obstacles to human capital accumulation through a simple analytical framework and present evidence from randomized interventions spanning early childhood programs to school-age initiatives, assessing policies that can effectively remove barriers to skill acquisition and establish foundations for sustained growth.

中文摘要

人力资本是促进增长、趋同和消除贫困努力的核心。借鉴Nelson-Phelps、Lucas的开创性宏观框架及后续发展，结合宏观和微观经济证据，我们考察了人力资本在推动创新和增长中的作用。我们强调了以教育水平为特征的不同类型人力资本如何在不同的发展阶段发挥作用。尽管有记录的受教育年限增加，但世界上最贫困地区的学习成果和教育质量仍然停滞不前，可能造成贫困陷阱，使得对物质资本和人力资本的投资都无法实现。我们通过一个简单的分析框架讨论了人力资本积累的障碍，并展示了从早期儿童项目到学龄期倡议的随机干预证据，评估了能够有效消除技能获取障碍并为持续增长奠定基础的政策。

No. 11NBER w34601

The Economics of Climate Innovation: Technology, Climate Policy, and the Clean Energy Transition

气候创新的经济学：技术、气候政策与清洁能源转型

Eugenie Dugoua, Jacob Moscona

Programs：EEE, PR；JEL：O3, O30, O31, O33, O38, Q5, Q55, Q58

Abstract

This chapter examines the economics of climate innovation and its role in the clean technology transition. It outlines the incentives, market failures, and policy levers that shape the development and diffusion of clean technologies; traces global patterns in technology development and deployment; and highlights frontier challenges and open questions related to climate adaptation, critical mineral supply chains, artificial intelligence, and geopolitics. The analysis explores the role of effective climate policy, stressing the relevance of coordinated approaches that match instruments to technology maturity and local context.

中文摘要

本章考察了气候创新的经济学及其在清洁技术转型中的作用。它概述了塑造清洁技术开发和扩散的激励、市场失灵和政策杠杆；追溯了技术开发和部署的全球模式；并强调了与气候适应、关键矿产供应链、人工智能和地缘政治相关的前沿挑战和未解决问题。分析探讨了有效气候政策的作用，强调了协调方法的相关性，这些方法将工具与技术成熟度和当地背景相匹配。
