No. 1NBER w30958

Loose Monetary Policy and Financial Instability

宽松货币政策与金融不稳定

Maximilian Grimm, Òscar Jordà, Moritz Schularick, Alan M. Taylor

Programs：AP, DAE, IFM, ME；JEL：E43, E44, E52, E58, G01, G21, N10

Abstract

Do periods of persistently loose monetary policy increase financial fragility and the likelihood of a financial crisis? This is a central question for policymakers, yet the literature does not provide systematic empirical evidence about this link at the aggregate level. In this paper we fill this gap by analyzing long-run historical data. We find that when the stance of monetary policy is accommodative over an extended period, the likelihood of financial turmoil down the road increases considerably. We investigate the causal pathways that lead to this result and argue that credit creation and asset price overheating are important intermediating channels.

中文摘要

持续宽松的货币政策时期是否会增加金融脆弱性和金融危机的可能性？这是政策制定者面临的一个核心问题，然而文献并未在总体层面提供关于这种联系的系统性经验证据。在本文中，我们通过分析长期历史数据来填补这一空白。我们发现，当货币政策立场在较长时期内保持宽松时，未来发生金融动荡的可能性会显著增加。我们研究了导致这一结果的因果路径，并认为信贷创造和资产价格过热是重要的中介渠道。

No. 2NBER w30957

Language Models and Cognitive Automation for Economic Research

语言模型与经济学研究的认知自动化

Anton Korinek

Programs：EFG, LS, PR；JEL：A10, B41, J23, O3

Abstract

Large language models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT have the potential to revolutionize research in economics and other disciplines. I describe 25 use cases along six domains in which LLMs are starting to become useful as both research assistants and tutors: ideation, writing, background research, data analysis, coding, and mathematical derivations. I provide general instructions and demonstrate specific examples for how to take advantage of each of these, classifying the LLM capabilities from experimental to highly useful. I hypothesize that ongoing advances will improve the performance of LLMs across all of these domains, and that economic researchers who take advantage of LLMs to automate micro tasks will become significantly more productive. Finally, I speculate on the longer-term implications of cognitive automation via LLMs for economic research.

中文摘要

大型语言模型（如ChatGPT）有潜力彻底改变经济学及其他学科的研究。我描述了六个领域的25个用例，在这些领域中，大型语言模型作为研究助手和导师开始变得有用：构思、写作、背景研究、数据分析、编码和数学推导。我提供了通用指令，并展示了如何利用每个用例的具体示例，将大型语言模型的能力从实验性分类到高度有用。我假设持续的进步将提高大型语言模型在所有领域的表现，并且利用大型语言模型自动化微观任务的经济学研究者将变得显著更高效。最后，我推测了通过大型语言模型实现认知自动化对经济学研究的长期影响。

No. 3NBER w30956

The Economic Opportunity Cost of Green Recovery Plans

绿色复苏计划的经济机会成本

Timothy Fitzgerald, Casey B. Mulligan

Programs：EEE；JEL：Q40, Q48, Q54, Q58

Abstract

Advocates in several countries have promoted a “green recovery” from the pandemic, with an emphasis on measures to address climate objectives. We evaluate proposals for the United States and find that as stated, ambitious plans to further cut emissions from transportation and electricity will require more inputs to produce the same outputs, resulting in recurring costs of up to $483 billion per year. We forecast that real GDP and consumption will be 2-3 percent less in the long run if policies are implemented as stated, underscoring the opportunity costs of achieving green objectives when resources might be more efficiently deployed.

中文摘要

多个国家的倡导者推动从疫情中进行“绿色复苏”，重点在于应对气候目标的措施。我们评估了美国的提案，发现按照所述，进一步削减交通和电力排放的雄心勃勃计划将需要更多投入来生产相同产出，导致每年高达4830亿美元的经常性成本。我们预测，如果政策按所述实施，长期内实际GDP和消费将减少2-3%，这突显了当资源可能被更有效配置时实现绿色目标的机会成本。

No. 4NBER w30955

Estimating Causal Effects of Fertility on Life Course Outcomes: Evidence Using A Dyadic Genetic Instrumental Variable Approach

估计生育对生命历程结果的因果效应：基于二元遗传工具变量方法的证据

Boyan Zheng, Qiongshi Lu, Jason Fletcher

Programs：AG, EH, LS；JEL：J10, J13, J22, J29

Abstract

The causal effects of fertility are a central focus in the social sciences, but the analysis is challenged by the endogeneity of fertility choices. Earlier work has proposed several “natural experiments” from twin births or gender composition of earlier births to assess whether having more children affects adults’ outcomes, though there are limitations to using rare (twins) and weak (gender composition) instrumental variables for fertility. This paper proposes a new “natural experiment” approach to assessing the causal effects of fertility by measuring the combination of couples’ genetics in predicting fertility—a dyadic genetic instrumental variable, where the key idea (exclusion restriction) is that the interactions of the couple’s genetics that shift the likelihood of fertility is unknown to the couples. We use a nationally representative sample of couples to examine the long-lasting effects of fertility on older adults’ life outcomes, including labor market outcomes, personality traits, and subjective wellbeing. We find that fertility reduces females’ extraversion and years of working and some evidence indicates that fertility reduces both males’ and females’ lifetime number of jobs worked.

中文摘要

生育的因果效应是社会科学的焦点，但分析因生育选择的内生性而面临挑战。早期研究提出了几种来自双胞胎出生或先前出生性别构成的“自然实验”，以评估生育更多孩子是否影响成人结果，尽管使用罕见（双胞胎）和弱（性别构成）的工具变量来研究生育存在局限性。本文提出了一种新的“自然实验”方法，通过测量夫妻基因组合来预测生育——一种二元遗传工具变量，其关键思想（排除限制）是，夫妻基因相互作用改变生育可能性的情况对夫妻而言是未知的。我们使用一个全国代表性的夫妻样本来考察生育对老年人生活结果的长期影响，包括劳动力市场结果、人格特质和主观幸福感。我们发现，生育降低了女性的外向性和工作年限，一些证据表明生育减少了男性和女性一生中从事的工作数量。

No. 5NBER w30954

Production Network Formation, Trade, and Welfare

生产网络形成、贸易与福利

Costas Arkolakis, Federico Huneeus, Yuhei Miyauchi

Programs：ITI；JEL：F10, R13

Abstract

We study the aggregate implications of endogenous production network formation in a multi-location general equilibrium trade model. Firms form supplier and buyer linkages under matching frictions, generating a gravity structure of production networks. We analytically characterize the welfare gains from trade cost reductions relative to a benchmark model with fixed production networks. We calibrate the model to Chilean firm-to-firm data and show that it matches observed domestic and international network responses to tariff reductions. We find that endogenous networks substantially increase the welfare gains from trade, due to inefficiently low equilibrium networks and amplification effects through relationship-formation costs.

中文摘要

我们研究了多区位一般均衡贸易模型中内生生产网络形成的总体影响。企业在匹配摩擦下形成供应商和买家联系，产生生产网络的引力结构。我们相对于一个固定生产网络的基准模型，分析性地刻画了贸易成本降低带来的福利收益。我们将模型校准到智利企业间数据，并表明它匹配了观察到的国内和国际网络对关税削减的反应。我们发现，由于均衡网络效率低下以及通过关系形成成本的放大效应，内生网络显著增加了贸易带来的福利收益。

No. 6NBER w30953

Measuring Domestic Factor Content in Bilateral and Sectoral-Level Trade Flows

衡量双边和部门层面贸易流中的国内要素含量

Zhi Wang, Shang-Jin Wei, Kunfu Zhu

Programs：ITI；JEL：F10

Abstract

Measuring country origins of factor content in bilateral or sector-level exports is important to understand evolution of regional and global value chains and the roles of individual country-sectors in these chains. This paper proposes a method to distinguish between measures based on backward and forward linkages and between net and gross factor content. It is consistent with the System of National Account Standard and has the adding-up property. In comparison, these properties do not hold for the “hypothetical extraction method” proposed by Los et al. (2016). We show a number of examples involving disaggregated trade in which the two methods diverge.

中文摘要

衡量双边或部门层面出口中要素含量的国家来源，对于理解区域和全球价值链的演变以及单个国家-部门在这些链条中的作用至关重要。本文提出了一种方法，以区分基于后向联系和前向联系的衡量指标，以及净要素含量和总要素含量。该方法与国民账户体系标准一致，并具有可加性。相比之下，这些性质对于Los等人（2016）提出的“假设提取法”并不成立。我们展示了涉及细分贸易的几个例子，其中两种方法存在差异。

No. 7NBER w30952

Dynamic Pricing Regulation and Welfare in Insurance Markets

保险市场中的动态定价监管与福利

Naoki Aizawa, Ami Ko

Programs：AG, EH, IO, PE；JEL：D14, G22, I13, L11, L51

Abstract

While the traditional role of insurers is to provide protection against individuals’ idiosyncratic risks, insurers themselves face substantial uncertainties due to aggregate shocks. To prevent insurers from passing these aggregate risks onto consumers, governments have increasingly adopted dynamic pricing regulations, which limit insurers’ ability to change premiums over time. We evaluate dynamic pricing regulation using an equilibrium model of the U.S. long-term care insurance market, featuring insurers’ lack of commitment and endogenous market structures. We find that stricter dynamic pricing regulation has a limited impact on improving consumer welfare, while it reduces insurer profits and increases market concentration.

中文摘要

虽然保险公司的传统角色是为个人特有的风险提供保障，但保险公司自身也面临着由总体冲击带来的巨大不确定性。为了防止保险公司将这些总体风险转嫁给消费者，政府越来越多地采用动态定价监管，限制保险公司随时间调整保费的能力。我们利用一个美国长期护理保险市场的均衡模型来评估动态定价监管，该模型考虑了保险公司缺乏承诺以及内生市场结构的特点。我们发现，更严格的动态定价监管对改善消费者福利的影响有限，同时会降低保险公司利润并增加市场集中度。

No. 8NBER w30951

Direct and Spillover Effects of Provider Vaccination Facilitation

医疗服务提供者促进疫苗接种的直接效应与溢出效应

Julie Berry Cullen, Maria K. Humlum, Agne Suziedelyte, Peter Rønø Thingholm

Programs：CH, EH, PE；JEL：I1, I12, I18

Abstract

We explore physicians’ role in moderating compliance with recommended vaccinations. Using administrative data on the universe of Danish children and their healthcare providers, we first construct and validate a measure of providers’ propensities to facilitate recommended vaccinations from birth to age 6 based on a two-way fixed effects model. We then show that the measure meaningfully affects uptake of the human papillomavirus (HPV) vaccine among adolescent patients and speeds recovery from a media-induced crisis to perceived HPV vaccine safety. Providers affect decisions beyond those of their own patients, influencing the uptake of patients’ younger cousins by one-fifth as much as own patients’ uptake.

中文摘要

我们探讨了医生在调节推荐疫苗接种依从性方面的作用。利用涵盖所有丹麦儿童及其医疗保健提供者的行政数据，我们首先基于一个双向固定效应模型，构建并验证了一个衡量医疗服务提供者从出生到6岁期间促进推荐疫苗接种倾向的指标。然后，我们表明该指标显著影响了青少年患者对人乳头瘤病毒（HPV）疫苗的接种率，并加速了因媒体引发的对HPV疫苗安全性信任危机后的恢复。医疗服务提供者的影响超出了其自身患者的范围，他们对患者表亲接种率的影响程度相当于对自身患者接种率影响程度的五分之一。

No. 9NBER w30950

The Effect of R&D on Quality, Productivity, and Welfare

研发对质量、生产率和福利的影响

Mons Chan, Amil Petrin, Frederic Warzynski

Programs：IO；JEL：L1

Abstract

In this paper we provide a methodology that jointly studies production and demand for multi-product firms using detailed firm-product level data from Denmark. We estimate marginal cost by combining production function estimation with a cost function that allows for quasi-fixed inputs. We use a discrete choice demand model that extends insights from Berry, Levinsohn and Pakes (1995) to obtain a measure of the demand shock (quality). We estimate the relationship between product (process) R&D and quality (efficiency), and find strong evidence that process innovation is related to higher efficiency, while product innovation is associated with higher product quality. We discuss the welfare implications of these two distinct innovation activities.

中文摘要

在本文中，我们提供了一种方法论，利用来自丹麦的详细企业-产品层面数据，联合研究多产品企业的生产与需求。我们通过将生产函数估计与允许准固定投入的成本函数相结合来估计边际成本。我们使用一个离散选择需求模型，该模型扩展了Berry、Levinsohn和Pakes（1995）的见解，以获得需求冲击（质量）的度量。我们估计了产品（过程）研发与质量（效率）之间的关系，并发现强有力的证据表明过程创新与更高的效率相关，而产品创新则与更高的产品质量相关。我们讨论了这两种不同创新活动的福利含义。

No. 10NBER w30949

Inclusion and Democratization Through Web3 and DeFi? Initial Evidence from the Ethereum Ecosystem

通过Web3和DeFi实现包容性与民主化？来自以太坊生态系统的初步证据

Lin William Cong, Ke Tang, Yanxin Wang, Xi Zhao

Programs：AP, CF, ME, PR；JEL：D63, E50, G29, H23, L14

Abstract

Web3 and DeFi are widely advocated as innovations for greater financial inclusion and democratization. We assemble the most comprehensive dataset to date on the largest Web3 ecosystem and use large-scale computing to conduct an initial investigation. We describe Ethereum’s network structure, time trends, and distributions of transactions, mining, and ownership. Mining income and Ether ownership are concentrated in exchanges and a few individual nodes. Network activities evolve from peer-to-peer to user-DApps/DeFi interactions, with significantly more transactions by large players. Moreover, high percentage transaction fees, congestion-induced fluctuation of gas prices, suboptimal reserve setting, and large return volatility of tokens present particular challenges for small, poor, unsophisticated, and new nodes, not to mention that the high failure rates hurt all users. Finally, we present suggestive causal evidence that base-fee burning mechanisms (e.g., EIP-1559) and airdrop programs (e.g., OmiseGo Airdrop) facilitate inclusion through token monetary redistribution.

中文摘要

Web3和DeFi被广泛倡导为促进金融包容性和民主化的创新。我们汇集了迄今为止关于最大Web3生态系统的最全面数据集，并使用大规模计算进行了初步调查。我们描述了以太坊的网络结构、时间趋势以及交易、挖矿和所有权的分布。挖矿收入和以太币所有权集中在交易所和少数个体节点。网络活动从点对点演变为用户与DApps/DeFi的交互，大型参与者的交易显著增多。此外，高比例的交易费用、拥堵导致的Gas价格波动、次优的储备设置以及代币收益的大幅波动，对小型、贫困、缺乏经验和新的节点构成了特殊挑战，更不用说高失败率对所有用户造成的损害。最后，我们提出了提示性因果证据，表明基础费用燃烧机制（例如EIP-1559）和空投计划（例如OmiseGo空投）通过代币货币再分配促进了包容性。

No. 11NBER w30948

Donor Contracting Conditions and Public Procurement: Causal Evidence from Kenyan Electrification

捐助者合同条件与公共采购：来自肯尼亚电气化的因果证据

Catherine Wolfram, Edward Miguel, Eric Hsu, Susanna B. Berkouwer

Programs：DEV, EEE, IO, PE；JEL：D73, F35, H5, L94, O19

Abstract

There is limited causal evidence on the effects of different public procurement regulations on project quality and value-for-money for projects funded by national governments and foreign aid donors. This paper uses policy and experimental variation to study how two key contracting features—namely, contract bundling and monitoring—affect outcomes of a large economic development project. We leverage an unusual feature of Kenya’s nationwide electrification program: the quasi-random allocation of multilateral funding sources across nearby villages. African Development Bank (AfDB) projects used bundled contracts while the World Bank (WB) employed unbundled contracts together with strengthened inspections. To measure impacts, we collect on-the-ground engineering assessments, power quality data, household surveys, and analyze original contracts. The analysis suggests a stark trade-off: WB procedures delayed construction completion by 16 months relative to AfDB sites but improved construction quality by a sizeable 0.6 standard deviations. To disentangle the effects of contract bundling versus monitoring, we conducted randomized audits that enhanced monitoring. The audits improve household connectivity, network size, and voltage at AfDB sites, but have no impact at WB sites, suggesting monitoring and unbundling contracts may be substitutes. Given the apparent trade-off, we investigate how net benefits depend on policymaker time preferences and infrastructure longevity.

中文摘要

关于不同的公共采购法规对由国家政府和外国援助捐助者资助的项目质量和性价比的影响，目前因果证据有限。本文利用政策变化和实验变异，研究两个关键的合同特征——即合同捆绑和监控——如何影响一个大型经济发展项目的结果。我们利用了肯尼亚全国电气化计划的一个不寻常特点：多边资金来源在邻近村庄之间的准随机分配。非洲开发银行（AfDB）的项目使用捆绑合同，而世界银行（WB）则采用非捆绑合同并加强检查。为了衡量影响，我们收集了实地工程评估、电能质量数据、家庭调查，并分析了原始合同。分析表明存在明显的权衡：与AfDB项目点相比，世界银行的程序使建设完工延迟了16个月，但将建设质量提高了0.6个标准差，幅度相当大。为了区分合同捆绑与监控的影响，我们进行了随机审计以加强监控。审计提高了AfDB项目点的家庭连接率、网络规模和电压，但对世界银行项目点没有影响，这表明监控与非捆绑合同可能是替代品。鉴于这种明显的权衡，我们研究了净收益如何取决于政策制定者的时间偏好和基础设施的寿命。

No. 12NBER w30947中国相关

Ideas Mobilize People: The Diffusion of Communist Ideology in China

思想动员人民：共产主义意识形态在中国的传播

Ying Bai, Ruixue Jia, Runnan Wang

Programs：POL；JEL：D70, D71, D83, N45, O12, P30

Abstract

Can ideas mobilize people into collective action? We provide a positive answer to this question by studying how exposure to the Communist ideology shaped an individual’s choice to join the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) during the party’s formative stage. The individuals we focus on are cadets at the Whampoa Military Academy, who subsequently fought in 20th-century China’s most important wars. Our identification strategy exploits the locality-time-content variation in the circulation of the New Youth magazine—the major platform to promote Communism after the Treaty of Versailles in 1919—as well as the variation in an individual’s location over time. By comparing the Whampoa cadets living in a locality with post-1919 New Youth available against those who had lived in the same locality but missed this channel, we demonstrate that the former were significantly more likely to join the CCP. In future political struggles, those whose party choice was more influenced by this ideology channel were less likely to quit the CCP and more likely to sacrifice their lives. Additionally, we document that family background cannot predict the party choice of these political pioneers but social networks can complement ideology exposure.
