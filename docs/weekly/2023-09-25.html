Adjusting for Scale-Use Heterogeneity in Self-Reported Well-Being

自我报告幸福感中量表使用异质性的调整

Daniel J. Benjamin, Kristen Cooper, Ori Heffetz, Miles S. Kimball, Jiannan Zhou

Programs：AG, EH, LS, PE；JEL：C83, D60, D63, D90, D91, I14, I31

Abstract

Analyses of self-reported-well-being (SWB) survey data may be confounded if people use response scales differently. We use calibration questions, designed to have the same objective answer across respondents, to measure dimensional (i.e., specific to an SWB dimension) and general (i.e., common across questions) scale-use heterogeneity. In a sample of ~3,350 MTurkers, we find substantial such heterogeneity that is correlated with demographics. We develop a theoretical framework and econometric approaches to quantify and adjust for this heterogeneity. We apply our new estimators in several standard SWB applications. Adjusting for general-scale-use heterogeneity changes results in some cases.

中文摘要

如果人们以不同方式使用回答量表，对自我报告幸福感（SWB）调查数据的分析可能会受到混淆。我们使用校准问题（这些问题旨在让所有受访者得到相同的客观答案）来衡量维度特定（即特定于SWB维度）和一般（即跨问题通用）的量表使用异质性。在一个约3350名MTurk用户的样本中，我们发现存在显著的此类异质性，并且与人口统计学特征相关。我们开发了一个理论框架和计量经济学方法来量化并调整这种异质性。我们在几个标准的SWB应用中应用了我们的新估计量。在某些情况下，调整一般量表使用异质性会改变结果。

No. 2NBER w31727

From Happiness Data to Economic Conclusions

从幸福感数据到经济结论

Daniel J. Benjamin, Kristen Cooper, Ori Heffetz, Miles S. Kimball

Programs：AG, EH, LS, PE；JEL：D60, D63, D9, I31

Abstract

Happiness data—survey respondents’ self-reported well-being (SWB)—have become increasingly common in economics research, with recent calls to use them in policymaking. Researchers have used SWB data in novel ways, for example to learn about welfare or preferences when choice data are unavailable or difficult to interpret. Focusing on leading examples of this pioneering research, the first part of this review uses a simple theoretical framework to reverse-engineer some of the crucial assumptions that underlie existing applications. The second part discusses evidence bearing on these assumptions and provides practical advice to the agencies and institutions that generate SWB data, the researchers who use them, and the policymakers who may use the resulting research. While we advocate creative uses of SWB data in economics, we caution that their use in policy will likely require both additional data collection and further research to better understand the data.

中文摘要

幸福感数据——调查受访者自我报告的幸福感（SWB）——在经济学研究中越来越常见，最近有人呼吁将其用于政策制定。研究人员以新颖的方式使用SWB数据，例如在选择数据不可得或难以解释时，用以了解福利或偏好。本综述的第一部分聚焦于这一开创性研究的典型案例，使用一个简单的理论框架来逆向推导出支撑现有应用的一些关键假设。第二部分讨论了支持这些假设的证据，并为生成SWB数据的机构和组织、使用这些数据的研究人员以及可能使用由此产生的研究成果的政策制定者提供实用建议。虽然我们倡导在经济学中创造性地使用SWB数据，但我们提醒，将其用于政策可能既需要额外的数据收集，也需要进一步的研究以更好地理解这些数据。

No. 3NBER w31726

Time Preferences and Food Choice

时间偏好与食物选择

Andy Brownback, Alex Imas, Michael A. Kuhn

Programs：CH, DEV, PE；JEL：D03, H20, I12, I39

Abstract

Healthy food choices are a canonical example used to illustrate the importance of time preferences in behavioral economics. However, the literature lacks a direct demonstration that they are well-predicted by incentivized time preference measures. We offer direct evidence by combining a novel, two-question, incentivized time preference measurement with data from a field experiment that includes grocery purchases and consumption. Our present-focus measure is highly predictive of food choice, capturing a number of behaviors consistent with self-control problems, which provides direct evidence for the common assumption that important aspects of nutrition are driven by time preferences.

中文摘要

健康的食物选择是行为经济学中用于说明时间偏好重要性的典型例子。然而，现有文献缺乏直接证明它们能被激励性的时间偏好测量所良好预测的证据。我们通过将一种新颖的、包含两个问题的激励性时间偏好测量与来自一项包含杂货购买和消费的实地实验的数据相结合，提供了直接证据。我们的当前关注度测量对食物选择具有高度预测性，捕捉到了一系列与自我控制问题一致的行为，这为营养的重要方面由时间偏好驱动的常见假设提供了直接证据。

No. 4NBER w31725

The Minimum Wage and Inequality Between Groups

最低工资与群体间不平等

Francine D. Blau, Isaac Cohen, Matthew L. Comey, Lawrence Kahn, Nikolai Boboshko

Programs：LS；JEL：J15, J16, J31, J38

Abstract

Using 1979-2019 Current Population Survey data, we study the effect of state and federal minimum wage policies on gender, race, and ethnic inequality. We find that minimum wages substantially reduce intergroup wage inequality at least up to the 20th wage percentile, with no evidence of adverse employment effects. We conduct counterfactual simulations of between-group inequality due to minimum wage changes since 1979. Declines in the real minimum wage in the 1980s slowed progress in narrowing between-group inequality. Relatively small changes in minimum wages during 1989-1998 and 1998-2007 meant little role for the minimum wage over those time spans. Since 2007, several states have steeply raised their minimum wages, especially raising Hispanics’ relative wages, because they earn low wages and reside disproportionately in those states. Finally, we find that raising the federal minimum wage to $12/hour in 2020 dollars ($14.49 in 2025Q2 dollars) would reduce existing between-group wage gaps below the 15th percentile by 25-50%.

中文摘要

利用1979-2019年当前人口调查数据，我们研究了州和联邦最低工资政策对性别、种族和民族不平等的影响。我们发现，最低工资至少在第20个工资百分位数以下显著减少了群体间工资不平等，且没有发现不利的就业效应的证据。我们对1979年以来因最低工资变化导致的群体间不平等进行了反事实模拟。1980年代实际最低工资的下降减缓了缩小群体间不平等的进展。1989-1998年和1998-2007年期间最低工资的相对较小变化意味着最低工资在这段时间内作用不大。自2007年以来，几个州大幅提高了最低工资，尤其提高了西班牙裔的相对工资，因为他们工资较低且不成比例地居住在这些州。最后，我们发现，将联邦最低工资提高到2020年美元计算的12美元/小时（2025年第二季度美元计算的14.49美元/小时），将使第15个百分位数以下现有的群体间工资差距缩小25-50%。

No. 5NBER w31724

Centralization and Organization Reproduction: Ethnic Innovation in R&D Centers and Satellite Locations

集权化与组织复制：研发中心与卫星地点的族裔创新

William R. Kerr

Programs：PR；JEL：F22, F23, J61, L22, L25, M51, O31, O32, R11, R12

Abstract

We study the relationship between firm centralization and organizational reproduction in satellite locations. For decentralized firms, the ethnic compositions of inventors in satellite locations mostly resemble their host cities, with little link to the inventor composition of their parent firms' R&D headquarters. For highly centralized firms, by contrast, organizational reproduction has an explanatory power equal to half or more of the host city effect. Reproduction is strongest when a firm exhibits a hands-on approach to the satellite facility, such as cross-facility team collaboration or internal talent mobility.

中文摘要

我们研究了企业集权化与卫星地点组织复制之间的关系。对于分权企业而言，卫星地点的发明人族裔构成主要与其所在城市相似，与母公司研发总部的发明人构成关联甚微。相比之下，对于高度集权的企业，组织复制对发明人族裔构成的解释力达到或超过所在城市效应的一半。当企业对卫星设施采取亲力亲为的方式（例如跨设施团队协作或内部人才流动）时，组织复制效应最为显著。

No. 6NBER w31723

Economic Consequences of a Regime Change: Overview

政权更迭的经济后果：概述

Assaf Razin, Efraim Sadka

Programs：IFM；JEL：F0, P1

Abstract

Regime changes toward autocracy typically reshape the judicial framework, effectively eroding the separation of powers and leading the nation toward an autocratic path. Recent instances of regime shift in Hungary, Poland, and Turkey offer templates of democratic erosion through manipulation and “democratic” means. Israel is in now amid a conflicting judicial overhaul. Whereas the manifestations of these patterns differ based on the distinct political, social, and historical contexts of each country, they invariably erode the Rule of Law and the democratic institutions. Their ideological stance sharply contrasts with the democratic norms upheld by major Western nations: the separation of powers, judicial autonomy, and the intricate web of political, civic, and social organizations cultivated since the Enlightenment. These century-old trends have been instrumental in fostering unprecedented prosperity. This paper overviews the economic consequences of regime changes from liberal democracy towards autocracy.

中文摘要

向威权主义的政权更迭通常会重塑司法框架，有效削弱权力分立，并引导国家走向威权道路。近期匈牙利、波兰和土耳其的政权转变案例，提供了通过操纵和“民主”手段侵蚀民主的模板。以色列目前正陷入一场相互冲突的司法改革之中。尽管这些模式的表现形式因各国独特的政治、社会和历史背景而异，但它们无一例外地侵蚀了法治和民主制度。其意识形态立场与西方主要国家所秉持的民主规范——即权力分立、司法自主，以及自启蒙运动以来培育的政治、公民和社会组织的复杂网络——形成鲜明对比。这些延续数百年的趋势曾极大地促进了前所未有的繁荣。本文概述了从自由民主向威权主义政权更迭的经济后果。

Bonus Question: How Does Flexible Incentive Pay Affect Wage Rigidity?

奖金问题：弹性激励薪酬如何影响工资刚性？

Meghana Gaur, John R. Grigsby, Jonathon Hazell, Abdoulaye Ndiaye

Programs：EFG, LS, ME；JEL：E24, E32, J33, J41, J64

Abstract

We introduce dynamic incentive contracts into a model of inflation and unemployment dynamics. Our main result is that wage cyclicality from incentives neither affects the slope of the Phillips curve for prices nor dampens unemployment’s response to shocks. The impulse response of unemployment in economies with flexible, procyclical incentive pay is first-order equivalent to that of economies with rigid wages. Likewise, the slope of the Phillips curve is the same in both economies. This equivalence is due to effort fluctuations, which make marginal costs rigid even if wages are flexible. Our calibrated model suggests that 46% of the wage cyclicality in the data arises from incentives, with the remainder attributable to bargaining and outside options. A standard model without incentives calibrated to weakly procyclical wages matches the impulse response of unemployment in our incentive pay model calibrated to strongly procyclical wages.

中文摘要

我们将动态激励合同引入一个通胀与失业动态模型。我们的主要结论是：由激励产生的工资周期性既不影响价格菲利普斯曲线的斜率，也不削弱失业对冲击的反应。在具有弹性、顺周期激励薪酬的经济体中，失业的脉冲响应与工资刚性经济体在首阶上等价。同样，两种经济体的菲利普斯曲线斜率也相同。这种等价性源于努力水平的波动，即使工资具有弹性，边际成本仍保持刚性。我们的校准模型表明，数据中46%的工资周期性源于激励，其余部分归因于议价和外部选择。一个不含激励、校准为弱顺周期工资的标准模型，其失业脉冲响应与我们的激励薪酬模型（校准为强顺周期工资）相匹配。

No. 8NBER w31721

Credit Supply Shocks and Firm Dynamics: Evidence from Brazil

信贷供给冲击与企业动态：来自巴西的证据

Samuel Bazzi, Marc-Andreas Muendler, Raquel F. Oliveira, James E. Rauch

Programs：CF, DEV, EFG, IO, LS, ME, PE, PR；JEL：D21, D22, D92, L25, L26, M13, O12

Abstract

We explore how financial constraints distort the entry decisions among otherwise productive entrepreneurs and limit growth of promising young firms. A model of liquidity-constrained entrepreneurs suggests that the easing of credit constraints can induce more entry of firms with greater long-run growth potential than the easing of conventional entry barriers would bring about. We explore this growth mechanism using a large-scale program to expand the supply of credit to small and medium enterprises in Brazil. Local credit supply shocks generate greater firm entry but also greater exit with no effect on short-run employment growth in the formal sector. However, credit expansions increase average capability among entering firms, which enter at larger size, survive longer, and grow faster. These firm dynamics are more pronounced in areas with weaker credit markets ex ante and consistent with local bank branches using cheap targeted credit lines to expand lending more broadly. Our findings provide new evidence on the general equilibrium effects of credit supply expansions.

中文摘要

我们探讨了金融约束如何扭曲原本富有生产力的企业家的进入决策，并限制有前途的年轻企业的增长。一个流动性受限企业家的模型表明，放松信贷约束可以比放松传统进入壁垒更能诱导具有更大长期增长潜力的企业进入。我们利用巴西一项旨在扩大中小企业信贷供给的大规模项目来探究这一增长机制。地方信贷供给冲击导致企业进入增加，但退出也增加，且对正规部门的短期就业增长没有影响。然而，信贷扩张提高了进入企业的平均能力，这些企业以更大规模进入、存活时间更长且增长更快。这些企业动态在事前信贷市场较弱的地区更为显著，并且与当地银行分支机构利用廉价定向信贷额度更广泛地扩大贷款的做法一致。我们的发现为信贷供给扩张的总体均衡效应提供了新证据。

No. 9NBER w31720

Cash Transfers and Social Preferences of Children

现金转移与儿童的社会偏好

Johannes Haushofer, Magdalena Larreboure, Sara Lowes, Leon Mait

Programs：CH, DEV, POL；JEL：C92, C93, D31, I38, O12

Abstract

We study the effects of an unconditional cash transfer program on social preferences of children. The program allocated $1,076 to randomly selected households in rural Kenya. We measure the social preferences of 4,022 children from 1,687 households with survey questions and incentivized behavioral games three years after the intervention. We distinguish between the direct effects on children of recipient households and the spillover effects on children of neighboring households. We do not find consistent evidence that children from treatment and spillover groups are more or less prosocial than children from the control group. Additionally, we find no persistent economic effects of the program. We find some evidence of reduced psychological well-being among adults and children in spillover households.

中文摘要

我们研究了一项无条件现金转移项目对儿童社会偏好的影响。该项目向肯尼亚农村随机选择的家庭发放了1076美元。在干预三年后，我们通过调查问卷和激励性行为游戏，测量了来自1687个家庭的4022名儿童的社会偏好。我们区分了对受助家庭儿童的直接影响和对邻近家庭儿童的溢出效应。我们没有发现一致证据表明来自处理组和溢出组的儿童比来自对照组的儿童更亲社会或更不亲社会。此外，我们未发现该项目具有持续的经济效应。我们发现了溢出组家庭中成人和儿童心理健康状况下降的一些证据。

No. 10NBER w31719

High-Dimensional Factor Models and the Factor Zoo

高维因子模型与因子动物园

Martin Lettau

Programs：AP；JEL：C38, G0, G12

Abstract

This paper proposes a new approach to the “factor zoo” conundrum. Instead of applying dimension-reduction methods to a large set of portfolio returns obtained from sorts on characteristics, I construct factors that summarize the information in characteristics across assets and then sort assets into portfolios according to these “characteristic factors”. I estimate the model on a data set of mutual fund characteristics. Since the data set is 3-dimensional (characteristics of funds over time), characteristic factors are based on a tensor factor model (TFM) that is a generalization of 2-dimensional PCA. I find that parsimonious TFM captures over 90% of the variation in the data set. Pricing factors derived from the TFM have high Sharpe ratios and capture the cross-section of fund returns better than standard benchmark models.

中文摘要

本文提出了一种解决“因子动物园”难题的新方法。我不对基于特征排序得到的大量投资组合收益应用降维方法，而是构建能够汇总跨资产特征信息的因子，然后根据这些“特征因子”将资产分类为投资组合。我在一个共同基金特征数据集上估计该模型。由于该数据集是三维的（基金特征随时间变化），特征因子基于张量因子模型（TFM），该模型是二维主成分分析（PCA）的推广。我发现，简约的TFM能够捕捉数据集中超过90%的变异。从TFM导出的定价因子具有较高的夏普比率，并且比标准基准模型更好地捕捉了基金收益的横截面特征。

Science, Philanthropy, and American Leadership

科学、慈善与美国领导力

Robert W. Conn, Peter F. Cowhey, Joshua S. Graff Zivin, Christopher L. Martin

Programs：PE, PR；JEL：H4, L30, O30

Abstract

Investments in the US Science, Technology, Engineering, Medicine and Innovation (STEMI) enterprise come from many different sectors and their combined effect crucially enhances the nation’s competitiveness. Philanthropy is an under-appreciated component of this ecosystem, providing $21.5 billion of research funding at universities and non-profits in 2021 or roughly 42% of the federal outlay at these institutions. In this paper, we argue that these decentralized and diverse set of philanthropic funders alter the incentives and behavior within the US research enterprise to make it more risk tolerant and more innovative than government or business funding alone would yield. It also enables significant innovation in the development of human capital in STEMI areas. We conclude with a comparison of the US research ecosystem to that of China to understand how the two systems differ, with a particular emphasis on the differentiating role that philanthropy may play in influencing the scientific and economic competitiveness of each nation.

中文摘要

对美国科学、技术、工程、医学与创新（STEMI）事业的投入来自众多不同部门，其综合效应极大地提升了国家的竞争力。慈善事业是这一生态系统中一个未被充分重视的组成部分，2021年它为大学和非营利组织提供了215亿美元的研究经费，约占联邦对这些机构拨款的42%。在本文中，我们认为，这些分散且多样化的慈善资助者改变了美国研究体系内的激励和行为，使其比仅靠政府或商业资助更具风险容忍度和创新性。它还促进了STEMI领域人力资本开发的重大创新。最后，我们将美国研究生态系统与中国进行比较，以理解两个体系的差异，特别强调了慈善在影响两国科学和经济竞争力方面可能发挥的差异化作用。

No. 12NBER w31717

Welfare Accounting

福利核算

Eduardo Dávila, Andreas Schaab

Programs：EFG；JEL：D60, E61

Abstract

This paper develops a welfare accounting decomposition that identifies and quantifies the ultimate origins of welfare gains and losses in general economies with heterogeneous individuals and disaggregated production. The decomposition---exclusively based on preferences and technologies---first separates efficiency from redistribution considerations. Efficiency comprises individual efficiency, which traces gains and losses to reallocating consumption and factor supply across individuals, and production efficiency, which captures allocative efficiency gains and losses due to adjusting intermediate inputs and factors, as well as technical efficiency gains and losses from primitive changes in technologies and factor endowments. Leveraging the decomposition, we characterize efficiency conditions in disaggregated production economies with heterogeneous individuals, extending classic efficiency results. In competitive economies, prices (and wedges) are directly informative about the welfare-relevant statistics that shape the welfare accounting decomposition, which allows us to characterize a generalized Hulten's theorem. We present several minimal examples and a rich application to monetary policy.

Programs：DEV；JEL：O12, O13, Q12, Q16

Abstract

Many countries subsidize agricultural inputs but require farmers to travel to retailers to access inputs, just as for normal purchases. What effect do travel costs have on subsidy take-up and input usage, particularly for remote farmers? We analyze Malawi's Farm Input Subsidy Program (FISP), and show that travel-cost-adjusted prices are substantially higher in remote areas. However, subsidy redemption is nearly universal. Programs：EFG, IFM, ITI；JEL：E23, F12, F63, O38

Abstract

What are a country’s policy options concerning the development of emerging technologies in a global economy? To address this question, we investigate optimal dynamic policies in an open economy where technology is endogenously accumulated through R&D innovation. Our key insight is that a country has incentives to influence foreign innovation efforts across sectors and over time—giving rise to optimal policies even when private innovation allocations are (Pareto) efficient. We derive explicit expressions for optimal taxes linked to both an intratemporal and an intertemporal motive to manipulate foreign technology. To affect foreign forward-looking innovation decisions, Home uses committed future trade policies (Ramsey) or innovation policies (Markov). For intratemporal price manipulation, Home government imposes higher tariffs in sectors where it has a comparative advantage.

Abstract

We investigate the consequences of anti-LGBT laws in Poland for suicide attempts and fatalities by applying border-area difference-in-differences models to county-level data. We find that annual suicide attempts increased by 16%, or 5 attempts per 100k capita, after the enactment of anti-LGBT statutes. This rise in suicide attempts was concentrated among men, and was associated with 11 additional suicides per 100k individuals aged 30-49. We also find an increase in suicide attempts in areas that deliberated, but subsequently rejected anti-LGBT resolutions, providing evidence that stigmatization of minority groups leads to declines in population mental health.

Cooking, health, and daily exposure to pollution spikes

Programs：DEV, EEE；JEL：I15, O12, Q53, Q56

Abstract

Many routine daily activities—such as cooking and commuting—cause large recurring pollution spikes that may impact health without significantly affecting average exposure. We study pollution spikes by combining experimental variation in cooking technology with high-frequency data on individual pollution exposure and time-use in Kenya. Improved cookstoves reduce PM2.5 spikes while cooking by 51.3μg/m³ (41%) and cause a 0.24 standard deviation reduction in self-reported respiratory symptoms.
