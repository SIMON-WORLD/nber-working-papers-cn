Using Machine Learning to Construct Hedonic Price Indices

利用机器学习构建特征价格指数

Michael Cafarella, Gabriel Ehrlich, Tian Gao, John C. Haltiwanger, Matthew D. Shapiro, Laura Zhao

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：C81, E31

Abstract

This paper uses machine learning (ML) to estimate hedonic price indices at scale from item-level transaction and product characteristics. The procedure uses state-of-the-art approaches from hedonic econometrics and implements them with a neural network ML approach. Applying the methodology to Nielsen Retail Scanner data leads to a large hedonic adjustment to the Tornqvist index for food product groups: Cumulative food inflation over the period from 2007 through 2015 is reduced by half from 5.9% to 2.8% -- owing to quality adjustment. These results suggest that quality improvement via product turnover is important even in product groups that are not normally considered to feature rapid technological progress. The approach in the paper thus demonstrates the feasibility and importance of implementing hedonic adjustment at scale.

中文摘要

本文运用机器学习（ML）方法，基于项目级交易数据和产品特征，大规模估计特征价格指数。该流程采用特征计量经济学中的前沿方法，并通过神经网络机器学习技术加以实现。将这一方法应用于尼尔森零售扫描数据后，发现对食品类产品的Tornqvist指数进行了大幅特征调整：2007年至2015年期间的累计食品通胀率因质量调整而从5.9%降至2.8%，减少了一半。这些结果表明，即使在通常不被认为具有快速技术进步特征的产品类别中，通过产品更替实现的质量提升也至关重要。因此，本文的方法论证了大规模实施特征调整的可行性与重要性。

Spillovers and Scalability in Job Ad Experiments: Evidence from Gender-Neutral Language

招聘广告实验中的溢出效应与可扩展性：来自性别中立语言的证据

Lucia Del Carpio, Thomas Fujiwara

Programs：DEV, LS, POL；JEL：J16, J7, M14, Z13

Abstract

Gendered-grammar languages are spoken by 39% of the global population, and recent years have seen increasing advocacy for adopting gender-neutral language to promote diversity. We present evidence from two experiments on the effects of gender-neutral language in job advertisements and its treatment spillovers. In a field experiment encompassing all job postings on a Spanish-language tech platform, ads randomly assigned gender-neutral language attracted more female applicants—but only when few other ads applicants viewed were also treated. A second experiment shows that gender-neutral language shapes female tech workers’ beliefs about job characteristics, particularly when the contrast with gendered language is salient. These findings are consistent with applicants interpreting gender-neutral language as a signal about job attributes, with effects that diminish as treatment becomes widespread. Short-run scalability is thus limited: small-scale interventions may produce meaningful impacts, but large-scale adoption may have negligible effects. However, longer-term effects may exist that our designs cannot capture.

中文摘要

全球39%的人口使用具有语法性别的语言，近年来倡导采用性别中立语言以促进多样性的呼声日益高涨。我们通过两项实验，提供了关于招聘广告中性别中立语言效果及其处理溢出效应的证据。在一项涵盖西班牙语科技平台所有招聘帖子的现场实验中，随机分配使用性别中立语言的广告吸引了更多女性求职者——但仅当求职者看到的其他广告中很少也采用相同处理时，这一效果才显著。第二项实验表明，性别中立语言会塑造女性科技工作者对工作特征的信念，尤其是在与性别化语言形成鲜明对比时，这种影响更为突出。这些发现与求职者将性别中立语言解读为关于工作属性的信号这一解释一致，且随着该处理方式的普及，其效果会减弱。因此，短期可扩展性有限：小规模干预可能产生有意义的影响，但大规模采用可能效果甚微。然而，我们的实验设计无法捕捉可能存在的长期效应。

No. 3NBER w31313

Laboratory Safety and Research Productivity

实验室安全与研究生产力

Alberto Galasso, Hong Luo, Brooklynn Zhu

Programs：PR；JEL：I19, I24, O31

Abstract

Are laboratory safety practices a tax on scientific productivity? We examine this question by exploiting the substantial increase in safety regulations at the University of California following the shocking death of a research assistant in 2008. Difference-in-differences analyses show that relative to “dry labs” that use theoretical and computational methods, the publication rates of “wet labs” that conduct experiments using chemical and biological substances did not change significantly after the shock. At the same time, we find that wet labs that used dangerous compounds more frequently before the shock reduced their reliance on flammable materials and unfamiliar hazardous compounds afterward, even though their overall research agenda does not appear to be affected. Our findings suggest that laboratory safety may shape the production of science, but they do not support the claim that safety practices impose a significant tax on research productivity.

中文摘要

实验室安全实践是否会对科研生产力造成负担？我们利用2008年加州大学一名研究助理不幸死亡后安全法规大幅加强这一事件来探讨此问题。双重差分分析显示，与使用理论和计算方法的“干实验室”相比，使用化学和生物物质进行实验的“湿实验室”在事件发生后，其发表率并未发生显著变化。同时，我们发现，那些在事件前更频繁使用危险化合物的湿实验室，在事件后减少了对易燃材料和不熟悉危险化合物的依赖，尽管其整体研究议程似乎未受影响。我们的研究结果表明，实验室安全可能影响科学产出，但并未支持安全实践会显著拖累研究生产力的说法。

When Cryptomining Comes to Town: High Electricity-use Spillovers to the Local Economy

当加密货币挖矿来到城镇：高电力使用对地方经济的溢出效应

Matteo Benetton, Giovanni Compiani, Adair Morse

Programs：AP, CF, EEE, PE；JEL：G10, G23, G5, Q4, Q52, R1, R23

Abstract

Cryptomining, the clearing of cryptocurrency transactions, uses large quantities of electricity. We document that cryptominers' use of local electricity implies higher electricity prices for existing small businesses and households. Studying the electricity market in Upstate NY and using the Bitcoin price as an exogenous shifter of the part of the supply curve faced by the community, we estimate the electricity demand functions for small businesses and households. Based on our estimates, we calculate counterfactual electricity bills, finding that small businesses and households paid an extra $92 million and $204 million annually in Upstate NY because of increased electricity consumption from cryptominers. Local governments in Upstate NY realize more business taxes, but this only offsets a small portion of the costs from higher community electricity bills. Using data on China, where electricity prices are fixed, we find that rationing of electricity in cities with cryptomining entrants deteriorates wages and investments, consistent with crowding-out effects on the local economy. Our results point to a yet-unstudied negative spillover from technology processing to local communities, which would need to be considered against welfare benefits.

中文摘要

加密货币挖矿（即处理加密货币交易）消耗大量电力。我们记录到，矿工对当地电力的使用导致现有小企业和家庭面临更高的电价。通过研究纽约州上游地区的电力市场，并利用比特币价格作为社区所面临电力供给曲线部分的外生变动因素，我们估算了小企业和家庭的电力需求函数。基于这些估计，我们计算了反事实的电费账单，发现由于矿工电力消耗的增加，纽约州上游地区的小企业和家庭每年分别额外支付了9200万美元和2.04亿美元。当地政府获得了更多的商业税收，但这仅能抵消社区电费上涨成本的一小部分。利用中国（电价固定）的数据，我们发现，在有加密货币挖矿企业进入的城市，电力配给导致工资和投资恶化，这与对地方经济的挤出效应一致。我们的结果指出了技术处理对当地社区的一种尚未被研究的负面溢出效应，在权衡福利收益时需要加以考虑。

Misallocation in Firm Production: A Nonparametric Analysis Using Procurement Lotteries

企业生产中的资源错配：基于采购抽签的非参数分析

Paul Carrillo, Dave Donaldson, Dina Pomeranz, Monica Singhal

Programs：DEV, EFG, IO, ITI, PE, PR；JEL：D24, D61, H57, L10, O40

Abstract

This paper develops new tools to study misallocation that do not require assumptions about the heterogeneity of firms’ technologies. We show how features of the distribution of marginal products can be identified from exogenous variation in firms’ input use and used both to test for misallocation and to quantify its resulting welfare losses. We apply this method to a setting with exogenous demand shocks from public procurement contracts for construction services in Ecuador. Our results reject the null of efficiency but our estimates of the resulting welfare losses from misallocation are small.

中文摘要

本文开发了研究资源错配的新工具，这些工具无需对企业技术异质性做出假设。我们展示了如何利用企业投入使用的外生变化来识别边际产品分布的特征，并以此检验资源错配的存在性以及量化由此产生的福利损失。我们将该方法应用于一个场景，该场景中厄瓜多尔公共采购合同（针对建筑服务）带来了外生需求冲击。我们的结果拒绝了效率为零的原假设，但估计出的资源错配导致的福利损失很小。

No. 6NBER w31310

Carbon Border Adjustments, Climate Clubs, and Subsidy Races When Climate Policies Vary

气候政策差异下的碳边境调整、气候俱乐部与补贴竞赛

Kimberly A. Clausing, Catherine Wolfram

Programs：EEE, ITI, PE；JEL：F18, H23, Q54, Q58

Abstract

Jurisdictions adopt heterogeneous climate policies that vary both in terms of ambition and in terms of policy approach, with some jurisdictions pricing carbon and others subsidizing clean production. We distinguish two types of policy spillovers associated with diverse policy approaches to climate change. First, when countries have different levels of climate ambition, free-riders will benefit at the expense of more committed countries. Second, when countries pursue different approaches, carbon-intensive producers within cost-imposing jurisdictions will be at a relative competitive disadvantage compared with producers in subsidizing jurisdictions. Carbon border adjustments and climate clubs are attempts to respond to these policy spillovers, but when countries have divergent policy approaches, one policy alone will not be able to address both types of spillovers. We also consider the policy dynamics that result from carbon border adjustments and climate clubs; both have the potential to encourage upward harmonization of climate policy, but they come with risks. Further, the pressures of international competition in the presence of divergent climate policy approaches may result in subsidy races, which come with their own potential risks and benefits.

中文摘要

各司法管辖区采取的气候政策各不相同，既体现在雄心水平上，也体现在政策方法上：一些地区对碳定价，另一些则补贴清洁生产。我们区分了与不同气候政策方法相关的两类政策溢出效应。首先，当各国气候雄心水平不同时，搭便车者将以更坚定承诺的国家为代价获益。其次，当各国采取不同方法时，实施成本政策辖区的碳密集型生产商相对于补贴辖区的生产商将处于竞争劣势。碳边境调整和气候俱乐部是应对这些政策溢出效应的尝试，但当各国政策方法存在分歧时，单一政策无法同时解决两类溢出效应。我们还考虑了碳边境调整和气候俱乐部所引发的政策动态；两者都有可能鼓励气候政策的向上协调，但也伴随着风险。此外，在气候政策方法存在分歧的情况下，国际竞争的压力可能导致补贴竞赛，这本身也带来潜在的风险和收益。

No. 7NBER w31309

Quality Adjustment at Scale: Hedonic vs. Exact Demand-Based Price Indices

大规模质量调整：特征价格指数与基于精确需求的价格指数之比较

Gabriel Ehrlich, John C. Haltiwanger, Ron S. Jarmin, David Johnson, Ed Olivares, Luke W. Pardue, Matthew D. Shapiro, Laura Zhao

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：C81, E31

Abstract

This paper explores methods for constructing price indices from item-level transactions data on prices, quantities, and product attributes. The paper evaluates approaches that are feasible at scale, i.e., across the wide range of products, disparate encoding of attributes, and rapid product turnover inherent in “big data” on economic transactions, while producing improved cost-of-living indices that reflect both substitution effects and quality change. The paper presents hedonic methods that estimate changing valuations of both observable and unobservable characteristics in the presence of product turnover. It also considers demand-based methods that account for product turnover and changing appeal of continuing products. The paper provides evidence of substantial quality-adjustment in prices for a wide range of goods, including food and high-tech consumer products. The paper also shows that hedonics can be implemented with well-encoded attributes using standard econometrics and with unstructured attribute data using machine learning.

中文摘要

本文探讨了利用包含价格、数量和产品属性的项目级交易数据构建价格指数的方法。文章评估了那些在大规模应用（即跨越经济交易“大数据”中固有的广泛产品类别、不同属性编码方式以及快速产品更替）中可行的方案，同时旨在生成能够同时反映替代效应和质量变化的改进型生活成本指数。本文提出了特征方法，用于估计在产品更替背景下可观测和不可观测特征价值的变化。文章还考虑了基于需求的方法，该方法能够处理产品更替以及存续产品吸引力变化的问题。本文提供了证据，表明在包括食品和高科技消费品在内的广泛商品中，价格存在显著的质量调整。文章还表明，对于编码良好的属性，可以使用标准计量经济学方法实现特征调整；对于非结构化属性数据，则可以使用机器学习方法。

No. 8NBER w31308

Top Talent, Elite Colleges, and Migration: Evidence from the Indian Institutes of Technology

顶尖人才、精英大学与移民：来自印度理工学院的证据

Prithwiraj Choudhury, Ina Ganguli, Patrick Gaulé

Programs：DEV, PR；JEL：F22, J61, O33, O38

Abstract

We study migration in the right tail of the talent distribution using a novel dataset of Indian high school students taking the Joint Entrance Exam (JEE), a college entrance exam used for admission to the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT). We find a high incidence of migration after students complete college: among the top 1,000 scorers on the exam, 36% have migrated abroad, rising to 62% for the top 100 scorers. We next document that students who attended the original “Top 5” Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) were 5 percentage points more likely to migrate for graduate school compared to equally talented students who studied in other institutions. We explore two mechanisms for these patterns: signaling, for which we study migration after one university suddenly gained the IIT designation; and alumni networks, using information on the location of IIT alumni in U.S. computer science departments.

中文摘要

我们利用一份新颖的数据集——即参加联合入学考试（JEE，即印度理工学院等名校的大学入学考试）的印度高中生数据——来研究人才分布右尾端的移民现象。我们发现，学生大学毕业后移民的比例很高：在考试成绩排名前1000名的学生中，36%已移民海外；而在排名前100名的学生中，这一比例上升至62%。接着，我们记录到，与同等才华但就读于其他院校的学生相比，就读于最初的“前五所”印度理工学院（IIT）的学生，其研究生阶段移民的可能性高出5个百分点。我们探讨了这两种模式的两种机制：信号机制（我们研究了一所大学突然获得IIT称号后的移民情况）和校友网络机制（利用美国计算机科学系中IIT校友的地理位置信息）。

The Effect of Means-Tested Transfers on Work: Evidence from Quasi-Randomly Assigned SNAP Caseworkers

经济状况调查式转移支付对工作的影响：来自准随机分配的SNAP案件工作者的证据

Jason B. Cook, Chloe N. East

Programs：CH, EH, LS, PE；JEL：H51, H53, I38, J22

Abstract

Canonical economic theory clearly predicts means-tested transfers will reduce work. We provide new evidence on this question in the context of the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)—the backbone of the U.S. safety net. It is challenging to identify the causal effects of SNAP, so we introduce an examiner design based on conditional random assignment of SNAP applicants to caseworkers using rich administrative data. We empirically establish that caseworkers help determine whether applicants receive benefits. We then use this as an instrument for SNAP receipt to produce new, comprehensive and generalizable evidence of the labor supply effects of modern SNAP. Our results shed light on why the canonical theory’s predictions might not always bear out.

中文摘要

经典经济理论明确预测，经济状况调查式转移支付会减少工作。我们以补充营养援助计划（SNAP）——美国安全网的支柱——为背景，提供了关于这一问题的新的证据。识别SNAP的因果效应具有挑战性，因此我们引入了一种基于条件随机分配的设计，利用丰富的行政数据将SNAP申请人分配给案件工作者。我们通过实证确定，案件工作者有助于决定申请人是否获得福利。然后，我们以此作为SNAP领取的工具变量，为现代SNAP的劳动供给效应提供新的、全面且可推广的证据。我们的结果揭示了为何经典理论的预测可能并不总是成立。

No. 10NBER w31306中国相关

Creating and Connecting US and China Science: Chinese Diaspora and Returnee Researchers

创建与连接美国和中国科学：华人散居与海归研究人员

Qingnan Xie, Richard B. Freeman

Programs：DEV, ITI, LS, PR；JEL：F1, F22, F63, I2, I20, J2, J20, J3, J30, O3, O30

Abstract

The close connection between US and China in scientific research and education in the 2000s produced a large group of China-born researchers who work in the US (“diaspora”) and a larger group of China-born researchers who gained US-research experience and returned to do their research in China (“returnee”). Analyzing 2018 Scopus data on research papers, we estimate that diaspora researchers contributed to 27% of US addressed papers, and that returnee researchers contributed to 38% of China addressed papers. Both the number of papers with diaspora authors and the number of papers with returnee authors far exceeded the usual measure of US-China collaborative work, papers with both US and China addresses. In terms of quality or impact, papers with diaspora or returnee authors averaged more citations and had higher proportions of publication in high CiteScore journals than other US-addressed or China-addressed papers. Finally, papers with diaspora and/or returnee authors were at the center of the US-China coauthor network and major conduits of research findings between the countries in the network of scientific citations. The benefits of the US-China research connection notwithstanding, the link between the countries’ research began to fray from 2018 through the early 2020s, with potential deleterious effects on each country’s future research output and on global science writ large to which US and China are the two biggest contributors.

中文摘要

21世纪初，美国与中国在科学研究和教育领域的紧密联系产生了一大批在美国工作的中国出生研究人员（“散居者”），以及更大一批获得美国研究经验后返回中国进行研究的研究人员（“海归”）。通过分析2018年Scopus研究论文数据，我们估计散居研究人员贡献了27%的美国地址论文，而海归研究人员贡献了38%的中国地址论文。拥有散居作者或海归作者的论文数量，均远远超过了通常衡量美中合作工作的指标——即同时拥有美国和中国地址的论文数量。在质量或影响力方面，拥有散居或海归作者的论文平均被引次数更高，并且在高CiteScore期刊上发表的比例也高于其他美国地址或中国地址论文。最后，拥有散居和/或海归作者的论文处于美中合著网络的中心，并且是两国之间在科学引文网络中研究成果的主要传导渠道。尽管美中研究联系带来了益处，但两国研究之间的联系从2018年到2020年代初开始出现裂痕，这可能对各自国家未来的研究产出以及全球科学（美中是其中最大的两个贡献者）产生有害影响。

Using Limited Trial Evidence to Credibly Choose Treatment Dosage when Efficacy and Adverse Effects Weakly Increase with Dose

当疗效和不良反应随剂量弱递增时，利用有限的试验证据可信地选择治疗剂量

Charles F. Manski

Programs：EH；JEL：C44, I1

Abstract

我们研究了土耳其2012年引入的一组基于地点的补贴的影响。利用企业层面的资产负债表数据以及国内生产网络的数据，我们首先评估了该政策的直接和间接影响。我们发现，在作为补贴计划重点的行业-省份配对中，经济活动有所增加，并且对受补贴企业的供应商和客户产生了正向溢出效应。借助一个动态的多区域、多行业一般均衡模型，我们随后评估了该计划的影响。基于校准后的模型，我们发现，从长期来看，该补贴计划在减少该国相对欠发达地区和较繁荣地区之间的不平等方面取得了适度成功。这些适度的长期效应是由于家庭能够响应补贴计划而迁移，以及投入产出联系跨越了土耳其境内的补贴区域。
