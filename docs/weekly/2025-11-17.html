No. 1NBER w34488

Social Dynamics of AI Adoption

人工智能采纳的社会动态

Leonardo Bursztyn, Alex Imas, Rafael Jiménez-Durán, Aaron Leonard, Christopher Roth

Programs：ED, PR；JEL：D83, D91, I20, O33

Abstract

Anxiety about falling behind can drive people to embrace emerging technologies with uncertain consequences. We study how social forces shape demand for AI-based learning tools early in the education pipeline. In incentivized experiments with parents—key gatekeepers for children’s AI adoption—we elicit their demand for unrestricted AI tools for teenagers’ education. Parental demand rises with the share of other teenagers using the technology, with social forces increasing willingness to pay for AI by more than 60%. Providing information about potentially adverse effects of unstructured AI use negatively shifts beliefs about the merits of AI, but does not change individual demand. Instead, this information increases parents’ preference for banning AI in schools. Follow-up experiments show that social information has little effect on beliefs about AI quality, perceived skill priorities, or support for bans, suggesting that effects operate through social pressure rather than social learning. Our evidence highlights social pressure driving individual technology adoption despite widespread support for restricting its use.

中文摘要

对落后的焦虑可能驱使人们接受后果不确定的新兴技术。我们研究社会力量如何塑造教育早期阶段对基于人工智能的学习工具的需求。在与家长（儿童采用人工智能的关键守门人）进行的激励实验中，我们引出了他们对青少年教育中不受限制的人工智能工具的需求。家长的需求随着使用该技术的其他青少年比例上升而增加，社会力量使对人工智能的支付意愿提高了60%以上。提供关于无结构人工智能使用潜在不利影响的信息会负面地改变对人工智能优点的信念，但不会改变个人需求。相反，这些信息增加了家长支持在学校禁止人工智能的偏好。后续实验表明，社会信息对关于人工智能质量的信念、感知的技能优先级或对禁令的支持几乎没有影响，这表明其效应是通过社会压力而非社会学习起作用的。我们的证据突显了社会压力推动个人技术采纳，尽管普遍支持限制其使用。

No. 2NBER w34487

A Cold Stop: Temperature, Unemployment and Joblessness Dynamics

寒流：温度、失业与失业动态

Joshua S. Graff Zivin, Anthony Lepinteur, Matthew J. Neidell, Adrian Nieto Castro

Programs：EEE, LS；JEL：I1, J2, Q5

Abstract

We provide new evidence that short-run temperature shocks affect unemployment dynamics. Linking daily weather data with three decades of Current Population Survey microdata, we show that cold, but not hot, temperatures significantly increase unemployment risk. This effect is concentrated in climate-exposed industries and driven by both increased job separations and longer unemployment durations. Separations appear to be driven by a rise in layoffs rather than quits, while the increase in unemployment duration is largely explained by a decline in employer vacancy postings. Taken together, temperature-induced joblessness dynamics are primarily demand-driven, rather than a result of changes in worker behavior.

中文摘要

我们提供了短期温度冲击影响失业动态的新证据。将每日天气数据与三十年的当前人口调查微观数据相关联，我们表明寒冷（而非炎热）温度会显著增加失业风险。这种效应集中在受气候影响的行业，并且是由工作分离增加和失业持续时间延长共同驱动的。工作分离似乎是由裁员增加而非辞职增加所驱动，而失业持续时间的增加则很大程度上可以用雇主职位空缺发布减少来解释。综合来看，温度引发的失业动态主要是需求驱动的，而非工人行为变化的结果。

Branching Fixed Effects: A Proposal for Communicating Uncertainty

分支固定效应：关于沟通不确定性的一个提议

Patrick M. Kline

Programs：EFG, LS, PE, TWP；JEL：C01, J30

Abstract

Economists often rely on estimates of linear fixed effects models developed by other teams of researchers. Assessing the uncertainty in these estimates can be challenging. I propose a form of sample splitting for network data that breaks two-way fixed effects estimates into statistically independent branches, each of which provides an unbiased estimate of the parameters of interest. These branches facilitate uncertainty quantification, moment estimation, and shrinkage. Algorithms are developed for efficiently extracting branches from large datasets. I illustrate these techniques using a benchmark dataset from Veneto, Italy that has been widely used to study firm wage effects.

中文摘要

经济学家经常依赖其他研究团队开发的线性固定效应模型的估计值。评估这些估计值的不确定性可能具有挑战性。我提出了一种针对网络数据的分割样本形式，它将双向固定效应估计分解为统计上独立的分支，每个分支都提供感兴趣参数的无偏估计。这些分支有助于不确定性量化、矩估计和收缩。我们开发了从大型数据集中高效提取分支的算法。我使用来自意大利威尼托的一个基准数据集来展示这些技术，该数据集已被广泛用于研究企业工资效应。

The Impact of State Paid Sick Leave Mandates on Medicaid-financed Prescription Medications

州级带薪病假强制令对医疗补助计划资助处方药的影响

Sumedha Gupta, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Christopher J. Ruhm, Kosali I. Simon

Programs：AG, CH, EH, LE, PE；JEL：H0, I1, K0

Abstract

The United States lacks a federal paid sick leave policy. However, 18 states and the District of Columbia have adopted or announced paid sick leave employer mandates to increase access to this benefit, creating a quasi-experimental setting to study whether paid sick leave affects healthcare use. People enrolled in Medicaid are an important population to study in terms of state paid sick leave policies as the majority of non-disabled enrollees are employed, but frequently work in jobs without paid sick leave. Given enrollees’ lower incomes, losing earnings to receive healthcare may be a significant barrier to care. In this study, we examine the effect of state paid sick leave policies on Medicaid-financed dispensed prescription medications. Using difference-in-differences methods that are robust to bias associated with a staggered treatment rollout, we show that Medicaid-financed dispensed prescription medications increase by 6.7% following adoption of a state paid sick leave policy. These findings suggest that state paid sick leave policies promote engagement with the healthcare system and use of healthcare services among financially constrained populations.

中文摘要

美国缺乏联邦层面的带薪病假政策。然而，已有18个州和哥伦比亚特区通过或宣布了雇主带薪病假强制令，以增加员工获得此项福利的机会，这为研究带薪病假是否影响医疗保健利用创造了一个准实验环境。医疗补助计划参保者是一个值得研究的重要群体，因为大多数非残疾参保者有工作，但往往从事没有带薪病假的工作。考虑到参保者收入较低，因接受医疗而损失收入可能成为就医的重大障碍。在本研究中，我们考察了州级带薪病假政策对医疗补助计划资助的处方药配发量的影响。使用对交错政策推行带来的偏差具有稳健性的双重差分方法，我们发现，在州级带薪病假政策实施后，医疗补助计划资助的处方药配发量增加了6.7%。这些发现表明，州级带薪病假政策促进了经济受限人群参与医疗保健系统并利用医疗服务。

No. 5NBER w34484

Do Test Scores Misrepresent Test Results? An Item-by-Item Analysis

考试成绩是否曲解了测试结果？一项逐题分析

Jesse Bruhn, Michael Gilraine, Jens Ludwig, Sendhil Mullainathan

Programs：ED；JEL：I20, I21, J24

Abstract

Much of the data collected in education is effectively thrown away. Students answer individual test questions, but administrators and researchers only see aggregate performance. All the item-level data are lost. Ex ante it is not clear this destroys much useful information, since the aggregate might be a sufficient statistic. Using data from Texas for 5 million students and 1.31 billion student-item responses, we show that in fact aggregation does destroy a great deal of valuable information in education: (1) Even conditional on a summary test measure, there is additional information in the item-level data; (2) This additional information is relevant for the student outcomes that education decisions seek to optimize; and (3) This information can be made practically useful for schools. Given how inexpensive storing, transmitting and analyzing such data would be, large gains could be had in education by simply using all the data we currently collect.

中文摘要

教育领域收集的大部分数据实际上被浪费了。学生回答单个测试题目，但管理者和研究者只看到总体表现。所有逐题层面的数据都丢失了。事前并不清楚这是否会破坏大量有用信息，因为总体指标可能是一个充分统计量。利用德克萨斯州500万名学生和13.1亿个学生-题目回答的数据，我们表明，事实上汇总确实破坏了教育中大量有价值的信息：(1) 即使在控制了测试总分指标后，逐题数据中仍包含额外信息；(2) 这些额外信息与教育决策试图优化的学生结果相关；(3) 这些信息可以变得对学校实际有用。鉴于存储、传输和分析此类数据的成本非常低廉，仅通过充分利用我们当前收集的所有数据，教育领域就能获得巨大收益。

No. 6NBER w34483

Economic Development According to Chandler

钱德勒视角下的经济发展

Niklas Engbom, Hannes Malmberg, Tommaso Porzio, Federico Rossi, Todd Schoellman

Programs：DEV, EFG；JEL：E0, O0

Abstract

Chandler (1977) shows that large firms require hierarchies of white-collar workers to coordinate complex production. We document that this insight continues to hold globally today, and we show that low education levels in developing countries limit the supply of white-collar workers and constrain firm size. We extend the occupational choice model of Lucas (1978) to allow entrepreneurs to reorganize their firms by allocating administrative tasks to hired professionals, which brings the firm closer to constant returns to scale. We calibrate the model to be consistent with cross-sectional microdata and validate it using quasi-experimental and experimental evidence on the effects of educational expansions and management training interventions. Skills explain two-thirds of the reorganization of production into large firms with economic development, while structural transformation and reductions in barriers are needed to explain the remaining shift.

中文摘要

钱德勒（1977）指出，大企业需要白领工人的层级结构来协调复杂生产。我们记录了这一点在全球范围内至今仍然成立，并表明发展中国家较低的教育水平限制了白领工人的供给，从而制约了企业规模。我们扩展了卢卡斯（1978）的职业选择模型，允许企业家通过将行政任务分配给雇佣的专业人士来重组其企业，这使企业更接近规模报酬不变。我们校准模型以与横截面微观数据一致，并利用关于教育扩张和管理培训干预效果的准实验和实验证据进行验证。技能解释了随着经济发展生产重组为大企业的三分之二，而结构转型和壁垒减少则是解释剩余转变所必需的。

No. 7NBER w34482

Violent Peers at School: Impacts and Mechanisms

校园中的暴力同伴：影响与机制

Victor Lavy, Assaf Yancu

Programs：CH, DEV, ED, LS；JEL：I25, J0

Abstract

This paper examines the impact of classroom exposure to peers with a history of violent behavior on academic achievement and the underlying mechanisms. This measure of peer violence departs significantly from earlier studies that measured potential peer violence based on the background characteristics of students. We exploit idiosyncratic treatment variations during the transition from primary to middle school for causal identification. We find that a higher proportion of violent peers negatively affects cognitive performance in tests in various subjects, particularly pronounced in mathematics and English, compared to Hebrew and science. These effects are more pronounced in girls than in boys. While boys’ performance is negatively influenced only by the presence of violent male peers, girls are adversely affected by both violent male and female peers. As for mechanisms, violent peers disrupt learning environments and lower teachers’ productivity, reflected in lower job satisfaction and perception of higher workloads. Violent peers also significantly increase the likelihood of other students engaging in physical fights, and reduce their homework time, especially for girls and students from low SES.

中文摘要

本文研究了课堂上接触有暴力行为史的同学对学业成绩的影响及其潜在机制。这种同伴暴力的衡量方式与早期基于学生背景特征衡量潜在同伴暴力的研究有显著不同。我们利用小学升初中过渡期间的特殊处理变异进行因果识别。我们发现，暴力同伴比例较高会对各科测试的认知表现产生负面影响，与希伯来语和科学相比，在数学和英语上尤为明显。这些影响在女生中比在男生中更为显著。男生的表现仅受到暴力男性同伴存在的负面影响，而女生则同时受到暴力男性和女性同伴的不利影响。关于机制，暴力同伴会扰乱学习环境并降低教师生产力，这体现在教师工作满意度降低和感知工作量增加上。暴力同伴还显著增加了其他学生参与肢体冲突的可能性，并减少了他们的家庭作业时间，尤其是对女生和来自低社会经济地位家庭的学生而言。

No. 8NBER w34481

Occupations, Human Capital Accumulation and Inequality

职业、人力资本积累与不平等

Andrés Erosa, Luisa Fuster, Gueorgui Kambourov, Richard Rogerson

Programs：EFG；JEL：E24, J24

Abstract

Two robust empirical facts are that mean wages and cross-sectional wage dispersion both increase over the life cycle. We study how these two changes vary across occupations and document a strong positive correlation: occupations with high mean wage growth over the life cycle also exhibit greater increases in cross-sectional wage dispersion. We develop a novel dynamic Roy model that features both static and dynamic comparative advantage and show that it can account for the variation in life cycle wage distributions across high and low wage occupations. Dynamic comparative advantage reflects individual heterogeneity in occupation specific learning abilities and is the dominant force that shapes occupation choice in our model. We highlight several important implications of dynamic comparative advantage and show that our model captures the data better than a benchmark model that features persistent shocks.

中文摘要

两个稳健的经验事实是，平均工资和横截面工资离散度都随生命周期增加。我们研究了这两个变化如何在不同职业间变化，并记录了一个强烈的正相关关系：生命周期中平均工资增长高的职业，其横截面工资离散度的增加也更大。我们开发了一个新颖的动态罗伊模型，该模型同时具有静态和动态比较优势，并表明它可以解释高工资和低工资职业间生命周期工资分布的变化。动态比较优势反映了个人在职业特定学习能力上的异质性，并且是我们模型中塑造职业选择的主导力量。我们强调了动态比较优势的几个重要含义，并表明我们的模型比一个具有持续性冲击的基准模型更好地捕捉了数据特征。

No. 9NBER w34480

The Impact of Pay Transparency in Job Postings on the Labor Market

招聘信息中薪酬透明度对劳动力市场的影响

David Arnold, Simon Quach, Bledi Taska

Programs：LS；JEL：J30, J31, J38

Abstract

This paper studies the labor market effects of recent state-level policies that require employers to disclose salary information in job postings. Leveraging a difference-in-differences design, we show that employers increased the fraction of postings with salary information by 30 percentage points. Across three datasets, we find consistent evidence of an increase in wages of 1.3-3.6%. We find no impacts on pay dispersion, employment, the number of postings, or skill and education requirements. Our evidence is consistent with pay transparency increasing competition in the labor market, leading to positive spillovers on incumbent workers and always-posting firms.

中文摘要

本文研究了近期州级政策要求雇主在招聘信息中披露薪资信息对劳动力市场的影响。利用双重差分设计，我们表明雇主将包含薪资信息的招聘信息比例提高了30个百分点。基于三个数据集，我们发现了一致的证据，表明工资增长了1.3-3.6%。我们没有发现对薪酬离散度、就业、招聘信息数量或技能与教育要求的影响。我们的证据与薪酬透明度增加劳动力市场竞争、从而对现有员工和始终发布薪资信息的公司产生正向溢出效应的观点一致。

No. 10NBER w34479

Rating Government Procurement Markets

政府采购市场评级

Tatyana Deryugina, Alminas Žaldokas, Anastassia Fedyk, Yuriy Gorodnichenko, James Hodson, Ilona Sologoub

Programs：IO, PE, POL；JEL：D73, L1, L78

Abstract

We develop a novel, scalable method for assessing the quality of public procurement systems using standard administrative data. Our approach compares the distribution of procurement opportunities to the distribution of contract awards across firms. We first derive a simple theoretical benchmark that relates the expected distribution of contract value winning firms, measured as a Herfindahl–Hirschman index (HHI), to the distribution of auction values, measured as a respective HHI, and the number of winning firms. Significant deviations of winning firms’ HHI from this benchmark indicate potential governance failures such as corruption or unchecked collusion. Our method requires no subjective input, is transparent and reproducible, and allows for meaningful comparisons across countries, industry sectors, and over time. We use procurement data from Ukraine and EU member states in 2018–2021 to assess the performance of five large sectors. Results indicate that Ukraine’s procurement performance in four of the five sectors is comparable to many other European countries. However, Ukraine’s construction sector consistently displays the largest excess concentration among all countries considered, consistent with anecdotal evidence of corruption in this sector. Overall, with minimal data requirements, our method offers a practical tool for cross-sector and cross-country assessment of procurement systems.

中文摘要

我们开发了一种新颖、可扩展的方法，利用标准行政数据评估公共采购系统的质量。我们的方法比较了采购机会的分布与企业间合同授予的分布。我们首先推导出一个简单的理论基准，该基准将中标企业的合同价值分布（以赫芬达尔-赫希曼指数衡量）与拍卖价值的分布（以相应的赫芬达尔-赫希曼指数衡量）以及中标企业数量联系起来。中标企业的赫芬达尔-赫希曼指数显著偏离此基准，则表明可能存在腐败或未受约束的合谋等治理失败。我们的方法不需要主观输入，透明且可复制，并允许在国家、行业部门和不同时期之间进行有意义的比较。我们使用乌克兰和欧盟成员国2018-2021年的采购数据来评估五个大型部门的表现。结果表明，乌克兰在五个部门中的四个部门的采购表现与许多其他欧洲国家相当。然而，乌克兰的建筑部门在所有考虑的国家中持续显示出最大的超额集中度，这与该部门腐败的轶事证据一致。总体而言，在数据需求极低的情况下，我们的方法为跨部门和跨国评估采购系统提供了一个实用工具。

Our Underappreciated International Reserve System

我们被低估的国际储备体系

Serkan Arslanalp, Barry Eichengreen, Chima Simpson-Bell

Programs：IFM；JEL：F0, F33

Abstract

We document some underappreciated aspects of the recent evolution of the international reserve system. These include the growing share of gold in global central bank reserves, the continuing emergence of nontraditional reserve currencies, and the stalling share of renminbi in reserves. These trends are consistent with our findings in our earlier papers. In addition we look to the future, pondering the potential implications of dollar-linked stablecoins, the expansion of the BRICS grouping of countries and their de- dollarization plans, the development of blockchain-based platforms such as Project mBridge for the direct exchange of central bank digital currencies, and questions about the dollar’s safe-haven status.

中文摘要

我们记录了国际储备体系近期演变中一些被低估的方面。这些包括黄金在全球央行储备中占比不断上升、非传统储备货币持续涌现，以及人民币在储备中占比停滞不前。这些趋势与我们早期论文中的发现一致。此外，我们展望未来，思考与美元挂钩的稳定币的潜在影响、金砖国家集团扩大及其去美元化计划、基于区块链的平台（如用于直接交换央行数字货币的mBridge项目）的发展，以及美元避险地位面临的问题。

No. 12NBER w34477

Middlemen in Search Equilibrium: A Survey

搜索均衡中的中间商：综述

Xun (Grace) Gong, Ziqi Qiao, Randall Wright

Programs：EFG；JEL：D0

Abstract

This essay surveys the literature on middlemen—i.e., intermediation in exchange—reviewing, extending and consolidating key developments in the field. This is important because intermediated trade is common in reality but absent in standard general equilibrium theory. We focus on research using search theory. In various models, agents may act as middlemen when they are good at search, bargaining, recognizing quality, storing inventories, using credit, etc. The theory applies to markets for goods, inputs or assets. We discuss versions with indivisible or divisible goods, fixed or endogenous participation, stationary and dynamic equilibria, and some implications for efficiency and volatility.

Abstract

We introduce an asset supply-and-demand approach to analyze the trajectory of US aggregate wealth, real interest rates, and fiscal sustainability. Our framework uses micro-founded and easy-to-implement sufficient statistics to quantify how shifts in demographics, inequality, and other forces affect asset market equilibrium.
