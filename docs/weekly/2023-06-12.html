No. 1NBER w31349

The Subtlety of Optimal Paternalism in a Population with Bounded Rationality

有限理性人口中最优家长式管理的微妙性

Charles F. Manski, Eytan Sheshinski

Programs：PE；JEL：H10

Abstract

We study the subtlety of optimal paternalism when a utilitarian planner has the power to design a discrete choice set for a heterogeneous population with bounded rationality. We first consider the planning problem in abstraction. We show that the policy that most effectively constrains or influences choices depends multiplicatively on the preferences of the population and the choice probabilities conditional on preferences that measure the suboptimality of behavior. We then study two settings in which the planner may mandate an action or decentralize decision making. One setting supposes that individuals measure utility with additive random error and maximize mismeasured rather than actual utility. Then optimal planning requires knowledge of the distribution of measurement errors. The other setting studies binary treatment choice when the planner can mandate a treatment conditional on publicly observed personal covariates or can enable individuals to choose their own treatments conditional on private information. Here we focus on situations where bounded rationality takes the form of deviations between subjective and objective probabilities of uncertain outcomes. To illustrate, we consider clinical decision making in medicine. In toto, our cautionary analysis shows that determination of optimal policy requires the planner to possess extensive knowledge that is rarely available. We warn that research in behavioral public economics should avoid overoptimistic claims regarding the nature of optimal paternalistic policies. We argue that credible study of utilitarian planning should consider not only the population but also the planner to be boundedly rational.

中文摘要

我们研究了当功利主义规划者有权为具有有限理性的异质人口设计离散选择集时，最优家长式管理的微妙性。我们首先抽象地考虑规划问题。我们表明，最有效约束或影响选择的政策，取决于人口的偏好以及衡量行为次优性的、以偏好为条件的概率的乘积。然后，我们研究了规划者可以强制采取行动或下放决策权的两种情境。一种情境假设个体以加性随机误差衡量效用，并最大化被误测的效用而非实际效用。那么，最优规划需要了解测量误差的分布。另一种情境研究二元治疗选择，此时规划者可以根据公开观察到的个人协变量强制实施治疗，或者允许个体根据私人信息选择自己的治疗。在这里，我们关注有限理性表现为不确定结果的主观概率与客观概率之间存在偏差的情况。为了说明，我们考虑医学中的临床决策。总之，我们的警示性分析表明，确定最优政策要求规划者拥有广泛且鲜有可用的知识。我们警告，行为公共经济学的研究应避免对最优家长式政策的性质做出过于乐观的断言。我们认为，对功利主义规划的可靠研究不仅应考虑人口，也应将规划者视为有限理性的。

War Discourse and the Cross Section of Expected Stock Returns

战争话语与预期股票收益的截面差异

David Hirshleifer, Dat Mai, Kuntara Pukthuanthong

Programs：AP, POL；JEL：G0, G02, G1, G10, G11, G4, G41

Abstract

A war-related factor model derived from textual analysis of media news reports explains the cross section of expected stock returns. Using a semi-supervised topic model to extract discourse topics from 7,000,000 New York Times stories spanning 160 years, the war factor predicts the cross section of returns across test assets derived from both traditional and machine learning construction techniques, and spanning 138 anomalies. Our findings are consistent with assets that are good hedges for war risk receiving lower risk premia, or with assets that are more positively sensitive to war prospects being more overvalued. The return premium on the war factor is incremental to standard effects.

中文摘要

一个基于媒体新闻报道文本分析得出的战争相关因子模型，解释了预期股票收益的截面差异。我们使用半监督主题模型，从跨越160年的700万篇《纽约时报》报道中提取话语主题，发现战争因子能够预测跨测试资产的收益截面差异，这些测试资产既来自传统构建技术，也来自机器学习构建技术，并涵盖138个异象。我们的发现与以下观点一致：能够较好对冲战争风险的资产获得较低的风险溢价，或者对战争前景更敏感的资产被更严重地高估。战争因子的回报溢价是独立于标准效应的增量效应。

No. 3NBER w31347

A Theory of Price Caps on Non-Renewable Resources

不可再生资源价格上限理论

Simon Johnson, Lukasz Rachel, Catherine Wolfram

Programs：EEE, IFM；JEL：F51, L13, L71, Q41

Abstract

What is the optimal response of an exhaustible resource producer to sanctions in the form of a price cap? This paper develops a dynamic framework incorporating stochastic prices, financial frictions, and market power to study this novel tool of statecraft. A binding price cap can incentivize increased extraction, stabilizing prices in the global market. But the cap’s effectiveness diminishes when the policy is leaky or is weakly enforced, as seen with the G7 price cap on Russian oil. Such imperfections introduce a trade-off between market stability and the welfare of the sanctioned producer. We provide a systematic approach to setting an optimal cap level in these circumstances.

中文摘要

面对以价格上限形式实施的制裁，可耗竭资源生产者的最优应对策略是什么？本文构建了一个包含随机价格、金融摩擦和市场势力的动态框架，以研究这一新颖的国家治理工具。具有约束力的价格上限可以激励增加开采量，从而稳定全球市场价格。但当政策存在漏洞或执行不力时，其有效性会降低，正如七国集团对俄罗斯石油实施的价格上限所示。这种不完善性在市场稳定与被制裁生产者的福利之间引入了权衡。我们提供了一种系统性的方法，用于在这些情况下设定最优的价格上限水平。

No. 4NBER w31346

Ph.D. Publication Productivity: The Role of Gender and Race in Supervision in South Africa

博士生的发表生产力：南非导师制中性别与种族的作用

Giulia Rossello, Robin Cowan, Jacques Mairesse

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：A14, I23, I24, J15, J16, O32

Abstract

We study whether student-advisor gender and race composition matters for publication productivity of Ph.D. students in South Africa. We consider all Ph.D. students in STEM graduating between 2000 and 2014, after the recent systematic introduction of doctoral programs in this country. We investigate the joint effects of gender and race for the whole sample and looking separately at the sub-samples of (1) white-white; (2) black-black; and (3) black-white student-advisor couples. We find significant productivity differences between male and female students. These disparities are more pronounced for female students working with male advisors when looking at the joint effects of gender and race for the white-white and black-black student-advisor pairs. We also explore whether publication productivity differences change significantly for students with a high, medium, or low “productivity-profile”. We find that female productivity gaps are U-shaped over the range of productivity. Female students working with male advisors have more persistent productivity gaps over the productivity distribution, while female students with a high (or low) “productivity-profile” studying with female advisors are as productive as male students with similar “productivity-profile” studying with male advisors.

中文摘要

我们研究了南非博士生中，学生与导师的性别和种族构成是否对发表生产力产生影响。我们考察了2000年至2014年间毕业的所有STEM领域博士生，这一时期正值该国系统性地引入博士项目。我们分析了性别和种族对全样本的联合效应，并分别考察了以下子样本：(1) 白人-白人；(2) 黑人-黑人；(3) 黑人-白人的学生-导师配对。我们发现男女学生之间存在显著的生产力差异。在考察白人-白人和黑人-黑人学生-导师配对中性别与种族的联合效应时，这些差异在由男性导师指导的女性学生中更为明显。我们还探讨了具有高、中、低“生产力特征”的学生，其发表生产力差异是否发生显著变化。我们发现，女性生产力差距在整个生产力分布上呈U形。由男性导师指导的女性学生在生产力分布上具有更持久的差距，而由女性导师指导的具有高（或低）“生产力特征”的女性学生，其生产力与由男性导师指导的具有相似“生产力特征”的男性学生相当。

No. 5NBER w31345

Public Education and Intergenerational Housing Wealth Effects

公共教育与代际住房财富效应

Michael Gilraine, James Graham, Angela Zheng

Programs：ED, EFG；JEL：E21, E24, I24, J62, R21, R23

Abstract

While rising house prices are known to benefit existing homeowners, we document a new channel through which house price shocks have intergenerational wealth effects. Using panel data from school zones within a large U.S. school district, we find that higher local house prices lead to improvements in local school quality, thereby increasing children's human capital and future incomes. We quantify this housing wealth channel using an overlapping generations model with neighborhood choice, spatial equilibrium, and endogenous school quality. We find that housing market shocks generate large intergenerational wealth effects that account for around one-third of total housing wealth effects.

中文摘要

虽然房价上涨已知会使现有房主受益，但我们记录了一种新的渠道，通过该渠道，房价冲击会产生代际财富效应。利用美国一个大学区内的学区面板数据，我们发现当地房价上涨会导致当地学校质量改善，从而增加儿童的人力资本和未来收入。我们通过一个包含邻里选择、空间均衡和内生学校质量的世代交叠模型来量化这一住房财富渠道。我们发现，住房市场冲击会产生巨大的代际财富效应，约占住房财富总效应的三分之一。

The Economic Determinants of Heat Pump Adoption

热泵采用的经济决定因素

Lucas W. Davis

Programs：EEE；JEL：H23, L51, Q41, Q42, Q48, Q54

Abstract

One concern with subsidies for low-carbon technologies is that they tend to go predominantly to high-income households. Previous research has shown, for example, that the top income quintile receives 60% of subsidies for rooftop solar and 90% of subsidies for electric vehicles. This paper finds that heat pumps are an important exception. Using newly available U.S. nationally representative data, the paper finds that there is remarkably little correlation between heat pump adoption and household income. Nationwide, 14% of U.S. households have a heat pump as their primary heating equipment, and adoption levels are essentially identical for all income levels ranging from the bottom of the income distribution (<$30,000 annually) to the top ($150,000+). Instead, the paper shows that heat pump adoption is strongly correlated with geography, climate, and electricity prices.

中文摘要

对低碳技术补贴的一个担忧是，这些补贴往往主要流向高收入家庭。例如，先前的研究表明，收入最高的五分之一人群获得了屋顶太阳能补贴的60%和电动汽车补贴的90%。本文发现，热泵是一个重要的例外。利用新获得的美国全国代表性数据，本文发现热泵采用与家庭收入之间的相关性非常小。在全美范围内，14%的美国家庭将热泵作为主要供暖设备，从收入分布底层（年收入低于3万美元）到顶层（年收入15万美元以上），所有收入水平的采用率基本相同。相反，本文表明，热泵采用与地理位置、气候和电价密切相关。

No. 7NBER w31343

The Elusive Quest for Disarmed Peace: Contest Games and International Relations

寻求解除武装的和平：竞争博弈与国际关系

Johann Caro-Burnett, Sebastian Galiani, Gustavo Torrens

Programs：DEV；JEL：F5

Abstract

This paper extends the canonical security competition one-shot game by developing a dynamic framework where states can invest in a technology to eliminate their rival. The model includes a settlement stage for negotiation, stochastic contestable resources, and it assumes diplomacy never fails–so payoff-dominated equilibria are not played. Within this setting, we fully characterize equilibrium behavior for all discount factors, comparing cases with high and low elimination costs. The dynamic structure reveals why, even when states coordinate on the best possible equilibrium outcome, long-lasting disarmed peace is rare. By combining the escalation of military capabilities with the constraining implications of effective diplomacy, the model rationalizes the persistent cycles of peace, arms races, and conflict observed in history. Our approach identifies strategic mechanisms that restrict the sustainability of disarmament and clarifies the conditions under which arms races or conflict are inevitable. These findings offer a deeper understanding of the recurrent nature of international conflict under repeated interaction.

中文摘要

本文通过构建一个动态框架扩展了经典的安全竞争一次性博弈，在该框架中，国家可以投资于消除对手的技术。该模型包含一个用于谈判的解决阶段、随机可争夺资源，并假设外交永远不会失败——因此，支付占优的均衡不会被采用。在此设定下，我们完整刻画了所有贴现因子下的均衡行为，比较了高消除成本和低消除成本的情况。动态结构揭示了为什么即使各国协调出可能的最佳均衡结果，持久的解除武装和平也极为罕见。通过将军事能力的升级与有效外交的约束性影响相结合，该模型解释了历史上观察到的和平、军备竞赛和冲突的持续循环。我们的方法识别了限制裁军可持续性的战略机制，并阐明了军备竞赛或冲突不可避免的条件。这些发现为理解重复互动下国际冲突的反复性提供了更深入的见解。

No. 8NBER w31342

Pandemic-Era Changes to Medicaid Enrollment and Funding: Implications for Future Policy and Research

疫情时期医疗补助注册与资助的变化：对未来政策与研究的影响

Laura Dague, Benjamin D. Ukert

Programs：EH；JEL：H7, I13, I18, I38

Abstract

The COVID-19 related public health emergency led to federal legislation that changed the landscape of Medicaid coverage for low-income people in the United States. Beginning in 2020, policy responses led to a surge in Medicaid enrollment due to federal rules preventing Medicaid disenrollment, and total Medicaid enrollment increased more from January 2020-2023 than the net increase in insurance coverage from 2013-2017 following the Affordable Care Act’s implementation. As these policies come to an end, it is crucial to understand their implications for the future of Medicaid. This paper provides an overview of Medicaid’s enrollment, renewal and funding policies, highlighting how these policies changed during and immediately following the acute phases of the COVID-19 pandemic, describes enrollment increases and their composition, reviews literature relevant to the policy and its unwinding, and identifies key areas for research. By examining this unprecedented period of Medicaid policy, we can inform future policy decisions and optimize safety net programs to be effective in a broad set of circumstances.

中文摘要

与COVID-19相关的公共卫生紧急事件导致联邦立法改变了美国低收入人群医疗补助覆盖的格局。从2020年开始，由于联邦规则禁止医疗补助注销，政策应对导致医疗补助注册人数激增，2020年1月至2023年期间医疗补助总注册人数的增长，超过了《平价医疗法案》实施后2013年至2017年保险覆盖的净增长。随着这些政策的结束，理解它们对医疗补助未来的影响至关重要。本文概述了医疗补助的注册、续期和资助政策，重点说明了这些政策在COVID-19大流行急性期及之后如何变化，描述了注册人数的增长及其构成，回顾了与政策及其退出相关的文献，并确定了关键的研究领域。通过审视这一医疗补助政策的空前时期，我们可以为未来的政策决策提供信息，并优化安全网计划，使其在广泛情况下都能有效发挥作用。

Techies and Firm Level Productivity

技术人员与企业层面生产率

James Harrigan, Ariell Reshef, Farid Toubal

Programs：IO, ITI, PR；JEL：D2, D24, O3, O33

Abstract

We study the impact of techies—engineers and other technically trained workers—on firm-level productivity. We first report new facts on the role of techies in the firm by using French administrative data and unique surveys. Techies are STEM-skill intensive and are associated with innovation, as well as with technology adoption, management, and diffusion within firms. Using structural econometric methods, we estimate the causal effect of techies on firm-level Hicks-neutral productivity in both manufacturing and non-manufacturing industries. We find that techies raise firm-level productivity, and this effect goes beyond the employment of R&D workers, extending to ICT and other techies. In non-manufacturing firms, the impact of techies on productivity operates mostly through ICT and other techies, not R&D workers. Engineers have a greater effect on productivity than technicians.

中文摘要

我们研究了技术人员——工程师和其他受过技术培训的工人——对企业层面生产率的影响。我们首先利用法国行政数据和独特调查，报告了关于技术人员在企业中作用的新事实。技术人员密集使用STEM技能，并与创新以及企业内的技术采用、管理和扩散相关联。使用结构计量经济学方法，我们估计了技术人员对制造业和非制造业企业希克斯中性生产率的因果效应。我们发现，技术人员提高了企业层面的生产率，且这种效应超出了研发工人的雇佣范围，延伸到了ICT和其他技术人员。在非制造业企业中，技术人员对生产率的影响主要通过ICT和其他技术人员实现，而非研发工人。工程师对生产率的影响大于技术员。

No. 10NBER w31340

Community-Based Health Programs and Child Vaccinations: Evidence from Madagascar

社区健康项目与儿童疫苗接种：来自马达加斯加的证据

Catalina Herrera-Almanza, Maria Rosales-Rueda

Programs：CH, DEV, EH；JEL：I12, I15, J13, O15

Abstract

Vaccinations are a cost-effective tool to prevent child mortality and morbidity; however, their access and take-up remain low in developing countries. We analyze the effects on child vaccinations of a large-scale community-based health worker program that aimed to reach remote areas distant from public health facilities in Madagascar. We identify these effects using a triple-difference design that leverages the time and geographic variation in the program rollout and the geocoded household distance to the closest health facility. Our findings indicate that, on average, the program did not improve the vaccination uptake in treated areas; however, the community health workers component had an additional effect on children’s vaccinations in the most remote areas from the closest health facility. Despite this improvement, we find that mothers’ religious affiliation might constitute a barrier for the vaccination uptake of the most remote and vulnerable children.

中文摘要

疫苗接种是预防儿童死亡和发病的具有成本效益的工具；然而，在发展中国家，其可及性和接种率仍然较低。我们分析了马达加斯加一个旨在覆盖远离公共卫生设施的偏远地区的大规模社区健康工作者项目对儿童疫苗接种的影响。我们利用三重差分设计来识别这些效应，该设计利用了项目推广的时间和地理变化，以及按地理编码计算的家庭与最近卫生设施的距离。我们的研究结果表明，平均而言，该项目并未改善处理地区的疫苗接种率；然而，社区健康工作者部分对距离最近卫生设施最偏远地区的儿童疫苗接种产生了额外影响。尽管有这种改善，但我们发现母亲的宗教信仰可能构成最偏远和最脆弱儿童接种疫苗的障碍。

No. 11NBER w31339

The 2021 Child Tax Credit, the Living Arrangements and Housing Affordability of Families with Low Incomes

2021年儿童税收抵免、低收入家庭的居住安排与住房可负担性

Natasha V. Pilkauskas, Katherine Michelmore, Nicole Kovski

Programs：CH；JEL：H20, J10, J12, J18

Abstract

Access to safe and stable housing is important for child and adult wellbeing. Yet many low-income households face severe challenges in maintaining stable housing. In this paper we examine the impact of the 2021 temporary expansion to the Child Tax Credit (CTC) on the living arrangements and housing affordability of families with low incomes. We employ a parameterized difference-in-differences method and use national data from a sample of parents who are receiving, or recently received, Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits (N~20,500). We find that the monthly CTC is associated with a higher likelihood that parents reported a change in their living arrangements as well as reduced household size, an effect largely driven by fewer mothers living with a partner (and not a reduction in doubling up). We also find that the credit reduced parents’ likelihood of reporting potential moves due to difficulties affording rent/mortgages as well as the amount and incidence of back-owed rent/mortgages.
