Trends in Competition in the United States: What Does the Evidence Show?

美国竞争趋势：证据表明了什么？

Carl Shapiro, Ali Yurukoglu

Programs：IO；JEL：L13, L40

Abstract

Has the United States economy become less competitive in recent decades? One might think so based on a body of research that has rapidly become influential for antitrust policy. We explain that the empirical evidence relating to concentration trends, markup trends, and the effects of mergers does not actually show a widespread decline in competition. Nor does it provide a basis for dramatic changes in antitrust policy. To the contrary, in many respects the evidence indicates that the changes observed in a number of industries are likely to reflect competition in action. We highlight research that points to targeted interventions that can enable antitrust enforcement policy to better promote and protect competition. Throughout the paper, we identify open questions and opportunities for future research in the cross-industry evidence-at-scale paradigm, the industry-specific study paradigm, and their intersection.

中文摘要

美国经济在近几十年是否变得不那么具有竞争力？基于一项迅速影响反垄断政策的研究，人们可能会这样认为。我们解释，与集中度趋势、加成率趋势以及并购效应相关的实证证据实际上并未显示竞争普遍下降，也未为反垄断政策的剧烈变革提供依据。相反，在许多方面，证据表明在多个行业中观察到的变化很可能反映了竞争的实际运作。我们重点介绍了那些指向针对性干预措施的研究，这些措施可以使反垄断执法政策更好地促进和保护竞争。在整篇论文中，我们指出了跨行业大规模证据范式、特定行业研究范式及其交叉领域中尚未解决的问题和未来研究的机会。

No. 2NBER w32761

How Much Will Global Warming Cool Global Growth?

全球变暖将在多大程度上冷却全球增长？

Ishan B. Nath, Valerie A. Ramey, Peter J. Klenow

Programs：EEE, EFG；JEL：E01, O44

Abstract

Does a permanent rise in temperature decrease the level or growth rate of GDP in affected countries? Differing answers to this question lead prominent estimates of climate damages to diverge by an order of magnitude. This paper combines indirect evidence on economic growth with new empirical estimates of the dynamic effects of temperature on GDP to argue that warming has persistent, but not permanent, effects on growth. We start by presenting a range of evidence that technology flows tether country growth rates together, preventing temperature changes from causing growth rates to diverge permanently. We then use data from a panel of countries to show that temperature shocks have large and persistent effects on GDP, driven in part by persistence in temperature itself. These estimates imply projected future impacts that are three to five times larger than level effect estimates and two to four times smaller than permanent growth effect estimates, with larger discrepancies for initially hot and cold countries.

中文摘要

气温的永久性上升是否会降低受影响国家的GDP水平或增长率？对这个问题的不同回答导致对气候损害的主要估计相差一个数量级。本文结合关于经济增长的间接证据与温度对GDP动态影响的新实证估计，论证了变暖对增长具有持续性但非永久性的影响。我们首先提出一系列证据，表明技术流动将各国增长率联系在一起，阻止温度变化导致增长率永久性分化。然后，我们使用跨国面板数据表明，温度冲击对GDP具有巨大且持续的影响，这部分是由温度本身的持续性驱动的。这些估计得出的未来影响预测是水平效应估计的三到五倍，是永久增长效应估计的二到四分之一，且对于最初炎热和寒冷国家之间的差异更大。

Limits on Regret as a Tool for Incentive Design

后悔作为激励设计工具的限制

Felipe Araujo, Alex Imas, Alistair Wilson

Programs：DEV, LS；JEL：D0, D03

Abstract

We demonstrate the pitfalls when extrapolating behavioral findings across different contexts and decision environments. We focus on regret theory and the use of “regret lotteries” for motivating behavior change. Here, findings from one-shot settings have been used to promote regret as a tool to boost incentives in recurrent decisions across many settings. Using theory and experiments, we replicate regret lotteries as the superior one-shot incentive; however, for repeated decisions the comparative static is entirely reversed. Moreover, the effects are extremely sensitive to details of regret implementation. Our results suggest caution should be used when designing incentive schemes that exploit regret.

中文摘要

我们展示了在不同情境和决策环境中推断行为发现时的陷阱。我们聚焦于后悔理论和利用“后悔彩票”来激励行为改变。在此，一次性情境中的发现已被用来推广后悔作为增强许多情境中重复决策激励的工具。通过理论和实验，我们复制了后悔彩票作为优越的一次性激励；然而，对于重复决策，比较静态完全逆转。此外，这些效应对后悔实施细节极为敏感。我们的结果表明，在设计利用后悔的激励方案时应谨慎。

Estimating the Potential Impact of Combined Race and Ethnicity Reporting on Long-Term Earnings Statistics

估计种族与族裔合并报告对长期收入统计的潜在影响

Kevin L. McKinney, John M. Abowd

Programs：LS；JEL：J15, J31

Abstract

We use place of birth information from the Social Security Administration linked to earnings data from the Longitudinal Employer-Household Dynamics Program and detailed race and ethnicity data from the 2010 Census to study how long-term earnings differentials vary by place of birth for different self-identified race and ethnicity categories. We focus on foreign-born persons from countries that are heavily Hispanic and from countries in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA). We find substantial heterogeneity of long-term earnings differentials within country of birth, some of which will be difficult to detect when the reporting format changes from the current two-question version to the new single-question version because they depend on self-identifications that place the individual in two distinct categories within the single-question format, specifically, Hispanic and White or Black, and MENA and White or Black. We also study the USA-born children of these same immigrants. Long-term earnings differences for the 2nd generation also vary as a function of self-identified ethnicity and race in ways that changing to the single-question format could affect.

中文摘要

我们利用社会保障管理局的出生地信息，结合纵向雇主-家庭动态计划的收入数据以及2010年人口普查详细的种族和族裔数据，研究对于不同自我认同的种族和族裔类别，长期收入差异如何因出生地而异。我们重点关注来自高度西班牙裔国家以及中东和北非国家的国外出生者。我们发现，在出生国内部，长期收入差异存在显著的异质性，其中一些差异在报告格式从当前的双问题版本变为新的单问题版本时将难以检测，因为它们依赖于自我认同，这种认同在单问题格式中将个体置于两个不同的类别中，具体而言，西班牙裔与白人或黑人，以及中东和北非裔与白人或黑人。我们还研究了这些移民在美国出生的子女。第二代人的长期收入差异也因自我认同的族裔和种族而异，其方式可能受到改为单问题格式的影响。

No. 5NBER w32757

Quantitative Tightening with Slow-Moving Capital

缓慢移动资本下的量化紧缩

Zhengyang Jiang, Jialu Sun

Programs：AP, IFM, ME；JEL：E5, G12

Abstract

We document shifts in investor composition during quantitative tightening, which suggest that investors adjust their portfolios at different speeds. To understand its implications for bond valuation, we develop a general equilibrium model which highlights the dynamic interaction between heterogeneous investors. In the model, long-term investors have higher risk-taking capacity, but face a portfolio adjustment cost; liquidity traders have lower risk-taking capacity, but can trade freely. Our model predicts a novel overshooting pattern: when the central bank unwinds its bond purchase, slow adjustment by long-term investors requires liquidity traders to absorb the imbalance, who demand a higher risk premium that creates excessive bond price decline and volatility in the short run. As a result, quantitative tightening is not simply a symmetric reversal of quantitative easing.

中文摘要

我们记录了量化紧缩期间投资者构成的转变，这表明投资者以不同速度调整其投资组合。为了理解其对债券估值的影响，我们开发了一个一般均衡模型，该模型强调了异质性投资者之间的动态互动。在该模型中，长期投资者具有更高的风险承担能力，但面临投资组合调整成本；流动性交易者风险承担能力较低，但可以自由交易。我们的模型预测了一种新颖的过度调整模式：当中央银行缩减其债券购买时，长期投资者的缓慢调整要求流动性交易者吸收失衡，后者要求更高的风险溢价，从而在短期内造成过度的债券价格下跌和波动。因此，量化紧缩并非量化宽松的简单对称逆转。

No. 6NBER w32756

Elephants in Equity Markets

股票市场中的大象

Hélène Rey, Adrien Rousset Planat, Vania Stavrakeva, Jenny Tang

Programs：AP, IFM；JEL：F30, G11, G23

Abstract

We introduce a novel empirical market-clearing–based decomposition of equity price growth. Using sample holdings covering only an average of 6% of market capitalization, we account for, on average, 88% of time variation in over 22,000 individual stock prices and 95% of fluctuations in 33 aggregate stock indices. Changes in portfolio weights contribute most to the variance of individual stock prices, while “safe haven” markets feature strong cross-stock substitution patterns that leave aggregate portfolio weight changes uncorrelated with aggregate indices. Equity markets are global and exchange rates play a key equilibrating role. We find that the behavior of active funds’ portfolio managers has a causal effect on stock prices by transmitting firm-specific news. Similarly, final funds’ investors play a key role in transmitting macroeconomic and risk aversion news to stock prices.

中文摘要

我们引入了一种新颖的基于实证市场出清的股票价格增长分解方法。使用仅覆盖平均6%市值的样本持仓，我们平均解释了超过22,000只个股价格时间变动的88%，以及33个股票综合指数波动的95%。投资组合权重的变化对个股价格方差贡献最大，而“避风港”市场则表现出强烈的跨股票替代模式，使得总体投资组合权重变化与综合指数不相关。股票市场是全球性的，汇率起着关键的均衡作用。我们发现，主动型基金投资组合经理的行为通过传递公司特定新闻对股票价格产生因果影响。同样，最终基金投资者在将宏观经济和风险厌恶新闻传递至股票价格方面发挥着关键作用。

No. 7NBER w32755

Mobilizing the Manpower of Mothers: Childcare under the Lanham Act during WWII

动员母亲劳动力：二战期间《兰哈姆法案》下的儿童保育

Joseph P. Ferrie, Claudia Goldin, Claudia Olivetti

Programs：CH, DAE, LS；JEL：J21, N32, N42

Abstract

The Lanham Act was a federal infrastructure bill passed by Congress in 1940 and eventually used to fund programs for the preschool and school-aged children of working women during WWII. It remains, to this day, the only example in US history of an (almost) universal, largely federally supported childcare program. We explore its role in enabling and increasing the labor supply of mothers during WWII using information on the program, war contracts, and employment at the city level. Use of Lanham Act funds for a wartime childcare program was initially controversial. However, the program was eventually well funded per child in average daily attendance and provided generally high-quality care. But it was late to start, limited in scope, and incapable of greatly increasing women’s employment in the aggregate. Childcare facilities were funded more in places that already had higher participation rates of mothers and where the wartime need was the greatest. The impact on the children served is still to be determined.

中文摘要

《兰哈姆法案》是国会于1940年通过的一项联邦基础设施法案，最终用于资助二战期间职业女性的学龄前和学龄儿童保育项目。至今，它仍是美国历史上唯一一个（几乎）普及的、主要由联邦支持的儿童保育项目的例子。我们利用该项目的相关信息、战时合同以及城市层面的就业数据，探讨其在二战期间促进和增加母亲劳动力供给方面的作用。使用《兰哈姆法案》资金用于战时儿童保育项目最初存在争议。然而，该项目最终按日均出勤人数计算，每个儿童获得了充足的资金，并提供了普遍高质量的保育服务。但它启动较晚、范围有限，且无法大幅提高总体女性就业率。儿童保育设施更多地是在那些母亲劳动参与率已经较高以及战时需求最大的地方获得资助。对受保育儿童的影响仍有待确定。

No. 8NBER w32754

Filtering with Limited Information

有限信息下的滤波

Thorsten Drautzburg, Jesús Fernández-Villaverde, Pablo A. Guerrón-Quintana, Dick Oosthuizen

Programs：EFG；JEL：C32, C53, E37, E44, O11

Abstract

We propose a new tool to filter non-linear dynamic models that does not require the researcher to specify the model fully and can be implemented without solving the model. If two conditions are satisfied, we can use a flexible statistical model and a known measurement equation to back out the hidden states of the dynamic model. The first condition is that the state is sufficiently volatile or persistent to be recoverable. The second condition requires the possibly non-linear measurement to be sufficiently smooth and to map uniquely to the state absent measurement error. We illustrate the method through various simulation studies and an empirical application to a sudden stops model applied to Mexican data.

中文摘要

我们提出了一种新的工具来滤波非线性动态模型，该工具不需要研究者完全指定模型，并且可以在不求解模型的情况下实施。如果满足两个条件，我们可以使用灵活的统计模型和已知的测量方程来反推出动态模型的隐藏状态。第一个条件是状态变量具有足够的波动性或持续性以便可恢复。第二个条件要求可能非线性的测量函数足够平滑，并且在无测量误差的情况下唯一地映射到状态变量。我们通过各种模拟研究以及一个应用于墨西哥数据的突然停止模型实证应用来说明该方法。

No. 9NBER w32753

Constructing BEA Highways and Streets Net Wealth Stocks with Detailed Types of Investment and Engineering-based Estimates of Depreciation

构建BEA公路与街道净财富存量：基于详细投资类型与工程折旧估算

Barbara M. Fraumeni, Robert Kornfeld

Programs：PE, PR；JEL：E01

Abstract

This paper builds on a previous paper by the authors (Kornfeld and Fraumeni, 2022) that primarily used U.S. Federal Highway Administration Highway Statistics data to disaggregate investment in highways and streets into more detailed types to produce updated estimates of net wealth stocks and depreciation. In this paper, major components of highways and streets other than pavement: grading, bridges and other structures, traffic management, safety, and environmental, are set equal to comprehensive revision updated versions of those derived in the earlier paper. All capital outlays, including those for pavement, and non-pavement net wealth stocks, are controlled to current BEA estimates. The engineering-based depreciation patterns are very dissimilar to the BEA patterns. The engineering-based net wealth pavement stock depreciation patterns fall from an efficiency level of about 0.055 to zero after 20 years of life; the BEA Hulten-Wykoff-based net wealth pavement stock depreciation patterns are at approximately the same efficiency level after 62 years, with a positive efficiency level continuing to infinity. BEA adopted Hulten-Wykoff default depreciation rates in the absence of other information (Fraumeni, 1997), however research by Fraumeni established engineering-based Picher pavement depreciation rates for highways and streets (Fraumeni, 1999, 2007).

中文摘要

本文在作者前期论文（Kornfeld and Fraumeni, 2022）基础上展开，该前期论文主要利用美国联邦公路管理局《公路统计》数据，将公路与街道投资细分为更详细的类型，以更新净财富存量与折旧的估算。本文中，除路面外的公路与街道主要组成部分——路基、桥梁及其他结构、交通管理、安全与环境——均设定为与前期论文推导结果经全面修订更新后的版本一致。所有资本支出（包括路面支出）及非路面净财富存量均受当前BEA估算值的控制。基于工程的折旧模式与BEA模式差异显著。基于工程的路面净财富存量折旧模式在20年使用寿命后从约0.055的效率水平降至零；而基于Hulten-Wykoff方法的BEA路面净财富存量折旧模式在62年后仍保持大致相同的效率水平，且正效率水平持续至无穷。BEA在缺乏其他信息时采用了Hulten-Wykoff默认折旧率（Fraumeni, 1997），但Fraumeni的研究已为公路与街道确立了基于工程的Picher路面折旧率（Fraumeni, 1999, 2007）。

No. 10NBER w32752

Adjusting Labor Along The Intensive Margins

劳动集约边际的调整

Daniel S. Hamermesh, Jeff Biddle

Programs：LS；JEL：E24, J21, J23

Abstract

We expand the analysis of cyclical changes in labor demand by decomposing changes along the intensive margin into those in days/week and in hours/day. Using large cross sections of U.S. data, 1985-2018, we observe around 1/4 of the adjustment in weekly hours occurring through changing days/week. There is no adjustment of days/week in manufacturing; but 1/3 of the adjustment outside manufacturing occurs through days/week. The desirability of bunched leisure implies that secular shifts away from manufacturing have contributed to increasing economic welfare.

中文摘要

我们通过将集约边际的变化分解为每周工作天数和每天工作小时数，扩展了对劳动力需求周期性变化的分析。利用1985-2018年美国大规模横截面数据，我们观察到每周工作小时数调整中约有四分之一是通过改变每周工作天数实现的。制造业中不存在每周工作天数的调整；但制造业以外部门中，三分之一的调整是通过每周工作天数实现的。集中休闲的合意性表明，远离制造业的长期结构性转变有助于提高经济福利。

No. 11NBER w32751

Social Insurance Spillovers: Evidence From Paid Sick Leave Mandates and Workers' Compensation

社会保险的溢出效应：来自带薪病假强制令与工伤保险的证据

Xiuming Dong, Johanna Catherine Maclean, David Powell

Programs：AG, EH, LE, LS, PE；JEL：I18, J28, J32

Abstract

Social insurance programs shield individuals from specific risks, but these programs are not necessarily independent of each other. The existence and scope of one program can potentially influence the use of others, especially when the risks covered by the programs are related. This study investigates the relationship between two mandated benefit programs in the United States: state paid sick leave (PSL) mandates and workers’ compensation. Unlike most developed countries, the U.S. lacks a federal PSL mandate; however, 15 states have implemented such policies. PSL mandates require firms to provide compensated time off for employee health-related needs, while workers’ compensation offers benefits to help workers recover from workplace injuries or illnesses. Using a difference-in-differences analysis, the study explores the impact of state PSL mandates on the usage of workers’ compensation benefits. The findings reveal meaningful spillover effects: when states adopt PSL requirements, there is a decrease in workers’ compensation benefit receipt. While some evidence suggests possible improvements in health, there are no observed reductions in workplace injury rates specifically, indicating that workers may substitute PSL benefits directly for workers’ compensation.

中文摘要

社会保险计划保护个人免受特定风险，但这些计划之间并非相互独立。一个计划的存在和范围可能影响其他计划的使用，尤其是当这些计划覆盖的风险相互关联时。本研究探讨了美国两项强制性福利计划之间的关系：州级带薪病假强制令与工伤保险。与大多数发达国家不同，美国缺乏联邦层面的带薪病假强制令；然而，已有15个州实施了此类政策。带薪病假强制令要求企业为员工因健康需求提供带薪休假，而工伤保险则为工人因工伤或职业病恢复提供福利。通过双重差分分析，本研究考察了州级带薪病假强制令对工伤保险福利使用的影响。研究结果揭示了显著的溢出效应：当各州实施带薪病假要求时，工伤保险福利的领取有所减少。虽然部分证据表明健康状况可能有所改善，但并未观察到工伤率的特定下降，这表明工人可能直接用带薪病假福利替代了工伤保险。

No. 12NBER w32750

The Micro and Macro Productivity of Nations

国家的微观与宏观生产率

Stephen Ayerst, Duc M. Nguyen, Diego Restuccia

Programs：DEV, EFG, PR；JEL：O11, O14, O4

Abstract

We examine aggregate productivity differences across nations using cross-country firm-level data and a quantitative model of production heterogeneity with distortions featuring operation decisions (selection) and productivity-enhancing investments (technology). Empirically, less developed countries feature higher distortions and larger dispersion in firm-level productivity, mostly resulting from the higher prevalence of unproductive firms. Pace of Adoption of Alternatives to Animal-Source Foods and Climate Goals

动物源食品替代品的采用速度与气候目标

Galina Hale, Vlad Onescu, Ritesh Bhangale

Programs：EEE, IO；JEL：L66, Q11, Q18, Q54

Abstract

The global food system is an important contributor to greenhouse gas emissions that lead to climate change. Animal agriculture is responsible for a large share of the food-system emissions, both directly and through the production of animal feed. Limiting global warming to the goals set forth by the international community will not be possible without rapid phasing out of a substantial share of animal-source food. We show that the rapid adoption of alternatives to animal-source foods, such as plant-only diet, or plant-based, cultured, or fermentation-derived analogs to animal products, can be consistent with climate goals. Importantly, the longer the delay in the adoption of alternatives, the larger the share of diet they will have to represent in 2050 for the food system to stay within its carbon budget.

Family Planning, Now and Later: Infertility Fears and Contraceptive Take-Up

计划生育：现在与未来——不孕恐惧与避孕措施采用

Natalie Bau, David J. Henning, Corinne Low, Bryce Steinberg

Programs：CH, DEV, EH, LS；JEL：I10, I12, J10, J13, O10, O12

Abstract

Early fertility is thought to be one of the key barriers to female human capital attainment in sub-Saharan Africa, yet contraceptive take-up remains puzzlingly low among women in critical periods for human capital investment. We study a barrier to hormonal contraceptive uptake among young, nulliparious women that, while recognized in the qualitative literature, has not been causally tested: the persistent (incorrect) belief — grounded in medical mistrust and an adverse history of population control policies — that these contraceptives cause later infertility. This belief creates a perceived tradeoff between current and future reproductive control. We use a randomized controlled trial with female undergraduates at the flagship university in Zambia to test two interventions to increase contraceptive use. Despite high rates of sexual activity, low rates of condom-use, and near zero desire for current pregnancy, only 5% of this population uses hormonal contraceptives at baseline. Providing a voucher to visit a local clinic — greatly reducing access costs — only temporarily increases contraceptive use. However, pairing this transfer with a carefully-designed treatment to change persistent incorrect beliefs that contraceptives cause infertility doubles the initial effect, persistently increases contraceptive take-up over 6 months, and reduces reported pregnancies by as much as 71%. This treatment reduces self-reports that contraceptives cause infertility, and compliers are more likely to cite fear of infertility as the reason for not using contraceptives at baseline. IV estimates indicate that eliminating the belief that contraceptives cause infertility would more than triple contraceptive use.

中文摘要

早期生育被认为是撒哈拉以南非洲女性人力资本积累的关键障碍之一，然而在人力资本投资关键时期的女性中，避孕措施采用率仍然低得令人费解。我们研究了年轻未育女性采用激素避孕的一个障碍，该障碍在定性文献中已得到认可，但尚未经过因果检验：一种持续存在的（错误）信念——基于医疗不信任和人口控制政策的不利历史——认为这些避孕措施会导致日后不孕。这种信念造成了当前生育控制与未来生育控制之间的感知权衡。我们利用在赞比亚旗舰大学对女本科生进行的一项随机对照试验，测试了两种旨在提高避孕措施使用率的干预措施。尽管性活跃率高、安全套使用率低且当前怀孕意愿近乎为零，但该人群中只有5%在基线时使用激素避孕。提供前往当地诊所的优惠券——大幅降低了获取成本——仅能暂时提高避孕措施使用率。然而，将这种转移支付与一项精心设计的、旨在改变避孕措施导致不孕这一持续错误信念的治疗相结合，会使初始效果翻倍，在6个月内持续提高避孕措施采用率，并将报告怀孕率降低高达71%。这种治疗减少了自我报告认为避孕措施导致不孕的情况，并且依从者在基线时更可能将不孕恐惧列为不使用避孕措施的原因。工具变量估计表明，消除避孕措施导致不孕的信念将使避孕措施使用率增加两倍以上。
