No. 1NBER w31222

Generative AI and Firm Values

生成式人工智能与企业价值

Andrea L. Eisfeldt, Gregor Schubert, Miao Ben Zhang

Programs：AP, CF, EFG；JEL：E0, G0

Abstract

How do recent advances in Generative AI affect firm value? We construct the first measure of firms’ workforce exposures to Generative AI and show that an “Artificial-Minus-Human” (AMH) portfolio earned 5% in the two weeks following the release of ChatGPT. The labor-exposure effect is more pronounced for firms with greater data assets and is distinct from the effect of firms’ product exposures to AI. We assess whether exposed workforces are substituted or complemented by Generative AI based on whether their exposed tasks are core or supplemental. Examining firms’ labor demand and profitability following the release of ChatGPT supports a labor-technology substitution channel.

中文摘要

生成式人工智能的最新进展如何影响企业价值？我们构建了首个衡量企业劳动力对生成式人工智能暴露程度的指标，并表明在ChatGPT发布后的两周内，“人工减人类”（AMH）投资组合获得了5%的收益。劳动力暴露效应在数据资产较多的企业中更为显著，并且与企业产品对人工智能的暴露效应不同。我们根据暴露任务是核心还是辅助，评估暴露的劳动力是被生成式人工智能替代还是互补。考察ChatGPT发布后企业的劳动力需求和盈利能力，支持了劳动力-技术替代渠道。

No. 2NBER w31221

The Unequal Economic Consequences of Carbon Pricing

碳定价的不平等经济后果

Diego R. Känzig

Programs：EEE, EFG, ME；JEL：E32, E62, H23, Q54, Q58

Abstract

This paper studies the economic impacts of carbon pricing. Exploiting institutional features of the European carbon market and high-frequency data, I identify carbon policy shocks and trace their dynamic effects. A restrictive carbon policy shock raises energy prices, reduces emissions, spurs green innovation, but decreases economic activity—disproportionately burdening poorer households. Not only are the poor more affected because of their higher energy spending, but they also experience larger income losses. These indirect, general-equilibrium effects via income and employment play an important role in the transmission of carbon pricing policies, accounting for about two-thirds of the aggregate consumption response.

中文摘要

本文研究碳定价的经济影响。利用欧洲碳市场的制度特征和高频数据，我识别出碳政策冲击并追踪其动态效应。限制性碳政策冲击会提高能源价格、减少排放、刺激绿色创新，但会降低经济活动——不成比例地加重贫困家庭的负担。贫困家庭不仅因其更高的能源支出而受到更大影响，而且他们还面临更大的收入损失。这些通过收入和就业产生的间接一般均衡效应在碳定价政策的传导中扮演重要角色，约占总体消费反应的三分之二。

No. 3NBER w31220

The Incidence of the Affordable Care Act’s Dependent Coverage Mandate

《平价医疗法案》受抚养人覆盖强制令的归宿

Sherry A. Glied, Hansoo Ko

Programs：EH, LS；JEL：H29, I13, J33

Abstract

Economic theory and empirical studies conclude that the cost of voluntary employer-sponsored health insurance falls on employees. However, the distribution of incidence and the mechanism through which incidence occurs have not been well-established. We provide new evidence about incidence by examining the dependent coverage mandate in the ACA, which requires group insurance to allow adult children to age 26 to remain on their parents’ policies. We establish that the incidence of the mandate fell on covered employees as a group. We then consider three situations in which the benefits of this new coverage to an employee differ from the costs to an employer. First, we compare incidence where the young adult dependent is the youngest child in the family to the situation where the child is not the youngest (so the family could add a dependent to existing family coverage). We find that incidence falls mainly on households where the newly-eligible child is the youngest in the household. Second, higher-income households face a lower tax price of coverage than do lower-income households. We find that the incidence of the mandate falls mainly on the highest income households. Finally, we find that the mandate leads to increased commuting time for parents of newly-eligible dependents.

中文摘要

经济理论和实证研究得出结论，自愿性雇主提供的健康保险的成本由员工承担。然而，归宿的分布以及归宿发生的机制尚未得到充分证实。我们通过研究《平价医疗法案》中的受抚养人覆盖强制令来提供关于归宿的新证据，该强制令要求团体保险允许成年子女在26岁前继续留在父母的保单中。我们确定该强制令的归宿落在了受保员工这个群体上。然后，我们考虑了三种情况，在这些情况下，这种新覆盖对员工的好处与雇主的成本不同。首先，我们比较了当年轻成年受抚养人是家庭中最年幼的孩子与孩子并非最年幼（因此家庭可以将受抚养人添加到现有的家庭覆盖中）时的归宿。我们发现，该强制令主要落在新符合条件的子女是家庭中最年幼子女的家庭上。其次，高收入家庭面临的保险税收价格低于低收入家庭。我们发现，该强制令的归宿主要落在最高收入家庭上。最后，我们发现该强制令导致新符合条件的受抚养人的父母通勤时间增加。

No. 4NBER w31219

An Alternative to the Basic Causal Requirement for Liability under the Negligence Rule

过失规则下责任基本因果要求的替代方案

Steven Shavell

Programs：LE；JEL：K1, K13

Abstract

The primary causal requirement that must be met for a negligent party to be held liable for a harm is a demonstration that the harm would not have occurred if the party had not been negligent. Thus, for a speeding driver to be found liable for harm done in a car accident, it must be shown that the accident would not have happened if the driver had obeyed the speed limit. The main point made here is that this basic causal requirement may be difficult to satisfy and hence may interfere with the discouragement of negligence. Therefore, an alternative and usually easier-to-meet causal requirement is proposed—that the harm would not have occurred if the party not been engaged in his activity (if the driver had not been driving).

中文摘要

要使过失方对损害承担责任，必须满足的主要因果要求是证明如果该方没有过失，损害就不会发生。因此，要让超速驾驶的司机对车祸造成的损害承担责任，必须证明如果司机遵守了限速规定，事故就不会发生。这里提出的主要观点是，这种基本的因果要求可能难以满足，因此可能妨碍对过失的威慑。因此，提出了一种替代的且通常更容易满足的因果要求——即如果该方没有从事其活动（如果司机没有开车），损害就不会发生。

No. 5NBER w31218

Inflation and GDP Dynamics in Production Networks: A Sufficient Statistics Approach

生产网络中的通胀与GDP动态：一个充分统计量方法

Hassan Afrouzi, Saroj Bhattarai

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：C67, E32, E52

Abstract

We derive closed-form solutions and sufficient statistics for inflation and GDP dynamics in multi-sector New Keynesian economies with arbitrary input-output linkages. Analytically, we show how (1) production linkages amplify inflation and GDP persistence in response to monetary and sectoral shocks and (2) monetary policies that stabilize price indices or the GDP gap affect shock propagation. Quantitatively, sectors with large input-output adjusted price stickiness have disproportionate effects relative to their GDP shares: The three sectors with the highest contribution to the persistence of aggregate inflation have GDP shares of around zero but explain 16% of monetary non-neutrality.

中文摘要

我们在具有任意投入产出关联的多部门新凯恩斯主义经济体中推导出通胀和GDP动态的闭式解和充分统计量。在分析上，我们展示了（1）生产关联如何放大货币和部门冲击下的通胀和GDP持续性，以及（2）稳定价格指数或GDP缺口的货币政策如何影响冲击传播。在数量上，具有较大投入产出调整后价格粘性的部门相对于其GDP份额具有不成比例的影响：对总体通胀持续性贡献最大的三个部门的GDP份额接近于零，但却解释了16%的货币非中性。

No. 6NBER w31217

The People and the Experts

民众与专家

William D. Nordhaus, Douglas Rivers

Programs：EFG, PE；JEL：C8, E7, G53

Abstract

Are speculators driving up oil prices? Should we raise energy prices to slow global warming? The present study takes a small number of such questions and compares the views of economic experts with those of the public. This comparison uses a panel of more than 2000 respondents from YouGov with the views of the panel of experts from the Initiative on Global Markets at the Chicago Booth School. We found that most of the US population is at best modestly informed about major economic questions and policies. The low level of knowledge is generally associated with the intrusion of ideological, political, and religious views that challenge or deny the current economic consensus. The intruding factors are highly heterogeneous across questions and sub-populations and are much more diverse than the narrowness of public political discourse would suggest. Many of these findings have been established for scientific subjects, but they appear to be equally important for economic views.

中文摘要

投机者是否在推高油价？我们是否应该提高能源价格以减缓全球变暖？本研究选取少量此类问题，比较了经济学专家的观点与公众的观点。这一比较使用了来自YouGov的2000多名受访者组成的样本，以及芝加哥大学布斯商学院全球市场倡议专家小组的观点。我们发现，大多数美国民众对主要经济问题和政策的了解充其量只是中等水平。低水平的知识通常与意识形态、政治和宗教观点的侵入有关，这些观点挑战或否定了当前的经济共识。这些侵入因素在不同问题和子群体之间高度异质，并且比狭隘的公共政治话语所暗示的要多样化得多。这些发现中有许多已在科学主题中得到证实，但它们对于经济观点似乎同样重要。

No. 7NBER w31216

Financial Crises and the Global Supply Network: Evidence from Multinational Enterprises

金融危机与全球供应网络：来自跨国企业的证据

Sergi Basco, Giulia Felice, Bruno Merlevede, Martí Mestieri

Programs：ITI；JEL：F14, F23, F44, L22, L23

Abstract

This paper empirically examines the effects of financial crises on the organization of production of multinational enterprises. We construct a panel of European multinational networks from 2003 through 2015. We use as a financial shock the increase in risk premia between August 2007 and July 2012 and build a multinational-specific shock based on the network structure before the shock. Multinationals facing a larger financial shock perform worse in terms of revenue, employment, and growth in the number of affiliates. Lower growth in the number of affiliates operates through a negative effect on domestic and foreign affiliates, and is concentrated in affiliates in a vertical relationship with the parent. These effects built up slowly over time. Negative effects are driven by multinationals with initially more leveraged parents, who reduce relatively more the number of foreign affiliates. These findings lend support to the hypothesis of financial frictions shaping multinational activity.

中文摘要

本文实证检验了金融危机对跨国企业生产组织的影响。我们构建了2003年至2015年欧洲跨国网络的样本。我们使用2007年8月至2012年7月期间风险溢价的上升作为金融冲击，并根据冲击前的网络结构构建了一个跨国企业特定的冲击。面临更大金融冲击的跨国企业在收入、就业和子公司数量增长方面表现更差。子公司数量增长放缓是通过对国内外子公司的负面影响实现的，并且集中在与母公司存在垂直关系的子公司中。这些效应随时间缓慢累积。负面效应是由最初杠杆率较高的母公司推动的，这些母公司相对更多地减少了外国子公司的数量。这些发现支持了金融摩擦塑造跨国企业活动的假设。

No. 8NBER w31215

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

多样性、公平与包容

Alex Edmans, Caroline Flammer, Simon Glossner

Programs：CF, LS；JEL：D63, G12, G32, J53, J71, J81

Abstract

This paper measures diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) using proprietary data on survey responses used to compile the Best Companies to Work For list. We identify 13 of the 58 questions as being related to DEI, and aggregate the responses to form our DEI measure. This variable has low correlation with gender and ethnic diversity in the boardroom, in senior management, and within the workforce, suggesting that DEI captures additional dimensions missing from traditional measures of demographic diversity. DEI is also unrelated to general workplace policies and practices, suggesting that DEI cannot be improved by generic initiatives. However, DEI is higher in small growth firms and firms with high financial strength. DEI is associated with higher future accounting performance across a range of measures, higher future earnings surprises, and higher valuation ratios, but demographic diversity is not. DEI perceptions among professional workers, such as R&D employees, are significantly correlated with the number and quality of patents. However, DEI exhibits no link with future stock returns.

中文摘要

本文使用用于编制“最佳工作公司”榜单的调查回复专有数据来衡量多样性、公平与包容（DEI）。我们从58个问题中识别出13个与DEI相关的问题，并汇总回复以形成我们的DEI衡量指标。该变量与董事会、高级管理层以及员工队伍中的性别和种族多样性相关性较低，这表明DEI捕捉到了传统人口多样性衡量指标所缺失的额外维度。DEI也与一般工作场所政策和实践无关，这表明DEI无法通过通用举措得到改善。然而，DEI在小型成长型公司和财务实力强的公司中更高。DEI与一系列指标衡量的未来更高会计业绩、更高的未来盈利惊喜以及更高的估值比率相关，但人口多样性则不然。研发员工等专业工作者对DEI的看法与专利数量和质量显著相关。然而，DEI与未来股票回报没有关联。

No. 9NBER w31214

Learning, Diversity and Adaptation in Changing Environments: The Role of Weak Links

变化环境中的学习、多样性与适应：弱联系的作用

Daron Acemoglu, Asuman Ozdaglar, Sarath Pattathil

Programs：IO, POL；JEL：D83, D85

Abstract

Adaptation to dynamic conditions requires a certain degree of diversity. If all agents take the best current action, learning that the underlying state has changed and behavior should adapt will be slower. Diversity is harder to maintain when there is fast communication between agents, because they tend to find out and pursue the best action rapidly. We explore these issues using a model of (Bayesian) learning over a social network. Agents learn rapidly from and may also have incentives to coordinate with others to whom they are connected via strong links. We show, however, that when the underlying environment changes sufficiently rapidly, any network consisting of just strong links will do only a little better than random choice in the long run. In contrast, networks combining strong and weak links, whereby the latter type of links transmit information only slowly, can achieve much higher long-run average payoffs. The best social networks are those that combine a large fraction of agents into a strongly-connected component, while still maintaining a sufficient number of smaller communities that make diverse choices and communicate with this component via weak links.

中文摘要

适应动态条件需要一定程度的多样性。如果所有主体都采取当前最佳行动，那么学习到潜在状态已改变且行为应调整的速度将会更慢。当主体之间沟通迅速时，多样性更难维持，因为他们倾向于快速发现并追求最佳行动。我们使用一个社交网络上的（贝叶斯）学习模型来探讨这些问题。主体可以快速学习，并且也可能有动机与通过强联系连接的其他主体协调。然而，我们表明，当潜在环境变化足够快时，任何仅由强联系组成的网络长期来看表现仅略优于随机选择。相比之下，结合强联系和弱联系的网络（后者仅缓慢传递信息）可以实现高得多的长期平均收益。最佳社交网络是那些将大部分主体整合到一个强连接组件中，同时仍保持足够数量的小社区，这些社区做出多样化选择并通过弱联系与该组件进行沟通的网络。

No. 10NBER w31213

The Labor Market Effects of Occupational Licensing in the Public Sector

公共部门职业许可的劳动力市场效应

Morris M. Kleiner, Wenchen Wang

Programs：LS；JEL：J08, J38, J44, J48, K23, K31

Abstract

In the U.S., occupational licensing is about twice as prevalent in the public sector than in the private sector. However, the influence of occupational regulation for public sector workers, and how it compares with that of private sector workers, has not been analyzed in detail. Our study examines how licensing is associated with key labor market outcomes of wages and part-time working status. Our results show that having an occupational license has positive associations on hourly wages and negative associations in both sectors on the probability of engaging in part-time work, mirroring licensing’s general relationships. When we disaggregate licensing’s associations by sector, its wage association is less in the public sector. Further, public sector licensed workers have an even lower probability of working part-time. We further examine how licensing differentially affects the wage distribution between the two sectors, and find that at the lower wage distribution, licensing’s wage associations are almost the same between the public and the private sector. The difference of licensing’s wage relationships between the two sectors becomes larger along the upper part of the wage distribution quantiles. Licensing increases the wage premia for private sector workers at the higher wage percentiles. which may make it more difficult for the public sector to attract and retain more highly-skilled workers.

中文摘要

在美国，公共部门的职业许可率大约是私营部门的两倍。然而，职业监管对公共部门工人的影响，以及它与私营部门工人的比较，尚未得到详细分析。我们的研究考察了许可如何与工资和兼职工作状态等关键劳动力市场结果相关联。我们的结果表明，拥有职业许可对小时工资有正向关联，并且在两个部门都对从事兼职工作的概率有负向关联，这反映了许可的一般关系。当我们按部门分解许可的关联时，其在公共部门的工资关联较小。此外，公共部门持证工人从事兼职工作的概率更低。我们进一步研究了许可如何对两个部门之间的工资分布产生不同影响，并发现在较低的工资分布上，许可的工资关联在公共部门和私营部门之间几乎相同。许可的工资关系在两个部门之间的差异沿着工资分布分位数的上端变得更大。许可增加了私营部门工人在较高工资百分位上的工资溢价，这可能使公共部门更难吸引和留住技能更高的工人。

No. 11NBER w31212

The Gender Well-being Gap

性别福祉差距

David G. Blanchflower, Alex Bryson

Programs：CH, LS；JEL：I30

Abstract

Given recent controversies about the existence of a gender wellbeing gap we revisit the issue estimating gender differences across 55 subjective well-being metrics – 37 positive affect and 18 negative affect – contained in 8 cross-country surveys from 167 countries across the world, two US surveys covering multiple years and a survey for Canada. We find women score more highly than men on all negative affect measures and lower than men on all but three positive affect metrics, confirming a gender wellbeing gap. The gap is apparent across countries and time and is robust to the inclusion of exogenous covariates (age, age squared, time and location fixed effects). It is also robust to conditioning on a wider set of potentially endogenous variables. However, when one examines the three ‘global’ wellbeing metrics - happiness, life satisfaction and Cantril’s Ladder - women are either similar to or ‘happier’ than men. This finding is insensitive to which controls are included and varies little over time. The difference does not seem to arise from measurement or seasonality as the variables are taken from the same surveys and frequently measured in the same way. The concern here though is that this is inconsistent with objective data where men have lower life expectancy and are more likely to die from suicide, drug overdoses and other diseases. This is the true paradox – morbidity doesn’t match mortality by gender. Women say they are less cheerful and calm, more depressed, and lonely, but happier and more satisfied with their lives, than men.

过去十年见证了数字钱包的惊人崛起，而新冠疫情进一步加速了其在全球的普及。此类电子钱包不仅提供通往外部银行账户的渠道，还提供内部支付选项，包括广受欢迎的“先买后付”（BNPL）。我们首次研究了来自一家全球领先的中国提供商（拥有超过10亿全球用户，其商业模式正被其他电子钱包提供商迅速趋同）的电子钱包交易，并匹配了商户和消费者信息。我们记录到，内部支付选项，尤其是BNPL，在线上和现场交易中均占主导地位。BNPL通过其在双边支付市场中的采用，极大地扩展了信贷的广延边际。虽然BNPL挤出了其他电子钱包支付选项，但它将金融科技信贷扩展到了服务不足的消费者。利用一项随机实验，我们还发现，通过BNPL提供的电子钱包信贷显著提升了消费者支出。然而，用户，尤其是那些将电子钱包作为唯一信贷来源的用户，在产生利息费用时会谨慎地调节借贷。这些见解很可能为那些从现金密集型社会向无现金社会转型的经济体提供信息，在这些经济体中，数字支付和金融科技信贷拥有最大的增长和市场潜力。
