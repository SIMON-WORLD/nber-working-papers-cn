English Language Requirement and Educational Inequality: Evidence from 16 Million College Applicants in China

英语要求与教育不平等：来自中国1600万高考考生的证据

Hongbin Li, Lingsheng Meng, Kai Mu, Shaoda Wang

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：F69, I24, I28

Abstract

This paper studies the unintended effect of English language requirement on educational inequality by investigating how the staggered rollout of English listening tests in China’s high-stakes National College Entrance Exam (NCEE) affected the rural-urban gap in college access. Leveraging administrative data covering the universe of NCEE participants between 1999 and 2003, we find that the introduction of English listening tests significantly lowered rural students’ exam score percentile ranks relative to their urban counterparts, resulting in a 30% increase in the rural-urban gap in college access. Our back-of-the-envelope calculations suggest that, as a result of this policy change, more than 54,000 rural students lost college seats to their urban peers between 1999 and 2003, and another 11,000 rural students who elite colleges could have admitted ended up in non-elite colleges, causing them significant future income losses.

中文摘要

本文通过研究中国高利害性高考中英语听力测试的分阶段引入如何影响大学入学的城乡差距，探讨了英语语言要求的意外效应。利用覆盖1999年至2003年间所有高考考生的行政数据，我们发现，英语听力测试的引入显著降低了农村学生相对于城市学生的考试成绩百分位排名，导致大学入学的城乡差距增加了30%。我们的粗略计算表明，由于这一政策变化，在1999年至2003年间，超过54,000名农村学生将大学名额让给了城市同龄人，另有11,000名本可被精英大学录取的农村学生最终进入了非精英大学，这给他们造成了显著的未来收入损失。

ChatGPT and Corporate Policies

ChatGPT与企业政策

Manish Jha, Jialin Qian, Michael Weber, Baozhong Yang

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, ME；JEL：C81, E22, G14, G31, G32, O33

Abstract

We create a firm-level ChatGPT investment score, based on conference calls, that measures managers' anticipated changes in capital expenditures. We validate the score with interpretable textual content and its strong correlation with CFO survey responses. The investment score predicts future capital expenditure for up to nine quarters, controlling for Tobin's q and other determinants, implying the investment score provides incremental information about firms' future investment opportunities. The investment score also separately forecasts future total, intangible, and R&D investments. High-investment-score firms experience significant negative future abnormal returns. We demonstrate ChatGPT's applicability to measure other policies, such as dividends and employment.

中文摘要

我们基于电话会议构建了一个企业层面的ChatGPT投资得分，用以衡量管理者预期的资本支出变化。我们通过可解释的文本内容以及该得分与首席财务官调查回应的强相关性来验证该得分。在控制了托宾q和其他决定因素后，该投资得分能预测未来长达九个季度的资本支出，这意味着该得分提供了关于企业未来投资机会的增量信息。该投资得分还能分别预测未来的总投资、无形资产投资和研发投资。高投资得分的企业经历了显著的未来负异常回报。我们展示了ChatGPT在衡量其他政策（如股息和就业）方面的适用性。

The Long-term Effects of Inflation on Inflation Expectations

通货膨胀对通胀预期的长期影响

Fabio Braggion, Felix von Meyerinck, Nic Schaub, Michael Weber

Programs：AG, AP, CF, ME；JEL：D14, E31, E71, G41, N14

Abstract

We study the long-term effects of inflation surges on inflation expectations. German households living in areas with higher local inflation during the hyperinflation of the 1920s expect higher inflation today, after partialling out determinants of historical inflation and current inflation expectations . Our evidence points towards transmission of inflation experiences from parents to children and through collective memory. Differential historical inflation also modulates the updating of expectations to current inflation, the response to economic policies affecting inflation, and financial decisions. We obtain similar results for Polish households residing in formerly German areas. Overall, our findings are consistent with inflationary shocks having a long-lasting impact on attitudes towards inflation.

中文摘要

我们研究了通胀飙升对通胀预期的长期影响。在控制了历史通胀和当前通胀预期的决定因素后，居住在20世纪20年代恶性通胀期间当地通胀率较高地区的德国家庭，如今预期通胀率更高。我们的证据指向通胀经历从父母向子女的传递，以及通过集体记忆的传递。历史通胀差异还会调节预期对当前通胀的更新、对影响通胀的经济政策的反应以及金融决策。我们对居住在原德国地区的波兰家庭也得到了类似的结果。总体而言，我们的发现与通胀冲击对通胀态度产生持久影响的结论一致。

No. 4NBER w32159

On Digital Currencies

论数字货币

Harald Uhlig

Programs：AP, IFM, ME；JEL：E31, E42, E44, E52, G12, G21

Abstract

I discuss private and central-bank-issued digital currencies, summarizing my prior research. I argue that prices of private digital currencies such as bitcoin follow random walks or, more generally, risk-adjusted martingales. For central bank digital currencies, I argue that they enhance the “CBDC trilemma” facing a central bank: out of the three objectives, price stability, efficiency, and monetary trust, it can achieve at most two.

中文摘要

我讨论了私人和央行发行的数字货币，并总结了我之前的研究。我认为，像比特币这样的私人数字货币的价格遵循随机游走，或者更一般地说，遵循风险调整后的鞅。对于央行数字货币，我认为它们加剧了央行面临的“CBDC三难困境”：在价格稳定、效率和货币信任这三个目标中，最多只能实现两个。

No. 5NBER w32158

How Do Voters Respond to Welfare Vis-a-Vis Public Good Programs? Theory and Evidence of Political Clientelism

选民如何回应福利项目与公共品项目？政治庇护主义的理论与证据

Pranab Bardhan, Sandip Mitra, Dilip Mookherjee, Anusha Nath

Programs：DEV, POL；JEL：H40, H75, H76, O10, P48

Abstract

Using rural household survey data from West Bengal, we find that voters respond positively to excludable government welfare benefits but not to local public good programs, while reporting having benefited from both. Consistent with these voting patterns, shocks to electoral competition induced by exogenous redistricting of villages resulted in upper-tier governments manipulating allocations across local governments only for excludable benefit programs. Using a hierarchical budgeting model, we argue these results provide credible evidence of the presence of clientelism rather than programmatic politics.

中文摘要

利用西孟加拉邦的农村家庭调查数据，我们发现选民对排他性的政府福利项目有积极回应，但对地方公共品项目则不然，尽管他们报告从两者中都受益。与这些投票模式一致，由外生村庄重新划分引起的选举竞争冲击，导致上级政府仅在排他性福利项目上操纵对地方政府的拨款。利用一个层级预算模型，我们认为这些结果为存在庇护主义而非纲领性政治提供了可信的证据。

No. 6NBER w32157

Declining Clientelism of Welfare Benefits? Targeting and Political Competition based Evidence from an Indian state

福利项目庇护主义的衰落？基于印度一个邦的瞄准与政治竞争证据

Pushkar Maitra, Sandip Mitra, Dilip Mookherjee, Sujata Visaria

Programs：DEV, POL；JEL：H53, H75, O17, O23, P43

Abstract

It has been argued that since 2014, under the BJP-led central government, welfare benefits in India have become better targeted and less prone to clientelistic control by state and local governments. Arguably this has helped to increase the vote share of the BJP vis-a-vis regional parties. We test these hypotheses using longitudinal data from 3500 rural households in the state of West Bengal. We fail to find evidence that the new “central” programs introduced after 2014 were better targeted than traditional “state” programs, or that the targeting of state programs improved after 2014. Households receiving the new “central” benefits introduced since 2014 were more likely to switch their political support to the BJP. However, changes in the scale, composition or targeting of these programs, in clientelistic effectiveness of traditional state programs or in household incomes, fail to account for the large observed increase in the voters' support for the BJP. Non-Hindus, especially recent immigrant non-Hindus, were much less likely to switch support to the BJP, even after controlling for benefits received and changes in household incomes. Our results suggest that ideology and identity politics were more important factors explaining the rising popularity of the BJP.

中文摘要

有人认为，自2014年以来，在印度人民党领导的中央政府下，印度的福利项目瞄准性更好，且不易受邦和地方政府庇护主义控制。这据称有助于提高印度人民党相对于地区政党的得票率。我们利用西孟加拉邦3500个农村家庭的纵向数据检验这些假设。我们没有发现证据表明2014年后引入的新“中央”项目比传统的“邦”项目瞄准性更好，或者邦项目的瞄准性在2014年后有所改善。获得2014年以来新“中央”福利的家庭更有可能将政治支持转向印度人民党。然而，这些项目规模、构成或瞄准性的变化、传统邦项目庇护主义有效性的变化或家庭收入的变化，都无法解释观察到的选民对印度人民党支持的大幅增加。非印度教徒，尤其是近期移民的非印度教徒，转向支持印度人民党的可能性要小得多，即使在控制了获得的福利和家庭收入变化之后也是如此。我们的结果表明，意识形态和身份政治是解释印度人民党日益受欢迎的更重要的因素。

No. 7NBER w32156

Robust Content Moderation: Theory and Applications

稳健的内容审核：理论与应用

Scott Duke Kominers, Jesse M. Shapiro

Programs：IO, POL, PR；JEL：D47, D82, D83, L82, L86

Abstract

A sender sends a signal about a state to a receiver who takes an action that determines a payoff. A moderator can block, flag, or even modify the sender’s signal before it reaches the receiver—but the moderator cannot control how the receiver will act on what they see, or what the sender will try to send. We find that the only way that the moderator can robustly change the outcome is by removing information. We discuss applications to social media content moderation, state censorship, “flooding the zone,” and AI filtering.

中文摘要

发送者向接收者发送关于某个状态的信号，接收者采取决定收益的行动。审核者可以在发送者的信号到达接收者之前阻止、标记甚至修改该信号——但审核者无法控制接收者将如何根据所见内容行动，也无法控制发送者将试图发送什么。我们发现，审核者能够稳健地改变结果的唯一方式是移除信息。我们讨论了在社交媒体内容审核、国家审查、“信息淹没”和人工智能过滤中的应用。

No. 8NBER w32155

Information-Based Pricing in Specialized Lending

专业化贷款中的信息定价

Kristian Blickle, Zhiguo He, Jing Huang, Cecilia Parlatore

Programs：CF, IO, PR；JEL：D43, D44, D82, G21, G23, L10

Abstract

We study specialized lending in a credit market competition model with private information. Two banks, equipped with similar data processing systems, possess general signals regarding the borrowers quality; the specialized bank, has access to an additional specialized signal. We study equilibria in which both lenders use general signals to screen loan applications. Conditional on making an offer, the specialized lender prices loans based on its specialized signal. This private-information-based pricing helps explain why loans made by specialized lenders have lower interest rates (lower winning bids) and better ex-post performance (fewer non-performing loans), which we support with robust empirical evidence.

中文摘要

我们在一个具有私人信息的信贷市场竞争模型中研究专业化贷款。两家银行配备相似的数据处理系统，拥有关于借款人质量的一般信号；专业化银行还能获得额外的专业化信号。我们研究了两种贷款人都使用一般信号筛选贷款申请的均衡。在提供报价的条件下，专业化贷款人根据其专业化信号对贷款进行定价。这种基于私人信息的定价有助于解释为什么专业化贷款人发放的贷款利率更低（中标价更低）且事后表现更好（不良贷款更少），我们通过稳健的实证证据支持了这一点。

No. 9NBER w32154

Gender Differences in Negotiations and Labor Market Outcomes: Evidence from an Information Intervention with College Students

谈判中的性别差异与劳动力市场结果：来自大学生信息干预的证据

Patricia Cortés, Jacob French, Jessica Pan, Basit Zafar

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：J16, J31

Abstract

We assess the role of information gaps in understanding gender differences in negotiation behavior by conducting a randomized information experiment on the 2018 to 2020 graduating cohorts of undergraduate business majors from Boston University. Prior to starting their job search, treated students were provided with objective information about the gender gap in negotiation among their peers along with the earnings changes conditional on negotiating. We find sizable immediate effects on negotiation intentions that persist to actual negotiation behavior, particularly for men. While the treatment affects women's negotiation behavior through belief-updating, the effects on men's behavior are primarily through increased salience of the information. Further, we find some evidence that gender-specific treatment spillovers likely contribute to the smaller average treatment effects on behavior for women. Overall, our findings suggest that such information interventions can help to nudge women who have potentially large financial returns to negotiation to realize these gains.

中文摘要

我们通过对波士顿大学2018至2020届本科商科毕业生进行随机信息实验，评估信息差距在理解谈判行为性别差异中的作用。在开始求职前，受处理的学生获得了关于同龄人中谈判性别差距以及谈判后收入变化的客观信息。我们发现这对谈判意图产生了显著的即时影响，并持续到实际谈判行为，尤其是对男性而言。虽然处理通过信念更新影响了女性的谈判行为，但对男性行为的影响主要是通过提高信息的显著性。此外，我们发现一些证据表明，特定性别的处理溢出效应可能导致了女性行为平均处理效应的减小。总体而言，我们的发现表明，此类信息干预有助于推动那些可能从谈判中获得巨大财务回报的女性实现这些收益。

No. 10NBER w32153

Taxing Top Wealth: Migration Responses and their Aggregate Economic Implications

对顶层财富征税：移民反应及其总体经济影响

Katrine Jakobsen, Henrik Kleven, Jonas Kolsrud, Camille Landais, Mathilde Muñoz

Programs：CF, LS, PE, PR；JEL：D31, E21, H20, H31, L26

Abstract

Using administrative data from Scandinavian countries, we provide evidence on international migration responses to wealth taxes and evaluate their aggregate economic implications. We find significant migration responses among the wealthy: a 1pp increase in the top wealth tax rate decreases the stock of wealthy taxpayers by about 2%. A large fraction of the wealthy are business owners, and their businesses are negatively affected by owner out-migration. The aggregate effects are nevertheless modest: the migration responses to a 1pp increase in the top wealth tax rate reduce employment by 0.02%, investments by 0.07%, and value-added by 0.10%.

中文摘要

利用斯堪的纳维亚国家的行政数据，我们提供了关于国际移民对财富税反应的证据，并评估了其总体经济影响。我们发现富人有显著的移民反应：最高财富税率提高1个百分点会使富裕纳税人的存量减少约2%。很大一部分富人是企业主，他们的企业受到所有者外迁的负面影响。然而，总体影响是适度的：最高财富税率提高1个百分点所引发的移民反应使就业减少0.02%，投资减少0.07%，增加值减少0.10%。

No. 11NBER w32152

Smooth Diagnostic Expectations

平滑诊断性预期

Francesco Bianchi, Cosmin L. Ilut, Hikaru Saijo

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：D91, E32, E71

Abstract

We introduce “smooth diagnosticity.” Under smooth diagnosticity, agents over-react to new information defined as the difference between the current information set and a previous information set. Since new information typically changes not just the conditional mean, but also the conditional uncertainty, changes in uncertainty surrounding current and past beliefs affect the severity of the Diagnostic Expectations (DE) distortion. Smooth DE nests the baseline DE of Bordalo et al. (2018) and implies a joint and parsimonious micro-foundation for various properties of survey data: (1) over-reaction of conditional mean to news, (2) stronger over-reaction for weaker signals and longer forecast horizons, and (3) over-confidence in subjective uncertainty. We embed Smooth DE in an analytical RBC model. The model accounts for over-reaction and over-confidence in surveys, as well as three salient properties of the business cycle: (1) asymmetry, (2) countercyclical micro volatility, and (3) countercyclical macro volatility.

中文摘要

我们引入了“平滑诊断性”概念。在平滑诊断性下，经济主体对定义为当前信息集与先前信息集之差的新信息反应过度。由于新信息通常不仅改变条件均值，还改变条件不确定性，围绕当前和过去信念的不确定性变化会影响诊断性预期（DE）扭曲的严重程度。平滑DE嵌套了Bordalo等人（2018）的基准DE，并为调查数据的各种特性提供了一个联合且简约的微观基础：（1）条件均值对新闻的反应过度；（2）对于较弱的信号和较长的预测期限，反应过度更强；（3）主观不确定性中的过度自信。我们将平滑DE嵌入一个分析性的真实商业周期（RBC）模型中。该模型解释了调查中的反应过度和过度自信，以及商业周期的三个显著特性：（1）非对称性；（2）逆周期的微观波动性；（3）逆周期的宏观波动性。

No. 12NBER w32151

The Essential Role of Altruism in Medical Decision Making

利他主义在医疗决策中的关键作用

Paul Gertler, Ada Kwan

Programs：DEV, EH, LS；JEL：I11, I12, I15

Abstract

Patients rely on medical care providers to act in their best interests because providers understand disease pathology and appropriate treatment much better than patients. Providers, however, not only give advice (diagnose) but also deliver (sell) treatments based on that advice. This creates a moral hazard dilemma where provider financial interests can diverge from patient interests, especially when providers are motivated more by profits than by altruism. We investigate how profit motivated versus altruistic preferences influence medical care decision making in the context of malaria in Kenya. We measured the appropriateness of care using data from an audit study that employed standardized patients (SP) who were trained to present as real patients the identical clinical case scenario to providers. The SPs were confirmed to be malaria negative before and after field work with a very reliable and sensitive blood test at a high-quality laboratory. We measured provider preferences using a lab in the field, real stakes, modified version of the dictator game. We find that more profit-motivated providers report higher rates of false-positive malaria test results than do more altruistic providers. Specifically, purely profit motivated providers report 30 percentage points more positives than providers who are altruistically motivated, and providers likely knew that the positive results that they reported to their patients were false. We also find that more profit motivated providers sold more unnecessary antimalarial drugs than did more altruistic providers. Based on mediation analysis, more profit-oriented providers sold more drugs not only because they knowingly reported more false positives, but also because they promoted drugs sales more conditional on a positive test result. Thus, profit motivated providers seem to have misrepresented test results to sell more unnecessary malaria-related drugs.
