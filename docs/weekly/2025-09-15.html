No. 1NBER w34255

How People Use ChatGPT

人们如何使用ChatGPT

Aaron Chatterji, Thomas Cunningham, David J. Deming, Zoe Hitzig, Christopher Ong, Carl Yan Shan, Kevin Wadman

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：J01, O3, O4

Abstract

Despite the rapid adoption of LLM chatbots, little is known about how they are used. We document the growth of ChatGPT’s consumer product from its launch in November 2022 through July 2025, when it had been adopted by around 10% of the world’s adult population. Early adopters were disproportionately male but the gender gap has narrowed dramatically, and we find higher growth rates in lower-income countries. Using a privacy-preserving automated pipeline, we classify usage patterns within a representative sample of ChatGPT conversations. We find steady growth in work-related messages but even faster growth in non-work-related messages, which have grown from 53% to more than 70% of all usage. Work usage is more common for educated users in highly-paid professional occupations. We classify messages by conversation topic and find that “Practical Guidance,” “Seeking Information,” and “Writing” are the three most common topics and collectively account for nearly 80% of all conversations. Writing dominates work-related tasks, highlighting chatbots’ unique ability to generate digital outputs compared to traditional search engines. Computer programming and self-expression both represent relatively small shares of use. Overall, we find that ChatGPT provides economic value through decision support, which is especially important in knowledge-intensive jobs.

中文摘要

尽管大型语言模型聊天机器人被迅速采用，但关于它们如何被使用却知之甚少。我们记录了ChatGPT消费产品从2022年11月推出到2025年7月的增长情况，此时它已被全球约10%的成年人口采用。早期采用者中男性比例过高，但性别差距已大幅缩小，并且我们在低收入国家发现了更高的增长率。利用一个保护隐私的自动化流程，我们在ChatGPT对话的代表性样本中对使用模式进行分类。我们发现与工作相关的消息稳步增长，但与工作无关的消息增长更快，后者已从占总使用量的53%增长到超过70%。工作用途在受过教育的高薪专业职业用户中更为常见。我们按对话主题对消息进行分类，发现“实用指导”、“寻求信息”和“写作”是最常见的三个主题，合计占所有对话的近80%。写作主导了与工作相关的任务，突显了聊天机器人相比传统搜索引擎生成数字输出的独特能力。计算机编程和自我表达的使用占比都相对较小。总体而言，我们发现ChatGPT通过决策支持提供经济价值，这在知识密集型工作中尤为重要。

Time for Mental Healthcare: Evidence from Paid Sick Leave Mandates

心理健康护理的时间：来自带薪病假强制令的证据

Matthew Eisenberg, Yimin Ge, Ezra Golberstein, Johanna Catherine Maclean

Programs：CH, EH, PE；JEL：H0, I1, I11

Abstract

We study the effects of state paid sick leave (PSL) mandates on mental healthcare use. To do so, we use all-payer health insurance claims data from IQVIA 2015-2022 combined with difference-in-differences methods. Findings suggest that overall mental healthcare use does not change post-PSL mandate. The null finding overall masks clinically relevant changes along the intensive margin: patients substitute away from lower levels of care to higher levels of care as time constraints are relaxed. Further, analysis of survey data hints that this change in treatment use improves mental health. Keywords: Paid sick leave; social insurance; social insurance.

中文摘要

我们研究了州级带薪病假（PSL）强制令对心理健康护理使用的影响。为此，我们使用了IQVIA 2015-2022年的全付款人健康保险索赔数据，并结合双重差分方法。研究结果表明，在PSL强制令实施后，总体心理健康护理使用没有变化。总体上的零结果掩盖了在集约边际上临床相关的变化：随着时间约束的放松，患者从较低级别的护理转向较高级别的护理。此外，对调查数据的分析暗示，这种治疗使用的变化改善了心理健康。关键词：带薪病假；社会保险；社会保险。

No. 3NBER w34253

Insuring Labor Income Shocks: The Role of the Dynasty

保障劳动收入冲击：家族的作用

Andreas Fagereng, Luigi Guiso, Luigi Pistaferri, Marius A. K. Ring

Programs：EFG, LS；JEL：D31, E21, E24, G11

Abstract

We provide empirical evidence on the importance of a relatively understudied channel of insurance against labor income shocks: transfers from (cash-rich) parents to (cash-short) children when the latter experience negative wage shocks. Matching population data for Norway across two generations, we establish several results. First, parents make a transfer—i.e., run down liquid assets—when adult children experience negative labor income shocks. Consistent with dynastic insurance, we observe no transfers when income shocks are positive. Second, parents' responses depend on the nature of the shock. If losses are temporary, parents dissave; if they are persistent, parents save in order to make future transfers. Parental transfers offset 43% of temporary and 27% of persistent losses. Third, insurance is lower when children have other smoothing options, like spousal labor supply, and is greater for shocks to their own child versus a child’s spouse. Support also increases if the spouse’s parents can contribute, suggesting “competition for attention.” Lastly, insurance flows are one-way: children do not insure their parents against income losses.

中文摘要

我们提供了关于一个相对未被充分研究的劳动收入冲击保险渠道重要性的实证证据：当子女经历负面工资冲击时，来自（现金充裕的）父母向（现金短缺的）子女的转移支付。通过匹配挪威两代人的总体数据，我们建立了几个结果。首先，当成年子女经历负面劳动收入冲击时，父母会进行转移支付——即减少流动资产。与家族保险一致，当收入冲击为正时，我们没有观察到转移支付。其次，父母的反应取决于冲击的性质。如果损失是暂时的，父母会动用储蓄；如果是持久的，父母会储蓄以便未来进行转移支付。父母的转移支付抵消了43%的暂时性损失和27%的持久性损失。第三，当子女有其他平滑选择（如配偶劳动供给）时，保险程度较低；并且对于子女自身而非子女配偶的冲击，保险程度更高。如果配偶的父母也能提供帮助，支持也会增加，这表明存在“注意力竞争”。最后，保险流动是单向的：子女不会为父母的收入损失提供保险。

Business Owners and the Self-Employed: Thirty-Three Million (and Counting!)

企业主与自雇者：三千三百万（且还在增加！）

Christopher Goetz, Henry R. Hyatt, Zachary Kroff, Kristin Sandusky, Martha Stinson

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：L26

Abstract

Entrepreneurs are known to be key drivers of economic growth, and the rise of online platforms and the broader "gig economy" has led self-employment to surge in recent decades. Yet the young and small businesses associated with this activity are often absent from economic data. In this paper, we explore a novel longitudinal dataset that covers the owners of tens of millions of the smallest businesses: those without employees. We produce three new sets of statistics on the rapidly growing set of nonemployer businesses. First, we measure transitions between self-employment and wage and salary jobs. Second, we describe nonemployer business entry and exit, as well as transitions between legal form (e.g., sole proprietorship to S corporation). Finally, we link owners to their nonemployer businesses and examine the dynamics of business ownership.

中文摘要

企业家被认为是经济增长的关键驱动力，而在线平台和更广泛的“零工经济”的兴起使得近几十年来自雇人数激增。然而，与此活动相关的年轻和小型企业往往在经济数据中缺失。在本文中，我们探索了一个新颖的纵向数据集，该数据集涵盖了数千万最小型企业（即没有雇员的企业）的所有者。我们针对快速增长的无雇员企业群体，生成了三组新的统计数据。首先，我们衡量了自雇与受薪工作之间的转换。其次，我们描述了无雇员企业的进入和退出，以及法律形式之间的转换（例如，从独资企业到S型公司）。最后，我们将所有者与其无雇员企业联系起来，并考察了企业所有权的动态变化。

No. 5NBER w34251

Human Capital and Racial Inequality in the US Labor Market

美国劳动力市场中的人力资本与种族不平等

Owen Thompson

Programs：CH, ED, LS, PE；JEL：I26, I30, J0, J01, J20, J70, J78

Abstract

If racial gaps in measures of human capital like educational attainment and standardized test scores were eliminated, what would happen to racial disparities in wages, employment, and other labor market outcomes? A credible answer to this question is foundational for understanding the nature and scope of racial inequality and discrimination in the United States. This article reviews and synthesizes a literature that studies this question by estimating the extent to which controlling for measures of human capital changes Black-white gaps in labor market outcomes, and discusses various conceptual and methodological issues related to interpreting this type of exercise. I show that while accurately interpreting this exercise and its many variants requires careful thinking, the results elucidate many important and subtle aspects of racial inequality in the United States.

中文摘要

如果人力资本衡量指标（如教育程度和标准化考试成绩）中的种族差距被消除，那么工资、就业和其他劳动力市场结果中的种族差异会发生什么变化？对这个问题的可信回答，对于理解美国种族不平等和歧视的性质与范围至关重要。本文回顾并综合了相关文献，这些文献通过估计控制人力资本衡量指标在多大程度上改变了黑人与白人在劳动力市场结果上的差距来研究这一问题，并讨论了与解释此类分析相关的各种概念和方法论问题。我表明，虽然准确解释这一分析及其众多变体需要仔细思考，但结果阐明了美国种族不平等的许多重要而微妙的方面。

Deep Learning for Solving Economic Models

用于求解经济模型的深度学习

Jesús Fernández-Villaverde

Programs：EFG；JEL：C45, C61, C63, C68

Abstract

The ongoing revolution in deep learning is reshaping research across many fields, including economics. Its effects are especially clear in solving dynamic economic models. These models often lack closed-form solutions, so economists have long relied on numerical methods such as value function iteration, perturbation, and projection techniques. Unfortunately, these approaches suffer from the curse of dimensionality, which makes global solutions computationally infeasible as the number of state variables increases. Deep learning offers a different approach: flexible tools that solve dynamic economic models by minimizing residuals in equilibrium conditions, and that can handle high-dimensional problems. This development promises to broaden the scope of quantitative economics. I illustrate the approach using the neoclassical growth model.

中文摘要

正在进行的深度学习革命正在重塑包括经济学在内的许多领域的研究。其影响在求解动态经济模型方面尤为明显。这些模型通常缺乏闭式解，因此经济学家长期以来依赖数值方法，如值函数迭代、摄动法和投影技术。不幸的是，这些方法受到维度诅咒的困扰，随着状态变量数量的增加，全局解在计算上变得不可行。深度学习提供了一种不同的方法：通过最小化均衡条件中的残差来求解动态经济模型的灵活工具，并且能够处理高维问题。这一发展有望拓宽定量经济学的范围。我使用新古典增长模型来说明这种方法。

More to Live for: Health Investment Responses to Expected Retirement Wealth in Chile

更多生存动力：智利预期退休财富对健康投资的影响

Grant Miller, Nieves Valdés, Marcos Vera-Hernández

Programs：AG, DEV, EH；JEL：H55, I12, I15, I18, J18

Abstract

An important but poorly understood way that economic development may influence health is through the private incentives that it creates for individuals to invest in their own health. In this paper, we study how individuals' forward-looking health investments respond to changes in expected future (but not current) wealth. Focusing on institutional features of Chile's public pension overhaul in 1981, we link administrative microdata to a detailed household panel survey, and we then exploit discrete breaks in the resulting cohort pension wealth profile using a fuzzy regression kink design (RKD). Although theoretically ambiguous, empirically we find that greater expected pension wealth increases the use of important preventive medical care (and to a lesser extent, promotes more costly healthy lifestyle behaviors) – leading to measurable increases in chronic disease diagnosis (a requisite for appropriate disease management), reductions in disease prevalence, and measurably lower mortality in old age (particularly due to chronic diseases). In general, these results provide new evidence that economic development can have a meaningful incentive effect on health.

中文摘要

经济发展可能通过为个人创造投资自身健康的私人激励来影响健康，这是一个重要但尚未被充分理解的途径。本文研究了个人的前瞻性健康投资如何响应预期未来（而非当前）财富的变化。我们聚焦于智利1981年公共养老金改革的制度特征，将行政微观数据与详细的家户面板调查相链接，并利用模糊回归断点设计（RKD）来利用由此产生的队列养老金财富曲线中的离散断点。尽管理论上存在不确定性，但实证发现，更高的预期养老金财富增加了重要预防性医疗服务的利用（并在较小程度上促进了更昂贵的健康生活方式行为），从而导致了慢性病诊断（这是适当疾病管理的必要条件）的可测量增加、疾病患病率的降低，以及老年期（尤其是因慢性病导致的）死亡率的显著下降。总体而言，这些结果提供了新的证据，表明经济发展可以对健康产生有意义的激励效应。

No. 8NBER w34248

Substituting Talent with Transactions: Acquisitions as Responses to Immigration Restrictions

用交易替代人才：企业并购作为对移民限制的回应

Jens Friedmann, Britta Glennon, Exequiel Hernandez

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：F22, G34, J24, J61, L2

Abstract

We examine how firms respond to talent scarcity caused by restrictive immigration policies. We argue that when firms cannot build capabilities internally through hiring, they alter their boundaries by engaging in corporate acquisitions to make up for the foregone talent and capabilities. Using data on 3,861 U.S. firms and their use of the H-1B visa program (2001-2020), we leverage two exogenous shocks—the 2004 H-1B cap reduction and the 2007-2008 visa lottery—and find causal evidence that firms make more acquisitions as their exposure to immigration restrictions rises. This effect is stronger for deals with purposes related to the skills of the foregone talent, for small acquisitions, for domestic targets, and for targets in places with higher concentrations of skilled workers.

中文摘要

我们研究了企业如何应对由限制性移民政策造成的人才稀缺。我们认为，当企业无法通过招聘在内部构建能力时，它们会通过进行企业并购来改变自身边界，以弥补失去的人才和能力。利用2001年至2020年间3861家美国企业及其使用H-1B签证计划的数据，我们借助两个外生冲击——2004年H-1B签证配额减少和2007-2008年签证抽签——并发现因果证据表明，随着企业面临的移民限制增加，它们会进行更多的并购。这种效应在与失去人才技能相关的并购目的、小型并购、国内目标企业以及位于高技能工人集中地区的目标企业中更为显著。

No. 9NBER w34247

Utilitarian or Quantile-Welfare Evaluation of Health Policy?

健康政策的效用主义评估还是分位数福利评估？

Charles F. Manski, John Mullahy

Programs：EH, PE；JEL：I1

Abstract

This paper considers quantile-welfare evaluation of health policy as an alternative to utilitarian evaluation. Manski (1988) originally proposed and studied maximization of quantile utility as a model of individual decision making under uncertainty, juxtaposing it with maximization of expected utility. That paper's primary motivation was to exploit the fact that maximization of quantile utility requires only an ordinal formalization of utility, not a cardinal one. This paper transfers these ideas from analysis of individual decision making to analysis of social planning. We begin by summarizing basic theoretical properties of quantile welfare in general terms rather than related specifically to health policy. We then propose a procedure to nonparametrically bound the quantile welfare of health states using data from binary-choice time-tradeoff (TTO) experiments of the type regularly performed by health economists. After this we assess related econometric considerations concerning measurement, using the EQ-5D framework to structure our discussion.

中文摘要

本文考虑将健康政策的分位数福利评估作为效用主义评估的替代方案。Manski（1988）最初提出并研究了分位数效用最大化作为不确定性下个体决策的模型，并将其与期望效用最大化并列。该论文的主要动机是利用分位数效用最大化仅需要效用的序数形式化而非基数形式化这一事实。本文将这些思想从个体决策分析转移到社会规划分析。我们首先以一般性术语而非专门针对健康政策来总结分位数福利的基本理论性质。然后，我们提出了一种程序，利用健康经济学家定期进行的二元选择时间权衡（TTO）实验数据，对健康状态的分位数福利进行非参数边界估计。在此之后，我们评估了关于测量的相关计量经济学考量，并使用EQ-5D框架来构建我们的讨论。

No. 10NBER w34246

Segregation, Spillovers, and the Locus of Racial Change

隔离、溢出效应与种族变迁的轨迹

Donald R. Davis, Matthew Easton, Stephan Thies

Programs：DAE, ITI, LS；JEL：J15, R1, R3

Abstract

We use a discrete choice framework to provide the first nesting of Thomas C. Schelling’s canonical models of racial segregation amenable to empirical examination. Using U.S. Census data from 1970–2000, we demonstrate a central role for spatial racial spillovers in shaping racial clustering, patterns of racial shares and housing prices at the boundary of racial clusters, and the locus of racial change. Our results on the locus of racial change conflict strongly with prominent prior results on racial tipping. Our theory provides a foundation for spatially stratified regressions. The strongest spatial effects in the prior work are not tipping, but the distinct biased White suburbanization. Tipping effects in urban areas remote from Minority clusters are small or insignificant. In urban areas proximate to Minority clusters they average less than half those reported in prior pooled results. Policies promoting racial integration must thus attend to the heterogeneous fragility of neighborhoods.

中文摘要

我们使用离散选择框架，首次对Thomas C. Schelling关于种族隔离的经典模型进行了嵌套，使其适用于实证检验。利用1970年至2000年的美国人口普查数据，我们证明了空间种族溢出效应在塑造种族聚集、种族集群边界处的种族份额和房价模式以及种族变迁轨迹方面发挥着核心作用。我们关于种族变迁轨迹的结果与先前关于种族临界点的著名结果存在强烈冲突。我们的理论为空间分层回归提供了基础。先前研究中最大的空间效应并非临界点，而是独特的偏向性白人郊区化。在远离少数族裔集群的城市地区，临界点效应很小或不显著。在靠近少数族裔集群的城市地区，其平均值不到先前汇总结果报告的一半。因此，促进种族融合的政策必须关注社区异质性的脆弱性。

No. 11NBER w34245

Abortion, Economic Hardship, and Crime

堕胎、经济困难与犯罪

Erkmen G. Aslim, Wei Fu, Caitlin K. Myers, Erdal Tekin, Bingjin Xue

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I12, I18, J13, K42

Abstract

We study how abortion access affects economic hardship and crime. Using a database of abortion provider locations and operations in Texas from 2009–2019, we exploit variation in travel distance to the nearest facility created by clinic closures following the enforcement of Texas HB-2 in 2013. We confirm previous evidence that increased distance to the nearest abortion facility reduces abortions and increases births. We provide novel evidence that reduced access to abortion also leads to significant economic hardship, reflected in lower labor force participation, rising debt, widening income inequality, and heightened housing insecurity. This financial strain translates into higher rates of financially motivated crime, such as theft and burglary, with no significant effect on violent crime. These effects extend beyond directly affected individuals, reflecting intrahousehold spillovers. These findings suggest far-reaching consequences of restricted access to reproductive healthcare.

Thus, most of the potential gains from more complete markets come from insuring household-level risks domestically and from intertemporal trade between fast-growing and slower-growing countries, rather than from additional risk sharing among similarly advanced economies.

Should States Reduce Teacher Licensing Requirements? Evidence from the Rise of For-Profit Training Programs in Texas

各州是否应降低教师资格要求？来自德克萨斯州营利性培训项目兴起的证据

Christa Deneault, Evan Riehl

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：I28, J44

Abstract

We provide a comprehensive analysis of a Texas policy that relaxed teacher licensing requirements and created a large for-profit training industry. Using detailed administrative data, we show that for-profit-trained teachers have higher turnover and lower value-added than standard-trained teachers. But the policy significantly increased the supply of certified teachers, reducing schools' reliance on uncertified teachers with even worse outcomes. Exploiting variation in policy exposure across schools, we find a zero net impact on student achievement due to these offsetting forces. Thus lower licensing requirements improved access to teaching and reduced training costs without harming students.

Happier at Work? The Impact of Working at an Employee-Owned Firm and Working from Home on Job Satisfaction

Richard B. Freeman, Huanan Xu

Programs：IO, LS, POL；JEL：J28, J54, J81, M54, P1

Abstract

This paper examines the impact of working for an ESOP firm and Working-From-Home (WFH) on job satisfaction in the National Longitudinal Survey of Youth 1997 (NLSY97) and the General Social Science (GSS) survey. It finds that job satisfaction is higher for employees in ESOPs than for observationally similar workers in non-ESOP firms and for WFH workers than for their non-WFH peers. Fixed effect analysis of the NLSY97 finds that both ESOP and WFH employment raise job satisfaction for the same worker when he/she changes work status. The channels through which the two conditions raise satisfaction appear to differ: ESOPs raise satisfaction by increasing worker participation on collective workplace or firm decisions while WFH raise satisfaction by increasing worker flexibility in their individual work activity. Both participation and flexibility also raise job satisfaction independently. The data further show ESOPs had more extensive WFH than non-ESOP firms during the Covid-19 pandemic.

Daniel W. Sacks, Justin R. Sydnor

Programs：EH；JEL：D9, I1, I12

Abstract

Can economic tools help inform the puzzlingly low rate of flu vaccination? Existing interventions focus on misinformation or nudges, not preferences over or beliefs about vaccine characteristics. Using an online experiment, we find a key role for effectiveness and short-run side-effects: equalizing only beliefs and preferences about these characteristics nearly eliminates the vaccination-intention gap between the vaccine hesitant and confident. Latent Polarization

潜在极化

Klaus Desmet, Ignacio Ortuño-Ortín, Romain Wacziarg

Programs：POL；JEL：D71, D72, Z1

Abstract

We develop a new method to endogenously partition society into groups based on homophily in values, using fractional hedonic games as a theoretical foundation. The between-group differentiation that results from this partition provides a novel measure of latent polarization in society. We implement this method empirically using U.S. data from the World Values Survey. For the last forty years, the degree of latent polarization of the U.S. public has been high and relatively stable. In contrast, the degree of values polarization between voters of the two main political parties has steadily increased since the 1990s, and is now converging toward that of underlying values-based clusters. Thus, growing partisan polarization in the U.S. is a reflection of partisan views becoming increasingly aligned with the main values-based clusters in society.

Five Facts About the First-Generation Excellence Gap

关于第一代卓越差距的五个事实

Uditi Karna, John A. List, Andrew Simon, Haruka Uchida

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：I21, I24, I30, J08

Abstract

Parents are crucial to children’s educational success, but the role of parental education in fostering academic excellence remains underexplored. Using longitudinal administrative data covering all North Carolina public school students, we document five facts about first-generation excellence gaps. We find large excellence gaps emerge by 3rd grade across all demographics and persist through high school. Yet, socioeconomic status and school quality explain only one-third of the gaps.
