When Does Acquisition Integration Succeed? Evidence from Inside the Integration Black Box

收购整合何时成功？来自整合黑箱内部的证据

Sinan Gokkaya, Xi Liu, René M. Stulz

Programs：CF；JEL：G3, G34, I25

Abstract

We penetrate the black box of post-acquisition integration by examining whether business unit leaders (BULs)—executives responsible for integrating and operating acquired targets—shape integration outcomes. Using natural language processing-based measures of realized integration outcomes and financial metrics, we find that BULs’ target-industry experience increases (decreases) integration success (failures) through superior realization of transaction synergies. Other BUL attributes are unrelated to integration outcomes. Consistent with the importance of integration for acquisition success, such experience enhances acquisition performance. BULs’ target-industry experience improves integration planning and their post-acquisition career outcomes but is unrelated to earlier stages of the acquisition process.

中文摘要

我们通过考察业务部门负责人——负责整合和运营收购目标的执行官——是否影响整合结果，来深入探究收购后整合的黑箱。利用基于自然语言处理的已实现整合结果度量和财务指标，我们发现，业务部门负责人的目标行业经验通过更好地实现交易协同效应，提高了整合成功（减少了失败）。业务部门负责人的其他属性与整合结果无关。与整合对收购成功的重要性相一致，这种经验提升了收购绩效。业务部门负责人的目标行业经验改善了整合规划及其收购后的职业结果，但与收购过程的早期阶段无关。

Understanding Support for Inefficient Environmental Policy Instruments

理解对低效环境政策工具的支持

Chenxi Jiang, Maximiliano Lauletta, Ro’ee Levy, Joseph S. Shapiro, Dmitry Taubinsky

Programs：EEE, LE, PE, POL；JEL：C83, D83, D9, H23, Q48, Q50, Q58

Abstract

Many governments use environmental standards rather than more cost-effective market-based instruments like pollution taxes or cap-and-trade markets. Using a nationally representative survey experiment, we study whether and why limited understanding of economic principles helps explain this practice. Holding environmental impacts constant, respondents prefer standards over market-based instruments, and prefer producer taxes and cap-and-trade over consumer taxes. These preferences reflect consumers’ beliefs about how these policies will affect electricity bills. Respondents also prefer the weakest environmental targets for consumer taxes and the strongest targets for standards, which suggests that policymakers face a tradeoff between policy stringency and cost effectiveness. A separate survey of environmental economists shows that they have strikingly different beliefs about the effects of environmental policies than the respondents in our representative survey. For example, typical respondents—in contrast to environmental economists—believe that environmental standards increase consumer energy bills less than market-based instruments do. Educational videos on pass-through and cost-effectiveness of policies affect policy support and close some of the gap between nationally representative respondents and experts, which suggests that economic literacy is a factor in voters’ preferences.

中文摘要

许多政府使用环境标准，而非更具成本效益的市场化工具，如污染税或总量控制与交易市场。通过一项具有全国代表性的调查实验，我们研究了对经济学原理的有限理解是否以及为何有助于解释这种做法。在保持环境影响不变的情况下，受访者更偏好标准而非市场化工具，并且更偏好生产者税和总量控制与交易，而非消费者税。这些偏好反映了消费者关于这些政策将如何影响电费账单的信念。受访者还偏好针对消费者税的最弱环境目标，以及针对标准的最强目标，这表明政策制定者面临着政策严格性与成本效益之间的权衡。另一项针对环境经济学家的独立调查显示，他们对环境政策效果的信念与我们代表性调查中的受访者截然不同。例如，与环境经济学家相反，典型受访者认为环境标准对消费者能源账单的增加幅度小于市场化工具。关于政策传导和成本效益的教育视频会影响政策支持，并缩小全国代表性受访者与专家之间的一些差距，这表明经济素养是影响选民偏好的一个因素。

Risk Preferences and the Willingness to Relocate to Danger: Evidence from Wartime Ukraine

风险偏好与搬迁至危险地区的意愿：来自战时乌克兰的证据

Yuriy Gorodnichenko, Marianna Kudlyak, Sophia Lobozynska, Iryna Skomorovych, Ulyana Vladychyn, Andriy Kovalyuk, Iryna Snovydovych

Programs：EFG, LS, ME；JEL：D15, D81, J61, R23

Abstract

We elicit reservation wage premia for relocating to two Ukrainian cities, using a household survey conducted in mid-April to mid-July 2024 during the Russian invasion of Ukraine: high-risk Kharkiv (near the frontline) and moderate-risk Kyiv. Risk tolerance is a strong predictor of willingness to move to Kharkiv—the most risk-averse have roughly half the odds of the most risk-tolerant—but matters much less for Kyiv. This asymmetry is difficult to reconcile with the hypothesis that risk tolerance merely proxies for general mobility preferences. Separately estimating the elasticity of intertemporal substitution (EIS≈0.04), we find that including it renders risk tolerance insignificant for Kyiv but not for Kharkiv—a pattern illuminated by the Epstein-Zin separation of risk aversion and the EIS: risk aversion adds predictive power only when danger is high, while the EIS operates equally for both cities as a common relocation-cost channel. The very low EIS implies that relocation incentives structured as future benefits may be ineffective; front-loaded subsidies are more likely to influence behavior.

中文摘要

我们利用2024年4月中旬至7月中旬在俄罗斯入侵乌克兰期间进行的一项家庭调查，引出了搬迁至两个乌克兰城市的保留工资溢价：高风险城市哈尔科夫（靠近前线）和中风险城市基辅。风险容忍度是搬迁至哈尔科夫意愿的有力预测指标——最厌恶风险者的几率大约是最能容忍风险者的一半——但对于基辅而言，其重要性要小得多。这种不对称性很难与风险容忍度仅仅是普遍流动偏好代理变量的假设相协调。通过分别估计跨期替代弹性（EIS≈0.04），我们发现，将其纳入模型后，风险容忍度对基辅不再显著，但对哈尔科夫仍然显著——这一模式通过Epstein-Zin对风险厌恶和EIS的分离得以阐明：风险厌恶仅在危险程度高时增加预测能力，而EIS则作为一个共同的搬迁成本渠道对两个城市同等发挥作用。极低的EIS意味着，以未来收益形式构建的搬迁激励可能效果不佳；前置补贴更有可能影响行为。

The Macroeconomic Effects of Bank Regulation: New Evidence from a High-Frequency Approach

银行监管的宏观经济效应：来自高频方法的新证据

Thomas Drechsel, Ko Miura

Programs：ME；JEL：E44, E51, E52, E58, G28

Abstract

Bank regulation supports financial stability, but might constrain economic activity. This paper estimates the macroeconomic effects of bank regulation using a high-frequency identification approach. We measure market surprises in a bank stock price index during a narrow time window around Federal Reserve speeches that discuss the US banking system and its regulation. We then develop a sign restriction procedure to elicit the variation in these market surprises that can be interpreted as news about bank regulation. News that bank regulation will be tighter than expected mitigates risk in the banking sector, but reduces economic activity by increasing banks' funding costs and tightening loan supply. A 10 basis point regulation-induced peak reduction in bank risk premiums is accompanied by a 15 basis point peak increase in the unemployment rate. Compared to previous studies, these magnitudes suggest a relatively high macroeconomic cost of tightening bank regulation, at least in the short run.

中文摘要

银行监管支持金融稳定，但可能制约经济活动。本文使用高频识别方法估计了银行监管的宏观经济效应。我们衡量了在美联储讨论美国银行体系及其监管的演讲前后一个狭窄时间窗口内，银行股票价格指数的市场意外变动。然后，我们开发了一个符号限制程序，以引出这些市场意外变动中可被解释为银行监管新闻的部分。银行监管将比预期更严格的新闻，会缓解银行部门的风险，但通过增加银行的融资成本和收紧贷款供给来减少经济活动。由监管引起的银行风险溢价峰值降低10个基点，伴随着失业率峰值上升15个基点。与以往研究相比，这些数值表明，至少在短期内，收紧银行监管的宏观经济成本相对较高。

Birthright Citizenship and Youth Crime

出生公民权与青少年犯罪

Leander Andres, Stefan Bauernschuster, Gordon B. Dahl, Helmut Rainer, Simone Schüller

Programs：CH, LS, PE；JEL：J15, K42

Abstract

This paper studies the impact of birthright citizenship on youth crime. We leverage a German reform which automatically granted birthright citizenship to eligible immigrant children born in Germany after January 1, 2000 and administrative crime data from three federal states. We find that immigrant youth who acquired citizenship at birth are substantially less likely to engage in criminal activity, with estimates indicating a 70% reduction in crime. These results are particularly relevant in light of ongoing debates in the U.S. about abolishing birthright citizenship. Our findings suggest that inclusive citizenship policies can reduce crime and its associated costs, which in turn could strengthen social cohesion.

中文摘要

本文研究了出生公民权对青少年犯罪的影响。我们利用德国的一项改革，该改革自动授予2000年1月1日之后在德国出生的符合条件的移民子女出生公民权，并使用了来自三个联邦州的行政犯罪数据。我们发现，在出生时获得公民身份的移民青少年从事犯罪活动的可能性显著降低，估计表明犯罪率降低了70%。这些结果对于美国关于废除出生公民权的持续辩论尤其具有现实意义。我们的研究结果表明，包容性的公民政策可以减少犯罪及其相关成本，这反过来又可以加强社会凝聚力。

Reason for Treason

叛国缘由

Xinyu Fan, Gary Richardson, Zhihao Xu, Sicheng Zhao

Programs：DAE, POL；JEL：D73, D74, M51, M52, N45

Abstract

Defections during war are extreme changes in loyalty. What motivates military officers to betray their motherland and serve the invaders? Using a novel dataset of career paths for over 2,800 high-ranking (colonels and generals) Nationalist (KMT) military officers during the Second Sino-Japanese War (as part of World War II), we examine defection cases to Japanese puppet regimes. Three findings emerge. First, high-ranking KMT officers who advanced more slowly in their careers were more likely to defect; suggesting that internal organizational recognition matters. Second, officers who were underpromoted compared to their schoolmates and townsmen were more likely to defect, suggesting that peer comparison matters. Third, officers were more likely to defect when their defected peers had better career prospects in the enemy’s camp, suggesting that external recognition matters.

中文摘要

战争期间的叛逃是忠诚度的极端转变。是什么促使军官背叛祖国并为入侵者服务？利用第二次世界大战期间（作为二战的一部分）第二次中日战争中超过2800名高级（上校和将军）国民党（KMT）军官职业生涯路径的新数据集，我们考察了向日本傀儡政权叛逃的案例。出现了三个发现。首先，职业生涯晋升较慢的国民党高级军官更有可能叛逃；这表明内部组织认可很重要。其次，与其同学和同乡相比晋升不足的军官更有可能叛逃，这表明同侪比较很重要。第三，当叛逃的同僚在敌方阵营拥有更好的职业前景时，军官更有可能叛逃，这表明外部认可很重要。

High Schools and the Uneven Rise in American Opportunity

高中与美国机遇的不均衡崛起

Alison Doxey, Ezra Karger, Peter Nencka

Programs：CH, DAE, ED, LS, PE；JEL：I2, I25, I28, N00, N31

Abstract

Between 1850 and 1910, the share of young Americans living in towns with high schools increased from 17% to 46%—the fastest expansion of school access in U.S. history. Using new data on every high school in the United States, we show that this expansion transformed economic opportunities for many young adults but widened class and racial inequalities. We find sharp increases in school attendance rates for high school-aged children in towns that opened a high school relative to children in nearby towns without one. Linking children to adult outcomes, we show that high schools increased women's labor force participation and job quality, while reducing the probability of early marriage and childbearing. Increased access to high school accounts for a third of the increase in women's labor force participation between 1870 and 1930. High schools had the largest effects on children from already-wealthy families, and did not, on average, benefit Black children. While the high school movement substantially narrowed gender gaps in labor market outcomes, it also widened existing race- and class-based disparities.

中文摘要

在1850年至1910年间，居住在有高中城镇的美国年轻人比例从17%增加到46%——这是美国历史上入学机会扩张最快的时期。利用关于美国每所高中的新数据，我们表明这种扩张改变了许多年轻人的经济机会，但扩大了阶级和种族不平等。我们发现，与附近没有高中的城镇的儿童相比，在开设高中的城镇，高中适龄儿童的入学率急剧上升。通过将儿童与其成年后的结果联系起来，我们表明高中提高了女性的劳动力参与率和工作质量，同时降低了早婚和生育的可能性。入学机会的增加解释了1870年至1930年间女性劳动力参与率增长的三分之一。高中对来自已经富裕家庭的儿童影响最大，并且平均而言，并未使黑人儿童受益。虽然高中运动显著缩小了劳动力市场结果中的性别差距，但它也扩大了现存的基于种族和阶级的不平等。

Who Pays for Payments?

谁为支付买单？

Mark L. Egan, Gregor Matvos, Amit Seru, Lulu Wang, Vincent Yao

Programs：AP, CF, IO；JEL：D14, E42, G0, G2, G5, L11, L81

Abstract

We use novel data on the composition and cost of payments across U.S. merchants to quantify consumer redistribution in the payment system. Cards charge interchange fees to merchants to fund consumer rewards. When merchants raise prices for all consumers in response to these costs, users of low-cost payment methods (e.g., cash and debit) cross-subsidize high-reward credit card users who shop at the same merchant. This standard mechanism implicitly assumes that consumers using different payment methods shop at the same merchants and that merchants face similar fees. We show instead that incidence depends on the joint distribution of payment choices across merchants. We document two key forces that shape redistribution. First, consumer sorting—where consumers who use different payment methods shop at different merchants—limits the exposure of cash and debit users to the effects of high interchange fees. Second, interchange fees vary across merchants; where users of different payment methods overlap, such as at large grocery stores, fees are lower due to sector discounts and private negotiations. We embed these forces in a sufficient-statistics framework that maps observed heterogeneity directly into redistribution. We estimate that interchange fees transfer approximately $30 billion every year from cash and debit users to credit card users. Consumer sorting and merchant fee heterogeneity reduce the magnitude of this regressive transfer by 25%, but do not eliminate it. Finally, we show that both the Durbin Amendment and the rise of premium credit cards have been regressive, highlighting how policy and innovation can reshape the incidence of platform fees.

中文摘要

我们利用关于美国商户支付构成和成本的新数据来量化支付系统中的消费者再分配。信用卡向商户收取交换费，以资助消费者奖励。当商户为了应对这些成本而提高所有消费者的价格时，在同一商户购物的低成本支付方式（例如现金和借记卡）用户，就会交叉补贴高奖励信用卡用户。这种标准机制隐含地假设使用不同支付方式的消费者在同一商户购物，并且商户面临相似的费用。相反，我们表明，实际负担取决于支付选择在商户间的联合分布。我们记录了塑造再分配的两个关键力量。首先，消费者分类——即使用不同支付方式的消费者在不同商户购物——限制了现金和借记卡用户对高交换费影响的暴露程度。其次，交换费因商户而异；在使用不同支付方式的用户重叠的地方，例如大型杂货店，由于行业折扣和私下谈判，费用较低。我们将这些力量嵌入一个充分统计量框架中，该框架将观察到的异质性直接映射到再分配。我们估计，交换费每年从现金和借记卡用户向信用卡用户转移约3000亿美元。消费者分类和商户费用异质性使这种累退性转移的规模减少了25%，但并未消除它。最后，我们表明，杜宾修正案和高端信用卡的兴起都具有累退性，突显了政策和创新如何重塑平台费用的实际负担。

Zero Energy Day: How Nationwide Blackouts Affect the Economy

零能源日：全国性停电如何影响经济

Luis E. Gonzales, Koichiro Ito, Mar Reguant

Programs：DEV, EEE, IO；JEL：Q4, Q40, Q43, Q5, Q50

Abstract

Electricity reliability is a central challenge for the energy transition, as growing energy demand, renewable energy integration, and natural disasters increase the risk of large-scale black- outs. However, the economic impacts of large-scale blackouts remain largely unknown. Combining electricity market data with high-frequency economic transaction data from Chile, we find that economic activity declined by 35 percent on the nationwide blackout day, but half of this loss was recovered on subsequent days, highlighting the importance of intertemporal substitution. Exploiting spatial variation in blackout severity, we show that accounting for endogenous recovery is critical when estimating the marginal value of lost load.

中文摘要

电力可靠性是能源转型的核心挑战，因为不断增长的能源需求、可再生能源整合以及自然灾害增加了大规模停电的风险。然而，大规模停电的经济影响在很大程度上仍然未知。通过将电力市场数据与来自智利的高频经济交易数据相结合，我们发现，在全国停电日，经济活动下降了35%，但这一损失的一半在随后的日子里得以恢复，这突显了跨期替代的重要性。利用停电严重程度的空间差异，我们表明，在估计失负荷边际价值时，考虑内生性恢复至关重要。

Multimodal Transport Networks

多式联运网络

Simon Fuchs, Woan Foong Wong

Programs：ITI；JEL：F11, R12, R42

Abstract

Over half of distance-weighted U.S. freight is shipped using more than one transport mode. We examine how multimodal transport networks shape the economic and environmental impacts of infrastructure investments and disruptions. We develop a tractable spatial equilibrium model of multimodal routing with mode-specific congestion at intermodal terminals. We estimate a modal substitution elasticity using road and rail data, and a terminal congestion elasticity using vessel-positioning data. Calibrated to the U.S. freight network, the model identifies key bottlenecks and quantifies $.46-$1.85 billion in real GDP gains from intermodal terminal improvements, with additional environmental benefits from shifting away from carbon-intensive road transport. Ignoring mode-specific congestion overstates welfare gains from highway improvements by 85%, while ignoring multimodal flexibility understates them by 22%. Losing rail network access is estimated to reduce real GDP by $230 billion.

Predicted Incrementality by Experimentation (PIE) for Ad Measurement

基于实验的预测增量（PIE）用于广告测量

Brett R. Gordon, Robert Moakler, Florian Zettelmeyer

Programs：IO, PR；JEL：L81, L82, L86, M3, M30, M31, M37

Abstract

Randomized controlled trials (RCTs) provide the most credible estimates of advertising incrementality but are difficult to scale. We propose Predicted Incrementality by Experimentation (PIE), which reframes ad measurement as a campaign-level prediction problem. PIE uses a sample of RCTs to learn a mapping from campaign features to causal effects, then applies it to campaigns not run as RCTs. Because the RCTs identify the causal effects, PIE can incorporate post-determined features—campaign-level aggregates such as test-group outcomes, exposure rates, and last-click conversions, computed after campaign completion. These metrics reflect the consumer behaviors that generate treatment effects, so they carry predictive information about incrementality even though they would be invalid controls in a causal model. Using 2,226 Meta ad experiments, PIE achieves an out-of-sample R2 = 0.88 for incremental conversions per dollar, compared to R2 = 0.19 for industry-standard 7-day last-click attribution. In a decision-making framework, PIE disagrees with RCT-based decisions in only 8–12% of campaigns, compared to 12–20% for last-click attribution. We conclude that PIE can help scale causal measurement from a limited number of RCTs to a large set of non-experimental campaigns.

Profit Regulation and Strategic Transfer Pricing by Vertically Integrated Firms: Evidence from Health Care

Pragya Kakani, Eric Yde, Genevieve P. Kanter, Richard G. Frank, Amelia M. Bond

Programs：EH, PE；JEL：I11, I13, I18, L14, L22, L41, L51

Abstract

We provide evidence of strategic transfer pricing by vertically integrated health care firms in response to insurer profit regulations. Insurers increased prices at vertically integrated pharmacies by 9.5% following the introduction of caps on insurer profits in Medicare Part D. We detect larger price increases by insurers that were at greatest risk of exceeding the allowable profit level. More than one-fifth of these higher prices were borne by the federal government. Our analysis illustrates that vertically integrated firms can evade profit regulation by “tunneling” profits to unregulated subsidiaries, undermining regulatory intent and increasing health care spending.
