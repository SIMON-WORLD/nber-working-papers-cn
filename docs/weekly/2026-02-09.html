Are Government Bonds Safe in Times of War and Pandemic?

政府债券在战争和疫情期间是否安全？

Zhengyang Jiang, Hanno Lustig, Stijn Van Nieuwerburgh, Mindy Z. Xiaolan

Programs：AP, EFG, ME；JEL：E43, E58, E6, E63

Abstract

We analyze real returns on U.S. and U.K. government debt during major wars and the COVID-19 pandemic over the past three centuries. Wars are associated with sharply negative real returns on outstanding government debt, with returns falling far below economic growth, in contrast to peacetime periods when returns exceed growth. Elevated surprise inflation and financial repression account for a cumulative 30% wedge between returns and growth over four years of war, implying that bondholders bear a substantial share of wartime fiscal costs. During wartime, government bonds also systematically underperform risky assets.

中文摘要

我们分析了在过去三个世纪的主要战争和COVID-19疫情期间，美国和英国政府债务的实际回报率。战争与未偿政府债务的急剧负实际回报率相关，回报率远低于经济增长，这与和平时期回报率超过增长的情况形成对比。在四年的战争期间，高企的意外通胀和金融抑制共同导致了回报率与增长率之间累计30%的差距，这意味着债券持有人承担了战时财政成本的很大一部分。在战争期间，政府债券的表现也系统性逊于风险资产。

Financial Incentives, Health Screening, and Selection into Mental Health Care: Experimental Evidence from College Students in India

经济激励、健康筛查与心理健康护理的选择：来自印度大学生的实验证据

Emily Breza, Kevin Carney, Vijaya Raghavan, Kailash Rajah, Thara Rangaswamy, Gautam Rao, Frank Schilbach, Sobia Shadbar, James Stratton

Programs：DEV, EH；JEL：D03, I1, O10

Abstract

In an RCT with college students in Chennai (N=340), we test how modest financial incentives and personalized feedback affect the uptake and targeting (by symptom severity) of free therapy. Despite 56% of students screening positive for at least mild depression or anxiety, only 3% in the control group took up therapy. A small cash incentive increased appointments by 9 percentage points (p = 0.06) on average without substantially affecting targeting. Personalized feedback and recommendations based on a mental health screening tool significantly improved targeting while keeping overall take-up largely unchanged. Combining these two treatments achieved both higher take-up and improved targeting, by increasing appointments among symptomatic individuals by 21 pp (p < 0.01) without affecting uptake by asymptomatic individuals. These findings suggest that low-cost incentives coupled with screening information can effectively increase uptake while allocating limited mental health care resources to those with greater need.

中文摘要

在一项针对金奈（N=340）大学生的随机对照试验中，我们测试了适度的经济激励和个性化反馈如何影响免费心理治疗的接受率及其针对性（按症状严重程度）。尽管56%的学生筛查出至少存在轻度抑郁或焦虑，但对照组中只有3%接受了治疗。一笔小额现金激励平均使预约率提高了9个百分点（p = 0.06），但对针对性没有实质性影响。基于心理健康筛查工具的个性化反馈和建议显著改善了针对性，同时总体接受率基本保持不变。将这两种干预措施结合使用，既实现了更高的接受率，又改善了针对性，使有症状个体的预约率提高了21个百分点（p < 0.01），且不影响无症状个体的接受率。这些发现表明，低成本的激励措施与筛查信息相结合，可以有效地提高接受率，同时将有限的心理健康护理资源配置给更有需求的人群。

Residential Segregation and Unequal Access to Local Public Services in India: Evidence from 1.5m Neighborhoods

印度居住隔离与地方公共服务获取的不平等：来自150万个邻里的证据

Sam Asher, Kritarth Jha, Paul Novosad, Anjali Adukia, Brandon Tan

Programs：DEV, ED；JEL：H4, H41, I24, J15, O15, R12, R13, R23

Abstract

We study residential segregation and access to public services across 1.5 million urban and rural neighborhoods in India. Muslim and Scheduled Caste segregation in India is high by global standards, and only slightly lower than Black-White segregation in the U.S. Within cities, public facilities and infrastructure are systematically less available in Muslim and Scheduled Caste neighborhoods. Nearly all regressive allocation is across neighborhoods within cities—at the most informal and least studied form of government. These inequalities are not visible in the aggregate data typically used for research and policy.

中文摘要

我们研究了印度150万个城乡邻里的居住隔离和公共服务获取情况。印度的穆斯林和表列种姓隔离程度按全球标准来看很高，仅略低于美国的黑白隔离水平。在城市内部，公共设施和基础设施在穆斯林和表列种姓邻里中系统性较少。几乎所有倒退性的资源配置都发生在城市内部的邻里之间——这是最非正式且研究最少的政府层面。这些不平等在通常用于研究和政策的汇总数据中并不明显。

International Currency Dominance

国际货币主导地位

Joseph Abadi, Jesús Fernández-Villaverde, Daniel Sanches

Programs：EFG, IFM；JEL：E42, E58, G21

Abstract

We present a micro-founded monetary model of the world economy to study international currency competition. Our model features "unipolar'' equilibria, with a single dominant international currency, and "multipolar'' equilibria, in which multiple currencies circulate internationally. Long-run equilibria are highly history-dependent and tend towards the emergence of a dominant currency. Governments can compete to internationalize their currencies by offering attractive interest rates on their sovereign debt, but large economies have a natural advantage in ensuring the dominance of their currencies. We calibrate the model to assess the quantitative importance of these mechanisms and study the international monetary system's dynamics under several counterfactual scenarios.

中文摘要

我们提出了一个基于微观基础的世界经济货币模型，以研究国际货币竞争。我们的模型具有“单极”均衡（单一主导国际货币）和“多极”均衡（多种货币在国际上流通）的特征。长期均衡高度依赖于历史路径，并倾向于出现一种主导货币。政府可以通过为其主权债务提供有吸引力的利率来竞争使其货币国际化，但大型经济体在确保其货币主导地位方面具有天然优势。我们对模型进行校准，以评估这些机制的定量重要性，并研究在几种反事实情景下国际货币体系的动态变化。

Extractive Taxation and the French Revolution

榨取性税收与法国大革命

Tommaso Giommoni, Gabriel Loumeau, Marco Tabellini

Programs：DAE, PE, POL；JEL：D74, H20, H31, N43, O23

Abstract

In this paper, we provide systematic evidence in support of the long-standing hypothesis that taxation was an important driver of the French Revolution. We first document that areas with heavier taxes experienced more riots between 1750 and 1789 and voiced more complaints against taxation in the cahiers de doléances of 1789. After showing that these effects are driven by indirect taxes, we exploit sharp spatial differences in the salt tax and the traites—the two principal indirect levies—to implement a regression discontinuity design (RDD).We find that unrest was higher on the high-tax side of the border. These effects intensified over time, peaking in the 1780s, and were stronger where fiscal disparities were larger and Enlightenment ideas more widespread. We further show that adverse weather shocks amplified unrest in high-tax municipalities. We then document that taxation fueled the spread of unrest during the Grande Peur—the wave of revolts that swept France in July 1789 and culminated in the abolition of feudal privileges. Finally, we link taxation to revolutionary politics in Paris, documenting that deputies from heavily taxed constituencies were more likely to frame the tax system as oppressive, support the Revolution, demand the abolition of the monarchy, and vote for the king’s execution.

中文摘要

在本文中，我们提供了系统性证据，支持税收是法国大革命重要驱动因素这一长期存在的假说。我们首先证明，在1750年至1789年间，税收负担较重的地区发生了更多的骚乱，并在1789年的《陈情书》中表达了对税收更多的抱怨。在表明这些影响是由间接税驱动之后，我们利用盐税和关税（两种主要的间接税）在空间上的显著差异，实施了一个断点回归设计。我们发现，在边境高税收一侧的动乱更高。这些影响随时间推移而加剧，在1780年代达到顶峰，并且在财政差距更大和启蒙思想更普及的地区更为强烈。我们进一步证明，不利的天气冲击加剧了高税收市镇的动乱。然后，我们记录了税收如何助长了“大恐慌”期间动乱的蔓延——这场席卷法国并于1789年7月达到顶峰、最终废除封建特权的起义浪潮。最后，我们将税收与巴黎的革命政治联系起来，证明来自高税收选区的代表更有可能将税收制度描述为压迫性的、支持革命、要求废除君主制，并投票支持处决国王。

Screen More, Sell Later: Screening and Dynamic Signaling in the Mortgage Market

更多筛查，更晚出售：抵押贷款市场中的筛查与动态信号传递

Manuel Adelino, Bin Wei, Feng Zhao

Programs：AP, CF；JEL：G01, G21, G23, G32, R30

Abstract

We develop a dynamic model of asset origination with unobservable screening effort and signaling of loan quality through delayed sale by extending Vanasco (2017). The theory predicts a positive relationship between screening effort and the strength of the signal. We test this central prediction using U.S. mortgage data, measuring screening effort by mortgage processing time. Consistent with the theory, loans that take longer to process are sold with longer delay and are less likely to default, even though observably riskier loans are processed more slowly. Our calibration reveals that the subprime mortgage market operated in a parameter range with endogenous fragility.

中文摘要

我们通过扩展Vanasco (2017)的模型，发展了一个关于资产发起的动态模型，其中包含不可观测的筛查努力以及通过延迟出售来传递贷款质量的信号。该理论预测筛查努力与信号强度之间存在正相关关系。我们使用美国抵押贷款数据检验了这一核心预测，以抵押贷款处理时间衡量筛查努力。与理论一致，处理时间更长的贷款被出售时延迟更长，且违约可能性更低，尽管可观测风险更高的贷款处理得更慢。我们的校准显示，次贷抵押贷款市场在一个具有内生脆弱性的参数区间内运行。

Interest Rates and Equity Valuations

利率与股票估值

Niels Joachim Gormsen, Eben Lazarus

Programs：AP；JEL：E44, F30, G1, G10, G12

Abstract

A large body of work has sought to measure the effect of interest rates on equity valuations. The challenge in doing so is that both are endogenous, and their comovement depends on the forces driving interest-rate changes. To address this problem, we develop and estimate a decomposition that splits movements in real rates into three structural drivers: changes in expected growth, risk, or “pure discounting.” We show that only pure discount-rate shocks transmit one-for-one to equity valuations, with little or negative transmission of growth and risk shocks. Implementing our decomposition with a global panel of growth expectations and asset prices, we find a weak unconditional relation between valuations and real rates but a strong relation with the pure discounting component, which explains 80% of cross-country valuation changes since 1990. In the U.S., we find that 35% of the interest-rate decline is attributable to pure discounting, implying that only a fraction of the change in rates has passed through directly to equities. We use the decomposition to revisit evidence on the role of interest rates in explaining price variation, and to study higher-frequency returns, cross-sectional rate exposures, duration-matched equity premia, and reactions to monetary policy.

中文摘要

大量研究试图衡量利率对股票估值的影响。这样做的挑战在于两者都是内生的，它们的共同变动取决于驱动利率变化的因素。为了解决这个问题，我们开发并估计了一种分解方法，将实际利率的变动分解为三个结构性驱动因素：预期增长的变化、风险的变化或“纯贴现”的变化。我们表明，只有纯贴现率冲击会一对一地传导至股票估值，而增长和风险冲击的传导作用很小甚至为负。使用全球增长预期和资产价格面板数据实施我们的分解，我们发现估值与实际利率之间存在微弱的不条件关系，但与纯贴现成分存在强关系，这解释了自1990年以来80%的跨国估值变化。在美国，我们发现35%的利率下降可归因于纯贴现，这意味着只有一部分利率变化直接传导至股票。我们利用这种分解重新审视了利率在解释价格变动中的作用证据，并研究了更高频率的回报、横截面利率敞口、久期匹配的股权溢价以及对货币政策的反应。

The Politics of AI

人工智能的政治学

Nicholas Bloom, Christos Makridis

Programs：IO, LS, POL, PR；JEL：J0

Abstract

Using new data from the Gallup Workforce Panel, we show that the apparent partisan divide in workplace AI adoption is largely an artifact of educational and occupational sorting rather than ideological differences in technology adoption. While Democrats are consistently more likely than Republicans to report frequent AI use—30.1% versus 25% in Q1:2026—and exhibit deeper task-level integration across a broader range of work activities, this raw gap shrinks to statistical insignificance once we control for educational attainment, and reverses sign in fully saturated models with occupation and industry fixed effects. Democrats work in more AI-exposed occupations and more AI-supportive organizational environments, but these differences reflect where people work and what skills they hold, not political identity per se. The one dimension that resists this compositional explanation is perceived job displacement risk: partisan differences were statistically indistinguishable through 2025 but a 4.2 percentage point gap has emerged by Q1:2026, suggesting that belief divergence may be beginning to outpace the structural factors that explain behavioral differences. In this sense, the “politics of AI” is better understood as the political geography of human capital and occupational structure than as an ideological cleavage in technology adoption.

中文摘要

利用盖洛普劳动力小组的新数据，我们表明，工作场所人工智能采用中明显的党派分歧在很大程度上是教育和职业分类的产物，而非技术采用中的意识形态差异。虽然民主党人报告频繁使用人工智能的比例始终高于共和党人——2026年第一季度为30.1%对25%——并且在更广泛的工作活动中展现出更深层次的任务级整合，但一旦我们控制了教育程度，这一原始差距就会缩小到统计上不显著，并且在包含职业和行业固定效应的完全饱和模型中，其符号会发生逆转。民主党人在更易受人工智能影响的职业和更支持人工智能的组织环境中工作，但这些差异反映的是人们的工作地点和所拥有的技能，而非政治身份本身。抵制这种构成性解释的一个维度是感知的工作替代风险：党派差异在2025年之前统计上无法区分，但到2026年第一季度出现了4.2个百分点的差距，这表明信念分歧可能开始超过解释行为差异的结构性因素。从这个意义上说，“人工智能的政治学”更应被理解为人力资本和职业结构的政治地理学，而非技术采用中的意识形态分歧。

Early Learning Across Decades: Advances in Measuring Head Start Effectiveness

跨越数十年的早期学习：衡量“开端计划”有效性的进展

Chloe Gibbs

Programs：CH；JEL：I24, I38, J13

Abstract

The Head Start program, launched in 1965 and targeted to children from disadvantaged backgrounds, remains the largest early childhood care and education (ECE) program in the United States and the only one deployed at the federal level. As such, the Head Start literature spans several decades and now allows for synthesis of findings from different contexts, time periods, and research designs. This paper provides a comprehensive assessment of the rigorous evidence measuring Head Start’s effects on children, their families, and society. The focus is on how (1) the contrast between program and counterfactual conditions, (2) takeup of the program among eligible populations, and (3) treatment–effect heterogeneity inform interpretation and applicability of key findings. The paper presents implications of the evidence for the modern-day Head Start program and an adjacent, policy-relevant research agenda.

中文摘要

“开端计划”始于1965年，针对来自弱势背景的儿童，至今仍是美国最大的早期儿童保育和教育项目，也是唯一一个在联邦层面部署的项目。因此，关于“开端计划”的文献跨越了数十年，现在可以综合来自不同背景、时期和研究设计的发现。本文对衡量“开端计划”对儿童、家庭和社会影响的严谨证据进行了全面评估。重点在于：(1) 项目条件与反事实条件之间的对比，(2) 合格人群对该项目的参与度，以及 (3) 处理效应异质性如何影响对关键发现的解读和适用性。本文提出了这些证据对现代“开端计划”项目以及一项相关的、具有政策意义的研究议程的启示。

Field Experiments in the Science of Science: Lessons from Peer Review and the Evaluation of New Knowledge

科学学中的实地实验：来自同行评审和新知识评估的教训

Kevin Boudreau

Programs：PR；JEL：A14, C93, D02, D83, G18, I23, O31, O38

Abstract

Scientific evaluation and peer review govern the allocation of resources and certification of knowledge in science, yet have been subjected to limited causal investigation. This chapter synthesizes randomized experiments embedded in live peer-evaluation systems at journals, conferences, and funding agencies, restricting attention to published studies. I organize this evidence using a Q–A–R–S framework that decomposes peer review into attributes of submissions (Q), authors (A), reviewers (R), and evaluation systems (S), and interpret outcomes through a view of the core problem of scientific evaluation as assessing new knowledge using the existing stock of knowledge.

中文摘要

科学评估和同行评审支配着科学资源的分配和知识的认证，然而对其进行的因果研究却很有限。本章综合了嵌入在期刊、会议和资助机构的实时同行评估系统中的随机实验，仅关注已发表的研究。我使用一个Q-A-R-S框架来组织这些证据，该框架将同行评审分解为投稿的属性（Q）、作者（A）、评审人（R）和评估系统（S），并通过一个视角来解释结果，该视角将科学评估的核心问题视为利用现有知识存量来评估新知识。

Searching for Fish in Trees (緣木求魚)? Economic Development when Context Matters

缘木求鱼？当背景重要时的经济发展

Jacob Moscona, Nathan Nunn, James A. Robinson

Programs：DEV, POL；JEL：A33, O10, O2, Z1

Abstract

In this chapter, we develop a framework for analyzing the determinants of economic development and their implications for policy. We distinguish between classical determinants—such as inputs into education and health, access to credit, and geography—and non-classical determinants—including cultural values, social norms, beliefs, identity, and social organization. We classify these determinants along two policy-relevant dimensions: whether they can be clearly ranked in terms of their contribution to development (vertical versus horizontal) and whether they can be directly altered through policy intervention (manipulable versus non-manipulable).We show that even for classical determinants, policy impacts are often hard to predict and are mediated by local social and cultural context. These issues are more pronounced for non-classical determinants, which are more complicated to change through policy intervention and more difficult to rank in welfare terms. In some cases, traits commonly viewed as obstacles to development may be well-adapted to local conditions or even supportive of economic performance, a possibility we refer to as “reverse vertical.” Building on Hirschman’s (1967) distinction between trait-making and trait-taking policymaking, we argue that interventions that attempt to directly transform non-classical determinants often rest on fragile assumptions about ranking and manipulability and risk generating unintended or adverse effects. Can citizen-led protests lead to meaningful economic redistribution? We study the effects of protests on fiscal redistribution using evidence from Nigeria. We digitized twenty-six years of public finance data from 1988 to 2016 to examine the effects of protests on intergovernmental transfers. We find that protests increase transfers to protesting regions, but only in areas that are politically aligned with disbursing governments. Two natural experiments- the large-scale 2012 Occupy Nigeria protests and 2015 presidential election- validate these results. Protesters also face increased police violence, particularly in non-aligned regions. The results show that protests can influence fiscal redistribution.

中文摘要

公民领导的抗议能否带来有意义的经济再分配？我们利用尼日利亚的证据研究了抗议对财政再分配的影响。我们将 1988 年至 2016 年共二十六年的公共财政数据数字化，以考察抗议对政府间转移支付的影响。我们发现，抗议增加了对抗议地区的转移支付，但仅限于与拨款政府政治立场一致的地区。两个自然实验——2012 年大规模的“占领尼日利亚”抗议活动和 2015 年总统选举——验证了这些结果。抗议者还面临增加的警察暴力，尤其是在非一致地区。结果表明，抗议可以影响财政再分配。
