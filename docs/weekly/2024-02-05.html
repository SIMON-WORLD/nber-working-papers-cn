No. 1NBER w32126

Education Inequalities in Latin America and the Caribbean

拉丁美洲和加勒比地区的教育不平等

Raquel Fernández, Carmen Pagés, Miguel Szekely, Ivonne Acevedo

Programs：DEV；JEL：I20, O15

Abstract

Education is a crucial asset for a country’s economic prospects and for its inhabitants. In addition to its direct impact on growth via the accumulation of human capital, it is a critical ingredient in producing an informed citizenry, enhancing their ability to obtain and exert human and political rights and their facility to adapt to changing environments (generated by, e.g., technological or climatic change) among other benefits. In this paper, we study education inequality in LAC (both in quantity and quality), assess how it emerges and amplifies or dampens existing inequalities, and examine the interaction of education inequality with other forms of inequality, primarily income and labor market outcomes. Our analysis is based on primary data from multiple sources.

中文摘要

教育是一个国家经济前景及其居民的关键资产。除了通过人力资本积累对增长产生直接影响外，教育还是培养知情公民、增强其获取和行使人权及政治权利的能力、以及适应（例如由技术或气候变化引起的）不断变化的环境的能力等益处的重要因素。在本文中，我们研究了拉美和加勒比地区的教育不平等（包括数量和质量），评估了这种不平等如何产生以及如何放大或抑制现有不平等，并考察了教育不平等与其他形式不平等（主要是收入和劳动力市场结果）之间的相互作用。我们的分析基于来自多个来源的原始数据。

No. 2NBER w32125

The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Algorithms

算法的不合理有效性

Jens Ludwig, Sendhil Mullainathan, Ashesh Rambachan

Programs：CH, ED, EH, LE, LS, PE；JEL：C45, C54, D61, H40, I00, K00

Abstract

We calculate the social return on algorithmic interventions (specifically their Marginal Value of Public Funds) across multiple domains of interest to economists—regulation, criminal justice, medicine, and education. Though these algorithms are different, the results are similar and striking. Each one has an MVPF of infinity: not only does it produce large benefits, it provides a “free lunch.” We do not take these numbers to mean these interventions ought to be necessarily scaled, but rather that much more R&D should be devoted to developing and carefully evaluating algorithmic solutions to policy problems.

中文摘要

我们计算了算法干预（具体而言是其公共资金边际价值）在多个经济学家感兴趣的领域——监管、刑事司法、医学和教育——中的社会回报。尽管这些算法各不相同，但结果相似且引人注目。每个算法的公共资金边际价值都是无穷大：它不仅产生巨大收益，还提供了“免费午餐”。我们不认为这些数字意味着这些干预措施必然应该被推广，而是认为应该投入更多的研发资源来开发和仔细评估针对政策问题的算法解决方案。

No. 3NBER w32124

Credible Numbers: A Procedure for Reporting Statistical Precision in Parameter Estimates

可信的数字：报告参数估计统计精度的程序

Nicolas Astier, Frank A. Wolak

Programs：EEE, IO；JEL：C01, C10, C12

Abstract

Econometric software packages typically report a fixed number of decimal digits for coefficient estimates and their associated standard errors. This practice misses the opportunity to use rounding rules that convey statistical precision. Using insights from the testing statistical hypotheses of equivalence literature, we propose a methodology that only reports decimal digits in a parameter estimate that reject a hypothesis of statistical equivalence. Applying this methodology to all articles published in the American Economic Review between 2000 and 2022, we find that over 60% of the printed digits in coefficient estimates do not convey statistically meaningful information according to our definition of a significant digit. If one additional digit beyond the last significant digit is reported for each coefficient estimate, then approximately one-third of the printed digits in our sample would not be reported.

中文摘要

计量经济学软件包通常为系数估计及其标准误报告固定的小数位数。这种做法错失了使用四舍五入规则来传达统计精度的机会。利用检验统计等价假设的文献中的见解，我们提出了一种方法，该方法仅报告参数估计中拒绝统计等价假设的小数位数。将这种方法应用于2000年至2022年间发表在《美国经济评论》上的所有文章，我们发现，根据我们对有效数字的定义，系数估计中超过60%的印刷数字不传达统计上有意义的信息。如果每个系数估计报告最后一个有效数字之后的一个额外数字，那么我们的样本中大约三分之一的印刷数字将不会被报告。

No. 4NBER w32123

The Determinants of Declining Internal Migration

国内移民减少的决定因素

William W. Olney, Owen Thompson

Programs：ITI, LS, PE；JEL：J31, J61, R23, R31

Abstract

Internal migration in the United States has declined substantially over the past several decades, which has important implications for individual welfare, macroeconomic adjustments, and other key outcomes. This paper studies the determinants of internal migration and how they have changed over time. We use administrative data from the IRS covering the universe of bilateral moves between every Commuting Zone (CZ) in the country over a 23 year period. This data is linked to information on local wage levels and home prices, and we estimate bilateral migration determinants in rich regression specifications that contain CZ-pair fixed effects. Consistent with theoretical predictions, results show that migration is decreasing with origin wages and destination home prices, and is increasing with destination wages and origin home prices. We then examine the contributions of earnings and home prices to the noted overall decline in internal migration. These analyses show that wages on their own would have led to an increase in migration rates, primarily because migrants are increasingly responsive to high earnings levels in potential destination CZs. However, these wage effects have been more than offset by housing related factors, which have increasingly impeded internal mobility. In particular, migration has become much less responsive to housing prices in the origin CZ, such that many households that would have left in response to high home prices several decades ago now choose to stay.

中文摘要

过去几十年来，美国的国内移民大幅减少，这对个人福利、宏观经济调整和其他关键结果具有重要影响。本文研究了国内移民的决定因素及其随时间的变化。我们使用来自美国国税局的行政数据，该数据涵盖了23年间全国每个通勤区之间的所有双边迁移。这些数据与当地工资水平和房价信息相关联，我们在包含通勤区对固定效应的丰富回归设定中估计了双边移民的决定因素。与理论预测一致，结果表明移民随迁出地工资和迁入地房价的上升而减少，随迁入地工资和迁出地房价的上升而增加。然后，我们考察了收入和房价对已观察到的国内移民总体下降的贡献。这些分析表明，仅工资因素本会导致移民率上升，这主要是因为移民对潜在迁入通勤区的高收入水平越来越敏感。然而，这些工资效应已被住房相关因素所抵消，这些因素日益阻碍了国内流动性。特别是，移民对迁出地通勤区房价的反应已变得非常不敏感，以至于许多几十年前会因高房价而离开的家庭现在选择留下。

No. 5NBER w32122

Experimental Estimates of College Coaching on Postsecondary Re-enrollment

大学辅导对高等教育再入学的实验估计

Lesley J. Turner, Oded Gurantz

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：I23, I24

Abstract

College attendance has increased significantly over the last few decades, but dropout rates remain high, with fewer than half of all adults ultimately obtaining a postsecondary credential. This project investigates whether one-on-one college coaching improves college attendance and completion outcomes for former low- and middle-income income state aid recipients who attended college but left prior to earning a degree. We conducted a randomized control trial with approximately 8,000 former students in their early- to mid-20s. Half of participants assigned to the treatment group were offered the opportunity to receive coaching services from InsideTrack, with all communication done remotely via phone or video. Intent-to-treat analyses based on assignment to coaching shows no impacts on college enrollment and we can rule out effects larger than a two-percentage point (5%) increase in subsequent Fall enrollment.

中文摘要

过去几十年中，大学入学率显著增加，但辍学率仍然很高，最终获得高等教育证书的成年人不到一半。本项目研究了一对一大学辅导是否改善了曾就读大学但未获得学位就离开的低收入和中等收入州助学金获得者的大学入学和完成情况。我们对约8000名20岁出头的前学生进行了一项随机对照试验。分配给治疗组的一半参与者有机会接受InsideTrack提供的辅导服务，所有沟通均通过电话或视频远程进行。基于辅导分配的意向性治疗分析显示，对大学入学没有影响，并且我们可以排除后续秋季入学率增加超过两个百分点（5%）的效应。

No. 6NBER w32121

Did Violence Against Asian-Americans Rise in 2020? Evidence from a Novel Approach to Measuring Potentially Racially-Motivated Attacks

2020年针对亚裔美国人的暴力行为是否增加？一种衡量潜在种族动机攻击的新方法提供的证据

Aleksei Knorre, Britte Van Tiem, Aaron Chalfin

Programs：LE；JEL：K4, K40, K42

Abstract

Did anti-Asian violence rise after the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic? Efforts to answer this question are compromised by the inherent difficulty of measuring racially-motivated crimes as well as concerns that reporting of racially-motivated hate crimes may have changed due to their increased salience during the pandemic. We pursue an alternative approach to studying whether anti-Asian violence rose after March 2020 that addresses each of these concerns. Using data from the FBI’s National Incident-Based Reporting System, we study inter-race violence occurring in public spaces. While public violence declined among all Americans after March 2020, the share of public violence directed at Asian-Americans by people who were previously unknown to them – or were acquaintances – rose more than it did for other Americans. While this relationship did not hold among an auxiliary sample of large US cities, the national evidence is consistent with a modest increase in racially- motivated violence directed towards Asian-Americans.

中文摘要

新冠疫情爆发后，针对亚裔的暴力行为是否增加？回答这个问题的努力受到衡量种族动机犯罪固有困难的影响，同时也因为疫情期间种族动机仇恨犯罪报道可能因其显著性提高而发生变化。我们采用另一种方法来研究2020年3月后针对亚裔的暴力行为是否增加，以解决上述每个问题。利用联邦调查局国家基于事件的报告系统的数据，我们研究了发生在公共空间的种族间暴力。虽然2020年3月后所有美国人的公共暴力行为有所下降，但由之前不认识的人或熟人针对亚裔美国人的公共暴力所占比例比其他美国人上升得更多。虽然这种关系在一个美国大城市辅助样本中不成立，但全国证据与针对亚裔美国人的种族动机暴力略有增加是一致的。

Competitive Effects of Charter Schools

特许学校的竞争效应

David N. Figlio, Cassandra Hart, Krzysztof Karbownik

Programs：ED；JEL：H75, I21, I22, I28

Abstract

Using a rich dataset that merges student-level school records with birth records, and leveraging three alternative identification strategies, we explore how increase in access to charter schools in twelve districts in Florida affects students remaining in traditional public schools (TPS). We consistently find that competition stemming from the opening of new charter schools improves reading—but not math—performance and it also decreases absenteeism of students who remain in the TPS. Results are modest in magnitude.

中文摘要

利用一个将学生层面的学校记录与出生记录相结合的数据集，并采用三种替代识别策略，我们探讨了佛罗里达州十二个学区中特许学校入学机会的增加如何影响留在传统公立学校的学生。我们一致发现，来自新开设特许学校的竞争改善了阅读成绩——但未改善数学成绩——并且还减少了留在传统公立学校学生的缺勤率。这些结果在幅度上是适度的。

No. 8NBER w32119

The Consequences of Abuse, Neglect and Cyber-bullying on the Wellbeing of the Young

虐待、忽视和网络欺凌对青少年福祉的影响

David G. Blanchflower, Alex Bryson

Programs：LS；JEL：I1, I31, J16

Abstract

Using cross-sectional data files for the United States we show that difficulties experienced in childhood - so-called Adverse Child Experiences (ACE)s - are strongly and significantly associated with mental health in adulthood. Our evidence is taken from eight Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) surveys from 2009-2023 which contain a special supplement asking respondents to recall abuse in their childhood. We find that poor mental health is on the rise in the United States, particularly among young women. This upward trend among the young is also apparent from the National Health Interview Surveys 1997-2021, the Healthy Minds surveys of 2007-2023 and the Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System (YRBSS) for high school students. We find ACEs are strongly correlated with poor mental health among both the young and older people and the effect is additive. The impact of living with a household member with poor mental health is large relative to other ACEs and is particularly pronounced among younger people. Being bullied, including electronically, is also strongly negatively associated with the wellbeing of high school students. Time spent in front of a screen has been rising over time for the young and has an independent negative impact on their mental health over and above bullying, one which is more pronounced for young women.

中文摘要

使用美国的横截面数据文件，我们表明童年时期经历的困难——即所谓的不良童年经历——与成年期的心理健康状况强烈且显著相关。我们的证据来自2009年至2023年的八次行为风险因素监测系统调查，这些调查包含一个特殊补充部分，要求受访者回忆童年时期的虐待经历。我们发现，美国心理健康状况不佳的情况正在上升，尤其是在年轻女性中。年轻人中的这一上升趋势在1997年至2021年的全国健康访谈调查、2007年至2023年的健康心态调查以及针对高中生的青少年风险行为监测系统中也很明显。我们发现不良童年经历与年轻人和老年人的心理健康状况不佳都密切相关，且这种影响是累加性的。与其他不良童年经历相比，与心理健康状况不佳的家庭成员同住的影响较大，并且在年轻人中尤为显著。遭受欺凌（包括电子欺凌）也与高中生的福祉呈强烈负相关。年轻人花在屏幕前的时间随着时间的推移而增加，并且除了欺凌之外，这对他们的心理健康产生了独立的负面影响，这种影响在年轻女性中更为明显。

No. 10NBER w32117

Difference-in-differences with a Continuous Treatment

连续处理变量的双重差分法

Brantly Callaway, Andrew Goodman-Bacon, Pedro H. C. Sant'Anna

Programs：LS；JEL：C01, C14

Abstract

This paper analyzes difference-in-differences designs with a continuous treatment. We show that treatment effect on the treated-type parameters can be identified under a generalized parallel trends assumption that is similar to the binary treatment setup. However, interpreting differences in these parameters across different values of the treatment can be particularly challenging due to selection bias that is not ruled out by the parallel trends assumption. We discuss alternative, typically stronger, assumptions that alleviate these challenges. We also provide a variety of treatment effect decomposition results, highlighting that parameters associated with popular linear two-way fixed-effect (TWFE) specifications can be hard to interpret, even when there are only two time periods. We introduce alternative estimation procedures that do not suffer from these TWFE drawbacks, and show in an application that they can lead to different conclusions.

中文摘要

本文分析了具有连续处理变量的双重差分设计。我们表明，在类似于二元处理设置的一般化平行趋势假设下，可以识别处理组处理效应类型的参数。然而，由于平行趋势假设并未排除的选择性偏差，解释这些参数在不同处理值之间的差异可能特别具有挑战性。我们讨论了通常更强的替代假设，以缓解这些挑战。我们还提供了各种处理效应分解结果，强调即使只有两个时期，与流行的线性双向固定效应规范相关的参数也可能难以解释。我们介绍了不受这些双向固定效应缺陷影响的替代估计程序，并在一个应用中表明它们可能导致不同的结论。

No. 11NBER w32116

How Do Surrogates Make Treatment Decisions for Patients with Dementia? An Experimental Survey Study

代理人为痴呆症患者做出治疗决策的方式：一项实验性调查研究

Lauren Hersch Nicholas, Kenneth M. Langa, Scott D. Halpern, Mario Macis

Programs：AG, EH；JEL：C99, I12, J14

Abstract

Despite the growing need for surrogate decision-making for older adults, little is known about how surrogates make decisions and whether advance directives would change decision-making. We conducted a nationally representative experimental survey that cross-randomized cognitive impairment, gender, and characteristics of advance care planning among hospitalized older adults through a series of vignettes. Our study yielded three main findings: first, respondents were much less likely to recommend life-sustaining treatments for patients with dementia, especially after personal exposure. Second, respondents were more likely to ignore patient preferences for life-extending treatment when the patient had dementia, and choose unwanted life-extending treatments for patients without dementia. Third, in scenarios where the patient's wishes were unclear, respondents were more likely to choose treatments that matched their own preferences. These findings underscore the need for improved communication and decision-making processes for patients with cognitive impairment and highlight the importance of choosing a surrogate decision-maker with similar treatment preferences.

中文摘要

尽管老年人对代理决策的需求日益增长，但关于代理人如何做出决策以及预先指示是否会改变决策过程，我们知之甚少。我们进行了一项具有全国代表性的实验性调查，通过一系列情景案例，交叉随机化了认知障碍、性别以及预先护理计划特征，对象是住院老年人。我们的研究得出三个主要发现：首先，受访者向痴呆症患者推荐维持生命治疗的可能性要低得多，尤其是在个人接触后。其次，当患者患有痴呆症时，受访者更有可能忽略患者对延长生命治疗的偏好，并为没有痴呆症的患者选择不想要的延长生命治疗。第三，在患者意愿不明确的情景中，受访者更有可能选择符合自身偏好的治疗。这些发现强调了改善认知障碍患者沟通和决策过程的必要性，并突出了选择具有相似治疗偏好的代理决策者的重要性。

No. 12NBER w32115

Whatever-It-Takes Policymaking during the Pandemic

疫情期间的“不惜一切代价”政策制定

Kathryn M.E. Dominguez, Andrea Foschi

Programs：IFM；JEL：E44, E58, F42, G14

Abstract

Central banks across the globe introduced large-scale asset purchase programs to address the unprecedented circumstances experienced during the pandemic. Many of these programs were announced as open-ended to shock-and-awe market participants and restore confidence in financial markets. This paper examines whether these whatever-it-takes announcements had larger effects than announcements with explicit limits on scale. We use a narrative approach to categorize announcements made by twenty-two central banks, and event study, propensity-score-matching, and local projection methods to measure the short-term effects of policy announcements on exchange rates and sovereign bond yields. We find that on average a central bank's first whatever-it-takes announcement lowers 10-year bond yields by an additional 47 basis points relative to size-limited announcements, suggesting that communication of potential policy scale matters. Our results for yields hold for both advanced and emerging economies, while exchange rates go in opposing directions, muting their response when we group all countries together.

John Beshears, Matthew Blakstad, James J. Choi, Christopher Firth, John Gathergood, David Laibson, Richard Notley, Jesal D. Sheth, Will Sandbrook, Neil Stewart

Programs：AG, PE；JEL：D14, D15, D90, G51, J32

Abstract

Does automatic enrollment into retirement saving increase household debt? We study the randomized roll-out of automatic enrollment pensions to ~160,000 employers in the United Kingdom with 2-29 employees. We find that the additional savings generated through automatic enrollment are partially offset by increases in unsecured debt. Over the first 41 months after enrollment, each additional month increases the average automatically enrolled employee’s pension savings by £33-£39, unsecured debt (such as personal loans and bank overdrafts) by £7, the likelihood of having a mortgage by 0.05 percentage points, and mortgage balances by £120. Automatic enrollment causes loan defaults to fall and credit scores to rise modestly.

The Causal Effects of Global Supply Chain Disruptions on Macroeconomic Outcomes: Evidence and Theory

全球供应链中断对宏观经济结果的因果影响：证据与理论

Xiwen Bai, Jesús Fernández-Villaverde, Yiliang Li, Francesco Zanetti

Programs：EFG；JEL：E32, E58, J64

Abstract

We study the causal effects and policy implications of global supply chain disruptions. We construct a new index of supply chain disruptions from the mandatory automatic identification system data of container ships, developing a novel spatial clustering algorithm that determines real-time congestion from the position, speed, and heading of container ships in major ports around the globe. We develop a model with search frictions between producers and retailers that links spare productive capacity with congestion in the goods market and the responses of output and prices to supply chain shocks. The co-movements of output, prices, and spare capacity yield unique identifying restrictions for supply chain disturbances that allow us to study the causal effects of such disruptions. We document how supply chain shocks drove inflation during 2021 but that, in 2022, traditional demand and supply shocks also played an important role in explaining inflation. Finally, we show how monetary policy is more effective in taming inflation after a global supply chain shock than in regular circumstances.

中文摘要

我们研究了全球供应链中断的因果影响和政策含义。我们利用集装箱船的强制性自动识别系统数据构建了一个新的供应链中断指数，开发了一种新颖的空间聚类算法，该算法根据全球主要港口集装箱船的位置、速度和航向确定实时拥堵情况。我们建立了一个包含生产者和零售商之间搜索摩擦的模型，将闲置生产能力与商品市场拥堵以及产出和价格对供应链冲击的反应联系起来。产出、价格和闲置产能的共同变动为供应链扰动提供了独特的识别限制，使我们能够研究此类中断的因果影响。我们记录了供应链冲击如何在2021年推动通胀，但在2022年，传统的需求和供给冲击在解释通胀方面也发挥了重要作用。最后，我们展示了在全球供应链冲击后，货币政策在抑制通胀方面比正常情况下更有效。

Optimal Trade Policy with International Technology Diffusion

国际技术扩散下的最优贸易政策

Yan Bai, Keyu Jin, Dan Lu, Hanxi Wang

Programs：ITI；JEL：F1, F13, O3, O49

Abstract

We study optimal dynamic trade policies in an Eaton-Kortum model with technology diffusion through trade. The process of innovation and diffusion is one in which new ideas are combined with insights from others. Trade thus affects technology by determining the distribution from which producers draw their insights. Our theory shows that optimal policies capture a dynamic motive for a country to alter global technology. These policies take into account selection effects, country endowments, and other alterations to trade patterns that affect the degree and quality of diffusion. We provide explicit formulas showing that a Home country would like to subsidize imports from places that improve the quality of learning at Home; or lower its export tax to another country if a) higher productivity in that country is good for the Home, and b) more exports to that country improve the quality of learning and, in turn, the country’s technology. We also calibrate the model using cross-country data and quantify dynamic trade policies and their attendant welfare implications.
