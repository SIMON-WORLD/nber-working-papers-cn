No. 1NBER w31870

Long-term Care in Germany

德国的长期护理

Johannes Geyer, Axel H. Börsch-Supan, Peter Haan, Elsa Perdrix

Programs：AG；JEL：H51, I13, I18

Abstract

This chapter provides an overview of the German long-term care insurance. We document care needs and wellbeing of the elderly population. Moreover, we provide a detailed description of the German long-term care institutions (sources of finance and types of benefits), the professional care work force, and informal caregivers. Finally, we document expenditures on long-term care and estimate the value of informal care. The cost of long-term care for the elderly (65+), including both cost of nursing home and home health agency, reached 61 billion euro in 2019. Half of these spending are for nursing homes while only about 22.5% of beneficiaries use these institutions. Out-of-pocket spending differs greatly between modes of care. Out-of-pocket expenditures make up only about 7% of total expenditures for home care. In nursing homes, 41% of expenditures are out-of-pocket payment. Most of the expenditures are covered by the long-term care insurance. The share of other governmental schemes in expenditures for inpatient care is relatively high. This is explained by a high rate of benefit recipients who cannot afford co-payments for nursing homes: about one-third of all nursing home residents receive means- and wealth-tested social assistance. If we add the costs of informal care the share of privately financed care amounts to nearly 60% of total expenditures.

中文摘要

本章概述了德国的长期护理保险。我们记录了老年人口的护理需求和福祉。此外，我们详细描述了德国的长期护理制度（资金来源和福利类型）、专业护理劳动力和非正式护理人员。最后，我们记录了长期护理支出并估算了非正式护理的价值。2019年，65岁及以上老年人的长期护理成本（包括养老院和家庭护理机构成本）达到610亿欧元。其中一半支出用于养老院，但只有约22.5%的受益人使用这些机构。不同护理模式下的自付支出差异很大。自付支出仅占家庭护理总支出的约7%。在养老院中，41%的支出是自付的。大部分支出由长期护理保险覆盖。其他政府计划在住院护理支出中的份额相对较高。这可以解释为有大量福利领取者无力承担养老院的共付额：约三分之一的养老院居民接受基于收入和财富调查的社会救助。如果加上非正式护理的成本，私人资助的护理份额接近总支出的60%。

No. 2NBER w31869

One Says Goodbye, Another Says Hello: Turnover and Compensation in the Early Care and Education Sector

一声告别，一声问候：早期保育与教育行业的员工流动与薪酬

Flavio Cunha, Marcos Lee

Programs：CH；JEL：I2, J24, J39, J49

Abstract

The quality of the early environment children experience influences their human capital development. We investigate retention and compensation in the Early Care and Education workforce by merging datasets from three different government agencies in Texas. We employ non-structural methods to compare turnover and pay in Early Care and Education with those in other sectors that employ similar workers. We estimate a dynamic discrete choice occupational model to quantify the labor supply and turnover elasticities in this industry. In addition, we simulate the impact of wage supplementation programs.

中文摘要

儿童早期所处环境的质量影响其人力资本发展。我们通过合并德克萨斯州三个不同政府机构的数据集，研究了早期保育与教育行业的员工留任与薪酬问题。我们采用非结构性方法，将早期保育与教育行业的员工流动率和薪酬与雇佣类似工人的其他行业进行比较。我们估计了一个动态离散选择职业模型，以量化该行业的劳动力供给弹性和员工流动弹性。此外，我们还模拟了工资补贴计划的影响。

No. 3NBER w31868

Identification using Revealed Preferences in Linearly Separable Models

利用线性可分模型中的显示偏好进行识别

Nikhil Agarwal, Pearl Z. Li, Paulo J. Somaini

Programs：IO；JEL：C51, C57

Abstract

Revealed preference arguments are commonly used when identifying models of both single-agent decisions and non-cooperative games. We develop general identification results for a large class of models that have a linearly separable payoff structure. Our model allows for both discrete and continuous choice sets. It incorporates widely studied models such as discrete and hedonic choice models, auctions, school choice mechanisms, oligopoly pricing and trading games. We characterize the identified set and show that point identification can be achieved either if the choice set is sufficiently rich or if a variable that shifts preferences is available. Our identification results also suggests an estimation approach. Finally, we implement this approach to estimate values in a combinatorial procurement auction for school lunches in Chile.

中文摘要

显示偏好论证常用于识别单主体决策模型和非合作博弈模型。我们为一类具有线性可分收益结构的大类模型开发了通用的识别结果。我们的模型允许离散和连续的选择集。它涵盖了广泛研究的模型，如离散选择模型、享乐选择模型、拍卖、学校选择机制、寡头定价和交易博弈。我们刻画了识别集，并表明如果选择集足够丰富，或者存在一个改变偏好的变量，则可以实现点识别。我们的识别结果也提出了一种估计方法。最后，我们应用该方法来估计智利学校午餐组合采购拍卖中的价值。

No. 4NBER w31867

The Impact of Right-to-Work Laws on Long Hours and Work Schedules

工作权利法对长时间工作和工作安排的影响

Rania Gihleb, Osea Giuntella, Jian Qi Tan

Programs：EH, LS；JEL：I1, J08

Abstract

Unions play a crucial role in determining wages and employment outcomes. However, union bargaining power may also have important effects on non-pecuniary working conditions. We study the effects of right-to-work laws, which removed agency shop protection and weakened union powers on long hours and non-standard work schedules that may adversely affect workers' health and safety. We exploit variation in the timing of enactment across US states and compare workers in bordering counties across adopting states and states that did not adopt the laws yet. Using the stacked approach to difference-in-differences estimates proposed by Cengiz et al. (2019), we find evidence that right-to-work laws increased the share of workers working long hours by 6%, while there is little evidence of an impact on hourly wages. The effects on long hours are larger in more unionized sectors (i.e. construction, manufacturing, and transportation). While the likelihood of working non-standard hours increases for particular sectors (education and public administration), there is no evidence of a significant increase in the overall sample.

中文摘要

工会在决定工资和就业结果方面发挥着关键作用。然而，工会的议价能力也可能对非金钱性的工作条件产生重要影响。我们研究了工作权利法的影响，这些法律取消了代理商店保护并削弱了工会权力，对可能损害工人健康与安全的长时间工作和非标准工作安排产生影响。我们利用美国各州颁布时间的变化，比较了采用该法律的州与尚未采用该法律的州之间边界县的工人。使用Cengiz等人（2019）提出的堆叠双重差分估计方法，我们发现证据表明，工作权利法使长时间工作工人的比例增加了6%，而对小时工资几乎没有影响。在工会化程度较高的行业（即建筑业、制造业和交通运输业），对长时间工作的影响更大。虽然特定行业（教育和公共管理）非标准工作时间工作的可能性增加，但在整体样本中没有证据表明显著增加。

No. 5NBER w31866

Job Transitions and Employee Earnings After Acquisitions: Linking Corporate and Worker Outcomes

并购后的工作转换与员工收入：连接企业与工人结果

David Arnold, Kevin S. Milligan, Terry Moon, Amirhossein Tavakoli

Programs：LS；JEL：E21, G34, J31, J42, L25

Abstract

This paper connects changes in employer characteristics through job transitions to employee earnings following mergers and acquisitions (M&As). Using firm balance sheet data linked to individual earnings data in Canada and a matched difference-in-differences design, we find that after M&As acquirers expand while targets shrink substantially relative to their matched control groups. Additionally, profit margins decrease for both acquirers and targets in the medium run. Furthermore, workers at target firms suffer losses in earnings, and this decline in earnings is entirely driven by workers who move to other firms after an M&A event. We find that workers leaving target firms after M&As move to larger firms with higher wage premiums, but with much worse match qualities on average. Taken together, it appears that job transitions to employers with poor match qualities primarily explain the post-M&A decline in worker earnings in our setting.

中文摘要

本文通过工作转换将雇主特征的变化与并购后员工的收入联系起来。利用加拿大企业资产负债表数据与个人收入数据，并采用匹配的双重差分设计，我们发现，并购后，收购方相对于其匹配对照组扩张，而目标公司则大幅收缩。此外，在中期内，收购方和目标公司的利润率均有所下降。进一步地，目标公司的工人遭受收入损失，而这种收入下降完全是由并购事件后跳槽到其他公司的工人所驱动的。我们发现，并购后离开目标公司的工人跳槽到了工资溢价更高但平均匹配质量差得多的大型企业。综合来看，在我们的设定中，跳槽到匹配质量差的雇主似乎主要解释了并购后工人收入的下降。

No. 6NBER w31865

The Deposit Business at Large vs. Small Banks

大银行与小银行的存款业务

Adrien d'Avernas, Andrea L. Eisfeldt, Can Huang, Richard Stanton, Nancy Wallace

Programs：AP, CF, EFG；JEL：E0, E40, E44, G0, G2, G21, G28

Abstract

The deposit business differs at large versus small banks. We provide a parsimonious model and extensive empirical evidence supporting the idea that much of the variation in deposit-pricing behavior between large and small banks reflects differences in preferences and technologies. Large banks offer superior liquidity services but lower deposit rates, and locate where customers value their services. In addition to receiving a lower level of deposit rates on average, customers of large banks also exhibit lower rate elasticities. As a result, despite the fact that the locations of large-bank branches have demographics typically associated with greater financial sophistication, large-bank customers earn lower average deposit rates. Our explanation for deposit pricing behavior challenges the idea that deposit pricing is mainly driven by pricing power derived from the large observed degree of concentration in the banking industry.

中文摘要

大银行与小银行的存款业务存在差异。我们提供了一个简洁的模型和广泛的实证证据，支持这样一种观点：大银行和小银行之间存款定价行为的许多差异反映了偏好和技术的不同。大银行提供更优越的流动性服务，但存款利率较低，并且位于客户看重其服务的地点。除了平均获得的存款利率水平较低外，大银行的客户还表现出较低的利率弹性。因此，尽管大银行分支机构的所在地通常具有与更高金融素养相关的人口统计特征，但大银行的客户获得的平均存款利率却较低。我们对存款定价行为的解释挑战了这样一种观点，即存款定价主要由银行业观察到的巨大集中度所带来的定价权驱动。

No. 7NBER w31864

Financial Windfalls, Portfolio Allocations, and Risk Preferences

意外之财、资产配置与风险偏好

Joseph S. Briggs, David Cesarini, Sean Chanwook Lee, Erik Lindqvist, Robert Östling

Programs：AP；JEL：G11, G5

Abstract

We investigate the impact of financial windfalls on household portfolio choices and risk exposure. Exploiting the randomized assignment of lottery prizes in three Swedish lotteries, we find a windfall gain of $100K leads to a 5-percentage-point decrease in the risky share of household portfolios. We show theoretically that negative wealth effects are consistent with both constant and decreasing relative risk aversion and analyze how our empirical estimates help distinguish between competing models of portfolio choice. We further show our results are quantitatively aligned with the predictions of a calibrated dynamic portfolio choice model with nontradable human capital and consumption habits.

中文摘要

我们研究了意外之财对家庭投资组合选择和风险敞口的影响。利用瑞典三种彩票中奖的随机分配，我们发现10万美元的意外之财导致家庭投资组合中风险资产份额下降5个百分点。我们从理论上证明，负财富效应与常数相对风险厌恶和递减相对风险厌恶都是一致的，并分析了我们的实证估计如何帮助区分竞争性的投资组合选择模型。我们进一步表明，我们的结果与一个包含不可交易人力资本和消费习惯的校准动态投资组合选择模型的预测在数量上一致。

No. 8NBER w31863

When do Treasuries Earn the Convenience Yield? — A Hedging Perspective

美国国债何时获得便利收益率？——一个对冲视角

Viral V. Acharya, Toomas Laarits

Programs：AP, IFM, ME；JEL：E4, E5, F3, G11, G12, G15

Abstract

We document that the convenience yield of U.S. Treasuries exhibits properties that are consistent with a hedging perspective of safe assets. The convenience yield tends to be low when the covariance of Treasury returns with the aggregate stock market returns is high. A decomposition of the aggregate stock-bond covariance into terms corresponding to the convenience yield, the frictionless risk-free rate, and default risk reveals that the covariance between stock returns and the convenience yield itself drives the effect in a substantive capacity. We show the convenience yield is reduced with heightened inflation expectations that erode the hedging properties of U.S. Treasuries and other fixed-income money-like assets, inducing a switch to alternatives such as gold; it is also reduced immediately prior to debt-ceiling standoffs and with increases in Treasury supply.

中文摘要

我们记录了美国国债的便利收益率表现出与安全资产对冲视角一致的特征。当国债收益率与股票市场总收益率的协方差较高时，便利收益率往往较低。将股票-债券总协方差分解为对应于便利收益率、无摩擦无风险利率和违约风险的项，表明股票收益率与便利收益率本身之间的协方差在很大程度上驱动了这一效应。我们表明，通胀预期上升会侵蚀美国国债和其他固定收益类货币型资产的对冲属性，导致转向黄金等替代品，从而降低便利收益率；在债务上限僵局之前以及国债供应增加时，便利收益率也会降低。

No. 9NBER w31862

Are Swedish House Prices Too High? Why the Price-to-Income Ratio Is a Misleading Indicator

瑞典房价过高吗？为什么房价收入比是一个误导性指标

Lars E.O. Svensson

Programs：AP, EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E43, G21, G50, R21, R30, R31

Abstract

Appropriate indicators of housing valuation are important for macroprudential policy and assessments of risks to financial stability. Overvalued housing may result in a correction and a fall in house prices, which if large could threaten financial and macroeconomic stability. \nAccording to ECB (2023) and European Systemic Risk Board (2022), Swedish owner-occupied housing (OOH) was overvalued by about 55% in 2021q2, the largest overvaluation in the EU and EEA; according to European Commission (2023c), by about 30% in 2022. These assessments affect warnings and recommendations issued for Swedish economic policy and the shocks in EBA stress tests of Swedish banks. \nBut these assessments are due to the use of misleading indicators: the deviations of price-to-income (PTI) and price-to-rent ratios from their historical averages. They disregard mortgage rates and other housing costs and lack scientific support. According to a large housing literature, it is not the purchase price but the user cost that is the appropriate measure of the cost of living in OOH, the cost of the housing services that the OOH delivers. From this point of view, “Are house prices too high?” is the wrong question. The right question is, “Are user costs too high?” \nNew improved estimates of an upper bound of the user cost are constructed, including an adjustment for a preference shift during the coronavirus crisis in favor of larger and better housing. The upper bound results from a conservative lower bound on house-price expectations below the few existing data points—regular time-series expectations data are not available. \nAccording to the user-cost-to-income (UCTI) ratio, Swedish owner-occupied houses have since 2010 instead become increasingly undervalued (not overvalued), by about 30% in 2019q4. Due to higher mortgage rates, they are less undervalued in 2025q1, but still about 25%. \nFor Sweden, the UCTI and PTI indicators are in fact strongly negatively correlated, with opposite signs. If the UCTI indicator is the right one, the PTI indicator is consistently wrong. \nThe valuation assessments of the ECB, the ESRB, the Commission, the OECD, and the IMF are scrutinized and compared. The problem of misleading indicators and overvaluation assessments—and resulting distorted warnings and recommendations—is not restricted to Sweden but concerns several other countries in the European Union.

中文摘要

适当的住房估值指标对于宏观审慎政策和金融稳定风险评估至关重要。估值过高的住房可能导致价格回调，如果幅度过大，可能威胁金融和宏观经济稳定。根据欧洲央行（2023）和欧洲系统性风险委员会（2022）的数据，2021年第二季度瑞典自住住房估值过高约55%，为欧盟和欧洲经济区最高；根据欧盟委员会（2023c）的数据，2022年估值过高约30%。这些评估影响了针对瑞典经济政策发出的警告和建议，以及欧洲银行管理局对瑞典银行压力测试中的冲击。但这些评估是由于使用了误导性指标：房价收入比和房价租金比与其历史均值的偏离。它们忽略了抵押贷款利率和其他住房成本，缺乏科学依据。根据大量住房文献，衡量自住住房居住成本（即自住住房提供的住房服务成本）的适当指标不是购买价格，而是使用者成本。从这个角度看，“房价过高吗？”是一个错误的问题。正确的问题是，“使用者成本过高吗？”我们构建了使用者成本上限的新改进估计，包括对新冠疫情期间偏好转向更大更好住房的调整。该上限源于对房价预期的保守下限估计，该下限低于现有的少数数据点——常规的时间序列预期数据不可得。根据使用者成本收入比，自2010年以来，瑞典自住住房反而变得越来越被低估（而非高估），2019年第四季度被低估约30%。由于抵押贷款利率上升，2025年第一季度被低估程度有所减轻，但仍约为25%。对于瑞典而言，使用者成本收入比和房价收入比指标实际上呈强烈负相关，符号相反。如果使用者成本收入比指标是正确的，那么房价收入比指标就一直是错误的。我们对欧洲央行、欧洲系统性风险委员会、欧盟委员会、经合组织和国际货币基金组织的估值评估进行了详细审查和比较。误导性指标和高估评估的问题——以及由此产生的扭曲警告和建议——并不仅限于瑞典，还涉及欧盟其他几个国家。

No. 10NBER w31861

Long-term Care in Italy

意大利的长期护理

Agar Brugiavini, Ludovico Carrino, Giacomo Pasini

Programs：AG, EH, PE；JEL：D1, I13

Abstract

The provision of long-term care (LTC) for senior citizens in Italy is at the center of the recent policy debate. Italy has witnessed a spectacular increase in the share of people aged 65 and over and in particular of people aged 80 and over, which could translate in large increases in the number of people in need of care. We show that individuals who are in need of LTC have lower economic resources than the average, so that many frail older people have little financial means to pay out-of-pocket for formal care. In fact, publicly provided care is highly fragmented, with stark differences emerging in terms of coverage and generosity across Italian regions. Hence, the supply of LTC is relying heavily on the informal support of members of the family, especially women, at the same time formal care is characterised by a significant underground economy of unskilled carers.

中文摘要

Abstract

We develop measures of labor-saving and labor-augmenting technology exposure using textual analysis of patents and job tasks. Using US administrative data, we show that exposure to labor-saving technologies negatively affects the earnings of exposed workers. This negative effect is pervasive across both blue- and white-collar workers and across workers of different ages or earnings relative to their peers. In contrast, labor-augmenting technologies have a heterogeneous impact on exposed workers. Programs：EEE, PE；JEL：C93, D12, Q25, Q55

Abstract

We compare the behavior and welfare effects of two popular interventions for resource conservation. The first intervention is social comparison reports (SC), which primarily provide consumers with information motivating behavioral change. The second intervention is real-time feedback (RTF), which primarily provides consumers with information facilitating behavioral change. In a field experiment with around 1,000 participants, we directly observe the interventions’ effects on participants’ behavior. Further, we elicit participants’ willingness to pay for receiving the interventions, both before and after having experienced them for one month. Abstract

Abstract

This paper examines the relationship between extreme socioeconomic disadvantage and poor health by providing the first detailed and accurate picture of mortality patterns among people experiencing homelessness in the U.S. Our analyses center on 140,000 people who were sheltered or unsheltered homeless during the 2010 Census, by far the largest sample ever used to study this population and the only sample designed to be nationally representative. We find that non-elderly people experiencing homelessness have 3.5 times the mortality risk of those who are housed, accounting for differences in demographic characteristics and geography, and that a 40-year-old homeless person faces a similar mortality risk to a housed person nearly twenty years older. Abstract

Difference-in-Difference (DID) estimators are a valuable method for identifying causal effects in the public health researcher’s toolkit. A growing methods literature points out potential problems with DID estimators when treatment is staggered in adoption and varies with time. Despite this, no practical guide exists for addressing these new critiques in public health research. We illustrate these new DID concepts with step-by-step examples, code, and a checklist. We draw insights by comparing the simple 2 × 2 DID design (single treatment group, single control group, two time periods) with more complex cases: additional treated groups, additional time periods of treatment, and with treatment effects possibly varying over time.
