Fiscal Inaction as Monetary Support

作为货币支持的财政不作为

George-Marios Angeletos, Chen Lian, Christian K. Wolf

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E52, E62

Abstract

How does the fiscal framework affect the central bank's ability to stabilize output and inflation? The textbook answer, which assumes Ricardian households, recommends that fiscal adjustment should be fast enough to allow for monetary dominance. We instead argue that, with non-Ricardian households, the central bank may indeed welcome slow, or even no, fiscal adjustment. On the demand side, slow fiscal adjustment helps stabilize aggregate spending; on the supply side, it eases tax distortions, improving the output-inflation trade off. And while the first channel favors slow fiscal adjustment only when the business cycle is dominated by demand shocks, the second channel extends this preference to supply shocks. A quantitative exercise affirms our lessons in the U.S. context, with the central bank preferring virtually no fiscal adjustment over the business cycle.

中文摘要

财政框架如何影响中央银行稳定产出和通胀的能力？教科书式的答案假设李嘉图式家庭，建议财政调整应足够迅速，以实现货币主导。我们反而认为，在存在非李嘉图式家庭的情况下，中央银行可能确实欢迎缓慢甚至不进行财政调整。在需求侧，缓慢的财政调整有助于稳定总支出；在供给侧，它缓解了税收扭曲，改善了产出-通胀权衡。虽然第一个渠道仅在商业周期由需求冲击主导时有利于缓慢的财政调整，但第二个渠道将这种偏好扩展到了供给冲击。一项量化练习在美国背景下证实了我们的结论，即中央银行在商业周期中偏好几乎不进行财政调整。

Digital Incentives in Surveys: Response Rates and Sociodemographic Effects in a Large-Scale Parental Nudge Intervention

调查中的数字激励：大规模家长助推干预中的响应率与社会人口学效应

Kalena Cortes, Brian Holzman, Melissa D. Gentry, Miranda I. Lambert

Programs：CH, ED, LS, PE；JEL：C93, I24, J15

Abstract

This study examines how digital incentives influence survey participation and engagement in a large randomized controlled trial of parents across six school districts. We test how incentive amount and information about vendor options affect response behavior and explore differences by language background. Incentivized parents were more likely to engage in the program, from starting the survey to choosing an essential-goods gift card. However, Spanish-speaking parents exhibited distinct patterns—greater survey participation rates but lower gift redemption rates. Increasing incentive value and providing advance information both improved engagement. Findings inform the design of equitable, effective digital incentive strategies for diverse populations.

Self- and Social Signaling: Evidence from Solar Adoption in California

自我信号与社会信号：来自加州太阳能采用的证据

Bryan K. Bollinger, Kenneth Gillingham, A. Justin Kirkpatrick

Programs：EEE；JEL：D64, H41, Q40

Abstract

Prosocial behavior plays a role in many economic contexts, and it has been explained by altruism, social pressure, signaling, and expectations of fairness and reciprocity. We examine prosocial behavior in a context that allows us to distinguish the role of self-signaling and social signaling from alternative explanations, including warm glow. Our context is residential solar, and self-signaling is separately identified from social signaling by the exogenous visibility of potential solar arrays. We show that the political affiliation of proximate peers influences the extent of self-signaling and is crowded out by the private benefits of installing solar.

中文摘要

The first 1000 days and beyond: The process of child development.

生命最初的1000天及之后：儿童发展的过程

Orazio Attanasio

Programs：CH, EFG；JEL：D1, J24

Abstract

This paper reviews recent developments in the economics of human development, focusing on the early years of life as a critical period for shaping long-term outcomes. Early childhood development is inherently multidimensional: cognitive and socioemotional skills evolve dynamically and interact with health, nutrition, and environmental influences. Economists have contributed to this field by providing a conceptual unifying framework that highlights how key drivers of development reflect the choices of individuals operating under incentives and constraints. Within this framework, the paper emphasizes two central challenges: understanding the interactions among multiple dimensions of development and identifying causal links - particularly the effects of different inputs at different ages. Measurement issues are a recurring theme, given the difficulty of assessing young children and the need for comparability across contexts. The paper also stresses these issues’ policy relevance for poverty reduction and social mobility by discussing early childhood interventions in both developed and developing countries.

中文摘要

本文回顾了人类发展经济学的最新进展，重点关注生命早期作为塑造长期结果的关键时期。儿童早期发展本质上是多维的：认知和社会情感技能动态演变，并与健康、营养和环境影响因素相互作用。经济学家通过提供一个概念性的统一框架为该领域做出了贡献，该框架强调了发展的关键驱动因素如何反映在激励和约束下运作的个体选择。在此框架内，本文强调了两个核心挑战：理解发展多个维度之间的相互作用，以及识别因果联系——特别是不同年龄阶段不同投入的影响。鉴于评估幼儿的困难以及跨背景可比性的需要，测量问题是一个反复出现的主题。本文还通过讨论发达国家和发展中国家的儿童早期干预措施，强调了这些问题对减贫和社会流动性的政策相关性。

What Are the Benefits of Metro Rail in Mumbai, India?

印度孟买地铁的效益是什么？

Palak Suri, Maureen L. Cropper

Programs：EEE；JEL：O18, R1, R3, R4

Abstract

We estimate the effects of the first metro rail line in Mumbai using administrative data on assessed property prices from 2011-18 for 723 subzones in the city. Comparing areas within 1 km of the metro with those beyond 1 km but within 3 km, we estimate the effects on property values for commercial, industrial, and residential properties. We find a significant and persistent increase in prices of 6-8% for residential and commercial land use categories in treated areas relative to control areas after Metro Line 1 opens. We show that commute time savings and improvements in employment accessibility are plausible mechanisms underlying these effects.

中文摘要

我们利用2011-2018年孟买市723个分区评估物业价格的行政数据，估算了该市首条地铁线路的影响。通过比较距地铁1公里以内的区域与距地铁1至3公里以内的区域，我们估算了地铁对商业、工业和住宅物业价值的影响。我们发现，在1号地铁线开通后，相对于控制区域，处理区域内的住宅和商业用地类别的价格出现了显著且持续的6-8%上涨。我们表明，通勤时间节省和就业可达性的改善是这些影响背后的合理机制。

Green Waste

绿色浪费

Ingvil Gaarder, Morten Grindaker, Tom G. Meling, Magne Mogstad

Programs：EEE, PE；JEL：D61, H23, Q48, Q54, Q58

Abstract

We develop and apply a framework to test for and measure green waste: the misallocation of public subsidies for green investment projects. Our context is a major Norwegian subsidy program to reduce carbon emissions. We apply the framework to detailed project-level data on carbon emissions and subsidy amounts for both marginal and inframarginal projects. We find that the decision-maker could have achieved the same level of emission reductions at less than half the cost. To isolate the sources of this green waste, we use data on both ex-ante expected and ex-post realized emission reductions for each project. We find that the decision-maker is able ex-ante to identify the projects with the highest ex-post emission reductions but unwilling to select them.

中文摘要

我们开发并应用了一个框架来检验和衡量绿色浪费：即绿色投资项目公共补贴的错配。我们的研究背景是挪威一个旨在减少碳排放的主要补贴项目。我们将该框架应用于边际项目和边际内项目的碳排放量和补贴金额的详细项目层面数据。我们发现，决策者本可以以不到一半的成本实现同等水平的减排。为了隔离这种绿色浪费的来源，我们使用了每个项目的事前预期减排量和事后实际减排量的数据。我们发现，决策者能够在事前识别出事后减排量最高的项目，但不愿意选择它们。

Bayesian Inference in IV Regressions

IV回归中的贝叶斯推断

Domenico Giannone, Michele Lenza, Giorgio Primiceri

Programs：LS, TWP；JEL：C01, C11, C12, C26

Abstract

It is well known that standard frequentist inference breaks down in IV regressions with weak instruments. Bayesian inference with diffuse priors suffers from the same problem. We show that the issue arises because flat priors on the first-stage coefficients overstate instrument strength. In contrast, inference improves drastically when an uninformative prior is specified directly on the concentration parameter—the key nuisance parameter capturing instrument relevance. The resulting Bayesian credible intervals are asymptotically equivalent to the frequentist confidence intervals based on conditioning approaches, and remain robust to weak instruments.

中文摘要

众所周知，在弱工具变量的IV回归中，标准频率学派推断会失效。具有扩散先验的贝叶斯推断也存在同样的问题。我们表明，问题源于第一阶段系数的平坦先验夸大了工具变量的强度。相比之下，当直接在集中参数（衡量工具变量相关性的关键 nuisance 参数）上指定无信息先验时，推断会得到极大改善。由此产生的贝叶斯可信区间在渐近上等价于基于条件化方法的频率学派置信区间，并且对弱工具变量保持稳健。

The Morality of Market Exchanges: Between Societal Values and Tradeoffs

市场交易的道德：社会价值观与权衡之间

Julio J. Elias, Nicola Lacetera, Mario Macis

Programs：POL；JEL：A10, D63, Z10

Abstract

Certain behaviors in markets are unambiguously unethical. In other cases, however, voluntary exchanges that can create gains from trade remain contested on moral grounds, because of what is traded or of the price at which the exchange occurs. This chapter offers a framework to analyze these contested markets and provides examples of two general instances. First, we examine “repugnant” transactions involving the human body—such as compensated organ donation and gestational surrogacy—where concerns about dignity, exploitation, and inequality conflict with welfare gains from expanding supply. Second, we study price gouging in emergencies, where demands for a “just price” clash with the incentive and allocation roles of price adjustments under scarcity. Across both cases, we synthesize evidence on societal attitudes and highlight how support for policy options depends on perceived trade-offs between autonomy, fairness and efficiency, and on institutional features that can separate compensation from allocation.

中文摘要

市场中的某些行为是明确不道德的。然而，在其他情况下，能够创造交易收益的自愿交换在道德上仍存在争议，争议源于交易的对象或交易发生的价格。本章提供了一个分析这些有争议市场的框架，并提供了两种一般情况的例子。首先，我们研究了涉及人体的“令人反感”的交易——例如有偿器官捐赠和代孕——在这些交易中，关于尊严、剥削和不平等的担忧与扩大供应带来的福利收益相冲突。其次，我们研究了紧急情况下的价格欺诈，在这种情况下，对“公平价格”的要求与稀缺条件下价格调整的激励和分配作用相冲突。在这两种情况下，我们综合了关于社会态度的证据，并强调了政策选项的支持如何取决于对自主、公平和效率之间感知到的权衡，以及取决于能够将补偿与分配分开的制度特征。

Entry and Exit in Treasury Auctions

国债拍卖中的进入与退出

Jason Allen, Ali Hortaçsu, Eric Richert, Milena Wittwer

Programs：AP, IO；JEL：D44, D47, G12, G28

Abstract

Hedge funds have become increasingly active in Treasury markets, yet we have little evidence on how this shift has affected the market. We focus on the primary market, where the government issues its debt through auctions. Using over 20 years of data from Canadian Treasury auctions, we document dealers exiting and hedge funds entering. To evaluate the costs and benefits of this trend for debt issuance, we develop an auction framework with endogenous participation. We find that the expected gains from greater hedge fund participation, driven by increased competition, are smaller than the costs that arise because hedge funds—unlike dealers—do not participate regularly. Currently, dealer participation remains sufficiently strong that policies aimed at stabilizing participation have little effect on funding costs.

中文摘要

对冲基金在国债市场中变得越来越活跃，然而我们对于这种转变如何影响市场的证据却很少。我们聚焦于一级市场，即政府通过拍卖发行债务的市场。利用加拿大国债拍卖超过20年的数据，我们记录了交易商退出和对冲基金进入的现象。为了评估这一趋势对债务发行的成本和收益，我们开发了一个具有内生参与性的拍卖框架。我们发现，由竞争加剧驱动的对冲基金参与度提高所带来的预期收益，小于因对冲基金（与交易商不同）不规律参与而产生的成本。目前，交易商的参与仍然足够强劲，以至于旨在稳定参与的政策对融资成本影响甚微。

Market Power and Capital Constraints

市场势力与资本约束

Milena Wittwer, Jason Allen

Programs：AP, IO；JEL：D40, D44, G12, G18, G20, L10

Abstract

We explore how traders' equity capitalization influences asset prices in a framework that accounts for market power. In our model, traders with capital constraints engage in transactions in an imperfectly competitive market. We demonstrate that looser capital constraints elevate both asset prices and price impact, the latter diminishing market liquidity. Using Canadian Treasury auction data, we illustrate how to apply our model to quantify these effects. We estimate the shadow costs of capital constraints by leveraging a temporary policy exemption during 2020-2021. We show that while these constraints are only infrequently binding, their relative impact when activated can be sizable.

中文摘要

我们探讨了交易者的股权资本化如何在一个考虑市场势力的框架中影响资产价格。在我们的模型中，面临资本约束的交易者在一个不完全竞争的市场中进行交易。我们证明，放松资本约束会同时提高资产价格和价格冲击，后者会降低市场流动性。利用加拿大国债拍卖数据，我们说明了如何应用我们的模型来量化这些效应。我们利用2020-2021年期间一项临时政策豁免来估计资本约束的影子成本。我们表明，虽然这些约束只是偶尔具有约束力，但它们在激活时的相对影响可能相当大。

Renewable Energy Expansion: Key Challenges and Emerging Opportunities

可再生能源扩张：关键挑战与新兴机遇

Koichiro Ito

Programs：EEE；JEL：Q40, Q50

Abstract

The global expansion of renewable energy is key to addressing climate change. After a decade of dramatic cost reductions—especially for solar photovoltaics and wind—renewables now approach cost parity with fossil generation. While this cost reduction has led to a steep increase in solar and wind power in several countries, the global share of solar and wind generation remains modest—approximately 15% as of 2023. This article reviews recent economic studies and identifies three central challenges and corresponding opportunities for scaling up renewable energy—underdeveloped regulatory and market design, insufficient intertemporal market integration, and inadequate spatial market integration. The insights from this review highlight effective ways to accelerate the global transition to renewable energy.

中文摘要

全球可再生能源的扩张是应对气候变化的关键。在经历了十年（尤其是太阳能光伏和风能）的成本急剧下降之后，可再生能源现在已接近与化石能源发电的成本平价。虽然这种成本下降导致一些国家的太阳能和风能发电量急剧增加，但全球太阳能和风能发电的份额仍然不大——截至2023年约为15%。本文回顾了近期经济学研究，并指出了扩大可再生能源规模的三个核心挑战及相应的机遇——不完善的监管和市场设计、跨期市场整合不足以及空间市场整合不足。本综述的见解强调了加速全球向可再生能源转型的有效途径。

Ruling for the Rich: the Supreme Court over Time

为富人裁决：最高法院的变迁

Andrea Prat, Fiona Scott Morton, Jacob Spitz

Programs：IO, LE；JEL：K0, K1

Abstract

To investigate the emergence of a pro-wealthy bias in the US Supreme Court, we develop a protocol to identify and analyze all cases involving economic issues from 1953 to the present. We categorize the parties in these cases as “rich” or “poor” according to their likelihood of being wealthy. A vote is pro-rich if that outcome would directly shift resources to the party that is more likely to be wealthy. Using this dataset, we estimate case-specific intercepts, justice-specific latent ideal points, and party-level time trends using the Bayesian methods pioneered by Martin and Quinn (2002). In the 1950s, justices appointed by the two parties appear similar in their propensity to cast pro-rich votes. Over the sample period, we estimate a steady increase in polarization, culminating in an implied party gap of 47 percentage points by 2022. The magnitude of the gap suggests the usefulness of an economic metric for prediction relative to ideologies such as originalism or textualism.

中文摘要

为了调查美国最高法院中亲富偏见的出现，我们开发了一个协议来识别和分析从1953年至今所有涉及经济问题的案件。我们根据这些案件中的当事人成为富人的可能性，将其分类为“富人”或“穷人”。如果某个投票结果会直接将资源转移给更可能富有的当事人，则该投票被视为亲富。利用这个数据集，我们使用Martin和Quinn（2002）开创的贝叶斯方法，估计了案件特定的截距项、大法官特定的潜在理想点以及当事人层面的时间趋势。在20世纪50年代，两党任命的大法官在做出亲富投票的倾向性上看起来相似。在整个样本期内，我们估计出极化程度稳步上升，最终到2022年隐含的党派差距达到47个百分点。这一差距的幅度表明，相对于原旨主义或文本主义等意识形态，经济指标在预测方面具有实用性。

From Treatment to Safety: The Role of Substance Use Treatment in Preventing Intimate Partner Violence

从治疗到安全：物质使用治疗在预防亲密伴侣暴力中的作用

Mevlude Akbulut-Yuksel, Dhaval M. Dave, Bilge Erten, Pinar Keskin, Catarina R. Meneses

Programs：CH, EH, LE, PE；JEL：I12, I18, J12

Abstract

Abstract

Abstract

We formalize the hypothesis that quality upgrading is subject to a key hold-up problem: producing high-quality beans requires long-term investments by farmers, but there is no guarantee that an exporter will pay a quality premium when the beans arrive at its mills. Calibration of the model suggests that one-third to two-thirds of the (substantial) gains from the program accrue to farmers, with the vertical restraint playing a critical role. Abstract
