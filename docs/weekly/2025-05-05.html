Healthcare Provider Bankruptcies

医疗保健提供者破产

Samuel Antill, Jessica Bai, Ashvin Gandhi, Adrienne Sabety

Programs：EH；JEL：I11

Abstract

Healthcare firms are filing for Chapter 11 bankruptcy at record rates. We find that bankruptcies increase healthcare staff turnover, worsen care, and harm patients. Using a difference-in- differences design, we estimate that a bankruptcy filing immediately increases staff turnover and worsens the firm’s performance on unannounced inspections. Next, using a patient- distance-to-facility instrument, we document that bankruptcies harm patients through increases in hospitalizations, physical restraints, and bedsores. Finally, we employ a randomized survey experiment of nursing home staff to confirm that bankruptcy filings increase voluntary departures and that replacement workers harm patients.

中文摘要

医疗保健企业正以创纪录的速度申请第11章破产。我们发现，破产增加了医疗保健人员的流动率，恶化了护理质量，并伤害了患者。使用双重差分设计，我们估计破产申请会立即增加员工流动率，并降低企业在突击检查中的表现。接下来，利用患者与设施距离的工具变量，我们记录了破产通过增加住院率、身体约束和褥疮来伤害患者。最后，我们通过对养老院员工进行随机调查实验，确认破产申请会增加自愿离职率，并且替代员工会伤害患者。

When Do FOMC Voting Rights Affect Monetary Policy?

FOMC投票权何时影响货币政策？

Vyacheslav Fos, Nancy R. Xu

Programs：AP, ME；JEL：D7, E5, E58, G10

Abstract

Using 472 FOMC meetings (1969–2019) and the exogenous rotation of voting rights among Reserve Bank presidents, we identify meetings where local economic conditions in voting districts significantly affect the Federal funds target rate (FFR), while those in non-voting districts show no effect. This voting-group effect persists after controlling for national conditions and Greenbook forecasts, implying that actual FFR decisions plausibly deviated from what average information and expectations would have suggested. Distortions are sizable, persistent, and priced into futures and Treasury markets prior to FOMC meetings. We demonstrate these findings using both components of the Fed’s dual mandate: inflation and unemployment rates.

中文摘要

利用1969年至2019年间472次FOMC会议以及储备银行行长投票权的外生轮换，我们识别出那些投票区的地方经济状况显著影响联邦基金目标利率的会议，而非投票区则无此效应。这种投票群体效应在控制全国状况和绿皮书预测后依然存在，表明实际的联邦基金利率决策可能偏离了平均信息和预期所建议的水平。这种扭曲是显著的、持续的，并在FOMC会议前被期货和国债市场定价。我们利用美联储双重使命的两个组成部分——通胀率和失业率——来证明这些发现。

Mobile Crisis Response Teams Support Better Policing: Evidence from CAHOOTS

移动危机响应团队支持更好的警务：来自CAHOOTS的证据

Jonathan Davis, Samuel Norris, Jadon Schmitt, Yotam Shem-Tov, Chelsea Strickland

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：I10, J22, J45, K42

Abstract

This paper studies the use of mobile crisis response teams—a non-uniformed pair consisting of a mental health worker and a medic—as a component of emergency response to 911 calls. We provide the first evaluation of the longest-running program in the United States, Crisis Assistance Helping Out on the Streets (CAHOOTS) in Eugene, Oregon, which responds to calls involving mental illness, homelessness, and addiction either instead of or in addition to police officers. We use two complementary research designs to understand the effects and possible scope of these programs. First, we find that a series of program expansions into new areas and times reduced the likelihood that a 911 call resulted in an arrest and increased access to medical services. The arrest reduction likely reflects CAHOOTS’ role in de-escalating tense situations and resolving incidents without coercive measures. CAHOOTS is most often dispatched to the same calls as the police, acting as a supplement rather than a substitute. Second, we exploit idiosyncratic variation in CAHOOTS availability in the post-expansion periods to estimate the effect of additional marginal program expansions. We find that they are used mostly for calls that would otherwise go unanswered, suggesting that the program has reached a scale where it can respond to the most urgent calls. We conclude that crisis response teams play an important role as a complement to the police rather than acting only as substitutes.

中文摘要

本文研究了移动危机响应团队——由一名心理健康工作者和一名医护人员组成的非制服搭档——作为911电话应急响应的一部分。我们首次评估了美国运行时间最长的项目，即俄勒冈州尤金市的“街头危机援助帮助”（CAHOOTS），该项目响应涉及精神疾病、无家可归和成瘾的电话，要么替代警察，要么作为警察的补充。我们使用两种互补的研究设计来理解这些项目的效果和可能范围。首先，我们发现一系列项目扩展到新区域和时间段，降低了911电话导致逮捕的可能性，并增加了获得医疗服务的机会。逮捕减少可能反映了CAHOOTS在缓和紧张局势和在不使用强制措施的情况下解决事件中的作用。CAHOOTS最常与警察同时被派往同一电话，作为补充而非替代。其次，我们利用扩展后时期CAHOOTS可用性的特殊变化来估计额外边际项目扩展的效果。我们发现这些扩展主要用于那些否则将无人响应的电话，表明该项目已达到能够响应最紧急电话的规模。我们得出结论，危机响应团队作为警察的补充而非仅仅替代，发挥着重要作用。

Fragile Financing? How Corporate Reliance on Shadow Banking Affects their Access to Bank Liquidity

脆弱的融资？企业对影子银行的依赖如何影响其获取银行流动性

Viral V. Acharya, Manasa Gopal, Sascha Steffen

Programs：CF；JEL：G01, G21, G23

Abstract

Greater reliance on nonbank financing makes firms fragile as it leads banks to limit their access to credit lines. Besides demonstrating this result in panel tests subject to range of controls and robustness checks, we employ the 2014–16 oil-price collapse as an exogenous rollover risk in nonbank financing of non-oil-sector firms by collateralized loan obligations (CLOs) exposed to oil sector firms. Nonbank-reliant firms with looming maturities faced reductions and wider spreads in bank credit lines after the shock, resulting in weaker financial and real performance in spite of their drawdowns of existing credit lines.

中文摘要

对非银行融资的更大依赖使企业变得脆弱，因为这导致银行限制其获得信贷额度。除了在面板测试中通过一系列控制和稳健性检验证明这一结果外，我们还利用2014-16年油价暴跌作为非银行融资的 exogenous 展期风险，该风险通过暴露于石油行业企业的抵押贷款债务工具（CLOs）影响非石油行业企业。在冲击后，依赖非银行融资且面临即将到期债务的企业，其银行信贷额度减少且利差扩大，导致尽管提取了现有信贷额度，但财务和实际业绩仍然疲弱。

Optimal Tariffs When Labor Income Taxes Are Distortionary

当劳动所得税具有扭曲性时的最优关税

Narayana R. Kocherlakota

Programs：IFM, PE；JEL：E60, F10, H21

Abstract

It is a classic result in trade theory that it is socially optimal to set the tariff on a good equal to the inverse of the elasticity of its foreign supply. However, this result is based on the assumption that the government can use lump-sum taxes. The paper considers a simple open representative agent economy and characterizes second-best tariffs when the government's only non-tariff source of revenue is linear labor income taxation. If public spending needs are sufficiently large, and import demand is more (less) income-elastic than non-import demand, then the second-best tariff is lower (higher) than the standard optimal tariff.

中文摘要

贸易理论中的一个经典结果是，将商品关税设定为其外国供给弹性的倒数在社会上是最优的。然而，这一结果基于政府可以使用一次性总付税的假设。本文考虑一个简单的开放代表性代理人经济体，并刻画当政府唯一的非关税收入来源是线性劳动所得税时的次优关税。如果公共支出需求足够大，并且进口需求的收入弹性大于（小于）非进口需求，那么次优关税低于（高于）标准的最优关税。

Paid Sick Leave and Child Maltreatment

带薪病假与儿童虐待

Monica Deza, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Alberto Ortega

Programs：CH, EH, LS；JEL：H07, I01, J2

Abstract

Child maltreatment is a major public health concern in the United States. In this study, we examine the impact of recent state paid sick leave mandates on child maltreatment reports over the period 2011-2022. Paid sick leave mandates confer financially protected time that can be used for health and family responsibilities as well as for actions such as court hearings that can reduce exposure to domestic violence. These benefits may reduce child maltreatment reports. Using difference-in-differences and event-study methods, we find that child maltreatment reports decline by 8% following the adoption of a state paid sick leave mandate.

中文摘要

儿童虐待是美国一个主要的公共卫生问题。在本研究中，我们考察了2011-2022年间近期各州带薪病假强制令对儿童虐待报告的影响。带薪病假强制令提供了有经济保障的时间，可用于健康和家庭责任，以及诸如减少家庭暴力暴露的法庭听证等行动。这些好处可能减少儿童虐待报告。使用双重差分和事件研究方法，我们发现，在州通过带薪病假强制令后，儿童虐待报告下降了8%。

Global Shocks, Institutional Development, and Trade Restrictions: What Can We Learn from Crises and Recoveries Between 1990 and 2022?

全球冲击、制度发展与贸易限制：我们能从1990年至2022年间的危机与复苏中学到什么？

Joshua Aizenman, Hiro Ito, Donghyun Park, Jamel Saadaoui, Gazi Salah Uddin

Programs：IFM, ITI；JEL：E62, E63, E65, F32, F41, F43, F45, G01

Abstract

The Global Financial Crisis and the COVID-19 pandemic were two major shocks to the world economy in the 21st century. In this study, we analyze the patterns of recessions and recoveries of 101 advanced and developing economies. We identify the turning points of recessions and expansions between 1990 and 2022, and perform cross-country analysis of domestic and external drivers of economic recovery. In addition to the standard independent variables, we include institutional development, political stability, the extent of democracy, and trade restrictions indexes, and explore their roles in explaining recessions and recovery patterns. For the whole sample, we find that deeper recessions are followed by stronger recoveries, in line with Friedman’s plucking model of the business cycle. However, the empirical evidence for the plucking model becomes weaker if institutional development is limited and trade restrictions are high. We show that recessions that create conflict and trade tensions differ sharply from those that do not. Finally, since developing countries tend to have weaker institutions and higher trade barriers, our evidence suggests that countercyclical monetary and fiscal policy may have to play a bigger role in cushioning global shocks in those countries. This, in turn, requires more robust and credible monetary and fiscal policy frameworks.

中文摘要

全球金融危机和COVID-19大流行是21世纪世界经济的两次重大冲击。在本研究中，我们分析了101个发达经济体和发展中经济体的衰退与复苏模式。我们识别了1990年至2022年间衰退和扩张的转折点，并对经济复苏的国内和外部驱动因素进行了跨国分析。除了标准自变量外，我们纳入了制度发展、政治稳定性、民主程度和贸易限制指数，并探讨了它们在解释衰退和复苏模式中的作用。对于整个样本，我们发现更深的衰退之后伴随着更强的复苏，这与弗里德曼的商业周期“拨动模型”一致。然而，如果制度发展有限且贸易限制较高，拨动模型的实证证据会变弱。我们表明，引发冲突和贸易紧张的衰退与那些没有引发这些问题的衰退截然不同。最后，由于发展中国家往往制度较弱且贸易壁垒较高，我们的证据表明，反周期的货币和财政政策可能需要在缓冲这些国家的全球冲击方面发挥更大作用。这反过来又要求更稳健和可信的货币和财政政策框架。

From Delay to PayDay: Easing Bureaucrat Access to Implementation Information Strengthens Social Protection Delivery

从延迟到发薪日：降低官僚获取实施信息成本强化社会保障交付

Eric Dodge, Yusuf Neggers, Rohini Pande, Charity M. Troyer Moore

Programs：DEV, POL；JEL：C93, D73, I38, O15

Abstract

Poor implementation frequently impedes social protection delivery in low-state-capacity settings. In such environments, reducing managers' information acquisition costs may improve their grasp of frontline issues and program performance. However, in the presence of managerial rent-seeking, increasing the principal's information may be key, and doing so just for managers can backfire. To assess this trade-off and implications for program performance, we collaborated with two Indian states to randomly vary bureaucrat access to “PayDash”, a digital platform for real-time tracking of worker payment processing for the national rural workfare program. In treated districts, PayDash expedited bureaucrat processing of workfare payments by 17%, and increased available worksites and participating household work days by 23% and 10%, respectively. Work provision rose relatively more during the agricultural lean season. PayDash has the same impact when offered to principals as to managers, and no further gains when offered to both, indicating that manager effort to acquire information constrains implementation. Consistent with information allowing principals to better condition performance incentives on managerial effort, PayDash for principals reduced manager posting transfers by 24%. PayDash strengthened state capacity at a considerably lower cost than hiring staff, while benefiting rural Indians by more than 170 times the costs.

中文摘要

在低国家能力环境中，糟糕的实施常常阻碍社会保障交付。在此类环境下，降低管理者的信息获取成本可能改善他们对一线问题的把握和项目绩效。然而，在存在管理寻租的情况下，增加委托人的信息可能至关重要，而仅为管理者这样做可能适得其反。为评估这一权衡及其对项目绩效的影响，我们与印度两个邦合作，随机改变官僚对“PayDash”的访问权限——这是一个用于实时追踪国家农村工作福利计划工人付款处理的数字平台。在受处理地区，PayDash将官僚处理工作福利付款的速度加快了17%，并将可用工作站点和参与家庭工作日分别增加了23%和10%。工作提供在农业淡季期间增长相对更多。当PayDash提供给委托人或管理者时，其影响相同，而同时提供给两者时没有进一步增益，这表明管理者获取信息的努力制约了实施。与信息使委托人能更好地根据管理努力调整绩效激励相一致，为委托人提供的PayDash使管理者调任转移减少了24%。PayDash以远低于雇佣员工的成本增强了国家能力，同时使印度农村居民受益超过成本的170倍。

Should I Stay or Should I Go? The Response of Labor Migration to Economic Shocks

留下还是离开？劳动力迁移对经济冲击的反应

Andrea Foschi, Christopher House, Christian Proebsting, Linda Tesar

Programs：DAE, EFG, ITI, LS, ME；JEL：E24, E32, F66, J61, R23

Abstract

We examine the responsiveness of labor participation, unemployment and labor migration to exogenous variations in labor demand. Our empirical approach considers four instruments for regional labor demand commonly used in the literature. Empirically, we find that labor migration is a significant margin of adjustment for all our instruments. Following an increase in regional labor demand, the initial increase in employment is accounted for mainly through a reduction in unemployment. Over time however, net labor in-migration becomes the dominant factor contributing to increased regional employment. After 5 years, roughly 60 percent of the increase in employment is explained by the change in population. Responses of labor migration are strongest for individuals aged 20-35. Based on historical data back to the 1950s, we find no evidence of a decline in the elasticity of migration to changes in employment.

中文摘要

我们考察了劳动力参与、失业和劳动力迁移对劳动力需求外生变化的反应。我们的实证方法考虑了文献中常用的四种区域劳动力需求工具变量。实证上，我们发现劳动力迁移是我们所有工具变量的一个重要调整边际。在区域劳动力需求增加后，就业的初始增长主要通过失业减少来解释。然而，随着时间的推移，净劳动力迁入成为促进区域就业增长的主导因素。5年后，大约60%的就业增长由人口变化解释。劳动力迁移的反应在20-35岁个体中最强。基于可追溯到1950年代的历史数据，我们没有发现迁移对就业变化弹性下降的证据。

Bank Financing of Global Supply Chains

全球供应链的银行融资

Laura Alfaro, Mariya Brussevich, Camelia Minoiu, Andrea F. Presbitero

Programs：CF, ED, IFM, ITI；JEL：F34, F42, G21

Abstract

Finding new international suppliers is costly, so most importers source inputs from a single country. We match shipment data with the U.S. credit register to analyze the role of banks in mitigating trade search costs during the 2018-2019 U.S.–China trade tensions. Importers of tariff-hit products from China were more likely to exit relationships with Chinese suppliers and to find new suppliers in other Asian countries. To finance their geographic diversification, tariff-hit firms increased credit demand, drawing on credit lines and taking out loans at higher rates. Banks offering trade finance services in Asia eased both financial and information frictions.

中文摘要

寻找新的国际供应商成本高昂，因此大多数进口商从单一国家采购投入品。我们将货运数据与美国信贷登记数据匹配，分析银行在2018-2019年中美贸易紧张期间缓解贸易搜寻成本中的作用。受关税影响的中国产品进口商更可能退出与中国供应商的关系，并在其他亚洲国家寻找新供应商。为资助其地理多元化，受关税影响的企业增加了信贷需求，动用信贷额度并以更高利率获得贷款。在亚洲提供贸易融资服务的银行同时缓解了金融和信息摩擦。

Assessing Reproducibility in Economics Using Standardized Crowd-sourced Analysis

利用标准化众包分析评估经济学的可复制性

Abel Brodeur, Seung Yong Sung, Edward Miguel, Lars Vilhuber, Fernando Hoces de la Guardia

Programs：DEV；JEL：A14, C10, C81

Abstract

This paper presents a framework to standardize crowd-sourced computational reproductions in economics through the Social Science Reproduction Platform (SSRP). The approach address four main challenges for computational reproductions: a lack of standardization, aggregation issues, existing incentives for “adversarial” interactions, and the loss of knowledge from analyses that are never published. We then summarize the first 487 reproductions uploaded on the SSRP. The results show substantial heterogeneity in the ability to successfully reproduce empirical results in economics research, with approximately 30% of recent studies meeting at least a basic definition of being computationally reproducible.

中文摘要

本文提出了一个通过社会科学复制平台（SSRP）标准化经济学众包计算复制的框架。该方法解决了计算复制的四个主要挑战：缺乏标准化、聚合问题、现有“对抗性”互动的激励，以及从未发表的分析导致的知识损失。然后，我们总结了SSRP上上传的前487个复制结果。结果显示，成功复制经济学研究实证结果的能力存在显著异质性，大约30%的近期研究至少满足计算可复制的基本定义。

How Much Can Families Afford to Pay for College?

家庭能负担多少大学费用？

Peter Hinrichs

Programs：ED, PE；JEL：G51, I22, I23

The Sources of Researcher Variation in Economics

经济学中研究者差异的来源

Nick Huntington-Klein, Claus C. Portner, Ian McCarthy, The Many Economists Collaborative on Researcher Variation

Programs：LS, PE；JEL：A10, C10

Abstract

We use a rigorous three-stage many-analysts design to assess how different researcher decisions—specifically data cleaning, research design, and the interpretation of a policy question—affect the variation in estimated treatment effects. A total of 146 research teams each completed the same causal inference task three times each: first with few constraints, then using a shared research design, and finally with pre-cleaned data in addition to a specified design. We find that even when analyzing the same data, teams reach different conclusions. In the first stage, the interquartile range (IQR) of the reported policy effect was 3.1 percentage points, with substantial outliers. Surprisingly, the second stage, which restricted research design choices, exhibited slightly higher IQR (4.0 percentage points), largely attributable to imperfect adherence to the prescribed protocol. By contrast, the final stage, featuring standardized data cleaning, narrowed variation in estimated effects, achieving an IQR of 2.4 percentage points. Reported sample sizes also displayed significant convergence under more restrictive conditions, with the IQR dropping from 295,187 in the first stage to 29,144 in the second, and effectively zero by the third. Our findings underscore the critical importance of data cleaning in shaping applied microeconomic results and highlight avenues for future replication efforts.

Saved by Medicaid: New Evidence on Health Insurance and Mortality from the Universe of Low-Income Adults

被医疗补助拯救：来自全美低收入成年人关于健康保险与死亡率的新证据

Angela Wyse, Bruce D. Meyer

Programs：EH, PE；JEL：H0, I1

Abstract

We examine the causal effect of health insurance on mortality using the universe of low-income adults, a dataset of 37 million individuals identified by linking the 2010 Census to administrative tax data. Our methodology leverages state-level variation in the timing and adoption of Medicaid expansions under the Affordable Care Act (ACA) and earlier waivers and adheres to a preregistered analysis plan, a rarely used approach in observational studies in economics. We find that expansions increased Medicaid enrollment by 12 percentage points and reduced the mortality of the low-income adult population by 2.5 percent, suggesting a 21 percent reduction in the mortality hazard of new enrollees. Mortality reductions accrued not only to older age cohorts, but also to younger adults, who accounted for nearly half of life-years saved due to their longer remaining lifespans and large share of the low-income adult population. These expansions appear to be cost-effective, with direct budgetary costs of $5.4 million per life saved and $179,000 per life-year saved falling well below valuations commonly found in the literature. Our findings suggest that lack of health insurance explains about five to twenty percent of the mortality disparity between high- and low-income Americans. We contribute to a growing body of evidence that health insurance improves health and demonstrate that Medicaid’s life-saving effects extend across a broader swath of the low-income population than previously understood.
