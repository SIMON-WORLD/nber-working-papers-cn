Mental Health, Substance Use, and Child Maltreatment

心理健康、药物滥用与儿童虐待

Mir M. Ali, Thanh Lu, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Angélica Meinhofer

Programs：AG, CH, EH, LE, PE；JEL：H0, I0, K0

Abstract

Child maltreatment is a pressing concern in the United States, with more than four million children referred to child protective services in 2022. Reducing child maltreatment is a national health objective given the substantial, negative consequences for children who experience maltreatment, both in the short- and long-term. Parental mental health and substance use disorders are strongly associated with child maltreatment. In this study, we use administrative data over the period 2004 to 2021 to study the relationship between the number of mental health and substance use treatment centers per county and child maltreatment reports. Our findings provide evidence that better access to mental health and substance use treatment reduces child maltreatment reports. In particular, an 8% increase in the supply of treatment would reduce maltreatment reports by 1%. These findings suggest that recent and ongoing efforts by the federal government to expand mental health and substance use treatment availability may lead to reduced child maltreatment.

中文摘要

儿童虐待是美国一个紧迫的问题，2022年有超过400万儿童被转介至儿童保护服务机构。鉴于遭受虐待的儿童在短期和长期内都会面临严重的负面后果，减少儿童虐待已成为一项国家健康目标。父母的心理健康和药物滥用障碍与儿童虐待密切相关。在本研究中，我们使用2004年至2021年期间的行政数据，研究每个县的心理健康和药物滥用治疗中心数量与儿童虐待报告之间的关系。我们的发现提供了证据，表明更好地获得心理健康和药物滥用治疗可以减少儿童虐待报告。具体而言，治疗供应量增加8%将使虐待报告减少1%。这些发现表明，联邦政府近期和正在进行的扩大心理健康和药物滥用治疗可及性的努力，可能会导致儿童虐待的减少。

No. 2NBER w32894

Conviction, Incarceration, and Recidivism: Understanding the Revolving Door

定罪、监禁与再犯：理解旋转门现象

John Eric Humphries, Aurelie Ouss, Kamelia Stavreva, Megan T. Stevenson, Winnie van Dijk

Programs：LE, LS, PE；JEL：J0, K4

Abstract

Noncarceral conviction is a common outcome of criminal court cases: for every individual incarcerated, there are approximately three who were recently convicted but not sentenced to prison or jail. We extend the binary-treatment judge IV framework to settings with multiple treatments and use it to study the consequences of noncarceral conviction. We outline assumptions under which widely-used 2SLS regressions recover margin-specific treatment effects, relate these assumptions to models of judge decision-making, and derive an expression that provides intuition about the direction and magnitude of asymptotic bias when a key assumption on judge decision-making is not met. We find that noncarceral conviction (relative to dismissal) leads to a large and long-lasting increase in recidivism for felony defendants in Virginia. In contrast, incarceration (relative to noncarceral conviction) leads to a short-run reduction in recidivism, consistent with incapacitation. Our empirical results suggest that noncarceral felony conviction is an important and overlooked driver of recidivism.

中文摘要

非监禁式定罪是刑事法庭案件的常见结果：每有一人被监禁，大约就有三人最近被定罪但未被判处监禁。我们将二元处理的法官工具变量框架扩展到多处理情形，并用以研究非监禁式定罪的后果。我们概述了广泛使用的两阶段最小二乘回归能够恢复边际特定处理效应的假设条件，将这些假设与法官决策模型联系起来，并推导出一个表达式，该表达式提供了当关于法官决策的关键假设不满足时渐近偏误方向和大小直观理解。我们发现，在弗吉尼亚州，对于重罪被告而言，非监禁式定罪（相对于撤销指控）会导致再犯率大幅且持久地上升。相比之下，监禁（相对于非监禁式定罪）会导致再犯率短期下降，这与 incapacitation 效应一致。我们的实证结果表明，非监禁式重罪定罪是再犯的一个重要且被忽视的驱动因素。

No. 3NBER w32893

Network Competition in the Airline Industry: An Empirical Framework

航空业的网络竞争：一个实证框架

Zhe Yuan, Panle Jia Barwick

Programs：IO；JEL：C51, L13, L14, L93

Abstract

The Hub-and-Spoke network is a defining feature of the airline industry. This paper is among the first in the literature to introduce an empirical framework for analyzing network competition among airlines. Airlines make market entry decisions and choose flight frequencies in the first stage, followed by price competition to attract passengers in the second stage. A key feature of this model is the linkage between direct and indirect flights, which is described by a technological relationship (and estimated using data) that proxies the Hub-and-Spoke network. The paper estimates the marginal costs of serving passengers and operating flights using first-order conditions, bounds the entry costs using inequalities derived from the reveal-preference argument, and employs a state-of-the-art econometric method to conduct inference for entry cost parameters. Ignoring network externality underestimates the benefits of operating an additional flight by 13.2%, and airlines would schedule 21.53% fewer one-stop flights had they made flight operation decisions independently for each market. To evaluate the impact of a hypothetical merger, the paper proposes a novel equilibrium concept that makes it feasible to compute the industry equilibria. Counterfactual analyses indicate that a hypothetical merger between Alaska and Virgin America would increase consumer surplus as the merged airline would offer direct flights in 10% more markets while the overall post-merger price effect would likely be muted.

中文摘要

枢纽辐射式网络是航空业的一个显著特征。本文是文献中最早引入分析航空公司间网络竞争的实证框架的论文之一。航空公司首先在第一阶段做出市场进入决策并选择航班频率，随后在第二阶段进行价格竞争以吸引乘客。该模型的一个关键特征是直达航班与中转航班之间的关联，这种关联通过一种技术关系（并使用数据进行估计）来描述，该关系近似于枢纽辐射式网络。本文利用一阶条件估计了服务乘客和运营航班的边际成本，利用显示偏好论证推导出的不等式约束了进入成本，并采用先进的计量经济学方法对进入成本参数进行推断。忽略网络外部性会使运营额外航班的收益被低估13.2%，如果航空公司独立地为每个市场做出航班运营决策，它们将安排21.53%更少的中转航班。为了评估假设合并的影响，本文提出了一种新颖的均衡概念，使得计算行业均衡成为可能。反事实分析表明，阿拉斯加航空与维珍美国航空的假设合并将增加消费者剩余，因为合并后的航空公司将在多10%的市场中提供直达航班，而合并后的总体价格效应可能被削弱。

No. 4NBER w32892

Self-, Peer-, and Teacher Perceptions under School Tracking

学校分轨制下的自我、同伴与教师认知

Ofer Malamud, Andreea Mitrut, Cristian Pop-Eleches, Miguel Urquiola

Programs：CH, ED；JEL：I21

Abstract

We examine student, teacher, and peer perceptions of effort, ability, performance, and self-confidence in Romania’s highly tracked schools. We find that: (1) students just above a cutoff—tracked into high-achieving classes—have less favorable self-perceptions than those just below (“big-fish-little-pond” effects); (2) students perceive peers in their classes more favorably (“in-group bias”); (3) this bias is stronger in lower-achieving classes; (4) students perceive themselves more positively than others perceive them (“illusory superiority”); (5) this bias is stronger among lower-achieving students (“Krueger-Dunning effects”). In short, being tracked into lower-achieving classes does not appear to negatively affect self-perceptions.

中文摘要

我们研究了罗马尼亚高度分轨学校中学生、教师和同伴对努力、能力、表现和自信的认知。我们发现：(1) 略高于分轨分数线——被分入高成就班级——的学生，其自我认知不如略低于分数线的学生（“大鱼小池塘”效应）；(2) 学生对自己班级同伴的认知更为积极（“内群体偏袒”）；(3) 这种偏袒在低成就班级中更强；(4) 学生对自己的评价比他人对他们的评价更积极（“虚幻优越感”）；(5) 这种偏袒在低成就学生中更强（“克鲁格-邓宁效应”）。简而言之，被分入低成就班级似乎并未对自我认知产生负面影响。

No. 5NBER w32891

Drivers of Racial Differences in C-Sections

剖宫产种族差异的驱动因素

Adriana Corredor-Waldron, Janet Currie, Molly Schnell

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I1, I14

Abstract

Black mothers with a trial of labor are 25 percent more likely to deliver by C-section than non-Hispanic White mothers. The gap is largest among mothers with the lowest risk and is reduced by only one-fifth when controlling for observed medical risk factors, sociodemographic characteristics, hospital, and physician or medical practice group. Remarkably, the gap disappears when performing a C-section is more costly due to a concurrent pre-labor C-section limiting surgical resources. This finding is consistent with provider discretion—rather than differences in unobserved medical risk—accounting for persistent racial disparities in delivery method. The additional intrapartum C-sections that occur among low-risk women when hospitals are unconstrained negatively impact infant health.

中文摘要

尝试顺产的黑人母亲通过剖宫产分娩的可能性比非西班牙裔白人母亲高25%。这一差距在风险最低的母亲中最大，并且在控制了观察到的医学风险因素、社会人口特征、医院以及医生或医疗实践团体后，仅减少了五分之一。值得注意的是，当由于同时进行的产前剖宫产限制了手术资源而导致实施剖宫产成本更高时，这一差距消失了。这一发现与提供者自由裁量权——而非未观察到的医学风险差异——解释了分娩方式中持续存在的种族差异这一观点一致。当医院不受限制时，低风险女性中发生的额外产时剖宫产会对婴儿健康产生负面影响。

No. 6NBER w32890

Pensioners Without Borders

无国界养老金领取者

Salla Kalin, Antoine B. Levy, Mathilde Muñoz

Programs：AG, ITI, PE；JEL：H21, H31, R12

Abstract

This paper examines the threat of tax competition and international migration in aging economies, where retirees represent a growing share of the tax base. In 2013, Portugal introduced a full tax exemption on foreign-source pensions for relocating retirees. Contrary to the traditional view that seniors “age in place,” flows of foreign EU pensioners to Portugal rose by a factor of 30 after the reform, and stocks by more than 3, with effects concentrated among wealthy, educated pensioners from high-tax countries. The implied migration elasticity of the foreign-pensioner stock is large (2–3) and rises over time. Retirement migration persists after temporary incentives expire, as delayed origin-country retaliation (source-based taxation) only partially offsets outflows. Peer effects and spatial clustering evidence social multipliers and generate housing and healthcare congestion in host regions. In our calibrated optimal tax model, large migration elasticities justify preferential rates on foreign pensions, but regressive windfall gains for domestic homeowners and the geographic concentration of congestion externalities make full exemptions unlikely to be optimal.

中文摘要

本文考察了老龄化经济体中的税收竞争和国际移民威胁，在这些经济体中，退休人员构成了税基中日益增长的一部分。2013年，葡萄牙对移居本国的退休人员的外国来源养老金实行了完全免税。与传统观点认为老年人“原地养老”相反，改革后，流入葡萄牙的欧盟养老金领取者数量增加了30倍，存量增加了3倍多，其影响集中在来自高税收国家的富裕、受过教育的养老金领取者身上。外国养老金领取者存量的隐含移民弹性很大（2-3），并且随着时间的推移而上升。在临时激励措施到期后，退休移民仍然持续，因为延迟的原籍国报复（来源地征税）仅部分抵消了流出。同伴效应和空间聚集性证明了社会乘数的存在，并在接收地区造成了住房和医疗保健拥堵。在我们校准的最优税收模型中，大的移民弹性为对外国养老金实行优惠税率提供了理由，但国内房主获得的累退性意外收益以及拥堵外部性的地理集中使得完全免税不太可能是最优的。

Measuring Bias in Job Recommender Systems: Auditing the Algorithms

衡量职位推荐系统中的偏见：算法审计

Shuo Zhang, Peter J. Kuhn

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：C99, J16, J71, M50, O33

Abstract

We use an algorithm audit of China’s four largest job boards to measure the causal effect of a job seeker’s gender on the jobs that are recommended to them, and to identify the algorithmic processes that generate those recommendations. Focusing on identical male and female worker profiles seeking jobs in the same industry-occupation cell, we find precisely estimated but modest amounts of gender bias: Jobs recommended to women pay 0.2 percent less, request 0.9 percent less experience, come from smaller firms, and contain .07 standard deviations more stereotypically female content such as requests for patience, carefulness, and beauty. The dominant driver of these gender gaps is content-based matching between posted job ads and the declared gender in new workers’ resumes. ‘Action-based’ mechanisms – based on a worker’s own actions or recruiters’ reactions to their resume – contribute relatively little to the gaps we measure.

中文摘要

我们通过对中国四大招聘网站进行算法审计，来衡量求职者性别对其被推荐职位的因果效应，并识别产生这些推荐的算法过程。聚焦于在同一行业-职业单元中求职的相同男性和女性工人档案，我们发现性别偏见虽然精确估计但程度不大：推荐给女性的工作薪资低0.2%，要求经验少0.9%，来自较小的公司，并且包含比平均水平高0.07个标准差更多的刻板女性化内容，例如要求耐心、细心和美貌。这些性别差距的主要驱动因素是发布的招聘广告与新工人简历中声明的性别之间的基于内容的匹配。基于“行动”的机制——基于工人自身的行动或招聘人员对其简历的反应——对我们所测量的差距贡献相对较小。

No. 8NBER w32888

Markups: A Search-Theoretic Perspective

加成率：一个搜寻理论的视角

Guido Menzio

Programs：EFG；JEL：D43, D83, L16

Abstract

I derive a formula for the equilibrium distribution of markups in the search- theoretic model of imperfect competition of Butters (1977), Varian (1980), and Burdett and Judd (1983). The level of markups and the sign of the relationship between a seller’s markup and its size depends on the extent of search frictions, as well as on other deep parameters. Markups are efficient. Markups are positive even though the varieties produced by sellers are perfect substitutes. Markups are heterogeneous even when all sellers operate the same production technology. Markups depend on size, even though the substitutability between a variety and the others does not depend on how much of that variety is consumed. Interpreting these markups through the lens of the monopolistic competition model of Dixit and Stiglitz (1977) would lead one to recover incorrect and unstable buyers’ preferences. Interpreting these markups through the lens of the Dixit-Stiglitz model would also leads to incorrect policy recommendations. These results are a cautionary note on recent work in macroeconomics.

中文摘要

我推导出了Butters (1977)、Varian (1980)以及Burdett和Judd (1983)的搜寻理论不完全竞争模型中加成率均衡分布的公式。加成率的水平以及卖方的加成率与其规模之间关系的符号，取决于搜寻摩擦的程度以及其他深层参数。加成率是有效率的。即使卖方生产的产品是完全替代品，加成率也为正。即使所有卖方都采用相同的生产技术，加成率也是异质的。即使一种产品与其他产品之间的替代性不取决于该产品的消费量，加成率也取决于规模。通过Dixit和Stiglitz (1977)的垄断竞争模型来解释这些加成率，会导致恢复出不正确且不稳定的买方偏好。通过Dixit-Stiglitz模型来解释这些加成率，也会导致错误的政策建议。这些结果是对近期宏观经济学研究的一个警示。

No. 9NBER w32887

Cash Bail and Trial Outcomes in an Early Twentieth-Century Southern Police Court

二十世纪初南方警察法庭中的现金保释与审判结果

Howard Bodenhorn

Programs：DAE；JEL：K14, N0

Abstract

Studies of modern misdemeanor adjudication find that courts set bail higher than is required to reasonably assure that nonviolent defendants who pose no immediate threat to the community will appear for trial. Some defendants languish in jail for extended periods during which time they lose income, employment, and the ability to provide an effective defense for themselves. This paper considers the downstream consequences of bail setting in an urban, southern police court in the 1910s. I find that defendants unwilling or unable to post cash bail were not more likely to be convicted or to be incarcerated than defendants who posted bail. Conditional on conviction, however, defendants who posted bail and returned for their hearings were about half as likely to serve time. Among those who served time, defendants who posted bail served just 6 percent as much time as defendants who did not post bail. The ability to post bail was correlated with unobserved income or wealth and I find evidence that defendants who did not post bail and served on the chain gang were employed in low-income jobs and likely faced a binding cash-in-advance constraint.

中文摘要

对现代轻罪判决的研究发现，法院设定的保释金高于合理确保不对社区构成直接威胁的非暴力被告出庭受审所需的金额。一些被告在监狱中长时间滞留，期间他们失去收入、工作以及为自己提供有效辩护的能力。本文考察了1910年代南方城市警察法庭中保释金设定的下游后果。我发现，与缴纳保释金的被告相比，不愿或无力缴纳现金保释的被告并不更可能被定罪或监禁。然而，在定罪条件下，缴纳保释金并返回听证的被告服刑的可能性大约只有未缴纳保释金者的一半。在服刑者中，缴纳保释金的被告服刑时间仅为未缴纳保释金者的6%。缴纳保释金的能力与未观测到的收入或财富相关，并且我发现证据表明，未缴纳保释金并在链囚队服刑的被告从事低收入工作，很可能面临有约束力的现金预付约束。

No. 10NBER w32886

Electric Vehicles and the Energy Transition: Unintended Consequences of a Common Retail Rate Design

电动汽车与能源转型：一种常见零售电价设计的意外后果

Megan R. Bailey, David P. Brown, Erica Myers, Blake C. Shaffer, Frank A. Wolak

Programs：EEE, IO；JEL：L94, Q41, R40

Abstract

The growth of electric vehicles (EVs) raises new challenges for electricity systems. We implement a field experiment to assess the effect of time-of-use (TOU) pricing and managed charging on EV charging behavior. We find that while TOU pricing is effective at shifting EV charging into off-peak hours, it unintentionally induces new and larger “shadow peaks” of simultaneous charging. These shadow peaks lead to greater exceedance of local capacity constraints and advance the need for distribution network upgrades. In contrast, centrally managed charging solves the coordination problem, reducing transformer capacity requirements, and is well-tolerated by consumers in our setting.

中文摘要

电动汽车的增长给电力系统带来了新的挑战。我们实施了一项实地实验，以评估分时电价和受控充电对电动汽车充电行为的影响。我们发现，虽然分时电价能有效将电动汽车充电转移到非高峰时段，但它意外地引发了新的、更大的同时充电“影子高峰”。这些影子高峰导致更频繁地超出本地容量限制，并提前了配电网络升级的需求。相比之下，集中管理的充电解决了协调问题，降低了变压器容量需求，并且在我们的环境中被消费者良好接受。

No. 11NBER w32885

Gender Diversity in Academic Entrepreneurship: Social Impact Motives and the NSF I-Corps Program

学术创业中的性别多样性：社会影响动机与NSF I-Corps项目

April Burrage, Nilanjana Dasgupta, Ina Ganguli

Programs：PR；JEL：J16, L26, O31

Abstract

This study examines gender differences in the social impact and commercial motives for academic entrepreneurship using the National Science Foundation's Innovation Corps (NSF I-Corps) program. I-Corps provides experiential entrepreneurship training to faculty and graduate student researchers at local I-Corps university sites and through a nationwide program. How the World Became Rich by Mark Koyama and Jared Rubin and Slouching Towards Utopia, by J. Bradford DeLong: A Review Essay

评马克·科亚马和贾里德·鲁宾的《世界如何变得富有》与J·布拉德福德·德隆的《蹒跚走向乌托邦》

Steven N. Durlauf

Programs：DAE, EFG, POL；JEL：N1, O4

Abstract

This essay provides a review of two important recent books on economic growth: How the World Became Rich by Mark Koyama and Jared Rubin and Slouching Towards Utopia, by J. Bradford DeLong. Each book is noteworthy for its erudition and breadth. I explore strengths and weaknesses of these books and make some proposals on new ways to conceptualize and study long run socioeconomic development. My discussion emphasizes the importance of contingency in determining long run inequalities across countries as well the potential for ideas from complexity theory to augment standard growth modelling.

中文摘要

本文评述了近期关于经济增长的两部重要著作：马克·科亚马和贾里德·鲁宾的《世界如何变得富有》以及J·布拉德福德·德隆的《蹒跚走向乌托邦》。每本书都以其博学和广度而著称。我探讨了这些书的优点和缺点，并就概念化和研究长期社会经济发展的新方法提出了一些建议。我的讨论强调了偶然性在决定各国长期不平等中的重要性，以及复杂性理论的思想在增强标准增长模型方面的潜力。
