Federal Reserve Structure, Economic Ideas, and Banking Policy During the “Quiet Period” in Banking

美联储结构、经济思想与银行业“平静期”的银行政策

Michael D. Bordo, Edward S. Prescott

Programs：ME；JEL：B20, E58, G2, H1, L1

Abstract

We evaluate the decentralized structure of the Federal Reserve System as a mechanism for generating and processing new ideas on banking policy in the 1950s and 1960s. We document that demand for research and analysis was driven by banking industry developments and legal changes that required the Federal Reserve and other banking regulatory agencies to develop guidelines for bank mergers. In response to these developments, the Board and the Reserve Banks hired industrial organization economists and young economists out of graduate school who brought in the leading theory of industrial organization at the time, which was the structure, conduct, and performance (SCP) paradigm. This flow of ideas into the Federal Reserve from academia paralleled the flow that was going on in monetary policy and macroeconomics at the time and contributed to the increased professionalization of research at the Federal Reserve. We document how several Reserve Banks, particularly Boston and Chicago, innovated by creating dissertation support programs, collecting specialized data, and creating the Bank Structure Conference, which became the clearinghouse for academic work on bank structure and later for bank risk and financial stability. We interpret these examples as illustrating an advantage that a decentralized central bank has in the production of knowledge.

中文摘要

我们评估了美联储系统的分散化结构，将其视为20世纪50年代和60年代银行政策新思想的生成和处理机制。我们记录到，对研究和分析的需求是由银行业发展和法律变化驱动的，这些变化要求美联储和其他银行监管机构为银行合并制定指导方针。为应对这些发展，美联储理事会和储备银行聘请了产业组织经济学家和刚从研究生院毕业的年轻经济学家，他们带来了当时领先的产业组织理论，即结构、行为和绩效（SCP）范式。这种思想从学术界流入美联储的过程，与当时货币政策与宏观经济学领域的思想流动并行，并促进了美联储研究专业化的提升。我们记录了多家储备银行（尤其是波士顿和芝加哥）如何通过创建论文支持项目、收集专门数据以及举办银行结构会议进行创新，该会议后来成为银行结构学术工作以及后来银行风险和金融稳定的信息交流中心。我们将这些例子解释为分散化中央银行在知识生产方面具有的优势。

No. 2NBER w33262

Rules vs. Discretion: Decoding FOMC Policy Deliberations

规则与自由裁量：解读FOMC政策审议

Michael D. Bordo, Klodiana Istrefi, Humberto Martínez

Programs：ME；JEL：E03, E50, E61

Abstract

This study provides evidence on the usage and preferences of Federal Reserves Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) regarding the balance between rules and discretion in policy decisions. Analyzing FOMC transcripts over 40 years, we find that while Discretion has been a consistent feature in the language of the FOMC, the use of the language of Rules surged notably in the mid-1990s, aligning with theoretical advancements in monetary policy. We identify that a rise in Discretion terminology occurs during economic downturns and periods of heightened uncertainty. In contrast, a rise in the language of Rules is supported by higher references to terms such as credibility and commitment, and is more prevalent among hawkish FOMC members. Our findings link the increased use of the language of Rules (Discretion) to tighter (easier) monetary policy, revealing a significant role of this debate in shaping policy outcomes, in particular periods.

中文摘要

本研究提供了关于美联储联邦公开市场委员会（FOMC）在政策决策中规则与自由裁量平衡的使用和偏好的证据。通过分析40年间的FOMC会议记录，我们发现，虽然自由裁量一直是FOMC语言中的一贯特征，但规则语言的使用在20世纪90年代中期显著增加，这与货币政策的理论进展相一致。我们识别出，自由裁量术语的增加发生在经济衰退和不确定性加剧时期。相比之下，规则语言的增加得到了对可信度和承诺等术语更多提及的支持，并且在鹰派FOMC成员中更为普遍。我们的发现将规则（自由裁量）语言使用的增加与更紧缩（更宽松）的货币政策联系起来，揭示了这一辩论在特定时期对政策结果形成的重要作用。

No. 3NBER w33261

Beliefs of Others: an Experiment

他人的信念：一项实验

Marina Agranov, Polina Detkova

Programs：POL；JEL：D03, D83

Abstract

We study how people think others update their beliefs upon encountering new evidence. We find that when two individuals share the same prior, one believes that new evidence cannot systematically shift the other’s beliefs in either direction (Martingale property). When the two have different priors, people think that any information brings others’ expected posteriors closer to their own prior, but this adjustment is less responsive to information quality than theory predicts. We identify the primary cause of this insensitivity and discuss the implications of our findings for strategic games with asymmetric information, information design, and, more broadly, for understanding societal polarization.

中文摘要

我们研究人们如何认为他人在遇到新证据时更新自己的信念。我们发现，当两个人拥有相同的先验信念时，一个人认为新证据不会系统性地将另一个人的信念向任一方向偏移（鞅性质）。当两人拥有不同的先验信念时，人们认为任何信息都会使他人的预期后验信念更接近自己的先验信念，但这种调整对信息质量的敏感度低于理论预测。我们识别了这种不敏感性的主要原因，并讨论了我们的发现对非对称信息下的策略博弈、信息设计以及更广泛地理解社会两极分化的启示。

No. 4NBER w33260

Cracks in the Glass Ceiling and Gender Equality: Do Exports Shatter the Glass Ceiling?

玻璃天花板的裂缝与性别平等：出口是否打破了玻璃天花板？

Bruno César Araújo, Lourenço S. Paz, James E. West

Programs：ITI, LS；JEL：F12, J16, J31

Abstract

Do female managers improve workplace outcomes for female employees? The evidence is mixed. We use Brazilian administrative employer-employee matched data over the large and unanticipated 1999 Brazilian real exchange rate devaluation and find that the share of female employees grows in female-led firms, but the male-female wage gap grows in both female- and male-led firms. This behavior is inconsistent with both theoretical models of taste-based employer discrimination and third-degree oligopsonistic labor markets by gender. Outcomes only differ between female- and male-led firms regarding managerial and supervisory employees, where female-led firms hire more female managerial and supervisory employees, yet the gender wage gap widens relative to male-led firms. We conclude that exports further crack the glass ceiling in female-led firms, though at the cost of a more expansive gender wage gap.

中文摘要

女性管理者是否改善了女性员工的工作场所结果？证据是混杂的。我们利用巴西1999年大规模且出乎意料的雷亚尔贬值期间的行政雇主-雇员匹配数据，发现女性领导的企业中女性员工比例增长，但在女性和男性领导的企业中，男女工资差距都在扩大。这种行为与基于偏好的雇主歧视理论和按性别划分的三级寡头垄断劳动力市场模型都不一致。女性和男性领导的企业之间的结果差异仅体现在管理和监督员工方面，女性领导的企业雇佣了更多的女性管理和监督员工，然而与男性领导的企业相比，性别工资差距却扩大了。我们得出结论，出口进一步打破了女性领导企业中的玻璃天花板，但代价是性别工资差距的扩大。

No. 5NBER w33259

Breaking Barriers to Financial Access: Cross-Platform Digital Payments and Credit Markets

打破金融准入壁垒：跨平台数字支付与信贷市场

Shashwat Alok, Pulak Ghosh, Nirupama Kulkarni, Manju Puri

Programs：CF, DEV, LE, ME, PE；JEL：D14, G24, G28, G51, J15, R21, R23, R31

Abstract

Can cross-platform digital payments improve credit access at scale? We study this question using India’s launch of the Unified Payments Interface (UPI), a nationwide cross-platform payment system. Using rarely available data on the universe of consumer loans, we show that digital payments increased financial deepening, allowing new borrowers to access formal credit. Banks mainly expanded lending to prime borrowers, while fintechs served all borrower segments—but only fintechs extended credit to new-to-credit customers. Credit growth was strongest in regions with affordable internet and recent bank account penetration. We find no evidence that this expansion increased default rates.

中文摘要

跨平台数字支付能否大规模改善信贷准入？我们利用印度推出的统一支付接口（UPI）——一个全国性的跨平台支付系统——来研究这个问题。利用罕见的消费者贷款全量数据，我们表明数字支付增加了金融深化，使新借款人能够获得正规信贷。银行主要向优质借款人扩大贷款，而金融科技公司服务于所有借款人群体——但只有金融科技公司向无信贷记录的新客户提供信贷。信贷增长在互联网价格可负担且近期银行账户普及率高的地区最为强劲。我们没有发现证据表明这种扩张增加了违约率。

Knowledge Suppression and Resilience under Censorship: Three-century Book Publications in China

审查制度下的知识压制与韧性：中国三个世纪的图书出版

Ying Bai, Ruixue Jia, Jiaojiao Yang

Programs：DEV, POL；JEL：D83, H11, N40, O31, Z18

Abstract

This study examines the short-, medium-, and long-term impacts of state censorship on knowledge production, focusing on the largest book-banning campaign in Chinese history, initiated during the compilation of the 'Siku Quanshu' (Complete Library in Four Sections) between 1772 and 1783. Analyzing publication data from over 161,000 books spanning the 1660s to the 1940s, we find that categories subjected to stricter censorship experienced significant declines in publication during the seven decades following the bans (1770s–1830s). However, beginning in the 1840s, political upheavals and the erosion of state control triggered a resurgence of publications in previously restricted categories. This pattern of suppression and recovery extends to ideas, as evidenced by keyword analysis of book titles. Further analysis highlights the central role of chilling effects in driving these dynamics, with the exit and entry of publishers playing a crucial role in both the suppression and subsequent revival of knowledge production.

中文摘要

本研究考察了国家审查对知识生产的短期、中期和长期影响，重点关注中国历史上最大规模的禁书运动，该运动始于1772年至1783年间《四库全书》的编纂。通过分析从1660年代到1940年代超过16.1万本书的出版数据，我们发现，在禁令实施后的七十年间（1770年代至1830年代），受到更严格审查的类别出版量显著下降。然而，从1840年代开始，政治动荡和国家控制的削弱引发了先前受限类别出版的复苏。这种压制与复苏的模式也延伸到了思想层面，图书标题的关键词分析证明了这一点。进一步分析强调了寒蝉效应在驱动这些动态中的核心作用，出版商的退出和进入在知识生产的压制及随后的复兴中都扮演了关键角色。

No. 7NBER w33257

Conditions Under Which College Students Can be Responsive to Text-based Nudging

大学生对基于文本的助推产生回应的条件

Lindsay C. Page, Katharine E. Meyer, Jeonghyun Lee, Hunter Gehlbach

Programs：ED；JEL：I21, I23, I24

Abstract

College success requires students to engage with their institution academically and administratively. Missteps with administrative processes can threaten student persistence and success. Through two experimental studies, one exploratory (N=13,657) and one pre-registered and confirmatory (N=11,561), we assessed the effectiveness of an artificially intelligent, text-based chatbot that proactively reached out to students to support navigation of administrative processes and use of campus resources. Across two-year and four-year college contexts, outreach was most effective when focused on discrete administrative processes–such as filing financial aid forms and managing registration holds–which were acute and time-sensitive and for which outreach could be targeted to those for whom it was relevant. We situate these experiments in the context of similar efforts targeting college students to formulate testable hypotheses about their effective use for promoting college success. Specifically, we hypothesize that proactive outreach will be most effective when: (1) framed as from a trusted source with whom students would expect to communicate; (2) targeted using data to ensure that communication is relevant to students’ personal circumstances; and (3) focused on well-defined, required, and often acute tasks.

中文摘要

大学成功要求学生从学术和行政两方面与所在院校保持互动。行政流程中的失误可能威胁到学生的持续学习和成功。通过两项实验研究——一项探索性研究（N=13,657）和一项预注册的验证性研究（N=11,561）——我们评估了一个人工智能驱动的基于文本的聊天机器人的有效性，该机器人主动联系学生，以支持他们完成行政流程和使用校园资源。在两年制和四年制大学背景下，当外联活动聚焦于离散的行政流程（例如提交经济援助表格和管理注册冻结）时最为有效，这些流程具有紧迫性和时效性，并且外联可以针对相关学生。我们将这些实验置于针对大学生的类似努力背景下，以提出关于其有效促进大学成功的可检验假设。具体而言，我们假设主动外联在以下情况下最为有效：（1）以学生期望沟通的、可信赖的来源名义发起；（2）利用数据进行精准定位，确保沟通与学生个人情况相关；（3）聚焦于定义明确、必需且通常紧迫的任务。

No. 8NBER w33256

Financial Inclusion Across the United States

美国金融包容性

Motohiro Yogo, Andrew Whitten, Natalie Cox

Programs：CF；JEL：D14, G51

Abstract

We study retirement and bank account participation for the universe of U.S. households with a member aged 50 to 59 in the administrative tax data. ZCTA-level average income, income inequality, and racial composition predict retirement account participation for low-income households, conditional on household income and regional price parities. Income inequality also predicts bank account participation for low-income households. We estimate the causal effect of access to an employer retirement plan on participation. Recent policy proposals for universal access with automatic enrollment could increase participation by 19 percentage points in the lowest income quintile over ten years.

中文摘要

我们利用行政税收数据，研究了全美所有至少有一名成员年龄在50至59岁之间的家庭的退休账户和银行账户参与情况。在控制家庭收入和地区价格差异的条件下，邮政编码区层面的平均收入、收入不平等程度和种族构成能够预测低收入家庭的退休账户参与率。收入不平等程度也能预测低收入家庭的银行账户参与率。我们估算了获得雇主退休计划对参与率的因果效应。近期提出的全民自动注册政策建议，可能在十年内使最低收入五分位群体的参与率提高19个百分点。

No. 9NBER w33255

Political Power and Market Power

政治权力与市场权力

Bo Cowgill, Andrea Prat, Tommaso Valletti

Programs：IO, POL, PR；JEL：L19

Abstract

Brandeis (1914) hypothesized that firms with market power will also attempt to gain political power. To explore this hypothesis empirically, we combine data on mergers with data on lobbying expenditures and campaign contributions in the US from 1999 to 2017. We pursue two distinct empirical approaches: a panel event study and a differential exposure design. Both approaches indicate that mergers are followed by large and persistent increases in lobbying activity, both by individual firms and by industry trade associations. There is also weaker evidence for an association of mergers with campaign contributions (PACs). We also find that mergers impact the extensive margin of political activity, for example, by impacting companies’ choice to establish their first in-house lobbying teams and/or first corporate PAC. We interpret these results within an oligopoly model augmented with endogenous regulation and lobbying.

中文摘要

布兰代斯（1914）假设，拥有市场权力的企业也会试图获取政治权力。为了实证检验这一假设，我们将1999年至2017年美国的并购数据与游说支出和竞选捐款数据相结合。我们采用了两种不同的实证方法：面板事件研究和差异化暴露设计。两种方法均表明，并购之后，无论是单个企业还是行业贸易协会的游说活动都会出现大幅且持续的增加。还有较弱的证据表明并购与竞选捐款（政治行动委员会）之间存在关联。我们还发现，并购影响了政治活动的广延边际，例如影响了企业选择建立首个内部游说团队和/或首个企业政治行动委员会的决定。我们在一个加入了内生监管和游说的寡头垄断模型框架内解释了这些结果。

No. 10NBER w33254

Financing, Ownership, and Performance: A Novel, Longitudinal Firm-Level Database

融资、所有权与绩效：一个新颖的企业层面纵向数据库

J. David Brown, Steven J. Davis, Lucia S. Foster, John C. Haltiwanger, John Sabelhaus

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：C81, E32, L26

Abstract

The Census Bureau’s Longitudinal Business Database (LBD) underpins many studies of firm-level behavior. It tracks longitudinally all employers in the nonfarm private sector but lacks information about business financing and owner characteristics. We address this shortcoming by linking LBD observations to firm-level data drawn from several large Census Bureau surveys. The resulting Longitudinal Employer, Owner, and Financing (LEOF) database contains more than 3 million observations at the firm-year level with information about start-up financing, current financing, owner demographics, ownership structure, profitability, and owner aspirations – all linked to annual firm-level employment data since the firm hired its first employee. Using the LEOF database, we document trends in owner demographics and financing patterns and investigate how these business characteristics relate to firm-level employment outcomes.

中文摘要

人口普查局的纵向商业数据库（LBD）是许多企业层面行为研究的基础。它纵向追踪了非农私营部门中的所有雇主，但缺乏关于企业融资和所有者特征的信息。我们通过将LBD观测数据与来自人口普查局几项大型调查的企业层面数据相链接，解决了这一缺陷。由此产生的纵向雇主、所有者与融资（LEOF）数据库包含了超过300万个企业-年度观测值，涵盖了初创融资、当前融资、所有者人口统计特征、所有权结构、盈利能力以及所有者抱负等信息——所有这些数据都与自企业雇佣第一名员工以来的年度企业层面就业数据相关联。利用LEOF数据库，我们记录了所有者人口统计特征和融资模式的趋势，并研究了这些企业特征如何与企业层面的就业结果相关联。

No. 11NBER w33253

Efficient Imperfect Competition with an Application to International Trade

有效的不完全竞争及其在国际贸易中的应用

Guido Menzio

Programs：EFG；JEL：D43, D83, F12, L16

Abstract

I study the equilibrium and the welfare effects of international trade when product markets are imperfectly competitive due of search frictions—as in Burdett and Judd (1983)—rather than product differentiation—as in Dixit and Stiglitz (1977). Markups are positive, even though there are multiple firms producing identical goods. Markups depend negatively on the number of firms producing identical goods, which, in turn, determines the extent of competition in the market. Markups may be increasing, constant, decreasing or non-monotonic in firm's size, depending on the extent of competition and on the distribution of marginal costs. The entry of firms and the quantity of output produced by each firm are efficient, even though the market is imperfectly competitive. International trade increases the measure of firms in the market, intensifies competition, lowers markups, and unambiguously increases welfare. These "natural" effects of trade emerge generically in the Burdett-Judd model of imperfect competition. In the Dixit-Stiglitz model of imperfect competition, these effects are an artifact of particular specifications of preferences.

Beyond Bruen: Can Firearm Training Replace Local Discretion in Concealed Carry Permitting?

超越布鲁恩案：枪支培训能否取代地方在隐蔽持枪许可中的自由裁量权？

John J. Donohue, Matthew Benavides, Amy L. Zhang, Alex Oktay

Programs：LE；JEL：H0, K0, K42

Abstract

The 2022 Supreme Court case NYSRPA v. Bruen struck down states’ discretion in issuing individuals firearm right-to-carry permits. As the country transitions towards more and more permissive concealed carry regulation, it has remained unclear how permitting processes and requirements affect personal and public safety. Leveraging a novel dataset of state laws spanning 2000- 2022, we find that more stringent concealed carry requirements, such as higher fees or more training hours, do not deter gun owners from obtaining carrying licenses, nor do they alter their behavior substantially enough to impact public safety outcomes including violent crimes, gun theft, or accidental shootings. As such, stricter training requirements are unable to counteract the effects of more permissive concealed carry issuance.

中文摘要

2022年最高法院审理的NYSRPA诉布鲁恩案推翻了各州在发放个人枪支携带许可证方面的自由裁量权。随着国家向越来越宽松的隐蔽持枪监管过渡，许可程序和要求如何影响个人和公共安全仍不清楚。利用2000年至2022年间各州法律的新数据集，我们发现更严格的隐蔽持枪要求，例如更高的费用或更多的培训时长，并不会阻止枪支拥有者获得携带许可证，也不会显著改变他们的行为以影响公共安全结果，包括暴力犯罪、枪支盗窃或意外枪击事件。因此，更严格的培训要求无法抵消更宽松的隐蔽持枪发放所带来的影响。

Toward an Understanding of the Political Economy of Using Field Experiments in Policymaking

理解在政策制定中使用实地实验的政治经济学

Guglielmo Briscese, John A. List

Programs：DEV, ED, EEE, PE, PR；JEL：C9, C93, H4, H41, O12, O36, P1

Abstract

Field experiments provide the clearest window into the true impact of many policies, allowing us to understand what works, what does not, and why. Yet, their widespread use has not been accompanied by a deep understanding of the political economy of their adoption in policy circles. This study begins with a large-scale natural field experiment that demonstrates the ineffectiveness of a widely implemented intervention. We leverage this result to understand how policymakers and a representative sample of the U.S. population update their beliefs of not only the policy itself, but the use of science and the trust they have in government. Policymakers, initially overly optimistic about the program’s effectiveness, adjust their views based on evidence but show reduced demand for experimentation, suggesting experiment aversion when results defy expectations. Among the U.S. public, support for policy experiments is high and remains robust despite receiving disappointing results, though trust in the implementing institutions declines, particularly in terms of perceptions of competence and integrity. Providing additional information on the value of learning from unexpected findings partially mitigates this trust loss. These insights, from both the demand and supply side, reveal the complexities of managing policymakers’ expectations and underscore the potential returns to educating the public on the value of open-mindedness in policy experimentation.

中文摘要

实地实验为许多政策的真实影响提供了最清晰的窗口，使我们能够理解哪些政策有效、哪些无效以及原因。然而，它们的广泛使用并未伴随着对其在政策圈中采纳的政治经济学的深入理解。本研究从一个大规模的自然实地实验开始，该实验证明了一项广泛实施的干预措施无效。我们利用这一结果来理解政策制定者和美国人口的代表性样本如何更新他们对政策本身、科学使用以及对政府信任的信念。政策制定者最初对该项目的有效性过于乐观，他们会根据证据调整观点，但对实验的需求却减少了，这表明当结果违背预期时存在实验厌恶。在美国公众中，对政策实验的支持度很高，并且在收到令人失望的结果后仍然保持强劲，尽管对实施机构的信任度有所下降，特别是在能力和诚信认知方面。提供关于从意外发现中学习价值的额外信息，部分缓解了这种信任损失。这些来自需求侧和供给侧的见解揭示了管理政策制定者期望的复杂性，并强调了教育公众在政策实验中保持开放心态价值的潜在回报。
