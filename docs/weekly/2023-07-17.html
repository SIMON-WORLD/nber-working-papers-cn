Education and Later-life Mortality: Evidence from a School Reform in Japan

教育与晚年死亡率：来自日本学校改革的证据

Kazuya Masuda, Hitoshi Shigeoka

Programs：AG, CH, ED, EH, LS, PE；JEL：H52, I12, I21, I28

Abstract

We examine the mortality effects of a 1947 school reform in Japan, which extended compulsory schooling from primary to secondary school by as much as 3 years. The abolition of secondary school fees also indicates that those affected by the reform likely came from disadvantaged families who could have benefited the most from schooling. Even in this relatively favorable setting, we fail to find that the reform improved later-life mortality up to the age of 87 years, although it significantly increased years of schooling. This finding suggests limited health returns to schooling at the lower level of educational attainment.

中文摘要

我们研究了日本1947年学校改革的死亡率影响，该改革将义务教育从小学延长至中学，最多延长了3年。中学学费的取消也表明，受改革影响的人很可能来自本可以从学校教育中获益最多的弱势家庭。即使在这种相对有利的环境下，我们未能发现该改革改善了至87岁的晚年死亡率，尽管它显著增加了受教育年限。这一发现表明，在较低的教育水平上，学校教育对健康的回报有限。

No. 2NBER w31471

Moonshot: Public R&D and Growth

登月计划：公共研发与增长

Shawn Kantor, Alexander T. Whalley

Programs：DAE, PR；JEL：H54, N12, N72, O32, R11

Abstract

We estimate the long-term effect of public R&D on growth in manufacturing by analyzing new data from the Cold War era Space Race. We develop a novel empirical strategy that leverages US-Soviet rivalry in space technology to isolate windfall R&D spending. Our results demonstrate that public R&D conducted by NASA contractors increased manufacturing value added, employment, and capital accumulation in space related sectors. While migration responses were important, they were not sufficient to generate a wedge between local and national effects. The iconic Moonshot R&D program had only modest economic effects for both the local and national space-related sectors.

中文摘要

我们通过分析冷战时期太空竞赛的新数据，估计了公共研发对制造业增长的长期影响。我们开发了一种新颖的实证策略，利用美苏在太空技术上的竞争来分离出意外的研发支出。我们的结果表明，由NASA承包商进行的公共研发增加了太空相关部门的制造业增加值、就业和资本积累。虽然移民响应很重要，但它们不足以在地方和国家效应之间产生差异。标志性的登月研发计划对地方和国家太空相关行业的经济影响都较为温和。

No. 3NBER w31470

Relationship between Social Security Programs and Elderly Employment in Japan

日本社会保障计划与老年人就业之间的关系

Takashi Oshio, Satoshi Shimizutani, Akiko S. Oishi

Programs：AG；JEL：H55, J14, J26

Abstract

This study examines how elderly employment is associated with social security programs and how it responds to recent reforms in Japan. To this end, we employed a rich and longitudinal dataset of middle-aged and older individuals collected between 2005 and 2018. By incorporating various factors related to social security incentives into a single index of implicit tax (ITAX), we confirmed that the index successfully captured the incentives and their changes incorporated in recent social security reforms. We further estimated the association of ITAX with an individual’s decisions concerning retirement and pension benefit claims. Lastly, we conducted counterfactual simulations to assess the effect of recent social security forms on retirement based on the estimated regression parameters. The results showed that a higher ITAX drove individuals, especially men, to retire and claim benefits earlier.

中文摘要

本研究考察了老年人就业如何与社会保障计划相关联，以及它如何响应日本近期的改革。为此，我们使用了2005年至2018年间收集的关于中老年人的丰富纵向数据集。通过将与社会保障激励相关的各种因素纳入一个单一的隐性税收指数，我们确认该指数成功捕捉了近期社会保障改革中所包含的激励及其变化。我们进一步估计了隐性税收指数与个人关于退休和养老金领取决策的关联。最后，我们基于估计的回归参数进行了反事实模拟，以评估近期社会保障形式对退休的影响。结果表明，较高的隐性税收促使个人，尤其是男性，更早退休并领取福利。

No. 4NBER w31469

Who Values Human Capitalists' Human Capital? The Earnings and Labor Supply of U.S. Physicians

谁看重人力资本家的人力资本？美国医生的收入与劳动力供给

Joshua D. Gottlieb, Maria Polyakova, Kevin Rinz, Hugh Shiplett, Victoria Udalova

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：I13, I18, J24, J31

Abstract

Is government guiding the invisible hand at the top of the labor market? We use new administrative data to measure physicians' earnings and estimate the influence of healthcare policies on these earnings, physicians' labor supply, and allocation of talent. Combining the administrative registry of U.S.~physicians with tax data, Medicare billing records, and survey responses, we find that physicians' annual earnings average $350,000 and comprise 8.6% of national healthcare spending. The age-earnings profile is steep; business income comprises one-quarter of earnings and is systematically underreported in survey data. There are major differences in earnings across specialties, regions, and firm sizes, with an unusual geographic pattern compared with other workers. We show that health policy has a major impact on the margin: 25% of physician fee revenue driven by Medicare reimbursements accrues to physicians personally. Physicians earn 6% of public money spent on insurance expansions. We find that these policies in turn affect the type and quantity of medical care physicians supply in the short run; retirement timing in the medium run; and earnings affect specialty choice in the long run.

中文摘要

政府是否在引导劳动力市场顶层的无形之手？我们使用新的行政数据来衡量医生的收入，并估计医疗政策对这些收入、医生劳动力供给以及人才配置的影响。通过将美国医生的行政注册数据与税收数据、医疗保险账单记录和调查回复相结合，我们发现医生的年收入平均为35万美元，占全国医疗支出的8.6%。年龄-收入曲线陡峭；营业收入占收入的四分之一，并且在调查数据中被系统性低估。不同专业、地区和公司规模之间存在重大收入差异，与其他工人相比呈现出不寻常的地理模式。我们表明，医疗政策在边际上具有重大影响：由医疗保险报销驱动的医生服务费收入的25%直接归属于医生个人。医生赚取了用于保险扩张的公共资金的6%。我们发现，这些政策反过来在短期内影响医生提供的医疗护理类型和数量；在中期影响退休时机；而收入在长期影响专业选择。

No. 5NBER w31468

The Electric Telegraph, News Coverage and Political Participation

电报、新闻报道与政治参与

Tianyi Wang

Programs：DAE, POL；JEL：D72, L82, L96, N41, N71, O3

Abstract

Using newly digitized data on the growth of the telegraph network in America during 1840-1852, the paper studies the impacts of the electric telegraph on national elections. I use proximity to daily newspapers with telegraphic connections to Washington to generate plausibly exogenous variation in access to telegraphed news from Washington. I find that access to Washington news with less delay had a robust positive effect on voter turnout in national elections. For mechanisms, I provide evidence that newspapers facilitated the dissemination of national news to local areas. In addition, text analysis on more than a hundred small-town weekly newspapers from the 1840s shows that the improved access to news from Washington led newspapers to cover more national political news, including coverage of Congress, the presidency, and sectional divisions involving slavery. The results suggest that the telegraph made newspapers less parochial, facilitated a national conversation and increased political participation. I find little evidence that access to telegraphed news from Washington affected party vote shares or Congressmen's roll call votes.

中文摘要

利用1840-1852年间美国电报网络增长的新数字化数据，本文研究了电报对全国选举的影响。我利用与华盛顿有电报联系的日报的邻近性，来生成获取来自华盛顿的电报新闻的外生性差异。我发现，获取华盛顿新闻的延迟时间更短，对全国选举的选民投票率产生了稳健的正向影响。关于机制，我提供了证据表明报纸促进了全国新闻向地方区域的传播。此外，对19世纪40年代一百多家小镇周报的文本分析表明，获取华盛顿新闻的改善导致报纸报道了更多的全国政治新闻，包括对国会、总统以及涉及奴隶制的地区分歧的报道。结果表明，电报使报纸不再那么狭隘，促进了全国性的对话，并增加了政治参与。我几乎没有发现证据表明，获取来自华盛顿的电报新闻影响了政党得票率或国会议员的唱名表决。

No. 6NBER w31467

Corporate Tax Disclosure

企业税收披露

Jeffrey L. Hoopes, Leslie Robinson, Joel Slemrod

Programs：PE；JEL：H25

Abstract

Policies that require, or recommend, disclosure of corporate tax information are becoming more common throughout the world, as are examples of tax-related information increasingly influencing public policy and perceptions. In addition, companies are increasing the voluntary provision of tax-related information. We describe those trends and place them within a taxonomy of public and private tax disclosure. We then review the academic literature on corporate tax disclosures and discuss what is known about their effects. One key takeaway is the paucity of evidence that many tax disclosures mandated with the aim of increasing tax revenue have produced additional revenue. We highlight many crucial unanswered questions, answers to which would inform future tax legislation and financial accounting rule making.

中文摘要

要求或建议披露企业税收信息的政策在全球范围内日益普遍，同时，税收相关信息越来越多地影响公共政策和公众认知的例子也在增加。此外，企业正在增加税收相关信息的自愿提供。我们描述了这些趋势，并将其置于公共和私人税收披露的分类框架中。然后，我们回顾了关于企业税收披露的学术文献，并讨论了已知的其影响。一个关键结论是，很少有证据表明许多旨在增加税收收入的强制性税收披露产生了额外收入。我们强调了许多关键但尚未解答的问题，对这些问题的解答将为未来的税收立法和财务会计规则制定提供信息。

No. 7NBER w31466

The Job Ladder: Inflation vs. Reallocation

工作阶梯：通货膨胀与重新配置

Giuseppe Moscarini, Fabien Postel-Vinay

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E24, E31, J60

Abstract

We introduce on-the-job search frictions in an otherwise standard monetary DSGE New-Keynesian model. Heterogeneity in productivity across jobs gives rise to a job ladder. Firms Bertrand-compete for employed workers according to the Sequential Auctions protocol of Postel-Vinay and Robin (2002). Outside job offers to employed workers, when accepted, reallocate employment up the productivity ladder; when declined, because matched by the current employer, they raise production costs and, due to nominal price rigidities, compress mark-ups, building inflationary pressure. When employment is concentrated at the bottom of the job ladder, typically after recessions, the reallocation effect prevails, aggregate supply expands, moderating marginal costs and inflation. As workers climb the job ladder, reducing slack in the employment pool, the inflation effect takes over. The model generates endogenous cyclical movements in the Neo Classical labor wedge and in the New Keynesian wage mark-up. The economy takes time to absorb cyclical misallocation and features propagation in the response of job creation, unemployment and inflation to aggregate shocks. The ratio between job-finding probabilities from job-to-job and from unemployment, a measure of the “Acceptance rate” of job offers to employed workers, predicts negatively inflation, independently of the unemployment rate.

中文摘要

我们在一个原本标准的货币DSGE新凯恩斯模型中引入了在职搜寻摩擦。工作间生产率的异质性产生了工作阶梯。企业根据Postel-Vinay和Robin（2002）的顺序拍卖协议，对在职工人进行伯特兰竞争。对在职工人的外部工作邀请，如果被接受，会将就业重新配置到生产率阶梯的上层；如果被拒绝（因为被当前雇主匹配），则会提高生产成本，并且由于名义价格刚性，会压缩加成率，从而形成通胀压力。当就业集中在工作阶梯的底部时（通常在经济衰退之后），重新配置效应占主导地位，总供给扩张，从而缓和边际成本和通货膨胀。随着工人攀登工作阶梯，减少了就业池中的闲置，通胀效应开始占据主导地位。该模型产生了新古典劳动楔子和新凯恩斯工资加成率的内生周期性波动。经济需要时间来吸收周期性的错配，并且在就业创造、失业和通货膨胀对总体冲击的反应中表现出传导机制。从工作到工作以及从失业到工作的求职概率之比，即对在职工人工作邀请的“接受率”的衡量指标，能够负向预测通货膨胀，且独立于失业率。

No. 8NBER w31465

The Linear Algebra of Economic Geography Models

经济地理模型的线性代数

Benny Kleinman, Ernest Liu, Stephen J. Redding

Programs：ITI；JEL：F10, F15, R12

Abstract

We provide sufficient statistics for nominal and real wage exposure to productivity shocks in a constant elasticity economic geography model. These exposure measures summarize the first-order general equilibrium elasticity of nominal and real wages in each location with respect to productivity shocks in all locations. They are readily computed using commonly-available trade data and the values of trade and migration elasticities. They have an intuitive interpretation in terms of underlying economic mechanisms. Computing these measures for all bilateral pairs of locations involves a single matrix inversion and therefore remains computational efficient even with an extremely high-dimensional state space. These sufficient statistics provide theory-consistent measures of locations’ exposure to productivity shocks for use in further economic and statistical analysis.

中文摘要

我们为恒定弹性经济地理模型中名义和实际工资对生产率冲击的暴露度提供了充分统计量。这些暴露度衡量指标总结了每个地点的名义和实际工资相对于所有地点生产率冲击的一阶一般均衡弹性。它们可以很容易地使用常见的贸易数据以及贸易和移民弹性值来计算。它们在潜在的经济机制方面具有直观的解释。计算所有双边地点对的这些衡量指标仅涉及一次矩阵求逆，因此即使在极高维度的状态空间下也能保持计算效率。这些充分统计量为在进一步的经济和统计分析中使用提供了理论一致的地点对生产率冲击暴露度的衡量指标。

No. 9NBER w31464

The Credit Supply Channel of Monetary Policy Tightening and its Distributional Impacts

货币政策紧缩的信贷供给渠道及其分配效应

Joshua Bosshardt, Marco Di Maggio, Ali Kakhbod, Amir Kermani

Programs：CF, ME；JEL：E20, E5, E52, G5, G51

Abstract

This paper studies how tightening monetary policy transmits to the economy through the mortgage market and sheds new light on the distributional consequences at both individual and regional levels. We find that credit supply factors, specifically restrictions on the debt-to-income (DTI) ratio, account for most of the decline in mortgages. These effects are even more pronounced for minority and middle-income borrowers, who find themselves excluded from the credit market. Additionally, regions with historically high DTI ratios exhibit greater reductions in mortgage originations, house prices, and consumption.

中文摘要

本文研究了紧缩性货币政策如何通过抵押贷款市场传导至经济，并揭示了其在个人和区域层面的分配后果。我们发现，信贷供给因素，特别是对债务收入比（DTI）的限制，解释了抵押贷款下降的大部分原因。这些影响对少数族裔和中等收入借款人更为显著，他们发现自己被排除在信贷市场之外。此外，历史上债务收入比（DTI）较高的地区在抵押贷款发放、房价和消费方面表现出更大的减少。

Firms with Benefits? Nonwage Compensation and Implications for Firms and Labor Markets

提供福利的企业？非工资补偿及其对企业与劳动力市场的影响

Paige Ouimet, Geoffrey Tate

Programs：CF, LS；JEL：G32, J01, J23, J32, J63

Abstract

Using administrative data on health insurance, retirement, and leave benefits, we find within-firm variation accounts for a dramatically lower percentage of total variation in benefits than in wages. We also document sharply higher between-firm variation in nonwage benefits than in wages. We argue that this pattern can be a consequence of nondiscrimination regulations, fairness concerns, and the high administrative burden of managing too many or complex plans. Consistent with this mechanism, we show that the presence of high-wage workers in unrelated divisions of a firm as well as workers hired in high-benefit local labor markets positively predicts their colleagues’ benefits, controlling for occupation, wages, state, and industry. We find that the resulting high benefits reduce turnover, particularly among low-wage workers, for whom the benefits comprise a larger percentage of total compensation. Moreover, firms with more generous benefits attract and retain more high-wage workers, but also reduce their reliance on low-wage workers more than low-benefit peers. Our results suggest that benefits disproportionately matter for worker-firm matching and, hence, compensation inequality.

中文摘要

利用关于健康保险、退休和休假福利的行政数据，我们发现，在福利总变异中，企业内变异所占的比例远低于工资。我们还记录了企业间在非工资福利方面的变异远高于工资。我们认为，这种模式可能是非歧视性法规、公平性关切以及管理过多或复杂计划的高行政负担的结果。与这一机制一致，我们表明，在控制了职业、工资、州和行业后，企业不相关部门中高工资工人的存在，以及在高福利地方劳动力市场雇用的工人，会正向预测其同事的福利。我们发现，由此产生的高福利降低了离职率，尤其是在低工资工人中，对他们而言，福利占总薪酬的比例更高。此外，提供更慷慨福利的企业能吸引和留住更多高工资工人，但与低福利同行相比，它们也更多地减少了对低工资工人的依赖。我们的结果表明，福利对工人-企业匹配以及由此产生的薪酬不平等具有不成比例的重要影响。

No. 11NBER w31462

Bank Branch Density and Bank Runs

银行网点密度与银行挤兑

Efraim Benmelech, Jun Yang, Michal Zator

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, IFM；JEL：E44, E52, G20, G21, G28

Abstract

Bank branch density, defined as the number of bank branches to total deposits, has significantly declined over the past decade, fueled by a confluence of branch closings and the almost doubling of deposits between 2016 and 2022. During this period, banks with low branch density benefited from large deposits inflows, leading to even lower density. But the virtuous cycle of deposits growth in these banks stopped spinning when investors became wary about their financial health. Stock prices of banks with low branch density plummeted during the 2023 Banking Crisis as these banks experienced larger outflows of uninsured deposits. Our results suggest that digital banking enabled banks to grow faster and attract uninsured deposits, but those large deposits inflows took the form of “hot money” that changed its course when economic conditions worsened.

中文摘要

银行网点密度（定义为银行网点数量与总存款之比）在过去十年中显著下降，这是由于网点关闭与2016年至2022年间存款几乎翻倍共同推动的。在此期间，网点密度低的银行受益于大量存款流入，导致密度进一步降低。但是，当投资者开始担忧这些银行的财务状况时，这些银行存款增长的正向循环停止了。在2023年银行业危机期间，网点密度低的银行股价暴跌，因为这些银行经历了更大的未保险存款外流。我们的结果表明，数字银行使银行能够更快增长并吸引未保险存款，但这些大量存款流入采取了“热钱”的形式，当经济状况恶化时，其流向发生了改变。

No. 12NBER w31461

Regulating Transformative Technologies

监管变革性技术

Daron Acemoglu, Todd Lensman

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：H21, O33, O41

Abstract

Transformative technologies like generative artificial intelligence promise to accelerate productivity growth across many sectors, but they also present new risks from potential misuse. We develop a multi-sector technology adoption model to study the optimal regulation of transformative technologies when society can learn about these risks over time. Socially optimal adoption is gradual and convex. If social damages are proportional to the productivity gains from the new technology, a higher growth rate leads to slower optimal adoption. Equilibrium adoption is inefficient when firms do not internalize all social damages, and sector-independent regulation is helpful but generally not sufficient to restore optimality.

在大规模移民时代，阿根廷是第二大目的地国家，接收了近六百万移民。在本文中，我们首先总结了描述移民长期经济同化及其对地方经济发展贡献的最新发现。回顾的证据表明，欧洲人在阿根廷经历了快速向上的流动性，且移民对经济发展过程做出了积极贡献。然后，我们将焦点转向意大利移民——这一目的地最大移民群体——的选择模式。我们对移民历史这一初始阶段的分析表明，移居阿根廷的意大利人在识字率方面是正向选择的，这补充了关于在目的地快速向上流动和对增长贡献的现有证据。
