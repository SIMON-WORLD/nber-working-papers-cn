Liquidity Dependence and the Waxing and Waning of Central Bank Balance Sheets

流动性依赖与中央银行资产负债表的扩张与收缩

Viral V. Acharya, Rahul S. Chauhan, Raghuram Rajan, Sascha Steffen

Programs：CF, EFG, LE, ME, PE, POL；JEL：G21

Abstract

When the Federal Reserve (Fed) expanded its balance sheet via quantitative easing (QE), commercial banks typically financed reserve holdings with uninsured demandable deposits. They also issued credit lines to corporations. In the aggregate, these bank-issued claims on liquidity did not shrink commensurately when the Fed halted QE and turned to quantitative tightening (QT). Consequently, banks that increased liquidity risk exposure – especially small and regional banks – became vulnerable to liquidity shocks, necessitating further liquidity provision by the Fed. The evidence suggests that the expansion and shrinkage of central bank balance sheets has led to liquidity dependence of banks on central banks.

中文摘要

当美联储通过量化宽松（QE）扩大其资产负债表时，商业银行通常用无保险的活期存款来融资准备金持有。它们还向企业提供信贷额度。总体而言，这些银行发行的流动性债权在美联储停止QE并转向量化紧缩（QT）时并未相应缩减。因此，增加流动性风险敞口的银行——尤其是小型和地区性银行——变得容易受到流动性冲击的影响，需要美联储进一步提供流动性。证据表明，中央银行资产负债表的扩张和收缩导致了银行对中央银行的流动性依赖。

No. 2NBER w31049

How Are Gender Norms Perceived?

性别规范是如何被感知的？

Leonardo Bursztyn, Alexander W. Cappelen, Bertil Tungodden, Alessandra Voena, David H. Yanagizawa-Drott

Programs：LS；JEL：J16, J22

Abstract

This paper provides global evidence on misperceptions of gender norms, drawing on newly collected, nationally representative data from 60 countries. We establish that misperceptions are pervasive across the world. Through a simple conceptual framework, we provide evidence that gender stereotyping and overweighting of minority views are ubiquitous and appear to account for the global structure of misperceptions. Both actual and perceived support are shown to be associated with relevant economic behaviors, including female employment and actions promoting women’s representation in leadership. Our paper suggests that misperceptions are ubiquitous and may shape gender inequality globally.

中文摘要

本文基于从60个国家新收集的具有全国代表性的数据，提供了关于性别规范误解的全球证据。我们确定误解在世界各地普遍存在。通过一个简单的概念框架，我们提供了证据表明性别刻板印象和对少数观点的过度加权是普遍存在的，并且似乎解释了误解的全球结构。实际支持和感知支持都被证明与相关的经济行为有关，包括女性就业和促进女性在领导层中代表性的行动。我们的论文表明，误解普遍存在，并可能在全球范围内塑造性别不平等。

Monetary Tightening and U.S. Bank Fragility in 2023: Mark-to-Market Losses and Uninsured Depositor Runs?

2023年货币紧缩与美国银行脆弱性：按市值计价的损失与未保险存款人挤兑？

Erica Xuewei Jiang, Gregor Matvos, Tomasz Piskorski, Amit Seru

Programs：CF, EFG, ME；JEL：G2, L5

Abstract

We develop a conceptual framework and an empirical methodology to analyze the effect of rising interest rates on the value of U.S. bank assets and bank stability. We mark-to-market the value of banks’ assets due to interest rate increases from Q1 2022 to Q1 2023, revealing an average decline of 10%, totaling about $2 trillion in aggregate. We present a model illustrating how asset value declines due to higher rates can lead to self-fulfilling solvency runs even when banks’ assets are fully liquid. Banks with high asset losses, low capital, and, critically, high uninsured leverage are most fragile. A case study of the failed Silicon Valley Bank confirms the model insights. Our empirical measures of bank fragility suggest that, in the absence of regulatory intervention, many U.S. banks would have been at risk of self-fulfilling solvency runs.

中文摘要

我们构建了一个概念框架和一套实证方法论，用以分析利率上升对美国银行资产价值及银行稳定性的影响。我们对2022年第一季度至2023年第一季度期间因利率上升导致的银行资产价值进行按市值计价，结果显示平均下降了10%，总计约2万亿美元。我们提出了一个模型，说明即使银行资产完全具有流动性，利率上升导致的资产价值下降也可能引发自我实现的偿付能力挤兑。资产损失高、资本金低且关键的是未保险杠杆率高的银行最为脆弱。对硅谷银行倒闭案例的研究证实了该模型的见解。我们的银行脆弱性实证度量表明，若无监管干预，许多美国银行本将面临自我实现的偿付能力挤兑风险。

No. 4NBER w31047

Complexity and Hyperbolic Discounting

复杂性与双曲贴现

Benjamin Enke, Thomas Graeber, Ryan Oprea

Programs：LE；JEL：D03

Abstract

A large literature shows that people discount financial rewards hyperbolically instead of exponentially. While discounting of money has been questioned as a measure of time preferences, it continues to be highly relevant in empirical practice and predicts a wide range of real-world behaviors, creating a need to understand what generates the hyperbolic pattern. We provide evidence that hyperbolic discounting reflects mistakes that are driven by the complexity of evaluating delayed payoffs. In particular, we document that hyperbolicity (i) is strongly associated with choice inconsistency and cognitive uncertainty, (ii) increases in overt complexity manipulations and (iii) arises nearly identically in computationally similar tasks that involve no actual payoff delays. Our results suggest that even if people had exponential discount functions, complexity-driven mistakes would cause them to make hyperbolic choices. We examine which experimental techniques to estimate present bias are (not) confounded by complexity.

中文摘要

大量文献表明，人们对金钱奖励的贴现方式是双曲型的而非指数型的。尽管对金钱的贴现作为时间偏好的度量方式受到质疑，但它在实证实践中仍高度相关，并能预测广泛的实际行为，因此需要理解是什么导致了双曲贴现模式。我们提供的证据表明，双曲贴现反映了由评估延迟回报的复杂性所驱动的错误。具体而言，我们记录了双曲性（i）与选择不一致性和认知不确定性密切相关，（ii）在显性的复杂性操纵下会增加，并且（iii）在涉及无实际回报延迟的计算相似任务中几乎相同地出现。我们的结果表明，即使人们拥有指数贴现函数，复杂性驱动的错误也会导致他们做出双曲型选择。我们检验了哪些用于估计现时偏好的实验技术（不）会受到复杂性的混淆。

No. 5NBER w31046

Helicopter Drops and Liquidity Traps

直升机撒钱与流动性陷阱

Manuel Amador, Javier Bianchi

Programs：EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E31, E52, E58, E61, E63

Abstract

During a liquidity trap, increases in the money supply have no real effects, as the nominal interest rate has reached its lower bound. We propose a theory of how helicopter drops of money can be effective during a liquidity trap. We develop a New Keynesian monetary model where the fiscal and monetary authorities are separated, and the latter faces balance sheet constraints. If the monetary authority can commit, helicopter drops are unnecessary in a liquidity trap, even under balance sheet constraints. However, we show that helicopter drops can help stabilize the economy when the monetary authority lacks commitment.

中文摘要

在流动性陷阱期间，由于名义利率已达到下限，货币供应量的增加没有实际效果。我们提出了一种理论，说明直升机撒钱如何在流动性陷阱期间发挥作用。我们构建了一个新凯恩斯主义货币模型，其中财政当局和货币当局是分离的，且后者面临资产负债表约束。如果货币当局能够承诺，即使在资产负债表约束下，流动性陷阱中也不需要直升机撒钱。然而，我们表明，当货币当局缺乏承诺能力时，直升机撒钱有助于稳定经济。

No. 6NBER w31045

From Syringes to Dishes: Improving Food Sufficiency through Vaccination

从注射器到餐盘：通过疫苗接种改善食物充足性

Erkmen G. Aslim, Wei Fu, Erdal Tekin, Shijun You

Programs：EH；JEL：I14, I18

Abstract

This paper examines the impact of COVID-19 vaccination on food insufficiency in the United States, using data from the Household Pulse Survey. Our primary research design exploits variation in vaccine eligibility across states over time as an instrumental variable to address the endogeneity of vaccination decision. We find that vaccination had a substantial impact on food hardship by reducing the likelihood of food insufficiency by 24%, with even stronger effects among minority and financially disadvantaged populations. These results are robust to alternative specifications and the use of regression discontinuity as an alternative identification strategy. We also show that vaccine eligibility had a positive spillover impact on food assistance programs, notably reducing participation in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program and the use of its benefits, suggesting that vaccination policies can help alleviate the government’s fiscal burden during public health crises. Our analysis offers detailed insights into the potential mechanisms linking vaccination to food insufficiency. We demonstrate that vaccination yields changes in both material circumstances and financial expectations. Specifically, vaccination increases the use of regular income for spending needs and reduces reports of insufficient food due to unaffordability. Additionally, we find that vaccination improves financial optimism, reflected in expectations for future employment income loss and the ability to meet mortgage and debt obligations. Our findings are consistent with the notion that this optimism, along with labor market recovery, diminished the need for precautionary savings, reduced reliance on government assistance, and encouraged household spending on essential goods like food, ultimately lowering food insufficiency.

中文摘要

本文利用家庭脉搏调查数据，研究了新冠疫苗接种对美国食物不足的影响。我们的主要研究设计利用各州疫苗接种资格随时间变化的差异作为工具变量，以解决疫苗接种决策的内生性问题。我们发现，疫苗接种通过将食物不足的可能性降低24%，对食物困难产生了显著影响，在少数族裔和财务弱势群体中效果更强。这些结果在替代性设定和使用断点回归作为替代识别策略时依然稳健。我们还表明，疫苗接种资格对食物援助计划产生了正向溢出效应，显著减少了补充营养援助计划的参与及其福利使用，表明疫苗接种政策有助于在公共卫生危机期间减轻政府的财政负担。我们的分析详细揭示了疫苗接种与食物不足之间的潜在机制。我们证明，疫苗接种带来了物质状况和财务预期的变化。具体而言，疫苗接种增加了使用常规收入满足支出需求的情况，并减少了因无力负担而导致食物不足的报告。此外，我们发现疫苗接种改善了财务乐观情绪，这体现在对未来就业收入损失以及偿还抵押贷款和债务能力的预期上。我们的发现与以下观点一致：这种乐观情绪，加上劳动力市场的复苏，减少了对预防性储蓄的需求，降低了对政府援助的依赖，并鼓励了家庭在食品等必需品上的支出，最终降低了食物不足。

No. 7NBER w31044

Global High-Resolution Estimates of the United Nations Human Development Index Using Satellite Imagery and Machine-learning

利用卫星图像和机器学习对联合国人类发展指数进行全球高分辨率估算

Luke Sherman, Jonathan Proctor, Hannah Druckenmiller, Heriberto Tapia, Solomon M. Hsiang

Programs：DEV, EEE, EFG, EH, PE；JEL：C1, C8, I32, R1

Abstract

The United Nations Human Development Index (HDI) is arguably the most widely used alternative to gross domestic product for measuring national development. This is in large part due to its multidimensional nature, as it incorporates not only income, but also education and health. However, the country-level resolution of the global HDI data released by the Human Development Report Office of the United Nations Development Programme (N=191 countries) has limited its use at the local level. Recent efforts used survey data to produce HDI estimates for first-level administrative units (e.g., states/provinces). Here, we build on recent advances in machine learning and satellite imagery to develop the first global estimates of HDI for second-level administrative units (e.g., municipalities/counties, N = 61,530) and for a global 0.1° × 0.1° grid (N=819,309). To accomplish this we develop and validate a generalizable downscaling technique based on satellite imagery that allows for training and prediction with observations of arbitrary shape and size. This enables us to train a model using provincial administrative data and generate HDI estimates at the municipality and grid levels. Our results indicate that more than half of the global population was previously assigned to the incorrect HDI quintile within each country, due to aggregation bias resulting from lower resolution estimates. We also illustrate how these data can improve decision-making. We make these high-resolution HDI estimates publicly available in the hope that they increase understanding of human wellbeing globally and improve the effectiveness of policies supporting sustainable development. We also make available the satellite features necessary to increase the spatial resolution of any other administrative data that is detectable via imagery.

中文摘要

联合国人类发展指数可以说是衡量国家发展方面最广泛使用的国内生产总值替代指标。这在很大程度上归因于其多维性质，因为它不仅包含收入，还包含教育和健康。然而，联合国开发计划署人类发展报告办公室发布的全球人类发展指数数据仅限于国家层面（N=191个国家），这限制了其在地方层面的使用。最近的研究利用调查数据对一级行政单位（如州/省）的人类发展指数进行了估算。在此，我们基于机器学习和卫星图像的最新进展，首次对二级行政单位（如市/县，N=61,530）以及全球0.1°×0.1°网格（N=819,309）的人类发展指数进行了全球估算。为实现这一目标，我们开发并验证了一种基于卫星图像的通用降尺度技术，该技术允许使用任意形状和大小的观测数据进行训练和预测。这使我们能够利用省级行政数据训练模型，并生成市级和网格级的人类发展指数估算值。我们的结果表明，由于低分辨率估算导致的聚合偏差，全球超过一半的人口此前被错误地归入其所在国家内的人类发展指数五分位组。我们还说明了这些数据如何能够改善决策。我们公开提供这些高分辨率的人类发展指数估算值，希望它们能增进全球对人类福祉的理解，并提高支持可持续发展的政策的有效性。我们还提供了必要的卫星特征，以便提高任何可通过图像检测到的其他行政数据的空间分辨率。

No. 8NBER w31043

The Last Mile of Monetary Policy: Inattention, Reminders, and the Refinancing Channel

货币政策的最后一公里：注意力不足、提醒与再融资渠道

Shane Byrne, Kenneth Devine, Michael King, Yvonne McCarthy, Christopher Palmer

Programs：CF, ME；JEL：D83, E58, G21, G28, G51

Abstract

Under-refinancing limits the transmission of accommodative monetary policy to the household sector and costs mortgage holders in many countries a significant fraction of income annually. We test whether targeted communication can reduce the attention frictions that inhibit transmission by partnering with a large bank to analyze a field experiment testing messages sent to 12,000 Irish households. While we find only small effects of disclosure design improvements, a reminder letter increases refinancing by 76%, from 8.9% to 15.7%. To interpret this reminder effect, we extend and estimate a mixture model of inattentive financial decision-making to allow for disclosure treatment effects on attention. We find that reminders increase the likelihood mortgage holders are attentive by over 60%, from 24% to 39%. A conservative back-of-the-envelope cost-effectiveness calculation implies that the average reminder letter generated €42 of mortgagor consumption (€605 per refinancing household). Our results illustrate that targeted central bank communication such as refinancing reminders could have a larger effect on refinancing than a standard policy rate cut. Reminders could further strengthen the refinancing channel and stimulate local consumption even when policy rates are at the zero-lower bound or set in a monetary union.

中文摘要

再融资不足限制了宽松货币政策向家庭部门的传导，并每年使许多国家的抵押贷款持有人损失相当大比例的收入。我们通过与一家大型银行合作，分析一项针对12,000个爱尔兰家庭发送信息的实地实验，检验有针对性的沟通能否减少阻碍传导的注意力摩擦。虽然我们发现披露设计改进的效果很小，但一封提醒信使再融资率从8.9%提高到15.7%，增加了76%。为了解释这种提醒效应，我们扩展并估计了一个注意力不足的金融决策混合模型，以允许披露处理对注意力的影响。我们发现，提醒使抵押贷款持有人保持注意力的可能性从24%提高到39%，增加了60%以上。一项保守的粗略成本效益计算表明，平均每封提醒信为抵押贷款持有人带来了42欧元的消费（每个再融资家庭605欧元）。我们的结果表明，有针对性的央行沟通（如再融资提醒）对再融资的影响可能大于标准的政策利率下调。即使在政策利率处于零下限或由货币联盟设定的情况下，提醒也能进一步加强再融资渠道并刺激本地消费。

Pivotal or Popular: The Effects of Social Information and Feeling Pivotal on Civic Actions

关键还是受欢迎：社会信息与感觉关键对公民行为的影响

Laura K. Gee, Anoushka Kiyawat, Jonathan Meer, Michael J. Schreck

Programs：PE；JEL：D64, H41

Abstract

We examine the combined effects of popularity and feelings of being important to reaching a goal by testing how people react to situations in which their own behavior is pivotal or not, as well as the popularity of the action. We conduct a laboratory experiment to cleanly fix beliefs about the person's likelihood of being pivotal in reaching a donation threshold that triggers a matching gift, varying both the pivotality and the number of other donors (popularity). The results are striking, with those whose action is pivotal being more than twice as likely to make a donation. We then conduct two field experiments to test these findings in real-world settings. Our results suggest that pivotality is a more important determinant of prosocial behavior.

中文摘要

我们通过测试人们在自身行为是否关键以及行为受欢迎程度不同情况下的反应，来研究受欢迎程度和感觉对达成目标的重要性两者的综合效应。我们进行了一项实验室实验，以清晰地固定人们对自身在达到触发配捐的捐赠门槛时是否关键的信念，同时改变关键性和其他捐赠者的数量（受欢迎程度）。结果令人瞩目，那些行为关键的人进行捐赠的可能性是其他人的两倍以上。随后，我们进行了两项实地实验，以在现实世界中检验这些发现。我们的结果表明，关键性是亲社会行为的更重要决定因素。

Personality Differences and Investment Decision-Making

人格差异与投资决策

Zhengyang Jiang, Cameron Peng, Hongjun Yan

Programs：AP；JEL：D91, G11, G41

Abstract

We survey thousands of affluent American investors to examine the relationship between personalities and investment decisions. The Big Five personality traits correlate with investors' beliefs about the stock market and economy, risk preferences, and social interaction tendencies. Two personality traits, Neuroticism and Openness, stand out in their explanatory power for equity investments. Investors with high Neuroticism and those with low Openness tend to allocate less investment to equities. We examine the underlying mechanisms and find evidence for both standard channels of preferences and beliefs and other nonstandard channels. We show consistent out-of-sample evidence in representative panels of Australian and German households.

A User's Guide to Inference in Models Defined by Moment Inequalities

矩不等式定义模型推断的用户指南

Ivan Canay, Gastón Illanes, Amilcar Velez

Programs：IO, TWP；JEL：C12, C14

Abstract

Models defined by moment inequalities have become a standard modeling framework for empirical economists, spreading over a wide range of fields within economics. From the point of view of an empirical researcher, the literature on inference in moment inequality models is large and complex, including multiple survey papers that document the non-standard features these models possess, the main novel concepts behind inference in these models, and the most recent developments that bring advances in accuracy and computational tractability. In this paper we present a guide to empirical practice intended to help applied researchers navigate all the decisions required to frame a model as a moment inequality model and then to construct confidence intervals for the parameters of interest. We divide our template into four main steps: (a) a behavioral decision model, (b) moving from the decision model to a moment inequality model, (c) choosing a test statistic and critical value, and (d) accounting for computational challenges. We split each of these steps into a discussion of the “how” and the “why”, and then illustrate how to take these steps to practice in an empirical application that studies identification of expected sunk costs of offering a product in a market.

中文摘要

由矩不等式定义的模型已成为实证经济学家的标准建模框架，广泛覆盖经济学内的多个领域。从实证研究者的角度来看，关于矩不等式模型推断的文献庞大且复杂，包括多篇综述论文，这些论文记录了这些模型所具有的非标准特征、这些模型推断背后的主要新概念，以及带来准确性和计算易处理性进步的最新发展。在本文中，我们提供了一份实证实践指南，旨在帮助应用研究者驾驭将模型构建为矩不等式模型所需的所有决策，然后为感兴趣的参数构建置信区间。我们将模板分为四个主要步骤：(a) 行为决策模型，(b) 从决策模型过渡到矩不等式模型，(c) 选择检验统计量和临界值，以及 (d) 应对计算挑战。我们将每个步骤分解为“如何做”和“为什么做”的讨论，然后通过一个实证应用来说明如何将这些步骤付诸实践，该应用研究在市场中提供产品的预期沉没成本的识别。

Fortunate Families? The Effects of Wealth on Marriage and Fertility

幸运的家庭？财富对婚姻和生育的影响

David Cesarini, Erik Lindqvist, Robert Östling, Anastasia Terskaya

Programs：CH, LS；JEL：D01, J12, J13

Abstract

We estimate the effects of large, positive wealth shocks on marriage and fertility in a sample of Swedish lottery players. Programs：PE, TWP；JEL：C72, D44, D82

Abstract

Two potentially asymmetric players compete for a prize of common value, which is initially unknown, by exerting efforts. A designer has two instruments for contest design. First, she decides whether and how to disclose an informative signal of the prize value to players. Second, she sets the scoring rule of the contest, which varies the relative competitiveness of the players. We show that the optimum depends on the designer’s objective. A bilateral symmetric contest—in which information is symmetrically distributed and the scoring bias is set to offset the initial asymmetry between players—always maximizes the expected total effort. However, the optimal contest may deliberately create bilateral asymmetry—which discloses the signal privately to one player, while favoring the other in terms of the scoring rule—when the designer is concerned about the expected winner’s effort. The two instruments thus exhibit complementarity, in that the optimum can be made asymmetric in both dimensions even if the players are ex ante symmetric. Our results are qualitatively robust to (i) affiliated signals and (ii) endogenous information structure. We show that information favoritism can play a useful role in addressing affirmative action objectives.

Abstract

Abstract
