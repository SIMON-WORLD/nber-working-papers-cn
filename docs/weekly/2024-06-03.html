No. 1NBER w32532

An Analysis of Pandemic-Era Inflation in 11 Economies

11个经济体疫情时期通胀分析

Olivier J. Blanchard, Ben S. Bernanke

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E30, E31, E32, E52

Abstract

In a collaborative project with ten central banks, we have investigated the causes of the post-pandemic global inflation, building on our earlier work for the United States. Globally, as in the United States, pandemic-era inflation was due primarily to supply disruptions and sharp increases in the prices of food and energy; however, and in sharp contrast to the 1970s, the inflationary effects of these supply shocks have not been persistent, in part due to the credibility of central bank inflation targets. As the effects of supply shocks have subsided, tight labor markets, and the rises in nominal wages, have become relatively more important sources of inflation in many countries. In several countries, including the United States, curbing wage inflation and returning price inflation to target may require a period of modestly higher unemployment.

中文摘要

在与十家中央银行合作的项目中，我们在先前对美国研究的基础上，调查了疫情后全球通胀的原因。全球范围内，如同美国一样，疫情时期的通胀主要是由供应中断以及食品和能源价格急剧上涨所致；然而，与1970年代形成鲜明对比的是，这些供应冲击的通胀效应并未持续，部分原因在于央行通胀目标的可信度。随着供应冲击的影响消退，在许多国家，劳动力市场紧张和名义工资上涨已成为相对更重要的通胀来源。在包括美国在内的几个国家，要抑制工资通胀并使价格通胀回归目标水平，可能需要一段失业率适度上升的时期。

No. 2NBER w32531

The Inflation Accelerator

通胀加速器

Andrés Blanco, Corina Boar, Callum J. Jones, Virgiliu Midrigan

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E3, E40

Abstract

We develop a tractable sticky price model in which the fraction of price changes evolves endogenously over time and, consistent with the evidence, increases with inflation. Because we assume that firms sell multiple products and choose how many, but not which, prices to adjust in every period, our model admits exact aggregation and reduces to a one-equation extension of the Calvo model. The model features a powerful inflation accelerator–a feedback loop between inflation and the fraction of price changes – which significantly increases the slope of the Phillips curve during periods of high inflation.

中文摘要

我们构建了一个易于处理的价格粘性模型，其中价格调整的比例随时间内生演变，并且与证据一致，该比例随通胀上升而增加。由于我们假设企业销售多种产品，并在每个时期选择调整多少种价格（而非哪些价格），我们的模型允许精确加总，并简化为卡尔沃模型的一个单方程扩展。该模型具有强大的通胀加速器——即通胀与价格调整比例之间的反馈循环——这在高通胀时期显著增加了菲利普斯曲线的斜率。

A Shared Interest: Do Bonds Strengthen Equity Monitoring?

共同利益：债券会加强股权监督吗？

Todd A. Gormley, Manish Jha

Programs：CF；JEL：G23, G30, G32, G34, K22

Abstract

Institutional investors conduct more governance research and are less likely to follow proxy advisor vote recommendations when a company’s bonds comprise a larger share of their assets. These findings are driven by bond holdings, shareholder proposals, and companies where fixed-income managers are more likely to be attentive and share an interest with equity investors in improving governance. The findings do not concentrate on companies or shareholder proposals where creditor-shareholder conflicts are likely. Overall, the findings suggest that corporate bond holdings influence how actively institutions monitor their equity positions and contribute to institutions’ overall incentive to be engaged stewards.

中文摘要

当一家公司的债券在其资产中占较大份额时，机构投资者会进行更多的治理研究，并且不太可能遵循代理顾问的投票建议。这些发现是由债券持有量、股东提案以及固定收益管理者更可能关注并与股权投资者在改善治理方面有共同利益的公司所驱动的。这些发现并不集中在可能存在债权人-股东冲突的公司或股东提案上。总体而言，这些发现表明，公司债券持有量会影响机构投资者积极监督其股权头寸的方式，并有助于增强机构投资者成为积极参与管理者的整体激励。

No. 4NBER w32529

Her Property Transactions: White Women and the Frequency of Female Ownership in the Antebellum Era

她的财产交易：内战前白人女性与女性所有权的频率

Benton Wishart, Trevon D. Logan

Programs：DAE；JEL：J1, N31, Z13

Abstract

The traditional historical narrative claims that White women were rarely involved in market transactions for enslaved people in the antebellum United States. Using transaction records, notary statements, and runaway advertisements, we provide the first quantitative estimates of the extent of White women’s involvement in antebellum slave transactions as owners of record. Contrary to the narrative, we find that White women were quite frequently noted as owners of record in transactions as both buyers and sellers. White women participated in more than 30% of the transactions in the largest market for enslaved people in the antebellum era. We also find that White women were especially likely to be owners involved in transactions with enslaved women, where they were listed as owners in nearly 40% of transactions. Linking transaction participants to the census, we find that White women owners were not more likely to be widows nor were they older than women in the general population. Overall, our results are consistent with the new historical narrative that White women were ubiquitous in enslavement transactions and this was a critical part of White women’s economic activity in the antebellum era.

中文摘要

传统历史叙述声称，在内战前的美国，白人女性很少参与被奴役者的市场交易。利用交易记录、公证人声明和逃亡广告，我们首次提供了白人女性在内战前奴隶交易中作为登记所有者参与程度的定量估计。与这种叙述相反，我们发现白人女性作为登记所有者，在交易中作为买方和卖方都相当频繁地出现。在内战前最大的被奴役者市场中，白人女性参与了超过30%的交易。我们还发现，白人女性尤其可能成为涉及被奴役女性的交易中的所有者，在近40%的交易中被列为所有者。将交易参与者与人口普查数据关联后，我们发现白人女性所有者并非更可能是寡妇，也不比一般女性人口年龄更大。总体而言，我们的结果与新的历史叙述一致，即白人女性在奴隶交易中无处不在，这是内战前白人女性经济活动的一个关键部分。

No. 5NBER w32528

The Concave Phillips Curve

凹形菲利普斯曲线

Narayana R. Kocherlakota

Programs：ME；JEL：E31, E52

Abstract

This paper derives the curvature properties of the short-run Phillips curve for a wide class of models with time-dependent pricing frictions. Contrary to conventional thinking, the Phillips curve is asymptotically horizontal for high levels of economic activity and asymptotically vertical for low levels of economic activity. Moreover, it is globally concave under relatively weak conditions that allow real marginal cost to be an unbounded convex function of economic activity. Intuitively, when economic activity is very high (low), substitution effects within the price index imply that inflation behaves as if prices are nearly fully sticky (flexible). Using (conventional) measures of inflation that understate the relevant substitution effects may lead to misleading conclusions about the curvature of the Phillips curve, and to corresponding errors in the formulation of monetary policy.

中文摘要

本文推导了在广泛的一类具有时间依赖定价摩擦的模型中短期菲利普斯曲线的曲率性质。与传统观点相反，菲利普斯曲线在高经济活动水平下渐近水平，在低经济活动水平下渐近垂直。此外，在相对宽松的条件下（允许实际边际成本成为经济活动的无界凸函数），该曲线全局呈凹形。直观上，当经济活动非常高（低）时，价格指数内的替代效应意味着通胀的表现仿佛价格几乎完全粘性（灵活）。使用低估相关替代效应的（传统）通胀衡量指标，可能会导致关于菲利普斯曲线曲率的误导性结论，以及货币政策制定中的相应错误。

No. 6NBER w32527

Unemployment Insurance Fraud in the Debit Card Market

借记卡市场中的失业保险欺诈

Umang Khetan, Jetson Leder-Luis, Jialan Wang, Yunrong Zhou

Programs：PE；JEL：G51, H53, J65, K42

Abstract

We study fraud in the unemployment insurance (UI) system using a dataset of 35 million debit card transactions. We apply machine learning techniques to cluster cards corresponding to varying levels of suspicious or potentially fraudulent activity. We then conduct a difference-in-differences analysis based on the staggered adoption of state-level identity verification systems between 2020 and 2021 to assess the effectiveness of screening for reducing fraud. Our findings suggest that identity verification reduced payouts to suspicious cards by 27%, while non-suspicious cards were largely unaffected by these technologies. Our results indicate that identity screening may be an effective mechanism for mitigating fraud in the UI system and for benefits programs more broadly.

中文摘要

我们利用一个包含3500万笔借记卡交易的数据集研究失业保险系统中的欺诈行为。我们应用机器学习技术对卡片进行聚类，以对应不同程度的可疑或潜在欺诈活动。然后，我们基于2020年至2021年间各州分阶段采用身份验证系统的情况，进行双重差分分析，以评估筛查在减少欺诈方面的有效性。我们的研究结果表明，身份验证使向可疑卡片的支付减少了27%，而无可疑卡片在很大程度上未受这些技术的影响。我们的结果表明，身份筛查可能是减轻失业保险系统以及更广泛福利项目中欺诈行为的有效机制。

Measuring and Predicting “New Work” in the United States: The Role of Local Factors and Global Shocks

衡量和预测美国的“新工作”：地方因素与全球冲击的作用

Gueyon Kim, Cassandra Merritt, Giovanni Peri

Programs：ITI, LS, PR；JEL：F1, J11, J14, J23, J24, J61, O33

Abstract

The evolution of work is of emerging importance to advanced economies' growth. In this study, we develop a new semantic-distance-based algorithm to identify “new work,” namely the new types of jobs introduced in the US. We characterize how “new work” relates to task content of jobs and skill characteristics of workers and document its geographic distribution and association with employment growth. Then, we analyze whether local factors associated in the previous literature with agglomeration economies and productivity growth as well as local exposures to global shocks—technology, trade, immigration, and population aging—predict the creation of “new work.” We find local supply of college educated in 1980 as the strongest predictor of “new work.” Using the historical location of 4-year colleges, a strong instrument for local college share, we find a positive and significant causal effect of local supply of human capital on “new work.”

中文摘要

工作的演变对发达经济体的增长日益重要。在本研究中，我们开发了一种新的基于语义距离的算法来识别“新工作”，即美国引入的新类型工作岗位。我们描述了“新工作”如何与工作的任务内容和工人的技能特征相关联，并记录了其地理分布以及与就业增长的关系。然后，我们分析了先前文献中与集聚经济和生产率增长相关的地方因素，以及地方对全球冲击（技术、贸易、移民和人口老龄化）的暴露程度，是否能够预测“新工作”的创造。我们发现，1980年地方大学毕业生供给是“新工作”最强的预测因子。利用四年制大学的历史位置作为地方大学份额的强工具变量，我们发现地方人力资本供给对“新工作”具有正向且显著的因果效应。

No. 8NBER w32524

Risky Oil: It's All in the Tails

风险石油：一切都在尾部

Christiane Baumeister, Florian Huber, Massimiliano Marcellino

Programs：EEE, IFM, ME；JEL：C11, C32, C53, Q41, Q47

Abstract

The substantial fluctuations in oil prices in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic and the Russian invasion of Ukraine have highlighted the importance of tail events in the global market for crude oil which call for careful risk assessment. In this paper we focus on forecasting tail risks in the oil market by setting up a general empirical framework that allows for flexible predictive distributions of oil prices that can depart from normality. This model, based on Bayesian additive regression trees, remains agnostic on the functional form of the conditional mean relations and assumes that the shocks are driven by a stochastic volatility model. We show that our nonparametric approach improves in terms of tail forecasts upon three competing models: quantile regressions commonly used for studying tail events, the Bayesian VAR with stochastic volatility, and the simple random walk. We illustrate the practical relevance of our new approach by tracking the evolution of predictive densities during three recent economic and geopolitical crisis episodes, by developing consumer and producer distress indices that signal the build-up of upside and downside price risk, and by conducting a risk scenario analysis for 2024.

中文摘要

新冠疫情和俄罗斯入侵乌克兰之后石油价格的大幅波动，凸显了全球原油市场中尾部事件的重要性，这要求进行仔细的风险评估。在本文中，我们通过建立一个通用的实证框架来关注石油市场尾部风险的预测，该框架允许石油价格具有灵活且可能偏离正态分布的预测分布。该模型基于贝叶斯加性回归树，对条件均值关系的函数形式保持不可知，并假设冲击由随机波动率模型驱动。我们表明，我们的非参数方法在尾部预测方面优于三个竞争模型：常用于研究尾部事件的分位数回归、具有随机波动率的贝叶斯VAR以及简单的随机游走。我们通过追踪最近三次经济和地缘政治危机事件期间预测密度的演变、开发指示上行和下行价格风险积累的生产者和消费者困境指数，以及进行2024年风险情景分析，来说明我们新方法的实际相关性。

No. 9NBER w32523

Latent Heterogeneity in the Marginal Propensity to Consume

边际消费倾向中的潜在异质性

Daniel Lewis, Davide Melcangi, Laura Pilossoph

Programs：EFG；JEL：D12, E21, E62

Abstract

We estimate the unconditional distribution of the marginal propensity to consume (MPC) using clustering regression applied to the 2008 economic stimulus payments. By deviating from the standard approach of estimating MPC heterogeneity using interactions with observables, we can recover the full distribution of MPCs. We find households spent between 4 and 133% of the rebate within a quarter, and individual households used rebates for different goods. While many observable characteristics correlate individually with our estimated MPCs, these relationships disappear when tested jointly, except for income and the average propensity to consume. Household observable characteristics explain only 8% of MPC variation, highlighting the role of latent heterogeneity.

中文摘要

我们利用聚类回归方法，基于2008年经济刺激支付数据，估计了边际消费倾向的无条件分布。通过偏离使用可观测变量交互项来估计MPC异质性的标准方法，我们能够恢复MPC的完整分布。我们发现，家庭在一个季度内花费了退税的4%至133%，并且不同家庭将退税用于不同的商品。虽然许多可观测特征与我们的估计MPC单独相关，但当联合检验时，这些关系消失，除了收入和平均消费倾向。家庭可观测特征仅解释了MPC变异的8%，凸显了潜在异质性的作用。

No. 10NBER w32522

The Exchange Rate as an Industrial Policy

作为产业政策的汇率

Pablo Ottonello, Diego J. Perez, William Witheridge

Programs：EFG, IFM, ITI；JEL：F0, F3, F4

Abstract

We study the role of exchange rates in industrial policy. We construct an open-economy macroeconomic framework with production externalities and show that the desirability of these policies critically depends on the dynamic patterns of externalities. When they are stronger in earlier stages of development, economies that are converging to the technological frontier can improve welfare by intervening in foreign exchange markets, keeping the exchange rate undervalued, and speeding the transition; economies that are not converging to the technological frontier are better off not using the exchange rate as an industrial policy tool. Capital-flow mobility and labor market dynamism play a central role in the effectiveness of these policies. We also discuss the role of capital controls as an industrial policy tool and use our framework to interpret historical experiences.

中文摘要

我们研究了汇率在产业政策中的作用。我们构建了一个包含生产外部性的开放经济宏观框架，并表明这些政策的可取性关键取决于外部性的动态模式。当外部性在发展的早期阶段更强时，正在向技术前沿收敛的经济体可以通过干预外汇市场、保持汇率低估并加速转型来改善福利；而未向技术前沿收敛的经济体则最好不使用汇率作为产业政策工具。资本流动性和劳动力市场活力在这些政策的有效性中起着核心作用。我们还讨论了资本管制作为产业政策工具的作用，并利用我们的框架来解释历史经验。

No. 11NBER w32521

Is the Decline in the Number of Community Banks Detrimental to Community Economic Development?

社区银行数量下降是否不利于社区经济发展？

Bernadette Minton, Alvaro G. Taboada, Rohan Williamson

Programs：CF；JEL：G2, G20, G21, G28

Abstract

Our research examines the impact of dwindling community bank numbers on community investment and economic development. Initially, we confirm the vital role of community banks’ small business lending in local development. Contrary to popular belief, we find that a decrease in community banks positively affects community investment, through small business loan (SBL) originations. Key factors include the local presence of other community banks and the continuity of the consolidating bank's presence. Interestingly, the effect remains neutral in underserved or distressed counties and diminishes when a large bank acquires a community bank without maintaining a local presence. Post-consolidation, community banks emerge larger and more robust, capable of issuing larger SBLs, while larger banks and Fintech firms contribute by providing smaller SBLs. Overall, our findings reinforce the critical contribution of community banks to local development, suggesting that a reduction in their numbers leads to a stronger, more stable banking infrastructure in the small business lending landscape.

中文摘要

我们的研究考察了社区银行数量减少对社区投资和经济发展的影响。首先，我们确认了社区银行的小企业贷款在地方发展中的关键作用。与普遍看法相反，我们发现社区银行数量的减少通过小企业贷款发放对社区投资产生积极影响。关键因素包括其他社区银行的本地存在以及合并银行持续存在的连续性。有趣的是，在服务不足或陷入困境的县，这种影响保持中性，并且当大型银行收购社区银行而未保持本地存在时，这种影响减弱。合并后，社区银行变得更大、更稳健，能够发放更大的小企业贷款，而大型银行和金融科技公司通过提供较小的小企业贷款做出贡献。总体而言，我们的发现强化了社区银行对地方发展的关键贡献，表明其数量减少导致小企业贷款领域出现更强大、更稳定的银行基础设施。

No. 12NBER w32520

The Effects of Tobacco 21 Laws on Smoking and Vaping: Evidence from Panel Data and Biomarkers

烟草21岁法案对吸烟和电子烟的影响：来自面板数据和生物标志物的证据

Chad D. Cotti, Philip DeCicca, Erik T. Nesson

Programs：EH；JEL：I12, I18, K32

Abstract

We use data from the Population Assessment of Tobacco Use and Health (PATH), a longitudinal data set including self-reported and biomarker measures of tobacco use, to examine the effects of state-level tobacco 21 (T21) laws on smoking and vaping. T21 laws reduce self-reported cigarette smoking among 18-to-20 year olds, concentrated in males. Initial non-users who “age-out” of treatment are less likely to subsequently initiate self-reported smoking or vaping. Treated smokers are less likely to buy their own cigarettes and more likely to buy cigarettes in a different state. Biomarker results are mixed, and we find some evidence of a reduction in nicotine exposure but less evidence for a reduction in exposure to tobacco. Finally, we test for non-classical measurement error. T21 laws reduce the probability that clinically identified likely cigarette smokers self-report as smokers, which may increase the apparent effect of T21 laws on cigarette smoking as measured by self-reports.

中文摘要

我们使用烟草使用与健康人口评估数据，这是一个包含自我报告和生物标志物测量的纵向数据集，来考察州级烟草21岁法案对吸烟和电子烟的影响。T21法案减少了18至20岁人群的自我报告吸烟，主要集中在男性。初始非使用者中“超龄”脱离治疗的人随后开始自我报告吸烟或电子烟的可能性较低。受治疗的吸烟者更少购买自己的香烟，更可能在不同州购买香烟。生物标志物结果不一，我们发现一些证据表明尼古丁暴露减少，但烟草暴露减少的证据较少。最后，我们检验了非经典测量误差。T21法案降低了临床认定的可能吸烟者自我报告为吸烟者的概率，这可能会增加T21法案对自我报告吸烟的明显效果。

No. 13NBER w32519

The Law of Small Numbers in Financial Markets: Theory and Evidence

金融市场中的小数定律：理论与证据

Lawrence J. Jin, Cameron Peng

Programs：AP；JEL：G0, G02, G11, G12

Abstract

We build a model of the law of small numbers (LSN)—the incorrect belief that even small samples represent the properties of the underlying population—to study its implications for trading behavior and asset prices. In our model, a belief in the LSN induces investors to expect short-term price trends to revert and long-term price trends to persist. As a result, asset prices exhibit short-term momentum and long-term reversals. The model can reconcile the coexistence of the disposition effect and return extrapolation. In addition, it makes new predictions about investor behavior, including return patterns before purchases and sales, a weakened disposition effect for long-term holdings, doubling down in buying, a positive correlation between doubling down and the disposition effect, and heterogeneous selling propensities to past returns. By testing these predictions using account-level transaction data, we show that the LSN provides a parsimonious way of understanding a variety of puzzles about investor behavior.

S. Borağan Aruoba, Eugene Oue, Felipe Saffie, Jonathan Willis

Programs：ME；JEL：E30, E52, L11

Abstract

We revisit micro real rigidities as a source of monetary non-neutrality in a menu-cost model with variable markups, using firm-level evidence to pin down key primitives. We embed a non-CES demand system in a quantitative monetary model and use firm-dynamics evidence to identify demand curvature and firm-level productivity and demand processes. The calibrated model matches untargeted micro pricing moments, the markup distribution, and cost pass-through, while generating comparable non-neutrality. The key innovation is an empirically supported placement of idiosyncratic demand shocks that shifts residual demand, thereby moving desired markups and prices under non-CES demand. The broader implication is that this calibrated model provides a portable framework linking monetary economics with trade and IO evidence.

Broadband Internet Access, Economic Growth, and Wellbeing

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I10, I12, I15

Abstract

Between 2000 and 2008, access to high-speed, broadband internet grew significantly in the United States, but there is debate on whether access to high-speed internet improves or harms wellbeing. We find that a ten percent increase in the proportion of county residents with access to broadband internet leads to a 1.01 percent reduction in the number of suicides in a county, as well as improvements in self-reported mental and physical health. We further find that this reduction in suicide deaths is likely due to economic improvements in counties that have access to broadband internet. Counties with increased access to broadband internet see reductions in poverty rate and unemployment rate. In addition, zip codes that gain access to broadband internet see increases in the numbers of employees and establishments. In addition, heterogeneity analysis indicates that the positive effects are concentrated in the working age population, those between 25 and 64 years old. This pattern is precisely what is predicted by the literature linking economic conditions to suicide risk.

Understanding Expert Choices Using Decision Time

利用决策时间理解专家选择

David Card, Stefano DellaVigna, Chenxi Jiang, Dmitry Taubinsky

Programs：ED, LS, PE, POL, PR；JEL：D03, O30

Abstract

Laboratory experiments find a robust relationship between decision times and perceived values of alternatives. This paper investigates how these findings translate to experts' decision making and information acquisition in the field. In a stylized model of expert choice between two alternatives, we show that (i) less-commonly chosen alternatives are more likely to be chosen later than earlier; (ii) decision time is higher when the likelihood of choosing each alternative is closer to fifty percent; and (iii) the ultimate quality of the chosen alternative may increase or decrease with decision time, depending on whether earlier or later signals are more informative. We test these predictions in the editorial setting, where we observe proxies for paper quality and signals available to editors. We document that (i) the probability of a positive decision rises with decision time; (ii) average decision time is higher when our estimated probability of a positive decision is closer to fifty percent; and (iii) paper quality is positively (negatively) related to decision time for papers with Reject (R&R) decisions. Structural estimates show that the additional information acquired in editorial delays is modest, and has little impact on the quality of decisions.

Abstract

Programs：PR；JEL：J24, L25, O50

Abstract

Business environments dominated by information flows and autonomous tasks, typical of knowledge-intensive industries, are likely to require enough social capital to be viable and productive. In this paper, we use new EUKLEMS-INTANProd industry-level data (Bontadini et al., 2023a) covering a panel of 19 countries and 20 industries over the 1995-2018 period to investigate the influence of a key element of social capital – trust – on labour productivity in intangible-intensive industries, controlling for hiring and firing regulations that can constrain the ability of managers to implement best practices productively. Productivity gains from relatively high levels of trust in knowledge-rich environments are estimated to be sizeable and our estimates survive a number of robustness checks.

Abstract

Monetary Policy Along the Yield Curve: Why Can Central Banks Affect Long-Term Real Rates?

沿收益率曲线的货币政策：为什么中央银行能够影响长期实际利率？

Paul Beaudry, Paolo Cavallino, Tim Willems

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E21, E43, E52, G51

Abstract

Evidence suggests that monetary policy can affect long-term real interest rates, but it is not clear what drives this outcome. We argue this occurs because very persistent policy-induced interest rate changes may have only weak effects on activity. This can arise when consumption-savings decisions are not primarily driven by intertemporal substitution, but also by life-cycle forces associated with retirement. Within such an environment, we show that the impact of highly persistent monetary policy shocks on activity is determined by two forces: an asset valuation effect, and the response of the average marginal propensity to consume out of financial wealth. Our quantitative analysis indicates that these forces likely cancel each other out, allowing monetary policy to (unconsciously) drive trends in long-run real rates. Our findings also imply that very precise knowledge of r* might not be essential to the successful conduct of monetary policy.

Programs：CH, ED, LS, PE；JEL：I21, J24

Abstract

High-dosage tutoring is an effective way to improve student learning (Nickow et al., 2024; Guryan et al., 2023). Finding ways to deliver high-dosage tutoring at large scale remains a challenge. Two primary challenges to scaling are cost and staffing. One possible solution is to reduce costs by substituting some tutor time with computer-assisted learning (CAL) technology. The question is: Does doing so compromise effectiveness? This paper provides evidence from a randomized controlled trial (RCT) of approximately 4,000 students in two large school districts in 2018- 19 and 2019-20. Abstract

Reducing gender-specific commuting barriers in developing countries can have heterogeneous effects on women’s labor outcomes.
