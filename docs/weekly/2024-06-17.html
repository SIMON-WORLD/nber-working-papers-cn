Judging the Judges: Indexing of Complex Information Reduces Injustice

评判法官：复杂信息的索引化减少不公正

Andrew Caplin, Andrei Gomberg, Joyce Sadka

Programs：LE；JEL：D8

Abstract

We demonstrate that cognitive constraints produce injustice in its most paradigmatic form: incorrect verdicts in judicial trials. Responding to a request for help, we conducted a field study in Mexican labor courts in which time pressure on the judges leads to their making mistakes, many of which are appealed. All we did was to index pages to random case files to help judges quickly find relevant information. We made no other changes whatsoever. Our treatment greatly reduced successful appeals, fully 50% in complex cases, and resulted in judicial opinions that were shorter and more on point. These findings have already informed recent reforms in Mexico's labor court procedures and have broader applicability.

中文摘要

我们证明认知约束会导致其最典型形式的不公正：司法审判中的错误判决。应请求提供帮助，我们在墨西哥劳动法院进行了一项实地研究，其中法官面临的时间压力导致他们犯错，其中许多错误被上诉。我们所做的只是将随机案卷的页面编入索引，以帮助法官快速找到相关信息。我们没有进行任何其他更改。我们的处理大大减少了成功上诉，在复杂案件中减少了整整50%，并使得司法意见更简短、更切题。这些发现已为墨西哥劳动法院程序近期的改革提供了信息，并具有更广泛的适用性。

No. 2NBER w32586

Market Design in Regulated Health Insurance Markets: Risk Adjustment vs. Subsidies

监管健康保险市场中的市场设计：风险调整与补贴

Liran Einav, Amy Finkelstein, Pietro Tebaldi

Programs：EH, IO, PE；JEL：G22, G28, H51, I13

Abstract

Health insurance is increasingly provided through managed competition, in which subsidies for consumers and risk adjustment for insurers are key market design instruments. We illustrate theoretically that, in markets with adverse selection, subsidies provide greater flexibility in tailoring premiums to heterogeneous buyers, and produce equilibria with lower markups and greater enrollment. We assess these effects quantitatively using estimates from the California ACA marketplace.

中文摘要

健康保险越来越多地通过管理竞争来提供，其中对消费者的补贴和对保险公司的风险调整是关键的市场设计工具。我们从理论上说明，在存在逆向选择的市场中，补贴在针对异质性买家定制保费方面提供了更大的灵活性，并产生具有更低加成率和更高参保率的均衡。我们利用加州ACA市场的估计来定量评估这些效应。

No. 3NBER w32585

Book-Value Wealth Taxation, Capital Income Taxation, and Innovation

账面财富税、资本所得税与创新

Fatih Guvenen, Gueorgui Kambourov, Burhan Kuruscu, Sergio Ocampo-Diaz

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, LS, ME, PE, PR；JEL：E21, E25, E60, H21, H24, J31

Abstract

When is a wealth tax preferable to a capital income tax? When is the opposite true? More generally, can capital taxation be structured to improve productivity, incentivize innovation, and ultimately increase welfare? We study these questions theoretically in an infinite-horizon model with entrepreneurs and workers, in which entrepreneurial firms differ in their productivity and are subject to collateral constraints. The stationary equilibrium features heterogeneous returns and misallocation of capital. We show that increasing the wealth tax increases aggregate productivity. The gains result from the “use-it-or-lose-it” effect of wealth taxes when returns are heterogeneous, which causes a reallocation of capital from entrepreneurs with low productivity to those with high productivity. Furthermore, if the capital income tax is adjusted to balance the government's budget, aggregate capital, output, and wages also increase. We then study the welfare maximizing combination of wealth and capital income taxes and show that the optimal mix shifts towards a higher wealth tax and a lower capital income tax as the capital intensity of production increases. For a range of plausible parameter values, the optimal wealth tax is positive, whereas the capital income tax can be positive or negative (a subsidy). We then endogenize the entrepreneurial productivity distribution by introducing either ex ante innovation or entrepreneurial effort in production and show that this strengthens our results: by allowing entrepreneurs to keep more of the upside relative to a capital income tax, a wealth tax incentivizes more innovation and entrepreneurial effort, leading to larger increases in productivity, output, and welfare.

中文摘要

何时财富税优于资本所得税？何时相反？更一般地说，资本税能否被设计成提高生产率、激励创新并最终增加福利？我们在一个包含企业家和工人的无限期界模型中从理论上研究这些问题，其中企业家企业具有不同的生产率并面临抵押约束。稳态均衡具有异质性回报和资本错配的特征。我们表明，提高财富税会提高总体生产率。收益源于当回报异质时财富税的“要么使用，要么失去”效应，这导致资本从低生产率企业家重新配置到高生产率企业家。此外，如果调整资本所得税以平衡政府预算，总资本、产出和工资也会增加。然后，我们研究财富税和资本所得税的福利最大化组合，并表明随着生产资本密集度的提高，最优组合会转向更高的财富税和更低的资本所得税。在一系列合理的参数值下，最优财富税为正，而资本所得税可以为正或负（补贴）。然后，我们通过引入事前创新或生产中的企业家努力来内生化企业家生产率分布，并表明这强化了我们的结果：通过允许企业家相对于资本所得税保留更多上行收益，财富税激励了更多创新和企业家努力，导致生产率、产出和福利的更大增长。

No. 4NBER w32584

Extraordinary Labor Market Developments and the 2022-23 Disinflation

非凡的劳动力市场发展与2022-23年的去通胀

Steven J. Davis

Programs：EFG, LS, ME；JEL：E31, E50, J30

Abstract

Two extraordinary U.S. labor market developments facilitated the sharp disinflation in 2022-23 without raising the unemployment rate. First, pandemic-driven infection worries and social distancing intentions caused a sizable drag on labor force participation that began to reverse in the first quarter of 2022, and perhaps earlier. As the reversal unfolded, it raised labor supply and reduced wage growth. Second, the pandemic-instigated shift to work from home (WFH) raised the amenity value of employment in many jobs and for many workers. This development lowered wage-growth pressures along the transition path to a new equilibrium with pay packages that recognized higher remote work levels and their benefits to workers. Surveys of business executives imply that the shift to WFH lowered average wage growth by two percentage points from spring 2021 to spring 2023. A direct inspection finds that average real wage growth from 2021 Q1 to 2024 Q1 in the U.S. economy was at least 3.5 to 4.4 ppts below the path suggested by pre-pandemic experience. This large shortfall in real wage growth aligns well with the interpretation of the 2022-23 disinflation offered here.

中文摘要

美国劳动力市场的两项非凡发展促进了2022-23年期间在不提高失业率的情况下实现急剧去通胀。首先，疫情引发的感染担忧和社交距离意愿对劳动力参与率造成了显著拖累，这种拖累从2022年第一季度（或许更早）开始逆转。随着逆转的展开，劳动力供给增加，工资增长放缓。其次，疫情引发的居家办公转变提高了许多工作岗位和许多工人的就业舒适度价值。这一发展在向新均衡的过渡路径上降低了工资增长压力，新均衡的薪酬方案承认了更高的远程工作水平及其对工人的益处。对商业高管的调查表明，从2021年春季到2023年春季，向居家办公的转变使平均工资增长率降低了两个百分点。直接检查发现，从2021年第一季度到2024年第一季度，美国经济中的平均实际工资增长至少比疫情前经验所暗示的路径低3.5至4.4个百分点。实际工资增长的这一巨大缺口与本文对2022-23年去通胀的解释高度吻合。

No. 5NBER w32583

Does the EITC Reduce Caregiving for Parents?

劳动所得税抵免是否会减少对父母的照料？

Katherine Michelmore, Anna Wiersma Strauss, Emily E. Wiemers

Programs：AG, CH, PE；JEL：H24, I38, J22

Abstract

Families provide substantial care to older adults with functional limitations. Policies that incentivize work have the potential to reduce this valuable care. This study uses the Health and Retirement Study (HRS) and a simulated benefit approach to examine the consequences of increases in the generosity of the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC), a work-contingent cash benefit, for the care that parents receive from their EITC-eligible daughters. We find that a $500 increase in EITC generosity reduces the care that parents age 65+ with functional limitations receive by 13-24 hours per month, and that this is due to a reduction in the likelihood of receiving intensive care (40+ hours per month). We assess the full effect of this reduction in caregiving by examining whether financial transfers increase as a substitute for care, whether other children or spouses fill the care gap, and whether paid caregiving increases in response to declines in family care. We find no evidence of increased financial transfers and that care gaps remain for older parents that are not filled by other children, spouses, or paid care providers. We conclude that an unintended consequence of the EITC is that the older parents of EITC recipients receive less care overall in response to increased EITC benefit generosity.

中文摘要

家庭为有功能限制的老年人提供了大量照料。激励工作的政策有可能减少这种宝贵的照料。本研究使用健康与退休研究数据和模拟工具方法，考察劳动所得税抵免（一种与工作挂钩的现金福利）慷慨程度的提高，对符合EITC条件的女儿所获得的父母照料的影响。我们发现，EITC慷慨程度的提高减少了父母从符合EITC条件的女儿那里获得的照料，尤其是对年长父母和有功能限制的父母而言。为了评估这种照料减少的全面影响，我们考察了财务转移是否作为减少照料的替代而增加，其他成年子女是否填补了符合EITC条件的女儿留下的照料缺口，以及付费照料是否因家庭照料的减少而增加。我们没有发现财务转移增加的证据，并且年长父母的照料缺口仍然存在，未被其他子女或付费照料提供者填补。我们得出结论，EITC的一个意外后果是，随着EITC福利慷慨程度的提高，EITC领取者的年长父母总体上从子女那里获得的照料减少了。

No. 6NBER w32582

Rent Guarantee Insurance

租金担保保险

Boaz Abramson, Stijn Van Nieuwerburgh

Programs：AP, CF, EFG, PE；JEL：E20, E62, G11, G18, G22, G5, R21, R38

Abstract

A rent guarantee insurance (RGI) policy makes a limited number of rent payments to the landlord on behalf of an insured tenant unable to pay rent due to a negative income or health expenditure shock. We introduce RGI in a rich quantitative equilibrium model of housing insecurity and show it increases welfare by improving risk sharing across idiosyncratic and aggregate states of the world, reducing the need for a large security deposits, and reducing homelessness which imposes large costs on society. While unrestricted access is not financially viable with either private or public insurance providers due to moral hazard and adverse selection, restricting access can restore viability. Private insurers must target better off renters to break even, while public insurers focus on households most at-risk of homelessness.

中文摘要

租金担保保险单代表无法支付租金的受保租户（因负面收入或健康支出冲击）向房东支付有限次数的租金。我们在一个丰富的住房不安全定量均衡模型中引入RGI，并表明它通过改善跨特定和总体状态的世界的风险分担、减少对大量保证金的需求以及减少给社会带来巨大成本的无家可归现象，从而提高了福利。虽然由于道德风险和逆向选择，无论是私人还是公共保险提供者，无限制的准入在财务上均不可行，但限制准入可以恢复可行性。私人保险公司必须瞄准较富裕的租户才能实现盈亏平衡，而公共保险公司则专注于最有可能无家可归的家庭。

No. 7NBER w32581

Automating Short-Term Payroll Savings: Evidence from Four Large U.K. Experiments

自动化短期工资储蓄：来自英国四项大型实验的证据

Sarah Holmes Berk, James J. Choi, Jay Garg, John Beshears, David Laibson

Programs：AG；JEL：D14, G51

Abstract

Automatic enrollment is often used to increase retirement savings. What are the effects of using it (or, alternatively, requiring an active enrollment choice) to increase short-term savings? We evaluate two experiments in the U.K. at employers that enable workers to set up payroll contributions to fund short-term savings accounts. In the first experiment (N = 7,404), employees at two firms were randomly assigned opt-in, opt-out, or active choice enrollment into the short-term savings program. Nine months later, participation was 48 percentage points higher under automatic enrollment than opt-in enrollment, and average balances were £114 higher. In the second experiment (N = 3,605), after years of offering opt-in payroll contributions to fund a short-term savings account, the employer changed to opt-out enrollment for new hires only. In tenure month 18, participation in the short-term savings program was 48 percentage points higher under automatic enrollment, and average balances were £193 higher.

中文摘要

自动注册常被用于增加退休储蓄。使用它（或者，替代地，要求主动注册选择）来增加短期储蓄的效果如何？我们评估了英国雇主的两项实验，这些实验使工人能够设立工资缴款来资助短期储蓄账户。在第一项实验（N = 7,404）中，两家公司的员工被随机分配到短期储蓄计划的默认加入、默认退出或主动选择注册。九个月后，自动注册下的参与率比默认加入注册高出48个百分点，平均余额高出114英镑。在第二项实验（N = 3,605）中，在提供多年默认加入工资缴款以资助短期储蓄账户后，雇主改为仅对新员工实行默认退出注册。在任职第18个月时，自动注册下短期储蓄计划的参与率高出48个百分点，平均余额高出193英镑。

No. 8NBER w32580

The Impact of Price Transparency in Outpatient Provider Markets

门诊服务市场价格透明化的影响

Kayleigh Barnes, Sherry A. Glied, Benjamin R. Handel, Grace Kim

Programs：EH；JEL：I11, L1

Abstract

Medical provider price transparency is often touted as a way to lower health care spending. But the impact of price transparency is theoretically ambiguous: it could lower health care spending via increased consumer price shopping or improved insurer bargaining but could also raise health care prices via improved provider bargaining or provider collusion. We conduct a randomized-controlled trial to examine the impact of a state-wide medical charge transparency tool in outpatient provider markets in New York State. In the experiment, individual providers’ billed charges (list prices) were released randomly at the level of the procedure and three-digit zipcode. We use a comprehensive commercial claims database to assess the impact of this intervention and find that it leads to a small increase in overall billed charges (+0.75%). This effect is concentrated among low-priced providers in markets with low out-of-network spending, suggesting that the transparency tool improves provider pricing information. We find no evidence of quantity effects. Results do not vary consistently across specialty groups, market concentration, frequency of service use, or frequency of website use. These results are consistent with price transparency having a minimal effect on consumer shopping while slightly improving provider information about competitors’ charges.

中文摘要

医疗服务价格透明化常被宣传为降低医疗保健支出的方式。但价格透明化的影响在理论上是不确定的：它可能通过增加消费者价格比较或改善保险公司议价来降低医疗保健支出，但也可能通过改善医疗服务提供者议价或提供者合谋来提高医疗价格。我们进行了一项随机对照试验，以考察纽约州门诊服务市场中全州医疗收费透明工具的影响。在实验中，个体提供者的收费价格（标价）在程序和三位邮政编码层面被随机公布。我们使用一个全面的商业索赔数据库来评估这一干预的影响，发现它导致总体收费价格小幅上涨（+0.75%）。这一效应集中在低价格提供者和网络外支出较低的市场，表明透明工具改善了提供者的定价信息。我们没有发现数量效应的证据。结果在不同专业组、市场集中度、服务使用频率或网站使用频率之间并不一致。这些结果与价格透明化对消费者购物影响极小，同时略微改善提供者关于竞争对手收费价格信息的观点一致。

No. 9NBER w32579

Property Insurance and Disaster Risk: New Evidence from Mortgage Escrow Data

财产保险与灾害风险：来自抵押贷款托管账户数据的新证据

Benjamin J. Keys, Philip Mulder

Programs：EEE, PE；JEL：G21, G22, G52, Q54, R31

Abstract

We develop a new dataset to study homeowners insurance using over 74 million premiums from 2014–2024 inferred from mortgage escrow payments. We document rapidly rising premiums and a doubling of the pass-through from disaster risk into premiums. Using variation in correlated wildfire and hurricane exposure, we show that the increase in the risk-to-premium gradient was accelerated by a repricing of catastrophic risk in global capital markets. Premium increases are capitalized into home values, reducing home price growth by over $40,000 in the most exposed zipcodes. The premium and home price effects are larger in areas facing rising climate risk.

中文摘要

我们利用从2014年至2024年从抵押贷款托管账户支付中推断出的超过7400万笔保费，开发了一个新的数据集来研究房主保险。我们记录了保费的快速上涨以及灾害风险向保费传导率的翻倍。利用相关野火和飓风暴露的差异，我们表明风险-保费梯度的增加因全球资本市场对巨灾风险的重新定价而加速。保费上涨被资本化到房屋价值中，使风险最高的邮政编码区的房价增长减少了超过40,000美元。保费和房价效应在面临日益增长的气候风险的地区更大。

No. 10NBER w32578

Fertility Beliefs and Outcomes: The Role of Relationship Status and Attractiveness

生育信念与结果：关系状态和吸引力的作用

Yifan Gong, Tyler Skura, Ralph Stinebrickner, Todd R. Stinebrickner

Programs：CH, ED, LS；JEL：J0, J12, J13

Abstract

Unique data from the Berea Panel Study provides new evidence about fertility outcomes before age 30 and beliefs about these outcomes elicited soon after college graduation. Comparing outcomes and beliefs yields a measure of belief accuracy. Individuals who are unmarried and not in relationships at age 24 are extremely optimistic about the probability of having children, while married individuals have very accurate beliefs. Novel attractiveness measures are central for understanding fertility beliefs and outcomes for females but not for males. Marriage is a mechanism that is relevant for understanding differences in beliefs, outcomes, and misperceptions across relationship and attractiveness groups.

中文摘要

来自伯里亚小组研究的独特数据提供了关于30岁前生育结果以及大学毕业后不久对这些结果的信念的新证据。比较结果和信念得出了信念准确性的衡量标准。24岁时未婚且未处于恋爱关系中的个体对生育子女的概率极为乐观，而已婚个体的信念非常准确。新颖的吸引力衡量标准对于理解女性的生育信念和结果至关重要，但对男性则不然。婚姻是一个机制，对于理解不同关系和吸引力群体在信念、结果和误解方面的差异具有相关性。

No. 11NBER w32577

The Chronic Disease Index: Analyzing Health Inequalities Over the Lifecycle

慢性病指数：分析生命周期中的健康不平等

Kaveh Danesh, Jonathan T. Kolstad, William D. Parker, Johannes Spinnewijn

Programs：AG, CH, EH, PE；JEL：I1, I12, I14

Abstract

The rich live longer than the poor, but relatively little is known about the evolution of health inequality across the lifecycle. Using rich administrative data from the Netherlands, we develop an index of chronic disease burden based on the projected contribution to old-age mortality. Chronic conditions account for one-third of the mortality gap in old age. Using our index we demonstrate that health inequality arises much earlier in life; by age 35, the bottom half of the income distribution has the same disease burden as those age 50 in the top half. Approximately 60% of the divergence across income groups is due to low-income individuals developing chronic illness at a faster rate, rather than chronically ill individuals sorting into lower-income groups. Using linked health survey data, we then examine the contributions of various mediators to the incidence of chronic diseases over the life cycle. Socioeconomic and geographic factors explain most of the variation, while individual health behaviors play a moderate role. Our findings align with calls to target health policies toward early-life social determinants of health.
