Diversity and Discrimination in the Classroom

课堂中的多样性与歧视

Dan Anderberg, Gordon B. Dahl, Cristina Felfe, Helmut Rainer, Thomas Siedler

Programs：CH, LS, PE；JEL：J15

Abstract

What makes diversity unifying in some settings but divisive in others? We examine how the mixing of ethnic groups in German schools affects intergroup cooperation and trust. We leverage the quasi-random assignment of students to classrooms within schools to obtain variation in the type of diversity that prevails in a peer group. We combine this with a large-scale, incentivized lab-in-field-experiment based on the investment game, allowing us to assess the in-group bias of native German students in their interactions with fellow natives (in-group) versus immigrants (out-group). We find in-group bias peaks in culturally polarized classrooms, where the native and immigrant groups are both large, but have different religious or language backgrounds. In contrast, in classrooms characterized by non-cultural polarization, fractionalization, or a native supermajority, there are significantly lower levels of own-group favoritism. In terms of mechanisms, we find empirical evidence that culturally polarized classrooms foster negative stereotypes about immigrants' trustworthiness and amplify taste-based discrimination, both of which are costly and lead to lower payouts. In contrast, accurate statistical discrimination is ruled out by design in our experiment. These findings suggest that extra efforts are needed to counteract low levels of inclusivity and trust in culturally polarized environments.

中文摘要

什么因素使得多样性在某些环境中促进团结，而在另一些环境中导致分裂？我们研究了德国学校中不同族裔群体的混合如何影响群体间的合作与信任。我们利用学生被准随机分配到校内不同班级的情况，来获得同伴群体中主导的多样性类型的变化。我们结合基于投资游戏的大规模、有激励的实地实验室实验，评估了德国本土学生在与本土同胞（内群体）和移民（外群体）互动时的内群体偏见。我们发现，在文化两极分化的班级中，内群体偏见达到顶峰——这些班级中本土群体和移民群体规模都很大，但具有不同的宗教或语言背景。相比之下，在非文化两极分化、碎片化或本土群体占绝对多数的班级中，对自己群体的偏袒程度显著较低。在机制方面，我们找到经验证据表明，文化两极分化的班级会强化对移民可信赖性的负面刻板印象，并放大基于偏好的歧视，这两者都会带来成本并导致更低的收益。相反，在我们的实验中，准确的统计歧视被设计排除在外。这些发现表明，在文化两极分化的环境中，需要付出额外努力来抵消低水平的包容性和信任。

No. 2NBER w32176

The Secular Decline of Bank Balance Sheet Lending

银行资产负债表贷款的长期下降

Greg Buchak, Gregor Matvos, Tomasz Piskorski, Amit Seru

Programs：CF, EFG, IO, ME；JEL：E50, G2, G20, G21, G22, G23, G24, G28, G29, L50

Abstract

This paper examines the decline of the bank balance sheet model of financial intermediation since 1970 and its regulatory implications. The share of private lending on bank balance sheets fell from 55% in the 1970s to 33% in 2023, while the deposit share of savings dropped from 21% to 13%. Loans as a percentage of bank assets also declined from 70% to 55%. We develop a model that captures the interaction between bank balance sheets and originate-to-distribute (OTD) intermediation through securities holdings. Our analysis identifies three key factors driving these trends: a shift in borrower demand toward informationally insensitive lending, a shift in saver demand away from deposits, and evolving regulations affecting bank balance sheets. Regulatory changes and technological advancements primarily drive these shifts. Borrower demand plays a dominant role in reducing balance sheet lending, particularly from the 1970s to the 1990s. Saver demand shifts away from deposits have reduced bank balance sheets but had a smaller impact on overall lending. Bank regulation, especially since the financial crisis, has altered bank balance sheet composition. Simulations of increased capital requirements show that while balance sheets contract in both the 1960s and 2020s, the impact on overall lending is smaller today due to the substitution of bank loans with debt securities. These results suggest the financial sector has become more resilient to regulatory changes, with the decline in bank balance sheet intermediation reshaping the trade-offs faced by macroprudential policies and financial regulation.

中文摘要

本文考察了自1970年以来金融中介的银行资产负债表模型的下降及其监管影响。银行资产负债表上的私人贷款份额从1970年代的55%下降到2023年的33%，而储蓄中的存款份额从21%下降到13%。贷款占银行资产的比例也从70%下降到55%。我们构建了一个模型，捕捉银行资产负债表与通过证券持有进行的“发起-分销”中介之间的相互作用。我们的分析确定了推动这些趋势的三个关键因素：借款人需求向信息不敏感贷款的转变、储蓄者需求从存款的转移，以及影响银行资产负债表的监管演变。监管变化和技术进步主要驱动了这些转变。借款人需求在减少资产负债表贷款中起主导作用，尤其是在1970年代至1990年代。储蓄者需求从存款转移减少了银行资产负债表，但对整体贷款的影响较小。银行监管，尤其是自金融危机以来，改变了银行资产负债表的构成。对提高资本要求的模拟显示，尽管在1960年代和2020年代资产负债表都会收缩，但由于银行贷款被债务证券替代，如今对整体贷款的影响更小。这些结果表明，金融部门对监管变化的韧性增强，银行资产负债表中介的下降重塑了宏观审慎政策和金融监管所面临的权衡。

From Dominant to Producer Currency Pricing: Dynamics of Chilean Exports

从主导货币定价到生产者货币定价：智利出口的动态变化

José De Gregorio, Pablo García, Emiliano E. Luttini, Marco Rojas

Programs：IFM；JEL：F14, F31, F41

Abstract

We revisit a central question for international macroeconomics: the response of export prices and quantities to movements in the exchange rate (ER). We use granular export data for Chile and study how the effects of ER movements vary over time with the currency of invoicing and the destination of exports. For prices, we find that the short-run effects of bilateral ER movements vanish when controlling for U.S. dollar ER, which supports dominant currency pricing. However, over longer horizons a more significant role is played by bilateral ER movements, in line with the predictions of producer currency pricing. These dynamics do not depend on the invoicing currency. The results we find for quantities support the view that bilateral exchange rate movements contribute to macroeconomic adjustment through export volumes over the medium term.

中文摘要

我们重新审视了国际宏观经济学的核心问题：出口价格和数量对汇率变动的反应。我们使用智利的微观出口数据，研究汇率变动的影响如何随计价货币和出口目的地随时间变化。对于价格，我们发现，在控制美元汇率后，双边汇率变动的短期效应消失，这支持了主导货币定价。然而，在更长的时期内，双边汇率变动发挥了更显著的作用，这与生产者货币定价的预测一致。这些动态变化不依赖于计价货币。我们关于数量的结果支持了以下观点：双边汇率变动通过出口数量在中期内促进宏观经济调整。

No. 4NBER w32174

Sectoral Debt and Global Dollar Cycles in Developing Economies

发展中经济体的部门债务与全球美元周期

Bada Han, Rashad Ahmed, Joshua Aizenman, Yothin Jinjarak

Programs：IFM；JEL：F34, F41, F44, F62, G51

Abstract

We explore the role of sectoral debt dynamics in shaping business cycles in a sample of 52 Emerging Market Economies (EMEs) and Frontier Market Economies (FMEs) from 2005 to 2021. Higher household debt levels and growth are associated with significantly slower GDP growth in more developed EMEs but not in less developed EMEs and FMEs. We also examine the relationship between US dollar cycles, sectoral debt levels and growth, and economic activity. Among developed EMEs, higher expected household debt growth magnifies the impact of US dollar fluctuations on economic activity, with significant but less persistent effects on consumption and more persistent effects on investment. Our empirical findings highlight the important role of household debt dynamics in relatively developed EMEs.

中文摘要

我们利用2005年至2021年52个新兴市场经济体和前沿市场经济体的样本，探讨了部门债务动态在塑造经济周期中的作用。在较发达的新兴市场经济体中，较高的家庭债务水平和增长与显著较慢的GDP增长相关，但在较不发达的新兴市场经济体和前沿市场经济体中则不然。我们还考察了美元周期、部门债务水平和增长与经济活动之间的关系。在发达的新兴市场经济体中，较高的预期家庭债务增长放大了美元波动对经济活动的影响，对消费有显著但较不持久的影响，对投资则有更持久的影响。我们的实证发现强调了家庭债务动态在相对发达的新兴市场经济体中的重要作用。

No. 5NBER w32173

The value of forecasts: Experimental evidence from India

预测的价值：来自印度的实验证据

Fiona Burlig, Amir Jina, Erin M. Kelley, Gregory V. Lane, Harshil Sahai

Programs：DEV, EEE；JEL：D81, O12, O13, Q12, Q54

Abstract

Unpredictable weather makes it challenging for households and firms to make optimal investment decisions. Technologies that reduce these risks are crucial for climate change adaptation. We experimentally evaluate one such technology—an accurate, localized, early monsoon onset forecast—in India, randomizing 250 villages into control, forecast, or insurance. The forecast changes farmers' beliefs about the coming growing season. In response, forecast farmers adapt their behavior along multiple margins, changing cultivated area, crop choice, farm input expenditure, and off-farm business. By enabling farmers to tailor their investments to coming weather, forecasts reduce farmers' risk exposure and increase aggregate welfare.

中文摘要

不可预测的天气使得家庭和企业难以做出最优投资决策。降低这些风险的技术对于气候变化适应至关重要。我们通过实验评估了这样一种技术——在印度提供准确、本地化的季风早期预报——随机将250个村庄分为对照组、预报组或保险组。预报改变了农民对即将到来的生长季节的信念。作为回应，获得预报的农民在多个方面调整了行为，改变了耕种面积、作物选择、农业投入支出和非农业务。通过使农民能够根据即将到来的天气调整投资，预报降低了农民的风险暴露并增加了总体福利。

No. 6NBER w32172

The Slanted-L Phillips Curve

倾斜L型菲利普斯曲线

Pierpaolo Benigno, Gauti B. Eggertsson

Programs：ME；JEL：E1, E10, E3, E30, E4

Abstract

A slanted-L curve is well-suited to represent the non-linearity of the celebrated Phillips curve. We show this using cross-country data of major industrialized economies since 2009, including the inflationary surge of the 2020s. At high unemployment rates, an increase in demand reduces unemployment without creating strong inflationary pressures. Meanwhile, supply shocks have a muted effect. At sufficiently low unemployment, there is a labor shortage, so that the economy is at full capacity. Then, higher demand is inflationary, and supply shocks are amplified. We derive a model of a slanted-L curve.

中文摘要

倾斜L型曲线非常适合表示著名的菲利普斯曲线的非线性。我们使用自2009年以来主要工业化经济体的跨国数据（包括2020年代的通货膨胀激增）来证明这一点。在高失业率时，需求增加会降低失业率，而不会产生强大的通胀压力。同时，供给冲击的影响被削弱。在失业率足够低时，存在劳动力短缺，经济处于满负荷状态。此时，更高的需求会引发通胀，而供给冲击会被放大。我们推导了一个倾斜L型曲线的模型。

No. 7NBER w32171

Who Gets the Job? The Consequences of Strategic Information Sharing within Social Networks

谁得到工作？社交网络中战略信息共享的后果

Gaurav Chiplunkar, Erin M. Kelley, Gregory V. Lane

Programs：DEV, LS；JEL：L14, M51, O12

Abstract

We study how job-seekers share information about jobs within their social network, and its implications for the quality of applicants and hires. We randomly increase the amount of competition for a job that we created and find that jobseekers are less likely to share information about the job, especially with their high ability peers. This lowers the quality of applicants, hires, and performance on the job — suggesting that disseminating job information through social networks may result in lower quality applicants than expected for competitive positions. While randomly offering higher wages attracts better talent, it is not able to fully overcome these strategic disincentives.

中文摘要

我们研究了求职者如何在其社交网络中分享关于工作的信息，以及这对申请人和被录用者质量的影响。我们随机增加了我们创建的一个职位的竞争程度，发现求职者分享该职位信息的可能性降低，尤其是与高能力同伴分享的可能性降低。这降低了申请人、被录用者的质量以及工作表现——这表明通过社交网络传播职位信息可能导致竞争性职位的申请人质量低于预期。虽然随机提供更高的工资能吸引更优秀的人才，但这无法完全克服这些战略性的抑制因素。

Do Information Frictions and Corruption Perceptions Kill Competition? A Field Experiment On Public Procurement in Uganda

信息摩擦和腐败认知会扼杀竞争吗？乌干达公共采购的实地实验

Emanuele Colonnelli, Francesco Loiacono, Edwin Muhumuza, Edoardo Teso

Programs：CF, DEV, POL；JEL：G3, H57, O12, P0

Abstract

We study whether information frictions and corruption perceptions deter firms from doing business with the government. We conduct two nationwide randomized controlled trials (RCTs) in collaboration with the national public procurement supervisory and anti-corruption agency in Uganda. The first RCT aims to increase firms’ information on available procurement opportunities, which is limited due to the lack of a centralized e- procurement system. We provide firms with direct and timely access to information about government tenders over a two-year period. The second RCT focuses on firms’ perceptions about the integrity of public entities, which we experimentally show are key drivers of firms’ participation in procurement. We provide firms with access to structured information on other firms’ perceptions and on anti-corruption audits. We find that increasing information on available procurement opportunities alone does not increase firm participation in public procurement. However, changing firms’ perceptions about the integrity of public entities increases firms’ total number of bids and total government contracts won. Based on our findings, our partner agency implemented several measures to further restore firms’ trust in public entities. Overall, our findings point to the limits of transparency reforms that aim to increase competition in public procurement without accounting for firms’ perceptions about government corruption and inefficiency.

中文摘要

我们研究了信息摩擦和腐败认知是否阻碍企业与政府开展业务。我们与乌干达国家公共采购监督和反腐败机构合作，进行了两项全国性的随机对照试验。第一项随机对照试验旨在增加企业关于可用采购机会的信息——由于缺乏集中的电子采购系统，这些信息是有限的。我们在两年内为企业提供关于政府招标的直接和及时信息。第二项随机对照试验聚焦于企业对公共实体诚信度的认知——我们通过实验证明，这些认知是企业参与采购的关键驱动因素。我们为企业提供关于其他企业认知和反腐败审计的结构化信息。我们发现，仅增加关于可用采购机会的信息并不会提高企业参与公共采购的程度。然而，改变企业对公共实体诚信度的认知会增加企业的总投标次数和获得的总政府合同数量。基于我们的发现，我们的合作机构实施了多项措施以进一步恢复企业对公共实体的信任。总体而言，我们的发现指出了透明度改革的局限性——如果不考虑企业对政府腐败和低效率的认知，这些改革旨在增加公共采购中的竞争。

No. 9NBER w32169

Demand Shocks as Technology Shocks

需求冲击作为技术冲击

Yan Bai, José-Víctor Ríos-Rull, Kjetil Storesletten

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：E21, E22, E30

Abstract

We provide a macroeconomic theory where demand for goods has a productive role. A search friction prevents perfect matching between producers and potential customers. Larger demand induces more search, which in turn increases GDP and measured TFP. We embed the product-market friction in a standard neoclassical model and estimate it using Bayesian techniques. Business cycles are driven by preference shocks, true technology shocks, and investment-specific shocks. Preference shocks have qualitatively similar effects as true productivity shocks. These shocks account for a large share of the fluctuations in consumption, GDP, and measured TFP and can be identified using shopping time data.

中文摘要

我们提出了一种宏观经济理论，其中商品需求具有生产性作用。搜索摩擦阻碍了生产者与潜在客户之间的完美匹配。更大的需求会引发更多的搜索，进而提高GDP和测算出的全要素生产率（TFP）。我们将产品市场摩擦嵌入一个标准的新古典主义模型中，并使用贝叶斯方法对其进行估计。经济周期由偏好冲击、真实技术冲击和投资特定冲击驱动。偏好冲击在定性上具有与真实生产率冲击相似的效果。这些冲击解释了消费、GDP和测算出的TFP波动中的很大一部分，并且可以使用购物时间数据进行识别。

No. 10NBER w32168

Climate Policy Reform Options in 2025

2025年气候政策改革方案

John Bistline, Kimberly A. Clausing, Neil Mehrotra, James H. Stock, Catherine Wolfram

Programs：EEE, PE；JEL：H23, Q48, Q54

Abstract

With the expiration of many tax cuts and unmet climate targets, 2025 could be a crucial year for climate policy in the United States. Using an integrated model of energy supply and demand, this paper aims to assess climate policies that the U.S. federal government may consider in 2025 and to evaluate emissions reductions, abatement costs, fiscal impacts, and household energy expenditures across a range of policy scenarios. The model results show several key findings. First, the emissions reductions of the Inflation Reduction Act are significantly augmented under scenarios that add a modest carbon fee or, to a lesser extent, that implement a clean electricity standard in the power sector. Second, net fiscal costs can be substantially reduced in scenarios that include a carbon fee, especially if fossil fuel exports are taxed. Third, expanding the IRA tax credits yields modest additional emissions reductions with higher fiscal costs. Finally, although none of the policy combinations across these scenarios achieve the U.S. target of a 50-52% economy-wide emissions reduction by 2030 from 2005 levels, the carbon fee and clean electricity standard scenarios achieve these levels between 2030 and 2035.

中文摘要

随着许多税收减免政策的到期以及气候目标未能实现，2025年可能成为美国气候政策的关键一年。本文利用一个能源供需综合模型，旨在评估美国联邦政府可能在2025年考虑的气候政策，并在一系列政策情景下评估减排量、减排成本、财政影响以及家庭能源支出。模型结果显示了几个关键发现。首先，在那些增加适度碳费或（在较小程度上）在电力部门实施清洁电力标准的情景下，《通胀削减法案》的减排效果得到显著增强。其次，在包含碳费的情景下，尤其是在对化石燃料出口征税的情况下，净财政成本可以大幅降低。第三，扩大《通胀削减法案》的税收抵免会带来适度的额外减排，但财政成本更高。最后，尽管这些情景中的政策组合均未实现美国到2030年将全经济范围排放量从2005年水平减少50-52%的目标，但碳费和清洁电力标准情景在2030年至2035年间达到了这些水平。

Do Medical Treatments Work for Work? Evidence from Breast Cancer Patients

医学治疗对工作有效吗？来自乳腺癌患者的证据

N. Meltem Daysal, William N. Evans, Mikkel Hasse Pedersen, Mircea Trandafir

Programs：EH, LS；JEL：I10, I14, I18, J20

Abstract

We investigate the effects of radiation therapy on the mortality and economic outcomes of breast cancer patients. We implement a 2SLS strategy within a difference-in-difference framework exploiting variation in treatment stemming from a medical guideline change in Denmark. Using administrative data, we reproduce results from an RCT showing the lifesaving benefits of radiotherapy. We then show therapy also has economic returns: ten years after diagnosis, treatment increases employment by 37% and earnings by 45%. Mortality and economic results are driven by results for more educated women, indicating that equalizing access to treatment may not be sufficient to reduce health inequalities.

中文摘要

我们研究了放射治疗对乳腺癌患者死亡率和经济结果的影响。我们在一个双重差分框架内实施了两阶段最小二乘策略，利用了丹麦一项医疗指南变更所导致的治疗差异。使用行政数据，我们重现了一项随机对照试验的结果，该试验显示了放射治疗的救命益处。然后，我们证明治疗也具有经济回报：诊断十年后，治疗使就业率提高了37%，收入提高了45%。死亡率和经济结果主要由受教育程度更高的女性的结果驱动，这表明平等获得治疗可能不足以减少健康不平等。

On the Effects of Monetary Policy Shocks on Income and Consumption Heterogeneity

论货币政策冲击对收入与消费异质性的影响

Minsu Chang, Frank Schorfheide

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：C11, C32, C52, E32

Abstract

In this paper we use the functional vector autoregression (VAR) framework of Chang, Chen, and Schorfheide (2024) to study the effects of monetary policy shocks (conventional and informational) on the cross-sectional distribution of U.S. earnings (from the Current Population Survey), consumption, and financial income (both from the Consumer Expenditure Survey). We find that a conventional expansionary monetary policy shock reduces earnings inequality, in large part by lifting individuals out of unemployment. There is a weakly positive effect on consumption inequality and no effect on financial income inequality, but credible bands are wide.

中文摘要

Heterogeneity, Uncertainty and Learning: Semiparametric Identification and Estimation

异质性、不确定性与学习：半参数识别与估计

Jackson Bunting, Paul Diegert, Arnaud Maurel

Programs：ED, EH, IO, LS, TWP；JEL：C14, C50, D83

Abstract

We provide semiparametric identification results for a broad class of learning models in which continuous outcomes depend on three types of unobservables: i) known heterogeneity, ii) initially unknown heterogeneity that may be revealed over time, and iii) transitory uncertainty. We consider a common environment where the researcher only has access to a short panel on choices and realized outcomes. We establish identification of the outcome equation parameters and the distribution of the three types of unobservables, under the standard assumption that unknown heterogeneity and uncertainty are normally distributed. We also show that, absent known heterogeneity, the model is identified without making any distributional assumption. We then derive the asymptotic properties of a sieve MLE estimator for the model parameters, and devise a tractable profile likelihood based estimation procedure. Monte Carlo simulation results indicate that our estimator exhibits good finite-sample properties.

中文摘要

我们为一类广泛的学习模型提供了半参数识别结果，在这些模型中，连续结果取决于三类不可观测因素：i) 已知的异质性，ii) 最初未知但可能随时间揭示的异质性，以及iii) 暂时的不确定性。我们考虑一个常见环境，其中研究者只能获得关于选择和已实现结果的短面板数据。我们在标准假设（即未知异质性和不确定性服从正态分布）下，建立了结果方程参数以及三类不可观测因素分布的识别。我们还表明，在缺乏已知异质性的情况下，该模型无需任何分布假设即可被识别。然后，我们推导了模型参数的筛子最大似然估计量的渐近性质，并设计了一个易于处理的基于轮廓似然的估计程序。蒙特卡洛模拟结果表明，我们的估计量具有良好的有限样本性质。

No. 14NBER w32163

The Cost of Money is Part of the Cost of Living: New Evidence on the Consumer Sentiment Anomaly

货币成本是生活成本的一部分：关于消费者情绪异常的新证据

Marijn A. Bolhuis, Judd N. L. Cramer, Karl Oskar Schulz, Lawrence H. Summers

Programs：DAE, EFG, ME；JEL：D14, E30, E31, E43, E52, G51, R31

Abstract

Unemployment is low and inflation is falling, but consumer sentiment remains depressed. This has confounded economists, who historically rely on these two variables to gauge how consumers feel about the economy. We propose that borrowing costs, which have grown at rates they had not reached in decades, do much to explain this gap. The cost of money is not currently included in traditional price indexes, indicating a disconnect between the measures favored by economists and the effective costs borne by consumers. We show that the lows in US consumer sentiment that cannot be explained by unemployment and official inflation are strongly correlated with borrowing costs and consumer credit supply. Concerns over borrowing costs, which have historically tracked the cost of money, are at their highest levels since the Volcker-era. We then develop alternative measures of inflation that include borrowing costs and can account for almost three quarters of the gap in US consumer sentiment in 2023. Global evidence shows that consumer sentiment gaps across countries are also strongly correlated with changes in interest rates. Proposed U.S.-specific factors do not find much supportive evidence abroad.

中文摘要

失业率很低，通胀正在下降，但消费者情绪仍然低迷。这让经济学家感到困惑，因为他们历来依赖这两个变量来衡量消费者对经济的感受。我们提出，借贷成本（其增长速度是几十年来所未见的）在很大程度上解释了这一差距。货币成本目前并未包含在传统的价格指数中，这表明经济学家偏好的衡量指标与消费者实际承担的有效成本之间存在脱节。我们表明，美国消费者情绪中无法用失业率和官方通胀解释的低点，与借贷成本和消费者信贷供给密切相关。对借贷成本的担忧（历史上与货币成本同步变化）正处于自沃尔克时代以来的最高水平。然后，我们制定了包含借贷成本的替代通胀衡量指标，这些指标可以解释2023年美国消费者情绪近四分之三的差距。全球证据表明，各国消费者情绪差距也与利率变化密切相关。提出的美国特定因素在国外并未找到太多支持性证据。
