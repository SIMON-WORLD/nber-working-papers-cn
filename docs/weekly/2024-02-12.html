No. 1NBER w32140

Applying AI to Rebuild Middle Class Jobs

应用人工智能重建中产阶级就业岗位

David Autor

Programs：EFG, LS；JEL：J01, J2, N30, O14

Abstract

While the utopian vision of the current Information Age was that computerization would flatten economic hierarchies by democratizing information, the opposite has occurred. Information, it turns out, is merely an input into a more consequential economic function, decision-making, which is the province of elite experts. The unique opportunity that AI offers to the labor market is to extend the relevance, reach, and value of human expertise. Because of AI’s capacity to weave information and rules with acquired experience to support decision-making, it can be applied to enable a larger set of workers possessing complementary knowledge to perform some of the higher-stakes decision-making tasks that are currently arrogated to elite experts, e.g., medical care to doctors, document production to lawyers, software coding to computer engineers, and undergraduate education to professors. My thesis is not a forecast but an argument about what is possible: AI, if used well, can assist with restoring the middle-skill, middle-class heart of the US labor market that has been hollowed out by automation and globalization.

中文摘要

尽管当前信息时代的乌托邦愿景是计算机化将通过信息民主化来扁平化经济层级，但实际情况恰恰相反。事实证明，信息仅仅是更重要的经济功能——决策——的输入，而决策是精英专家的领域。人工智能为劳动力市场提供的独特机会是扩展人类专业知识的关联性、覆盖范围和价值。由于人工智能能够将信息和规则与获得的经验相结合以支持决策，它可以被用来使更多拥有互补知识的工人能够执行一些目前由精英专家垄断的高风险决策任务，例如医疗保健（医生）、文件制作（律师）、软件编码（计算机工程师）和本科教育（教授）。我的论点不是预测，而是关于可能性的论证：如果运用得当，人工智能可以帮助恢复美国劳动力市场中被自动化和全球化掏空的中等技能、中产阶级核心。

No. 2NBER w32139

Out-group Penalties in Refugee Assistance: A Survey Experiment

难民援助中的外群体惩罚：一项调查实验

Cristina Cattaneo, Daniela Grieco, Nicola Lacetera, Mario Macis

Programs：LS, PE, POL；JEL：C99, D02, D64, J15

Abstract

We study out-group biases in attitudes toward refugees, and the effect of European Union (EU) immigration policies on these views, using an online survey experiment including 4,087 Italian participants. We assess attitudes using donations to a randomly assigned group: Italian victims of violence or refugees fleeing wars in Ukraine or African countries. We also employ a novel measure, the share donated in cash. While donations indicated less support for African and Ukrainian refugees compared to Italian victims, the cash measure revealed a stronger prejudice against distant out-groups, with participants giving African refugees a smaller proportion of cash donations. This result was mainly driven by individuals with right-leaning political views. Providing information about immigration policy reforms that give the EU a more substantial role in receiving and allocating refugees had no impact. Textual analysis supports these findings.

中文摘要

我们通过一项包含4087名意大利参与者的在线调查实验，研究了对难民态度的外群体偏见，以及欧盟移民政策对这些观点的影响。我们通过向随机分配的群体（意大利暴力受害者、逃离乌克兰战争的难民或逃离非洲国家的难民）进行捐赠来评估态度。我们还采用了一种新颖的衡量标准：现金捐赠的比例。虽然捐赠显示对非洲和乌克兰难民的支持少于对意大利受害者的支持，但现金衡量标准揭示了对更遥远外群体的更强偏见，参与者给予非洲难民的现金捐赠比例较小。这一结果主要由持右翼政治观点的个体驱动。提供关于赋予欧盟在接收和分配难民方面更实质性作用的移民政策改革信息没有产生影响。文本分析支持了这些发现。

Tariff Rate Uncertainty and the Structure of Supply Chains

关税税率不确定性与供应链结构

Sebastian Heise, Justin R. Pierce, Georg Schaur, Peter K. Schott

Programs：ITI；JEL：F13, F15

Abstract

We show that reducing the probability of a trade war promotes long-term importer-exporter relationships that ensure provision of high-quality inputs via incentive premia. Empirically, we introduce a method for distinguishing between these Japanese versus spot-market (i.e., American) relationships in customs data, show that their use varies intuitively across trading partners and products, and find that Japanese importing from China increases after a reduction in the possibility of a trade war. Extending the standard general equilibrium trade model to encompass potential trade wars and relational contracts, we estimate that eliminating Japanese procurement reduces welfare about a third as much as moving to autarky.

中文摘要

我们表明，降低贸易战的可能性会促进长期的进口商-出口商关系，这种关系通过激励溢价确保高质量投入品的供应。实证上，我们引入了一种方法，用于在海关数据中区分这些日本式关系与现货市场（即美国式）关系，展示了它们在不同贸易伙伴和产品间的使用符合直觉，并发现日本从中国的进口在贸易战可能性降低后增加。将标准的一般均衡贸易模型扩展到涵盖潜在贸易战和关系合同，我们估计，消除日本式采购会使福利减少约三分之一，其程度相当于走向自给自足。

No. 4NBER w32137

Monetary Policy Wedges and the Long-term Liabilities of Households and Firms

货币政策楔子与家庭和企业的长期负债

Jules H. van Binsbergen, Marco Grotteria

Programs：AP, CF, IFM, ME；JEL：E40, E43, E44, E5, E50, E52, E58, G21, G51

Abstract

We examine the transmission of monetary policy shocks to the long-duration liabilities of households and firms using high-frequency variation in 10-year swap rates around FOMC announcements. We find that four weeks after the announcement mortgage rates move one-for-one with 10-year swap rates, leaving little explanatory power for mortgage concentration, bank market power, or credit risk. Variation in credit risk does materially affect monetary policy transmission to corporate bonds. Expected future short rates and term premia play a significant role in driving both mortgage rates and corporate bond yields, which explains the Federal Reserve’s increased focus on these quantities.

中文摘要

我们利用联邦公开市场委员会公告前后10年期互换利率的高频变动，考察了货币政策冲击向家庭和企业长期负债的传导。我们发现，公告四周后，抵押贷款利率与10年期互换利率一对一变动，抵押贷款集中度、银行市场势力或信用风险几乎没有解释力。信用风险的变化确实对货币政策向公司债券的传导产生实质性影响。预期的未来短期利率和期限溢价在推动抵押贷款利率和公司债券收益率方面都起着重要作用，这解释了美联储对这些变量日益增加的关注。

No. 5NBER w32136

The Government Patent Register: A New Resource for Measuring U.S. Government-Funded Patenting

政府专利登记簿：衡量美国政府资助专利的新资源

Daniel P. Gross, Bhaven N. Sampat

Programs：DAE, PR；JEL：N42, N72, O31, O34, O38

Abstract

We introduce new historical administrative data identifying U.S. government-funded patents since the early twentieth century. In addition to the funding agency, the data report whether the government has title to the patent (“title” patents) or funded a patent assigned to a private organization (“license” patents). The data include a large number of “license” patents that cannot be linked to government funding from patent text or other sources. Combining the historical data with modern administrative sources, we present a public, consolidated data series measuring U.S. government-funded patents—including funding agencies—through 2020, and we provide code to extend this series in the future. We use the data to document long-run patterns in U.S. government-funded patents and federal patent policy, propose ways in which these data can be used in future research, and discuss limitations of the data.

中文摘要

我们引入了新的历史行政数据，用于识别自20世纪初以来美国政府资助的专利。除了资助机构外，这些数据还报告了政府是否拥有专利所有权（“所有权”专利），或者是否资助了分配给私人组织的专利（“许可”专利）。这些数据包含大量无法通过专利文本或其他来源与政府资助关联的“许可”专利。通过将历史数据与现代行政来源相结合，我们提供了一个公开的、整合的数据序列，用于衡量截至2020年的美国政府资助专利（包括资助机构），并提供了未来扩展该序列的代码。我们利用这些数据记录了美国政府资助专利和联邦专利政策的长期模式，提出了这些数据在未来研究中可能的使用方式，并讨论了数据的局限性。

No. 6NBER w32135

Discrete Choice, Complete Markets, and Equilibrium

离散选择、完全市场与均衡

Simon Mongey, Michael E. Waugh

Programs：EFG, IO, ITI, LS；JEL：D5, D52, E2, F1, R13

Abstract

This paper characterizes the allocations that emerge in general equilibrium economies populated by households with preferences of the additive random utility type that make discrete consumption, employment or spatial decisions. We start with a complete markets economy where households can trade claims contingent upon the realizations of their preference shocks. We (i) establish a first and second welfare theorem, (ii) illustrate that in the absence of ex-ante trade, discrete choice economies are generically inefficient, (iii) show that complete markets are not necessary and a much smaller set of securities decentralizes the efficient allocation. We illustrate the relevance of these results in several canonical settings and for measuring how welfare changes in response to changes in prices.

中文摘要

本文刻画了由具有加性随机效用类型偏好的家庭组成的一般均衡经济中出现的配置，这些家庭做出离散的消费、就业或空间决策。我们从家庭可以交易依赖于其偏好冲击实现状态的或有债权的完全市场经济开始。我们（i）建立了第一和第二福利定理，（ii）说明了在缺乏事前交易的情况下，离散选择经济通常是低效的，（iii）表明完全市场并非必要，而一个更小的证券集合就能分散化实现有效配置。我们在几个经典设定中以及衡量福利如何随价格变化而变化时，说明了这些结果的相关性。

No. 7NBER w32134

Behavioral Responses to Wealth Taxation: Evidence from Colombia

对财富税的行为反应：来自哥伦比亚的证据

Juliana Londoño-Vélez, Javier Avila-Mahecha

Programs：DEV, PE；JEL：H24, H26, O23

Abstract

We study behavioral responses to personal wealth taxes in Colombia. We utilize tax microdata from 1993 to 2016 linked with the leaked Panama Papers to investigate offshoring to the country’s key tax havens. We leverage variation from discrete jumps in tax liability and four major reforms to the wealth tax system, including changes in tax rates and duration, using bunching and difference-in-difference techniques. We find compelling evidence that taxpayers instantly reduce the wealth they declare in response to a wealth tax. Moreover, these effects can persist for years even after the wealth tax is no longer in place, providing the first evidence of a hysteresis effect for a temporary tax policy. The response is driven by misreporting items that authorities cannot cross-verify, such as overstating debt and understating non-third-party-reported business assets. Additionally, the wealthiest taxpayers respond to wealth tax increases by hiding assets in hard-to-track entities in tax havens.

中文摘要

我们研究了哥伦比亚个人财富税的行为反应。我们利用1993年至2016年的税收微观数据，并与泄露的巴拿马文件相关联，以调查向该国主要避税天堂的离岸转移。我们利用税收负债的离散跳跃以及财富税体系的四次重大改革（包括税率和持续时间的变化）带来的变化，采用聚束和双重差分技术。我们发现了令人信服的证据，表明纳税人会立即减少因财富税而申报的财富。此外，这些效应即使在财富税不再实施后也可能持续多年，这首次为临时税收政策提供了滞后效应的证据。这种反应是由虚报当局无法交叉验证的项目驱动的，例如夸大债务和低估非第三方报告的企业资产。此外，最富有的纳税人通过将资产隐藏在避税天堂难以追踪的实体中，来应对财富税的增加。

No. 8NBER w32133

What Drives U.S. Import Price Inflation?

是什么推动了美国进口价格通胀？

Mary Amiti, Oleg Itskhoki, David Weinstein

Programs：IFM, ITI；JEL：E31, F14, F42

Abstract

Inflation has risen sharply in many countries since the COVID-19 outbreak. Economists have debated the underlying causes. In this paper, we examine the drivers of the global import price inflation, which peaked at approximately 11 percent a year. We find that a common global component closely tracks movements in aggregate U.S. import prices until late 2022. Afterward, idiosyncratic U.S. demand shocks started to dominate.

中文摘要

自新冠疫情爆发以来，许多国家的通胀率急剧上升。经济学家们一直在争论其根本原因。在本文中，我们研究了全球进口价格通胀的驱动因素，该通胀率峰值约为每年11%。我们发现，直到2022年底，一个共同的全球成分紧密追踪美国进口价格总量的变动。此后，美国特有的需求冲击开始占据主导地位。

No. 9NBER w32132

Making a Song and Dance About It: The Effectiveness of Teaching Children Vocabulary with Animated Music Videos

载歌载舞：用动画音乐视频教儿童词汇的有效性

Ariel Kalil, Susan Mayer, Philip Oreopoulos, Rohen Shah

Programs：CH, ED；JEL：D91, I20, J10, O15

Abstract

Programs that engage young children in movement and song to help them learn are popular but experimental evidence on their impact is sparse. We use an RCT to evaluate the effectiveness of Big Word Club (BWC), a classroom program that uses music and dance videos for 3-5 minutes per day to increase vocabulary. We conducted a field experiment with 818 preschool and kindergarten students in 47 schools in three U.S. states. We find that treated students scored higher on a test of words targeted by the program (0.30 SD) after four months of use and this effect persisted for two months.

中文摘要

让幼儿通过运动和唱歌来学习的项目很受欢迎，但关于其影响的实验证据却很少。我们使用随机对照试验来评估Big Word Club（BWC）的有效性，这是一个课堂项目，每天使用音乐和舞蹈视频3-5分钟来增加词汇量。我们在美国三个州的47所学校对818名学前班和幼儿园学生进行了现场实验。我们发现，经过四个月的使用，接受干预的学生在项目目标词汇测试中得分更高（0.30个标准差），并且这种效果持续了两个月。

No. 10NBER w32131

Populism, Polarization and Green Investment

民粹主义、两极分化与绿色投资

Anders Anderson, David T. Robinson

Programs：CF, PR；JEL：G40, G51, G53, Q54

Abstract

We use detailed, individual-level, panel data to relate growing political polarization to (i) changing beliefs about the pace of global warming and (ii) the propensity to make climate-friendly investment decisions. Individuals in our study were exogenously exposed to forest fires to varying degrees in the summer of 2018. While exposure increased the perceived pace of climate change on average, the response is attenuated in areas experiencing increases in right-wing populism, especially when local environmental media coverage was stronger. Changing beliefs also predict climate-friendly investment, but this is heavily mediated by the same political dynamics.

中文摘要

我们利用详细的个体层面面板数据，将日益加剧的政治两极分化与（i）关于全球变暖速度的信念变化以及（ii）做出气候友好型投资决策的倾向联系起来。我们研究中的个体在2018年夏季不同程度地外生地暴露于森林火灾。虽然平均而言，暴露增加了对气候变化感知速度，但在右翼民粹主义上升的地区，这种反应被削弱，尤其是在当地环境媒体报道较强时。变化的信念也预测了气候友好型投资，但这在很大程度上受到相同政治动态的中介作用。

No. 11NBER w32130

Declining Responsiveness at the Establishment Level: Sources and Productivity Implications

企业层面响应性的下降：来源与生产率影响

Russell Cooper, John C. Haltiwanger, Jonathan Willis

Programs：EFG, PR；JEL：E20, J63, L23, O44

Abstract

This paper studies competing sources of declining dynamism. Evidence shows that an important component of this decline is accounted for by the reduction in the response of employment to shocks in US establishments. Using a plant level dynamic optimization problem as a framework for analysis, four potential reasons for this decline are studied: (i) a change in exogenous processes for profits, (ii) an increase in impatience, (iii) increased market power and (iv) increasing adjustment costs. We identify and quantity the contribution of each of these factors building on a simulated method of moments estimation of our structural model. Our results indicate that the reduction in responsiveness largely reflects increased costs of employment adjustment. Changes in market power, as captured by changes in the curvature of the revenue, function play a minimal role. But, in the presence of rising adjustment costs, measured sales-weighted markups using the recently popular indirect production approach rise substantially, along with rising dispersion and skewness of such measured markups.

中文摘要

本文研究了经济活力下降的竞争性来源。证据表明，这种下降的一个重要组成部分是美国企业对冲击的就业响应减少。使用工厂层面的动态优化问题作为分析框架，研究了这种下降的四个潜在原因：（i）利润外生过程的变化，（ii）不耐烦程度的增加，（iii）市场力量的增强，以及（iv）调整成本的增加。我们基于结构模型的模拟矩估计方法，识别并量化了每个因素的贡献。我们的结果表明，响应性的降低在很大程度上反映了就业调整成本的增加。市场力量的变化（通过收入函数曲率的变化来捕捉）只发挥了微小作用。但是，在调整成本上升的情况下，使用最近流行的间接生产方法衡量的销售加权加成率大幅上升，同时这些衡量指标的离散度和偏度也在增加。

No. 12NBER w32129

Exorbitant Privilege and the Sustainability of US Public Debt

过高特权与美国公共债务的可持续性

Jason Choi, Duong Q. Dang, Rishabh Kirpalani, Diego J. Perez

Programs：AP, EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：F0, F34

Abstract

We study the extent to which the perceived cost of losing the exorbitant privilege the US holds in global safe asset markets sustains the safety of its public debt. Our findings indicate that the loss of this special status in the event of a default significantly augments the debt capacity for the US. Debt levels would be up to 30% lower if the US did not have this special status. Most of this extra debt capacity arises from the loss of the convenience yield on US Treasuries, which makes debt more expensive following its loss and provides strong incentives to repay debt.

中文摘要

我们研究了美国在全球安全资产市场所拥有的过高特权在违约情况下丧失的感知成本，在多大程度上维持了其公共债务的安全性。我们的研究结果表明，在违约事件中失去这种特殊地位显著增加了美国的债务能力。如果美国没有这种特殊地位，债务水平将降低高达30%。这种额外的债务能力大部分源于美国国债便利收益的丧失，这使得债务在丧失后变得更加昂贵，并为偿还债务提供了强有力的激励。

No. 13NBER w32128

The Role of International Financial Integration in Monetary Policy Transmission

国际金融一体化在货币政策传导中的作用

Jing Cynthia Wu, Yinxi Xie, Ji Zhang

Programs：AP, EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E40, E5, F30

Abstract

Motivated by empirical evidence, we propose an open-economy New Keynesian model with financial integration that allows financial intermediaries to hold foreign long-term bonds. We find financial integration features an amplification for a domestic monetary policy shock and a negative spillover for a foreign shock. These results hold for conventional and unconventional monetary policies. Among various aspects of financial integration, the bond duration plays a major role, and our results cannot be replicated by a standard model of perfect risk sharing between households. Finally, we observe an important interaction between financial integration and trade openness, and demonstrate trade alone does not have an economically meaningful impact on monetary policy transmission.

中文摘要

受实证证据的启发，我们提出了一个包含金融一体化的开放经济新凯恩斯模型，该模型允许金融中介持有外国长期债券。我们发现，金融一体化会放大国内货币政策冲击，并对国外冲击产生负向溢出效应。这些结果对常规和非常规货币政策均成立。在金融一体化的各个方面中，债券期限起着主要作用，而我们的结果无法通过家庭间完美风险分担的标准模型复制。最后，我们观察到金融一体化与贸易开放度之间存在重要的相互作用，并证明仅靠贸易本身对货币政策传导没有经济意义上的显著影响。

No. 14NBER w32127

The Role of Non-Pecuniary Considerations: Location Decisions of College Graduates from Low Income Backgrounds

非金钱因素的作用：来自低收入背景大学毕业生的区位决策

Yifan Gong, Todd R. Stinebrickner, Ralph Stinebrickner, Yuxi Yao

Programs：ED, LS；JEL：J0, J32, J38, J61

Abstract

We examine the initial post-college geographic location decisions of students from hometowns in the Appalachian region that often lack substantial high-skilled job opportunities, focusing on the role of non-pecuniary considerations. Novel survey questions in the spirit of the contingent valuation approach allow us to measure the full non-pecuniary benefits of each relevant geographic location, in dollar equivalents. A new specification test is designed and implemented to provide evidence about the quality of these non-pecuniary measures. Supplementing perceived location choice probabilities and expectations about pecuniary factors with our new non-pecuniary measures allows us to estimate a stylized model of location choice and obtain a comprehensive understanding of the importance of pecuniary and non-pecuniary factors. We also combine the non-pecuniary measures with realized location and earnings outcomes to characterize inequality in overall welfare.

中文摘要

我们研究了来自阿巴拉契亚地区家乡（这些地区通常缺乏大量高技能就业机会）的学生在大学毕业后的初始地理区位决策，重点关注非金钱因素的作用。基于条件估值法精神的新型调查问题使我们能够以美元等价物衡量每个相关地理区位的全部非金钱收益。我们设计并实施了一项新的规范检验，以提供关于这些非金钱度量质量的证据。将感知的区位选择概率和对金钱因素的预期与我们新的非金钱度量相结合，使我们能够估计一个简约的区位选择模型，并全面理解金钱和非金钱因素的重要性。我们还结合非金钱度量与实际的区位和收入结果，来刻画总体福利中的不平等。
