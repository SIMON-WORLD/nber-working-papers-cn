No. 1NBER w31170

Does Monetary Policy Matter? The Narrative Approach after 35 Years

货币政策重要吗？35年后的叙事方法

Christina D. Romer, David H. Romer

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E31, E52, E58, E65, N12

中文摘要

宏观经济识别的叙事方法利用定性资料，如报纸或政府记录，来提供有助于建立因果关系的信息。本文以货币政策影响的新研究作为焦点应用，讨论了严谨叙事分析的要求。我们阅读了1946年至2016年期间美联储政策制定会议的历史纪要和记录，以识别并非针对当前或预期实际活动发展而采取的重大紧缩性和扩张性货币政策变化。我们发现，此类货币冲击对失业、产出和通胀产生了符合预期方向的大幅且显著的影响。对现有政策记录的分析表明，2022年可能发生了一次紧缩性货币冲击。基于先前冲击效应的实证估计，可以预期2023年和2024年实际GDP和通胀将受到显著的负面影响。

Abstract

The narrative approach to macroeconomic identification uses qualitative sources, such as newspapers or government records, to provide information that can help establish causal relationships. This paper discusses the requirements for rigorous narrative analysis using fresh research on the impact of monetary policy as the focal application. We read the historical minutes and transcripts of Federal Reserve policymaking meetings to identify significant contractionary and expansionary changes in monetary policy not taken in response to current or prospective developments in real activity for the period 1946 to 2016. We find that such monetary shocks have large and significant effects on unemployment, output, and inflation in the expected directions. Analysis of available policy records suggests that a contractionary monetary shock likely occurred in 2022. Based on the empirical estimates of the effect of previous shocks, one would expect substantial negative impacts on real GDP and inflation in 2023 and 2024.

No. 2NBER w31169

Logrolling in Congress

国会中的投票交易

Marco Battaglini, Valerio Leone Sciabolazza, Eleonora Patacchini

Programs：POL；JEL：D72, D74, D91

中文摘要

我们研究了美国国会议员之间的投票交易。通过追踪五届立法机构内法案的唱名表决，以及政客在学生时代建立的个人关系，我们记录了有联系的议员共同投票的倾向，这种倾向取决于该法案对政客立法议程的显著程度。尽管这种行为似乎并未提升美国国会议员的立法效率，但投票交易是预测未来晋升至领导职位的有力指标。

Abstract

We study vote trading among U.S. Congress members. By tracking roll-call votes within bills across five legislatures and politicians' personal connections made during the school years, we document a propensity of connected legislators to vote together that depends on how salient the bill is to the politicians' legislative agenda. Although this activity does not seem to enhance U.S. Congress members' legislative effectiveness, vote trading is a strong predictor of future promotions to position of leadership.

No. 3NBER w31168

International Diversification, Reallocation, and the Labor Share

国际多元化、重新配置与劳动份额

Joel M. David, Romain Rancière, David Zeke

Programs：IFM；JEL：F2, F21, F23, F4, F66

中文摘要

日益增长的国际金融多元化如何影响企业层面和总体的劳动份额？我们利用一个应对总体风险的企业劳动选择的新框架来研究这一问题。该理论意味着劳动风险溢价和劳动份额的横截面特征，这些特征在企业层面数据中表现为加成率。国际风险分担导致劳动向风险较高/劳动份额较低的企业重新配置，同时企业内部劳动份额上升，这与关键的微观层面事实相符。我们利用跨国企业层面数据记录了与理论一致的若干实证模式，即：(i) 风险较高的企业劳动份额较低；(ii) 国际金融多元化与向风险较高/劳动份额较低的企业重新配置相关。我们的估计表明，近几十年来，重新配置效应主导了内部效应；净效应上，金融一体化程度的提高使美国企业劳动份额下降了约2.5个百分点，约占自1970年代以来总降幅的三分之一。

Abstract

How does growing international financial diversification affect firm-level and aggregate labor shares? We study this question using a novel framework of firm labor choice in the face of aggregate risk. The theory implies a cross-section of labor risk premia and labor shares that appear as markups in firm-level data. International risk sharing leads to a reallocation of labor towards riskier/low labor share firms alongside a rise in within-firm labor shares, matching key micro-level facts. We use cross-country firm-level data to document a number of empirical patterns consistent with the theory, namely: (i) riskier firms have lower labor shares and (ii) international financial diversification is associated with a reallocation towards risky/low labor share firms. Our estimates suggest the reallocation effect has dominated the within effect in recent decades; on net, increased financial integration has reduced the corporate labor share in the US by about 2.5 percentage points, roughly one-third of the total decline since the 1970s.

No. 4NBER w31167

How Far Goods Travel: Global Transport and Supply Chains from 1965-2020

货物运输多远：1965-2020年的全球运输与供应链

Sharat Ganapati, Woan Foong Wong

Programs：ITI；JEL：F14, F15, L91, R4, R41

中文摘要

本文探讨了过去半个世纪全球运输使用量的演变及其对供应链的影响。随着运输成本下降三分之一，单位实际产出的运输使用量增加了一倍以上。新兴经济体参与世界贸易以及国家间更长距离的贸易推动了这一使用量的增长，从而鼓励了更长的供应链。我们讨论了这一时期的技术进步，以及它们与运输成本和供应链关联的内生反应之间的相互作用。涉及更多国家和更长距离的供应链反映了可靠高效的运输，但也更容易受到 disruptions 的影响，这凸显了在政策制定和未来工作中考虑运输与供应链相互关联的重要性。

Abstract

This paper considers the evolution of global transportation usage over the past half century and its implications for supply chains. Transportation usage per unit of real output has more than doubled as costs decreased by a third. Participation of emerging economies in world trade and longer-distance trade between countries contribute to this usage increase, thereby encouraging longer supply chains. We discuss technological advances over this period, and their interactions with endogenous responses from transportation costs and supply chain linkages. Supply chains involving more countries and longer distances are reflective of reliable and efficient transportation, but are also more exposed to disruptions, highlighting the importance of considering the interconnectedness of transportation and supply chains in policymaking and future work.

No. 5NBER w31166

Do Banks Hedge Using Interest Rate Swaps?

银行是否使用利率互换进行对冲？

Lihong McPhail, Philipp Schnabl, Bruce Tuckman

Programs：AP, CF；JEL：G21, G32

中文摘要

我们分析了银行是否使用利率互换来对冲其资产（主要是贷款和证券）的利率风险。通过检查美国最大250家银行关于单个互换头寸的监管数据，我们发现银行持有大量互换头寸，平均名义价值为4340亿美元。然而，在考虑抵消性互换头寸后，平均银行几乎未表现出来自互换的净利率风险：利率上升100个基点仅使其互换价值减少银行股权的0.1%。银行间互换头寸的差异表明，银行使用互换来对冲利率风险，从而促进了银行部门内的风险转移。此外，我们发现，互换交易商银行主要出于做市目的持有互换头寸，而非交易商银行则使用互换来满足借款人对固定利率贷款的需求。尽管名义金额巨大，但我们的研究结果表明，互换头寸在对冲银行资产总体利率风险方面并不具有经济显著性。相反，银行主要依赖其存款特许经营权来对冲利率风险。

Abstract

We analyze whether banks use interest rate swaps to hedge the interest rate risk of their assets, primarily loans and securities. By examining regulatory data on individual swap positions from the largest 250 U.S. banks, we find that banks hold large swap positions with an average notional value of $434 billion. However, after accounting for offsetting swap positions, the average bank exhibits almost no net interest rate risk from swaps: a 100-basis-point increase in rates reduces the value of its swaps by only 0.1% of bank equity. The variation in swap positions across banks indicates that banks use swaps to hedge interest rate risk, thereby facilitating risk transfer within the banking sector. Additionally, we find that swap dealer banks primarily hold swap positions for market making, while non-dealer banks use swaps to meet borrower demand for fixed-rate loans. Despite the substantial notional amounts, our findings suggest that swap positions are not economically significant in hedging the overall interest rate risk of bank assets. Instead, banks primarily rely on their deposit franchise to hedge interest rate risk.

No. 6NBER w31165

CHOICE – What Can Go Wrong?

选择——可能出什么问题？

Daniel McFadden

Programs：AG；JEL：D01, D04, D15, D18, D47, D83, D91, L14, L15

中文摘要

理性消费者通过去中心化自组织市场中的交易实现帕累托最优的优雅经济图景，因市场不完善和与消费者自身利益不一致的选择而变得模糊。行为经济学记录了这些选择错误，并考虑了能够减轻不良选择对个人伤害的干预措施。本文更广泛地探讨了当卖方设计并营销产品以利用消费者错误时产生的市场扭曲，并提出了缓解这些扭曲的市场管理政策。我举例说明了消费者在收集、筛选和处理信息方面缺乏注意力和勤奋对市场结果产生的巨大影响。我讨论了一个两阶段决策模型的识别和估计，其中注意力在第一阶段决定，而备选方案之间的选择在第二阶段描述。该模型中的阶段由不可观测因素联系，并通过每个阶段特有的可用信息进行识别。我使用该模型来量化健康保险市场中注意力不足的影响。

Abstract

The elegant economic picture of rational consumers achieving Pareto optimality through trade in decentralized self-organized markets is blurred by market imperfections and choices inconsistent with consumer self-interest. Behavioral economics has documented these errors in choice, and considered interventions that can mitigate harm to individuals from bad choices. This essay considers more broadly market distortions that arise when sellers design and market products to exploit consumer errors, and suggests market management policies to mitigate these distortions. I give examples in which consumers’ lack of attention and diligence in collecting, filtering, and processing information has an outsize effect on market outcomes. I discuss identification and estimation of a two-stage model of decision-making, with attention determined in the first stage, and choice among alternatives described in the second stage. The stages in this model are linked by unobservable factors and identified by available information specific to each. I use this model to quantify the effects of inattention in a market for health insurance.

No. 7NBER w31164

Collateral Advantage: Exchange Rates, Capital Flows and Global Cycles

抵押品优势：汇率、资本流动与全球周期

Michael B. Devereux, Charles Engel, Steve Pak Yeung Wu

Programs：AP, IFM, ME；JEL：F30, F40, G15

中文摘要

我们构建了一个两国新凯恩斯模型，其中美国政府债务作为银行资产负债表中的优质抵押品资产具有优势。该模型能够解释美元和美国债券收益对全球经济低迷（尤其是当低迷与全球金融危机相关时）的观测反应。在我们的模型中，美国享有“过度特权”，因为其政府债券被美国和国外的银行视为优质抵押品。在全球压力时期，美元升值，美国政府债券获得的“便利收益”增加。出现“收缩”——每个国家减少其外国资产持有量——其关键决定因素是价格和收益的内生反应。此外，该模型显示，尽管全球经济低迷期间美国国内吸收相对于世界其他地区下降，但美国实际汇率升值，从而解决了Maggiori（2017）强调的“储备货币悖论”。

Abstract

We construct a two-country New Keynesian model in which US government debt has an advantage as a superior collateral asset in the balance sheets of banks. The model can account for the observed response of the US dollar and US bond returns to a global downturn, in particular when the downturn is associated with a global financial crisis. In our model, the U.S. enjoys an “exorbitant privilege” as its government bonds are desired by banks both in the U.S. and abroad as superior collateral. In times of global stress, the dollar appreciates and the “convenience yield” earned by U.S. government bonds increases. There is “retrenchment” - each country reduces its holdings of foreign assets - a critical determinant of which is the endogenous response of prices and returns. In addition, the model displays a U.S. real exchange rate appreciation despite that domestic absorption in the US falls relative to the rest of the world during a global downturn, thus addressing the “reserve currency paradox” highlighted by Maggiori (2017).

No. 8NBER w31163

Wage Inequality in American Manufacturing, 1820-1940: New Evidence

美国制造业的工资不平等，1820-1940年：新证据

Jeremy Atack, Robert A. Margo, Paul Rhode

Programs：DAE；JEL：N31, N32, N62

中文摘要

经济史学家普遍认为，从19世纪初到第二次世界大战前夕，美国制造业的工资不平等遵循倒U型路径。然而，以往的文献未能完全记录这一路径随时间的变化，也未能充分评估企业组织和技术变革等解释因素的作用。我们提供了新的证据，使我们能够更好地记录倒U型及其成因。在论文的第一部分，我们利用美国劳工部1899年的“手工与机器劳动”研究，论证了19世纪制造业企业内部工资不平等上升，主要原因是劳动分工的深化。在第二部分，我们利用马萨诸塞州的州报告数据，构建了生产工人工资不平等的新时间序列，该序列从1890年代初下降到1930年代末，主要是由于分布左尾的压缩。行业面板数据分析表明，电气化是压缩背后的主要因素。

Abstract

The consensus view among economic historians is that wage inequality in American manufacturing followed an inverted-U path from the early nineteenth century until just before World War Two. The previous literature, however, has been unable to fully document this path over time, or fully assess the role of explanatory factors such as changes in firm organization and technology. We provide fresh evidence that allow us to better document the inverted-U and its causes. In the first part of the paper, we use the U.S. Department of Labor’s 1899 “Hand and Machine Labor” study to argue that wage inequality within manufacturing establishments rose over the nineteenth century, primarily because of increasing division of labor In the second part, we use data for Massachusetts from state reports to construct a new time series on wage inequality among production workers, which declined from the early 1890s to the late 1930s, mainly because of compression in the left tail of the distribution. Analysis of industry panel data suggest that electrification was the main factor behind the compression.

Intergenerational Child Mortality Impacts of Deworming: Experimental Evidence from Two Decades of the Kenya Life Panel Survey

驱虫治疗对儿童死亡率的代际影响：来自肯尼亚生命面板调查二十年的实验证据

Michael W. Walker, Alice H. Huang, Suleiman Asman, Sarah J. Baird, Lia Fernald, Joan Hamory Hicks, Fernando Hoces de la Guardia, Satoshi Koiso, Michael Kremer, Matthew N. Krupoff, Michelle Layvant, Eric Ochieng, Pooja Suri, Edward Miguel

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：H51, I15, I25, I5

中文摘要

我们利用一个包含6500多名原始参与者及其后代、长达23年的纵向数据集，评估了肯尼亚一项随机学校驱虫干预措施对接受者子女死亡率的影响。治疗组个体子女的5岁以下死亡率下降了22%（每1000例活产减少17例死亡）。我们发现，亲代健康状况、教育水平、生活水平改善、城市居住增加、生育延迟以及医疗保健使用增加共同促成了这一下降。这些结果为儿童健康投资具有显著的代际收益提供了证据。

Abstract

We assess the impacts of a randomized school-based deworming intervention in Kenya on the mortality of recipients’ children using a 23-year longitudinal data set of over 6,500 original participants and their offspring. The under-5 mortality rate fell by 22% (17 deaths per 1000 live births) for children of treatment group individuals. We find that a combination of improved health, education and living standards, increased urban residence, delayed fertility, and greater use of health care in the parent generation contributed to the reduction. The results provide evidence for meaningful intergenerational benefits of child health investments.

No. 10NBER w31161

Generative AI at Work

工作中的生成式人工智能

Erik Brynjolfsson, Danielle Li, Lindsey R. Raymond

Programs：CF, IO, LE, LS, PR；JEL：D8, J24, M15, M51, O33

中文摘要

新的人工智能工具有潜力改变员工的工作和学习方式，但关于它们对工作的影响，我们知之甚少。在本文中，我们利用来自5179名客户支持代理的数据，研究了基于生成式人工智能的对话助手的逐步引入。使用该工具后，以每小时解决的问题数量衡量的生产率平均提高了14%，其中新手和低技能工人的生产率提高了34%，但对经验丰富和高技能工人的影响微乎其微。我们提供了提示性证据，表明人工智能模型传播了能力较强工人的最佳实践，并帮助新工人沿着经验曲线向下移动。此外，我们发现人工智能辅助改善了客户情绪，提高了员工留存率，并可能促进工人学习。我们的结果表明，使用生成式人工智能可以提高生产率，但不同工人之间的效果存在很大异质性。

Abstract

New AI tools have the potential to change the way workers perform and learn, but little is known about their impacts on the job. In this paper, we study the staggered introduction of a generative AI-based conversational assistant using data from 5,179 customer support agents. Access to the tool increases productivity, as measured by issues resolved per hour, by 14% on average, including a 34% improvement for novice and low-skilled workers but with minimal impact on experienced and highly skilled workers. We provide suggestive evidence that the AI model disseminates the best practices of more able workers and helps newer workers move down the experience curve. In addition, we find that AI assistance improves customer sentiment, increases employee retention, and may lead to worker learning. Our results suggest that access to generative AI can increase productivity, with large heterogeneity in effects across workers.

No. 11NBER w31160

Anatomy of a Run: The Terra Luna Crash

挤兑剖析：Terra Luna崩溃事件

Jiageng Liu, Igor Makarov, Antoinette Schoar

Programs：CF, EFG；JEL：E42, E44, G21

中文摘要

Terra是继比特币和以太坊之后的第三大加密货币生态系统，于2022年5月在三天内崩溃，市值蒸发500亿美元。崩溃的核心是对一个基于区块链的借贷协议（Anchor）的挤兑，该协议向其稳定币（UST）存款人承诺高收益。利用来自Terra区块链的详细数据和交易所的交易数据，我们表明Terra的挤兑是一个跨多条链和多种资产的复杂现象。它不太可能是由第三方集中市场操纵所致，而是由对系统可持续性日益增长的担忧所引发。一旦少数UST大户在2022年5月7日调整了头寸，其他大交易者便纷纷效仿。区块链技术使投资者能够相互监控彼此的行动，并加速了挤兑的速度。更富有、更精明的投资者最先挤兑，损失小得多；而较贫穷、较不精明的投资者挤兑较晚，损失更大。系统的复杂性使得即使是内部人士也难以理解风险的积累。最后，我们得出了关于在一个不存在监管安全网、假名交易公开可见且市场参与者有动力监控系统财务健康状况的环境中金融脆弱性的更广泛教训。

Abstract

Terra, the third largest cryptocurrency ecosystem after Bitcoin and Ethereum, collapsed in three days in May 2022 and wiped out $50 billion in valuation. At the center of the collapse was a run on a blockchain-based borrowing and lending protocol (Anchor) that promised high yields to its stablecoin (UST) depositors. Using detailed data from the Terra blockchain and trading data from exchanges, we show that the run on Terra was a complex phenomenon that happened across multiple chains and assets. It was unlikely due to concentrated market manipulation by a third party but instead was precipitated by growing concerns about the sustainability of the system. Once a few large holders of UST adjusted their positions on May 7th, 2022, other large traders followed. Blockchain technology allowed investors to monitor each other's actions and amplified the speed of the run. Wealthier and more sophisticated investors were the first to run and experienced much smaller losses. Poorer and less sophisticated investors ran later and had larger losses. The complexity of the system made it difficult even for insiders to understand the buildup of risk. Finally, we draw broader lessons about financial fragility in an environment where a regulatory safety net does not exist, pseudonymous transactions are publicly observable, and market participants are incentivized to monitor the financial health of the system.

No. 12NBER w31159

Foreign Technology Adoption as a Flying Propeller

作为飞行螺旋桨的外国技术采用

Yunfang Hu, Takuma Kunieda, Kazuo Nishimura, Ping Wang

Programs：DEV, EFG, ITI；JEL：E20, F21, O40

中文摘要

我们构建了一个外商直接投资（FDI）和外国技术采用的动态一般均衡模型，纳入了采用壁垒、国际技术溢出和相对价格优势。更高的FDI转化效率、更低的采用壁垒或更强的国际技术溢出，加上更低的FDI相对价格，可以推动经济体表现出雁行范式，从而摆脱中等收入陷阱并追赶世界前沿。我们将模型校准到八个代表性亚洲经济体，包括亚洲四小龙和欠发达国家。增长核算显示，全要素生产率、FDI转化效率和外国技术溢出推动了亚洲四小龙的增长奇迹，而降低的采用壁垒和有利的FDI相对价格对于欠发达亚洲经济体的增长更为关键。反事实分析证实，技术体现型FDI起到了飞行螺旋桨的作用，解释了其经济增长的近三分之二。

Abstract

We construct a dynamic general equilibrium model of foreign direct investment (FDI) and foreign technology adoption, incorporating adoption barriers, international technology spillover, and relative price advantages. A higher FDI conversion efficacy, a lower adoption barrier, or a stronger international technology spillover, together with a lower relative price of FDI, can propel an economy to exhibit a flying geese paradigm escaping from a middle-income trap and catching up with the world frontier. We calibrate the model to eight representative Asian economies, including Asian Tigers and less-developed countries. Programs：ME；JEL：C11, C22, E51, E52

中文摘要

我们重新审视菲利普斯曲线的时间变化性，将带有断点的贝叶斯面板新方法应用于美国和欧盟的细分数据。我们的方法能够准确估计菲利普斯曲线断点的数量和发生时间。它还能让我们确定那些菲利普斯曲线表现出类似不稳定模式的行业、城市或国家集群，并考察各通胀序列变化中的领先-滞后模式。我们发现，美国行业数据以及欧盟国家（尤其是较贫穷国家）的菲利普斯曲线明显趋于平坦。相反，在都市统计区层面数据和工资菲利普斯曲线中，平坦化的证据较弱。美国区域数据和欧盟数据表明，价格菲利普斯曲线存在一个拐点，当经济过热运行时，该曲线仍然相对陡峭。

Abstract
