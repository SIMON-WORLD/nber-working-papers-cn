How Replaceable Is a Low-Wage Job?

低薪工作的可替代性有多高？

Evan K. Rose, Yotam Shem-Tov

Programs：LS, PE；JEL：J01, J64

中文摘要

我们利用关联的雇主-雇员工资记录和家庭调查，研究了失去低薪工作的长期后果。对于时薪15美元或以下的全职工作者，因企业层面的特异性收缩而导致的失业，会在六年后造成13%的收入减少，以及超过40,000美元的累计收入损失。长期下降主要源于就业和工时的减少，而非工资率：失业者报告失业并正在找工作的可能性是原来的两倍。相比之下，初始时薪在15至30美元之间的工作者，其长期收入损失相当，但主要由小时工资的下降驱动。将动态工作阶梯模型校准到这些估计值，意味着相对于失业，拥有一份时薪15美元的全职工作所带来的租金价值约为20,000美元，超过月收入的七倍。

Abstract

We study the long-run consequences of losing a low-wage job using linked employer-employee wage records and household surveys. For full-time workers earning $15 per hour or less, job loss due to an idiosyncratic, firm-wide contraction generates a 13% reduction in earnings six years later and over $40,000 cumulative lost earnings. Most of the long-run decrease stems from reductions in employment and hours as opposed to wage rates: job losers are twice as likely to report being unemployed and looking for work. By contrast, workers initially earning $15-$30 per hour see comparable long-run earnings losses driven primarily by reductions in hourly wages. Calibrating a dynamic job ladder model to the estimates implies that the rents from holding a full-time $15 per hour job relative to unemployment are worth about $20,000, more than seven times monthly earnings.

No. 2NBER w31446

(Breaking) Intergenerational Transmission of Mental Health

（打破）心理健康的代际传递

Aline Bütikofer, Rita Ginja, Krzysztof Karbownik, Fanny Landaud

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I14, I18, J12, J62

中文摘要

我们利用挪威全国人口的行政数据中的医疗就诊信息，估计了跨代和跨世系的健康关联。父母有心理健康诊断，其青少年子女被诊断出心理健康问题的概率会高出9.3个百分点（40%）。在集约边际上，身体健康和心理健康关联相似，而世系估计解释了代际持续性的约40%。我们还表明，一项针对有心理健康诊断成年人的年幼子女提供额外健康资源的政策，将亲子心理健康关联降低了约40%。

Abstract

We estimate health associations across generations and dynasties using information on healthcare visits from administrative data for the entire Norwegian population. A parental mental health diagnosis is associated with a 9.3 percentage point (40%) higher probability of a mental health diagnosis of their adolescent child. Intensive margin physical and mental health associations are similar, and dynastic estimates account for about 40% of the intergenerational persistence. We also show that a policy targeting additional health resources for the young children of adults diagnosed with mental health conditions reduced the parent-child mental health association by about 40%.

No. 3NBER w31445

The Effects of Cryptocurrency Wealth on Household Consumption and Investment

加密货币财富对家庭消费和投资的影响

Darren Aiello, Scott R. Baker, Tetyana Balyuk, Marco Di Maggio, Mark J. Johnson, Jason D. Kotter

Programs：AP, CF；JEL：G23, G50, G51, R31

中文摘要

本文利用数百万账户的交易级数据来识别加密货币投资者，并评估个人加密货币财富波动如何影响家庭消费、股权投资和当地房地产市场。我们估计出，来自未实现加密货币收益的边际消费倾向（MPC）是来自未实现股票收益MPC的两倍多，但小于来自外生现金流冲击的MPC。这种MPC主要由现金/支票支出和抵押贷款的增加驱动。此外，家庭出售加密货币以增加可自由支配支出和住房支出。因此，加密货币财富导致房价上涨——在加密货币回报率高之后，加密货币财富较高的县房价增长更快。我们的结果表明，加密货币通过消费和对其他资产类别的投资，对实体经济产生了显著的溢出效应。

Abstract

This paper uses transaction-level data across millions of accounts to identify cryptocurrency investors and evaluate how fluctuations in individual crypto wealth affect household consumption, equity investment, and local real estate markets. We estimate an MPC out of unrealized crypto gains that is more than double the MPC out of unrealized equity gains but smaller than the MPC from exogenous cash flow shocks. This MPC is mostly driven by increases in cash/check spending and mortgages. Moreover, households sell crypto to increase both discretionary as well as housing spending. As a result, crypto wealth causes house price appreciation—counties with higher crypto wealth see higher growth in home values following high crypto returns. Our results indicate that cryptocurrencies have substantial spillover effects on the real economy through consumption and investment into other asset classes.

No. 4NBER w31444

Getting the Right Tail Right: Modeling Tails of Health Expenditure Distributions

正确拟合右尾：健康支出分布尾部的建模

Martin Karlsson, Yulong Wang, Nicolas R. Ziebarth

Programs：EH；JEL：C10, C13, I10, I13

中文摘要

健康支出数据几乎总是包含极端值，这意味着其潜在分布具有厚尾。这可能导致方差和高阶矩无穷大，并使常用的最小二乘法产生偏差。为了适应极端值，我们提出了一种恢复健康支出分布右尾的估计方法。它将流行的两部分模型扩展为一种新颖的三部分模型。我们将所提出的方法应用于德国最大私人健康保险公司之一的索赔数据。我们的研究结果表明，估计的医疗支出年龄梯度与标准最小二乘法存在显著差异。

Abstract

Health expenditure data almost always include extreme values, implying that the underlying distribution has heavy tails. This may result in infinite variances as well as higher-order moments and bias the commonly used least squares methods. To accommodate extreme values, we propose an estimation method that recovers the right tail of health expenditure distributions. It extends the popular two-part model to develop a novel three-part model. We apply the proposed method to claims data from one of the biggest German private health insurers. Our findings show that the estimated age gradient in health care spending differs substantially from the standard least squares method.

No. 5NBER w31443

Instrumental Variable Methods Reconcile Intention-to-Screen Effects Across Pragmatic Cancer Screening Trials

工具变量方法调和实用主义癌症筛查试验中的意向筛查效应

Joshua Angrist, Peter Hull

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：C21, C26, C93, I12

中文摘要

实用主义癌症筛查试验模拟真实世界场景，其中患者和医生是治疗的最终决定者。对此类试验的意向筛查分析保持了基于随机化的同类比较，但依从性差异（被分配至筛查组的受试者实际未接受筛查）使得意向筛查效应难以在不同试验和试验点之间进行比较。我们展示了工具变量方法如何解决非依从性挑战，通过比较来自五项随机试验中17个试验点的估计值，这些试验测量了筛查对结直肠癌发病率的影响。虽然依从率和意向筛查估计值在试验内部和试验之间差异很大，但按方案筛查效应的工具变量估计值却惊人地一致。应用简单的工具变量工具，包括图形分析和正式统计检验，显示了依从性差异如何解释意向筛查效应的变化。此外，筛查依从者的人口统计学特征与完整试验研究样本相似。这些发现论证了癌症筛查效应的工具变量估计的临床相关性。

Abstract

Pragmatic cancer screening trials mimic real-world scenarios in which patients and doctors are the ultimate arbiters of treatment. Intention-to-screen (ITS) analyses of such trials maintain randomization-based apples-to-apples comparisons, but differential adherence (the failure of subjects assigned to screening to actually get screened) makes ITS effects hard to compare across trials and sites. We show how instrumental variables (IV) methods address the nonadherence challenge in a comparison of estimates from 17 sites in five randomized trials measuring screening effects on colorectal cancer incidence. While adherence rates and ITS estimates vary widely across and within trials, IV estimates of per-protocol screening effects are remarkably consistent. An application of simple IV tools, including graphical analysis and formal statistical tests, shows how differential adherence explains variation in ITS impact. Screening compliers are also shown to have similar demographic characteristics to those of the full trial study sample. These findings argue for the clinical relevance of IV estimates of cancer screening effects.

No. 6NBER w31442

Pack-Crack-Pack: Gerrymandering with Differential Turnout

打包-拆分-打包：基于差异化投票率的选区划分

Laurent Bouton, Garance Genicot, Micael Castanheira, Allison L. Stashko

Programs：POL；JEL：D72

中文摘要

本文研究政党对选举地图的操纵，通常称为选区划分。我们分析的核心是认识到并非所有选区居民都会投票。这对选区划分很重要，因为选区必须拥有相同的人口规模，但只有选民才对选举结果至关重要。我们提出了一个允许个体间投票率异质性的选区划分模型。该模型揭示了选区划分者的一种新策略：模式是沿着投票率维度进行打包-拆分-打包。具体而言，政党受益于将低投票率的支持者与高投票率的反对者打包在一起，同时创建将中等至高投票率支持者与较低投票率反对者相结合的拆分选区。这些发现产生了关于党派支持、投票率和选举地图之间关系的可检验实证含义。利用基于比较2020年美国选区重划周期中民主党和共和党提出的地图的新颖实证策略，我们检验了这些预测并找到了支持性证据。

Abstract

This paper studies the manipulation of electoral maps by political parties, commonly referred to as gerrymandering. At the core of our analysis is the recognition that not all inhabitants of a district vote. This is important for gerrymandering as districts must have the same population size, but only voters matter for electoral outcomes. We propose a model of gerrymandering that allows for heterogeneity in voter turnout across individuals. This model reveals a new strategy for the gerrymanderers: the pattern is to pack-crack-pack along the turnout dimension. Specifically, parties benefit from packing low-turnout supporters and high-turnout opponents, while creating cracked districts that combine moderate-to-high-turnout supporters with lower-turnout opponents. These findings yield testable empirical implications about the relationship between partisan support, turnout rates, and electoral maps. Using a novel empirical strategy based on comparing maps proposed by Democrats and Republicans during the 2020 U.S. redistricting cycle, we test these predictions and find supporting evidence.

No. 7NBER w31441

Policy Diffusion Through Elections

通过选举的政策扩散

Hitoshi Shigeoka, Yasutora Watanabe

Programs：AG, EH, PE, POL；JEL：D04, D78, H73, H75

中文摘要

交错双重差分设计在政策评估中普遍存在，但关于政策扩散的机制知之甚少：这些政策如何以及为何在辖区间传播？在本研究中，我们强调了选举在政策扩散中的作用，背景是市政选举因历史原因而不同步。首先，我们利用邻居的选举周期作为邻居政策采纳的工具变量，实证展示了政策扩散的存在。其次，我们进一步展示了市政选举周期与邻居政策采纳的交互作用，并表明市政当局仅在其自身选举时机跟随邻居的政策，这表明政策通过选举进行扩散。

Abstract

Staggered difference-in-differences designs are pervasive in policy evaluations but little is known about the mechanisms of policy diffusion: How and why do such policies spread across jurisdictions? In this study, we highlight the role of elections in policy diffusion in settings where municipal elections are asynchronous due to historical reasons. First, we empirically show the presence of policy diffusion using neighbors’ election cycles as instruments for neighbors’ policy adoption. Second, we further demonstrate interactions of municipalities’ election cycles with neighbors’ adoption and show that they follow neighbors’ policy only during their own election timing, indicating that policy diffuses through elections.

No. 8NBER w31440

Law-Abiding Immigrants: The Incarceration Gap Between Immigrants and the US-born, 1870–2020

守法的移民：1870-2020年移民与美国本土出生者之间的监禁率差距

Ran Abramitzky, Leah Platt Boustan, Elisa Jácome, Santiago Pérez, Juan David Torres

Programs：DAE, LS；JEL：K4, N31

中文摘要

我们提供了首个具有全国代表性的移民和美国本土出生者监禁率长期序列（1870-2020年）。作为一个群体，移民在150年间一直拥有比美国本土出生者更低的监禁率。此外，相对于美国本土出生者，移民的监禁率自1960年以来有所下降：如今的移民被监禁的可能性低60%（相对于美国本土出生的白人低30%）。这种相对下降发生在来自所有地区的移民中，并且不能由移民可观测特征或移民政策的变化来解释。相反，这种下降是受教育程度较低的移民与其美国本土出生同龄人之间结果更广泛分化的一部分。

Abstract

We provide the first nationally representative long-run series (1870–2020) of incarceration rates for immigrants and the US-born. As a group, immigrants have had lower incarceration rates than the US-born for 150 years. Moreover, relative to the US-born, immigrants’ incarceration rates have declined since 1960: immigrants today are 60% less likely to be incarcerated (30% relative to US-born whites). This relative decline occurred among immigrants from all regions and cannot be explained by changes in immigrants’ observable characteristics or immigration policy. Instead, the decline is part of a broader divergence of outcomes between less-educated immigrants and their US-born counterparts.

No. 9NBER w31439

Time Use, College Attainment, and The Working-from-Home Revolution

时间利用、大学学历与居家办公革命

Benjamin W. Cowan

Programs：CH, EH, LS；JEL：I12, I24, J22, J24, J32

中文摘要

我证明了COVID-19大流行后居家办公的深刻变化集中在拥有大学学历的个体中。相对于2015-19年，在“后疫情”时期（2021年8月至2022年12月）的工作日，大学毕业生居家工作的时间增加了78分钟；对于非毕业生，这一数字为22分钟。居家工作占全部工作时间的比例（对于任何有工作的人）在毕业生中增加了22%，在非毕业生中增加了7%。两组人群的平均工作分钟数变化不大。每日用于出行（例如通勤）的时间在大学毕业生中减少了21分钟，在非毕业生中减少了6分钟。我考察了同期大学毕业生相对于非毕业生的时间利用模式变化。大学毕业生经历了从外出就餐向在家就餐的相对转变，空闲时间增加，以及与孩子相处的时间增加，后一种效应集中在父亲身上。因此，虽然大学毕业生中育儿方面的性别差距可能因居家办公革命而缩小，但父母大学学历差异导致的子女结果差距可能因此加剧。

Abstract

I demonstrate that the profound change in working from home (WFH) in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic is concentrated among individuals with college degrees. Relative to 2015-19, the number of minutes worked from home on “post-pandemic” (August 2021-December 2022) weekdays increased by 78 minutes for college graduates; for non-graduates, it was 22 minutes. The share of work done at home (for those who worked at all) increased by 22% for graduates and 7% for non-graduates. Average minutes worked changed little for either group. Daily time spent traveling (e.g., commuting) fell by 21 minutes for college graduates and 6 minutes for non-graduates. I examine how time-use patterns change for college graduates relative to non-graduates over the same period. College graduates experience a relative shift from eating out to eating at home, an increase in free time, and an increase in time spent with children, with the latter effect being concentrated among fathers. Thus, while the gender gap in childcare among college graduates may be diminished by the WFH revolution, gaps in children’s outcomes by parents’ college attainment may be exacerbated by it.

Political Preferences and Transport Infrastructure: Evidence from California’s High-Speed Rail

政治偏好与交通基础设施：来自加州高铁的证据

Pablo D. Fajgelbaum, Cecile Gaubert, Nicole Gorton, Eduardo Morales, Edouard Schaal

Programs：ITI, POL；JEL：D72, O18, R10, R42

中文摘要

我们研究政治偏好如何塑造了加州高铁项目，这是一个在2008年通过公投批准的大型交通项目。选民的支持对其居住区预计的经济收益反应显著，这些收益通过一个与加州高铁计划相匹配的高铁定量模型来衡量。鉴于这种反应，一种比较拟议网络与替代设计的显示性偏好方法，识别出规划者对政治支持的强烈偏好。最优的无政治偏见设计本应将车站更靠近加州人口密集的大都市区，但在这些地区更难争取选票，从而增加了预计的经济收益。

Abstract

We study how political preferences shaped California’s High-Speed Rail (CHSR), a large transportation project approved by referendum in 2008. Voters’ support responded significantly to the projected economic gains in their tract of residence, as measured by a quantitative model of high-speed rail matched to CHSR plans. Given this response, a revealed-preference approach comparing the proposed network with alternative designs identifies strong planner’s preferences for political support. The optimal politically-blind design would have placed the stations nearer to California’s dense metro areas, where it was harder to sway votes, thus increasing the projected economic gains.

Certification and Recertification in Welfare Programs: What Happens When Automation Goes Wrong?

福利项目中的认证与再认证：当自动化出错时会发生什么？

Derek Wu, Bruce D. Meyer

Programs：PE；JEL：H53, I38

中文摘要

行政负担如何影响不同福利项目的注册？谁在特定阶段被筛选出去？本文研究了与福利个案工作者协助自动化相关的行政负担增加的影响，利用印第安纳州一个独特的自然实验，其中IBM公司远程处理了三分之二县的申请。使用涵盖近300万项目受助人的关联行政记录，结果显示，在自动化一年后，SNAP、TANF和Medicaid的注册人数分别下降了15%、24%和4%，这些异质性下降很大程度上可归因于跨项目再认证成本的差异。较早处理且贫困率较高的县福利领取下降幅度更大。更贫困的个体在退出时被筛选出去，而较不贫困的个体在进入时被筛选出去，这是一个新颖的区别，如果仅关注总体平均变化的典型目标定位衡量指标，将会遗漏这一点。在IBM的自动化系统解散后，Medicaid注册人数的下降表现出显著的持久性，这表明行政负担增加可能产生长期后果。

Abstract

How do administrative burdens influence enrollment in different welfare programs? Who is screened out at a given stage? This paper studies the impacts of increased administrative burdens associated with the automation of welfare caseworker assistance, leveraging a unique natural experiment in Indiana in which the IBM Corporation remotely processed applications for two-thirds of all counties. Using linked administrative records covering nearly 3 million program recipients, the results show that SNAP, TANF, and Medicaid enrollments fell by 15%, 24%, and 4% one year after automation, with these heterogeneous declines largely attributable to cross-program differences in recertification costs. Earlier-treated and higher-poverty counties experienced larger declines in welfare receipt. More needy individuals were screened out at exit while less needy individuals were screened out at entry, a novel distinction that would be missed by typical measures of targeting which focus on average changes overall. The decline in Medicaid enrollment exhibited considerable permanence after IBM's automated system was disbanded, suggesting potential long-term consequences of increased administrative burdens.

No. 12NBER w31436

Access and Exposure to Local News Media in the Digital Era: Evidence from U.S. Media Markets

数字时代地方新闻媒体的获取与接触：来自美国媒体市场的证据

Minji Bang, Lucie L'Heude, Andrew Postlewaite, Holger Sieg

Programs：IO, PE, POL；JEL：C0, L0, P0, R0

中文摘要

利用一项针对美国大型媒体市场成年人的新综合调查，我们显示少数族裔和低技能个体（他们深受地方经济冲击的影响）通常比高技能和白人个体对地方新闻有更强的偏好和更强的接触。同时，这些弱势个体受到了数字革命对新闻供应影响的负面冲击。特别是，高技能和白人个体更迅速地接受了在线和社交媒体，而低技能和少数族裔个体仍然严重依赖地方电视和其他传统新闻提供商。这些提供商选择的差异很重要，因为数字革命降低了传统新闻提供商的质量，而在线和社交媒体的质量和数量则大幅提升。为了更深入地了解数字革命的福利后果并评估潜在的政策干预，我们开发并估计了一个新闻生产与地方新闻需求模型。我们的模型基于一个时间分配、离散捆绑选择框架。我们的发现表明，地方报纸（电视）的消失平均会减少923美元（1064美元）的福利，这远高于大多数市场的年度订阅成本。最后，我们研究了补贴在线或社交媒体以弥补地方报纸或电视台消失的政策。

Abstract

Using a new comprehensive survey of adults in large U.S. media markets we show that minority and low-skill individuals, who are heavily exposed to shocks to the local economy, typically have stronger preferences for and stronger ex- posure to local news than high-skill and white individuals. At the same time, these disadvantaged individuals have been negatively affected by the impact of the digital revolution on news provision. In particular, high-skill and white indi- viduals have more rapidly embraced online and social media while low-skill and minority individuals still heavily rely on local television and other traditional news providers. These differences in provider choices are important because the digital revolution has reduced the quality of traditional news providers while the quality and quantity of online and social media have substantially in- creased. To gain additional insights into the welfare consequence of the digital revolution and assess potential policy interventions, we develop and estimate a model of news production and demand for local news. Our model is based on a time-allocation, discrete bundle-choice framework. Our findings suggest that the loss of the local newspaper (television) reduces welfare on average by $923 ($1064) which is well above the annual subscription costs in most markets. Finally, we study policies that subsidize online or social media to offset the loss of the local newspaper or television station.

No. 13NBER w31435

The Incidence of Adverse Selection: Theory and Evidence from Health Insurance Choices

逆向选择的负担：来自健康保险选择的理论与证据

Michael Geruso, Timothy Layton, Adam Leive

稀疏模型虽然长期以来受到社会科学家的青睐和追求，但在经济预测等方面可能不如大模型有效或稳定（Giannone et al., 2021）。为了在利用大数据获得卓越实证表现的同时实现经济解释所需的稀疏性，我们引入了一个通用框架，该框架通过新的决策树联合聚类观测值，并使用贝叶斯先验局部选择变量，以对具有潜在分组异质性的面板数据进行建模。我们在贝叶斯聚类模型（BCM）中推导出解析边际似然作为全局分裂准则，以纳入经济指导、处理参数和模型不确定性并防止过拟合。我们将BCM应用于资产定价，并为数据驱动的资产聚类和宏观经济体制估计非共同因子模型。我们发现：（i）与收益波动率、规模和价值的非线性交互作用相关的横截面异质性；（ii）由市场波动率和估值预测的因子相关性的结构性变化；以及（iii）MKTRF和SMB作为共同因子，以及跨特征管理-市场时机聚类的多个非共同因子。BCM有助于解释与波动率或规模相关的异常现象、利用组内检验并缓解“因子动物园”问题。总体而言，BCM在美国股票的定价和投资方面优于基准共同因子模型，例如，多个聚类的样本外横截面R²超过25%，由ME-B/M 5×5投资组合构建的切点组合夏普比率提高了两倍。

Abstract

Sparse models, though long preferred and pursued by social scientists, can be ineffective or unstable relative to large models, for example, in economic predictions (Giannone et al., 2021). To achieve sparsity for economic interpretation while exploiting big data for superior empirical performance, we introduce a general framework that jointly clusters observations (via new decision trees) and locally selects variables (with Bayesian priors) for modeling panel data with potential grouped heterogeneity. We derive analytical marginal likelihoods as global split criteria in our Bayesian Clustering Model (BCM), to incorporate economic guidance, address parameter and model uncertainties, and prevent overfitting. We apply BCM to asset pricing and estimate uncommon-factor models for data-driven asset clusters and macroeconomic regimes. We find (i) cross-sectional heterogeneity linked to (non-linear interactions of) return volatility, size, and value, (ii) structural changes in factor relevance predicted by market volatility and valuation, and (iii) MKTRF and SMB as common factors and multiple uncommon factors across characteristics-managed-market-timed clusters. BCM helps explain volatility- or size-related anomalies, exploit within-group tests, and mitigate the “factor zoo” problem. Overall, BCM outperforms benchmark common-factor models in pricing and investments in U.S. equities, e.g., attaining out-of-sample cross-sectional R2s exceeding 25% for multiple clusters and Sharpe ratio of tangency portfolios tripling built from ME-B/M 5 × 5 portfolios.

The Largest Insurance Expansion in History: Saving One Million Lives Per Year in China

史上最大的保险扩张：中国每年挽救超过一百万条生命

Jonathan Gruber, Mengyun Lin, Junjian Yi

Programs：AG, CH, DEV, EH, LS, PE；JEL：H4, I13

中文摘要

新型农村合作医疗制度（NCMS）于2003年至2008年在中国推广，为8亿农村人口提供了保险。我们将总体死亡率数据与个人调查数据相结合，并根据项目推广和不同地区农村人口比例的异质性来识别NCMS的影响。我们发现总体死亡率显著下降，该项目在其高峰期每年挽救超过一百万条生命，并解释了同期中国预期寿命全部增长的78%。我们使用关于死亡率、其他健康结果和医疗利用的微观数据证实了这些死亡率效应。

Abstract

The New Cooperative Medical Scheme (NCMS) rolled out in China from 2003-2008 provided insurance to 800 million rural Chinese. We combine aggregate mortality data with individual survey data, and identify the impact of the NCMS from program rollout and heterogeneity across areas in their rural share. We find that there was a significant decline in aggregate mortality, with the program saving more than one million lives per year at its peak, and explaining 78% of the entire increase in life expectancy in China over this period. We confirm these mortality effects using micro-data on mortality, other health outcomes, and utilization.

Combining Human Expertise with Artificial Intelligence: Experimental Evidence from Radiology

将人类专业知识与人工智能相结合：来自放射学的实验证据

Nikhil Agarwal, Alex Moehring, Pranav Rajpurkar, Tobias Salz

Programs：EH, IO, PR；JEL：C50, C90, D47, D83

中文摘要

如果人类拥有AI无法获取的信息（上下文信息），那么使用人工智能（AI）预测的完全自动化可能并非最优。我们通过与专业放射科医生进行信息实验来研究人机协作。结果显示：（i）提供AI预测平均而言并未提高表现，而（ii）提供上下文信息则提高了表现。放射科医生未能从AI辅助中获益，是因为信念更新中的错误——他们低估了AI预测，并将自己的信息和AI预测视为统计独立。除非这些错误能够得到纠正，否则最优的人机协作设计是将病例分配给人类或AI，而很少分配给AI辅助的人类。

Abstract

Full automation using Artificial Intelligence (AI) predictions may not be optimal if humans have information not available to the AI (contextual information). We study human-AI collaboration using an information experiment with professional radiologists. Results show that providing (i) AI predictions does not improve performance on average, whereas (ii) contextual information does. Radiologists do not realize the gains from AI assistance because of errors in belief updating – they underweight AI predictions and treat their own information and AI predictions as statistically independent. Unless these mistakes can be corrected, the optimal human-AI collaboration design delegates cases either to humans or to AI, but rarely to AI assisted humans.

Think Globally, Act Globally: Opportunities to Mitigate Greenhouse Gas Emissions in Low- and Middle-Income Countries

全球思考，全球行动：在低收入和中等收入国家减缓温室气体排放的机遇

Rachel Glennerster, Seema Jayachandran

Programs：DEV, EEE, ITI；JEL：F18, O13, Q54, Q56

中文摘要

减少温室气体排放是一种全球公共品，这使得在应对这一挑战时采取全球行动是有效的。我们阐述了几个理由，说明寻求减缓气候变化的高收入国家如果将资源投资于低收入和中等收入国家的机会，可能会产生更大的影响。具体来说，一些最简单和最便宜的选择在高收入国家已被利用，低收入和中等收入国家的土地和劳动力成本更低，新建绿色设施比改造为绿色设施更便宜，并且在一体化经济中，全球目标定位至关重要。我们还讨论了经济上的反对论点，例如在低收入和中等收入国家监测排放水平的挑战、伦理考量、不将减缓资金重复计算为发展援助的重要性，以及可能有助于实现这一机遇的政策步骤。

Abstract

Reductions in greenhouse gas emissions are a global public good, which makes it efficient to act globally when addressing this challenge. We lay out several reasons that high-income countries seeking to mitigate climate change might have greater impact if they invest their resources in opportunities in low- and middle-income countries. Specifically, some of the easiest and cheapest options have already been tapped in high-income countries, land and labor costs are lower in low- and middle-income countries, it is cheaper to build green than to retrofit green, and global targeting matters in integrated economies. We also discuss economic counterarguments such as the challenge of monitoring emissions levels in low- and middle-income countries, ethical considerations, the importance of not double-counting mitigation funding as development aid, and policy steps that might help to realize this opportunity.
