The Geography of Opportunity after the Civil War: Black and White Americans’ Intra- and Intergenerational Mobility into Property Ownership

内战后机遇的地理分布：黑人与白人美国人在财产所有权上的代内与代际流动性

William J. Collins, Nicholas C. Holtkamp

Programs：DAE, DEV, LS；JEL：I3, J70, N31

Abstract

This paper sheds new light on historical black-white disparities in wealth and economic mobility by examining datasets of linked census records. First, we compare black and white men’s intra- and intergenerational mobility into property ownership between 1870, the first census taken after the Civil War, and 1900. Conditional on not owning property in 1870, black men’s mobility rate into property ownership was far lower than white men’s. If black men’s post-1870 mobility had mirrored that of landless white men, the black-white home ownership gap in 1900 would have been small. Second, we show that for black men located in cotton-intensive counties in 1870, the likelihood of owning property in 1900 was far lower than for black men located elsewhere. This is salient in national samples as well as in samples restricted to the states of the former Confederacy, with and without extensive controls. The pattern is connected to the prevalence of sharecropping and relatively high black population shares. For white men, the difference in upward mobility between cotton-intensive and other areas was much smaller. Many black households acquired land and homes of their own in this era, an important channel for economic advance, but their geographic concentration in areas with fewer opportunities to acquire property implied low aggregate rates of upward mobility.

中文摘要

本文通过分析关联的人口普查记录数据集，为历史上黑人与白人在财富和经济流动性上的差距提供了新的视角。首先，我们比较了1870年（内战后首次人口普查）至1900年间黑人与白人男性在财产所有权上的代内与代际流动性。在1870年未拥有财产的条件下，黑人男性进入财产所有权的流动率远低于白人男性。如果黑人男性在1870年后的流动性能与无土地的白人男性相当，那么1900年的黑白住房拥有率差距将会很小。其次，我们发现，对于1870年位于棉花密集型县的黑人男性，其在1900年拥有财产的可能性远低于位于其他地区的黑人男性。这一现象在全国样本以及仅限于前邦联各州的样本中均显著存在，无论是否加入大量控制变量。这种模式与分成制佃农的盛行以及相对较高的黑人人口比例有关。对于白人男性而言，棉花密集型地区与其他地区之间向上流动性的差异要小得多。在这一时期，许多黑人家庭获得了自己的土地和房屋，这是经济进步的重要渠道，但他们在地理上集中于财产获取机会较少的地区，导致了总体向上流动率较低。

Decoupling Taste-Based versus Statistical Discrimination in Elections

在选举中分离基于偏好的歧视与统计性歧视

Amanda de Albuquerque, Frederico Finan, Anubhav Jha, Laura Karpuska, Francesco Trebbi

Programs：DEV, LS, POL；JEL：D72, P0, P16

Abstract

We present a methodology for decoupling taste-based versus statistical discrimination in political behavior. We combine a flexible empirical model of voting, featuring vertical and horizontal candidate differentiation in gender, ability, and policy positions, with a large-scale micro-targeted electoral experiment aimed at increasing female candidate vote shares. Our structural econometric approach allows to separately identify preference parameters driving taste-based discrimination and beliefs parameters driving statistical discrimination through expectations about ability and policy positions of female politicians. Our application to Brazilian municipal elections uncovers substantial levels of taste-based and statistical discrimination. Counterfactual political campaigns show promise in reducing both.

中文摘要

我们提出了一种在政治行为中分离基于偏好的歧视与统计性歧视的方法。我们将一个灵活的投票实证模型（该模型在性别、能力及政策立场上包含纵向和横向的候选人差异化）与一项旨在提高女性候选人得票率的大规模微观定向选举实验相结合。我们的结构计量经济学方法能够分别识别驱动基于偏好的歧视的偏好参数，以及通过关于女性政治家能力和政策立场的预期来驱动统计性歧视的信念参数。我们将该方法应用于巴西市政选举，发现了显著的基于偏好的歧视和统计性歧视。反事实的政治竞选活动在减少这两种歧视方面显示出潜力。

Inflation, Expectations and Monetary Policy: What Have We Learned and to What End?

通胀、预期与货币政策：我们学到了什么，目标何在？

Olivier Coibion, Yuriy Gorodnichenko

Programs：EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E30, E4, E5

Abstract

We review recent research and experiences linking inflation and expectations, emphasizing what has been learned since 2020. One clear lesson is that the inflation expectations of most economic agents have been and remain unanchored. The unanchored nature of inflation expectations, in combination with supply shocks, can explain much of the inflation surge and subsequent disinflation when viewed through the lens of an expectations-augmented Phillips curve, both in the U.S. and abroad. New policy frameworks are unlikely to address this feature of expectations. Only a communication strategy that breaks what we refer to as the “cycle of selective inattention” is likely to be successful, but it is probably already too late to stop the next inflation surge.

中文摘要

我们回顾了近期关于通胀与预期之间联系的研究与经验，重点强调了自2020年以来的发现。一个明确的教训是，大多数经济主体的通胀预期过去是、现在仍然是脱锚的。通胀预期的脱锚性质，结合供给冲击，可以通过预期增强型菲利普斯曲线的视角解释美国及海外通胀飙升及随后的反通胀过程的大部分。新的政策框架不太可能解决预期的这一特征。只有一种打破我们称之为“选择性忽视循环”的沟通策略才有可能成功，但可能已经为时已晚，无法阻止下一次通胀飙升。

Recovering from COVID

从新冠疫情中复苏

James H. Stock, Mark W. Watson

Programs：EFG, EH, ME；JEL：E32, I00, I12

Abstract

The COVID business cycle was unique. The recession was by far the deepest and shortest in the U.S. postwar record and the recovery was remarkably rapid. The cycle saw an unprecedented reallocation of employment and consumption away from in-person services towards goods that can be consumed at home and outdoors. This paper provides a simple empirical model that attributes these and other anomalies in real economic activity to a single unobserved shock. That shock is closely connected to COVID deaths, and diminishes in importance over the expansion, consistent with self-protective measures like masking, COVID fatigue, and eventually the availability of the vaccine. The COVID shock and anomalous COVID dynamics largely disappeared by late 2022. It appears that macrodynamics have returned to normal and that the structural shifts wrought by the pandemic have had limited effects on the underlying economic trends of key indicators, despite notable changes like the prevalence of remote work. The greatest macroeconomic legacy of the COVID business cycle has been on the national debt.

中文摘要

新冠商业周期是独一无二的。此次衰退是美国战后记录中最深且最短的，复苏也异常迅速。该周期见证了就业和消费从面对面服务向可在家庭和户外消费的商品前所未有的重新配置。本文提供了一个简单的实证模型，将实际经济活动中的这些及其他异常现象归因于一个单一未观测到的冲击。该冲击与新冠死亡人数密切相关，并在经济扩张过程中重要性逐渐减弱，这与戴口罩等自我保护措施、新冠疲劳以及最终疫苗的可用性相一致。新冠冲击和异常的新冠动态在2022年底基本消失。宏观动态似乎已恢复正常，尽管出现了远程工作普及等显著变化，但疫情造成的结构性转变对关键指标的潜在经济趋势影响有限。新冠商业周期最大的宏观经济遗产体现在国家债务上。

Real-world Effectiveness of the Influenza Vaccine in Young Children

流感疫苗在幼儿中的真实世界有效性

Michael L. Anderson, Carlos Dobkin, Devon Gorry, Hung-Fu Tseng

Programs：CH, EH；JEL：I10, I12, I18

Abstract

Influenza causes substantial illness and healthcare utilization among children. Randomized clinical trials (RCTs) demonstrate that the influenza vaccine reduces influenza illness detectable via active surveillance, but RCTs typically have insufficient samples to examine economically meaningful outcomes such as healthcare provider visits. In this study we document that children aged two through five whose well-child visits occur when the seasonal influenza vaccine is broadly available are 23.4 percentage points more likely to be vaccinated than those whose visits do not. Using large administrative healthcare datasets, we leverage this variation in vaccination rates to show that the influenza vaccine reduces outpatient and emergency department visits significantly. The results imply that making pediatric influenza vaccinations more convenient could substantially increase vaccination rates and reduce healthcare expenditures.

中文摘要

流感导致儿童出现大量疾病和医疗资源使用。随机临床试验表明，流感疫苗可减少通过主动监测发现的流感疾病，但临床试验通常样本量不足，无法检验具有经济意义的结果，如医疗服务提供者就诊。在本研究中，我们记录了年龄在2至5岁之间的儿童，如果其健康儿童就诊时间恰逢季节性流感疫苗广泛可用的时期，其接种疫苗的可能性比就诊时间不在该时期的儿童高出23.4个百分点。利用大型行政医疗数据集，我们利用疫苗接种率的这种变化来证明流感疫苗能显著减少门诊和急诊就诊。结果表明，提高儿童流感疫苗接种的便利性可以大幅提高接种率并减少医疗支出。

The Structural Transformation of Innovation

创新的结构性转型

Diego A. Comin, Danial Lashkari, Martí Mestieri

Programs：DEV, EFG, PR；JEL：E02, O1, O4, O5

Abstract

We document the structural transformation of innovation using historical patent data since the 1850s, along with R&D expenditure and TFP growth for the post-war period. Over time, innovation has shifted from agricultural sectors to manufacturing, and, more recently, to services. We develop and quantify a multi-sector semi-endogenous growth model of structural change in innovation and production, incorporating the classical demand-pull and technology-push drivers of innovation. Sectors differ in their innovation technologies, and the extent to which they benefit from knowledge spillovers (technology-push). Nonhomothetic demand shifts the market shares toward income-elastic sectors along the growth process (demand-pull). A calibrated version of our model replicates the structural transformations of innovation and production observed in the US data. Using the model, we evaluate the future impact of Baumol’s disease on aggregate productivity and find it to be minimal. Our results suggest that aggregate productivity growth may recover in the coming decades as the service sector becomes increasingly innovation-driven.

中文摘要

我们利用自1850年代以来的历史专利数据，以及战后时期的研发支出和全要素生产率增长，记录了创新的结构性转型。随着时间的推移，创新已从农业部门转向制造业，并在近期转向服务业。我们开发并量化了一个包含创新和生产结构性变化的多部门半内生增长模型，该模型融合了经典的创新需求拉动和技术推动驱动因素。各部门在创新技术以及从知识溢出（技术推动）中获益的程度上存在差异。非位似需求在增长过程中将市场份额转向收入弹性高的部门（需求拉动）。我们模型的校准版本复制了美国数据中观察到的创新和生产的结构性转型。利用该模型，我们评估了鲍莫尔病对未来生产率的潜在影响，发现其影响微乎其微。我们的结果表明，随着服务业日益成为创新驱动，未来几十年全要素生产率增长可能会恢复。

Internal Migration and the Spatial Reorganization of Agriculture

内部迁移与农业的空间重组

Raahil Madhok, Frederik Noack, Ahmed Mushfiq Mobarak, Olivier Deschenes

Programs：DEV, EEE；JEL：J43, O13, O15, Q15, R11

Abstract

This paper studies how agricultural production responds to the loss of agricultural labor during the process of urbanization and structural transformation. Using household microdata from India and exogenous variation in migration opportunities induced by urban income shocks, we show that agricultural households do not systematically replace lost labor with increased capital. Instead, they cultivate less land and lower their use of agricultural technology, reducing crop production. Resulting changes in land and crop prices induce non-migrant households to expand agricultural investments and production. In aggregate, market adaptation mitigates over three-fourths of the direct agricultural losses from urbanization. Spatial reorganization moves food production from land near urban areas toward more remote areas with lower emigration.

中文摘要

本文研究了在城市化与结构性转型过程中，农业生产如何应对农业劳动力的流失。利用印度家庭微观数据以及由城市收入冲击引发的迁移机会的外生变化，我们表明农业家庭并未系统地用增加的资本来替代流失的劳动力。相反，他们耕种更少的土地，降低农业技术的使用，从而减少了作物产量。由此导致的土地和作物价格变化促使非迁移家庭扩大农业投资和生产。总体而言，市场适应缓解了城市化对农业造成的直接损失的四分之三以上。空间重组将粮食生产从靠近城市地区的土地转移到移民迁出率更高的更偏远地区。

Local Monetary Policy

地方性货币政策

Vyacheslav Fos, Tommaso Tamburelli, Nancy R. Xu

Programs：CF, ME, POL；JEL：D7, E5, E51, E58

Abstract

When Federal Reserve districts experience high inflation or low unemployment but lack voting rights to influence FOMC decisions, credit extended to commercial banks through the Discount Window (DW) declines. Our identification strategy is based on the exogenous rotation of voting rights among Reserve Banks and on within borrower-time and district-time variation in DW loans and Federal Home Loan Bank (FHLB) loans, implying that factors related to changes in macroeconomic conditions, local credit demand, or borrower characteristics do not drive the results. The effect on bank funding sources translates into changes in the composition of loans extended by commercial banks.

How Damaging is Shouting "Fire" in a Crowded Theatre?

在拥挤的剧院里喊“着火了”会造成多大损害？

Joshua S. Gans

Programs：PR；JEL：C72, D61, K42

Abstract

The canonical example of unprotected speech—falsely shouting “fire” in a crowded theatre—presumes such an act inevitably causes harmful panic. This paper challenges that presumption through a game-theoretic analysis of evacuation dynamics. I model a theatre as an N × M grid where rational patrons navigate spatial constraints while evacuating. Strategic conflicts arise only when patrons are equidistant from exits, creating localized “clash games” that admit multiple equilibria, including numerous pure-strategy equilibria that achieve zero-collision evacuations. Using global games methodology, I show that strategic uncertainty uniquely selects an equilibrium, where those entering from rows have precedence over those already in aisles. However, introducing a panic mechanism based on accumulated waiting time reveals a tension: the strategically optimal East-priority equilibrium proves most vulnerable to behavioral breakdown, while the alternating equilibrium exhibits superior robustness to panic. These findings suggest that whether false alarms cause harmful disorder depends critically on the interaction between spatial geometry, strategic behavior, and psychological limits—not merely on the alarm itself.

中文摘要

不受保护的言论的典型例子——在拥挤的剧院里虚假地喊“着火了”——假定这种行为不可避免地会导致有害的恐慌。本文通过博弈论分析疏散动态来挑战这一假定。我将剧院建模为一个N×M的网格，其中理性的顾客在疏散时需应对空间限制。只有当顾客与出口距离相等时，才会出现战略冲突，从而产生局部化的“冲突博弈”，这类博弈允许多重均衡，包括许多实现零碰撞疏散的纯策略均衡。利用全局博弈方法，我表明战略不确定性唯一地选择了一个均衡，在该均衡中，从排内进入的顾客优先于已在过道中的顾客。然而，引入基于累积等待时间的恐慌机制揭示了一种张力：战略上最优的“东侧优先”均衡在行为崩溃方面最为脆弱，而交替均衡则表现出对恐慌的卓越稳健性。这些发现表明，虚假警报是否会导致有害的混乱，关键取决于空间几何、战略行为和心理极限之间的相互作用，而不仅仅是警报本身。

Remote Work, Employee Mix, and Performance

远程工作、员工构成与绩效

Cevat Giray Aksoy, Nicholas Bloom, Steven J. Davis, Victoria Marino, Cem Ozguzel

Programs：CH, EFG, IO, LS, PE, PR；JEL：J0

Abstract

We study the shift to fully remote work at a large call center in Turkey, highlighting three findings. First, fully remote work increased the share of women, including married women, rural and smaller-town residents. By accessing groups with traditionally lower labor-force participation the firm was able to increase its share of graduate employees by 14% without raising wages. Second, workforce productivity rose by 10%, reflecting shorter call durations for remote employees. This was facilitated by a quieter home working environment, avoiding the background noise in the office. Third, fully remote employees with initial in-person training saw higher long-run remote productivity and lower attrition rates. This underscores the advantages of initial in-person onboarding for fully remote employees.

中文摘要

我们研究了土耳其一家大型呼叫中心向完全远程工作的转变，突出了三个发现。首先，完全远程工作提高了女性（包括已婚女性）、农村和小城镇居民的比例。通过接触传统上劳动参与率较低的群体，该公司能够在未提高工资的情况下，将其毕业生员工比例提高14%。其次，员工生产率提高了10%，这反映了远程员工通话时长的缩短。这得益于更安静的家庭工作环境，避免了办公室的背景噪音。第三，接受过初始面对面培训的完全远程员工，其长期远程生产率更高，离职率也更低。这凸显了为完全远程员工提供初始面对面入职培训的优势。

The Old Keynesian Model

旧凯恩斯主义模型

Robert J. Barro

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：E12

Abstract

Articles from the 1970s applied a general-disequilibrium framework to the determination of output and employment with sticky nominal prices and wages. Quantities are determined on the short sides of the goods and labor market and involve non-price rationing. With general excess supply, where prices and wages are “too high,” output and employment are determined by the Keynesian demand multiplier, based on the marginal propensity to consume. In the case of general excess demand, where prices and wages are “too low,” output and employment are determined by a supplier multiplier, based on the marginal propensity to work. In these two cases, output and employment are independent of the real wage. However, starting from general market clearing, deviations from the general-market-clearing real wage in either direction reduce output and employment. The New Keynesian Model, which also relies on sticky prices, amounts to an extension of the general-excess-supply case of the Old Keynesian Model. The New Keynesian Model assumes that quantities are always demand determined, but this assumption is tenable only under general excess supply. A promising alternative to the non-price rationing of quantities in the Old Keynesian Model is a setup with search-and-matching frictions in the markets for goods and labor.
