The Missing Link? Using LinkedIn Data to Measure Race, Ethnic, and Gender Differences in Employment Outcomes at Individual Companies

缺失的环节？利用领英数据衡量个别公司在种族、民族和性别方面的就业结果差异

Alexander Berry, Elizabeth M. Maloney, David Neumark

Programs：LS；JEL：J15, J16, J7

中文摘要

加强反歧视法律的执行有助于减少美国在种族、民族和性别方面经济结果的差异。然而，检测可能存在的歧视并采取行动所需的数据——特别是特定公司内部种族、民族和性别差异的数据——并未公开。在本文中，我们探索并开发了利用从公开可用的领英数据中提取信息来衡量公司劳动力种族、民族和性别构成的方法。我们使用基于姓名和照片的预测工具来识别种族、民族和性别。我们通过验证练习，将抓取的领英数据估算结果与两个来源（美国社区调查数据和公司多样性或EEO-1报告）进行比较，表明可以利用领英数据获得关于劳动力人口统计构成（按种族、民族和性别划分）的相当可靠的衡量指标。我们应用这些方法研究了两家大型公司中被雇佣员工以及经历大规模裁员员工的种族、民族和性别构成。最后，我们探索了利用领英数据衡量晋升中的种族、民族和性别差异。在对裁员和晋升的分析中，我们发现了所研究的一些公司存在歧视的暗示性证据，包括针对黑人和西班牙裔女性的“交叉性”歧视证据。

Abstract

Stronger enforcement of discrimination laws can help to reduce disparities in economic outcomes with respect to race, ethnicity, and gender in the United States. However, the data necessary to detect possible discrimination and to act to counter it is not publicly available – in particular, data on racial, ethnic, and gender disparities within specific companies. In this paper, we explore and develop methods to use information extracted from publicly available LinkedIn data to measure the racial, ethnic, and gender composition of company workforces. We use predictive tools based on both names and pictures to identify race, ethnicity, and gender. We show that one can use LinkedIn data to obtain reasonably reliable measures of workforce demographic composition by race, ethnicity, and gender, based on validation exercises comparing estimates from scraped LinkedIn data to two sources: ACS data, and company diversity or EEO-1 reports. We apply our methods to study the race, ethnic, and gender composition of workers who were hired and those who experienced mass layoffs at two large companies. Finally, we explore using LinkedIn data to measure race, ethnic, and gender differences in promotion. In our analyses of layoffs and promotions, we find suggestive evidence of discrimination at some of the companies we study, including evidence of “intersectional” discrimination against black and Hispanic women.

No. 2NBER w32293

Book Value Risk Management of Banks: Limited Hedging, HTM Accounting, and Rising Interest Rates

银行账面价值风险管理：有限对冲、持有至到期会计与利率上升

João Granja, Erica Xuewei Jiang, Gregor Matvos, Tomasz Piskorski, Amit Seru

Programs：AP, CF；JEL：G2, G21, G28

中文摘要

我们记录了在2022年利率上升时，银行限制了对其资产会计价值的影响，但使大多数长期资产暴露于利率风险之下。呼叫报告和证券交易委员会数据显示，只有约6%的美国银行资产通过衍生品进行了对冲，即使是最重度使用者也只对冲了其投资组合的一小部分。银行没有对冲市场价值风险，而是依赖持有至到期会计来保护账面资本，在利率开始上升后将大约1万亿美元的证券重新分类为持有至到期。更脆弱的银行，尤其是那些由监管较宽松的州级机构监管的银行，更有可能转向持有至到期。我们建立了一个简单模型，说明资本监管、会计规则和银行激励如何相互作用，决定银行是对冲利率风险还是进行资本重组。资本监管可以降低挤兑风险，尤其是在股东不愿对冲或筹集新资本时。持有至到期会计可以帮助资本充足的银行避免过于严格的资本要求带来的成本，但它也可能使较弱、资本适中的银行粉饰资本比率，使其容易遭受挤兑。在不考虑挤兑风险的情况下将存款特许经营权价值纳入监管资本，可能进一步削弱资本监管的有效性。这些发现对银行业监管资本会计和风险管理实践具有重要启示。

Abstract

We document that as interest rates rose in 2022, banks limited the impact on the accounting value of their assets but left most long-duration assets exposed to interest rate risk. Call report and SEC data show that only about 6% of U.S. banking assets were hedged with derivatives, and even the heaviest users hedged only a small share of their portfolios. Rather than hedge market-value risk, banks relied on held-to-maturity (HTM) accounting to shield book capital, reclassifying roughly $1 trillion of securities as HTM once rates began to rise. More vulnerable banks, especially those overseen by less stringent state regulators, were more likely to shift into HTM. We develop a simple model of how capital regulation, accounting rules, and bank incentives interact in determining whether banks hedge interest rate risk or recapitalize. Capital regulation can reduce run risk, particularly when equity holders are unwilling to hedge or raise new capital. HTM accounting can help well-capitalized banks avoid the costs of excessively tight capital requirements, but it can also enable weaker, moderately-capitalized, banks to window-dress capital ratios, leaving them susceptible to runs. Incorporating deposit franchise value into regulatory capital without considering run risk may further weaken the effectiveness of capital regulation. These findings carry important implications for regulatory capital accounting and risk-management practices across the banking sector.

No. 3NBER w32292

The Influence of Occupational Licensing on Workforce Transitions to Retirement

职业许可对劳动力向退休过渡的影响

Yun taek Oh, Morris M. Kleiner

Programs：LS；JEL：J0, J26, J3, J30, J32, J33, J40, J44

中文摘要

离开劳动力市场的方式一直是劳动力市场结果中研究不足的一个方面。职业许可等劳动力市场制度可能影响个人向退休过渡的方式。工人从职业生涯工作过渡到完全退休的时间和方式可能影响退休前后的福祉。以往对退休路径的研究侧重于退休过渡的模式和结果，但尚未分析职业许可对退休过渡的影响。在本研究中，我们使用当前人口调查和收入与项目参与调查来调查职业许可如何影响美国晚期职业工人对退休路径的选择。我们的结果表明，持有执照的工人在退出劳动力市场时，不太可能选择转行，但更可能减少工作时间。这些结果与持有执照的工人以更优退休选择形式获得更多福利的发现一致，表明这些工人即使在职业生涯末期也倾向于拥有更高的工资、福利和灵活性。

Abstract

Ways of leaving the labor force has been an understudied aspect of labor market outcomes. Labor market institutions such as occupational licensing may influence how individuals transition to retirement. When and how workers transition from career jobs to full retirement may contribute to pre- and post-retirement well-being. Previous investigations of retirement pathways focused on the patterns and outcomes of retirement transitions, yet the influence of occupational licensing on retirement transition has not been analyzed. In this study, we use the Current Population Survey and Survey of Income and Program Participation to investigate how occupational licensing influences American later-career workers’ choice of retirement pathways. Our results show that licensed workers are less likely to choose to change careers but more likely to reduce work hours in transitioning out of the workforce. These results are consistent with the findings that licensed workers receive more benefits in the form of preferable retirement options, suggesting that these workers tend to have higher wages, benefits, and flexibility even toward the end of their careers.

No. 4NBER w32291

Aggregate Demand Externality and Self-Fulfilling Default Cycles

总需求外部性与自我实现的违约周期

Jess Benhabib, Feng Dong, Pengfei Wang, Zhenyang Xu

Programs：EFG；JEL：E22, E32, E44, G12

中文摘要

我们建立了一个具有迪克西特-斯蒂格利茨式需求外部性的自我实现违约周期模型，以解释数据中观察到的反复出现的集群违约现象。文献报告称，仅凭可观测的基本面因素不足以解释这种集群现象。总产出的下降会降低企业价值并增加其违约概率。随着违约使更多企业退出生产，总产出进一步下降，形成正反馈循环，产生多重均衡和自我实现的违约周期。我们使用博格丹诺夫-塔肯斯分岔进行的全局分析揭示了存在多条甚至无限条满足所有均衡条件的路径。此外，在完美预见均衡中可能出现一族周期轨道。我们的模型与以下观点一致：经济周期很大程度上源于经济内部力量倾向于内生地产生周期性机制。

Abstract

We develop a model of self-fulfilling default cycles with demand externality a la Dixit- Stiglitz to explain the recurrent clustered defaults observed in the data. The literature reports that observable fundamental factors alone are insufficient to explain the cluster. A decline in aggregate output reduces the value of firms and increases their probability of default. As defaults take more firms out of production, aggregate output declines further, creating a positive feedback loop that generates multiple equilibria and self-fulfilling default cycles. Our global analysis using Bogdanov-Takens bifurcation reveals the existence of multiple or even infinite paths that satisfy all equilibrium conditions. Moreover, a family of periodic orbits can emerge in the perfect foresight equilibrium. Our model is consistent with the view that business cycles arise largely because the economy’s internal forces tend to endogenously generate cyclical mechanisms (Beaudry et al., 2020).

No. 5NBER w32290

Contracts with Interdependent Preferences

具有相互依赖偏好的契约

Debraj Ray, Marek Weretka

Programs：DEV, IO；JEL：D21, D90

中文摘要

一个委托人与多个代理人签订契约，如拉齐尔和罗森（1981）以及格林和斯托基（1983）所述。除了假设代理人具有相互依赖的偏好外，该设定是经典的。我们刻画了成本有效的契约，并将报酬共同变动方向——“连带责任”（正向）或“锦标赛”（负向）——与假定的偏好相互依赖结构联系起来。我们还研究了偏好相互依赖对委托人收益的影响。我们识别出两种不对称性。首先，最优契约倾向于连带责任而非锦标赛，尤其是在较大的团队中，其含义在文中精确阐述。其次，当机制设计问题通过旨在消除多重均衡的稳健性约束得到增强时，委托人可能偏好通过对抗性而非利他性偏好联系起来的团队。

Abstract

A principal contracts with multiple agents, as in Lazear and Rosen (1981) and Green and Stokey (1983). The setup is classical except for the assumption that agents have interdependent preferences. We characterize cost effective contracts, and relate the direction of co-movement in rewards — “joint liability” (positive) or “tournaments” (negative) — to the assumed structure of preference interdependence. We also study the implications of preference interdependence for the principal’s payoffs. We identify two asymmetries. First, the optimal contract leans towards joint liability rather than tournaments, especially in larger teams, in a sense made precise in the paper. Second, when the mechanism-design problem is augmented by robustness constraints designed to eliminate multiple equilibria, the principal may prefer teams linked via adversarial rather than altruistic preferences.

No. 6NBER w32289

Enforcement Risk and Screening in Rental Markets: Evidence from Eviction Moratoria

租赁市场中的执行风险与筛选：来自驱逐暂停令的证据

Alina Arefeva, Kay Jowers, Qihui Hu, Christopher Timmins

Programs：PE；JEL：D83, G18, G21, G32, J15, R31

中文摘要

我们将手工收集的州级驱逐暂停令数据与一项涵盖超过25,000次租赁咨询的全国性实地实验相结合，研究合同执行如何影响租赁住房市场中的筛选。利用暂停令到期的时间差异，我们表明当驱逐执行暂停时，物业经理对少数族裔租户的歧视加剧。通过将咨询与租户地址历史记录关联，我们发现未回复转化为不同的迁入模式，并通过放弃的租赁机会降低了业主的回报。一个简单的搜索模型将这些反应合理化，即物业经理在执行暂停时重新优化其行为。

Abstract

We combine a hand-collected dataset on state eviction moratoria with a nationwide field experiment of more than 25,000 rental inquiries to study how contract enforcement affects screening in rental housing markets. Using staggered moratorium expirations, we show that property managers discriminated more against minority renters when eviction enforcement was suspended. Linking inquiries to tenant address histories, we find that non-responses translated into different move-in patterns and reduced property owners’ returns through foregone leasing opportunities. A simple search model rationalizes these responses as property managers re-optimize when enforcement is suspended.

No. 7NBER w32288

How Does Expropriation Risk Affect Innovation?

征收风险如何影响创新？

Jose-Miguel Benavente, Claudio Bravo-Ortega, Pablo Egaña-delSol, Bronwyn H. Hall

Programs：DEV, PR；JEL：O17, O30, O50

中文摘要

我们基于罗默（1990）的模型，分析了征收风险如何降低创新激励并将资源从创新部门重新配置。我们的框架预测，由于存在征收风险，预期利润降低和专利贬值，导致研发支出、研发中的人力资本份额、专利数量、技术进步和经济增长均较低。基于LASSO工具变量方法和一个涵盖近二十年的新颖综合数据集的实证分析，证实了我们的理论预测。我们发现了稳健的证据，表明征收风险（如腐败）通过减少研发支出、研发中的人力资本、专利数量、科学出版物以及作为技术进步代理变量的经济复杂性指数，对创新产生负面影响。这些发现突显了征收风险在国家层面对创新和经济发展的不利影响。

Abstract

We analyze how expropriation risk reduces incentives for innovation and reallocates resources from the innovative sector, building on Romer’s(1990) model. Our framework predicts the R&D expenditure, the share of human capital in R&D, the number of patents, technical progress, and economic growth are all lower due to lower expected profits and patent devaluation in the presence of expropriation risks. Empirical analyses, based on a LASSO Instrumental Variable approach and a novel comprehensive dataset spanning nearly two decades, confirm our theoretical predictions. We find robust evidence that expropriation risk, such as corruption, negatively impacts innovation by reducing R&D expenditure, human capital in R&D, number of patents, scientific publications, and the Economic Complexity Index, which is our proxy for technical progress. These findings highlight the detrimental effects of expropriation risk on innovation and economic development at the country level.

No. 8NBER w32287

Blended Finance

混合融资

Caroline Flammer, Thomas Giroux, Geoffrey Heal

Programs：AP, CF, DEV, EEE, PE；JEL：G23, H41, O1, Q01, Q14

中文摘要

混合融资——利用公共和慈善资金撬动私人资本——已成为为可持续发展融资的潜在工具。然而，人们对混合融资契约的设计知之甚少。我们开发了一个概念框架，其中开发金融机构在项目间分配优惠资本，以最大化社会影响，同时撬动私人投资者。我们的框架强调了可持续性影响与使项目具有商业可行性所需的优惠程度之间的权衡，并刻画了开发金融机构如何最优地组合不同工具，包括优惠债务、次级股权层级和风险管理设施。然后，我们利用一家主要开发金融机构的交易层面数据，提供了关于混合融资的实证证据。与我们的概念框架一致，预期可持续性影响更高的项目获得了更大的优惠性。此外，在政治风险和不对称信息较高的国家，优惠性更高，并且这些项目更可能包含风险管理条款。我们的发现为开发金融机构如何部署催化资本以动员私人投资于可持续发展提供了新证据。

Abstract

Blended finance—the use of public and philanthropic funding to crowd in private capital—has emerged as a potential tool for financing sustainable development. Yet little is known about the design of blended finance contracts. We develop a conceptual framework in which development finance institutions (DFIs) allocate concessional capital across projects to maximize societal impact while crowding in private investors. Our framework highlights a trade-off between sustainability impact and the amount of concessionality required to make projects commercially viable and characterizes how DFIs optimally combine different instruments, including concessional debt, junior equity tranches, and risk management facilities. We then provide empirical evidence on blended finance using deal-level data from a major DFI. Consistent with our conceptual framework, projects with higher anticipated sustainability impact receive greater concessionality. Moreover, concessionality is higher in countries with greater political risk and information asymmetries, and these projects are more likely to include risk management provisions. Our findings provide new evidence on how DFIs deploy catalytic capital to mobilize private investment in sustainable development.

No. 9NBER w32286

Promoting Public Health with Blunt Instruments: Evidence from Vaccine Mandates

用钝器促进公共卫生：来自疫苗强制令的证据

Rahi Abouk, John S. Earle, Johanna Catherine Maclean, Sungbin Park

Programs：EH, LS, PE；JEL：H70, I1, I11, J20

中文摘要

我们研究了2021年美国通过的针对医疗行业从业者的新冠疫苗强制令的效果。美国医疗行业长期存在劳动力短缺问题，这早在新冠疫情之前就已存在。新冠疫苗强制令对劳动力短缺的影响在事前是不确定的。如果强制令提高了医疗行业的感知安全性，边际劳动者可能会被吸引到医疗行业，从而缓解短缺。另一方面，如果边际劳动者对疫苗犹豫或反感，那么强制令可能会将劳动者推离该行业，加剧短缺。我们将2021年至2022年当前人口调查的月度数据与双重差分方法相结合，研究州级疫苗强制令对在医疗行业工作概率以及进入和离开该行业的就业转换的影响。我们的研究结果表明，疫苗强制令可能加剧了医疗劳动力短缺：在州级强制令实施后，在医疗行业工作的概率下降了6%。这种效应在医疗特定职业的工人中更大，他们因强制令而离开该行业的比率更高，并且比非医疗职业的工人更慢被替代。研究结果揭示了寻求实现多重健康目标的卫生政策制定者所面临的权衡取舍。

Abstract

We study the effect of mandates requiring COVID-19 vaccination among healthcare industry workers adopted in 2021 in the United States. There are long-standing worker shortages in the U.S. healthcare industry, pre-dating the COVID-19 pandemic. The impact of COVID-19 vaccine mandates on shortages is ex ante ambiguous. If mandates increase perceived safety of the healthcare industry, marginal workers may be drawn to healthcare, relaxing shortages. On the other hand, if marginal workers are vaccine hesitant or averse, then mandates may push workers away from the industry and exacerbate shortages. We combine monthly data from the Current Population Survey 2021 to 2022 with difference-in-differences methods to study the effects of state vaccine mandates on the probability of working in healthcare, and of employment transitions into and out of the industry. Our findings suggest that vaccine mandates may have worsened healthcare workforce shortages: following adoption of a state-level mandate, the probability of working in the healthcare industry declines by 6%. Effects are larger among workers in healthcare-specific occupations, who leave the industry at higher rates in response to mandates and are slower to be replaced than workers in non-healthcare occupations. Findings suggest trade-offs faced by health policymakers seeking to achieve multiple health objectives.

No. 10NBER w32285

Explorations of Cumulative Advantage Using Data on French Physicists

利用法国物理学家数据探索累积优势

Bronwyn H. Hall, Jacques Mairesse

Programs：PR；JEL：J24, M5, O31, O38

中文摘要

我们使用一个包含法国国家科学研究中心（CNRS）约1500名物理学家的庞大数据集，研究累积优势在其发表生涯中的作用。通过以每位物理学家发表论文数量和引用加权论文数量的时间序列来衡量产出，我们发现累积优势预测的简单典型事实对这些物理学家仅微弱成立。然而，包含固定效应、生命周期效应和过去生产力的回归分析提供了强有力的证据，表明累积优势对未来生产力至关重要。在未来的工作中，我们计划将此样本与其他具有不同科学研究体系国家的样本进行比较。

Abstract

We use a large dataset of approximately 1500 physicists employed by the Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS) in France to investigate the role of cumulative advantage in their publication career. Measuring output by time series of the number of publications and the number of cite-weighted publications for each physicist, we find that the simple stylized facts predicted by cumulative advantage hold only weakly for these physicists. However, regressions with fixed effects, life cycle effects, and past productivity provide strong evidence that cumulative advantage matters for future productivity. In future work, we plan to compare this sample with those from other countries that have different systems for the conduct of scientific research.

No. 11NBER w32284

Deposit Insurance, Deposit Competition, and Bank Instability

存款保险、存款竞争与银行不稳定性

Matthew S. Jaremski, Steven Sprick Schuster

Programs：DAE；JEL：G21, H42, N22

中文摘要

在危机期间，如果未投保的储户将资金转移到更安全的机构，缺乏完全的存款保险覆盖可能导致额外的金融不稳定。由于现代系统的广泛覆盖使得这种动态难以检验，我们研究了美国邮政储蓄系统（该系统在某些邮局提供保险账户）在大萧条时期所扮演的角色。邮政储蓄与同一城镇银行储户提款的增加有关。使用识别邮政储蓄外生变化的工具变量，我们发现当附近邮局接受存款时，银行更有可能倒闭。

Abstract

The lack of full deposit insurance coverage might lead to additional financial instability during crises if uninsured depositors move funds to more secure institutions. Because the broad coverage of modern systems makes this dynamic hard to test, we study the role that the U.S. Postal Savings System, which provided insured accounts at some post offices, played in the Great Depression. Postal savings was associated with increased depositor withdrawals at banks in the same town. Using an IV that identifies exogenous variation in postal saving, we find that banks were more likely to close when a nearby post office accepted deposits.

No. 12NBER w32283

Project Development with Delegated Bargaining: The Role of Elevated Hurdle Rates

委托谈判下的项目开发：高门槛利率的作用

John W. Barry, Bruce I. Carlin, Alan D. Crane, John Graham

中文摘要
