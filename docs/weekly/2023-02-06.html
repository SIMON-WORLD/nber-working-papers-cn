Do Conflict of Interests Disclosures Work? Evidence from Citations in Medical Journals

利益冲突披露有效吗？来自医学期刊引用的证据

Christian Leuz, Anup Malani, Maximilian Muhn, Laszlo Jakab

Programs：CF, EH, LE；JEL：D83, D84, G18, K20, L51, M40, O31

Abstract

Financial ties between drug companies and medical researchers are thought to bias studies published in medical journals. To enable readers to account for such bias, most medical journals require authors to disclose potential conflicts of interest. We examine whether disclosure reduces article citations, indicating a discount. A challenge to estimating this effect is selection as drug companies may seek out higher quality authors. Our analysis confirms this positive association. Including observable controls for article and author quality attenuates but does not eliminate this relation. We perform three tests. First, we show that the positive association is weaker for review articles, which are more susceptible to bias. Second, we examine article recommendations to family physicians among articles that are a priori more homogenous in quality. We find a significantly negative association between disclosure and expert recommendations, consistent with discounting. Third, we conduct an analysis within author and article, exploiting journal policy changes that result in conflict disclosure by an author. We examine the effect of this disclosure on citations to a previously published article by the same author. This analysis reveals a negative citation effect. Overall, our evidence is consistent with the notion that other researchers discount articles with disclosed conflicts.

中文摘要

制药公司与医学研究人员之间的财务关系被认为会偏倚医学期刊发表的研究。为使读者能够考虑这种偏倚，大多数医学期刊要求作者披露潜在的利益冲突。我们考察披露是否会减少文章引用，从而表明存在折扣。估计这一效应的挑战在于选择性问题，因为制药公司可能寻求更高质量的作者。我们的分析证实了这种正相关关系。纳入文章和作者质量的可观测控制变量会减弱但不会消除这种关系。我们进行了三项检验。首先，我们表明，对于更容易受偏倚影响的综述文章，这种正相关关系较弱。其次，我们在先验质量更同质的文章中，考察了向家庭医生推荐的文章。我们发现，披露与专家推荐之间存在显著的负相关关系，这与折扣效应一致。第三，我们在作者和文章内部进行分析，利用导致作者披露利益冲突的期刊政策变化。我们考察了这种披露对同一作者先前发表文章引用的影响。该分析揭示了负面的引用效应。总体而言，我们的证据与其他研究者对披露冲突的文章打折扣的观点一致。

No. 2NBER w30926

Harm Reduction: When Does It Improve Health, and When Does it Backfire?

减害：何时改善健康，何时适得其反？

John Cawley, Davide Dragone

Programs：EH；JEL：D11, I12, I18

Abstract

Some harm reduction strategies encourage individuals to switch from a harmful addictive good to a less harmful addictive good; examples include e-cigarettes (substitutes for combustible cigarettes) and methadone and buprenorphine (substitutes for opioids). These have proven to be controversial. Advocates argue that people struggling with addiction benefit because they can switch to a less harmful substance, but opponents argue that this could encourage abstainers to begin using the harm-reduction method or even, eventually, the original addictive good. This paper builds on theories of addiction to model the introduction of a harm reduction method, and demonstrates the conditions under which each side is correct.

中文摘要

一些减害策略鼓励个体从有害成瘾品转向危害较小的成瘾品；例如电子烟（可燃香烟的替代品）以及美沙酮和丁丙诺啡（阿片类药物的替代品）。这些策略已被证明存在争议。支持者认为，挣扎于成瘾问题的人可以转而使用危害较小的物质而受益，但反对者认为，这可能会鼓励原本不使用者开始使用减害方法，甚至最终使用原始成瘾品。本文基于成瘾理论，对减害方法的引入进行建模，并论证了每一方观点成立的条件。

Which Firms Gain from Digital Advertising? Evidence from a Field Experiment

哪些企业从数字广告中获益？来自实地实验的证据

Weijia Dai, Hyunjin Kim, Michael Luca

Programs：PR；JEL：D8, M3

Abstract

Measuring the returns of advertising opportunities continues to be a challenge for many businesses. We design and run a field experiment in collaboration with Yelp across 18,294 firms in the restaurant industry to understand which types of businesses gain more from digital advertising. We randomly assign 7,209 restaurants to freely receive Yelp’s standard ads package for three months. The scale of the experiment gives us a unique opportunity to assess the heterogeneity in advertising effectiveness across a variety of business attributes. We find that restaurants that receive advertising on Yelp observe on average a 7-19% increase in a wide range of purchase intention outcomes, as well as a 5% increase in customer reviews. We find that gains are heterogeneous across firms, with independent and higher-rated businesses observing larger gains, as well as those with more reviews and higher pre-experiment organic traffic.

中文摘要

衡量广告机会的回报对许多企业来说仍然是一个挑战。我们与Yelp合作，在餐饮行业的18294家企业中设计并开展了一项实地实验，以了解哪些类型的企业从数字广告中获益更多。我们随机分配7209家餐厅免费获得Yelp的标准广告套餐三个月。实验的规模为我们评估广告效果在不同企业属性间的异质性提供了独特机会。我们发现，在Yelp上投放广告的餐厅，在一系列购买意愿指标上平均增长了7-19%，同时客户评论数量增加了5%。我们发现，收益在企业间存在异质性，独立经营和评分较高的企业收益更大，评论数量较多以及实验前自然流量较高的企业也是如此。

No. 4NBER w30924

New Institutional Economics and Cliometrics

新制度经济学与计量史学

Eric C. Alston, Lee J. Alston, Bernardo Mueller

Programs：DAE；JEL：B52, F63, N01, P0, P50

Abstract

The New Institutional Economics (NIE) has its early roots in Cliometrics. Cliometrics began with a focus on using neoclassical theory to develop and test hypotheses in economic history. But empirical consideration of economic and political development within and across countries is limited, absent consideration of the institutional context. The NIE as applied in economic history first focused on the roles of transaction costs and property rights. From this micro-institutional perspective, the NIE expanded its focus to the role of institutions and norms on economic development as well as how economic forces along with political institutional variance influences outcomes both within and across countries. This involves considering both forces that impede and promote economic and political convergence across countries as well the forces that determine a transition to a new economic or political trajectory altogether. Testing for the determinants of economic and political development is plagued with omitted variables and endogeneity concerns, a constraint which has recently prompted scholars to draw on complexity theory to further supplement the NIE and Cliometrics.

中文摘要

新制度经济学（NIE）的早期根源在于计量史学。计量史学最初侧重于运用新古典理论来提出和检验经济史中的假说。但若不考虑制度背景，对国内及跨国经济与政治发展的实证研究就会受到限制。应用于经济史的NIE首先关注交易成本和产权的作用。从这种微观制度视角出发，NIE将其关注点扩展到制度与规范对经济发展的作用，以及经济力量与政治制度差异如何共同影响国内及跨国结果。这涉及考虑阻碍和促进各国经济与政治趋同的力量，以及决定向全新经济或政治轨迹转型的力量。检验经济与政治发展的决定因素受到遗漏变量和内生性问题的困扰，这一限制近期促使学者们借鉴复杂性理论，以进一步补充NIE和计量史学。

No. 5NBER w30923

Bitcoin Mining Meets Wall Street: A Study of Publicly Traded Crypto Mining Companies

比特币挖矿遇上华尔街：对上市加密矿企的研究

Hanna Halaburda, David Yermack

Programs：AP, CF, EEE, ME, PR；JEL：G23, L23, L94

Abstract

This paper studies the operations and financial valuations of 13 cryptocurrency mining companies that are listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange and have facilities in North America. We find that miners using Texas wind power are offline more than other miners, in a more erratic pattern. Yet, despite having relatively low activity levels, these Texas miners are more profitable than those using more stable sources of energy such as hyrdo power or solar power, as reflected in significantly higher enterprise values. Our model shows that miners using sustainable energy may be more profitable than those using conventional sources, despite the shutdowns, as they benefit from extremely low prices when there is oversupply of sustainable energy (e.g., strong winds). The model also shows that it may be beneficial for the electric utility to offer miners compensation for curtailment of their activity when there is undersupply of energy (e.g. lack of wind), which we also observe in our sample. This compensation further increases profits of the miners. We find a negative and significant beta between crypto mining stocks and an index of electric utilities, suggesting that ownership of a crypto mining company might provide a useful channel for risk management in the electric power industry.

中文摘要

本文研究了13家在纳斯达克上市且设施位于北美的加密货币挖矿公司的运营和财务估值。我们发现，使用德克萨斯州风电的矿工比其他矿工离线时间更长，且模式更不稳定。然而，尽管活动水平相对较低，这些德克萨斯州的矿工比使用水电或太阳能等更稳定能源的矿工更盈利，这体现在显著更高的企业价值上。我们的模型表明，使用可持续能源的矿工可能比使用传统能源的矿工更盈利，尽管存在停机，因为他们受益于可持续能源（如强风）供应过剩时的极低价格。该模型还表明，当能源供应不足（如无风）时，电力公司向矿工提供补偿以削减其活动可能是有益的，我们在样本中也观察到了这一点。这种补偿进一步增加了矿工的利润。我们发现加密挖矿股票与电力公用事业指数之间存在显著负贝塔值，这表明持有加密挖矿公司的股份可能为电力行业提供有用的风险管理渠道。

No. 6NBER w30922

Distorted Innovation: Does the Market Get the Direction of Technology Right?

扭曲的创新：市场能否正确引导技术方向？

Daron Acemoglu

Programs：IFM, LS, PR；JEL：C65, J23, J24, L65, O14, O31, O33

Abstract

In the presence of markup differences, externalities and other social considerations, the equilibrium direction of innovation can be systematically distorted. This paper builds a simple model of endogenous technology, which generalizes existing comparative static results and characterizes potential distortions in the direction of innovation. I show that empirical findings across a number of different areas are consistent with this framework's predictions and I use data from several studies to estimate its key parameters. Combining these numbers with rough estimates of differential externalities and markups, I provide suggestive evidence that equilibrium distortions in the direction of technology can be substantial in the context of industrial automation, health care, and energy, and correcting these distortions could have sizable welfare benefits.

中文摘要

在存在加价差异、外部性及其他社会考量的情况下，创新的均衡方向可能被系统性扭曲。本文构建了一个简单的内生技术模型，该模型概括了现有的比较静态结果，并刻画了创新方向上的潜在扭曲。我表明，多个不同领域的实证发现与该框架的预测一致，并利用多项研究的数据估计了其关键参数。将这些数字与差异外部性和加价的粗略估计相结合，我提供了提示性证据，表明在工业自动化、医疗保健和能源领域，技术方向的均衡扭曲可能相当严重，而纠正这些扭曲可能带来可观的福利收益。

No. 7NBER w30921

Back to the 1980s or Not? The Drivers of Inflation and Real Risks in Treasury Bonds

回到1980年代还是并非如此？国债通胀风险与真实风险的驱动因素

Carolin Pflueger

Programs：AP, ME；JEL：E0, E31, E40, G10, G12

Abstract

I use nominal and real bond risks as new moments to discipline a New Keynesian asset pricing model, where supply shocks, demand shocks, and monetary policy are the fundamental drivers of inflation. Endogenously time-varying risk premia imply that nominal bond risks—as measured by their stock market beta—are a forward-looking indicator of stagflation risks. Calibrating the model separately for the 1980s and the 2000s, I show that positive nominal bond betas in the 1980s resulted from a “perfect storm” of supply shocks and a reactive monetary policy rule, but not from either supply shocks or monetary policy in isolation.

中文摘要

我使用名义和真实债券风险作为新的矩条件来约束一个新凯恩斯主义资产定价模型，其中供给冲击、需求冲击和货币政策是通胀的基本驱动因素。内生时变的风险溢价意味着名义债券风险——以其股票市场贝塔值衡量——是滞胀风险的前瞻性指标。分别针对1980年代和2000年代校准模型后，我表明，1980年代的正名义债券贝塔值是由供给冲击和反应性货币政策规则的“完美风暴”共同导致的，而非供给冲击或货币政策单独作用的结果。

No. 8NBER w30920

Digital Hermits

数字隐士

Jeanine Miklós-Thal, Avi Goldfarb, Avery M. Haviv, Catherine Tucker

Programs：IO, PR；JEL：L5, L51, L86, M3

Abstract

When a user shares multi-dimensional data about themselves with a firm, the firm learns about the correlations of different dimensions of user data. We incorporate this type of learning into a model of a data market in which a firm acquires data from users with privacy concerns. User data is multi-dimensional, and each user can share no data, only non-sensitive data, or their full data with the firm. As the firm collects more data and becomes better at drawing inferences about a user’s privacy-sensitive data from their non-sensitive data, the share of new users who share no data (“digital hermits”) grows. At the same time, the share of new users who share their full data also grows. The model therefore predicts a polarization of users’ data sharing choices away from non-sensitive data sharing to no sharing and full sharing.

中文摘要

当用户向企业分享关于自身的多维数据时，企业会学习用户数据不同维度之间的相关性。我们将这种学习行为纳入一个数据市场模型，在该模型中，企业从有隐私担忧的用户那里获取数据。用户数据是多维的，每个用户可以选择不分享任何数据、仅分享非敏感数据或分享全部数据给企业。随着企业收集更多数据并更擅长从用户的非敏感数据推断其隐私敏感数据，不分享任何数据的新用户（“数字隐士”）比例会增长。同时，分享全部数据的新用户比例也会增长。因此，该模型预测用户的数据分享选择会出现两极分化，从分享非敏感数据转向不分享和分享全部数据。

No. 9NBER w30919

Task Mismatch and Salary Penalties: Evidence from the Biomedical PhD Labor Market

任务错配与薪资惩罚：来自生物医学博士劳动力市场的证据

Holden A. Diethorn, Gerald R. Marschke

Programs：ED, LS, PR；JEL：I26, J24, J31, J44

Abstract

We use the labor market for doctorates in the biomedical sciences, where career dislocation is common, as a case study of skill-task mismatch and its consequences. Using longitudinal, worker-level data on biomedical doctorates, we investigate mismatch as an explanation for the negative pecuniary returns to postdoc training. Our data contain unique worker-level job task information that allows us to compare the skills acquired in the years just after graduation to the tasks required in later employment. Our findings reveal a postdoc salary penalty when task mismatch is high, which is frequent, and a salary premium when skills align with tasks. Differences in accumulated task-specific human capital explain the between-sector heterogeneity in the returns to postdoctoral training, including the large and persistent salary penalties from postdoctoral training in industry, and the penalty overall. Task mismatch as a cost of pursuing risky careers in science and in other fields requiring large upfront investments in task-specific human capital has received little attention in the empirical labor literature.

中文摘要

我们以生物医学科学博士的劳动力市场（其中职业错位很常见）作为技能-任务错配及其后果的案例研究。利用生物医学博士的纵向个体层面数据，我们研究错配作为博士后培训负货币回报的解释。我们的数据包含独特的个体层面工作任务信息，使我们能够比较毕业几年后获得的技能与后续就业中所需的任务。我们的发现揭示了当任务错配程度高时（这种情况很常见）存在博士后薪资惩罚，而当技能与任务匹配时则存在薪资溢价。累积的任务特定人力资本差异解释了博士后培训回报的部门间异质性，包括产业界博士后培训带来的巨大且持久的薪资惩罚以及总体上的惩罚。任务错配作为追求科学及其他需要大量前期投资于任务特定人力资本的冒险职业的成本，在实证劳动文献中很少受到关注。

No. 10NBER w30918

Financial Crisis in a Socialist Setting: Impact on Political Behavior, Social Trust, and Economic Values

社会主义背景下的金融危机：对政治行为、社会信任和经济价值观的影响

Ran Abramitzky, Netanel Ben-Porath, Victor Lavy, Michal Palgi

Programs：CH, DAE, ED, LS, POL；JEL：J0, P00

Abstract

Research on the political and social impacts of financial crises has focused chiefly on free market economies, hindering our understanding of their effects in other settings. We exploit an episode of a financial crisis that hit the Israeli kibbutzim to study its impact in a socialist context. Contrary to findings in capitalistic economies, the crisis led to increased support of liberalized labor markets and reduced support for leftist political parties. These effects persisted in the long run, especially among the young. The crisis also reduced trust in leadership, but trust was restored shortly after agreements to settle the debt were signed, relieving the severity of the crisis. Our findings suggest that economic shocks may have different effects in a free market and socialist systems, in both cases leading individuals to question their current system.

中文摘要

关于金融危机政治和社会影响的研究主要集中于自由市场经济，这阻碍了我们对其他背景下其影响的理解。我们利用一场袭击以色列基布兹的金融危机事件，研究其在社会主义背景下的影响。与资本主义经济中的发现相反，这场危机导致对自由化劳动力市场的支持增加，并减少了对左翼政党的支持。这些影响在长期内持续存在，尤其是在年轻人中。危机还降低了对领导层的信任，但在签署债务清偿协议以缓解危机严重性后不久，信任得以恢复。我们的发现表明，经济冲击在自由市场和社会主义体系中可能产生不同影响，在两种情况下都会导致个体质疑他们当前的体系。

No. 11NBER w30917

Reversals and the Returns to Liquidity Provision

反转与流动性供给的回报

Wei Dai, Mamdouh Medhat, Robert Novy-Marx, Savina Rizova

Programs：AP；JEL：G10, G12

Abstract

Different aspects of liquidity impact the performance of short-run reversals in different ways, consistent with the predictions of microstructure models. Higher volatility is associated with faster, initially stronger reversals, while lower turnover is associated with more persistent, ultimately stronger reversals. These facts also hold outside the US and explain several seemingly disparate results in the literature.

中文摘要

流动性的不同方面以不同方式影响短期反转的表现，这与微观结构模型的预测一致。较高的波动性与更快、初始更强的反转相关，而较低的换手率与更持久、最终更强的反转相关。这些事实在美国以外也成立，并解释了文献中几个看似不同的结果。

No. 12NBER w30916

Missing Discussions: Institutional Constraints in the Islamic Political Tradition

缺失的讨论：伊斯兰政治传统中的制度约束

A. Arda Gitmez, James A. Robinson, Mehdi Shadmehr

Programs：POL；JEL：D70, D72, D78, H11

Abstract

Institutional constraints to prevent abuses of power have been considered essential in the West. An intellectual tradition emerged to justify them. We identify a puzzle: such an intellectual tradition did not exist in the Islamic world, despite the recognition of potential for abuse. We develop a model to explain this difference in normative traditions. Islamic law was more encompassing than divine law in the West, making it easier for citizens to identify and address abuses of power through collective action. Islamic tradition’s core assumptions made the collective action approach to accountability preferable to the institutional approach favored in the West.

中文摘要

防止权力滥用的制度约束在西方被认为至关重要，并产生了一种为其辩护的知识传统。我们识别出一个谜题：在伊斯兰世界，尽管认识到滥用的可能性，但这样的知识传统并不存在。我们开发了一个模型来解释这种规范传统的差异。伊斯兰法比西方的神法更具包容性，这使得公民更容易通过集体行动识别和应对权力滥用。伊斯兰传统的核心假设使得集体行动的问责方法比西方偏好的制度方法更受青睐。

No. 13NBER w30915

Growth and Risk: A View from International Trade

增长与风险：来自国际贸易的视角

Pravin Krishna, Andrei A. Levchenko, Lin Ma, William F. Maloney

Programs：EFG, ITI；JEL：F14, O3, O4

Abstract

This paper studies the cross-country patterns of risky innovation and growth through the lens of international trade. We use a simple theoretical framework of risky quality upgrading by firms under varying levels of financial development to derive two predictions. Abstract

Programs：LS；JEL：J21, J39, J64, J68

Abstract

How has the internet affected search and hiring, and what are the implications for aggregate unemployment? Answering these questions empirically has proven difficult due to selection in internet use and difficulty in measuring the search activities of both sides of the labor market. This paper overcomes these challenges by combining plausibly exogenous variation in the availability of high-speed internet in Norway with large-scale survey and administrative data on hiring firms, job seekers, and vacancies. Our empirical analysis shows that the internet expansion led more firms to recruit online and caused 9% shorter vacancy durations and 13% fewer unsuccessful hiring attempts. While the expansion increased job-finding rates by 2.4% and starting wages by 6% among the unemployed, we find no evidence of changes in job-to-job mobility or wage growth for employees. Through the lens of the calibrated model, we find that better search technology is the main driving force behind our quasi-experimental evidence. Our calculations indicate that the steady-state unemployment rate fell by as much as 14% due to the broadband internet expansion.

Abstract

We document a robust dynamic inconsistency in risky choice. Using a unique brokerage dataset and a series of experiments, we compare people's initial risk-taking plans to their subsequent decisions. Abstract
