The Heterogeneous Earnings Impact of Job Loss Across Workers, Establishments, and Markets

失业对工人、企业和市场的异质性收入影响

Susan Athey, Lisa K. Simon, Oskar Nordström Skans, Johan Vikström, Yaroslav Yakymovych

Programs：LS；JEL：C45, J21, J31, J65

Abstract

Using rich Swedish administrative data, we apply causal machine learning methods to study how earnings losses after job displacement vary with observable characteristics that may be relevant for targeting policy interventions for workers. Heterogeneity in effects is as large within as across worker groups defined by age and schooling, and as large within as across establishments. A substantial portion of cross-establishment heterogeneity can be explained by industry and local labor market characteristics, suggesting a role for place- and industry-based targeting. The largest losses are concentrated among already vulnerable workers, indicating that well-designed targeting policies can improve both efficiency and equity.

中文摘要

利用丰富的瑞典行政数据，我们应用因果机器学习方法研究失业后收入损失如何随可观察特征变化，这些特征可能与针对工人的政策干预目标定位相关。效应的异质性在按年龄和受教育程度划分的工人群体内部与群体之间一样大，在企业内部与企业之间也一样大。跨企业异质性的很大一部分可以由行业和当地劳动力市场特征来解释，这表明基于地点和行业的定位策略可以发挥作用。最大的损失集中在已经脆弱的工人身上，这表明设计良好的定位政策可以提高效率和公平性。

The Elasticity of Corporate Taxable Income Across Countries

跨国企业应税收入弹性

Claudio Agostini, Zareh Asatryan, Laurent Bach, Govindadeva Bernier, Marinho Bertanha, Katarzyna A. Bilicka, Anne Brockmeyer, Jaroslav Bukovina, Guillermo Falcone, Pablo Garriga, Yuxuan He, Petr Janský, Evangelos Koumanakos, Tomáš Lichard, Tomás Martins, Ján Palguta, Elena Patel, João Pereira dos Santos, Louis Perrault, Thomas Schwab, Nathan Seegert, Oliver Škultéty, Kristina Strohmaier, Maximilian Todtenhaupt, Guillermo Vuletin, Branislav Žúdel

Programs：PE；JEL：C14, H25

Abstract

Do firms respond similarly to corporate tax incentives across countries? We provide globally comparable estimates of the corporate elasticity of taxable income using administrative tax return data from sixteen countries and a unified empirical framework. Exploiting bunching at a common kink, zero taxable income, we estimate elasticities ranging from 0.08 to 1.9, with an average of 0.79. To explain this heterogeneity, we link elasticities to tax policy, firm characteristics, and country fundamentals. These differences imply that identical corporate tax reforms can generate sharply different revenue effects across countries, leading to substantial heterogeneity in the efficiency costs of corporate taxation.

中文摘要

企业是否对不同国家的企业所得税激励措施做出相似的反应？我们利用来自十六个国家的行政纳税申报数据和一个统一的实证框架，提供了具有全球可比性的企业应税收入弹性估计。利用在共同拐点（零应税收入）处的聚集现象，我们估计出的弹性范围从0.08到1.9，平均值为0.79。为了解释这种异质性，我们将弹性与税收政策、企业特征和国家基本面因素联系起来。这些差异意味着，相同的企业所得税改革在不同国家可能产生截然不同的收入效应，从而导致企业所得税效率成本的巨大异质性。

Feel as a Determinant of College Choice: Evidence from Campus Tour Weather

感觉作为大学选择的决定因素：来自校园参观天气的证据

Olivia Feldman, Joshua M. Hyman, Matthew L. McGann

Programs：ED；JEL：I20, I23

Abstract

The feeling or impression that students get about enrolling in a particular college may be an important determinant of their college application decision. Combining institutional records on college campus tour participants over the last decade with hourly weather information, we leverage tour weather as a plausibly exogenous shock to students' "feel" for attending the toured college. We find that poor tour weather reduces participants’ likelihood of applying. Tour participants, for example, are 10 percent less likely to apply when their tour is hot and 8 percent less likely when precipitation occurs during their tour. Using administrative data documenting where all tour participants enroll in college, we find that tour weather has little to no impact on the quality or type of college that participants ultimately attend. Nevertheless, our results suggest that students' "feel" for attending a college can play an important role in the college application decision.

中文摘要

学生在就读某所特定大学时获得的感觉或印象，可能是其大学申请决策的重要决定因素。结合过去十年大学校园参观参与者的机构记录与逐时天气信息，我们将参观天气作为对学生就读所参观大学“感觉”的一个外生冲击。我们发现，糟糕的参观天气会降低参与者申请的可能性。例如，当参观天气炎热时，参与者申请的可能性降低10%；当参观期间出现降水时，申请可能性降低8%。利用记录所有参观参与者最终就读大学情况的行政数据，我们发现参观天气对参与者最终就读大学的质量或类型几乎没有影响。尽管如此，我们的结果表明，学生对就读某所大学的“感觉”在大学申请决策中可能扮演重要角色。

No. 4NBER w34943

The Changing Landscape of International Development: An Introduction

国际发展格局的变迁：导论

Pascaline Dupas, Pinelopi Koujianou Goldberg, Rohini Pande

Programs：DEV；JEL：O1, O4, P0

Abstract

Since the late 1980s, extreme poverty has declined sharply, life expectancy and schooling have increased, and electoral democracy has expanded. However poverty reduction has slowed in recent years, particularly following the COVID-19 pandemic, amid intensifying conflict, fragility, climate risks, democratic backsliding, and the erosion of global trends—including trade integration and geopolitical stability—that once supported growth. These dynamics raise three interrelated questions: what barriers impede further progress; where will future growth in lower-income countries come from; and how can growth be broadly shared. Taking stock of 15 chapters forthcoming in Volume 6 of the Handbook of Development Economics, we discuss how external conditions, state capacity and policy choices shape development; analyze the shifting growth drivers, including trade, technology and the rise of services; discuss persistent inequality and distributional tensions; and conjecture that investing in institutions and people pays off.

中文摘要

自20世纪80年代末以来，极端贫困大幅下降，预期寿命和受教育年限有所增加，选举民主得以扩展。然而，近年来减贫速度放缓，尤其是在新冠疫情之后，同时伴随着冲突加剧、脆弱性、气候风险、民主倒退以及曾支撑增长的全球趋势（包括贸易一体化和地缘政治稳定）的侵蚀。这些动态引发了三个相互关联的问题：哪些障碍阻碍了进一步进展；低收入国家未来的增长将从何而来；以及增长如何能被广泛共享。基于对即将出版的《发展经济学手册》第6卷中15个章节的总结，我们讨论了外部条件、国家能力和政策选择如何塑造发展；分析了包括贸易、技术和服务业崛起在内的增长驱动因素的转变；讨论了持续存在的不平等和分配紧张局势；并推测投资于制度和人力资本会带来回报。

Dollarization Waves: New Evidence From a Comprehensive International Bond Database

美元化浪潮：来自一个全面国际债券数据库的新证据

Swapan-Kumar Pradhan, Eswar S. Prasad, Előd Takáts, Judit Temesvary

Programs：IFM；JEL：F3, F41, G15

Abstract

We investigate how the U.S. dollar’s prominence in the denomination of international debt securities has evolved in recent decades, using a comprehensive global dataset with far more extensive coverage than datasets used in prior literature. We find no monotonic dollarization or de-dollarization trend; instead, the dollar’s share exhibits a wavelike pattern. We document three dollarization waves since the 1960s. The last wave, following the global financial crisis, lifted the dollar’s share nearly back to its level at the euro’s launch in 2000. We show that closer alignment of a country’s domestic currency to a reserve currency (e.g., the U.S. dollar) correlates with higher shares of issuance in that currency. Our findings are robust to composition and currency valuation effects as well as alternative data definitions.

中文摘要

我们利用一个覆盖范围远超以往文献所用数据集的全面全球数据集，研究了美元在国际债务证券计价中的主导地位近几十年来如何演变。我们没有发现单调的美元化或去美元化趋势；相反，美元的份额呈现出波浪状模式。我们记录了自20世纪60年代以来的三次美元化浪潮。最近一次浪潮，即全球金融危机之后，将美元的份额几乎推回到了2000年欧元启动时的水平。我们表明，一国本币与储备货币（例如美元）的挂钩程度越紧密，与该国以该货币发行的债券份额越高相关。我们的发现对于构成效应、货币估值效应以及替代性数据定义都是稳健的。

Institutional Shocks and Migration Adjustment: Evidence from the Syrian Crisis and Brexit’s Referendum and Implementation

制度冲击与移民调整：来自叙利亚危机以及英国脱欧公投与实施的证据

Assaf Razin

Programs：AP, IFM；JEL：F0, F29, F47, P0, P5

Abstract

This paper interprets Brexit as a two-stage institutional rupture that reshaped migration through expectations, exposure, and stress channels. Using a UK–Germany Difference-in-Differences framework, I show that the 2016 referendum generated a sharp decline in UK net migration relative to Germany, driven primarily by a contraction in immigration rather than simple outward flight. The 2021 termination of EU free movement reinforced this adjustment on the gross inflow margin, completing the structural reallocation of mobility flows. Migration responses were not uniform: countries more deeply integrated into EU mobility networks prior to 2016 experienced significantly stronger contractions, confirming that Brexit propagated along pre-existing exposure channels.

中文摘要

本文将英国脱欧解释为一个两阶段的制度断裂，通过预期、暴露和压力渠道重塑了移民格局。利用英国-德国双重差分框架，我表明，与德国相比，2016年公投导致英国净移民急剧下降，这主要是由移民流入的收缩而非简单的向外逃离所驱动。2021年欧盟自由流动的终止在总流入维度上强化了这一调整，完成了流动性流动的结构性重新配置。移民反应并非一致：在2016年之前更深地融入欧盟流动性网络的国家经历了显著更强的收缩，这证实了英国脱欧是沿着既有的暴露渠道传播的。

No. 7NBER w34940

Market Feedback about Emerging Technologies

关于新兴技术的市场反馈

Sean S. Cao, Itay Goldstein, Jie He, Yabo Zhao

Programs：AP, CF；JEL：D22, E22, G14, G31, J23

Abstract

How do firms make decisions on investments in emerging technologies? We find that firms adjust AI/green investments in response to market reactions to announcements of such emerging-technology investment plans. Through a battery of tests, we show that this pattern is more likely due to active managerial learning from the market than alternative explanations such as a passive reflection of underlying fundamentals that drive both market reactions and corporate actions. Firms are more likely to act on feedback about emerging technologies than about other investments, particularly when market participants have more expertise in these areas and when firms face greater uncertainty.

中文摘要

企业如何做出关于新兴技术投资的决策？我们发现，企业会根据市场对其宣布此类新兴技术投资计划的反应来调整人工智能/绿色投资。通过一系列检验，我们表明，这种模式更可能是由于管理者主动从市场学习，而非其他解释，例如被动反映同时驱动市场反应和企业行为的潜在基本面。与对其他投资相比，企业更有可能根据关于新兴技术的反馈采取行动，尤其是在市场参与者对这些领域拥有更多专业知识以及企业面临更大不确定性的时候。

No. 8NBER w34939

The Skill Premium in Times of Rapid Technological Change

技术快速变革时期的技能溢价

Tarek Alexander Hassan, Aakash Kalyani, Pascual Restrepo

Programs：EFG, LS, PR；JEL：E0, J2

Abstract

This paper shows that the pace of technology creation is a key driver of the skill premium. It develops a model in which skilled workers have a comparative advantage in learning new technologies. As technologies age, they become standardized and accessible to other workers. The skill premium is determined by the interplay between the pace of technology creation and standardization. A rapid pace of technology creation leads to a sustained increase in the skill premium. We calibrate the model using novel text-based data on new technologies and their changing demand for skills as they age. These data show that new technologies are initially skill intensive but become less so as they age. The data also point to an increased pace of new technology creation starting in the 1970s and tapering off in the 2000s. In response to this rapid pace of technology creation, the model generates a 32 percent increase in the college premium, which begins to reverse in the 2010s. Our framework also explains why the college premium is higher in dense cities, why its increase was mainly urban, and why it rose first for young workers and later for older workers.

中文摘要

本文表明，技术创造的速度是技能溢价的一个关键驱动因素。它构建了一个模型，其中技能型工人在学习新技术方面具有比较优势。随着技术老化，它们变得标准化，其他工人也能掌握。技能溢价由技术创造和标准化的速度之间的相互作用决定。快速的技术创造速度导致技能溢价的持续上升。我们利用基于文本的新技术数据以及这些技术随年龄增长对技能需求的变化来校准模型。这些数据表明，新技术最初是技能密集型的，但随着时间推移会变得不那么技能密集。数据还指出，新技术创造的速度从20世纪70年代开始加快，在21世纪初逐渐放缓。作为对这种快速技术创造速度的回应，该模型产生了32%的大学溢价的增长，这一增长在2010年代开始逆转。我们的框架还解释了为什么大学溢价在人口密集的城市更高，为什么其增长主要发生在城市，以及为什么它首先在年轻工人中上升，后来才在年长工人中上升。

No. 9NBER w34938

Reducing Waste through Anti-Fraud Enforcement: Evidence from Hospital Admission Cases

通过反欺诈执法减少浪费：来自医院入院案例的证据

David H. Howard, Jetson Leder-Luis

Programs：AG, EH, LE；JEL：D73, I18, K42

Abstract

The use of federal anti-fraud laws to address unnecessary medical care is controversial. Targeted providers frequently argue that, in their judgment, the treatment in question was appropriate. We examine the effects of anti-fraud litigation against hospitals for over-admitting patients from the emergency department, using 100% Medicare claims for 2005-2019 and a design based on the staggered rollout of these lawsuits. We find that anti-fraud lawsuits reduced admission rates by 3.6 percentage points without increasing mortality rates. We estimate five-year savings to Medicare of $1.3 billion. Our results suggest that anti-fraud enforcement can be successful in reducing costly, unnecessary care.

中文摘要

利用联邦反欺诈法律来解决不必要的医疗护理问题是有争议的。被针对的医疗服务提供者经常辩称，根据他们的判断，所涉治疗是适当的。我们利用2005-2019年100%的医疗保险索赔数据，并基于这些诉讼交错推出的设计，研究了针对医院从急诊科过度收治患者的反欺诈诉讼的影响。我们发现，反欺诈诉讼使入院率降低了3.6个百分点，同时并未增加死亡率。我们估计，这为医疗保险在五年内节省了13亿美元。我们的结果表明，反欺诈执法可以成功地减少昂贵且不必要的护理。

No. 10NBER w34937

General Social Agents

通用社会智能体

Benjamin S. Manning, John J. Horton

Programs：PR；JEL：D01, D03

Abstract

Useful social science theories predict behavior across settings. However, applying a theory to make predictions in new settings is challenging: rarely can it be done without ad hoc modifications to account for setting-specific factors. We argue that AI agents put in simulations of those novel settings offer an alternative for applying theory, requiring minimal or no modifications. We present an approach for building such "general" agents that use theory-grounded natural language instructions, existing empirical data, and knowledge acquired by the underlying AI during training. To demonstrate the approach in settings where no data from that data-generating process exists--as is often the case in applied prediction problems--we design a heterogeneous population of 883,320 novel games. AI agents are constructed using human data from a small set of conceptually related but structurally distinct "seed" games. In preregistered experiments, on average, agents predict initial human play in a random sample of 1,500 games from the population better than (i) a cognitive hierarchy model, (ii) game-theoretic equilibria, and (iii) out-of-the-box agents. For a small set of separate novel games, these simulations predict responses from a new sample of human subjects better even than the most plausibly relevant published human data.

中文摘要

有用的社会科学理论能够跨情境预测行为。然而，应用一个理论在新情境中进行预测是具有挑战性的：几乎总是需要针对情境特定因素进行临时修改才能做到。我们认为，置于这些新情境模拟中的人工智能智能体提供了一种应用理论的替代方案，只需最少或无需修改。我们提出了一种构建此类“通用”智能体的方法，该方法使用基于理论的自然语言指令、现有的经验数据以及底层人工智能在训练期间获取的知识。为了在不存在该数据生成过程数据的情境中（这在应用预测问题中经常出现）演示该方法，我们设计了一个包含883,320个新颖游戏的异质性群体。人工智能智能体是利用来自一小部分概念相关但结构不同的“种子”游戏的人类数据构建的。在预注册的实验中，平均而言，这些智能体对来自该群体中随机抽取的1,500个游戏的初始人类行为的预测，优于（i）认知层级模型、（ii）博弈论均衡以及（iii）开箱即用的智能体。对于一小部分独立的新颖游戏，这些模拟对来自新一批人类受试者的反应的预测，甚至优于最可能相关的已发表人类数据。

No. 11NBER w34936

Who Gets What in Education: Can School Matching Improve Student Achievement?

教育中的谁得到什么：学校匹配能否提高学生成绩？

Atila Abdulkadiroglu, Parag A. Pathak, Christopher R. Walters

Programs：ED, LS, PE；JEL：I21

Abstract

We examine two approaches to improving urban school systems: changing who gets to go to existing schools (reallocation) and restructuring school portfolios through closures and reconstitution (resource augmentation). Using data from New York City high schools, we estimate models of school effects allowing for both vertical school quality differences and horizontal student-specific match effects. While sophisticated reallocation policies that optimize student-school matches can generate modest educational gains, they are constrained by limited seats at highly effective schools. Simple resource-augmentation policies targeting replacement of low-performing schools achieve comparable improvements with less systemic disruption. Analysis of NYC's school closures reveals that basic graduation rate metrics effectively identify struggling schools, suggesting complex value-added models may be unnecessary for targeting closure decisions. Our findings indicate that capacity constraints, rather than poor school matching, primarily drive educational inequality.

中文摘要

我们研究了改善城市学校系统的两种方法：改变谁可以去现有的学校（重新分配），以及通过关闭和重组来调整学校组合（资源扩充）。利用纽约市高中的数据，我们估计了允许存在纵向学校质量差异和横向学生特定匹配效应的学校效应模型。虽然优化学生-学校匹配的复杂重新分配政策可以产生适度的教育收益，但它们受到高效学校名额有限的制约。针对替换表现不佳学校的简单资源扩充政策，在系统性干扰较小的情况下实现了类似的改进。对纽约市学校关闭的分析表明，基本的毕业率指标能有效识别陷入困境的学校，这表明复杂的增值模型可能并非针对关闭决策所必需。我们的研究结果表明，教育不平等的主要驱动因素是容量限制，而非糟糕的学校匹配。

No. 12NBER w34935

Tariffs, Automation, and Business Dynamism

关税、自动化与商业活力

Stéphane Auray, Michael B. Devereux, Aurélien Eyquem

Programs：IFM；JEL：F30, F40, F41

Abstract

Can protectionism revive domestic production, slow automation, and help routine workers? We address this question in a dynamic open-economy model with heterogeneous firms, endogenous entry, and task-based production in which routine tasks can be performed by workers or robots. Import tariffs reallocate demand toward domestic goods, reshape markups and entry incentives, and generate fiscal revenues rebated to households. Why People Disagree About What Drives Stock Prices

为什么人们对股票价格的驱动因素存在分歧

Andrew Atkeson, Fabrizio Perri, Jonathan Heathcote

Programs：AP, EFG；JEL：E0, G12

Abstract

We show that, to a first-order approximation, estimates of fluctuations in Shiller’s fundamental price relative to observed price depend primarily on forecasts of long-horizon expected returns. Researchers using different measures of cash flow and valuation may reach different conclusions about the extent to which values fluctuate excessively relative to fundamentals, but that is only because return forecasts based on different cash-flow-to-value measures will be different. Using U.S. equity data, we demonstrate that the amount of persistence in expected returns, rather than the amount of short-run return predictability, is the key determinant of implied excess volatility. Disagreements about stock market valuation therefore reduce to disagreements about long-run expected returns.

中文摘要

我们表明，在一阶近似下，对席勒基础价格相对于观察价格波动的估计，主要取决于对长期预期收益的预测。使用不同现金流和估值指标的研究人员可能会得出关于价值相对于基本面过度波动程度的不同结论，但这仅仅是因为基于不同现金流-价值比率的收益预测会有所不同。利用美国股票数据，我们证明了预期收益的持续性程度，而非短期收益可预测性的程度，是隐含过度波动性的关键决定因素。因此，关于股票市场估值的分歧归结为对长期预期收益的分歧。

The Labor Market Consequences of Rapid Sectoral Shifts

快速部门转移的劳动力市场后果

John R. Grigsby, Nathan Zorzi

Programs：EFG；JEL：E0, E20, E24, E60, E64, J20, J23, J24, J3, J62, Q52, Q58

Abstract

Sectoral shifts require costly labor reallocation for workers, fueling concerns about how quickly they occur. We study how the pace of such sectoral shifts affects workers at risk of displacement. We develop a life-cycle model with skill heterogeneity and job ladders where labor demand gradually rises in one sector and declines in another. The model reveals three novel insights. First, workers’ lifetime earnings are strongly non-linear and even nonmonotonic in the horizon over which the transition unfolds. Second, more and more workers benefit on the extensive margin as the transition accelerates, but the tail of losses becomes thicker on the intensive margin. Third, labor market frictions are important to quantify these non-linearities. We apply our model to the climate transition and find substantial earnings losses from a transition ending in 2060. Completing the transition ten years earlier reduces average losses, but raises losses in the tail by a fifth.

中文摘要

部门转移要求工人进行代价高昂的劳动力重新配置，这加剧了人们对转移发生速度的担忧。我们研究了这种部门转移的速度如何影响面临失业风险的工人。我们开发了一个具有技能异质性和工作阶梯的生命周期模型，其中劳动力需求在一个部门逐渐上升，在另一个部门逐渐下降。该模型揭示了三个新颖的见解。首先，工人的终生收入在转型展开的时间跨度上呈现出强烈的非线性，甚至是非单调性。其次，随着转型加速，越来越多的工人在广延边际上受益，但在集约边际上损失的尾部变得更厚。第三，劳动力市场摩擦对于量化这些非线性至关重要。我们将模型应用于气候转型，发现到2060年结束的转型会导致可观的收入损失。提前十年完成转型会降低平均损失，但会使尾部损失增加五分之一。

The Market Value of Reproductive Rights: Evidence from U.S. Housing Markets

生育权的市场价值：来自美国住房市场的证据

Daniel L. Dench, Kelly Lifchez, Jason M. Lindo, Jancy Ling Liu

Programs：CH, EH, LE, LS, PE；JEL：I18, J13, K23, R21, R23, R31

Abstract

We estimate the market value of reproductive rights as capitalized into U.S. housing markets. We do so using a synthetic difference-in-differences design to evaluate the effects of total abortion bans following the 2022 Dobbs decision, and drawing on housing market indices from Zillow and vacancy rate data from the U.S. Census Bureau's Housing Vacancy Survey. The results indicate that total abortion bans reduced rents by an average of 2.2% from July 2022 through June 2025, with the effect reaching 4.0% in the most recent year. Over the same horizon, bans increased rental vacancy rates by an average of 1.1 percentage points, with the effect reaching 1.8 percentage points in the most recent year. Estimates for home values and homeowner vacancy rates are similar in magnitude but less precise.

Mergers and Non-contractible Benefits: The Employees' Perspective

并购与非契约性利益：员工视角

Wei Cai, Andrea Prat, Jiehang Yu

Programs：LS, PR；JEL：D23, G34, J31

Abstract

Incomplete contract theory, supported by anecdotal evidence, suggests that when a firm is acquired, workers may be adversely affected in non-contractible aspects of their work experience. This paper empirically investigates this prediction by combining M\\&A events from the Refinitiv database and web-scraped Glassdoor review data. We find that: (a) Controlling for pre-trends, mergers lead to lower satisfaction, especially on non-contractible dimensions of the employee experience (about 6% of a standard deviation); (b) The effect is stronger in the target firm than in the acquiring firm; (c) Text analysis of employee comments indicates that the decline in satisfaction is primarily associated with perceived breaches of implicit contracts. Our findings indicate that mergers may reduce workers' job utility through non-monetary channels.

中文摘要

不完全契约理论（并有轶事证据支持）表明，当一家公司被收购时，员工在工作体验的非契约性方面可能会受到不利影响。本文通过结合 Refinitiv 数据库中的并购事件和网络爬取的 Glassdoor 评论数据，对这一预测进行了实证研究。我们发现：(a) 在控制事前趋势的情况下，并购导致员工满意度下降，尤其是在员工体验的非契约性维度上（约 0.06 个标准差）；(b) 这种效应在目标公司中比在收购公司中更强；(c) 对员工评论的文本分析表明，满意度的下降主要与感知到的隐性契约被违背有关。我们的研究结果表明，并购可能通过非货币渠道降低员工的工作效用。

How does AI Distribute the pie? Large Language Models and the Ultimatum Game.

人工智能如何分配蛋糕？大语言模型与最后通牒博弈

Douglas K.G. Araujo, Harald Uhlig

Programs：AP, EFG；JEL：C70, C90, D91

Abstract

As Large Language Models (LLMs) are increasingly tasked with autonomous decision making, understanding their behavior in strategic settings is crucial. We investigate the choices of various LLMs in the Ultimatum Game, a setting where human behavior notably deviates from theoretical rationality. We conduct experiments varying the stake size and the nature of the opponent (Human vs. AI) across both Proposer and Responder roles. Three key results emerge. First, LLM behavior is heterogeneous but predictable when conditioning on stake size and player types. Second, while some models approximate the rational benchmark and others mimic human social preferences, a distinct “altruistic” mode emerges where LLMs propose hyper-fair distributions (greater than 50%). Third, LLM Proposers forgo a large share of total payoff, and an even larger share when the Responder is human. These findings highlight the need for careful testing before deploying AI agents in economic settings.

中文摘要

随着大语言模型越来越多地承担自主决策任务，理解它们在战略环境中的行为至关重要。我们研究了各种大语言模型在最后通牒博弈中的选择，这是一个人类行为显著偏离理论理性的场景。我们通过实验改变了赌注大小和对手的性质（人类 vs. 人工智能），并涵盖了提议者和回应者两种角色。出现了三个关键结果。首先，大语言模型的行为是异质性的，但在以赌注大小和玩家类型为条件时是可预测的。其次，虽然一些模型接近理性基准，另一些模型模仿人类的社会偏好，但出现了一种独特的“利他”模式，即大语言模型提出超公平分配方案（大于 50%）。第三，作为提议者的大语言模型放弃了总收益的很大一部分，当回应者是人类时，放弃的份额甚至更大。这些发现强调了在将人工智能代理部署到经济环境中之前进行仔细测试的必要性。

Pay Now, Buy Never: The Economics of Consumer Prepayment Schemes

现在付款，永不消费：消费者预付计划的经济学

Yixuan Liu, Hua Zhang, Eric Zou

Programs：PE；JEL：D12, D90, G23, G41, K20, L14, L81, M31

Abstract

Prepaid consumption is a common feature of modern consumer markets and is often presented as a mutually beneficial arrangement: consumers receive upfront discounts, and firms secure future sales. We analyze a large-scale Pay Now, Buy Later (PNBL) program in which consumers prepay for restaurant credit with bonuses, and spend the balance later. Using detailed transaction data from over 4 million consumers, we document widespread balance breakage: approximately 40% of prepaid value is never used. Because many consumers underutilize their balances, merchants recover significantly more than the bonus cost. The median firm earns roughly $5.5 in breakage profit for every $1 of bonus credit issued. While PNBL participation does lead to modest increases in consumer spending over time, firms gain substantially more from breakage than from any loyalty-driven revenue. These findings challenge the prevailing win–win narrative: PNBL schemes often result in a significant transfer from consumers to firms. We develop a stylized contract model to illustrate the misaligned incentives firms face, and show through counterfactual analysis that a simple escrow policy with an appropriately chosen deposit requirement can realign firm incentives and generate more consumer-serving outcomes.

中文摘要

预付消费是现代消费市场的一个常见特征，通常被描述为一种互利的安排：消费者获得预付折扣，企业确保未来销售。我们分析了一个大规模的“现在付款，以后消费”计划，在该计划中，消费者预付资金以获取餐厅消费积分和奖励，并在之后使用余额。利用超过 400 万消费者的详细交易数据，我们记录了广泛的余额破损现象：大约 40% 的预付价值从未被使用。由于许多消费者未充分利用其余额，商家收回的资金远超奖励成本。中位数企业每发放 1 美元的奖励积分，就能获得大约 5.5 美元的破损利润。虽然参与“现在付款，以后消费”计划确实会导致消费者支出随时间适度增加，但企业从破损中获得的收益远大于任何由忠诚度驱动的收入。这些发现挑战了普遍的双赢叙事：“现在付款，以后消费”计划通常导致从消费者到企业的显著财富转移。我们开发了一个简约的契约模型来说明企业面临的激励错位，并通过反事实分析表明，一项简单的、包含适当存款要求的托管政策可以重新调整企业激励，并产生更有利于消费者的结果。

When Incentives Aren't Enough: Evidence on Inattention and Imperfect Memory from HIV Medication Adherence

当激励不足时：来自HIV药物依从性的关于注意力不集中和记忆不完美的证据

Hang Yu, Jared Stolove, Dean Yang, James Riddell IV, Arlete Mahumane

Programs：DEV, EH；JEL：D91, I12, O15

Abstract

Financial incentives are widely used to encourage beneficial behaviors, but their effectiveness may be limited by inattention and imperfect memory. We study this in a randomized trial of HIV medication adherence in Mozambique. Financial incentives alone increase adherence by 10.6 percentage points, while pairing incentives with reminders increases adherence by 24.3 percentage points. We develop a model in which inattention to daily adherence and imperfect memory of payment eligibility reduce incentive effectiveness and show that reminders mitigate both frictions. Detailed medication refill data support the model’s predictions. The results suggest combining incentives with reminders can substantially increase program effectiveness.

中文摘要

经济激励被广泛用于鼓励有益行为，但其有效性可能受到注意力不集中和记忆不完美的限制。我们在莫桑比克进行的一项关于HIV药物依从性的随机试验中研究了这一点。仅提供经济激励使依从性提高了 10.6 个百分点，而将激励与提醒相结合则使依从性提高了 24.3 个百分点。我们开发了一个模型，其中对每日服药依从性的注意力不集中和对付款资格的记忆不完美降低了激励的有效性，并表明提醒可以缓解这两种摩擦。详细的药物补充数据支持了该模型的预测。结果表明，将激励与提醒相结合可以显著提高项目的有效性。
