No. 1NBER w32827

The Rise of Teamwork and Career Prospects in Academic Science

学术科学中团队合作的兴起与职业前景

Mabel Andalón, Catherine de Fontenay, Donna K. Ginther, Kwanghui Lim

Programs：LS；JEL：I23, J24, J4

Abstract

Teamwork has become more important in recent decades. We show that larger teams generate an unintended side effect: individuals who finish their PhD when the average team in their field is larger have worse career prospects. Our analysis combines data on career outcomes from the Survey of Doctorate Recipients with publication data that measures team size from ISI Web of Science. As average team size in a field increased over time, junior academic scientists became less likely to secure research funding or obtain tenure and were more likely to leave academia relative to their older counterparts. The team size effect can fully account for the observed decline in tenure prospects in academic science. The rise in team size was not associated with the end of mandatory retirement. However, the doubling of the NIH budget was associated with a significant increase in team size. Our results demonstrate that academic science has not adjusted its reward structure, which is largely individual, in response to team science. Failing to address these concerns means a significant loss as junior scientists exit after a costly and specialized education in science.

中文摘要

近几十年来，团队合作变得更加重要。我们表明，更大的团队会产生一个意想不到的副作用：那些在所在领域平均团队规模较大时完成博士学位的个人，其职业前景更差。我们的分析结合了来自博士获得者调查的职业结果数据，以及来自ISI Web of Science的衡量团队规模的发表数据。随着一个领域平均团队规模随时间推移而增加，初级学术科学家获得研究资助或获得终身教职的可能性降低，并且相对于年长的同行，他们更有可能离开学术界。团队规模效应可以完全解释学术科学中观察到的终身教职前景下降。团队规模的增加与强制退休的结束无关。然而，NIH预算翻倍与团队规模的显著增加相关。我们的结果表明，学术科学尚未调整其奖励结构（该结构在很大程度上是个人化的），以应对团队科学。未能解决这些问题意味着重大损失，因为初级科学家在接受了昂贵且专业化的科学教育后选择退出。

No. 2NBER w32826

Personal Bankruptcy Law and Innovation around the World

全球范围内的个人破产法与创新

Douglas Cumming, Randall Morck, Zhao Rong, Minjie Zhang

Programs：CF；JEL：G33, G5, K35, O3, O4, P1, P50

Abstract

Because corporate limited liability protects the founder’s personal assets, creditors often require founders of new, small and risky firms to contract around limited liability by pledging their personal assets as collateral for loans to their firms. This makes personal bankruptcy law (PBL) relevant to corporate finance. We find that pro-debtor PBL reforms increase the number of patents filed, citations to those patents, and début patents by firms with no previous patents. These reforms also redistribute innovation across industries in closer alignment to its distribution in the U.S., which we take to approximate industry innovative potential. These effects are driven by firms without histories of high intensity patenting and are damped in countries that impose minimum capital requirements on new firms. Firms with largescale legacy technology may avoid radical innovations that devalue that technology. Consequently, new, initially small and risky firms often develop the disruptive innovations that contribute most to economic growth. Consistent with this, we also find pro-debtor PBL reforms increasing allocative investment efficiency. Our difference-in-differences regressions use patents and PBL reforms for 33 countries from 1990 to 2002, with subsequent years used to measure citations to patents in this period.

中文摘要

由于公司有限责任保护了创始人的个人资产，债权人通常要求新成立、小型且高风险企业的创始人通过将其个人资产作为企业贷款的抵押品来绕过有限责任。这使得个人破产法与公司金融相关。我们发现，有利于债务人的个人破产法改革增加了专利申请数量、这些专利的引用次数，以及无先前专利企业的首项专利。这些改革还使创新在各行业间的分布更接近于美国的分布，我们将其视为近似于行业的创新潜力。这些效应是由没有高强度专利历史的企业驱动的，并且在对新企业设定最低资本要求的国家中被削弱。拥有大规模传统技术的企业可能会避免会贬低该技术的激进创新。因此，新成立、最初规模小且风险高的企业往往开发出对经济增长贡献最大的颠覆性创新。与此一致，我们还发现有利于债务人的个人破产法改革提高了配置投资效率。我们的双重差分回归使用了1990年至2002年33个国家的专利和个人破产法改革数据，随后的年份用于衡量这一时期专利的引用次数。

Corruption as a Shared Dilemma: Survey Evidence from Legislators and Citizens in Three Countries

作为共同困境的腐败：来自三个国家立法者和公民的调查证据

Raymond Fisman, Matteo F. Ferroni, Miriam Golden

Programs：PE, POL；JEL：D72, D73, D78

Abstract

We conduct parallel surveys of legislators and citizens in three countries to study their tolerance for corruption. In Italy, Colombia, and Pakistan legislators and citizens respond similarly to hypothetical scenarios involving trade-offs between, for example, probity and efficiency: both perceive corruption as undesirable but prevalent. These novel descriptive data further reveal that legislators generally have accurate beliefs about public opinion on corruption and understand its relevance to voters. An informational treatment updates legislators’ beliefs about public opinion. The treatment produces downward adjustments among legislators who initially overestimated citizens’ anti-corruption preferences. We also present descriptive data that tolerance of corruption is predicted by politician attributes, most notably motivations for entering politics. Finally, results reconfirm partisan bias by voters in evaluations of corruption. Overall, results suggest that barriers to effective anti-corruption policies are unlikely to lie with lack of information by legislators or by their deliberate commitment to corrupt activities.

中文摘要

我们在三个国家对立法者和公民进行了平行调查，以研究他们对腐败的容忍度。在意大利、哥伦比亚和巴基斯坦，立法者和公民对涉及例如廉洁与效率之间权衡的假设情景反应相似：两者都认为腐败是不可取的但普遍存在。这些新颖的描述性数据进一步揭示，立法者通常对公众关于腐败的意见有准确的信念，并理解其与选民的相关性。一项信息干预更新了立法者对公众意见的信念。该干预导致那些最初高估公民反腐败偏好的立法者向下调整。我们还提供了描述性数据，表明对腐败的容忍度可由政治家属性预测，最显著的是进入政界的动机。最后，结果再次证实了选民在评估腐败时的党派偏见。总体而言，结果表明，有效反腐败政策的障碍不太可能在于立法者缺乏信息或他们故意参与腐败活动。

No. 4NBER w32824

Urban roadway in America: the amount, extent, and value

美国城市道路：数量、范围与价值

Erick Guerra, Gilles Duranton, Xinyu Ma

Programs：PE；JEL：R14, R42

Abstract

We predict the amount, share, and value of land dedicated to roadways within and across 316 US Primary Metropolitan Statistical Areas. Despite the amount and value of land dedicated to roadway, our study provides the first such estimate across a broad range of metropolitan areas. Our basic approach is to estimate roadway widths using a 10% sample of widths provided by the Highway Performance and Monitoring System and apply our estimates to the rest of the roadway system. Multiplying estimated widths by segment length and netting out double counting at intersections provide estimates of land area. We also match roadway segments and areas to existing land value estimates and satellite-based measures of urbanized land. We find that a little under a quarter of urbanized land—roughly the size of West Virginia—is dedicated to roadway. This land was worth around $4.1 trillion dollars in 2016 and had an annualized value that was higher than the total variable costs of the trucking sector and the total annual federal, state, and local expenditures on roadway. Conducting a back-of-the-envelope cost-benefit analysis, we found that the country likely has too much land dedicated to urban roads.

中文摘要

我们预测了美国316个主要大都市统计区内及之间用于道路的土地数量、份额和价值。尽管用于道路的土地数量和价值巨大，但我们的研究首次提供了跨广泛大都市区域的此类估计。我们的基本方法是使用高速公路性能与监测系统提供的10%宽度样本来估计道路宽度，并将我们的估计应用于其余道路系统。将估计的宽度乘以路段长度，并扣除交叉路口的重复计算，得到土地面积估计值。我们还将道路路段和区域与现有的土地价值估计值以及基于卫星的城市化土地测量值进行匹配。我们发现，略低于四分之一的城市化土地——大约相当于西弗吉尼亚州的面积——用于道路。这些土地在2016年价值约4.1万亿美元，其年化价值高于卡车运输部门的总可变成本以及联邦、州和地方在道路上的年度总支出。通过进行粗略的成本效益分析，我们发现该国可能拥有过多用于城市道路的土地。

No. 5NBER w32822

Local Projections

局部投影法

Òscar Jordà, Alan M. Taylor

Programs：EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：C01, C14, C22, C26, C32, C54

Abstract

A central question in applied research is to estimate the effect of an exogenous intervention or shock on an outcome. The intervention can affect the outcome and controls on impact and over time. Moreover, there can be subsequent feedback between outcomes, controls and the intervention. Many of these interactions can be untangled using local projections. This method’s simplicity makes it a convenient and versatile tool in the empiricist’s kit, one that is generalizable to complex settings. This article reviews the state-of-the art for the practitioner, discusses best practices and possible extensions of local projections methods, along with their limitations.

中文摘要

应用研究中的一个核心问题是估计外生干预或冲击对结果的影响。干预措施会立即并随时间推移影响结果和控制变量。此外，结果、控制变量和干预措施之间可能存在后续反馈。许多此类相互作用可以使用局部投影法来厘清。该方法的简洁性使其成为实证研究者工具箱中一个方便且多功能的工具，并且可以推广到复杂情境。本文为实践者回顾了最新技术，讨论了局部投影方法的最佳实践和可能的扩展，以及它们的局限性。

No. 6NBER w32821

Dynamic Optimization Meets Budgeting: Unraveling Financial Complexities

动态优化遇上预算：解开财务复杂性

Guidon Fenig, Luba Petersen

Programs：ME；JEL：C91, E03, E21, E70

Abstract

This paper explores sources of complexity in dynamic optimization, examining how individuals navigate variation in incomes, prices, and returns in ten-period consumption-saving decisions. Our findings reveal that dynamic optimization poses significant challenges, resulting in suboptimal choices even in straightforward scenarios with stable parameters, full information, no uncertainty, and opportunities to learn. These challenges intensify in scenarios involving complexities such as inflation and compounding returns, marked by a pronounced tendency to over-smooth consumption.

中文摘要

本文探讨了动态优化中的复杂性来源，考察了个人如何在十期消费-储蓄决策中应对收入、价格和回报的变化。我们的研究结果表明，动态优化带来了重大挑战，即使在参数稳定、信息完全、无不确定性且有学习机会的简单情景中，也会导致次优选择。这些挑战在涉及通货膨胀和复利回报等复杂性的情景中加剧，表现为明显的过度平滑消费倾向。

No. 7NBER w32820

50 Years of Breakthroughs and Barriers: Women in Economics, Policy, and Leadership

突破与障碍的50年：经济学、政策与领导力中的女性

Francine D. Blau, Lisa M. Lynch

Programs：LS；JEL：J0, J01, J10, J16, J2, J21, J24, J7, J70

Abstract

This paper provides an overview of what has happened over the past fifty years for women as they worked to break through professional barriers in economics, policy, and institutional leadership. We chart the progress of women in higher education at the college level and beyond and then go on to examine women’s representation at the upper levels of academia, government, law, medicine, and management. We begin our description of trends in 1972 when Title IX was enacted, prohibiting sex-based discrimination in federally funded educational programs. The data paint a picture of considerable progress but also persistent inequities. We then go on to consider possible explanations for the continuing gender differences and some of the empirical evidence on the factors identified.

中文摘要

本文概述了过去五十年来女性在经济学、政策和机构领导力方面努力突破职业壁垒的情况。我们描绘了女性在高等教育（大学及以上层次）中的进展，然后进一步考察了女性在学术界、政府、法律、医学和管理领域高层中的代表性。我们从1972年《教育修正案第九条》颁布时开始描述趋势，该法案禁止在联邦资助的教育项目中基于性别的歧视。数据描绘了一幅取得显著进步但同时也存在持续不平等的图景。接着，我们探讨了持续存在的性别差异的可能解释，以及关于已识别因素的一些实证证据。

No. 8NBER w32819

Tax Incidence Anomalies

税收归宿异常现象

Youssef Benzarti

Programs：PE；JEL：H0, H22

Abstract

This paper reviews the literature on the incidence of consumption and labor taxes and focuses on the empirical results that show stark departures from the canonical model of tax incidence, which I refer to as anomalies. In particular, there is mounting evidence questioning three fundamental implications of the canonical model: (1) that statutory incidence is irrelevant for economic incidence, (2) that the relative magnitude of the demand and supply elasticities is a sufficient statistic for tax incidence, and (3) that incidence is symmetric for increases and decreases. I review this empirical evidence and draw implications for the canonical model’s relevance.

中文摘要

本文回顾了关于消费税和劳动税归宿的文献，并聚焦于那些显示出与经典税收归宿模型显著偏离的实证结果，我将其称为异常现象。特别是，越来越多的证据质疑了经典模型的三个基本含义：（1）法定归宿与经济归宿无关，（2）需求和供给弹性的相对大小是税收归宿的充分统计量，以及（3）税收增加和减少的归宿是对称的。我回顾了这些实证证据，并探讨了其对经典模型相关性的启示。

No. 9NBER w32818

Ethnic Identity and Anti-Immigrant Sentiment: Evidence from Proposition 187

族群认同与反移民情绪：来自187号提案的证据

Francisca M. Antman, Brian Duncan

Programs：LE, LS, POL；JEL：D72, J15, Z13

Abstract

Political discourse has often stoked racial and ethnic divisions, raising the possibility that individuals’ self-reported racial and ethnic identities may change in response to an increasingly hostile environment. We shed light on this question by measuring the impacts of local support for California’s Proposition 187, one of the first and most well-known ballot measures widely seen to be anti-immigrant and anti-Latino, on individuals’ willingness to identify ethnically as Hispanic and specifically, Mexican. Linking data on self-reported ethnicity, ancestry, and parental place of birth with county-level voter support for Proposition 187, we show that individuals with stronger ties to Mexican ancestry or parentage are less likely to identify ethnically as Mexican in response to support for Proposition 187, just as individuals with weaker ties to Mexican ancestry are more likely to identify as Mexican. This is consistent with our predictions that anti-minority sentiment may drive individuals with more observable ties to a minority group to reduce their willingness to identify due to heightened fear of discrimination and hostility. At the same time, anti-minority sentiment may raise the salience of ethnicity and race and thus increase the willingness to identify as a minority for those with weaker observable ties, who are relatively more protected from adverse impacts. To our knowledge, this is the first paper to document a connection between political discourse and endogenous ethnic identity.

中文摘要

政治言论常常煽动种族和民族分裂，这引发了一种可能性：个人自我报告的种族和民族身份可能会因日益敌对的环境而改变。我们通过衡量加州187号提案（最早且最著名的被广泛视为反移民和反拉丁裔的投票措施之一）的地方支持度对个人认同为西班牙裔（特别是墨西哥裔）意愿的影响来阐明这一问题。将自我报告的族裔、血统和父母出生地数据与县级对187号提案的选民支持度联系起来，我们发现，与墨西哥血统或父母关系更紧密的个人，在面对对187号提案的支持时，认同为墨西哥裔的可能性更低；而与墨西哥血统关系较弱的个人，认同为墨西哥裔的可能性则更高。这与我们的预测一致：反少数族裔情绪可能促使与少数族裔群体有更明显联系的个人，因对歧视和敌意的恐惧加剧而降低认同意愿。同时，反少数族裔情绪可能提高族裔和种族的显著性，从而增加那些可观察联系较弱、相对更能免受不利影响的个人认同为少数族裔的意愿。据我们所知，这是第一篇记录政治话语与内生族群认同之间联系的论文。

No. 10NBER w32817

State Auto-IRA Policies and Firm Behavior: Lessons from Administrative Tax Data

州自动IRA政策与企业行为：来自行政税收数据的教训

Adam Bloomfield, Lucas Goodman, Manita Rao, Sita Slavov

Programs：AG, LS, PE；JEL：D14, H75, J26, J32

Abstract

Several states have recently attempted to boost retirement saving by adopting “auto-IRA” policies that require employers not currently offering an employer-sponsored retirement plan (ESRP) to either (1) establish an ESRP or (2) enroll employees in state-facilitated Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs). We identify the effect of these state policies on firm decisions to offer ESRPs, exploiting the phased rollout of these policy treatments across states and employer size categories. Using U.S. tax microdata, we estimate that about 17% of treated firms have been induced to offer an ESRP by these policies, although there is substantial heterogeneity in these effects across firm and worker characteristics. This effect is large considering that, for employers, establishing and maintaining an ESRP is more costly than utilizing the state-facilitated IRAs. We explore both rational and behavioral explanations for why firms might choose the higher-cost option to comply with state auto-IRA policies.

中文摘要

几个州最近试图通过采用“自动IRA”政策来促进退休储蓄，这些政策要求目前不提供雇主赞助退休计划（ESRP）的雇主要么（1）建立ESRP，要么（2）让员工加入州政府推动的个人退休账户（IRA）。我们利用这些政策处理在各州和雇主规模类别中的分阶段推出，识别了这些州政策对企业提供ESRP决策的影响。使用美国税收微观数据，我们估计约有17%的受处理企业被这些政策诱导提供了ESRP，尽管这些效应在不同企业和工人特征之间存在显著的异质性。考虑到对雇主而言，建立和维护ESRP的成本高于使用州政府推动的IRA，这一效应是巨大的。我们探讨了企业为何可能选择成本更高的选项来遵守州自动IRA政策的理性和行为解释。

No. 11NBER w32816

Relative-Price Changes as Aggregate Supply Shocks Revisited: Theory and Evidence

作为总供给冲击的相对价格变化再探：理论与证据

Hassan Afrouzi, Saroj Bhattarai, Edson Wu

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：C67, E32, E52

Abstract

We provide theory and evidence that relative price shocks can cause aggregate inflation and act as aggregate supply shocks. Empirically, we show that exogenous positive energy price shocks have a positive impact not only on headline but also on U.S. core inflation while depressing U.S. real activity. In a two-sector monetary model with upstream and downstream sectors and heterogeneous price stickiness, we analytically characterize how upstream shocks propagate to prices. Using panel IV local projections, we show that the responsiveness of sectoral PCE prices to energy price shocks is in line with model predictions. Motivated by post-COVID inflation in the U.S., a model experiment shows that a one-time relative price shock generates persistent movements in headline and core inflation similar to those observed in the data, even in the absence of aggregate slack. The model also emphasizes that monetary policy stance plays an important role in propagation of such shocks.
