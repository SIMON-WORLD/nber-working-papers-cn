Capital Flow Stability and Policy Challenges in Southeast Asia: Historical Perspectives from the 19th to the 21st Century

资本流动稳定性与东南亚政策挑战：19至21世纪的历史视角

Christopher M. Meissner, Kensuke Molnar-Tanaka

Programs：DAE, IFM；JEL：F21, F33, F36, N20

Abstract

Over the last 200 years, economies have accumulated significant experience in managing capital flows in the face of globalization. This study examines management of capital flows since the 1800s with an eye towards providing historical lessons for Southeast Asia today. We start with the global sterling/gold standard regime of the late 19th century globalization and then discuss the tumultuous inter-war period. We then examine policies in Southeast Asian countries since the 1950s. In the 1980s and 1990s, many economies faced increasing financial instability related to the resumption of global capital flows, most noticeably in Southeast Asia during the Asian Financial Crisis. The paper examines the historical importance of exchange rate policies for capital flow stability. Capital flow management in the 21st century faces various challenges such as enhanced state-intervention and digital currencies.

中文摘要

在过去200年间，经济体在应对全球化过程中积累了管理资本流动的丰富经验。本研究考察了自19世纪以来的资本流动管理，旨在为当今东南亚提供历史教训。我们从19世纪末全球化时期的全球英镑/金本位制入手，随后讨论了动荡的两次世界大战间期。接着，我们分析了20世纪50年代以来东南亚国家的政策。在20世纪80年代和90年代，许多经济体因全球资本流动的恢复而面临日益加剧的金融不稳定，最显著的是东南亚在亚洲金融危机期间。本文考察了汇率政策对资本流动稳定性的历史重要性。21世纪的资本流动管理面临着各种挑战，例如国家干预的增强和数字货币的出现。

No. 2NBER w33144

Nonlinear Pricing and Misallocation

非线性定价与资源错配

Gideon Bornstein, Alessandra Peter

Programs：EFG；JEL：D4, E2, L1, O4

Abstract

This paper studies the effect of nonlinear pricing on markups and misallocation. We develop a general equilibrium model of firms that are allowed to set a quantity-dependent pricing schedule—contrary to the typical assumption in macroeconomic models. Without the restriction to linear pricing, markup heterogeneity is no longer a sign of misallocation. Larger firms charge higher markups, yet the allocation of resources across firms is efficient. Further, we point to a new source of misallocation. In general equilibrium, high-taste consumers are allocated too much of each good, low-taste consumers too little. When labor supply is elastic, firms’ market power depresses aggregate labor, but this effect is independent of the level of the aggregate markup in the economy. Using micro data from the retail sector, we show that nonlinear pricing is prevalent and quantify the model. We find that the welfare losses from misallocation across consumers under nonlinear pricing are substantially larger than those from misallocation across firms under linear pricing.

中文摘要

本文研究了非线性定价对加成率和资源错配的影响。我们构建了一个允许企业设定依赖于数量的定价方案的一般均衡模型——这与宏观经济模型中的典型假设相反。在没有线性定价限制的情况下，加成率异质性不再是资源错配的标志。大企业收取更高的加成率，但资源在企业间的配置是有效的。此外，我们指出了一种新的资源错配来源。在一般均衡中，高品味消费者被分配了过多的每种商品，而低品味消费者则过少。当劳动供给具有弹性时，企业的市场势力压低了总劳动量，但这种效应与经济中总加成率的水平无关。利用零售部门的微观数据，我们展示了非线性定价的普遍性并对模型进行了量化。我们发现，在非线性定价下，消费者之间的资源错配造成的福利损失远大于在线性定价下企业之间的资源错配造成的福利损失。

No. 3NBER w33143

Trade, Trees, and Lives

贸易、树木与生命

Xinming Du, Lei Li, Eric Zou

Programs：DEV, EEE, EH, ITI；JEL：F18, O13, Q23, Q53

Abstract

This paper shows a cascading mechanism through which international trade-induced deforestation results in a decline of health outcomes in cities distant from where trade activities occur. We examine Brazil, which has ramped up agricultural export over the last two decades to meet rising global demand. Using a shift-share research design, we first show that export shocks cause substantial local agricultural expansion and a virtual one-for-one decline in forest cover. We then construct a dynamic area-of-effect model that predicts where atmospheric changes should be felt – due to loss of forests that would otherwise serve to filter out and absorb air pollutants as they travel – downwind of the deforestation areas. Leveraging quasi-random variation in these atmospheric connections, we establish a causal link between deforestation upstream and subsequent rises in air pollution and premature deaths downstream, with the mortality effects predominantly driven by cardiovascular and respiratory causes. Our estimates reveal a large telecoupled health externality of trade deforestation: over 700,000 premature deaths in Brazil over the past two decades. This equates to $0.18 loss in statistical life value per $1 agricultural exports over the study period.

中文摘要

本文展示了一种级联机制，通过这种机制，国际贸易导致的森林砍伐使得远离贸易活动发生的城市的健康结果下降。我们以巴西为研究对象，该国在过去二十年中为满足日益增长的全球需求而加大了农业出口。使用份额转移研究设计，我们首先表明出口冲击导致了当地农业的大规模扩张和森林覆盖率的几乎一对一的下降。然后，我们构建了一个动态影响区域模型，该模型预测了由于森林损失（这些森林原本可以在空气污染物传播过程中过滤和吸收它们），砍伐区域下风向地区应感受到的大气变化。利用这些大气联系中的准随机变化，我们建立了上游森林砍伐与下游空气污染和过早死亡增加之间的因果关系，其中死亡率影响主要由心血管和呼吸系统原因驱动。我们的估计揭示了一个巨大的贸易森林砍伐远程耦合健康外部性：在过去二十年中，巴西有超过70万人过早死亡。这相当于研究期间每1美元农业出口造成0.18美元的统计生命价值损失。

No. 4NBER w33142

Liquidity Shocks and Firm Exports: Evidence from Cash Shortages During India’s Demonetization

流动性冲击与企业出口：来自印度废钞期间现金短缺的证据

Ritam Chaurey, Ryan Kim, Pravin Krishna

Programs：CF, DEV, IFM, ITI, ME；JEL：E50, F1, F14, O16

Abstract

This paper examines how liquidity shocks caused by currency shortages impact exports. We explore this in the context of India’s 2016 currency demonetization, a sudden government policy announcement that led to 86% of the country’s currency in circulation being rendered illegal within hours. Our analysis uses novel data, including high-frequency customs transaction records matched with exporting firms and their balance sheets, as well as with inter-district domestic trade. While the cash shortages do not directly affect exporting firms, we find a significant and immediate decline in real exports for firms whose domestic customers experience liquidity shocks.

中文摘要

本文研究了由现金短缺引起的流动性冲击如何影响出口。我们在印度2016年货币废钞的背景下探讨了这一点，这是一项突然的政府政策公告，导致该国流通中86%的货币在数小时内变为非法。我们的分析使用了新颖的数据，包括与出口企业及其资产负债表以及地区间国内贸易相匹配的高频海关交易记录。虽然现金短缺并不直接影响出口企业，但我们发现，对于其国内客户遭受流动性冲击的企业，实际出口出现了显著且即时的下降。

No. 5NBER w33141

Information Span in Credit Market Competition

信贷市场竞争中的信息广度

Zhiguo He, Jing Huang, Cecilia Parlatore

Programs：CF, IO, PR；JEL：G21, L13, L52, O33, O36

Abstract

We develop a credit market competition model that distinguishes between information span (breadth) and signal precision (quality), capturing the rise of fintech/nonbank lending where traditionally subjective (soft) information is transformed into objective (hard) data. Borrower quality depends on multidimensional fundamentals, assessed through hard or soft signals. Two banks observe private hard signals, but only the specialized bank receives a soft signal. Expanding the span of hard information enables the non-specialized bank to evaluate characteristics previously only available to the specialist, and reducing its winners curse. By contrast, greater precision of hard signals strengthens the specialized banks informational advantage.

中文摘要

我们构建了一个信贷市场竞争模型，该模型区分了信息广度（宽度）和信号精度（质量），捕捉了金融科技/非银行贷款的兴起，其中传统上主观的（软）信息被转化为客观的（硬）数据。借款人质量取决于多维基本面，通过硬信号或软信号进行评估。两家银行观察到私有的硬信号，但只有专业银行收到一个软信号。扩大硬信息的广度使得非专业银行能够评估以前只有专家才能获得的特征，并减轻其赢家诅咒。相比之下，硬信号精度的提高则加强了专业银行的信息优势。

No. 6NBER w33140

Evaluating Minimum Taxation

评估最低税制

James R. Hines Jr.

Programs：PE；JEL：H21, H25, H87

Abstract

Minimum tax rules constrain only the lowest-tax jurisdictions. Because higher minimum tax rates expand the circle of affected countries and therefore the impact of any further changes, there can be dominated regions over which no parameter values would make a minimum tax efficient. Applying a Taylor approximation to the distribution of statutory corporate tax rates in 2020, the range 4%-27% is a dominated region: there may be an efficient minimum rate below 4%, or higher than 27%, but there is no efficient world minimum tax rate between 4% and 27%. Minimum taxes set at popular rates are particularly inefficient – a minimum tax rate of 15% yields value that, when positive, is equivalent to offsetting less than one percent of the effect of tax competition.

中文摘要

最低税规则仅约束税率最低的司法管辖区。由于更高的最低税率会扩大受影响国家的范围，从而增加任何进一步变化的影响，因此可能存在一个支配区域，在该区域内没有任何参数值能使最低税有效。对2020年法定公司税率的分布应用泰勒近似，4%-27%的范围是一个支配区域：可能存在低于4%或高于27%的有效最低税率，但不存在介于4%和27%之间的有效全球最低税率。设定在流行税率上的最低税尤其低效——15%的最低税率产生的价值，当为正时，相当于抵消了税收竞争影响的不到百分之一。

No. 7NBER w33139

Concentrating Intelligence: Scaling and Market Structure in Artificial Intelligence

集中智能：人工智能中的规模与市场结构

Anton Korinek, Jai Vipra

Programs：CF, IO, PR；JEL：D43, K21, L4, L86, O33

Abstract

This paper examines the evolving structure and competition dynamics of the rapidly growing market for foundation models, with a focus on large language models (LLMs). We describe the technological characteristics that shape the AI industry and have given rise to fierce competition among the leading players. The paper analyzes the cost structure of foundation models, emphasizing the importance of key inputs such as computational resources, data, and talent, and identifies significant economies of scale and scope that may create a tendency towards greater market concentration in the future. We explore two concerns for competition, the risk of market tipping and the implications of vertical integration, and we evaluate policy remedies that aim to maintain a competitive landscape. Looking ahead to increasingly transformative AI systems, we discuss how market concentration could translate into unprecedented accumulation of power, highlighting the broader societal stakes of competition policy.

中文摘要

本文考察了快速增长的基础模型市场（特别是大型语言模型）的演变结构和竞争动态。我们描述了塑造人工智能行业并导致领先参与者之间激烈竞争的技术特征。本文分析了基础模型的成本结构，强调了计算资源、数据和人才等关键投入的重要性，并识别了可能导致未来市场集中度提高的显著规模经济和范围经济。我们探讨了竞争的两个担忧，即市场倾斜的风险和垂直整合的影响，并评估了旨在维持竞争格局的政策补救措施。展望未来日益变革性的人工智能系统，我们讨论了市场集中度如何转化为前所未有的权力积累，强调了竞争政策的更广泛社会利害关系。

No. 8NBER w33138

Industrial Policies and Innovation: Evidence from the Global Automobile Industry

产业政策与创新：来自全球汽车行业的证据

Panle Jia Barwick, Hyuk-Soo Kwon, Shanjun Li, Yucheng Wang, Nahim B. Zahur

Programs：EEE, IO, PR；JEL：H20, L5, L60, L62, O3

Abstract

This paper examines the impact of industrial policies (IPs) on innovation in the global automobile industry. We compile the first comprehensive dataset linking global IPs with patent data related to the auto industry from 2008 to 2023. We document a major shift in policy focus: by 2022, nearly half of all IPs targeted electric vehicles (EV)-related sectors, up from almost none in 2008. In the meantime, there has been a clear technological transition from internal combustion engine (GV) technologies to EV innovations. Our analysis finds a positive relationship between policy support and innovation activity. At the country level, a one-standard-deviation increase in five-year cumulative EV-targeted IPs is associated with a four-percent rise in new EV patent applications. Firm-level analyses (using OLS, IV, and PPML) indicate that a ten-percent increase in EV financial incentives received by automakers and EV battery producers leads to a similar four-percent increase in EV innovations. We confirm the importance of path dependence in the direction of technology change in the automobile industry but find no evidence that EV-targeted IPs stimulate innovation in GV technologies.

中文摘要

本文考察了产业政策对全球汽车行业创新的影响。我们编制了首个将全球产业政策与2008年至2023年汽车行业相关专利数据联系起来的综合数据集。我们记录了政策重点的重大转变：到2022年，近一半的产业政策针对电动汽车相关领域，而2008年几乎为零。与此同时，从内燃机技术到电动汽车创新的技术转型已经清晰可见。我们的分析发现政策支持与创新活动之间存在正相关关系。在国家层面，五年累计电动汽车目标产业政策每增加一个标准差，与新的电动汽车专利申请量增加百分之四相关。企业层面分析（使用OLS、IV和PPML）表明，汽车制造商和电动汽车电池制造商获得的电动汽车财政激励每增加百分之十，会导致电动汽车创新同样增加约百分之四。我们确认了汽车行业技术变革方向中路径依赖的重要性，但未发现电动汽车目标产业政策刺激内燃机技术创新的证据。

No. 9NBER w33137

Income Inequality and Job Creation

收入不平等与就业创造

Sebastian K. Doerr, Thomas Drechsel, Donggyu Lee

Programs：EFG, ME；JEL：D22, D31, E44, E60, L25

Abstract

We propose a novel channel through which rising income inequality affects job creation and macroeconomic outcomes. High-income households save relatively more in stocks and bonds but less in bank deposits. A rising top income share thereby increases the relative financing cost for bank-dependent firms, which in turn create fewer jobs. Exploiting variation in top income shares across US states and an instrumental variable strategy, we provide evidence for this channel. We then build a general equilibrium macro model with heterogeneous households and heterogeneous firms and calibrate it to our empirical estimates. The model shows that the secular rise in top incomes accounts for 13% of the decline in the employment share of small firms since 1980. Through the new channel, rising inequality also reduces the labor share and aggregate output. Model experiments show that ignoring the link between inequality and job creation understates welfare effects of income redistribution.

中文摘要

我们提出了一个新的渠道，通过该渠道，不断上升的收入不平等会影响就业创造和宏观经济结果。高收入家庭相对更多地储蓄于股票和债券，而较少存入银行存款。因此，顶层收入份额的上升增加了依赖银行融资的企业的相对融资成本，这些企业进而会创造更少的就业岗位。利用美国各州顶层收入份额的差异以及工具变量策略，我们为这一渠道提供了证据。随后，我们构建了一个包含异质性家庭和异质性企业的一般均衡宏观模型，并根据我们的实证估计对其进行校准。该模型表明，自1980年以来，顶层收入的长期上升解释了小企业就业份额下降的13%。通过这一新渠道，不断上升的不平等还降低了劳动收入份额和总产出。模型实验显示，忽视不平等与就业创造之间的联系会低估收入再分配的福利效应。

No. 10NBER w33136

Central Bank Information or Neo-Fisher Effect?

央行信息效应还是新费雪效应？

Stephanie Schmitt-Grohé, Martín Uribe

Programs：EFG, IFM, ME；JEL：E3, E5

Abstract

The central bank information (CBI) effect and the neo-Fisher effect produce similar outcomes: under both, a monetary tightening fails to reduce inflation and output. Separate estimates of these effects run the risk of confounding one with the other. To disentangle these two channels, we introduce into a new-Keynesian model, among other sources of fluctuations, a permanent monetary shock that generates neo-Fisher effects and a preference shock to which the central bank responds that creates CBI effects. We estimate the model on postwar quarterly U.S. data. We find that both effects are important: The neo-Fisher effect explains about one third of changes in inflation. And shutting down the Fed’s direct response to the preference shock causes a doubling of the variance of inflation and a twenty percent increase in the variance of output. The results are robust to assuming that private agents have imperfect information, suggesting the presence of a central bank information channel, but the absence of a central bank information advantage, at least at quarterly frequency.

中文摘要

央行信息效应和新费雪效应会产生相似的结果：在两种效应下，货币紧缩都未能降低通胀和产出。分别估计这些效应存在将两者混淆的风险。为了区分这两个渠道，我们在一个新凯恩斯模型中引入了多种波动来源，其中包括一个产生新费雪效应的永久性货币冲击，以及一个央行据此做出反应并产生央行信息效应的偏好冲击。我们利用战后美国季度数据对模型进行估计。我们发现这两种效应都很重要：新费雪效应解释了约三分之一的通胀变化。而关闭美联储对偏好冲击的直接反应会导致通胀方差翻倍，产出方差增加20%。该结果在假设私人主体拥有不完全信息时依然稳健，这表明存在央行信息渠道，但不存在央行信息优势，至少在季度频率上是如此。

No. 11NBER w33135

Education and Preferences for Desired Traits in Children

教育与对儿童期望特质的偏好

Naci H. Mocan, Nur Orak

Programs：CH, ED, EH, LS, PE；JEL：D0, I20, Z10

Abstract

There is a strong correlation between the preferences and beliefs of parents and their children. Also, children of more educated parents tend to have different preferences and beliefs than those of less educated parents. However, evidence on whether education influences adults’ preferences regarding the attributes they wish to instill in children is missing. This paper seeks to fill that gap. Utilizing compulsory education reforms implemented in 19 European countries and data from the Integrated Values Survey—which includes questions about essential qualities children should be encouraged to learn at home—we demonstrate that women who acquired additional education due to these reforms are more likely to prioritize imagination, determination and perseverance, and a sense of responsibility as important traits to instill in children. Conversely, they are less inclined to regard religiosity, obedience, and unselfishness as essential attributes for children to learn at home. In addition, we find that education reduces women's religiosity. These effects are primarily driven by Catholic women and women living in majority-Catholic countries. In contrast, education does not have a significant impact on men's religiosity or the child attributes they consider important.

Elite Strategies for Big Shocks: The Case of the Fall of the Ming

应对重大冲击的精英策略：以明朝灭亡为例

Carol H. Shiue, Wolfgang Keller

Programs：CH, DAE, POL；JEL：N35

Abstract

This paper documents persistence in the power of elite families in Central China despite dynastic change. We study the impact of the fall of the Ming Dynasty (1368-1644) on couples and their descendants (treatment of people), and present evidence on the response of multigenerational family lines to a big shock. Local Ming elites suffered a decline in influence in the short run, but in the long-run their descendants recovered and tightened their grip on power in their role as the elites of the new Qing Dynasty (1644-1911). In contrast to the recovery of family lines, the fall of Ming had a more persistently negative impact on the regions that historically were most strongly negatively affected by the shock (treatment of regions). The paper suggests that the elite reversal is due to trauma caused by Ming destruction that shifted norms towards the most socially respectable career paths based on the civil service exam; these norms were, to a greater degree, intergenerationally transmitted in family lines that suffered more from the destruction in the fall of the Ming dynasty.

Small Changes, Big Impact: Nudging Employees Toward Sustainable Behaviors

小改变，大影响：助推员工走向可持续行为

Laura Cappellucci, Lan Ha, Jeremy Honig, Christopher R. Knittel, Amy Vetter, Richard Wilner

Programs：EEE, IO, LS, PE；JEL：C93, Q40, Q53

Abstract

We designed and conducted three randomized control trials in partnership with a large biopharmaceutical company operating over 160 plasma donation centers, with the aim of promoting sustainable behaviors in a workplace setting. Specifically, we focused on reducing operational errors that led to dropped collection materials, long freezer door open times, and improper recycling practices. To achieve these goals, we employed social norms to nudge employees towards 1) reducing wasted collection materials, 2) minimizing the duration of freezer door openings, and 3) improving recycling practices. We found an average reduction of roughly 70 percent in plastic waste from dropped collection materials and the costs associated with these materials. The frequency of freezer door alarms decreased by over 80 percent, and the duration of alarms decreased by over 45 percent, depending on the empirical specification. We also observed a roughly 40 percent reduction in uncollapsed cardboard, with no statistically significant results for other types of contaminants. Importantly, for each of the interventions, we do not find evidence that the treatment effects waned over time or affected business operations. Our study provides significant implications for promoting sustainable behaviors in a workplace setting, filling an important gap in the literature on the effectiveness of nudges in the workplace.

Expecting Climate Change: A Nationwide Field Experiment in the Housing Market

预期气候变化：住房市场中的全国性实地实验

Daryl Fairweather, Matthew E. Kahn, Robert D. Metcalfe, Sebastian Sandoval Olascoaga

Programs：EEE；JEL：Q54, R2

Abstract

Climate change presents new risks for property in the United States. Due to the high cost and sometimes unavailability of location-specific property risk data, home buyers can greatly benefit from acquiring knowledge about these risks. To explore this, a large-scale nationwide natural field experiment was conducted through Redfin to estimate the causal impact of providing home-specific flood risk information on the behavior of home buyers in terms of their search, bidding, and purchasing decisions. Redfin randomly assigned 17.5 million users to receive information detailing the flood risk associated with the properties they searched for on the platform. Our analysis reveals several key findings: (1) the flood risk information influences every stage of the house buying process, including the initial search, bidding activities, and final purchase; (2) individuals are willing to make trade-offs concerning property amenities in order to own a property with a lower flood risk; (3) the impact of the flood risk information on behavior is more pronounced for users conducting searches in high flood risk areas, but does not differ significantly between buyers in Republican and Democrat Counties; and (4) the information resulted in changes to property prices and altered the market's hedonic equilibrium, providing a new finding that climate adaptation can be forward-thinking and proactive.

Using Information to Curb Racial Discrimination

利用信息遏制种族歧视

Christopher R. Knittel, Donald MacKenzie, Michiko Namazu, Bora Ozaltun, Dan Svirsky, Stephen Zoepf

Programs：EEE, IO, LS, PE；JEL：D83, J71, L92

Abstract

We test whether more information about customers decreases racial bias. Our setting is the market for shared mobility services. Prior work by Ge et al. (2020) found that Uber drivers are two times more likely to cancel a ride if the passenger’s name is one used predominantly by African Americans. In a randomized control trial, we test whether two alterations to the Uber platform app reduce racial discrimination. Within the standard Uber app, drivers see only the passenger’s rating before accepting a ride. Once they accept the ride, they see the name of the passenger. In the first intervention, we increased the size of the font of the rating to draw attention to the quality of the passenger. In the second intervention, the passenger’s name appears from the beginning. Using the control group observations, we confirmed that the more likely African Americans were to use a name, the more likely a driver cancels the ride. However, increasing the font size of the passenger’s rating eliminates this racial bias. In contrast, we do not find much evidence that showing the name on the initial screen reduces or increases cancellation rates.
