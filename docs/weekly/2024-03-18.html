Tunneling and Hidden Profits in Health Care

医疗保健中的利益输送与隐藏利润

Ashvin Gandhi, Andrew Olenski

Programs：EH；JEL：G3, I10, I11, L24

Abstract

This study examines whether healthcare providers tunnel profits and assets to commonly-owned related parties by making inflated payments for their goods and services. Such practices allow providers to understate their profitability—which may encourage regulators to increase reimbursements and relax quality standards—and shield assets from malpractice liability. Using uniquely detailed nursing home financial data, we find evidence of widespread tunneling to related-party real estate and management companies. Our estimates suggest that 68% of nursing home profits are tunneled to related parties and that accounting for tunneled profits and assets raises the implied typical investment IRR from 4.83% to 13.11%.

中文摘要

本研究考察了医疗保健提供者是否通过向共同拥有的关联方支付虚高的商品和服务费用，从而向其输送利润和资产。这种做法使提供者能够低估其盈利能力——这可能会鼓励监管机构提高报销标准和放宽质量标准——并将资产从医疗事故责任中隔离出来。利用极其详细的养老院财务数据，我们发现了向关联方房地产和管理公司进行广泛利益输送的证据。我们的估计表明，68%的养老院利润被输送给关联方，而将输送的利润和资产考虑在内后，隐含的典型投资内部收益率从4.83%提高到13.11%。

The Impact of Municipal Broadband Restrictions on COVID-19 Labor Market Outcomes

市政宽带限制对新冠疫情劳动力市场结果的影响

Saket S. Hegde, Jessica Van Parys

Programs：LS；JEL：J2, K2, L86

Abstract

The COVID-19 pandemic initiated a trend in “work-from-home (WFH),” but workers need reliable and fast internet connections (e.g., broadband) to work from home. Yet, as of January 2020, 18 states had legally restricted local governments and cooperatives from building their own broadband infrastructure and/or providing broadband internet to their communities. Such policies reduced broadband access and competition in states with restrictions compared to states without restrictions leading up to the pandemic (Whitacre and Gallardo 2020). We use CPS data from 2018-2023 to estimate a dynamic difference-in-differences model that shows how labor force participation (LFP) rates changed in states with and without broadband restrictions, before and after the COVID-19 pandemic. We focus on married women with children, a population with more elastic labor supply that may especially value the flexibility that WFH offers (Dettling 2017). We find that married mothers’ LFP and employment decreased by 1.7% and 2.2%, respectively, in states with restrictions after the pandemic compared to states without restrictions. Labor force outcomes for women without children and married men with children were unaffected by broadband restrictions. The results suggest that married women with children were less able to remain in the workforce in states where their ability to WFH was limited by broadband restrictions.

中文摘要

新冠疫情开启了一种“居家办公”的趋势，但工人需要可靠且快速的互联网连接（例如宽带）才能在家工作。然而，截至2020年1月，有18个州在法律上限制了地方政府和合作社自行建设宽带基础设施和/或向其社区提供宽带互联网。与没有限制的州相比，此类政策在疫情前减少了有限制州的宽带接入和竞争（Whitacre and Gallardo 2020）。我们使用2018-2023年的当前人口调查数据来估计一个动态双重差分模型，该模型展示了在新冠疫情前后，有和没有宽带限制的州的劳动力参与率如何变化。我们重点关注有孩子的已婚女性，这是一个劳动力供给弹性更大、可能特别看重居家办公所提供的灵活性的群体（Dettling 2017）。我们发现，与没有限制的州相比，在疫情后有限制州的州，已婚母亲的劳动力参与率和就业率分别下降了1.7%和2.2%。没有孩子的女性和有孩子的已婚男性的劳动力市场结果未受宽带限制的影响。结果表明，在宽带限制限制了她们居家办公能力的州，有孩子的已婚女性更难以留在劳动力队伍中。

No. 3NBER w32256

Banks in Space

空间中的银行

Ezra Oberfield, Esteban Rossi-Hansberg, Nicholas Trachter, Derek T. Wenning

Programs：CF, EFG, IO, ITI；JEL：D20, G21, G24, L23, L25, R12, R19

Abstract

We study the spatial expansion of banks in response to banking deregulation in the 1980s and 90s. During this period, large banks expanded rapidly, mostly by adding new branches in new locations, while many small banks exited. We document that large banks sorted into the densest markets, but that sorting weakened over time as large banks expanded to more marginal markets in search of locations with a relative abundance of retail deposits. This allowed large banks to reduce their dependence on expensive wholesale funding and grow further. To rationalize these patterns we propose a theory of multi-branch banks that sort into heterogeneous locations. Our theory yields two forms of sorting. First, span-of-control sorting incentivizes top firms to select the largest markets and smaller banks the more marginal ones. Second, mismatch sorting incentivizes banks to locate in more marginal locations, where deposits are abundant relative to loan demand, to better align their deposits and loans and minimize wholesale funding. Together, these two forms of sorting account well for the sorting patterns we document in the data.

中文摘要

我们研究了20世纪80年代和90年代银行业放松管制后银行的空间扩张。在此期间，大型银行迅速扩张，主要通过在新地点增设新分行，而许多小银行则退出了市场。我们记录了大型银行向最密集市场聚集的现象，但这种聚集随时间推移而减弱，因为大型银行扩张到更边缘的市场，寻找零售存款相对充裕的地点。这使得大型银行能够减少对昂贵批发融资的依赖并进一步增长。为了合理化这些模式，我们提出了一个多分行银行理论，这些银行会聚集到异质性地点。我们的理论产生了两种聚集形式。首先，控制跨度聚集激励顶级公司选择最大的市场，而较小的银行则选择更边缘的市场。其次，错配聚集激励银行定位于更边缘的地点，即存款相对于贷款需求充裕的地方，以更好地匹配其存款和贷款并最小化批发融资。这两种聚集形式共同很好地解释了我们在数据中记录的聚集模式。

No. 4NBER w32255

Scenarios for the Transition to AGI

向通用人工智能过渡的情景

Anton Korinek, Donghyun Suh

Programs：DAE, EFG, LS, PR；JEL：E24, J23, J24, O33, O41

Abstract

We analyze how output and wages behave under different scenarios for technological progress that may culminate in Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), defined as the ability of AI systems to perform all tasks that humans can perform. We assume that human work can be decomposed into atomistic tasks that differ in their complexity. Advances in technology make ever more complex tasks amenable to automation. The effects on wages depend on a race between automation and capital accumulation. If automation proceeds sufficiently slowly, then there is always enough work for humans, and wages may rise forever. By contrast, if the complexity of tasks that humans can perform is bounded and full automation is reached, then wages collapse. But declines may occur even before if large-scale automation outpaces capital accumulation and makes labor too abundant. Automating productivity growth may lead to broad-based gains in the returns to all factors. By contrast, bottlenecks to growth from irreproducible scarce factors may exacerbate the decline in wages.

中文摘要

我们分析了在可能最终实现通用人工智能的技术进步的不同情景下，产出和工资如何变化。通用人工智能被定义为人工智能系统能够执行人类所能执行的所有任务的能力。我们假设人类工作可以分解为原子化任务，这些任务在复杂性上有所不同。技术进步使得越来越复杂的任务变得适合自动化。对工资的影响取决于自动化与资本积累之间的竞赛。如果自动化进展足够缓慢，那么人类总有足够的工作可做，工资可能会永远上涨。相反，如果人类能执行的任务的复杂性存在上限，并且实现了完全自动化，那么工资就会崩溃。但即使在完全自动化之前，如果大规模自动化超过资本积累并使劳动力过于充裕，工资也可能下降。自动化带来的生产率增长可能导致所有要素回报的广泛增长。相反，来自不可复制的稀缺要素的增长瓶颈可能会加剧工资的下降。

No. 5NBER w32254

Regulation of Access, Pricing, and Planning of High Voltage Transmission in the U.S.

美国高压输电的准入、定价与规划监管

Joe DeLosa III, Johannes P. Pfeifenberger, Paul Joskow

Programs：EEE, IO；JEL：L51, L94, Q48

Abstract

The U.S. regulation of high-voltage transmission is highly complex and, as a result, generally poorly understood. The complexity is created by separate, but overlapping, jurisdictional authorities of the U.S. federal regulators and those of individual states, districts, and territories. While U.S. federal regulators have authority over stand-alone transmission service and the regional wholesale power markets that use the transmission grid, state regulators have jurisdiction over both (1) retail electricity services that include the distribution network, the retail cost of transmission service, and often generation service; and (2) the permitting of most new transmission facilities within their states’ boundaries. Some of these federal and state regulatory authorities overlap and some of them do not apply to non-jurisdictional transmission providers (such as certain municipal utilities, cooperatives, and federal power marketing agencies) and states (such as Texas) that are not synchronized with the larger regional grid. We summarize this complex structure of transmission regulation in the U.S. and the history of regulations that have created the industry structure and regulatory frameworks that exist today. We provide an overview of how transmission investments are priced and recovered and the planning processes that individual transmission owners and regional grid operators use to plan the necessary expansion of the high-voltage transmission grid. We also point out some of the economic inefficiencies that are created by a combination of balkanized regulatory structures and outdated industry planning practices.

中文摘要

美国对高压输电的监管极为复杂，因此通常难以被充分理解。这种复杂性源于美国联邦监管机构与各州、地区和领地监管机构之间相互独立但又存在重叠的管辖权。联邦监管机构对独立输电服务以及使用输电网络的区域批发电力市场拥有管辖权，而州监管机构则对以下两方面拥有管辖权：（1）包含配电网络、输电服务零售成本以及通常还包括发电服务的零售电力服务；（2）其州界内大多数新建输电设施的许可审批。这些联邦和州监管权力有些相互重叠，有些则不适用于非管辖范围内的输电供应商（例如某些市政公用事业、合作社和联邦电力营销机构）以及不与更大区域电网同步的州（例如得克萨斯州）。我们总结了美国这种复杂的输电监管结构，以及造就了当今行业结构和监管框架的监管历史。我们概述了输电投资如何定价和回收，以及各个输电业主和区域电网运营商用于规划高压输电网络必要扩建的规划流程。我们还指出了碎片化的监管结构与过时的行业规划实践相结合所造成的一些经济低效问题。

Re-assessing the Spatial Mismatch Hypothesis

重新评估空间错配假说

David Card, Jesse Rothstein, Moises Yi

Programs：LS；JEL：J31, R23

Abstract

We use detailed location information from the Longitudinal Employer-Household Dynamics (LEHD) database to develop new evidence on the effects of spatial mismatch on the relative earnings of Black workers in large US cities. We classify workplaces by the size of the pay premiums they offer in a two-way fixed effects model, providing a simple metric for defining “good” jobs. We show that: (a) Black workers earn nearly the same average wage premiums as whites; (b) in most cities Black workers live closer to jobs, and closer to good jobs, than do whites; (c) Black workers typically commute shorter distances than whites; and (d) people who commute further earn higher average pay premiums, but the elasticity with respect to distance traveled is slightly lower for Black workers. We conclude that geographic proximity to good jobs is unlikely to be a major source of the racial earnings gaps in major U.S. cities today.

中文摘要

我们利用纵向雇主-家庭动态数据库（LEHD）中的详细位置信息，就空间错配对美国大城市黑人劳动者相对收入的影响提供了新的证据。我们在一个双向固定效应模型中，根据工作场所提供的薪酬溢价大小对其进行分类，从而为定义“好”工作提供了一个简单的衡量标准。我们表明：（a）黑人劳动者的平均工资溢价几乎与白人相同；（b）在大多数城市，黑人劳动者比白人居住地更靠近工作地点，也更靠近好工作；（c）黑人劳动者的通勤距离通常比白人短；（d）通勤距离更远的人获得的平均薪酬溢价更高，但黑人劳动者相对于通勤距离的弹性略低。我们得出结论，地理上靠近好工作不太可能是当今美国主要城市种族收入差距的主要来源。

Non-robustness of Diffusion Estimates on Networks with Measurement Error

存在测量误差的网络扩散估计的非稳健性

Arun G. Chandrasekhar, Paul Goldsmith-Pinkham, Tyler McCormick, Samuel A. Thau, Jerry Wei

Programs：DEV, EFG, ME, PR；JEL：C01, D85, E0

Abstract

Network diffusion models are used to study things like disease transmission, information spread, and technology adoption. However, small amounts of mismeasurement are extremely likely in the networks constructed to operationalize these models. We show that estimates of diffusions are highly non-robust to this measurement error. First, we show that even when measurement error is vanishingly small, such that the share of missed links is close to zero, forecasts about the extent of diffusion will greatly underestimate the truth. Second, a small mismeasurement in the identity of the initial seed generates a large shift in the locations of expected diffusion path. We show that both of these results still hold when the vanishing measurement error is only local in nature. Such non-robustness in forecasting exists even under conditions where the basic reproductive number is consistently estimable. Possible solutions, such as estimating the measurement error or implementing widespread detection efforts, still face difficulties because the number of missed links are so small. Finally, we conduct Monte Carlo simulations on simulated networks, and real networks from three settings: travel data from the COVID-19 pandemic in the western US, a mobile phone marketing campaign in rural India, and in an insurance experiment in China.

中文摘要

网络扩散模型被用于研究疾病传播、信息扩散和技术采用等现象。然而，在为实现这些模型而构建的网络中，出现少量测量误差的可能性极高。我们表明，扩散估计对这些测量误差高度不稳健。首先，我们证明，即使测量误差小到可以忽略不计，以至于遗漏链接的比例接近于零，关于扩散程度的预测也会严重低估真实情况。其次，初始种子节点身份的一个微小测量误差会导致预期扩散路径位置发生巨大偏移。我们表明，即使这种可忽略的测量误差仅具有局部性质，这两个结果仍然成立。即使在基本再生数可以被一致估计的条件下，这种预测的非稳健性依然存在。可能的解决方案，例如估计测量误差或实施广泛的检测工作，仍然面临困难，因为遗漏的链接数量非常少。最后，我们在模拟网络以及来自三个真实场景的网络（美国西部COVID-19大流行期间的旅行数据、印度农村的一次手机营销活动以及中国的一个保险实验）上进行了蒙特卡洛模拟。

No. 9NBER w32250

Size Matters: Matching Externalities and the Advantages of Large Labor Markets

规模重要：匹配外部性与大型劳动力市场的优势

Enrico Moretti, Moises Yi

Programs：LS, PE；JEL：H0, J0, R0

Abstract

Economists have long hypothesized that large and thick labor markets facilitate the matching between workers and firms. We use administrative data from the LEHD to compare the job search outcomes of workers originally in large and small markets who lost their jobs due to a firm closure. We define a labor market as the Commuting Zone×industry pair in the quarter before the closure. To account for the possible sorting of high-quality workers into larger markets, the effect of market size is identified by comparing workers in large and small markets within the same CZ, conditional on workers fixed effects. In the six quarters before their firm’s closure, workers in small and large markets have a similar probability of employment and quarterly earnings. Following the closure, workers in larger markets experience significantly shorter non-employment spells and smaller earning losses than workers in smaller markets, indicating that larger markets partially insure workers against idiosyncratic employment shocks. A 1 percent increase in market size results in a 0.015 and 0.023 percentage points increase in the 1-year re-employment probability of high school and college graduates, respectively. Displaced workers in larger markets also experience a significantly lower need for relocation to a different CZ. Conditional on finding a new job, the quality of the new worker-firm match is higher in larger markets, as proxied by a higher probability that the new match lasts more than one year; the new industry is the same as the old one; and the new industry is a “good fit” for the worker’s college major. Consistent with the notion that market size should be particularly consequential for more specialized workers, we find that the effects are larger in industries where human capital is more specialized and less portable. Our findings may help explain the geographical agglomeration of industries--especially those that make intensive use of highly specialized workers--and validate one of the mechanisms that urban economists have proposed for the existence of agglomeration economies.

中文摘要

经济学家长期以来假设，大型且厚实的劳动力市场有助于促进工人与企业之间的匹配。我们使用LEHD的行政数据，比较了原本处于大型和小型市场中因企业倒闭而失业的工人的求职结果。我们将劳动力市场定义为倒闭前一个季度的通勤区×行业配对。为了考虑高质量工人可能向大型市场自选择的问题，我们通过比较同一通勤区内大型和小型市场的工人，并控制工人固定效应，来识别市场规模效应。在企业倒闭前的六个季度，小型和大型市场的工人具有相似的就业概率和季度收入。在企业倒闭后，大型市场的工人经历的非就业期显著更短，收入损失也小于小型市场的工人，这表明大型市场为工人提供了部分抵御特殊就业冲击的保障。市场规模每增加1%，高中和大学毕业生的一年内再就业概率分别提高0.015和0.023个百分点。大型市场中的失业工人搬迁到不同通勤区的需求也显著更低。在找到新工作的条件下，大型市场中新的工人-企业匹配质量更高，其代理指标包括新匹配持续超过一年的概率更高、新行业与旧行业相同、以及新行业与工人的大学专业“匹配良好”。与市场规模对更专业化工人影响尤为显著的观念一致，我们发现，在人力资本更专业化且可移植性更低的行业中，这种效应更大。我们的发现可能有助于解释产业的地理集聚现象——尤其是那些密集使用高度专业化工人的产业——并验证了城市经济学家提出的关于集聚经济存在的一种机制。

No. 10NBER w32249

The Effect of Price Caps on Advertising to Physicians: Evidence from the 340b Drug Pricing Program

价格上限对医生广告的影响：来自340b药品定价计划的证据

Sylvia Hristakeva, Julie Holland Mortimer, Eric Yde

Programs：IO；JEL：I1, I11, L0, L2, M30, M37, M39

Abstract

We study the effect of price caps on the provision of costly effort by pharmaceutical firms using variation in drug discounts generated by a price regulation program that allows eligible hospitals to purchase outpatient drugs at steep discounts. These discounts directly affect drug manufacturers’ markups, and may change firms’ incentives to exert promotional effort targeted towards physicians at these hospitals. We find that the effects of price regulation on pharmaceutical firm effort depend crucially on the design of the regulations. Using detailed data on marketing payments from pharmaceutical firms to physicians, we observe that physicians receive 13% fewer promotional payments after their hospitals take up the program. The design of the price caps imply that discounts tend to increase with a drug’s age. Consistent with theoretical predictions, we find that pharmaceutical firms shift promotional payments away from older drugs and towards newer drugs, which are less affected by the price caps. Understanding these strategic, non-price adjustments is important for policymakers seeking to design effective regulations targeting specific products.

Crises have emphasized the role of central banks in promoting financial stability, as financial stability is key to achieving price and economic stability, but this role increases risks to independence. Goals for central banks other than price and economic stability, complemented by financial stability, can make it more difficult for them to stabilize both inflation and economic activity.

在21世纪的前二十五年里，世界经济经历了数代人以来最严重的金融危机、一场全球大流行病以及通胀的再度抬头，这为中央银行提供了重要启示。价格稳定具有重大益处，并且是央行的职责所在。在复杂且不确定的环境中实现价格稳定，需要可信地承诺一个名义锚，对通胀做出强有力的反应，并预防性地抑制经济过热。相关的挑战意味着，央行沟通和透明度是货币政策策略和战术的关键要素。危机凸显了央行在促进金融稳定方面的作用，因为金融稳定是实现价格和经济稳定的关键，但这一角色增加了独立性面临的风险。除了价格和经济稳定（辅以金融稳定）之外，为央行设定的其他目标，可能会使它们更难同时稳定通胀和经济活动。

The Effect of Early Childhood Programs on Third-Grade Test Scores: Evidence from Transitional Kindergarten in Michigan

早期儿童项目对三年级考试成绩的影响：来自密歇根州过渡性幼儿园的证据

Jordan S. Berne, Brian A. Jacob, Tareena Musaddiq, Anna Shapiro, Christina Weiland

Programs：CH, ED, LS, PE；JEL：I20, I21, I24, I28, J01

Abstract

Transitional Kindergarten (TK) is a relatively recent entrant into the U.S. early education landscape, combining features of public pre-K and regular kindergarten. We provide the first estimates of the impact of Michigan’s TK program on 3rd grade test scores. Using an augmented regression discontinuity design, we find that TK improves 3rd grade math scores by 0.29 standard deviations relative to a counterfactual that includes other formal and informal learning options. This impact is notably large relative to the prior pre-K literature. Estimates for English Language Arts (ELA) are imprecise but suggestive of a positive effect as well.
