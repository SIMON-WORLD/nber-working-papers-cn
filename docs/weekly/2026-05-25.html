The Sisyphean Pursuit of Evidence for Poverty Traps

为贫困陷阱寻找证据的西西弗斯式追求

Dean Karlan, Amol Singh Raswan, Christopher R. Udry

Programs：DEV；JEL：I32, I38, J24, O12, R23

Abstract

Much of development economics, both micro and macro, posits theories and explores the empirics of poverty traps. A common theory centers around asset-threshold poverty traps in which marginal returns to capital increase sharply over a certain threshold and financial markets are incomplete. This combination leads households above this threshold to prosper while those below continuously fall back, trapped in poverty. Yet in economics, as best as we can tell, far more papers assume such thresholds than empirically demonstrate them. In a key recent exception, Balboni et al. (2022) (hereinafter “BBBGH”) argues that data from Bangladesh identifies clear evidence of such a threshold at slightly above the cost of a cow for recent recipients of a big push transfer program. We apply this analysis to data from seven other similar programs in low-income countries and find no evidence of an asset-threshold poverty trap. We then return to BBBGH and argue that their data do not support the identification of a poverty trap, with the point of disagreement being a log-transformation challenge and a potential geographic confound. In long-term representative rural panel data from Bangladesh and Ghana we find no evidence of divergence away from any particular threshold. While the threshold theory is undoubtedly true for some households, we argue that the pursuit of clear evidence of an asset-threshold poverty trap for a broad population is likely an aspirational goal rendered unachievable by the intersection of a multitude of possible mechanisms as well as heterogeneous agents.

中文摘要

发展经济学的大部分研究，无论是微观还是宏观，都提出了贫困陷阱的理论并探索其经验证据。一个常见的理论围绕资产阈值贫困陷阱展开，在该陷阱中，资本的边际回报在超过某个阈值后急剧增加，且金融市场不完善。这种组合导致高于此阈值的家庭繁荣，而低于此阈值的家庭则持续倒退，陷入贫困陷阱。然而，在经济学中，据我们所知，假设存在此类阈值的论文远多于经验证明其存在的论文。在最近一个关键例外中，Balboni等人 (2022)（以下简称“BBBGH”）认为，来自孟加拉国的数据为近期接受大推动转移支付项目的受助者识别出了略高于一头牛成本的明确阈值证据。我们将此分析应用于低收入国家其他七个类似项目的数据，并未发现资产阈值贫困陷阱的证据。然后，我们回到BBBGH，认为他们的数据并不支持贫困陷阱的识别，分歧点在于对数转换挑战和潜在的地理混杂因素。在来自孟加拉国和加纳的长期代表性农村面板数据中，我们没有发现偏离任何特定阈值的分化证据。虽然阈值理论对某些家庭无疑是正确的，但我们认为，为广泛人群寻找资产阈值贫困陷阱的明确证据很可能是一个因多种可能机制与异质性主体的交织而无法实现的理想目标。

Distribution Costs

分销成本

Cian Ruane, Alessandra Peter

Programs：EFG；JEL：O11, R40

Abstract

We provide the first direct estimates of distribution expenses incurred by manufacturing plants and quantify their importance for aggregate consumption and measured misallocation. Using a novel measure from the Indian Annual Survey of Industries, we document three facts: distribution expenses amount to over half of labor costs, are over three times larger as a share of sales for plants in the largest decile relative to the smallest, and declined by one third from 2000 to 2010. We develop a model of heterogeneous manufacturing firms that rely on distribution services to sell across space. The improvements in distribution over that time period raised manufacturing consumption by 24.5%. The gains materialize quickly, but unevenly: large firms expand while many small local firms shrink or exit. Distribution costs also matter for measured misallocation: standard TFPR measures generate spurious dispersion and a positive relationship with size. In the ASI, accounting for distribution costs lowers measured TFPR dispersion by 5.1% and the elasticity of TFPR with respect to plant size by 7.0%.

中文摘要

我们提供了制造业工厂所产生分销费用的首次直接估计，并量化了其对总消费和测算资源错配的重要性。利用来自印度年度工业调查的一个新颖指标，我们记录了三个事实：分销费用超过劳动力成本的一半；对于最大十分位数的工厂，其占销售额的比例是最小十分位数工厂的三倍以上；并且从2000年到2010年下降了三分之一。我们建立了一个异质性制造业企业的模型，这些企业依赖分销服务在空间上进行销售。在此期间分销的改善使制造业消费提高了24.5%。这些收益迅速实现，但并不均衡：大型企业扩张，而许多小型本地企业收缩或退出。分销成本对测算的资源错配也很重要：标准的TFPR指标会产生虚假的离散度以及与规模的正相关关系。在ASI中，考虑分销成本后，测算的TFPR离散度降低了5.1%，TFPR相对于工厂规模的弹性降低了7.0%。

The Word Is Not Enough: Testing the Effects of Information Treatments on Perceived Corruption in Ukraine

仅凭言语是不够的：检验信息干预对乌克兰腐败感知的影响

Yuriy Gorodnichenko, Ilona Sologoub, Yuriy Fedyk

Programs：DEV；JEL：D73, O17, O52, P2

Abstract

Using a representative sample of more than 7,000 Ukrainians, we study how information treatments affect corruption perceptions and prosocial behavior. We document a large gap between perceived and experienced corruption: while most respondents view corruption as widespread and a major national problem, far fewer report direct exposure. Through a randomized controlled trial, we find that informing citizens about successful prosecutions raises perceived government willingness to fight corruption but does not reduce overall corruption perceptions. Communicating the scale of corruption alone generates no significant effects. Information treatments have little effect on hypothetical or actual donations and volunteering, suggesting a limited pass-through from changed beliefs to prosocial action. Thus, while information interventions can strengthen institutional credibility, they alone are not enough to tangibly improve civic engagement or reduce perceptions of corruption.

中文摘要

利用一个超过7000名乌克兰人的代表性样本，我们研究了信息干预如何影响腐败感知和亲社会行为。我们记录了感知腐败与经历腐败之间的巨大差距：虽然大多数受访者认为腐败普遍存在且是一个重大的国家问题，但报告直接接触腐败的人数要少得多。通过一项随机对照试验，我们发现，告知公民成功起诉的信息会提高人们对政府打击腐败意愿的感知，但不会降低整体的腐败感知。仅传达腐败规模的信息不会产生显著效果。信息干预对假设性或实际的捐赠和志愿服务几乎没有影响，这表明从信念改变到亲社会行为的传导有限。因此，虽然信息干预可以增强制度可信度，但仅凭它们本身并不足以切实改善公民参与或减少腐败感知。

Nonresponse Imputations and Related Measurement Issues in the CPI for Shelter

住房CPI中的无应答插补及相关测量问题

Lara Loewenstein, Hugh G. Montag, Randal Verbrugge

Programs：TWP；JEL：C81, E31, R31

Abstract

Shelter is the largest component of US consumer price index (CPI) inflation; therefore, the accuracy of shelter inflation is critical for the accuracy of overall CPI inflation. Nonresponse in the BLS Housing Survey, which underpins the measurement of CPI shelter inflation, has increased since 2000 and now represents roughly 40 percent of total observations. Missing rent data are currently imputed using a class-mean approach based on rent tier, potentially resulting in biased imputations, as we find that nonresponse is correlated with factors beyond rent tier. We study alternative simple imputation methods based on variables correlated with both nonresponse and rent growth, including structure type and tenure length. A simple model demonstrates that alternative methods could yield sharply different index biases. However, in practice, we find that these alternative methods yield similar shelter inflation indexes, suggesting that any index bias may be modest.

中文摘要

住房是美国消费者价格指数通胀的最大组成部分；因此，住房通胀的准确性对于整体CPI通胀的准确性至关重要。支撑CPI住房通胀测量的BLS住房调查中的无应答率自2000年以来有所上升，目前约占总观测值的40%。缺失的租金数据目前使用基于租金等级的类均值方法进行插补，这可能导致有偏的插补，因为我们发现无应答与租金等级以外的因素相关。我们研究了基于与无应答和租金增长均相关的变量（包括建筑类型和租住时长）的替代简单插补方法。一个简单模型表明，替代方法可能产生截然不同的指数偏差。然而，在实践中，我们发现这些替代方法产生了相似的住房通胀指数，这表明任何指数偏差可能都是适度的。

The Price of Protection: Tariff Incidence and Import Collapse under the Infamous Smoot-Hawley Tariff

保护的价格：臭名昭著的斯穆特-霍利关税下的关税归宿与进口崩溃

Kris James Mitchener, Mathieu Pedemonte

Programs：DAE, ITI；JEL：F10, F13, F14, F63, F68, N12, N72

Abstract

Using newly digitized monthly data on the quantities and prices of imports as well as product-level data on tariff rates, we estimate that in the first year after the passage of the Smoot-Hawley Tariff Act, imports facing rate increases fell swiftly and dramatically relative to imports not affected by tariffs: for a one-percentage-point increase in the tariff rate, they declined by an average of 4%. We also estimate that the incidence of Smoot-Hawley was almost entirely borne by U.S. importers. Using an open-economy model, we attribute our high measured short-run trade elasticity of greater than 4 to fixed exchange rates that the U.S. maintained with most trade partners in the first 15 months after enactment. Our model also suggests that Smoot-Hawley accounted for 27% of the decline in total US imports in the first year after enactment. Finally, we construct both partial equilibrium and general equilibrium welfare estimates of Smoot-Hawley. Both methods deliver welfare losses of about 0.2% of GDP, reflecting the high measured elasticity of substitution and low US import-GDP ratio.

中文摘要

利用新数字化的月度进口数量和价格数据以及产品层面的关税率数据，我们估计，在《斯穆特-霍利关税法》通过后的第一年，面临税率提高的进口相对于未受关税影响的进口迅速且显著下降：关税率每提高一个百分点，进口平均下降4%。我们还估计，斯穆特-霍利关税的负担几乎完全由美国进口商承担。通过一个开放经济模型，我们将测量到的高于4的短期贸易弹性归因于美国在法案实施后前15个月与大多数贸易伙伴维持的固定汇率。我们的模型还表明，斯穆特-霍利关税占法案实施后第一年美国总进口下降的27%。最后，我们构建了斯穆特-霍利关税的局部均衡和一般均衡福利估计。两种方法均得出约0.2% GDP的福利损失，这反映了测量到的高替代弹性和美国较低的进口-GDP比率。

Electoral Incentives and Government Transparency: Evidence from Freedom of Information Requests

选举激励与政府透明度：来自信息自由请求的证据

Raymond Fisman, Aron Malatinszky, Eyub Yegen

Programs：LE, PE, POL；JEL：D72, D73, H70

Abstract

Transparency is a basic requirement of government accountability, allowing citizens to access the information necessary to monitor, reward, and punish politicians and bureaucrats. Yet transparency inevitably involves discretion by the same government that is being evaluated. In this paper, we show that reelection incentives affect transparency, by comparing the responsiveness of freedom-of-information requests made via the online platform MuckRock just before state and municipal elections to those filed elsewhere at the same time. Since identical requests are often made simultaneously to many agencies across the U.S., we may assess whether an agency rejects a request while holding constant the timing, submitter, and request content, varying only whether the request is filed shortly before an election. We first document that FOIA rejection rates are positively correlated with a standard measure of state-level corruption. Our main analyses then show that rejections and delays of FOIA requests in state and municipal agencies are higher ahead of (state and city) elections in high-corruption settings. Overall, our results suggest that discretion may be used to limit access to information relevant to political accountability.

中文摘要

透明度是政府问责制的基本要求，使公民能够获取监督、奖励和惩罚政治家及官僚所需的信息。然而，透明度不可避免地涉及被评估政府自身的自由裁量权。在本文中，我们通过比较在州和地方选举前夕通过在线平台MuckRock提交的信息自由请求与同时期在其他地方提交的请求的响应率，表明连任激励会影响透明度。由于相同的请求通常同时提交给美国各地的许多机构，我们可以评估一个机构是否拒绝请求，同时控制时间、提交者和请求内容，仅变化请求是否在选举前不久提交。我们首先记录了FOIA拒绝率与州级腐败的标准衡量指标呈正相关。我们的主要分析随后表明，在高腐败环境中，州和市政机构在（州和市）选举前对FOIA请求的拒绝和延迟更高。总体而言，我们的结果表明，自由裁量权可能被用来限制获取与政治问责相关的信息。

Scaling Point-in-Time Language Models

扩展时点语言模型

Bryan T. Kelly, Semyon Malamud, Johannes Schwab, Teng Andrea Xu

Programs：AP；JEL：C14, C45, G11, G14, G17

Abstract

Large language models trained on unrestricted internet corpora inevitably embed information from the future, introducing lookahead bias that compromises the validity of backtests and causal inference in finance and the social sciences. Point-in-time language models—trained exclusively on text available up to each calendar date—eliminate this leakage by construction, but existing efforts typically produce models that lag substantially behind their unconstrained counterparts. We show that this performance gap can be narrowed through scale. Training decoder-only transformers with up to 4 billion parameters on 1 trillion chronologically filtered tokens from FineWeb, we construct a sequence of monthly model checkpoints spanning 2013–2024. Across a range of common-sense reasoning and language understanding benchmarks, our models approach the performance of leading open-weight models of comparable size (such as Gemma-3-4B and LLaMA-7B) trained on temporally unrestricted data, although a performance gap remains on several tasks. Finally, in a strict out-of-sample economic evaluation task, portfolios built from point-in-time embeddings achieve robust positive Sharpe ratios and perform close to full-sample counterparts that violate temporal validity, indicating that chronologically consistent language models can extract economically meaningful signals without relying on look-ahead bias. We release the complete pipeline—including dataset construction, training infrastructure, and evaluation code—to enable reproducible point-in-time language modeling and to support research applications that require strict temporal validity.

中文摘要

在不受限制的互联网语料库上训练的大型语言模型不可避免地嵌入了未来的信息，引入了前瞻性偏差，损害了金融和社会科学中回测和因果推断的有效性。时点语言模型——仅使用截至每个日历日期可用的文本进行训练——通过构造消除了这种信息泄漏，但现有的努力通常产生的模型性能明显落后于其不受限制的对应模型。我们表明，这种性能差距可以通过规模扩展来缩小。通过在来自FineWeb的1万亿个按时间顺序过滤的令牌上训练参数多达40亿的解码器专用Transformer，我们构建了一个跨越2013-2024年的月度模型检查点序列。在一系列常识推理和语言理解基准测试中，我们的模型接近在时间上不受限制的数据上训练的同等规模领先开源模型（如Gemma-3-4B和LLaMA-7B）的性能，尽管在若干任务上仍存在性能差距。最后，在一个严格的样本外经济评估任务中，基于时点嵌入构建的投资组合实现了稳健的正夏普比率，并且表现接近违反时间有效性的全样本对应组合，这表明时间一致的语言模型可以在不依赖前瞻性偏差的情况下提取具有经济意义的信号。我们发布了完整的流程——包括数据集构建、训练基础设施和评估代码——以实现可复现的时点语言建模，并支持需要严格时间有效性的研究应用。

Complex Modern Portfolio Theory

复杂现代投资组合理论

Oliver Hellum, Theis I. Jensen, Bryan T. Kelly, Semyon Malamud

Programs：AP；JEL：C49, G1, G11

Abstract

The literature has long wrestled with the practical usefulness of Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT), and extensive evidence shows its performance decreases rapidly with the number of assets (N). We present several new and counterintuitive facts about MPT. Most importantly, the performance of MPT in fact increases with N once the number of assets exceeds the number of training observations (T). This finding holds in a variety of settings: in conjunction with popular portfolio regularization methods, in a variety of asset universes, and when T is large or small.

中文摘要

文献长期以来一直在探讨现代投资组合理论（MPT）的实际有用性，大量证据表明其性能随着资产数量（N）的增加而迅速下降。我们提出了关于MPT的几个新的且反直觉的事实。最重要的是，一旦资产数量超过训练观测值数量（T），MPT的性能实际上会随着N的增加而提高。这一发现在多种设置下成立：结合流行的投资组合正则化方法、在各种资产类别中，以及当T较大或较小时。

How Much Has Shale Gas Saved U.S. Consumers?

页岩气为美国消费者节省了多少钱？

Lucas W. Davis

Programs：EEE, IO, PR；JEL：Q41, Q42, Q48

Abstract

It may seem like a distant memory now, but as of the mid-2000s, U.S. natural gas production had been flat for a decade, and the U.S. was importing liquefied natural gas (LNG), with plans to import much more. Then shale gas happened. Advances in hydraulic fracturing and horizontal drilling caused U.S. natural gas production to increase significantly, and the U.S. went from being a net importer of natural gas to being the world's largest exporter. This paper calculates how much shale gas has saved U.S. natural gas consumers. Using price differences between the United States, Europe and Japan, we calculate that U.S. natural gas consumers have saved $3.1-$4.3 trillion between 2007 and 2025, equivalent to $164-$227 billion annually. Access to low-price U.S. natural gas has been particularly valuable during major supply shocks such as the war in Ukraine, and the benefits of shale gas have been experienced broadly across sectors and states.

中文摘要

现在可能已是遥远的记忆，但截至2000年代中期，美国天然气产量已停滞十年，美国正在进口液化天然气（LNG），并计划进口更多。随后页岩气出现了。水力压裂和水平钻井技术的进步使美国天然气产量大幅增加，美国从天然气净进口国转变为全球最大的出口国。本文计算了页岩气为美国天然气消费者节省了多少钱。利用美国、欧洲和日本之间的价格差异，我们计算出美国天然气消费者在2007年至2025年间节省了3.1-4.3万亿美元，相当于每年1640-2270亿美元。在乌克兰战争等重大供应冲击期间，获得低价美国天然气的机会尤其宝贵，页岩气的好处已广泛惠及各个行业和州。

Will The University Endowment Tax Slow Scientific Progress? Evidence from Elite Economics PhD Programs

大学捐赠税会减缓科学进步吗？来自精英经济学博士项目的证据

Joshua Angrist, Marc Diederichs, Glenn Ellison

Programs：ED, LS, PR；JEL：A23, I23, I28

Abstract

The 2025 university endowment tax hike and other sources of financial pressure may lead the schools that train the most prolific economics researchers to reduce graduate enrollment. Will this affect long-run research output? We use a novel sample of MIT Economics PhD program applicants to estimate the research value-added of eight elite schools. Our estimates mitigate selection bias by controlling for MIT admissions committee rankings—a remarkably strong predictor of long-run research success—and for applicant aspirations as revealed by their application portfolios. While rank controls substantially reduce estimated gaps between elite and non-elite graduates, large differences in value-added remain. Graduates of high-tax and other top-eight schools produce 60-75% more impact-adjusted publications than do comparable graduates from non-top-eight US schools. The elite-school advantage is especially pronounced for top five journal publications. Differences in research success within the elite tier, however, are relatively modest. Explaining Movements in Government Debt

解释政府债务的变动

Tatiana Kirsanova, Eric M. Leeper, Campbell B. Leith, Ding Liu

Programs：EFG；JEL：E50, E62

Abstract

Standard New Keynesian models with time-consistent policy predict minimal debt responses to conventional shocks, as a debt stabilization bias dominates tax-smoothing motives. We show that two mechanisms can generate debt movements of the magnitude observed in the data: increases in policymaker myopia and declines in real interest rates, such as during flight-to-safety episodes. Other potential drivers—changes in markups, debt maturity, government transfers, or large recessions—cannot account for such fluctuations.

中文摘要

State Dependence of Monetary Policy During Global Supply Chain Disruptions

全球供应链中断期间货币政策的路径依赖性

Xiwen Bai, Jesús Fernández-Villaverde, Yiliang Li, Francesco Zanetti

Programs：EFG；JEL：C32, E31, E32, E52

Abstract

We study how global supply chain disruptions affect monetary policy transmission. Post-pandemic evidence indicates surging transportation costs, goods-market imbalances, and rising prices. We develop a model in which logistical bottlenecks (upstream slack coexisting with downstream shortages) steepen the aggregate supply curve. This convexity amplifies price responses to monetary policy while dampening output effects. Threshold VAR and Local Projection estimates are consistent with this mechanism: during disruptions, contractionary policy reduces prices more at smaller output cost, easing the stabilization trade-off.

中文摘要

我们研究了全球供应链中断如何影响货币政策的传导。疫情后的证据表明，运输成本飙升、商品市场失衡以及物价上涨。我们构建了一个模型，其中物流瓶颈（上游闲置与下游短缺并存）使总供给曲线变得陡峭。这种凸性放大了价格对货币政策的反应，同时抑制了产出效应。阈值向量自回归（VAR）和局部投影（Local Projection）的估计结果与这一机制一致：在供应链中断期间，紧缩性政策以较小的产出成本更有效地降低价格，从而缓解了稳定目标的权衡困境。
