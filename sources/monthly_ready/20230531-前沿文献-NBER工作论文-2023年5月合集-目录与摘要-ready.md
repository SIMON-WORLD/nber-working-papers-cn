#### 1. Chinese Insurance Markets: Developments and Prospects {#31292}

【**Abstract**】: This paper studies the implications of central bank credibility for long-run inflation and inflation dynamics. We introduce central bank lack of commitment into a standard non-linear New Keynesian economy with sticky-price monopolistically competitive firms. Inflation is driven by the interaction of lack of commitment and the economic environment. We show that long-run inflation increases following an unanticipated permanent increase in the labor wedge or decrease in the elasticity of substitution across varieties. In the transition, inflation overshoots and then gradually declines. Quantitatively, the inflation response is large, as is the welfare loss from lack of commitment relative to inflation targeting.

【**摘要**】：本文研究了央行可信度对长期通胀和通胀动态的影响。我们将央行缺乏承诺引入一个标准的非线性新凯恩斯主义经济模型中，该模型包含具有粘性价格和垄断竞争的企业。通胀由缺乏承诺与经济环境之间的相互作用驱动。我们表明，在劳动楔子意外永久性增加或品种间替代弹性下降之后，长期通胀会上升。在过渡期间，通胀会先过度调整，然后逐渐下降。从数量上看，通胀反应很大，与通胀目标制相比，缺乏承诺造成的福利损失也很大。

#### 87. Who Benefits from State Corporate Tax Cuts? A Local Labor Market Approach with Heterogeneous Firms: Further Results {#31206}

【**Author**】: Juan Carlos Suárez Serrato, Owen M. Zidar

【**Abstract**】: This paper estimates the incidence of state corporate taxes using new data and methods for estimating the effects on profits. We extend Suarez Serrato and Zidar (2016) by developing two new identification approaches that use the effects of business taxes on the labor demand of incumbent firms and local productivity to identify profit effects. We estimate these reduced-form effects using data from Census, show how reduced-form moments identify incidence and parameters, and provide incidence estimates using a variety of reduced-form approaches as well as a structural model. #### 88. Work Hours Mismatch {#31205}

【**Author**】: Marta Lachowska, Alexandre Mas, Raffaele Saggio, Stephen A. Woodbury

【**Abstract**】: This paper uses a revealed preference approach applied to administrative data from Washington to document and characterize work-hour constraints. Workers have limited discretion over hours at a given employer, and there is substantial mismatch between workers who prefer long hours and employers that provide short hours. Voluntary job transitions suggest that the ratio of the marginal rate of substitution of earnings for hours (MRS) to the wage rate is on the order of 0.5-0.6 for prime-age workers. The average absolute deviation between observed hours and optimal hours is about 15%, and constraints on hours are particularly acute among low-wage workers. On average, observed hours tend to be less than preferred levels, and workers would require a 12% higher wage with their current employer to be as well off as they would be after moving to an employer offering ideal hours. These findings suggest that hours constraints are an equilibrium feature of the labor market because long-hour jobs are costly to employers, and that employers offer high-wage/long-hour packages to increase their overall value of employment.

【**摘要**】：本文采用显示性偏好方法，利用华盛顿州的行政数据来记录和描述工作时间约束的特征。工人在特定雇主处对工作时间的自主权有限，并且偏好长时间工作的工人与提供短时间工作的雇主之间存在显著的错配。自愿的工作转换表明，对于壮年工人而言，收入对工作时间的边际替代率与工资率的比率大约在0.5-0.6之间。观察到的工时与最优工时之间的平均绝对偏差约为15%，而低工资工人面临的时间约束尤为严重。平均而言，观察到的工时往往低于偏好水平，工人需要在其当前雇主处获得12%的更高工资，才能达到与转到提供理想工时的雇主后相同的福利水平。这些发现表明，工时约束是劳动力市场的一个均衡特征，因为长时间工作对雇主而言成本高昂，并且雇主提供高工资/长时间工作组合是为了提高就业的整体价值。

【**NBER**】: NBER Working Paper No. 31205，https://www.nber.org/papers/w31205

#### 89. War Discourse and Disaster Premia: 160 Years of Evidence from Stock and Bond Markets {#31204}

【**Author**】: David Hirshleifer, Dat Y. Mai, Kuntara Pukthuanthong

【**Abstract**】: Using a semi-supervised topic model on 7,000,000 New York Times articles spanning 160 years, we test whether topics of media discourse predict future stock market excess returns to test rational and behavioral hypotheses about market valuation of disaster risk. #### 90. Randomized Regulation: The Impact of Minimum Quality Standards on Health Markets {#31203}

【**Author**】: Guadalupe Bedoya, Jishnu Das, Amy Dolinger

【**Abstract**】: We report results from the first randomization of a regulatory reform in the health sector. The reform established minimum quality standards for patient safety, an issue that has become increasingly salient following the Ebola and COVID-19 epidemics. In our experiment, all 1348 health facilities in three Kenyan counties were classifi ed into 273 markets, and the markets were then randomly allocated to treatment and control groups. Government inspectors visited health facilities and, depending on the results of their inspection, recommended closure or a timeline for improvements. The intervention increased compliance with patient safety measures in both public and private facilities (more so in the latter) and reallocated patients from private to public facilities without increasing out-of-pocket payments or decreasing facility use. In treated markets, improvements were equally marked throughout the quality distribution, consistent with a simple model of vertical differentiation in oligopolies. Our paper thus establishes the use of experimental techniques to study regulatory reforms and, in doing so, shows that minimum standards can improve quality across the board without adversely affecting utilization.

【**摘要**】：我们报告了卫生领域首次监管改革随机化实验的结果。该改革建立了患者安全的最低质量标准，这一问题在埃博拉和新冠疫情后日益突出。在我们的实验中，肯尼亚三个县的所有1348家医疗机构被划分为273个市场，然后这些市场被随机分配到处理组和对照组。政府检查员访问医疗机构，并根据检查结果建议关闭或设定改进时间表。该干预措施提高了公立和私立机构（后者更为显著）对患者安全措施的合规性，并将患者从私立机构重新分配到公立机构，同时未增加自付费用或减少机构使用率。在处理组市场中，质量分布各处的改善同样显著，这与寡头垄断中垂直差异化的简单模型一致。因此，我们的论文确立了使用实验技术研究监管改革的方法，并在此过程中表明，最低标准可以在不负面影响利用率的情况下全面改善质量。

#### 91. The Rise of E-Wallets and Buy-Now-Pay-Later: Payment Competition, Credit Expansion, and Consumer Behavior {#31202}

【**Author**】: Wenlong Bian, Lin William Cong, Yang Ji

【**Abstract**】: The past decade has witnessed a phenomenal rise of digital wallets, and the COVID-19 pandemic further accelerated their adoption globally. Such e-wallets provide not only a conduit to external bank accounts but also internal payment options, including the ever-popular Buy-Now-Pay-Later (BNPL). We examine, for the first time, e-wallet transactions matched with merchant and consumer information from a world-leading provider based in China, with over 1 billion users globally and a business model that other e-wallet providers quickly converge to. We document that internal payment options, especially BNPL, dominate both online and on-site transactions. BNPL has greatly expanded credit access on the extensive margin through its adoption in two-sided payment markets. While BNPL crowds out other e-wallet payment options, it expands FinTech credit to underserved consumers. Exploiting a randomized experiment, we also find that e-wallet credit through BNPL substantially boosts consumer spending. Nevertheless, users, especially those relying on e-wallets as their sole credit source, carefully moderate borrowing when incurring interest charges. The insights likely prove informative for economies transitioning from cash-heavy to cashless societies where digital payments and FinTech credit see the largest growth and market potential.

【**摘要**】：过去十年见证了数字钱包的惊人崛起，而新冠疫情进一步加速了其在全球的普及。此类电子钱包不仅提供通往外部银行账户的渠道，还提供内部支付选项，包括广受欢迎的“先买后付”（BNPL）。我们首次研究了来自一家全球领先的中国提供商（拥有超过10亿全球用户，其商业模式正被其他电子钱包提供商迅速趋同）的电子钱包交易，并匹配了商户和消费者信息。我们记录到，内部支付选项，尤其是BNPL，在线上和现场交易中均占主导地位。BNPL通过其在双边支付市场中的采用，极大地扩展了信贷的广延边际。虽然BNPL挤出了其他电子钱包支付选项，但它将金融科技信贷扩展到了服务不足的消费者。利用一项随机实验，我们还发现，通过BNPL提供的电子钱包信贷显著提升了消费者支出。然而，用户，尤其是那些将电子钱包作为唯一信贷来源的用户，在产生利息费用时会谨慎地调节借贷。这些见解很可能为那些从现金密集型社会向无现金社会转型的经济体提供信息，在这些经济体中，数字支付和金融科技信贷拥有最大的增长和市场潜力。
